FUT continue breakthrough year by securing EPL playoffs berth over G2

FUT battled past G2 in a hard-fought 2-1 series to clinch the penultimate spot in the playoffs of ESL Pro League Season 23, recovering from a blowout loss on their pick of Mirage (4-13) with narrow 13-11 wins on Dust2 and Overpass to earn an arena appearance in front of a crowd in Sweden.

The team around Aulon "⁠Krabeni⁠" Fazlija enjoyed a meteoric rise up the rankings in 2025 and has continued to impress in the new year with their breakthrough Big Event appearances. They fell just shy of reaching the playoffs at IEM Krakow and PGL Cluj-Napoca, losing in the former to G2 in a match that would have put them through to the Tauron Arena, but have gotten revenge online in EPL to secure an appearance in the Annexet.

G2 had a clear game plan when the series got underway: take the fight to FUT and get ahead of their aggression. That mentality and disruptive effort paid off in spades on Mirage, where FUT struggled to get much of a look in and only scrounged four rounds by the break — with three coming from clutches, including a 1v4 by Nikita "⁠cmtry⁠" Samolotov. G2 didn't let off the gas after the swap and quickly closed out the map with a five-round spree, led by 1.82 and 1.71-rated showings from Matúš "⁠MATYS⁠" Šimko and Mario "⁠malbsMd⁠" Samayoa.

FUT won their first pistol of the series on Dust2 and took the conversion to go up 2-0, but G2 again played to combat the aggression and won five straight as the map began to look much like Mirage. But this time, FUT adapted and poked holes in G2's defaults to go on a 10-1 run to hit map point, with a solo site hold by Nikita "⁠HeavyGod⁠" Martynenko providing G2 a comeback opportunity that was only shut down at the eleventh hour.

FUT added another pistol win on the Overpass decider to go up 3-0, but again fell flat on the offense against a red-hot malbsMd. The Guatemalan rifler was nigh-unstoppable in Bathrooms and led G2's charge to an 8-4 lead, but completely disappeared after the swap as FUT began to recover.

Džiugas "⁠dziugss⁠" Steponavičius, cmtry, and Laurențiu "⁠lauNX⁠" Țârlea repeatedly found multi-kills and openings to limit G2's options in the second half, with a clutch by MATYS offering one of the only glimpses of hope in an otherwise lackluster T side. malbsMd's terrible second half finally turned around when he found a critical triple kill to keep G2 alive at 12-10, but he had come online too late as FUT converted in the round after to clinch the series.
