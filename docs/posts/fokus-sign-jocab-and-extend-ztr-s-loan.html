FOKUS sign jocab and extend ztr's loan

FOKUS has announced the addition of Jacob "⁠jocab⁠" Nerheden to its starting five as Sebastian "⁠volt⁠" Maloș made the move to INFINITE.

The 21-year-old Swede is a product of the Young Ninjas youth system, where he played from January 2024 to November 2025 before joining Johnny Speeds and posting a 1.14 rating across 209 maps with the Swedish team.

The Swede joins FOKUS amid a revamp in his former team that left their roster down to just him and Ludvig "⁠HEAP⁠" Alonso before the announcements of Hampus "⁠hampus⁠" Poser and William "⁠draken⁠" Sundin.

This is the second big change for FOKUS this off-season after they lost Georgi "⁠Jorko⁠" Mitev to Liquid and brought on Paavo "⁠podi⁠" Heiskanen as his replacement.
