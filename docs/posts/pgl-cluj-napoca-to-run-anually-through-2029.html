PGL Cluj-Napoca to run anually through 2029

Emil Boc, the mayor of Cluj-Napoca, has announced via a post on Instagram that the Transylvanian city will host PGL events in 2027, 2028, and 2029, extending the partnership between the city and PGL to a five-year commitment after the first two iterations in 2025 and 2026.

PGL Cluj-Napoca has been the first tournament of the year in 2025 and 2026 for PGL, taking place in February, and the Romanian TO already has date blocks secured for February 13-21 in 2027 and 09-21 in 2028.

PGL Cluj-Napoca has grown greatly from one year to the next, from featuring just four top 10 teams in its first iteration to missing only two of them in 2026, making it a premier event on the calendar in its sophomore year.

The tournament's playoffs for future events will return to the city's multi-purpose BT Arena in front of a crowd, the same as in 2025 and 2026.
