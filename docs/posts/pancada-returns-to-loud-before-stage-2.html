This marks a reunion with the LOUD core of Matias "saadhak" Delipetro and Felipe "Less" de Loyola that won Champions in 2022.

After winning Champions, LOUD lost two stars to rival team Sentinels, who acquired both Gustavo "Sacy" Rossi and pANcada. The 2023 season was troublesome for Sentinels; the team did not meet the expectations with the talent the roster had, finishing seventh in the league and failing to qualify for any Masters tournament in 2023.

Heading into 2024, the immediate success Sentinels had with substitute Jordan "Zellsis" Montemurro inadvertently led to the benching of pANcada. pANcada would not come back into the roster and the organization announced his restricted free agency with no buyout.

LOUD were one of the best teams from VCT Americas at both Kickoff and Masters Madrid. However, they slumped in Stage 1 of VCT 2024 and lost to 100 Thieves 100 Thieves North America Rank #2 bang Sean Bezerra Cryocells Matthew Panganiban eeiu Daniel Vucenovic Boostio Kelden Pupello Asuna Peter Mazuryk in the knockout round of the regional playoffs, leading them to miss out on Masters Shanghai.
