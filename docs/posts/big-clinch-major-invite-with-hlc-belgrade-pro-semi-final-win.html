BIG clinch Major invite with HLC Belgrade PRO semi-final win

BIG have laid claim to a place in the IEM Cologne Major after moving into the grand final of HLC Belgrade PRO 2026 over magic.

Absent from top LANs in 2026, BIG have been slogging through a number of local LANs in the weeks building up to the Major invite deadline on April 6, notching a runner-up finish at BC Game Masters Championship Season 1 in Bucharest, Romania, and a lackluster 13-16th showing at Roman Imperium Cup VII in Vila Nova de Gaia, Portugal.

In a last-ditch effort to lock in an invitation for their home Major, BIG signed up for HLC Belgrade PRO, where a top-three finish would guarantee their spot. After a nail-biting win over ex-Zero Tenacity in the quarterfinals, BIG managed to take down magic in the semifinals thanks to an impressive performance from Benjamin "⁠blameF⁠" Bremer, who averaged a 1.70 rating in the series.
