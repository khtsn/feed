Inner Circle to attend PGL Bucharest as Imperial withdraw "to prioritize the Major race"

Inner Circle have been invited to PGL Bucharest as a replacement for Imperial, the tournament organizer announced on Friday.

Imperial had secured a spot at the Romanian LAN by winning the South American closed qualifier. MIBR, the losing finalist, were later invited to the main event through the global VRS following paiN's withdrawal.

In a statement to Dust2 Brasil, Imperial explained that they have "decided to prioritize the Major race."

"Since PGL Bucharest ends after the cut-off date for the ranking that determines the invites, the points accrued there would not impact our campaign," the Brazilian organization added.
