Virtus.pro add mir as IGL

Virtus.pro have announced that Nikolay "⁠mir⁠" Bityukov has returned to the roster to serve as the team's IGL, the 30-year-old having last been a part of the team across 2023-2024.

The organization have also said that Vladimir "⁠b1st⁠" Krasikov and Vadim "⁠tO0RO⁠" Arkov will be returning to VP.Prodigy for their next events, but did not explicitly state whether or not this was a long-term change.

mir returns to Virtus.pro having not played regular competitive Counter-Strike since 2024, his last official as a full-time part of Virtus.pro's roster coming in April of 2024 at IEM Chengdu. Since that time he has only played one HLTV-recorded official, standing in for Timur "⁠FL4MUS⁠" Marev at IEM Katowice 2025.

The news represents the first part of Virtus.pro's rebuild following the removal of Ilya "⁠Perfecto⁠" Zalutskiy, Evgenii "⁠FL1T⁠" Lebedev and Petr "⁠fame⁠" Bolyshev after their torrid start to the year.

After losing five of five competitive series across the start of 2026, including to RUSTEC and WW, and slipping to #100 in the VRS, Virtus.pro put their CS division on hold and benched and transfer-listed the aforementioned trio.
