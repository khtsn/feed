Super DraculaN semi-finals set

Digital Crusade Super DraculaN Season 1 has reached the semi-final stage after two teams topped their groups and two more came through the quarter-finals.

Off the back of their Major debut in Cologne, Sharks made waves in Bucharest after earning top billing in Group A with wins over Eternal Fire, Inner Circle and Acend.

They will rematch Inner Circle on the upper side of the single elimination bracket next on Saturday after the Ukrainian side secured a swift win over Walczaki, powered by a 2.09-rated performance from #11 Prospect David "⁠Dawy⁠" Bibik.

Echo went into the tournament as underdogs, but a broken nose and carry performances from Jamie "⁠leakz⁠" Bugge West Jensen and Jason "⁠salazar⁠" Salazar later, they topped Group B to face off against Acend, who reached the top-four stage with a convincing victory over INFINITE.
