G2 outclass Astralis in BLAST Rivals

G2 have cruised into the playoffs of BLAST Rivals Season 1 thanks to a 2-0 victory over Astralis, outclassing their opposition on Dust2 (13-5) and Overpass (13-9).

With their win G2 have earned an upper bracket bout with Vitality, where the winner will earn a semi-final berth, whilst Astralis will have to fight for their tournament life against FUT.

This series was smooth sailing for G2 as they simply overran Astralis across the majority of the match, displaying impressive individual levels and excellent protocols on both maps.

Astralis meanwhile were somewhat out of sorts, Gytis "⁠ryu⁠" Glušauskas posting an absolute stinker whilst Love "⁠phzy⁠" Smidebrant and Jakob "⁠jabbi⁠" Nygaard also struggled.

G2 kicked the series off by strolling to victory on Dust2, dominating the T side with potent firepower and clean trades before sealing the deal comfortably on the CT side thanks to a second round force-buy win.

Overpass was much the same story, G2 romping to a massive half-time lead with a dominant CT side and pushing onward to 12 once guns came up on their offense. Astralis showed a bit of fight, but it was on the favoured CT side, and G2 enjoyed some high-impact moments from their individuals to get them over the line with little fuss.
