Official: fnatic replace Maden with Br4tkO

fnatic have announced the signing of Viktor "⁠Br4tkO⁠" Kondratets from Lazer Cats, confirming a previous report by HLTV. The 18-year-old will replace Pavle "⁠Maden⁠" Bošković, who moves to the team's bench after just three months.

The signing bring fnatic one step closer to a full-Ukrainian roster after the additions of former Passion UA trio Dmytro "⁠jambo⁠" Semera, Nikita "⁠jackasmo⁠" Skyba, and Rodion "⁠fEAR⁠" Smyk, with only the organization's Swedish veteran, Freddy "⁠KRIMZ⁠" Johansson, left standing.

Br4tkO averaged a 1.05 rating over 98 maps during his nearly year-long stint with Lazer Cats, and has already spent time practicing with fnatic.
