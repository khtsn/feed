StarLadder announce tier-2 VRS LAN for April

The Eastern European-focused LORGAR RANKINGS will run from April 25-26.

StarLadder have announced a new LAN event they will be hosting in partnership with LORGAR, LORGAR RANKINGS, set to run from April 25-26, with qualifiers for the event beginning in March.

The single-elimination BO3 LAN final will take place in a studio environment in the Schengen Area, touting a $50,000 prize pool. The event will feature four teams invited from the European VRS at the April 6 cutoff date, rounded out by four teams from the event's closed qualifier.

The event will use various regional open qualifiers hosted on FACEIT, running from March 12-15, sourcing teams from a variety of Eastern and Southern European countries such as Poland, Czechia, and Ukraine, as well as broader regions like the Balkans and Baltics.

The top 16 teams from these national open qualifiers will move to the closed qualifier, running from March 20 to April 10, where the top four will move on to the LAN final.
