G2 make playoffs after 2-1 against EDG

G2 Esports have defeated EDward Gaming 2-1 in the group D winner's match. They will now advance to playoffs as the final top seed, while EDG descend to the decider match. This victory guarantees at least one team from each region will appear in playoffs.

Icebox went EDG's way, taking G2's map 13-8. G2 answered in kind, closing out a close Haven 13-11. They then denied a comeback on the decider, with another 13-11 on Bind.

The victory means G2 earns a break prior to playoffs, while EDG will need to defeated either Paper Rex or FUT Esports in the group's decider match.
