kAlash departs HOTU

Altair "⁠kAlash⁠" Askarov, who was on loan with HOTU from AMKAL since October 2025, will no longer play on the team, joining Suleımen "⁠dukefissura⁠" Orazaly as the second successive change following a 9-12th exit at IEM Rio.

kAlash averaged a 1.03 rating on HOTU since joining in October 2025, not long after AMKAL's dissolution in the summer, and was a part of the team peaking in 19th place on the VRS before a mid-season drop-off that put them outside of the Major qualification range.

During that Major race, kAlash helped HOTU reach second at IstanbuLAN and win Roman Imperium Cup V. The team later played at the top-tier IEM Rio 2026, going out in 9-12th place with losses to Aurora and Natus Vincere with a victory over Legacy in between.

"During this time he became an important part of the team, helped us in key matches, and contributed to the team's results," the organization posted on Telegram. "Thank you for the work done, professionalism and full commitment."

HOTU is now down to:

David "⁠n0rb3r7⁠" Danielyan

Rustem "⁠mou⁠" Telepov (coach)

Suleımen "⁠dukefissura⁠" Orazaly (bench)
