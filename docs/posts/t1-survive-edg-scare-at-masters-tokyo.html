T1 survive EDG scare at Masters: Tokyo

T1 T1 Korea Unranked iNTRO Kang Seung-gyun (강승균) xeta Son Seon-ho (손선호) Sayaplayer Ha Jeong-woo (하정우) ban Joseph Seungmin Oh Munchkin Byeon Sang-beom (변상범) Carpe Lee Jae-hyeok (이재혁) beat out EDward Gaming EDward Gaming China Unranked ZmjjKK Zheng Yongkang (郑永康) nobody Wang Senxu (王森旭) Muggle Tang Shijun (唐时俊) CHICHOO Wan Shunji (万顺治) Smoggy Zhang Zhao (张钊) Haodong Guo Haodong (郭浩东) in a nailbiter of a series 2-1. The Koreans took victory in the first match of Group A at Masters: Tokyo, while EDward Gaming are one match away from elimination.

T1 survived a scare from EDG on their map of Fracture. The Koreans continued a trend from the VCT Pacific Playoffs of winning pistols by taking both in the map. Despite ZmjjKK 's best efforts with 23 kills Sayaplayer managed to drop 26 of his own to lead T1 to a 13-10 map win.

EDG's map pick of Haven seemed to be a reversal of the script as the Chinese team took both pistols and the strong performance from ZmjjKK on Fracture carried over to map two. Despite a close first half, EDG demolished T1 in the second half losing only a single round with ZmjjKK and Smoggy both posting 22 kills. EDG had thrashed T1 in a 13-7 map victory to even the series 1-1.

With the momentum now on their side, EDG took the first series point to lead 12-11. However, T1 took the final round of regulation to force overtime with a fast A hit. The Koreans carried this momentum into overtime, narrowly winning a 1v5 attempt from Smoggy and cleanly taking their offensive round with a fast B hit to take the map 14-12 and the series 2-1.
