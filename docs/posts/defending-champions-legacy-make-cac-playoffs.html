Defending champions Legacy make CAC playoffs

Legacy ended day one of CS Asia Championship by defeating TYLOO in a two-map series (Overpass 19-16, Ancient 16-12).

The Brazilian squad, who beat NRG in their best-of-one opener, have now secured a playoff spot and are set to appear in the Shanghai Yuanshen Gymnasium, where they lifted the trophy seven months ago.

Legacy got off to a hot start in the series and racked up a 7-2 lead early on, with TYLOO eventually bringing it back to overtime after a strong end to both halves. Legacy then secured two map points after a flawless CT side, with Bruno "⁠latto⁠" Rebelatto's 1vs2 ensuring the Brazilian took the lead in the series.

Ancient was an equally close-fought affair, one that once again went the way of Legacy after overtime with a 16-12 scoreline.
