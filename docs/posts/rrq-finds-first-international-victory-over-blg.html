RRQ finds first international victory over BLG

In the lower bracket of the Champions group stage, the number one seed from CN Bilibili Gaming faced off against Pacific's two seed Rex Regum Qeon. The Pacific team managed to find a 2-1 victory, sending China's best team home early without a win.

All three maps were convincing wins, with BLG taking Sunset 13-7, and RRQ taking Lotus and Abyss 13-8 and 13-4 respectively.

RRQ will go on to face MIBR to decide who will go to playoffs, while BLG becomes the second CN team to be eliminated.
