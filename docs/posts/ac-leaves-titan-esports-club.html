AC joined Titan in 2024 as part of an early mid-season rebuild attempt. Following a disappointing result at Kickoff that saw them fall short of the playoffs, they signed him and integrated him into the team in place of QiuYe . The changes continued throughout the course of the season, with the team also making roster moves ahead of Stage 2. Titan ended the regular season with a 3-7 record, ranking them last in China.

Since the end of the season, TEC has made significant efforts to climb the standings in 2025. LockM , who joined ahead of 2024, also retired.

AC was the team's Controller player throughout the 2024 season, most often on Omen or Viper. In the 2024 OFF//SEASON, Titan participated in WALL-E Cup, FGC Invitational, and Superb Cup. In the events, AC was used in different roles, such as the Initiator or Sentinel roles. He did not play in the Superb Cup, which was the last off-season event for the team.
