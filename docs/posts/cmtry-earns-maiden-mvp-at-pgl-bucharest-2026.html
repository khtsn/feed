cmtry earns maiden MVP at PGL Bucharest 2026

Nikita "⁠cmtry⁠" Samolotov has been awarded the PGL Bucharest 2026 HLTV x 1XBET MVP after leading FUT to the title in Romania.

It was a tight battle, with Džiugas "⁠dziugss⁠" Steponavičius, Jakob "⁠jabbi⁠" Nygaard, Dmytro "⁠dem0n⁠" Myroshnychenko, and Laurențiu "⁠lauNX⁠" Țârlea throwing their hat into the ring at one point or another.

FUT's remarkably close statistics in map wins.

FUT won Bucharest as a team: lauNX was in the thick of the MVP race after map two of the final, dem0n had three peaks in the final, and dziugss was the rating leader overall.

But in the end, cmtry's 1.28 rating in map wins and solid grand final (where dziugss was quiet before the map three loss, and dem0n entered the race late with a big peak in map four) made sure the medal remained his. cmtry also had by far the best Swing in round wins (+10.6%) in his team, even though the quintet were all close in KPRW.

"I'm feeling so good," he told HLTV after the match. "I always say it after the game, I'm so excited, I'm so happy. I'm so happy for achieving MVP, I'm so happy for winning this tournament, I'm so happy for everything. So happy."
