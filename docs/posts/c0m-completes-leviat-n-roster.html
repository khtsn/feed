C0M completes Leviatán roster

Leviatán Leviatán Latin America South Rank #1 tex Ian Botsch kiNgg Francisco Aravena Mazino Roberto Rivas aspas Erick Santos C0M Corbin Lee have picked up world champion Corbin "C0M" Lee after Agustin "nzr" Ibarra was benched recently.

C0M had been with the Evil Geniuses core since 2022. The roster didn't qualify for international tournaments, with a top-eight finish at VCT North America LCQ. Following their acceptance to the VCT Americas league 2023, EG made their international debut at LOCK//IN, where they fell to Talon Esports. EG continued to make strides after narrowly qualifying for Americas playoffs, leading to their appearance at Masters: Tokyo and Valorant Champions.

Evil Geniuses shocked the world in Tokyo as they dominated the competition, only to be stopped by FNATIC in the grand finals. At Valorant Champions, EG continued their dominance from groups to the upper finals – following a setback by Paper Rex, EG bested LOUD in a five-map thriller. Evil Geniuses etched their place in history after exacting their revenge against Paper Rex in the Grand Finals.

Following Champions, Evil Geniuses let go of their reserve roster; the shuffled continued by the majority of their roster going their separate ways – leaving only Jawgemo and coach Potter.

After being overshadowed by KRU for most of 2021, Leviatán made a statement in their international debut at Masters: Copenhagen, placing top six. LEV continued their momentum at Champions 2022 with a playoff appearance. In 2023, after finishing outside the top four at LOCK//IN, the roster faltered finishing in fifth-place in the playoffs. Americas LCQ marked their last chance to qualify for Champions, ultimately falling short to KRU in the grand finals marking the end of their 2023 season.

Leviatán bolstered their roster with the additions of aspas and tex joining alongside Mazino, nzr, and kiNgg. The new roster made its tournament debut at Superdome Colombia, hoisting the trophy. Despite their victory Leviatàn opted to bench nzr, stating that it was a sudden and unexpected change.
