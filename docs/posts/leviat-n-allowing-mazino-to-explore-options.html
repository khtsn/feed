Leviatán allowing Mazino to explore options

After spending two seasons with LEVIATÁN LEVIATÁN Latin America South Unranked tex Ian Botsch kiNgg Francisco Aravena aspas Erick Santos C0M Corbin Lee , Roberto "Mazino" Rivas announced that he will now be allowed to explore options with a new team.

He revealed that his contract with the organization is set to expire, but that he is still a restricted free agent.

"My contract with Leviatán is coming to an end soon, and even if we are exploring an extension, I've also been allowed to explore all my options as a Restricted Free Agent," Mazino said.

Before being signed by Leviatán following the 2022 season, Mazino played for KRÜ Esports KRÜ Esports Latin America South Unranked keznit Angelo Mori mta Nicolás González Shyy Fabian Usnayo Melser Marco Eliot Machuca Amaro heat Olavo Marcelo , with whom he'd reached back-to-back Champions berths, highlighted by a semifinal finish at Champions Berlin. He, alongside keznit , was signed by Leviatán as the team's fifth and sixth player. The duo were also founding members of the Argentinian organization. keznit has since returned to KRÜ.

The former CS:GO player played a key role in Leviatán's success after a less-than-ideal start to their 2024 season. After being eliminated in the Swiss Stage of Masters Shanghai, they parted ways with head coach goked and promoted assistant Itopata to head coach.

Most well-known for his play on Harbor and Breach, Mazino and Leviatán enjoyed a successful 2024, lifting the organization's first trophy at VCT: Americas Stage 2 and achieving a third-placed finish at Champions Seoul.

"I'm open to any region and I will try my best to win," he said.
