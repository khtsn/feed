G2 and Liquid swap malbsMd and NertZ

NertZ will reunite with sAw and SunPayus in G2

G2 and Liquid have completed a trade that sends Mario "⁠malbsMd⁠" Samayoa to Liquid and Guy "⁠NertZ⁠" Iluz to G2, making Liquid an American team once again while the Israeli rejoins his former ENCE coach Eetu "⁠sAw⁠" Saha and teammate Álvaro "⁠SunPayus⁠" García.

G2 bids farewell to malbsMd

The trade has Major implications for Liquid, whose spot in Cologne was in peril as they sit outside of the top 17 in Europe with the April 6 cut-off fast approaching.

Returning to an American core puts them back in a qualifying spot in the Americas VRS and releases some of the pressure as they head to BLAST Rotterdam after Roman Imperium Cup VI, where they are currently playing with Justinas "⁠jL⁠" Lekavicius as a stand-in.

malbsMd struggled to find his best form after the departures of NiKo and m0NESY from G2

On the other side of the trade, NertZ can put behind a period with Liquid in which he underperformed compared to his breakout 2023 — when he was named Rookie of the Year and 13th best player of the year — and 2024 seasons.

NertZ's time in Liquid saw his rating drop to 1.05, a far cry from his 1.13 in ENCE and 1.12 in HEROIC, which he will hope to bounce back from under the orders of sAw.

Both teams still need to play five matches with their new players in order to be eligible before the Major cut-off, which explains why G2 will attend BC.Game Masters and Stake Ranked Episode 1 and Liquid — whose attendance at the Major is still far from secured — will attend DraculaN and WINAMAX Counter-Strike 2 Trophy on top of BLAST Rotterdam.
