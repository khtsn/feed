Wronski steps down from Global Esports, retires

1 day ago

Global Esports Global Esports Asia-Pacific Rank #36 Bazzi Park Jun-ki (박준기) t3xture Kim Na-ra (김나라) SkRossi Ganesh Gangadhar Lightningfast Abhirup Choudhury Monyet Cahya Nugraha AYRIN Jordan He announced Michael "WRONSKI" Wronski's departure, who later revealed he is ending his esports career short. WRONSKI has not played since VCT Pacific's Week 5 in late April.

The VCT's only Australian player was onboarded in mid-October — at the same time as Korean up and comers Kim "t3xture" Na-ra (김나라) and Park "Bazzi" Jun-ki (박준기) — after a year dominating the local scene with ORDER ORDER Inactive WRONSKI Michael Wronski rDeeW Riley Wilkinson Texta Matthew O’Rourke disk0 Cameron Mac Maple Elvin Sun . The three players were part of Global's plan to build a ten-man roster, a plan the team completed by signing on Cahya "Monyet" Nugraha.

Oceania's no longer has a player in the VCT. (Photo by Riot Games)

Global struggled in the VCT Pacific in this opening year, finding just three spaced out wins across the league. WRONSKI wasn't fielded in those matches: he was absent for the team's win in Week 4, and made his final appearance in Global's Week 5 match, where they lost 2-1 to eventual league champions Paper Rex Paper Rex Asia-Pacific Rank #1 mindfreak Aaron Leonhart Jinggg Wang Jing Jie f0rsakeN Jason Susanto d4v41 Khalish Rusyaidee something Ilya Petrov .

WRONSKI stated on Twitter he has decided to "end [his] career short," explaining that "this industry isn't for [him]."

Global Esports have a chance at redemption in the Pacific LCQ, where one Champions 2023 spot is up for grabs. The team will debut against Rex Regum Qeon Rex Regum Qeon Asia-Pacific Rank #21 2ge James Goopio EJAY Eroll Jule Delfin xffero David Monangin Lmemore Hagai Kristen Yesyurun Emman Emmanuel Morales fl1pzjder Saibani Rahmad on July 19 and, should they win that match, against ZETA DIVISION ZETA DIVISION Japan Rank #2 Laz Ushida Koji crow Maruoka Tomoaki Dep Yuuma Hisamoto SugarZ3ro Shota Watanabe TENNN Tenta Asai the following day. Global lost to both squads during league play.
