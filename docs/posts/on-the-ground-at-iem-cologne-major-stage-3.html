Take a look back at our interviews from day two

The second day of the IEM Cologne Major featured two big upsets, with BetBoom and 9z advancing to the 2-0 pool after beating Falcons and Vitality, respectively.

After the former, Kirill "⁠Boombl4⁠" Mikhailov said this was "the most unexpected season" of his career, in which his team had to play with two stand-ins to make an unlikely run to the Major before the cut-off, and has continued performing.

Read more

Boombl4: "It's the most unexpected season of my career"

9z's coach, Bruno "⁠BIT⁠" Fukuda Lima, discussed the team's progress and getting revenge over Vitality for Rotterdam, adding another notable win to the Argentine organization's record against Dan "⁠apEX⁠" Madesclaire's men.

Read more

BIT: "Somehow, we always make history with Vitality"

Sid "⁠sidde⁠" Macedo spoke about the lessons FURIA took away from their loss at PGL Astana and how the team is trying to deal with frustration on the server.

""Frustration comes a lot, at least for us, with the pressure," the Brazilian coach said. "When we are pressured, we start getting frustrated because we feel like we should be winning games."

Read more

sidde: "We are working on letting go of things that go wrong in our games"

Andrey "⁠B1ad3⁠" Gorodenskiy, meanwhile, was happy that his team was able to "wake up" and "activate [their] potential" after a one-sided loss to Spirit on day one, and took being 1-1 in stride.

Read more

B1ad3 jokes about going 1-1: "People with 3-0 are not winning Majors. And we don't want to be 3-0"

Finally, Eetu "⁠sAw⁠" Saha discussed G2 emerging victorious in a rematch against FUT after the teams faced in Stage 2, and offered his thoughts on what Álvaro "⁠SunPayus⁠" García needs to do to turn his recent downturn in form around.

Read more

sAw: "[SunPayus] needs to find his form again; that comes from not overthinking"

By Nohte

B1ad3: "This win is important because our goal was to wake up [and] activate our potential"

vs.

B1ad3 on getting confidence back from Spirit loss by beating Legacy: "This win is important for us because our goal for today was to wake up, first of all, to activate our potential. Yesterday we didn't play how we can play, and I didn't see us being super fired up, super enthusiastic, like with a lot of vocals, speaking to each other for micro plans, very, very loud, and like I said before, an enthusiastic voice.

"It's very important to activate yourself as a team because this is how we know how we can play; we are not a young team, we've played with this lineup for a long time, so we know our potential. And if we lose, we can always understand why we lose, we are not delusional, we can understand what we need to fix, what didn't work, all this stuff.

"For today, as I said, the goal was to show it to ourselves, like we need to see it by ourselves, that we are different, that we are more vocal, that we are more activated, that we are better at creating ideas to be a step ahead constantly. And like I said before, with each match we're going to play better and better."

sidde on post-Astana lessons: "We didn't play serious enough when it mattered"

vs.

sidde on what FURIA took away from Astana: "Our impressions after Astana, they were like, 'Okay, we still know how to play, we just need to learn how to close games again. Actually, before Astana, we had a rough moment in terms of how we were playing. We were not playing to our qualities and the level that we can play, but during Astana I felt like we were playing pretty okay.

"So the conversation after that was just like, 'Hey guys, it's okay, we just have some small tweaks to do. Our game is back where we know it can be, but we need to play more serious in moments that we need to close the game.' That's how we lost to Falcons, by the way, we didn't play serious enough when it mattered. So I knew when watching Astana, but also the scrims afterward with the way we were practicing, that we still know how to play. It was just a matter of us learning how to close games, and I think we are displaying a good level here."
