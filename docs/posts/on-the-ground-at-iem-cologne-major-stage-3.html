On the ground at IEM Cologne Major Stage 3

B1ad3 on getting confidence back from Spirit loss by beating Legacy: "This win is important for us because our goal for today was to wake up, first of all, to activate our potential. Yesterday we didn't play how we can play, and I didn't see us being super fired up, super enthusiastic, like with a lot of vocals, speaking to each other for micro plans, very, very loud, and like I said before, an enthusiastic voice.

"It's very important to activate yourself as a team because this is how we know how we can play; we are not a young team, we've played with this lineup for a long time, so we know our potential. And if we lose, we can always understand why we lose, we are not delusional, we can understand what we need to fix, what didn't work, all this stuff.

"For today, as I said, the goal was to show it to ourselves, like we need to see it by ourselves, that we are different, that we are more vocal, that we are more activated, that we are better at creating ideas to be a step ahead constantly. And like I said before, with each match we're going to play better and better."
