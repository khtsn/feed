On the ground at IEM Cologne Major Stage 3

Video link

NiKo on G2 performance: "I had a very good first map [against G2] in Budapest too. The game just started going my way, I played very well, took my chances. I felt good. Nothing to prove, just wanted to win. We lost the map anyway, that sucks a little bit, but I'm happy with how I played today."

On improvement with karrigan: "Now mostly what we are focused on after Astana and Shanghai is polishing the game a little bit and making sure our map pool is a bit bigger, because we were very short with the maps at those two events.

"Even though we had a bootcamp, we still feel we need much more work. I left with the feeling that we need a lot more work, but we're taking it step by step. It's been great having Finn so far, and what he's very good at is improving throughout the event. This is what we're looking to do at the Major. I'm happy we had such a tight game here already, now we can take a lot from this. If we polish mistakes we did in this game, I would say it should have been more clean, but we'll see...
