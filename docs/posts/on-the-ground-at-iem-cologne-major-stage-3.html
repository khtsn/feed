Our interviews from the Stage 3 newcomers

BetBoom's coach, Artem "⁠Fierce⁠" Ivanov, spoke to HLTV to discuss how the team has developed since his addition at the start of the year.and share his thoughts on making the Major playoffs despite playing with two stand-ins.

Listen to the interview on HLTV's Spotify channel.

HLTV spoke to Laurențiu "⁠lauNX⁠" Țârlea moments after FUT's unceremonious elimination from the IEM Cologne Major.

The Romanian talked about crumbling under the pressure, evaluated FUT's first season at tier-one and made an ominous remark about the team's future after this off-season:

Listen to the interview on HLTV's Spotify channel

On his thoughts after elimination: "It's kind of hard. I'm not feeling anything, it's... just hard."

On struggling against 9z: "They're playing good Counter-Strike, each one is playing good, shooting hard. Their communication was also great."

On The MongolZ not playing up to form: "I think it's because of... I don't know, the pressure. The round is going, and we are kind of losing, and that pressure is making our communication go off. "

On the upcoming break and whether MongolZ will change how much they practice next season: "No, I think during the break we will just train individually to make us improve."

We spoke to three teams that qualified yesterday, Vitality, Aurora and Falcons, as well as the trio that were eliminated, Monte, MOUZ and Legacy.

Robin "⁠ropz⁠" Kool spoke about Vitality's shaky run to the playoffs, admitting the individuals are not up to par just yet. "We're trying to find our form, I think for everyone. ZywOo is playing really well, but the rest of us are trying to do our best to play better."

A visibly exhausted Nikola "⁠NiKo⁠" Kovač barely holding onto his voice told us about the barn-burner double-overtime finish on Anubis against Natus Vincere yesterday.

"I was just trying to stay calm because I was getting too crazy with the shouting and I just wanted to focus and I just wanted to stay calm. The match was crazy. It can't go easy with us. We have to make it entertaining. It's been a very fucking long day and long game."

NiKo: "We were joking with Finn that maybe we'll repeat FaZe in Budapest. Maybe a different outcome in the final"

Özgür "⁠woxic⁠" Eker was happy to make it to his first LANXESS Arena after seven years of failed attempts. "This is the first time for me to play in LANXESS. I've been here three or four times with my former teams, but this is the first time I'll reach the German crowd.

"The feeling is amazing, and thank you to my teammates that nobody stepped down, nobody spoke with bad emotions to each other, everyone was just believing in each other."

Following MOUZ's elimination, Dennis "⁠sycrone⁠" Nielsen spoke about the unclear future of the team.

"We will go home and evaluate what we have and what we saw at the end of this season. Like we do most post-seasons, we'll look at the market and see what we think makes sense for our team," he explained. "I'm set on xertioN as an IGL. "

Monte's coach, Alexander "⁠kakafu⁠" Szymanczyk, admitted there's always the chance his players get picked apart by bigger teams in the off season, but hopes to keep the team together.

"At any given moment, somebody can get bought out or leave, find another opportunity," he said. "I think in our space we just need to appreciate what we have in the moment. What happens tomorrow happens tomorrow. We don't know. I obviously wish this group can stay together."

kakafu: "I really hope we can stay together, but we will see what happens"

Eduardo "⁠dumau⁠" Wolkmer reflected on Legacy's season and the disappointing Major result. "The season was good because we won a trophy, but to be very honest, the goal for everyone is the Major, and playing good is enough as a team, is enough to win, is enough to be in the playoffs and finals. But I think every team has to deal with adversities, and I didn't think we did it well."
