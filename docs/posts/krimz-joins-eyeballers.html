KRIMZ joins EYEBALLERS

EYEBALLERS have announced the signing of Freddy "⁠KRIMZ⁠" Johansson, with the Swedish veteran set to take the spot of the outgoing Jonatan "⁠bobeksde⁠" Persson.

"It feels right to be here. EYEBALLERS is a Swedish project with people I know and trust, and lining up next to JW again after all these years is special," KRIMZ said in EYEBALLERS's press release. "I've still got a lot to give, and I'm excited to do it at home, in front of Swedish fans."

KRIMZ was recently benched by fnatic to end the longest-running active tenure of a player under a single organization amid the organization's slow transition to a full-Ukrainian roster.

The legendary Swede reunites with Jesper "⁠JW⁠" Wecksell on the roster, with whom he played together from 2014-2021, notably winning two Major titles at ESL One Cologne and ESL One Katowice in 2015.

"I've shared a server with Freddy through some of the best moments of my career, so getting to do it again, this time at EYEBALLERS, means a lot to me personally," JW said.
