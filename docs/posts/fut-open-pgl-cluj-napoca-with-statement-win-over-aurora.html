FUT open PGL Cluj-Napoca with statement win over Aurora

PGL Cluj-Napoca began with FUT getting the better of Aurora in a three-map series (Mirage 13-6, Anubis 13-16, Overpass 13-6), while Natus Vincere breezed past Astralis.

FUT, fresh off their success at IEM Krakow, were the more cohesive team throughout, and could have closed out the series in two maps if not for losing a 12-7 lead on Aurora's home map of Anubis.

The young international squad was also able to count on six out of six pistols throughout the series, while Džiugas "⁠dziugss⁠" Steponavičius and Laurențiu "⁠lauNX⁠" Țârlea provided the necessary firepower to get them over the line.
