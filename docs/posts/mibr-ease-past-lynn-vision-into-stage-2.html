MIBR ease past Lynn Vision into Stage 2

insani produced a monstrous 2.11 rating to help MIBR into the next stage

MIBR have booked their spot in the next stage of IEM Cologne Major, doing so via a comfortable 2-0 victory over Lynn Vision.

Despite being one of the favorites to progress through Stage 1 with a 3-0 record, MIBR opened their account in Germany with a disappointing loss at the hands of THUNDER dOWNUNDER. Since then they have been on a recovery mission, which they laboured through with close wins over TYLOO and Liquid.

insani and brnz4n hold firm on the B rush

Against Lynn Vision, however, MIBR truly delivered on their status as pre-stage favorites. The Brazilians were simply too hot for Lynn Vision to handle, the Chinese side waning under the pressure to the extent they seemed beaten before halftime had even hit on the second map of Inferno.

MIBR took a few rounds to get used to Lynn Vision's spread, slow-paced defaults on Anubis, but once they got a handle on things, it was one-way traffic, Felipe "⁠insani⁠" Yuji running rampant as his team romped to victory.

kl1m hits a collat, a crazy flick and a USP kill to hold B solo in 3v5

The second map was, somehow, even more one-sided. MIBR were in control from the off, and when Lynn Vision finally did enough to put MIBR's economy under pressure, Klimentii "⁠kl1m⁠" Krivosheev produced a magnificent 4K round as the solo B defender, crushing Lynn Vision's mental and essentially sealing the series for his team.
