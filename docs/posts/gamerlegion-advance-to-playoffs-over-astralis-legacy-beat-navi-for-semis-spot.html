GamerLegion advance to playoffs over Astralis; Legacy beat NAVI for semis spot

The final round of group stage matches has come to a conclusion at IEM Atlanta, with GamerLegion knocking out Astralis to secure a spot in the playoffs and Legacy beating Natus Vincere to get the top seed in the group and a spot straight in the semi-finals.

Astralis and GamerLegion kicked off their do-or-die lower bracket final on Mirage, where the Janusz "⁠Snax⁠" Pogorzelski-led squad made good on their pick and dominated. GL's four other players were the ones who really shone on the scoreboard, particularly Oldřich "⁠PR⁠" Nový with +11.60% swing and a 1.91 rating.

The map changed to Ancient and Astralis won the pistol and followed it up, but GamerLegion turned it around early on and, leading 3-2, Milan "⁠hypex⁠" Polowiec won a fantastic 1v4 with the AWP to keep the ball rolling.

By half-time, Astralis were in a pit, down one map and staring at a six-round deficit at the half. GamerLegion hit double digits in pistols, and from there went on to close it out 13-8 to get the victory.

The series between Natus Vincere and Legacy got underway on Inferno, where the Aleksi "⁠Aleksib⁠" Virolainen squad defended their way to a 6-2 start, but the Brazilians brought it back to even by the half.

Natus Vincere were let loose on the attack, reaching 11 rounds with a five-round streak after guns were in play. In the final stretch, they ran the clock down back-to-back times to get Legacy within one round, 11-10, and Andrei "⁠arT⁠" Piovezan's men punished with a five-round streak that took them to 13-11 and a first map victory.

Natus Vincere looked good on Ancient, where they got off to an 8-4 lead on the defense, and although Legacy gave it their best on the CT side, a 1v2 clutch by Drin "⁠makazze⁠" Shaqiri helped his team go on to even out the series, 13-8.

Legacy got off to a flying start on the CT side of Dust2, with high energy, and a red-hot Bruno "⁠latto⁠" Rebelatto led his team to a 9-3 lead with 17 kills at the half. The 23-year-old then downed three with Glocks, but it was not enough to stop Natus Vincere from winning the first round of the second half.

Even then, Natus Vincere could not rally, as Legacy dominated with guns in play to secure the map 13-6 after a final ace by Eduardo "⁠dumau⁠" Wolkmer.
