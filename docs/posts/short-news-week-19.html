Short news: Week 19

AaB's Counter-Strike squad has been rebranded to EAC (Esports Academy Copenhagen) and the association with AaB, a Danish football team, has ended.

According to a statement by EAC, the "organization, operations, and players... remain unchanged," and it is merely a rebranding of the team.

"The organization will now continue fully under its own established identity and brand because we believe that we have exceeded expectations and now need another professional step forward," the statement said.

AaB's squad are currently sat at #72 in the global VRS, and have enjoyed a successful year thus far, notably taking first at Parken Challenger Championship Season 4 on LAN.
