What’s your office personality? I corresponded with more than three dozen people about their work experiences. Inspired by these conversations, I created a quiz that explores different working styles, one that’s designed for self-reflection and discussion.

Psychologists have identified what’s known as the “Big Five” personality traits, which are consistent across populations: extroversion, openness, conscientiousness, agreeableness and neuroticism.

Two traits in particular play a powerful role in shaping workplace behavior: extroversion, the degree to which social interaction energizes someone, and openness, which refers to someone’s creativity and appetite for novel experiences. I designed the quiz with these traits in mind.
