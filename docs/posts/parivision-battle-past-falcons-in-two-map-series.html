PARIVISION battle past Falcons in two-map series

PARIVISION have secured a semi-final berth at BLAST Open Rotterdam thanks to a 2-0 win over Falcons, besting the European combine on Mirage (13-11) and Ancient (13-10).

With this victory PARIVISION have secured themselves a semi-final matchup with Natus Vincere, pitting rivals Drin "⁠makazze⁠" Shaqiri and Ivan "⁠zweih⁠" Gogin against each other once again.

Falcons came into this match hoping to avenge two previous losses to PARIVISION this season, but found themselves fighting back on both maps after slow starts, and were unable to complete the comebacks. They also suffered from a poor performance by Nikola "⁠NiKo⁠" Kovač, who didn't find his first frag in the series until the second half of the first map.

PARIVISION raced out to a convincing lead in the first half of Mirage in the face of a disjointed Falcons T side, their good reads making things hard for Falcons amidst a woeful zero frag performance by NiKo.

kyousuke takes down four to power a half-buy win

With the sides switched Falcons began to grow into the game, their resurgence kicked off by a game-changing Maksim "⁠kyousuke⁠" Lukin multi-kill on a half-buy.

PARIVISION struggled to find the gaps in the defense as the Falcons' crossfires prevailed and their individuals heated up, but some high-impact moments from Andrey "⁠BELCHONOKK⁠" Yasinskiy and Dzhami "⁠Jame⁠" Ali were enough for PARIVISION inch their way over the line.

nota finishes off crisp 4K with 1v2 clutch

On their own pick of Ancient Falcons kicked off their CT side with a pistol win, but a monstrous Emil "⁠nota⁠" Moskvitin 4K put PARIVISION in the ascendency and they never looked back on their offense.

BELCHONOKK continued his fine form from the first map, whilst Vladislav "⁠xiELO⁠" Lysov also stepped up to provide impact, granting their team a 7-5 lead at the half. Much like on Mirage, Falcons found better footing in the second half, but some clever Jame reads ensure PARIVISION claimed the 2-0 series win.
