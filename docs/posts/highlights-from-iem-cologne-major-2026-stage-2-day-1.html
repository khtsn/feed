Highlights from IEM Cologne Major 2026 Stage 2 Day 1

B8, who finished Stage 1 of the Major with a flawless 3-0 record, capped off with a 2-0 win over M80, are now facing drastically different circumstances in Stage 2. The Ukrainian side suffered a tough opening loss to FUT, and have now been taken down by M80 in their second Major bout with the American-German side, putting them one best-of-three loss from elimination.

G2 remain undefeated in Stage 2 of the Cologne Major after a hard-fought victory against Monte in a triple-overtime affair.

After wasting two match points in regulation, G2 had to come back from 15-14 and 18-15 deficits to win the game.
