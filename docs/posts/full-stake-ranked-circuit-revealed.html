Full Stake Ranked circuit revealed

G2 won the first iteration of Stake Ranked

Stake and StarLadder have announced the full Stake Ranked circuit for 2026, which will feature five further LAN events in addition to the first tournament, which concluded on April 4 with G2 claiming the title.

G2 secure maiden title with NertZ at Stake Ranked Episode 1

Also included as part of the announcement were the six teams directly invited to the $100,000 Stake Ranked Episode 2 event via the global VRS, who are FaZe, Ninjas in Pyjamas, 3DMAX, HEROIC, 9z and Alliance.

Episode 2, which will take place May 27-30 in the Schengen visa region, features the aforementioned six invited teams and a further two who will be found through qualifiers. An open qualifier will be hosted on FACEIT April 21–22, from which two teams will proceed to the closed qualifier.

14 teams will be directly invited to the closed qualifier, joining the two open qualifier teams for a 16-team, single-elimination BO3 bracket. The top two teams from the closed qualifier will proceed to the main event.
