OG bench arrozdoce

The 24-year-old broke out with SAW in 2024, averaging a 1.08 rating and helping the Portuguese squad attain its best form in a run that included qualifying for the first CS2 Major, PGL Copenhagen, and reaching the IEM Cologne semi-finals.

arrozdoce went international after his time in SAW, but failed to materialize on his promise in a season with Ninjas in Pyjamas, posting a 1.01 rating between January and July of 2025 before a transfer to OG.

In OG, he once again didn't live up to his early promise, averaging the same 1.01 rating from July of 2025 until his benching in May of 2026 in a struggling team that played the majority of their maps online and at second-tier VRS events.

arrozdoce won one title with OG, at Digital Crusade DraculaN Season 2, while his best personal performances came in top four runs at Fragadelphia Blocktober 2025 and DreamHack Knockout Stockholm 2025 with 1.14 and 1.13 ratings.
