Falcons vs FURIA headlines Astana quarter-finals

Falcons will take on FURIA in the quarter-finals

The field at PGL Astana 2026 has been whittled down to the final eight teams, who have been seeded into a single-elimination bracket based on their Swiss stage record.

9z, the surprise package of the tournament so far, will go up against magic in the first round. Spirit, the other team to go through the Swiss Stage with a perfect 3-0 record, will face G2.

But all eyes will be on the mouth-watering clash between Falcons and FURIA, two teams that went 3-1 in the Swiss stage. They recently clashed in IEM Rio's third-place decider (Damjan "⁠kyxsan⁠" Stoilkovski's last match with Falcons), with the European team picking up a hard-fought 2-0 win.

The remaining quarter-final bout will pit Aurora, the final team to make it out of the Swiss stage, against the new-look MOUZ side.
