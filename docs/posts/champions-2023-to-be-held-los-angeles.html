Champions 2023 to be held Los Angeles

Riot has revealed information about Valorant Champions 2023 in a recent weibo post. According to the post, the Shrine Auditorium and KIA Forum in Los Angeles will be the destinations for the finale of the 2023 VCT competition year.

According to the post, two competitions will be held at the two venues, with a preliminary competition being held at the Shrine Auditorium from Aug. 6 - Aug. 20 featuring 16 teams from Americas, Pacific, and EMEA. The top four teams from the two-week competition moving on to the finals, which will be held at the KIA Forum from Aug. 24 - Aug. 26.

A set date has not been given for when tickets will go on sale, but according to the post, more event details and ticket information will be revealed in the coming months.
