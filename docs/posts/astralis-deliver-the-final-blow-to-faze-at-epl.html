Astralis deliver the final blow to FaZe at EPL

Astralis have eliminated FaZe from ESL Pro League S23 after an overtime affair on the Inferno decider.

The loss continues a dismal period of results for Russel "⁠Twistzz⁠" Van Dulken and company, who have won just three out of their eleven matches in 2026.

The series began on Dust2, which became FaZe's consistent map pick in recent times, but Astralis got off to a hot start on the map thanks to Rasmus "⁠HooXi⁠" Nielsen securing a force-buy win for his team.

FaZe came back to finish the half 7-5, and flexed their muscles on their CT side to take the lead in the series thanks to a 13-7 victory.

Despite looking much better on the first map, Ancient was a completely different story. Astralis ran away with a 0-9 scoreline and brought the map home without much difficulty, and kept their momentum to find a 9-3 lead on the decider.

FaZe then came alive to bring the back map into overtime, but the European combine was unable to get control of the match. The chaos eventually allowed Gytis "⁠ryu⁠" Glušauskas to find a bizarre 1vs2 to get Astralis a match point, which HooXi's troops converted to stay alive at EPL.

FaZe in Major danger

FaZe's chances of qualifying for the Cologne Major stood at around 50% before this loss, as per Mischief, and their chances have decreased even further after today's loss.

They have BLAST Open Rotterdam to play before the Major invite cut-off and a chance to sign up for some of the open LANs to boost their chances, but their Major appearance is far from a given.
