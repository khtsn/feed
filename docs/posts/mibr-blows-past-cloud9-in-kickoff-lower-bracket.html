MIBR blows past Cloud9 in Kickoff lower bracket

MIBR has defeated Cloud9 in the second elimination series of Kickoff Americas. Following their 2-1 win, the Brazilian squad will next play against Evil Geniuses, as the teams look to remain in the lower bracket and fight for a spot at 2025's first international event.

Cloud9 opened Lotus, their map pick, with a 13-7 win. Abyss followed, with MIBR dominating their attack to win the map 13-10. MIBR kept their momentum going, winning the decider map, Pearl, 13-7 and closing out the series 2-1.
