Assistant coach-turned-player Septem7 leaves All Gamers

All Gamers All Gamers China Unranked Shr1mp Yang Yong (杨勇) Youze K1ra Huang Zhihao (黄志豪) f4ngeer Roman Smirnov iamgrq Gao Ruiqi (高锐琦) has announced the departure of Liu "Septem7" Zhening (刘哲宁), ending a nearly 18-month-long stay with the organization.

Septem7 joined ahead of the 2025 season as an assistant coach, backing up head coach Fire3 . After Fire3 departed due to health issues, he worked under Paris and then ED101 in a season of constant change for AG. The team continued to make changes through the following offseason, which resulted in a breakout run at China Kickoff that earned the team its first-ever international appearance at Masters Santiago.

Following a 1-2 finish in Santiago's playoffs, AG moved Septem7 back into a playing role for Stage 1 to return to a six-man roster. He ended up playing just two VCT matches for AG, logging four maps on the Controller role and one as Gekko with the team going 1-1. He also competed throughout the entirety of the team's EWC Qualifiers, but match data for that event has not been released.

AG was Septem7's second-ever coaching job, as he had previously been a coach for Qing Jiu Club Qing Jiu Club China Unranked HanChe Huang Jingxuan (黄敬轩) after initially wrapping up his playing career in 2023.
