TOBIZ after Tricked knock HEROIC out of BCG Masters: "Super disappointing, embarrassing"

HEROIC have been eliminated from BC.Game Masters Championship Season 1 after a 0-2 loss to Tricked in the first round of the playoffs (11-13 Overpass, 7-13 Nuke).

Christoffer "⁠Chr1zN⁠" Storgaard's troops had completed a run to the trophy at DraculaN Season 5 just one month prior and beat PARIVISION to reach Stage 2 of ESL Pro League Season 23. But a 0-3 exit in that stage, and now a loss to Tricked in Bucharest, have dealt blows to the team's confidence and endangered their IEM Cologne Major invite.
