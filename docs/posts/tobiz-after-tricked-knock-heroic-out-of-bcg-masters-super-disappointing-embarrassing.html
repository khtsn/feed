TOBIZ after Tricked knock HEROIC out of BCG Masters: "Super disappointing, embarrassing"

An exit in the Round of 16 has cost the Norwegian organization valuable VRS points ahead of the April 6 cut-off.

HEROIC have been eliminated from BC.Game Masters Championship Season 1 after a 0-2 loss to Tricked in the first round of the playoffs (11-13 Overpass, 7-13 Nuke).

Christoffer "⁠Chr1zN⁠" Storgaard's troops had completed a run to the trophy at DraculaN Season 5 just one month prior and beat PARIVISION to reach Stage 2 of ESL Pro League Season 23. But a 0-3 exit in that stage, and now a loss to Tricked in Bucharest, have dealt blows to the team's confidence and endangered their IEM Cologne Major invite.

"Super disappointing, embarrassing, a lot of negative emotions right now," Tobias "⁠TOBIZ⁠" Theo said to HLTV when asked about the result and losing valuable VRS points. "On paper, we should beat these guys 10/10 times, but something didn't click today.

"We have to evaluate that and see what we can do better because we have two other tournaments in a few days here. We can't be... we can be sad right now, but we need to look ahead and not let this affect us too much. It can happen, and unfortunately, it just happened to us today."

It is a grim result for HEROIC, who now sit just above the button at 16th on our list of predicted invites for the IEM Cologne Major and have two LANs to play ahead of the April 6 cut-off.

"No, no update, no comment," TOBIZ said. "I haven't heard anything. We just focus on playing until I hear something, and you'll probably hear it from HEROIC first."

It is the second big elimination of the tournament handed out by Tricked, who also sent Passion UA packing in the group stage. The Danish team will next face HOTU in the quarter-finals.
