Live updates from BLAST Rivals semi-finals

On FaZe's semi-final performance: "I mean, I think they can leave this game with one thing at the back of their heads. I feel like once they got to the T-side, they played completely fearless. They were rushing the sites, then they were creating a fake from it. I think a lot of it was maybe on-the-fly calls as well, they were just thinking we have nothing to lose, let's just play like this. If they play like that without any fear, and any doubts, it will do good for them in the future."

On worries during the comeback: "I'm glad we had plenty of rounds, first of all. w0nderful's clutch at the end of T-side became hugely important in this game, because it gave us more leverage. I do think you can't run away with the game doing the same stuff every round, so we knew they had to slow down and play their playbook, they cannot rush us on the sites, and we were putting more effort into making sure they don't rush us and try to make them play slowly, where we can play our best."

On the biggest change for his team this season: "A lot of teams have made roster changes, I think that's our strength. We're kind of peaking with this lineup, our protocols, our playbook, and the fact that makazze can play the way he plays currently are all the reasons why I think we are where we are right now. I felt we were pretty confident we would reach the final in this tournament, and our biggest challenge will be tomorrow, highly likely against Vitality."
