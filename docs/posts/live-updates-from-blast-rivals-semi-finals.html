Live updates from BLAST Rivals semi-finals

FaZe have gone for a bit of a shakeup heading into their semi-final versus Natus Vincere, picking Dust2 this time around after their Anubis pick did not work in their favor in their group stage clash against NAVI. Natus Vincere have gone for an Ancient pick yet again, with Anubis set to serve as the decider if required.
