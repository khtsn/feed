Spirit vs Aurora, G2 vs FUT among day two matchups in Cologne

G2 and FUT will meet in a rematch of their Stage 2 bout

The second round of matches of Stage 3 at the IEM Cologne Major has been set following the conclusion of the first day.

Riding a wave of momentum after a dominant victory against NAVI, Spirit have been matched up against Aurora in what will mark the first clash between the two teams since last year's Cologne event.

Defending Major champions Vitality will take on 9z, whom they soundly beat at BLAST Open Rotterdam, while Falcons will go up against BetBoom. The last 1-0 pool match will pit FURIA against MOUZ.

In the 0-1 pool, all eyes will be on the mouthwatering clash between FUT and G2. The two teams met in the third round of Stage 2, with FUT running out 2-1 victors to book a spot in Stage 3 with a perfect 3-0 record.

Natus Vincere will be out for revenge against Legacy after the recent 2-1 loss to the Brazilians during their IEM Atlanta title-winning run.
