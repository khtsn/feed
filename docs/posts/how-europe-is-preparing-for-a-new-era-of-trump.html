A creative set play allowed M8 to steal round five, but it would be their only successful attack round.

M8 mounted a comeback in the second half, winning the pistol and subsequent bonus to cut Heretics' lead to just two rounds. However, Heretics regained control in the first buy round and maintained its dominance to close the map 13-8.
