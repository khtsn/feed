Gentle Mates bench deLonge

Gentle Mates coach Eloy "⁠deLonge⁠" Suárez has been moved to the bench, ending the 31-year-old's eight-month stint at the helm of the organization's CS squad.

"Grateful for this year of grinding from the bottom," the Spanish coach's post to X read. "This Iberian Soul/M8 chapter has left me with wonderful memories and amazing teammates."

deLonge had long been associated with the current Gentle Mates core prior to their signing by the organization in August of this year, having coached the team during their time as Iberian Soul and KOI before that, beginning in January 2024.

During that time the squad attended PGL Copenhagen Major as KOI and came close to qualifying for StarLadder Budapest Major and the upcoming IEM Cologne Major as Gentle Mates.

The Spanish side were in contention for a Cologne Major berth right up until just prior to the April 6 invite cut-off, but disappointing finishes at BC Game Masters and Stake Ranked Episode 1 saw the team slip out of invite range in the final few days of the race.
