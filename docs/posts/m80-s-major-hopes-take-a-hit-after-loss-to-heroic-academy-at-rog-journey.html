Sumljiv

Earlier in the day, Alliance got an upset win over GamerLegion to keep their dream alive.

The loss is a costly one for M80, whose high chance of making the Major has dropped following the upset.

Losing to HEROIC Academy, who were ranked No. 115 in the VRS before the match, cost them three places in the ranking, and also means they miss out on other potential gains from the Swedish tournament.

While HLTV's projection still has them making the Major, results at FERJEE In House 2026 have the chance to significantly shake up the Americas ranking, meaning M80 need a good result at DraculaN S6 to avoid heartbreak at the Major invite cut-off.
