ZETA DIVISION triumph in Pacific LCQ grand final, qualify to Champions

ZETA DIVISION ZETA DIVISION Japan Rank #2 Laz Ushida Koji crow Maruoka Tomoaki Dep Yuuma Hisamoto SugarZ3ro Shota Watanabe TENNN Tenta Asai have emerged as victors of the Pacific LCQ in a 3-1 grand final showing over Team Secret Team Secret Asia-Pacific Rank #11 invy Adrian Jiggs Reyes lenne Lim You Xiang Lionel BORKUM Jim Timbreza JessieVash Jessie Cuyco DubsteP Jayvee Paguirigan Jremy Jeremy Cabrera . Their victory qualified ZETA to Champions as the final Pacific representative as well as being the sole Japanese team in the event.

The four-map series saw ZETA draw first blood beginning on Fracture, where they clinched out a 14-12 overtime victory over Team Secret. Determined to stay competitive through the series, TS were able to claim their pick on map two, Ascent, in a convincing 13-9 showing. A comp change on Split seeing Jremy move to Reyna and DubsteP to Raze fell flat for TS, with ZETA taking map three 13-7. The final map, Lotus, ended 13-6 for ZETA, capping off the series 3-1.

ZETA's qualification for Champions comes off the back of a heartbreaking failure to qualify for Masters: Tokyo during the Pacific League playoffs. With Champions now being on the horizon for ZETA DIVISION, another chance awaits for them to rewrite their history once more.
