9z begin Astana campaign with dominant win over PARIVISION

9z secured an upset win against PARIVISION in the first round of the PGL Astana group stage following a one-sided two-map affair (Inferno 13-6, Ancient 13-8).

The series began with a superstar performance by Luciano "⁠luchov⁠" Herrera, who was absolutely electric during 9z's defensive half, where he averaged a 3.73 rating, secured two aces, and helped repel PARIVISION's B-centric offense.

While a quieter T-side meant his map rating fell to 2.68, the Argentinian was still the driving force behind 9z's confident 13-6 victory.

Ancient was another similar affair, with Franco "⁠dgt⁠" Garcia being 9z's fragger this time around. The Uruguayan finished the map with 29 kills, 134 ADR, and a 2.32 rating to come close to luchov's exploits on Inferno, as 9z breezed to a 13-8 win on Ancient and secure a spot in the 1-0 pool for Sunday's matches.
