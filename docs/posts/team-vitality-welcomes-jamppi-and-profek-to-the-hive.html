Team Vitality welcomes Jamppi and PROFEK to the hive

Team Vitality Team Vitality Europe Rank #26 Jamppi Elias Olkkonen PROFEK Dawid Święć Derke Nikita Sirmitev Chronicle Timofey Khromov Sayonara Ștefan Mîtcu has signed Elias "Jamppi" Olkkonen and Dawid "PROFEK" Święć to their roster for the 2026 season.

This move comes after the pair departed BBL Esports BBL Esports Europe Rank #5 sociablEE Volkan Yonal LewN Burak Alkan one day prior, having respectively served as the in-game leader and Controller for the Turkish organization's first international lineup. While the BBL never made it to the international stage, coming one match win away from qualifying for both Masters Toronto and Champions Paris, they established themselves as major contenders in EMEA with a fourth-place finish in Stage 1 and a third-place finish in Stage 2.

By comparison, Vitality's season saw higher highs but lower lows. After building a bona fide superteam with the signings of Nikita "Derke" Sirmitev and Felipe "Less" de Loyola Basso, they opened with a Kickoff win and a fourth-place result at Masters Bangkok but later rapidly descended down the regional standings, exiting early in the playoffs of both stages and failing to return to global competition.

Despite their 2025 struggles and the departure of Less, Vitality's moves this offseason indicate that their aspirations remain high for 2026, as the signings of Jamppi and PROFEK follow up the blockbuster signing of three-time international trophy winner Timofey "Chronicle" Khromov.

“I'm very happy and proud to be part of this project,” PROFEK remarked. “We have very ambitious goals, and I believe that with this team we will achieve success.”
