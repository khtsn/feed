VCT Americas Stage 1 groups revealed

The groups for VCT Americas Stage 1 were drawn following the Americas Kickoff grand final, sorting the region's 12 teams into two groups of six.

Teams were seeded into five pools based on their placement at Kickoff.

Matches will be played round-robin within groups, and the top four teams from each group will qualify for the Stage 1 playoffs. The top three teams from playoffs will qualify for Masters Toronto. Groups will be re-drawn before Stage 2.

In 2024, VCT regions had just 11 teams, resulting in groups having six and five teams respectively. With the number of teams increasing to 12 in 2025, groups now have six teams each. 2Game Esports, who gained entry into VCT following Americas Ascension, will be looking to build toward qualification for Champions, which will extend their stay in VCT by one year.
