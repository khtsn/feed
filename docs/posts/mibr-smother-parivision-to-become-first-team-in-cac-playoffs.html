MIBR smother PARIVISION to become first team in CAC playoffs

PARIVISION endured another dire Mirage before insani put them to the sword on Ancient.

MIBR have progressed to the playoffs of CAC after smashing PARIVISION in Shanghai.

CAC was presented as an opportunity for PARIVISION to arrest their decline in form, and they started well with a win over Liquid in round one. But they have been sent back to Earth now after being thoroughly humbled by MIBR on both maps.

PARIVISION won just two T rounds out of 19 available, a shocking return for the second-highest ranked team at the event.

Dzhami "⁠Jame⁠" Ali's troops are 2-6 on Mirage in their last eight outings, and their only two wins were against Wildcard in multi-overtime and Fisher College. Against MIBR, with the exception of a nice 2v5 attempt, they lacked the firepower to change that statline.

Emil "⁠nota⁠" Moskvitin and Vladislav "⁠xiELO⁠" Lysov's lack of form continues to be a huge problem for the team, with the former ending the series on a 0.55 rating and the latter just 0.47.

Any chance of turning it around on Ancient soon dissipated as Felipe "⁠insani⁠" Yuji hit a seventh gear with 20 kills in the first 10 rounds and a 7-0 record in opening duels over the first half. A 2.38 final rating is enough for his second best of all time at MVP events.

Making it to playoffs is a boon for an MIBR side who have been out of action since PGL Bucharest at the start of April. Taking that time off to prepare for CAC and the Major, it appears, is now paying off.
