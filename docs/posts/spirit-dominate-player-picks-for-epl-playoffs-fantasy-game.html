Playoffs - ESL Pro League Season 23

Format

  • You can create your teams on the Fantasy page here.

  • Eight-team single-elimination bracket with a third-place decider.

  • All matches are best-of-three except for the best-of-five grand final.

Things to note 

  • Teams that win their quarter-finals matches will guarantee themselves two matches: either a best-of-five grand final in case of a win in the semis or a best-of-three third-place decider in case of a loss. Consider this when making a strategy for the game.

Player prices & pick stats

  • Spirit dominate the player picks with donk, magixx, and tN1R occupying the top three, with the remaining two Spirit players also being in the top ten. MOUZ, the other big favorite in the bracket, is not even close in terms of popularity.

  • donk leads the charge with 42% of players having the Russian superstar on their team. Whether it is worth it or not to go heavy on one player, considering the bracket run and his immense price of $240k, is up to the players.
