BetBoom to face GamerLegion for Stage 2 berth as day one ends

Boombl4 ends day one as the highest-rated player at 1.53

The first day of IEM Cologne Major Stage 1 has come to an end with four teams nearing qualification after reaching the 2-0 pool and four coming within a loss from elimination at 0-2.

After a merciless first round of play, the second round saw most of the favorites prevail in their matchups bar one, a HEROIC side building up a hefty losing streak in the past month.

A win away from Stage 2

The 1-0 pool went largely as expected, though it was not for the lack of trying of some of the underdogs.

THUNDER dOWNUNDER gave B8 a scare before the Ukrainian side made a second-half T-side recovery on Dust2, and FlyQuest took GamerLegion the distance, only to lose out to some vintage Janusz "⁠Snax⁠" Pogorzelski clutches.
