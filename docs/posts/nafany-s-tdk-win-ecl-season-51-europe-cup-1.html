nafany's TDK win ECL Season 51 Europe Cup 1

nafany and Ax1Le's new team have enjoyed a successful start to 2026

TDK have claimed the ESL Challenger League Season 51 Europe Cup 1 title, taking down Alliance 2-1 in the grand final to clinch the $12,000 top prize and claim a berth for the Season 51 Europe Finals.

TDK's run was a gruelling test that saw them battle through six BO3 series over the course of 11 days, their most notable wins coming over GenOne, MOUZ NXT and Alliance.

The fragging load was shared across the entire team throughout their run, with the average ratings of the players sitting between 1.16-1.20.

In winning this tournament TDK have not only claimed a notable online title and a spot in the season's finals event, but also boosted their VRS points total significantly, claiming 149 points across their run to rocket themselves up 33 places in the live ranking, to #62.

Their trophy-winning campaign continues the excellent form TDK have displayed since forming at the start of the year, as across 19 series played the team have only suffered a single loss to MOUZ NXT, which they have since avenged twice.

TDK have two more online events ongoing, NODWIN Clutch Series 5, in which they are 2-0 in the swiss group stage, and CCT Europe Series 16, in which they have reached the quarter-finals.
