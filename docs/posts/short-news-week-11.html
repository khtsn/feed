    By Striker

    Short: Aurora and M80 confirmed at DraculaN 6

    The two teams have joined the likes of FaZe, Liquid, Monte and fnatic at the 24-team open LAN taking place in Bucharest on March 29-April 2, just before the IEM Cologne Major invite cut-off.

    While M80 are under pressure on the Americas ranking, Aurora are safe to qualify for Cologne, so it appears the Turkish side is maximizing its potential for an invite straight to Stage 3 amid fierce competition for those six spots.
