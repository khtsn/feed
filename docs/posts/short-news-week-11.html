Short news: Week 11

Speaking to Pley.gg ahead of BLAST Open Rotterdam and asked about his comments in 2025 about potentially not playing the game five years down the road, Dan "⁠apEX⁠" Madesclaire revealed he is in the process of renegotiating his contract with Vitality.

"I've been playing Counter-Strike for 20 years, you need to stop at some point," he began. "But of course, when you win that much, it's not the moment. [...] I just want to retire at a good point, not [while] winning, because you can't win and stop cause then you have regrets.

"I'll do my best to keep winning as much as I can, and see when it's done. I'm actually re-negotiating my contract with Vitality, so we're on the right path to do a few more years. But how many, I don't know; it's all about the future return. How long we will stay that good, how long this team keeps winning, but I will be sure that I give my best and we give our best to do that for the longest period."
