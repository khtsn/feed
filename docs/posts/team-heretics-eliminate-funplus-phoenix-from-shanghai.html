Team Heretics eliminate FunPlus Phoenix from Shanghai

Team Heretics Team Heretics Europe Rank #2 MiniBoo Dominykas Lukaševičius Wo0t Mert Alkan RieNs Enes Ecirli benjyfishy Benjy David Fish Boo Ričardas Lukaševičius have eliminated FunPlus Phoenix FunPlus Phoenix China Rank #2 Autumn Kale Dunne Life Qu Donghao (瞿东豪) AAAAY Zhang Yang (张杨) BerLIN Zhang Bolin (張柏霖) Lysoar Liang Youhao (梁优好) from Masters Shanghai, beating them 2-1 in the Swiss stage. With this win, Team Heretics have secured their spot in the playoff section of Masters Shanghai.

The map veto began with Team Heretics picking Sunset and FPX picking Lotus, leaving Icebox as the potential third map.

Team Heretics began the series on fire, looking just as strong on Sunset as they had all 2024 long, even with their substitute. Wo0t flexed on duelist, playing an outstanding Raze to win the map 13-4. He dropped 27 kills in only 17 rounds, leading the map with five first kills.

Lotus heavily swung back to FPX, as Life awoke to duel Wo0t as Raze. After developing an early lead on Lotus, rounds went back and forth. Life looked much better than he did on map one, stealing a round with a 3K on round 11 to help FPX maintain an 8-4 lead at the half. Both AAAAY and BerLIN were crucial with their respective Gekko and Astra utility and some nice shots to assist Life in securing the map 13-6 to take the series to Icebox.

Icebox meant the return of triple controller for FPX, this time with the newest agent Clove in the mix. Heretics won the first pistol and converted their bonus round and were well-studied on how FPX played Icebox defense, allowing them to get easy first kills. Wo0t returned to Jett but looked just as comfortable as he was on Raze earlier in this series. Team Heretics entered the half up 10-2 again, but FPX used their knowledge of triple controller attack on Icebox to mount a comeback.

Thanks to constant flanks from BerLIN on Harbor, AAAAY's clutch shots, and collective chaos from both sides, the map went deep into overtime. Both teams struggled to defend, leading to the back-and-forth nature of rounds. In the end, Team Heretics proved too strong on their attack side to stop and won the map 17-15.
