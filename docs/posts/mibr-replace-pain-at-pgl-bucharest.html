MIBR replace paiN at PGL Bucharest

PGL has announced that paiN have withdrawn from PGL Bucharest 2026, with MIBR set to inherit their invite as the next eligible team.

MIBR were invited to the Closed qualifier for the event but placed second after losing the marathon best-of-five final to Imperial, but have now received a second chance.

The Linus "⁠LNZ⁠" Holtäng-led team has shown imperious form in domestic competition so far in 2026, winning BetBoom Circuit X Redemption Curitiba, FiRe CONTER Season 1, and ECL S51 South America Cup 1, and amassed a 19-game winning streak during those tournaments.

MIBR now complete a team list void of some of the biggest names, as only three top-10 teams have accepted invites to the studio LAN in Romania.
