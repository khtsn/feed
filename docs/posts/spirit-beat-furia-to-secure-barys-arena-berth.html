Spirit beat FURIA to secure Barys Arena berth

Spirit became the second team to progress through the PGL Astana group stage after defeating FURIA in a three-map series (Dust2 13-8, Overpass 12-16, Mirage 13-11).

The victory means they have locked in their appearance in the Barys Arena, where they lifted the trophy just over a year ago.

Meanwhile, FURIA drop to the 2-1 pool to face Gentle Mates.

The series began on Dust2, where Spirit's star duo of Danil "⁠donk⁠" Kryshkovets and Dmitry "⁠sh1ro⁠" Sokolov batted away anything FURIA had to offer in the opening exchanges. FURIA rallied late on and brought the game back to 8-11, but lost a crucial anti-eco to eventually lose the map 8-13.

Overpass was a different story, as FURIA dominated their offensive side and found themselves 8-4 up, a scoreline that would have been even more lopsided if not for Mareks "⁠YEKINDAR⁠" Gaļinskis's mishap.

Their CT side was a different story, with their B defense especially being caught by timings several times across the half, but Gabriel "⁠FalleN⁠" Toledo's troops found their groove in overtime and closed out a 16-12 victory.

Mirage was another cagy and sloppy affair, one that was tipped in Spirit's favor after another donk masterclass, as the Russian rifler put up 23 kills, 106 ADR, and a 1.58 rating.
