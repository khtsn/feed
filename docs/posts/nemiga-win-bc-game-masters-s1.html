Nemiga win BC Game Masters S1

Nemiga have come out on top of BC Game Masters Season 1 to win the $15,000 grand prize after a hard-fought victory over Metizport in the grand final.

Aliaksandr "⁠1eeR⁠" Nahorny's charges had to come back from a map down in the final to win on an Overpass decider, where they had slipped to a 9-11 deficit on the T side before sweeping the match.

The BCG Masters victory marks Nemiga's second tournament victory since they brought in Daniel "⁠syph0⁠" Ageyev and Aleksandr "⁠KaiR0N-⁠" Anashkin and reinstated 1eeR to the lineup earlier in the year.

Combined with the other online win at A1 Gaming League Season 11, the organization has made it up to 36th place on the Valve Regional Standings, the closest it has been to the top 30 since August last year.

A current IEM Cologne Major invite projection places the team at 24th in Europe by the April 6 deadline. Before then, Nemiga will have a chance to make up even more ground at MPKBK CIS LAN #4 among the likes of HOTU and K27 at the end of March.
