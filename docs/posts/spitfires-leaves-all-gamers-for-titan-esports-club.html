Trace picking up Spitfires is the latest move in a musical chairs-esque changing of players in VCT China ahead of the start of Stage 1. The chain began with Abo departing Titan and joining Trace, and ends with Spitfires replacing his spot in Titan's starting roster.

Spitfires had been with AG since the start of the 2024 season, but had not suited up for the team since last year. The team's unbeaten run through China Kickoff and ensuing 1-2 record at Masters Santiago was with Spitfires on the bench. Throughout his tenure with AG, Spitfires has been a peripheral member before. In 2025, he only played during China Stage 2 as a primary Duelist, while he played as a Duelist and Sentinel in the 2024 season through all three league phases.

While former coach Septem7 looks set to step in for AG, Spitfires joins a Titan squad that is looking for a Duelist player to complement Dynamite . At EVO Act 1, the team tried out Ra1ny as a Duelist to pair with Dynamite, but it seems to have moved in a different direction.
