Why do people say it’s hard to live off 100K in the Bay Area?

Living off 100K as an single individual in the Bay Area should be very doable. 100K pre-tax is 70K post-tax. A 2 bedroom 2 bath costs $3400 with decent utilities. Assuming you room with someone else, you pay $1700 per month or 20.4K per year. If you have a 25K car, then the yearly payment is $5.2K
