NAVI fight off elimination in Rio against HOTU

iM put in a series-high 1.63 rating as NAVI beat HOTU

Natus Vincere move on to Group B's lower bracket final at IEM Rio after a 2-0 victory over HOTU, setting up a playoff qualification match later on Wednesday against the winner of Aurora vs B8.

"It's a shame we need to play some of these tier-1 tournaments with this format," Aleksi "⁠Aleksib⁠" Virolainen said before the match. "It's not the best thing in the world. But at the same time, we understand we need to be sharp the whole day.

"We can't lose focus when we go for a break. It's going to be pretty hectic. We just need to get some food in, try to get some small preparation and stay sharp individually."
