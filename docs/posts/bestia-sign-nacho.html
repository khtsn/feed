BESTIA sign nacho

BESTIA have opted for a change after only two events this season.

BESTIA have announced the addition of Ignacio "⁠nacho⁠" Rodriguez, filling the gap left on the roster by the recent benching of Cristhian "⁠timo⁠" Perez.

BESTIA bench timo

nacho arrives having spent the last year playing for ShindeN whilst participating in regional competitions in South America. The team enjoyed some decent peaks during his tenure, placing second in CCT Season 2 Season 8 and Cajunto Copa do Brasil, but largely struggled to make consistent deep runs.

During his time with ShindeN nacho was the team's highest-rated player, posting a 1.10 average rating over 236 maps, whilst taking the highest percentage of opening duels on both T and CT side.

nacho joins a BESTIA side that ended 2025 in good form, placing third in ESL Challenger League finals and winning DraculaN Season 4. Thus far this year the team narrowly missed out on an IEM Rio spot and had to settle for top four in FiRe CONTER Season 1, losing out to RED Canids in both instances.
