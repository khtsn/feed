FNATIC make history as first team to win two majors

FNATIC FNATIC Europe Rank #1 Leo Leo Jannesson Derke Nikita Sirmitev Boaster Jake Howlett Chronicle Timofey Khromov Alfajer Emir Ali Beder beat Evil Geniuses Evil Geniuses North America Rank #3 Boostio Kelden Pupello ScrewFace Kyle Jensen Demon1 Max Mazanov Ethan Ethan Arnold BcJ Brendan Jensen icy Jacob Lange C0M Corbin Lee Apoth Vincent Le Reformed Jeffrey Lu jawgemo Alexander Mor 3-0 in the grand finals of VCT Masters: Tokyo. With the win, FNATIC are now the first team to win two trophies in VALORANT, that too consecutively.

FNATIC, with the upper bracket advantage, chose to ban out Fracture and Pearl, then picked Lotus as the first map of play. EG selected Split as the second map, followed by FNATIC's choice of Bind. Map four was Ascent, while Haven was the decider. However, FNATIC won Lotus 13-8, followed by a 13-11 victory on Split and a 14-12 victory on Bind to give them the trophy.

Four out of FNATIC's five players had a positive kill differential, compared to two of EG's. Derke had 16 first kills throughout the series, playing only the Raze, while Alfajer on the sentinel role had 65 kills over three maps with a +23 kill differential, the highest on the server.
