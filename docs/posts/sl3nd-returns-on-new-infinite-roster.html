sl3nd returns on new INFINITE roster

INFINITE have announced four members of their new European roster, headlined by ex-GamerLegion AWPer Henrich "⁠sl3nd⁠" Hevesi.

The Hungarian player will thus return to activity after being benched by GamerLegion in June of 2025, a year during which he averaged a 0.98 rating as the team failed to secure deep runs in tier-one tournaments.

Since then, the 21-year-old played just four HLTV-covered games across the nine months, but stated he is ready to return in February.

He is joined by ex-Fingers Crossed members Rasmus "⁠kreaz⁠" Johansson and Martin "⁠Dytor⁠" Handl, as well as Mihnea "⁠mhN1⁠" Gheață, who has been a part of INFINITE since August 2023 and recently represented the organization's Romanian lineup at DraculaN Season 5.

INFINITE stated that they are "still on the hunt for the fifth" and that the final piece of the puzzle will be announced in due course.
