Falcons to meet BC.Game as CAC opening matchups revealed

s1mple and karrigan will face each other in Shanghai

Perfect World has announced the opening matchups for the 2026 CS Asia Championships, which will take place between May 20-24 in Shanghai.

The 16 participating teams — including Falcons, PARIVISION, The MongolZ and MOUZ — have been split into two double-elimination groups, featuring best-of-one games in the opening round. The top three teams from each group will advance to the single-elimination playoffs, which will culminate in a best-of-five final.
