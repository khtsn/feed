Aurora sign ash

Aurora have announced the signing of Ashley "⁠ash⁠" Battye, bringing the former GamerLegion coach on board following the recent departure of Sezgin "⁠Fabre⁠" Kalaycı.

The move represents the first addition in Aurora's ongoing rebuild, which has also featured long-standing IGL Engin "⁠MAJ3R⁠" Küpeli stepping down and the benching of Caner "⁠soulfly⁠" Kesici.

MAJ3R steps down from Aurora

By bringing in ash, Aurora have bolstered their team with one of the most highly-touted coaches in the game.

The 35-year-old put himself on the map in 2023 when he led GamerLegion all the way to the final of BLAST.tv Paris Major, doing so despite modest resources and a lack of big-name players.

GamerLegion's squad was picked apart following their Major exploits and in the years since they would continue to regularly lose their best pieces to bigger teams, yet ash would always find a way to build a competitive squad with the resources available to him, leading the British coach to build a formidable reputation.

ash opted to step away from GamerLegion towards the end of 2025 due to suffering from burnout, stating "I need a break to clear my head and work on myself." In early 2026 he made clear his intention to seek opportunities beyond GamerLegion upon his return, telling HLTV in an interview that he wanted to "play with the best players" and "win trophies."
