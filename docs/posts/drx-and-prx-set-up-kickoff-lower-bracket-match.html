The first match of the day between DRX and FULL SENSE was a battle of Thailand versus Korea, with FS picking the first map of Corrode. Game two was a lot closer, with the PatMen bowl officially happening twice at Kickoff. The match immediately started with an entertaining map one of Corrode. Global reached a 9-3 lead at the half despite losing the pistol, with a stalwart defense against one of the most aggressive teams in VCT history. Yet, the PRX defense was better, ending the game 13-10 with only one round lost on defense. Breeze was the only one-sided map of the series, as Global won it 13-6 to force map three. Split was a game of momentum, but PRX seemed to be in control when the team was up 11-4. Global made another gutsy comeback to take the map to overtime, but Paper Rex won both sides quickly to end the map 14-12 and the series 2-1.

PRX earned the right to face DRX next, while Global showed off a lot of promise at Kickoff to fight the regional favorites closely twice.
