KRÜ Esports KRÜ Esports Latin America South Unranked keznit Angelo Mori Dantedeu5 Jesús Federico Larrosa Mazino Roberto Rivas ' Fabian "Shyy" Usnayo has announced that he has been allowed to explore offers with other teams for the 2026 season.

He shared that his contract KRÜ runs until the end of the 2025 season, and that while he is still in talks with the organization, he has been allowed to seek other opportunities.

Shyy has been with KRÜ since the start of the 2024 season, joining after spending a year and a half with LEVIATÁN LEVIATÁN North America Unranked Sato Eduardo Kenzo Nagahama Sato kiNgg Francisco Aravena . He played an integral role in that squad as one of the team's go-to Initiator players, often joining forces with the likes of kiNgg and adverso to form double and even triple Initiator comps. Trips to Masters Copenhagen and Champions Istanbul ended the 2022 season on a high note for Shyy and LEV, but personnel changes in the offseason made it so that he moved to the team's Sentinel role, the role that he is most associated with today.

In 2023, the first year of the partnership era, Shyy mainly played Killjoy for LEVIATÁN, while also occasionally playing Skye. At the end of the season, which ended with LEV placing 5th-6th in Americas and finising second at the Americas LCQ, Shyy departed for KRÜ. He's played the team's Sentinel and secondary Controller role since then, but now departs after two seasons of service for the Argentinian organization.

"My main focus is Sentinel, but I'm open to any role and region," Shyy said.

Shyy is the third member of KRÜ's organization to depart after over two seasons of service. Head coach Atom and Melser recently entered restricted free agency after three years with the team.
