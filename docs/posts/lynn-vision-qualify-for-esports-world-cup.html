Lynn Vision qualify for Esports World Cup

Lynn Vision have qualified for the Esports World Cup following their title-winning run at Hero Esports Asian Champions League.

The Chinese side beat JiJieHao in the grand final of the tournament, which took place in Shanghai, China over the past week, to clinch the spot as well as $80,000 in prizemoney.

The Hero Esports ACL is the only regional qualifier for the Esports World Cup, a $2 million, 32-team competition preceded by a 128-team LAN qualifier that was originally slated to take place in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia on August 7-23.

However, the ongoing conflict in Iran has reportedly forced a relocation of the massive multi-game festival to Paris, France, according to GamesBeat.
