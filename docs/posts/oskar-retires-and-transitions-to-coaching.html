oskar retires and transitions to coaching

The Czech veteran has decided to hang up the mouse and pursue the next chapter in his career.

Tomáš "⁠oskar⁠" Šťastný has announced his decision to retire as a player and focus on coaching as the next step in his career.

"Looking back on my turbulent career (doing wrong decisions), I'm proud of everything I’ve achieved, it's more than I could have ever dreamed of when I started," oskar wrote in an X post.

"However, I’ve reached a point where I no longer have the energy and motivation to continue as a player. I plan to transition into coaching, with 20 years of experience, I believe my knowledge of CS can be a great asset to many teams and players."

oskar enjoyed the best part of his CS:GO career in MOUZ, where he won four Big Events and placed in HLTV Top 20 Players of the Year list twice, peaking at No. 14 in 2018.

Since departing MOUZ in 2019, the Czech sniper turned out for the likes of HellRaisers, Sprout, and SINNERS, coming agonizingly close to securing a Shanghai Major spot with the latter.

oskar stated that he is looking for opportunities as a head coach, assistant coach, or analyst.
