2023 Oscars: Every Look From the Not-So-Red Carpet

Hollywood’s brightest stars battle to make the biggest splash on the champagne carpet before the 95th Academy Awards.

For the 95th Academy Awards, some things changed — crisis management team, check; red carpet replaced with neutral color, check — but other things stayed very much the same.

Stars came out in force. The carpet this year was a mix of classic and experimental. Black, white and bold colors dominated one new look after another. Florence Pugh rocked black shorts with a dramatic cape, looking like a punk princess while the new Disney princess herself, Halle Bailey, who stars in the coming “Little Mermaid” reboot, floated in with a baby blue sheer gown.

The nominees did not disappoint. Angela Bassett looked as regal as her Oscar-nominated role in “Black Panther: Wakanda Forever.” Michelle Yeoh, who has been keeping us guessing on each red carpet she has graced so far, arrived in dreamy cloud white. Paul Mescal wore a Gucci suit with flared pants. And Hong Chau told her own story of how she asked Prada to add a Mandarin collar to her dress, a nod to her heritage.

On this not-so-red carpet, the stars still brought it.
