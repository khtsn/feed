Falcons send FaZe to 1-1 pool in Cluj-Napoca

Falcons have secured their spot in the 2-0 pool at PGL Cluj-Napoca by virtue of a 2-1 win over FaZe, winning Anubis (13-9) and Ancient (13-4) either side of a loss on Dust2 (11-13).

FaZe opted for a gamble in the veto by picking Anubis, a map they haven't played for almost a year, in an attempt to even the odds in this matchup. It was a gamble that failed to pay off.

NiKo pops off with clean FAMAS 4k

The first half of Anubis was dominated by Falcons' T side as they battered the middle part of the map with fast plays, something FaZe struggled to answer. FaZe picked up a force-buy win early on their T half to give themselves some runway, but a string of retakes secured Falcons the map win.

It seemed we were in for a quick 2-0 as Falcons rushed out to a 5-0 lead on their offense on Dust2, thanks to Maxim "⁠kyousuke⁠" Lukin running riot, but Russel "⁠Twistzz⁠" Van Dulken and Jakub "⁠jcobbb⁠" Pietruszewski put up some strong rounds to keep the scoreline close.

m0NESY and TeSeS pull off 2v4 to claim series point

FaZe took over the game in the second half, Helvijs "⁠broky⁠" Saukants stepping to help crack open rounds as Falcons struggled for impact outside of kyousuke, sending the series to a decider.

Ancient seemed a good place for FaZe to be with the series on the line as it's a map Falcons have struggled on as of late, but FaZe folded in the face of Ilya "⁠m0NESY⁠" Osipov and René "⁠TeSeS⁠" Madsen. The Falcons duo ran riot as FaZe struggled to get anything going on their T side, and a loss to a force-buy in the second half spelled disaster for FaZe.
