TYLOO, Sharks and Wildcard among first teams invited to DraculaN 7

TYLOO will attend 'Super DraculaN' later this month

Digital Crusade has unveiled half of the 16 teams that will compete at DraculaN Season 7, a $150,000 tier-2 LAN that has been dubbed 'Super DraculaN' by the Romanian tournament organizer.

TYLOO, Sharks and Wildcard headline the first batch of teams announced on Friday. The first two teams played in Stage 1 of the Cologne Major, where the Chinese squad advanced to Stage 2 with a 3-2 record, while the Brazilian outfit was eliminated in 12th-14th place.

Six more teams will be invited through May's Global VRS. The final two spots will go to wildcard invites.

DraculaN Season 7 will take place from June 23-28, with the Halos Events Center hosting two days of playoff matches. The Romanian event will be one of three international LANs that will be held during the player break, along with XSE Pro League (a tier-1 tournament with $1 million) and Stake Ranked Episode 3.
