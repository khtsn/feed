PARIVISION clear Spirit to reach Rotterdam playoffs

PARIVISION have laid claim to a place in the playoffs of BLAST Open Rotterdam, annihilating Spirit on Anubis (13-3) before battling past them on Ancient (13-8) to secure a 2-0 series victory.

PARIVISION now join the likes of Natus Vincere and Aurora as squads who have guaranteed themselves a spot in the top six of the event. PARIVISION will now be preparing for an upper-bracket final against the winner of Vitality's clash with The MongolZ for a chance to secure a direct placing in the semi-final.

Spirit, conversely, will now be heading down to the lower bracket in an effort to keep their Rotterdam campaign alive, with two more wins needed from them to clinch a playoff spot. The team's first hurdle will be 9z, who stunned MOUZ earlier in the day.

Though Spirit picked Anubis to kick off the series, PARIVISION enjoyed far more success on the map. Kicking things off with a quad kill in the pistol round from Vladislav "⁠xiELO⁠" Lysov, PARIVISION dominated on the T-side of Anubis. Spirit finally got their first round after a quad kill from Danil "⁠donk⁠" Kryshkovets, but the team struggled to find much else throughout the half, ultimately concluding their CT-side with just three rounds to their name.

PARIVISION had no trouble wrapping up Anubis on the defense, scooping up the second pistol round to lock down a lopsided 13-3 map win. Despite the drubbing his team endured on their map pick, donk finished with a whopping 2.05 rating and +9.78% Swing, predicated on his six multikills and 131.9 ADR.

Spirit fared better on PARIVISION's pick of Ancient, accruing an early 4-1 lead on the CT-side off the back of a pistol round win, but PARIVISION managed to keep them honest with a four-round streak of their own. Spirit rallied in the latter part of the half to end with a 7-5 scoreline, but found no headway once on the T-side, securing just a single round before PARIVISION romped ahead to a 13-8 win.
