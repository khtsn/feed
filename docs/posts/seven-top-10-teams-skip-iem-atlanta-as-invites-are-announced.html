Seven top 10 teams skip IEM Atlanta as invites are announced

Vitality were one of just three top 10 teams to accept an invite to Atlanta

ESL has announced the list of teams that have accepted invites to IEM Atlanta, taking place from May 11-17, and its global and Americas qualifiers, which are due to kick off on Tuesday.

It amounts to the tournament organizer's worst-attended tournaments in recent memory, as it will only feature three of the current top 10 teams: Vitality, FaZe and Natus Vincere.

The remainder of the world's elite has instead likely decided to attend PGL Astana, which will be running alongside IEM Atlanta, from May 7-17. Invites to the Kazakhstani tournament will go out at the beginning of March.

The split comes as somewhat of a surprise given that, historically, the top teams preferred ESL events to PGL's when faced with a choice, as, along with a competitive prizepool and club share, they offer end-of-year bonuses based on viewership.

However, the sudden shift can be explained by two factors. In an attempt to sweeten the deal and combat Atlanta, PGL announced Astana will have $1.6 million on offer, a $350,000 increase compared to other PGL events and a $600,000 improvement on Atlanta.
