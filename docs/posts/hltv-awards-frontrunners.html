HLTV Awards frontrunners

The HLTV Awards won't be handed out until January, when the entire 2026 season is taken into account, but with the first half of the year almost in the books, it's a good time to assess which players and teams have built the strongest cases for recognition so far.

This update serves as a snapshot of the current front-runners based solely on results and performances before the start of the Cologne Major. Therefore, the results of the first Major are certain to shake up the standings as we move into the summer break, as will the plethora of tournaments happening in the fall season.

Another thing to note is that the placement of the five frontrunners in each category is purely alphabetical.

The HLTV Awards by 1xBet winners will be announced in Belgrade. Serbia, on January 9, 2027.

Player of the Year

Danil "⁠donk⁠" Kryshkovets

The "Big Three," namely ZywOo, donk, and m0NESY are all present in the top five, as is 2025 podium finisher ropz and his Vitality teammate flameZ.

Team of the Year

Falcons

In the team of the year department, Vitality's inclusion in the top five comes as no surprise after their five trophies this season. Natus Vincere are also present as the only multi-trophy winners, as are PARIVISION, Spirit, and Falcons.

Rookie of the Year

Anarbileg "⁠cobrazera⁠" Uuganbayar

A diverse field of emerging talent makes up the Rookie of the Year race. FUT trio dem0n, Krabeni, and dziugss all make the cut, with Ninjas in Pyjamas's xKacpersky and The MongolZ's cobrazera also making the pre-Major top five.

Opener of the Year

Danil "⁠donk⁠" Kryshkovets

In the Opener of the Year category, two-time winner donk is joined by fellow countryman kyousuke and Israeli duo flameZ and xertioN, with Natus Vincere's makazze also appearing among the frontrunners.

Anchor of the Year

Valeriy "⁠b1t⁠" Vakhovskiy

In the Anchor of the Year category, last year's winner mezii is joined by HeavyGod, b1t, dziugss, and zweih in a list of players who have excelled in some of the game's most demanding positions.

Closer of the Year

Kaike "⁠KSCERATO⁠" Cerato

The Closer of the year category features two Brazilians in KSCERATO and latto, with the duo joined by NiKo, last year's winner ropz, and Wicadia.

AWPer of the Year

Ilya "⁠m0NESY⁠" Osipov

In the AWPer of the Year category, the usual big names donning the Big Green are present, with ZywOo, m0NESY, and sh1ro all featuring alongside w0nderful, while molodoy earns his place after taking home the Rookie of the Year award in 2025.

IGL of the Year

Aleksi "⁠Aleksib⁠" Virolainen

In the IGL of the Year list, seasoned veterans apEX, Aleksib, FalleN, and Jame are joined by magixx, who took over leadership at the start of the year.

Coach of the Year

Andrey "⁠B1ad3⁠" Gorodenskiy

Last but not least, the Coach of the Year category features two-thirds of last year's top three in XTQZZZ and hally, with the duo joined by B1ad3, dastan, and zonic.
