RED Canids promote reNTU to replace history

RED Canids have announced that Renan "⁠reNTU⁠" Cardoso has been moved up from the academy roster to play for the main team. The 20-year-old will take up AWPing duties following the recent dismissal of Allan "⁠history⁠" Lawrenz.

reNTU picked up CS2 only in January after previously playing CS:GO. One month later, he joined RED Canids Academy and helped the team win Liga Gamers Club 2026 Serie A February Cup over paiN academy.

reNTU made his first appearances for RED Canids on Wednesday, averaging a 1.24 rating as the team beat METANOIA Wolves and Fluxo to top their BetBoom Circuit X Mayhem São Paulo group.

“I know that to stay on the team I need to perform well and do my part," he told Dust2 Brasil. "That’s something I apply to everything in my life.

"When you’re not at your best, you let everyone around you down. So, ultimately, that’s the best way for the team to improve. I want to win these championships by playing well, communicating well and doing my part, because I know that if I play well I’ll stay on the team, and that’s my goal.”
