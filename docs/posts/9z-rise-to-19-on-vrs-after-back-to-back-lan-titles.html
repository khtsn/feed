9z rise to #19 on VRS after back-to-back LAN titles

dgt has looked rejuvenated back at 9z

9z have won their second LAN title in the space of two weeks after beating Marsborne in the BetBoom Circuit X Pantanal Cuiabá 2026 grand final.

Added to their win at BetBoom RUSH B Summit Season 2 on March 1, 9z have been able to move up to 19th in the live VRS just a month-and-a-half after Franco "⁠dgt⁠" Garcia was re-signed from paiN in January.

Luciano "⁠luchov⁠" Herrera and former MIBR player Ignacio "⁠meyern⁠" Meyer are also fairly new additions, having joined in November 2025.

The new lineup has gone from strength to strength, with a 52-20 map win-loss record helping them to three event wins already.

Such strong form locally means they are all but guaranteed to attend the IEM Cologne Major. They will also be able to test their mettle at BLAST Open Rotterdam, with an opener pencilled in against Vitality after their win in the BLAST Rising qualifier.

The big surprise from the Brazilian LAN came via North Americans Marsborne. The team, led by #31 prospect Adam "⁠Grizz⁠" Golden, made the grand final after wins over Imperial, BESTIA, RED Canids, and Galorys to climb 25 places in the VRS to 51st.

However, 9z proved too tough of an opponent in the best-of-five final, and it was the South Americans who departed with the $14,000 first prize.
