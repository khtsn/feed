PGL Cluj-Napoca round two matchups set

The first day of PGL Cluj-Napoca's all best-of-three Swiss group stage has come to an end with almost all of the favorites advancing unscathed to the 1-0 pool.

Aurora were the sole exception as they fell 1-2 to FUT in their opening game, and will take on paiN in the 0-1 pool to avoid an early trip to the elimination round.

Aulon "⁠Krabeni⁠" Fazlija's side, as a reward for their win, will take on FURIA for the third time in the past month. The record stands at 1-1 with FURIA narrowly scraping by online in BLAST Bounty and FUT getting revenge at IEM Krakow two weeks prior.

Vitality and The MongolZ will lock horns for the first time this year after facing each other nine times in 2025, with Anarbileg "⁠cobrazera⁠" Uuganbayar set to play the former for the first time.

The two other matches headlining the 1-0 pool will see Falcons take on FaZe and Natus Vincere face MOUZ, with the last meetings between the pairings dating back to September and October, respectively.

The 0-1 pool features PARIVISION up against G2, Astralis vs. 3DMAX, and B8 facing HEROIC.
