BESTIA bench timo

BESTIA have announced the benching of Cristhian "⁠timo⁠" Perez, bringing an end to the Uruguayan player's year-long tenure with the team.

The Argentine organization made the move to make room for the expected signing of Ignacio "⁠nacho⁠" Rodriguez, who last played for ShindeN before he parted ways with the organization last week.

In his year with BESTIA, timo helped the team climb to current 37th place on the Global VRS, their highest placing on the list since late 2024.

With the 21-year-old, the team most notably qualified for the BLAST.tv Austin Major, but were replaced ahead of the event after failing to obtain visas in time for the deadline.

More recently, they won Digital Crusade DraculaN Season 4 to close 2025 on a high note, ahead of teams such as fnatic, Inner Circle, ENCE and CYBERSHOKE.

BESTIA have:

Nicolás "⁠Noktse⁠" Dávila

Julián "⁠Zote⁠" Acosta (coach)

Cristhian "⁠timo⁠" Perez (benched)
