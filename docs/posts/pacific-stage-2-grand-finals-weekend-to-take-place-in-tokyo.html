Pacific Stage 2 Grand Finals Weekend to take place in Tokyo

Riot has announced that the VCT Pacific Stage 2 Grand Finals Weekend will take place in Tokyo, Japan on August 30-31.

The #VCTPacific Stage 2 Grand Finals Weekend will be held in Tokyo, Japan!

This will mark the first time that the Pacific region's finals weekend will be held outside of Korea. It will also be Tokyo's first VCT event since hosting Masters Tokyo in 2023. A specific venue for the matches has not been revealed.

The final weekend of league play typically consists of the lower finals and the grand final, as it was for the end of the Pacific League in 2023 and Stage 2 in 2024.

The Pacific region's season begins in a few days at Pacific Kickoff, with matches from January 17 to February 9. Per Riot's preseason overview, Stage 1 internationally will begin March 22 and end May 16, while Stage 2 begins July 12 and ends August 29.

Pacific Kickoff will take place in Seoul at the Sangam SOOP Colosseum. Venue information for Stage 1 and the earlier matches of Stage 2 has yet to be disclosed.
