FrosT transformed GE's presence in VCT, overseeing a transformation that brought the org from a Pacific minnow to a force to be reckoned with. The change came in true FrosT fashion, with unconventional comps and unique approaches highlighting GE's successes since his appointment last November.

Set to now coach his third team in the last 12 months, FrosT settles into a house with an exterior that is new to him, but has a floor plan that he is more than familiar with. He'll work most closely with Crws , who was his IGL on an all-Thai TALON team.
