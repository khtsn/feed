LEVIATÁN promotes academy members for 2026 roster

LEVIATÁN LEVIATÁN North America Rank #8 spike Rodrigo Lombardi blowz Guilherme Oliviera Neon Bruno Rodríguez Sato Eduardo Kenzo Nagahama Sato kiNgg Francisco Aravena has announced their 2026 roster, consisting of Francisco "kiNgg" Aravena, Eduardo "Sato" Kenzo Nagahama Sato, Guilherme "blowz" Oliviera, Bruno "Neon" Rodríguez, and Rodrigo "spike" Lombardi, with Rodrigo "Onur" Dalmagro and Cristian "Jhein" Camaño joining as the coaching staff.

Most of Leviatán's newcomers join from their largely successful academy roster from 2025. The two young rookie players, Neon and blowz, coached by Onur and Jhein, were a dominant force in LATAM Challengers. Now becoming eligible for VCT, the Duelist and Initiator duo will make their debut on the big stage.

The other new addition is spike, from 2Game Esports 2Game Esports Brazil Rank #7 . After winning Ascension in 2024, spike unfortunately had to sit out the majority of 2G's VCT Americas campaign due to being underage. When he did finally take the stage, he showed flashes of his talent, but 2G was largely outclassed by the rest of Americas, ending the year with only two wins to their name. The Duelist player, largely known for his Neon mastery, will get another chance to show his worth on the Americas stage with Leviatán.

Returning to Leviatán from 2025 are Sato and kiNgg. Sato was a late addition to the roster, only coming in for Stage 2. When he arrived, however, he was an immediate highlight reel, and instantly shot Leviatán back into relevancy after a disappointing first half of the year. kiNgg, on the other hand, has been Leviatán's long time IGL, and will be tasked with leading this new crop of young South American talent through the 2026 season.
