Betclic beat AM to win Parken Challenger Championship Season 2

Betclic have been crowned champions of Parken Challenger Championship Season 2 after sweeping AM 2-0 in the Danish LAN's grand final. The win also earned Betclic a Wildcard invite to CCT Global Finals, alongside Season 1 winner GamerLegion, with the event set to run from April 23-26.

The Poles advanced from the round-robin group stage with a 3-2 record in Group B, losing maps to Voca and AM, but shifted into a higher gear in the double-elimination playoffs bracket. They needed three maps each time, but completed an undefeated run in the upper bracket with wins over AaB, MASONIC, and Johnny Speeds to secure a spot in the title decider.
