NIP miss out on big VRS payout after narrow loss to PARIVISION

With pressure high in the final weeks of the race to the IEM Cologne Major, NIP missed out on a win that may have secured them the spot.

xKacpersky's Ancient 30-bomb was not enough

The second day of BLAST Open Rotterdam has kicked off with PARIVISION claiming a 2-1 win over Ninjas in Pyjamas to move on in the Group A upper bracket.

True to their identity, Dzhami "⁠Jame⁠" Ali and company had to squeeze out a hard-fought victory in their opening match of the tournament as they so often do.

They edged out Ancient 13-11 despite a slow start on the CT side, denying Kacper "⁠xKacpersky⁠" Gabara a well-earned win as the 30-bombing Pole was let down by the team.

Ninjas in Pyjamas then went flawless on the T side of Mirage for an equalizer on the back of Artem "⁠r1nkle⁠" Moroz, before PARIVISION came out victors from down 6-8 on the deciding Dust2 thanks to a superb defensive half.

It is a massive opportunity missed for Ninjas in Pyjamas to gain crucial ground on the Valve Regional Standings in the final weeks of the race to the IEM Cologne Major invite deadline in early April.

Currently sitting on the cusp at a projected 15th place in Europe out of 17 spots, a win against fifth-ranked PARIVISION would have seen them climb over 70 points and several spots deeper into invite territory.

Ninjas in Pyjamas still have an opportunity to recover in the lower bracket, where they'll first face off against the loser of Spirit vs. Liquid, while PARIVISION will play the winner for a spot in the playoffs.
