LOUD become first Americas team to reach Madrid

LOUD LOUD Brazil Rank #1 Quick Gabriel Lima saadhak Matias Delipetro Less Felipe de Loyola tuyz Arthur Andrade cauanzin Cauan Pereira became the seventh team to qualify for Masters Madrid, taking down Evil Geniuses Evil Geniuses North America Rank #1 Derrek Derrek Ha NaturE Nicholas Garrison supamen Phat Le Apoth Vincent Le jawgemo Alexander Mor to reach the VCT Americas Kickoff grand final. EG is eliminated from the event, while LOUD will play the winner of NRG Esports NRG Esports North America Rank #3 crashies Austin Roberts Victor Victor Wong Marved Jimmy Nguyen Ethan Ethan Arnold Demon1 Max Mazanov and Sentinels Sentinels North America Rank #5 TenZ Tyson Ngo Zellsis Jordan Montemurro zekken Zachary Patrone johnqt Mohamed Amine Ouarid Sacy Gustavo Rossi for the Americas first seed.

After Evil Geniuses took down LOUD on their map pick of Ascent in a comfortable 13-7 fashion with stellar performances from jawgemo and supamen , it looked as though LOUD could be in trouble. The Brazilian roster answered by allowing just 12 rounds across the next two maps to take the series and the first Americas spot at Masters Madrid.

Despite a couple strong performances from Derrek and Apoth , the EG roster in total struggled to find kills across the last two maps. Less and tuyz were the stars for LOUD on the scoreboard, but the entire team looked great in the series.
