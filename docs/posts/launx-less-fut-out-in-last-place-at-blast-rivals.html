lauNX-less FUT out in last place at BLAST Rivals

HooXi starred in Astralis' 2-0 win over FUT

FUT have been eliminated in last place of BLAST Rivals following a 0-2 loss to Astralis in the Group A lower bracket.

Playing with coach András "⁠coolio⁠" Fercsák instead of Laurențiu "⁠lauNX⁠" Țârlea, who was sidelined from the event by a stomach ulcer, expectations were low for the young squad.

And the lack of the Romanian's aggressive presence was felt as FUT struggled in opening up rounds without him, ending the event on just a 33% opening kill rate on T sides over five maps they played against Vitality and Astralis.

In the second series, that was especially on display on Mirage, where Astralis dominated with Rasmus "⁠HooXi⁠" Nielsen leading the way with his second-highest-rated map in CS2 at 1.79.

FUT were much more competitive on Nuke, but despite wining the second pistol round and getting out to a 9-5 lead, they faltered on the offense once again, where they had struggled all tournament.

Astralis move on to the quarter-finals following the victory, and are set on a collision course with the loser of Natus Vincere vs. GamerLegion.
