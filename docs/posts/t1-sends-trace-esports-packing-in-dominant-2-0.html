T1 sends Trace Esports packing in dominant 2-0

T1 T1 Korea Rank #3 stax Kim Gu-taek (김구택) Meteor Kim Tae-oh (김태오) Sylvan Go Young-sup (고영섭) BuZz Yu Byeong-cheol (유병철) iZu Ham Woo-ju (함우주) has defeated Trace Esports Trace Esports China Rank #2 LuoK1ng Zhang Zhanpeng (张展鹏) Kai Lu Zhinan (卢智楠) FengF Song Xuefeng (宋学锋) heybay Ho Shun Hei Biank Zhong Jianfei (钟剑飞) 2-0, eliminating them from Masters Bangkok. They will continue on in the Swiss stage, with one match standing between them and playoffs.

The series played out on Lotus and Haven. T1 won Lotus 13-3 and Haven 13-8.

Both teams were coming off 2-0 losses in their opening matches of the tournament. Trace were put down in dominant fashion by G2 Esports, but T1 had a relatively competitive series with Team Vitality. Facing elimination, T1 decided to adjust their starting roster, subbing out carpe and bring out Sylvan to face Trace. Sylvan was T1's initial starting controller, playing with T1 throughout all of the offseason and the beginning of Kickoff, but has been sitting out for the past four matches with carpe taking his spot.

Trace Esports are eliminated from Masters Bangkok, going home without winning a single map. T1 will go on to face one of DRX, G2 Esports, or the winner of Sentinels vs Team Liquid for a spot at playoffs.
