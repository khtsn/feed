The tournament started with the group stage, where each team was matched against four out of five other teams, with the top four teams moving on to the playoffs. The four teams coming out of playoffs were seeded into a gauntlet style bracket, placing Gen.G directly to the grand final, FPX in the semifinal, and Paper Rex and G2 meeting in the quarterfinal. Starting in the quarterfinal, Paper Rex notched their third win against G2 of the year. The teams traded the first two maps, both ending in close 13-10s, but the third map was a blowout for Paper Rex, Jinggg dominating on Phoenix.

Moving to the semifinal, Paper Rex made quick work of FPX in the rematch. A swift 2-0 brought them to the grand final, where Gen.G awaited. For more than a year, Gen.G has had Paper Rex's number, defeating them in all six of their last matches. However, with Paper Rex's new addition, the tables had turned.
