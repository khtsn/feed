The series began on EDG's map pick of Lotus. Both teams brought out similar compositions featuring Neon, Breach, and a less conventional Sova, with the only difference being EDG's signature Clove to XLG's Cypher.

The second half closely resembled the first. EDG managed to force overtime, but a 1v3 from happywei in the final round won Lotus 14-12 for XLG despite losing both bonuses.
