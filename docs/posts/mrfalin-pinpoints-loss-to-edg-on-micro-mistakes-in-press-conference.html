MrFaliN pinpoints loss to EDG on micro mistakes in press conference

FUT Esports FUT Esports Europe Unranked MrFaliN Furkan Yeğen yetujey Eray Budak AtaKaptan Ata Tan cNed Mehmet Yağız İpek qRaxs Doğukan Balaban and EDward Gaming EDward Gaming China Unranked S1Mon Hsien Meng-Hsun (谢孟勋) ZmjjKK Zheng Yongkang (郑永康) nobody Wang Senxu (王森旭) CHICHOO Wan Shunji (万顺治) Smoggy Zhang Zhao (张钊) capped off day four of Champions Seoul , with a swift 2-0 victory for the Chinese side in a rematch against the Turkish team. FUT spoke to members of the media regarding the loss in their post-match press conference, saying their loss was solely due to micro mistakes throughout the match.

Were there any unexpected challenges or surprises during the match that threw off your strategies?

Gais: "No, there wasn't. There wasn't any."

How are you feeling after the match against EDG? Were there any factors that led to the loss?

MrFaliN: "Obviously, I feel sad about it. I can also obviously say that EDG did some stuff in the game that managed to help them get the win, but I have to say that we had some micro mistakes in the game, which led to us losing it. It was due to our micro mistakes."

Were there any specific players or strategies from EDG that you found particularly hard to counter?

cNed: "No."

Did you learn more from this loss? What have you learned from this match and what can you fix for the next match?

cNed: "I think there wasn't any big problem during this game. It was just about getting momentum, losing some micro situations. That's just the situation, sometimes you win, sometimes you lose."

FUT didn't play Bind in Stage 2, despite leaving it open and having it in the veto. Would you say that not having any officials in Stage 2 on Bind led to some rustiness or inconsistency on the map?

MrFaliN: "I don't think there's much relevance in this. As I said, I think we lost the match due to our micro mistakes. It was not due to our game plan, but micro mistakes were why we lost Bind and the game."
