This American Life

    What’s it like when all eyes are on you?

    April 27, 2024, 6:40 a.m. ET

    New York Times Audio

    On This Week’s Episode:

    Gladiators in the Colosseum. Sideshow performers. Reality television. Throughout our history, we’ve loved to gawk at the misery and majesty of others.

    In this episode, “This American Life” asks: What’s it like when the tables are turned and all eyes are on you?

    This is a rerun of an episode that first aired in October 2015.

    The New York Times Audio app is home to journalism and storytelling and provides news, depth and serendipity. It is available to Times news subscribers on iOS. If you haven’t already, download the app and sign up for our weekly newsletter.
