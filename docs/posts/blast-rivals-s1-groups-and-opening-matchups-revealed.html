BLAST Rivals S1 groups and opening matchups revealed

BLAST has revealed the groups and opening matchups for the upcoming BLAST Rivals 2026 Season 1, set to begin on April 27 in Fort Worth, Texas.

The eight-team pool has been split into two groups, with the winner of each group advancing straight to the semi-finals, while the second and third-place teams will secure quarter-final spots.

Vitality have been put into Group A and are set to face off against FUT in their first match, with Astralis set to meet G2 in the other group opener.

In Group B, Natus Vincere will get the action underway with a match against FaZe before FURIA take on GamerLegion.
