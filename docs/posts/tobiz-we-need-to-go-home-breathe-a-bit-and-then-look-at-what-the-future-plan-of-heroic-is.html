TOBIZ: "We need to go home, breathe a bit, and then look at what the future plan of HEROIC is"

HEROIC endured a rough season ever since Alimzhan "⁠Alkaren⁠" Bitimbay was forced into inactivity due to visa issues, prompting the team's coach Tobias "⁠TOBIZ⁠" Theo to describe their early exit from the Cologne Major as "pretty symptomatic for the whole season."

The European combine's solitary win came against the struggling Gaimin Gladiators, but TOBIZ refuses to put the lack of results only on the AWPer change.

"At some point, we're not using it as an excuse any more," he told HLTV in an exit interview. "It becomes more of everybody else's mistakes that led us to defeat, not Simon [yxngstxr] at all."

Listen to the interview here.

Commiserations on the loss, probably not the way you wanted this tournament to go, even if it got somewhat close in the end. Are there any positives that you can take from this whole tournament from your side?

Right now it's very hard to see the good rounds. Right now it's the bad rounds, bad situations, and bad team mistakes we are doing that are popping into the head. Against Gaimin Gladiator we played well, even if people are saying they're bad and so on. I think we played well and showed glimpses of what we actually do in practice.

For the two games on the opening day, I think we made too many individual mistakes and they just cost us. That has been pretty symptomatic for the whole season, I feel like. So not a lot of good stuff to take away from this, but for sure something to evaluate on.

To ask about the whole season. There were some positives, like making the Major and some runs here and there, but overall I guess it's not what you expected. What do you think is the biggest reason that kind of never got really going for you guys this year?

It's hard to say. It was a turbulent start, especially with Alkaren and the whole visa situation, and having Simon [yxngstxr] rushed in and having to play a new position, which I think he has done fantastically. At some point, we're not using it as an excuse any more, it becomes more of everybody else's mistakes that led us to defeat, not Simon at all.

Looking at the year overall, there are some nice wins here and there, but the consistency is just not there. I think we have a hard time analyzing what is going wrong exactly, whether you're playing a Major or you're playing a DraculaN game, we still have the same issues when we're losing. We need to be honest and have a bigger talk about what the issues are, and how we can try to deal with them or fix them in the future.

Just to ask about the future. You announced that this will be the roster until the end of this season, so can you share anything about the future of HEROIC?

Right now, we have to go home and evaluate everything, evaluate me, evaluate the players, everything. So no, I don't think I can give any answers to that. We need to go home, breathe a little bit, and then look at what the future plan of HEROIC is. We already received some invites and talked about tournaments, so that is in place, but we have to see.
