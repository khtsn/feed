Team Liquid allows trexx to explore options

Nikita "trexx" Cherednichenko has announced that he has been allowed by Team Liquid Team Liquid Europe Rank #7 nAts Ayaz Akhmetshin keiko Georgio Sanassy kamo Kamil Frąckowiak to explore his options with a new team this offseason.

"I'm allowed to explore my options by Team Liquid as restricted F/A," trexx said. "We managed to dominate EMEA in such short period of time and I'm thankful for that, now it's time to move into the next chapter."

The 2025 season has been a hectic one for trexx. He started the year with Team Vitality Team Vitality Europe Rank #23 Derke Nikita Sirmitev Kicks Kimmie Laasner Chronicle Timofey Khromov KovaQ Blendi Kovaci Less Felipe de Loyola Basso , the squad he had been with since the start of 2024, and entered the year as part of a new EMEA super team alongside new signings Derke and Less . After winning EMEA Kickoff, trexx and Vitality entered Masters Bangkok as one of the favorites, but fell short of those lofty expectations, finishing fourth.

trexx was then moved to the bench in favor of CyvOph , and spent his time on the sidelines until the start of EMEA Stage 2, when he was picked up by Team Liquid. Joining the team as a go-to Initiator player allowed for Team Liquid to pair trexx with paTiTek as part of double-Initiator team comps.
