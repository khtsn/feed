CS Asia Championships 2026

Format

  • You can create your teams on the Fantasy page here.

  • Two eight-team double-elimination group brackets.

  • Six-team single-elimination playoff bracket with a third-place decider.

  • All matches are BO3 except for the BO1 opening round and BO5 grand final.

Teams

Things to note 

  • This Fantasy includes both the group stage and playoffs due to the format, schedule, and overall strength of the field.

  • Teams making it to group upper finals will get one round of padding. Winners of the upper finals will get another round of padding for skipping quarter-finals.

Player prices & pick stats

  • Maksim "⁠kyousuke⁠" Lukin and Finn "⁠karrigan⁠" Andersen top the pick count with 42% and 39% teams featuring the Falcons duo respectively. karrigan goes as a silver card at $188,000 despite his abysmal 0.73 rating in Astana. Whether the rating, role, and booster points can compensate for the potential negative rating points is up to you.

  • Marco "⁠Snappi⁠" Pfeiffer is the second-cheapest player (after Robin "⁠ScrunK⁠" Röpke) at $170,000, earning the position of most-picked bronze card in this Fantasy game. He might be a must if you want to have m0NESY, but whether he will avoid going deep into the red is questionable as Ninjas in Pyjamas are the third-lowest ranked team at the event.
