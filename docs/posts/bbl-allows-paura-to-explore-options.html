“Even though our talks with BBL for 2025 is ongoing, they have allowed me to explore my options as a restricted free agent,” he said.

The sentinel player, who in 2024 expanded to play a number of initiator roles, was BBL's in-game leader this past year.

This past season, BBL failed to qualify for the knockout rounds of any competitions they were in. A 3-3 start in EMEA Stage 1 saw them finish in a five-way tie on win-loss record in Group Alpha, but they finished fourth and missed out on playoffs.
