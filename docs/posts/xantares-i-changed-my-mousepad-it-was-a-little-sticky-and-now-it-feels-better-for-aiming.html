XANTARES: "I changed my mousepad, it was a little sticky, and now it feels better for aiming"

Aurora romped past BetBoom in the LANXESS Arena to reach the semi-finals of the IEM Cologne Major, starting off the series dominantly thanks to Ismailcan "⁠XANTARES⁠" Dörtkardeş having his best Big Event playoff map in a true arena.

"Lately, I've had a lot of health issues, so I need a little bit of a vacation break, I hope," he told HLTV after the match. "If I can [do that], I can be better. Right now, I can say I feel amazing, I hope we can beat FURIA or 9z [on Saturday] as well. We're going to do our best."

The Turkish rifler was also hopeful of Aurora making a run to the final, saying if his team can work on its weaknesses before Saturday, he believes a deep run or even a trophy finish is possible.

"If everyone feels comfortable and is playing their best CS, then I don't think a lot of teams will have too many chances against us," he said.

Listen to the interview here.

Congratulations, you're into the semis. A big moment for Aurora and Turkish CS. What are you thinking after that and how are you feeling?

I feel amazing. We did good preparation against them, and lately we improved a lot. Everyone understands everything a bit [better], and we didn't feel pressure as well, so it was a fun game for us.

It was actually your best playoff map in a proper arena on Nuke, talk to me about that.

This is a lie, actually (smiles), but it's okay, I also played very good in EWC. But I don't know, lately, I've had a lot of health issues, so I need a little bit of a vacation break, I hope. If I can [do that], I can be better. Right now, I can say I feel amazing, I hope we can beat FURIA or 9z [on Saturday] as well. We're going to do our best.

What is contributing to your form right now?

Uh... to be honest, I changed my mousepad, maybe that's why (laughs). It was like a little bit sticky, and now it feels better for aiming. I always play for my team, I don't play for myself. I play for them always.

You're going to play the winner of FURIA and 9z on the day after tomorrow. Thoughts on that potential matchup, who you'd like to face, and how you think it's going to go?

We played against 9z last week, but they were not at 100% power because the day before, they played against Spirit. It was a hard day for them because all the games were very close for them, so they didn't play the best CS against us; it was a quick game. For [Saturday], I'm pretty sure they will play so much better against FURIA, maybe they have a good chance actually.

The bracket is pretty favorable for you (XANTARES nods), I think you know that. What do you think your odds are of making the final and how much would that mean to you?

We came here with a big hope to make the final or win the tournament, but now we have a good chance to win, actually. [Saturday] is very key, and we need to start the work from now because we have a little bit of weakness now, and maybe [on Saturday] we can be better.

Other players as well, maybe they'll feel a bit more comfortable, and if everyone feels comfortable and is playing their best CS, then I don't think a lot of teams will have too many chances against us.
