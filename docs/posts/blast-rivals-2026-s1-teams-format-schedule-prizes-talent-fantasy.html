Teams and format

Eight teams will participate in BLAST Rivals 2026 S1, all of which have received invites through the VRS ranking.

Five top 10 teams have accepted invitations to the Texas event, namely Vitality, Natus Vincere, FURIA, FUT, and Astralis, with G2, FaZe, and GamerLegion coming in as underdogs.

FUT will be playing without Laurențiu "⁠lauNX⁠" Țârlea, as head coach András "⁠coolio⁠" Fercsák will step in for the Romanian due to health issues.

The teams have been split into two groups as follows, with the top three teams in each group progressing to the playoffs. The group winners will earn a ticket directly to the semi-final, while second and third-placed teams will battle it out in the quarters.

Schedule and opening matchups

The tournament's group stage will be played across two days and start on Wednesday, April 29, with Vitality taking on FUT.

The group-stage will then feed into a six-team playoff bracket in the Dickies Arena, where the action is set to kick off on May 1.
