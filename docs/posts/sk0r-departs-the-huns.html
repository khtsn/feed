sk0R departs The Huns

Tengis "⁠sk0R⁠" Batjargal and The Huns have parted ways, bringing an end to his second stint with the Mongolian team, the organization announced on Thursday.

He leaves the team following a year-long tenure, during which the team took part in several tier-one events without much success after qualifying through their region.

That included the StarLadder Budapest Major's first stage, which marked the Mongolian organization's first participation at a Major, and where the team went out with a 1-3 record.

sk0R averaged a 1.13 rating over the 12 months he served under The Huns, which largely came against regional competition.

Like the rest of the team, he struggled when they faced off against international opposition, posting a 0.93 rating over 17 maps against top-30 teams.
