FURIA set up Falcons clash after eliminating NRG

FURIA have set up a clash with Falcons to decide the final playoff spot from Group A at BLAST Open after beating NRG 2-1.

NRG secured their map pick of Mirage with a powerful CT side, bouncing back after a poor T side. The American side bagged a 3-0 lead off the pistol win but were thoroughly outclassed for the rest of the first half once FURIA got guns up, giving the Brazilian team a 8-4 score at halftime.

When the sides switched NRG posted an even more resolute defense than FURIA had, taking advantage of FURIA's static offensive throughout the half with information plays, before stopping FURIA dead with efficient crossfires when the bombsite hits came through.

A CT-side pistol win greeted NRG on Nuke but FURIA answered back immediately with an Upper pop on a force-buy, after which the Brazilian side never looked back: both Kaike "⁠KSCERATO⁠" Cerato and Danil "⁠molodoy⁠" Golubenko popped up with multi-kills in man-down situations to keep FURIA rolling all the way to a convincing 13-2 win.

molodoy ended the map with 149.4 ADR, and a 2.63 rating that is his career high effort, while KSCERATO was just behind on a 2.33.

Dust2 was more competitive, after an Josh "⁠oSee⁠" Ohm 1v3 clutch sent NRG to a 4-1 lead. But molodoy and Mareks "⁠YEKINDAR⁠" Gaļinskis were in frightening form before FURIA won a 2v5 and 2v4 to put the map and series to bed.
