SAG Awards 2025 Red Carpet: See All the Looks

See what film and television stars wore as they arrived for the Screen Actors Guild Awards.

Feb. 23, 2025Updated 6:45 p.m. ET

In a season of many awards, the Screen Actors Guild ceremony does something few others can: It focuses strictly on the stars. (Those behind the camera will get their time in the spotlight next weekend.) On Sunday night, television and film actors were to be celebrated at the SAG Awards, now in their 31st year, for their work as individuals and as ensembles or casts.

Those not at the Shrine Auditorium in Los Angeles can tune in on Netflix, with red carpet coverage beginning at 7 p.m. Eastern and the ceremony, hosted by Kristen Bell, at 8 p.m.
