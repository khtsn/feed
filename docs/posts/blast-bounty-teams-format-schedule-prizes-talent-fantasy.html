BLAST Bounty returns next week

Teams and format

A total of 32 teams will participate in BLAST Bounty 2026 Season 2, of which 28 came from the Global VRS as of June 2 and four as Wildcard invites.

Some notable teams missing from the list are Natus Vincere, 9z, BetBoom, Legacy and PARIVISION.

The event will start online with the first two rounds of the single-elimination playoffs, each of which begins with a draft phase where the teams seeded in the bottom half pick from the top half.

The top-eight teams will then have three days to travel to Malta to prepare for the LAN portion of the tournament taking place from July 30-August 2.

Prizes

BLAST Bounty 2026 Season 2 boasts a $1.15 million prize pool, split between prize money ($500,000) and club share ($650,000).

Instead of traditional prize money, each team in attendance will be assigned a "bounty" corresponding to their seeding in the event. The winning team secures 50% of their opponent's bounty as a cash prize, while the remaining 50% is carried over to the next match and gets added to the winning team's total bounty.
