Inner Circle win Super DraculaN over Acend

Inner Circle have won Super DraculaN Season 1 after a 3-1 win over Acend in the best-of-five final.

The Ukrainian core, or organization, had never won an event covered by HLTV before this and have shot up to 27th on the VRS after taking home the trophy from the first edition of the $150,000 Bucharest event.

Their run took them past AM, 9INE, Sashi, Walczaki, Sharks, and finally Acend where all five players contributed to the win late on Sunday evening.

Inner Circle did have a wobble after dropping Mirage from 10-6 up, but bounced back over the next three maps with in-game leader Viktor "⁠onic⁠" Babii's 1.49 rating on Ancient putting the bow on a strong 3-1 victory.

The team endured a dip in form this year after losing Artem "⁠cairne⁠" Mushynskyi, but have come back alive since adding Daniil "⁠headtr1ck⁠" Valitov with a bronze at BCG Masters S2 and a gold here.

Earlier in the tournament, heavy favorites GamerLegion were eliminated in last-place, which sees them lose 58 points on the VRS in another damaging result after a disappointing Major.
