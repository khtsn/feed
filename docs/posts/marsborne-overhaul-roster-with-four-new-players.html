Marsborne overhaul roster with four new players

Marsborne have announced a sweeping roster overhaul following the end of IEM Cologne Major's qualifying cycle, benching every player except Adam "⁠Grizz⁠" Golden.

Heading into the final stretch of the qualifying cycle, the North American side sat just outside the top 10 in the Americas VRS, but early exits at FERJEE In House at BetBoom Circuit X Mayhem São Paulo saw them drop to 19th by the invite cut-off.

"For the team and for the organization moving forward, we felt like things were starting to get very bloated, and so we had to make a directional change for what the future of Marsborne is going to look like," Marsborne head coach Hunter "⁠Lucid⁠" Tucker said in a video posted to X.

To fill the vacancies, Marsborne signed BOSS' Chance "⁠WUMBO⁠" Boyer and Mark "⁠marekiew⁠" Petrov, as well as insane players duo Adam "⁠freshie⁠" Paterson and Bogdan "⁠ogwizard⁠" Savula.

BOSS coach Ben "⁠ben1337⁠" Smith has also been brought in as an assistant to Lucid, who is combining the roles of head coach and general manager.

"One thing that we have heard from North American fans is that there's a serious repetition of old talents that get recycled on rosters over and over again," Lucid said.

"We are going to break that cycle and build a new, younger, and hopefully exciting team for everybody to follow."

The new Marsborne roster is expected to make its first appearance at Fragadelphia 20, which will take place between April 24-26 in Las Vegas.

The roster now looks as follows:

Adam "⁠Grizz⁠" Golden

Hunter "⁠Lucid⁠" Tucker (head coach)

Connor "⁠chop⁠" Sullivan (benched)
