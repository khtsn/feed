Highlights from IEM Cologne Major 2026 Stage 2 Day 2

B8 5-7 GamerLegion

Two of the 3-0 favorites for Stage 1 have their backs against the wall to kick off Stage 2, fighting to keep their Major run alive in the 0-2 pool.

A force-buy conversion helped GamerLegion take a 5-2 lead on the defense of Inferno, but B8 eventually got three back to prevent a blowout as we head into the second half.
