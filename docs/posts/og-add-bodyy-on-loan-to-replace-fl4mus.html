OG add bodyy on loan to replace FL4MUS

OG have announced the addition of Alexandre "⁠bodyy⁠" Pianaro on loan from 3DMAX, with the Frenchman replacing Timur "⁠FL4MUS⁠" Marev on the team's active roster.

It is the first roster change of the year for the Danish organization as it looks to curb a sharp decline on the VRS.

OG began the year ranked #49 after debuting with FL4MUS and Casper "⁠cadiaN⁠" Møller in a 3-4th finish at DreamHack Knockout Stockholm, but tumbled down to #92 in the latest ranking update after last-place finishes in BLAST Bounty, CCT Season 3 Series 12, and a 9-12th exit in the IEM Atlanta Closed Qualifier after advancing through the open bracket.

bodyy joins OG after he was moved to 3DMAX's bench in February to make room for Kévin "⁠misutaaa⁠" Rabier. The 29-year-old rifler averaged a 1.03 rating during his year-long stint with the French organization, and arrives on OG as a replacement for FL4MUS, who has been moved to the organization's inactive roster after joining on loan from Virtus.pro in November.

"Happy to join OG for the next few weeks on a temporary loan," bodyy said in a post on X. "Good to see some familiar faces again. Time to give everything to climb the VRS ranking and see if we can get back into the Major race. Big challenge ahead, but that’s the fun part. Plenty of LAN events to come, let’s see where we are in a month."

bodyy will debut for OG at Roman Imperium Cup VI, scheduled to run from March 12-15 in Vila Nova de Gaia, Portugal. The team has been placed in Group D alongside BC.Game and Alliance.
