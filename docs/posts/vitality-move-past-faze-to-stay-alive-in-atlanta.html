Vitality move past FaZe to stay alive in Atlanta

Despite a 2-0 victory, Vitality were far from their imperious best on Nuke.

Vitality have eliminated FaZe from IEM Atlanta in a 2-0 win (Nuke 16-14, Dust2 13-5) to continue their lower-bracket run in the American tournament.

Their next opponent will be B8, who sent FUT home earlier in the day.

The series started very well for Vitality, who batted away anything the FaZe Nuke T-side had to offer, but Russel "⁠Twistzz⁠" Van Dulken's men soon came back into the game and reduced the deficit 5-7 despite Robin "⁠ropz⁠" Kool winning a trademark 1vs3 clutch.

FaZe's momentum continued as they extended their lead to 11-8, at which point Vitality's individuals started to wake up. Mathieu "⁠ZywOo⁠" Herbaut was crucial in securing overtime for his team before another ropz clutch and Shahar "⁠flameZ⁠" Shushan's ace secured a 16-14 victory.

On Dust2, Vitality were much closer to their usual level. They breezed to a 13-5 victory despite losing both pistols, with ZywOo leading the way with a 1.86 rating on his team's map pick.

FaZe's post-karrigan era off to an unconvincing start

"The Vitality plot armor hit late on, and we just couldn't close it out," Twistzz said in a post-match broadcast interview about FaZe's impressive Nuke display against the undisputed number-one in the world, but FaZe flattered to deceive in Atlanta otherwise.

They were comfortably beaten by paiN in their opener and while a win against NRG will give the team some confidence, FaZe have much to do ahead of their next event at Stake Ranked Episode 2.
