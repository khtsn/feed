ESL announce opening matchups for IEM Rio

ESL has announced the opening matchups and groups for IEM Rio, which is slated to run from April 13-19.

The 16 teams have been split into two groups of eight and will compete in a double-elimination, best-of-three bracket, following ESL's usual format — which will change in 2027 — with the group winners advancing directly to the semi-finals and the second and third-place teams heading to the quarter-finals.

The event will be Vitality's first of three chances this season to complete their second ESL Grand Slam, and will look to carry on their 22-map winstreak in an opener against RED Canids.

Highlights from the opening round include Spirit taking on Liquid, Falcons going up against 3DMAX, and B8 facing Natus Vincere in a rematch of the teams' opener at BLAST Open Rotterdam.
