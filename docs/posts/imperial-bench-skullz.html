Imperial bench skullz

Imperial have benched Felipe "⁠skullz⁠" Medeiros after six months in the starting lineup.

The squad made it to Stage 3 in Budapest, but did not play any other Big Events with skullz after failing to succeed enough locally to reach invite status.

The 23-year-old averaged a 1.01 rating over 110 maps, far below the level he would have hoped for having dropped a tier after troublesome stints in Liquid and FURIA. The potential he showcased in paiN — a 1.16 rating — has yet to be fully realized in CS2.
