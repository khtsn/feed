Juv3nile joins Global Esports as head coach

Global Esports Global Esports Asia-Pacific Unranked has picked up Preston "Juv3nile" Dornon as their head coach for the 2025 season.

Juv3nile most recently served LEVIATÁN LEVIATÁN Latin America South Unranked Demon1 Max Mazanov tex Ian Botsch kiNgg Francisco Aravena aspas Erick Santos C0M Corbin Lee as an assistant coach. His addition in June of 2024 came as part of a larger coaching shift for the team, as they had just parted ways with former head coach Alex "goked" Kie and promoted previous assistant coach Dimitar "Itopata" Staev in his stead following an early exit in the Swiss stage of Masters Shanghai.

Leviatán proceeded to find successes throughout the rest of the year, winning Americas Stage 2 and going on an impressive run to place third at Champions 2024.

Global Esports entered the 2024 season with an completely different roster compared to 2023. They promoted previous substitute Abhirup "Lightningfast" Choudhury, brought in Niko "Polvi" Polvinen from EMEA, and rounded out the roster with the likes of Gary "blaZek1ng" Dastin, Benedict "Benkai" Tan, and Russel "Russ" Mendes as the in-game leader.

While the team managed to pick up a win against BLEED during Kickoff, they were quickly dispatched by ZETA DIVISION and exited in the group stage of the event. The rest of the season continued similarly; the team ultimately ended the regular season with a 3-7 record, failing to qualify for the playoffs of either stage and finishing ninth in the league.

Since then, Global Esports once again parted ways with their entire roster in favor of another rebuild. Juv3nile is the first of many pieces to be added to the squad for the coming year.
