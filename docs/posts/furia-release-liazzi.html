FURIA release liazzi

After only five months, FURIA FURIA Brazil Rank #3 kon4n Vitor Hugo mwzera Leonardo Serrati liazzi Felipe Galiazzi havoc Ilan Eloy Khalil Khalil Schmidt have parted ways with Felipe "liazzi" Galiazzi. The change follows a winless group stage at Kickoff, sent home after losses to NRG and MIBR.

Previously a duelist, liazzi joined FURIA as their controller player in October 2023. With this new role he helped the team to a win at MEG 2023 and top-two finish at Superdome Colombia. Unfortunately, they would go out in last place in Convergence 2023 after losses to Team Vitality and Global Esports.

The poor results continued into Americas Kickoff, where FURIA was eliminated without winning a single map. Compared to his previous performances, liazzi went from a 1.18 rating at the beginning of 2023 to averaging a 0.97 rating throughout the off-season and Kickoff.

Now down to four players, FURIA have two weeks before their first match of the VCT regular season. The new roster will debut against KRÜ Esports KRÜ Esports Latin America South Rank #5 keznit Angelo Mori Klaus Nicolas Ferrari mta Nicolás González Shyy Fabian Usnayo Melser Marco Eliot Machuca Amaro , a team they have defeated twice in five previous encounters.
