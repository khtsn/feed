China Ascension 2025 playoff teams locked

The group stage of China Ascension 2025 has concluded. The four teams still standing will face off in playoffs beginning Oct. 16, where they will vie for one open spot in VCT CN in the VCT CN Arena in Shanghai, China.

The event kicked off with group A's opening matches, where Zhu Chao Gaming defeated Octagonal Disposition Gaming in 2-1 fashion while DRG sailed past Thunder Gaming in a clean 2-0.

SpiritZ1 cleans up TG in their opening match with four quick kills.

From there, DRG comfortably qualified for playoffs with a 2-0 over ZCG, going undefeated in maps without letting their opponents reach double digits in a single one. All five DRG members ranked top ten by K:D across the group stage, but it was SpiritZ1 who dominated individually, putting up a tournament-leading 307 ACS and 1.10 KPR.

Down in the lower bracket, ODG battled for survival with a 2-1 win over TG. The group decider was a rematch between ODG and ZCG that came down to the wire, but ODG eked out the final map 15-13 to win the series 2-1.

Rare Atom was the first team to make it out of group B by defeating Any Questions Gaming and Ambitious Legend Gaming. While they didn't drop a map, they had close shaves against ALG in the upper bracket with the series going 13-11 and 15-13 in their favor. Led statistically by zhe and Satoshi with KPRs of 0.94 and 0.86 respectively, the two-time Ascension finalists will seek to finally clear the last hurdle to VCT in their third consecutive playoffs run.

The final team to qualify for playoffs was AQG, who defeated CCG 2-0 and ALG 2-1 to secure their spot. Champions 2024 winner S1Mon leads the squad by the numbers, currently ranking third in the event by ACS and KPR with 246 ACS and 0.89 KPR while playing multiple roles.

S1mon slips through the smoke and wipes out CCG.
