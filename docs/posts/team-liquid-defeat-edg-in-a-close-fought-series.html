Team Liquid now advances to the upper semifinals, while EDG has been sent down to the lower bracket.

The three map series played out on Haven, Split, and Lotus. Haven was EDG's pick and Split was Team Liquid's, with Lotus being the decider. Liquid won their opponent's map choice of Haven 13-9, but EDG bounced back to win Split 13-8. It came down to map three, Lotus, which Team Liquid eventually won, 13-11.

This series seemed to be a battle between the duelists, with both EGD's ZmjjKK and TL's Sayf putting up 60+ kills each across all three maps. Map three in particular showed TL struggling to close it out, with EDG coming back from 12-7, almost pushing it to OT.
