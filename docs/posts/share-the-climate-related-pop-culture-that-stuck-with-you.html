Share the Climate-Related Pop Culture That Stuck With You

The Climate Forward team wants to hear about climate-focused books, art, film, TV and music that moved you in 2023.

Dec. 21, 2023, 1:19 p.m. ET

2023: a year when climate change felt inescapable, and a year when climate change started to feel ubiquitous in popular culture. In this week’s Climate Forward newsletter, we listed the climate culture pieces that resonated with us.

Now, we want to hear from you. Which works of climate-related pop culture from this year made an impact on you? Books, films, songs, performances, TikTok videos, paintings and more are all fair game.

We might use what you share with us as part of an upcoming newsletter that lists our readers’ recommendations. We won’t share your response with anyone outside our newsroom or use your contact information beyond following up with you. And we won’t publish your submission without first contacting you to verify that your information and response are accurate.
