Trace Esports defeat TYLOO in China opener

Trace Esports have defeated TYLOO 2-0 in the first-ever VCT China match. They advance to the Group A Winner's match, where they will face off against either FunPlus Phoenix or NOVA Esports for a spot in playoffs. TYLOO, meanwhile, will next play the loser of FPX vs. NOVA in the Elimination match.

The match began on Ascent, which Trace claimed with a decisive 13-4. Continuing on Sunset, their map pick, they were able to win 13-3 to close out the series.

The following matchup will be crucial for Trace, as they will be offered an opportunity at avoiding a highly-competitive play-in stage and earn a short break before playoffs.
