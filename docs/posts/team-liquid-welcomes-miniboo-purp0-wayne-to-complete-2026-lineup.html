Team Liquid Team Liquid Europe Rank #8 purp0 Semyon Borchev wayne Wayne Chang MiniBoo Dominykas Lukaševičius nAts Ayaz Akhmetshin kamo Kamil Frąckowiak has announced their full lineup for the 2026 season. Dominykas "MiniBoo" Lukaševičius, Semyon "purp0" Borchev, and Wayne "wayne" Chang have been added to the roster alongside returning members Ayaz "nAts" Akhmetshin and Kamil "kamo" Frąckowiak.

In addition to the announcement, the team shared a video featuring nAts and coaches Ivo "LohaN" Albino and Daniel "yaotziN." Roczniak where the trio provided insight into the construction of the roster.

“I think I was looking for players that were mainly passionate about the game, that were grinding the game a lot,” said LohaN. “From the players that we trialed, the players that we [interviewed], I truly believe that these are the players that fit this profile the best.”
