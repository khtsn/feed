FUT survive T1 comebacks to reach playoffs

The first time FUT met T1, the Turks held the Koreans to win a mere 11 rounds over two maps. That game sent both teams in opposite directions in Group B, but here in the decider, they met once more. Although T1 put up a better fight, FUT still closed out the series 2-0 to reach the playoffs.

T1 mounted comebacks on both Bind and Pearl after falling into large deficits early on. Big clutches and multikills nearly kept them in the series, but FUT managed to close. With the win, FUT sent a Turkish team to international playoffs for the first time in VCT history.

MrFaliN was a standout performer for FUT. Not only did he call a dynamic strategic game, but he also put up the most kills and least deaths across the series at 45-25. The rest of his team finished with higher ratings than all of T1 but Sayaplayer, their only consistent performer.

Individual moments just barely failed to sustain T1.
