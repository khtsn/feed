max: "luchov is an amazing player. I wanted him a few years ago, but we couldn't buy him"

9z made the most of a tough spree of matchups in Astana, besting three of the tournament's top-ranked teams — PARIVISION, Falcons, and MOUZ — to secure the first playoffs berth in the Barys Arena.

It is an impressive result for the South American side, who are using the event to gauge where they stand after winning several domestic titles since the start of the year. Speaking to HLTV after 9z beat MOUZ in the 2-0 pool, Maximiliano "⁠max⁠" Gonzalez shared where he believes his team stands now.

"I think we're close to tier-one, but not yet," he said. "We have a lot of work to do yet, but this tournament helped us a lot with the confidence. Now we know that we can get to tier-one, so now we'll focus on the playoffs, and then the Major."

The Uruguayan captain also sang his praises for birthday boy Luciano "⁠luchov⁠" Herrera, who ended the group stage with an impressive 1.35 rating.

"luchov is an amazing player," max said. "I wanted him a few years ago, but at that moment, we couldn't buy him. But last year he came, and he's doing an amazing job. I'm very happy with his performance and how he is as a person. He's really a good person, so I'm very happy."

Listen to the interview here.

Congratulations, a huge win for you, and a huge run so far in Astana. Tell me how you're feeling after that.

Yes, we came to this tournament to prove ourselves against the best teams in the world, so making it to the playoffs was our goal. And it was luchov's birthday today, so I'm very happy about that.

Talk to me about the teams you took down here. You beat PARIVISION, you beat Falcons, you beat MOUZ to get in the playoffs. Big names, so what are your thoughts after making that run?

For me, it's incredible to beat them. To learn about CS, I watched them, so playing against them and winning is incredible for me.

luchov is a vocal person. He likes to say what he wants to do, so when he is in that mood, I just follow him

As you said, it's luchov's birthday; a nice little gift for him. He seems like he's really been unlocked here. Talk to me about his performance and how he's playing, how he's doing this so easily here.

luchov is an amazing player. I wanted him a few years ago, but at that moment, we couldn't buy him. But last year he came, and he's doing an amazing job. I'm very happy with his performance and how he is as a person. He's really a good person, so I'm very happy.

Can you tell me more about him as a person and how he is in the server? For people who aren't part of the same community, they don't know much about him.

He's a vocal person. He likes to say what he wants to do, so when he is in that mood, I just follow him and try to help the team follow him in his ideas. He speaks a lot, and he helps me a lot in being an IGL.

I think we're close to tier-one, but not yet. We have a lot of work to do

I talked to BIT after your first win here, and he talked about wanting to come here and prove yourself against the best teams because it's hard to know where you actually stand when you haven't been playing the biggest events. Where do you think you stand now?

I think we're close to tier-one, but not yet. We have a lot of work to do yet, but this tournament helped us a lot with the confidence. Now we know that we can get to tier-one, so now we'll focus on the playoffs, and then the Major.

Your last chance at a tier-one event, you had a pretty difficult road. You played Spirit, Vitality, and MOUZ, so when you walked away from that, what were you thinking?

We learned a lot in that tournament. Vitality and Spirit were really tough games, but that helped us to talk [about] a lot of things we needed to talk [about]. So we learned from that, and yeah, we got better.

Can you tell me more about the team dynamic and how you lead in this team?

I'm trying to be a positive leader when we are not winning, and trying to be more hard when we are winning. I try to be the person who needs to cheer up the team when everything is going bad; I try to be more positive and keep the team up.

BIT helps me a lot with the tactics, exp, our analyst, as well. So I think in our team, everyone has the right to talk and give his ideas, and I'm trying to be a captain who hears my players and hears what they want to do.

Something people noticed after you beat Falcons yesterday, HUASO has these Cars [Crocs] [max laughs]. Tell me about that, is there a story behind it?

Yeah, that is a funny thing because he loves to play [like that], he's comfortable in that way. He's a little child (laughs) in some ways, and in other ways, he's a really good man. It's a funny thing that he likes, and he's comfortable playing like that.

Obviously this is a big arena appearance secured for you. How do you think you're going to do going into that? What do you want to do when you get on the stage?

I don't know, actually. I don't know what we're going to do. But I'm very happy because the Kazakhstan fans were incredible outside of the hotel and outside of the tournament, so I'm very happy to play with all of them in the crowd.

I heard luchov tell BanKs you have a chance to win the competition. How do you feel about that?

Yeah, the chance is [there], and we have a few days now. We are going to watch our games, talk about a lot of things, and prepare well for the arena.
