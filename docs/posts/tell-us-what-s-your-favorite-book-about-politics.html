Tell Us: What’s Your Favorite Book About Politics?

Share your thoughts with the On Politics newsletter.

By Jess Bidgood

Jess Bidgood writes the On Politics newsletter.

Published Aug. 27, 2024Updated Aug. 28, 2024, 4:19 p.m. ET

Summer is drawing to a close. A long weekend is upon us. And I know there’s only one way you want to spend it: with a good book about politics!

I want to hear about your favorite book about politics. Think broadly. Political memoirs are fine, but I’m also interested in hearing about novels or nonfiction books about slices of political history that you love, and that you think matter today.

Let me know, using this form, and I may use your answer in an upcoming newsletter.

I’ll read every response I receive, and reach out to respondents to learn more. I will not publish any part of your response without first contacting you and hearing back from you. And I won’t use your contact information for anything besides following up with you, nor will I share it with anyone outside our newsroom.
