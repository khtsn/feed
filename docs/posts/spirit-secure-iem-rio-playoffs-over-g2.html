Spirit secure IEM Rio playoffs over G2

Spirit became the penultimate playoff team at IEM Rio after defeating a Nemanja "⁠huNter-⁠" Kovač-less G2 squad in two maps (Mirage 13-7, Dust2 13-11).

"I feel we are extremely shaky at this point. But overall form is pretty fine, we're capable of playing the game," Boris "⁠magixx⁠" Vorobiev said after the match.

The series began on Mirage, where Guy "⁠NertZ⁠" Iluz stopped Spirit's hot start with a crucial triple kill to win a force-buy at the start of the second half.

That helped G2 reduce the deficit to 7-9, but a pair of magixx clutches meant the Russian squad ran away to a healthy lead once again and eventually secured a 13-7 win.

Dust2 was a closer affair, as G2's chances were aided massively by winning both pistol rounds and Danil "⁠donk⁠" Kryshkovets not finding much impact on his home map.

magixx was able to pick up the slack from his star, however, topping the server with a 1.52 rating and leading his squad into the playoffs.
