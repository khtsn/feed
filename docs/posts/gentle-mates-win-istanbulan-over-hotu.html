Gentle Mates have had few opportunities to play LANs so far in 2026, having failed to move through the online portion of BLAST Bounty 2026 Season 1 after falling to Liquid.

Too low on the Valve Regional Standings (VRS) to secure direct invites to notable LANs, Alejandro "⁠alex⁠" Masanet's troops have been taking part in online qualifiers, ultimately securing a place at IEM Rio thanks to their successful run through the European qualifier, while missing out on a BLAST Open Rotterdam spot after losing to eventual qualifier winners Ninjas in Pyjamas in the first round.

Accordingly, Gentle Mates' succesful IstanbuLAN campaign provides the Spaniards with a VRS boon. Gentle Mates began the event at #28 in the global standings, but have now risen to #18 following their victory. Gentle Mates were not the only team to benefit from their participation in the Turkish event, as HOTU and 9INE similarly rose in the VRS as a result of their match wins and prize earnings.

Gentle Mates will be looking to go back-to-back in local LAN victories, as they are set to take part in Roman Imperium Cup V against the likes of Passion UA, Ninjas in Pyjamas, fnatic, and 100 Thieves. Gentle Mates will make their debut in the Portuguese event on February 19 against Famalicão.
