BC.Game bench krazy

BC.Game have announced the benching of Adones "⁠krazy⁠" Nobre, ending his six-month tenure in the organization.

The Portuguese rifler joined the team as part of the ex-SAW core, which included Christopher "⁠MUTiRiS⁠" Fernandes and António "⁠aragornN⁠" Barbosa, in January this year. The aforementioned duo was moved to the bench in April, with krazy now following suit two months later.
