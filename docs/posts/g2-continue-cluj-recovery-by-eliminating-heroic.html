G2 continue Cluj recovery by eliminating HEROIC

G2's recovery from an 0-2 start at PGL Cluj-Napoca continues thanks to a comfortable 2-0 win over HEROIC, eliminating their opponents from the competition with wins on Mirage (13-6) and Ancient (13-9).

After losses to Vitality and PARIVISION left G2 staring in the face of an early exit in Romania, the European side have taken advantage of some easier matchups against paiN and HEROIC to keep their campaign alive and play themselves into form.

SunPayus spares G2's blushes with 1v3 clutch

HeavyGod ends 3k with tidy clutch to give G2 series point
