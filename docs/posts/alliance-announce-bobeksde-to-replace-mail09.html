Alliance announce bobeksde to replace MaiL09

Alliance have announced Jonatan "⁠bobeksde⁠" Persson as their replacement for Liam "⁠MaiL09⁠" Tügel, after the 17-year-old starlet returned to his parent organization.

"We did everything in our power to extend [MaiL09's] loan," said CEO Jonathan "Loda" Berg. "We even put across the highest trade offer in the history of our organization, but we were unfortunately not able to come to an agreement with Metizport."

bobeksde had been plying his trade with EYEBALLERS, where he performed admirably against top opposition at BLAST Bounty. He's also had stints with 9INE, Johnny Speeds, and GODSENT.

But he has big shoes to fill after MaiL09 posted a 1.19 rating during his loan with Alliance, a good way clear of bobeksde's 1.03 so far this year.
