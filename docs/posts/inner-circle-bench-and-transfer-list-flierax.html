Inner Circle bench and transfer-list Flierax

Inner Circle have pulled the trigger on a change after a slow start to the year.

Inner Circle have announced the benching and transfer-listing of Artem "⁠Flierax⁠" Khioara, bringing to an end the 20-year-old's five-month stint on the organization's starting roster.

With Flierax's removal Inner Circle are now left with only two members of the former TNL roster that joined the organization in September of last year.

"Changes are part of every team's journey," Inner Circle's statement on X read. "We want to thank Flierax for the time, effort, and dedication he gave to the team and wish him the best in the future."

Flierax put himself on the map as part of TNL in 2025, the team cementing themselves as one of the best sides outside of the top 30 with numerous deep runs in online competitions and lower-tier LANs, all of which earned the squad a transfer to Inner Circle in September.

As part of Inner Circle, Flierax and his teammates continued to impress initially, picking up wins over the likes of 3DMAX and GamerLegion at ESL Pro League and maintaining their good online form, but performances began to wane towards the end of the year after Dmytro "⁠nifee⁠" Tediashvili was benched due to personal reasons.
