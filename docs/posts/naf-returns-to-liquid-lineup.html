NAF returns to Liquid lineup

NAF sat out IEM Krakow due to family matters

Keith "⁠NAF⁠" Markovic has returned to Liquid's starting roster, the organization has confirmed in a post showing the Canadian at its facility in Utrecht, the Netherlands.

The 28-year-old had missed the team's last appearance at IEM Krakow due to "urgent family matters," but is now set to return to the server as the team prepares for its next outing in ESL Pro League Season 23.

That will begin with two online Swiss stages, with Liquid starting in Stage 1 and their first match pitting them against Passion UA on Sunday.

Liquid will be under pressure to put up a good result in Pro League. As things stand, they are projected to miss the IEM Cologne Major by a staggering 180 points on the Valve Regional Standings.

Before the invites go out on the April 6 cut-off, the team will also attend BLAST Open Rotterdam. That will effectively mark their last chance to make up ground on the ranking, unless they choose to sign up for some of the last-minute open LANs organized all around Europe.
