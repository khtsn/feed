The story of a mother whose son is being held hostage in the Gaza Strip.

Oct. 20, 2023, 6:00 a.m. ET

Listen and follow The Daily

Warning: This episode contains strong language and descriptions of violence.

When Hamas attacked Israel, they took more than 200 hostages back with them into the Gaza Strip, including grandparents and children as young as nine months old. It was one of the largest mass abductions in recent history.

Now, the fate of those hostages is at the center of a deepening crisis in the Middle East, and a looming ground invasion of Gaza. Today, we hear from the mother of one of these hostages.

On today’s episode

  • Rachel Goldberg, the mother of Hersh Goldberg-Polin, who is being held hostage by Hamas.

Sabrina Tavernise contributed reporting.
