Spirit cruise to Stage 3 with flawless record

Spirit have capped off their dominant Stage 2 campaign with a one-sided win over 9z.

Spirit are the first team from Stage 2 to book a place in Stage 3, with the Russian side annihilating 9z in their 2-0 match to confirm their spot.

Spirit, who are coming off a title-winning campaign at PGL Astana, did not start among the likes of fellow contenders Vitality, Natus Vincere, and Falcons in Stage 3, instead starting as the highest-ranked side in Stage 2. Boris "⁠magixx⁠" Vorobiev's men hardly broke a sweat in their first three games of the Major, suffering only 10 lost rounds across the four maps they played against the likes of BetBoom, MIBR, and 9z.

Danil "⁠donk⁠" Kryshkovets was, without a doubt, the star of the show during Spirit's bout with 9z. On the first half of Spirit's pick of Mirage alone, donk racked up six multikills, 150.3 ADR, a 17-8 K/D, and +9.41% Swing, helping his team quickly accrue a 9-3 lead. Spirit quickly swept up the remaining four rounds they needed once on the CT-side, with donk claiming eight kills and just one death across those four rounds.

donk somehow managed to top his Mirage performance once the series headed to 9z's pick of Nuke. There, the Russian phenom garnered 100% KAST, +18.34% Swing, 144.9 ADR, and a 23-7 K/D, netting him a 2.76 map rating, his second-highest-rated map on LAN and the third-highest of his career.
