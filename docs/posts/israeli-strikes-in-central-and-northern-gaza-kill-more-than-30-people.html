Israeli Strikes in Central and Northern Gaza Kill More Than 30 People

Israeli airstrikes pummeled two areas in central Gaza and a town in the north of the enclave on Sunday morning, killing more than 30 people and wounding several others, according to local rescue and emergency services.

In central Gaza on Sunday morning, a strike on a home in Nuseirat killed four people, the Palestinian Civil Defense said in a statement.

Strikes in nearby Al Bureij hit three houses, according to Mahmoud Basal, a spokesman for the Civil Defense, an emergency rescue group. He said that 13 people were killed there and several others were wounded, adding that rescuers were still searching for people under the rubble.
