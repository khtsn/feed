IEM to return to Brazil in 2027

ESL's 2026 event was held in Rio de Janeiro

ESL has announced that it will host an event in Brazil in 2027 on May 3-9, with the host city yet to be announced by the tournament organizer.

The event will follow the usual IEM format and award a $1,000,000 prize pool. The tournament will feature 16 teams, 11 of which will receive their invites via the global VRS. One invite each will be awarded through the North and South American VRS, with the final three teams coming from qualifiers, two global and one Americas.

The tournament will begin with a group stage with two groups of eight teams, played in a double-elimination best-of-three format, with each group awarding four playoff spots.
