He will fill the vacant position in the roster left by Dominykas "MiniBoo" Lukaševičius, who recently departed the team.

ComeBack first gained notoriety as the Duelist for BBL PCIFIC BBL PCIFIC Europe Unranked Crewen Ali Eren Sargin Loita Utku Kart Lar0k Yusuf Kaan Kanber Rosé Eren Erzan lovers rock Umut Pekdoğan , who rose from Premier all the way to the grand finals of EMEA Ascension 2024 before being defeated by Apeks. Still not eligible to play due to his age at the beginning of the 2025 season, he continued to play with BBL PCIFIC in Challengers Türkiye for the first half of the year, where he consistently put up impressive numbers in the region and in Challengers EMEA events.

ComeBack later joined Gentle Mates as part of their Stage 2 rebuild just before he turned 18 in June. While the team managed to take maps against strong teams, they were unable to close out matches, ending the Stage winless at the bottom of the standings. As an Ascension team, they were subsequently relegated from VCT EMEA but returned as a partnership team following the removal of KOI.

This move marks the first player addition for Heretics since October 2023, changing up the Los Niños roster that had been together for two years and achieved heights including second-place finishes at Masters Shanghai and Champions 2024. While still one of EMEA's best teams in 2025, the team struggled to replicate their peak, going out in last place at Masters Toronto before resurging to end the year with a top-six finish at Champions Paris.
