NRG sign hallzerk and bring back Jeorge

oSee was moved to the bench to make room for the Norwegian sniper.

NRG have announced the signing of Håkon "⁠hallzerk⁠" Fjærli and return of Jeorge "⁠Jeorge⁠" Endicott, with the former replacing Josh "⁠oSee⁠" Ohm on the active roster and the latter filling the spot left vacant after Alexander "⁠br0⁠" Bro was benched and subsequently acquired by Eternal Fire.

Jeorge was moved to NRG's bench at the end of February to make room for Michael "⁠Grim⁠" Wince, and spent recent months playing for Voca in North America, averaging a 1.18 rating over 55 maps.

hallzerk has sat on the sidelines since February, when he was benched by Passion UA to make room for Santino "⁠try⁠" Rigal. The Norwegian sniper averaged a 1.10 rating during his stints in Passion UA and Complexity, and returning to activity with NRG reunites him with his former teammate, Grim.

He joins as a replacement for oSee, whose tenure with NRG ends after two-and-a-half years. The American sniper averaged a 1.11 rating during that stint, but had his numbers bolstered by domestic competition and struggled in his international appearances.

His average dropped to a 1.02 on LAN in 2026 and level took a turn for the worse in the last four months of the season, ending with a 0.79, 0.86, and 0.85 ratings at IEM Atlanta, CAC, and the Cologne Major, respectively.
