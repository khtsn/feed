Our Coverage of the Israel-Hamas War

  • A Looming Invasion: Israel’s new unity government agrees that Hamas must be destroyed. But as the Israeli military prepares for a major operation in Gaza, there is little appetite for a reoccupation.

  • A Growing Crisis in Gaza: The Gaza Strip, pounded for days by hundreds of Israeli airstrikes in retaliation for the Hamas attacks, is on the brink of a humanitarian catastrophe.

  • No Way Out: Israel’s leadership has repeatedly urged civilians in Gaza to flee the territory while they can. But the only viable exit is a border crossing into Egypt, which so far is keeping the door firmly shut.

  • Taking on the Attackers: Israel Ziv, a retired Israeli Army general who rushed to confront Hamas attackers as the conflict erupted, has become a public symbol of Israel’s D.I.Y. spirit — and of the failure of its military and intelligence agencies.

  • Palestinian Americans React: Palestinian Americans have long grappled with the complicated history of their ancestral home and the foreign policy of their adopted one. Here’s what some said about the events that unfolded since Hamas attacked Israel.

  • An Outpouring of Pictures: The images streaming onto our phones from Israel and Gaza, don’t just document the story — they shape it, our critic writes.
