At Least 10 Killed After Vehicle Drives Into Crowd in New Orleans

At least 10 people were killed and 30 more were injured after a vehicle drove into crowds on New Orleans’ Bourbon Street overnight, according to New Orleans city officials.

An online video stream from the popular nightlife area showed police officers gathered in a cordoned-off area of the street.

The incident occurred at the intersection of Canal and Bourbon Streets during New Year’s Eve celebrations, which regularly bring large, raucous crowds to New Orleans’ French Quarter.
