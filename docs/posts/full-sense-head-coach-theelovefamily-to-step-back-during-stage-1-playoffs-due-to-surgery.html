FULL SENSE head coach THEELOVEFAMILY to step back during Stage 1 playoffs due to surgery

FULL SENSE FULL SENSE Asia-Pacific Unranked Leviathan Thanyathon Nakmee Killua Tanate Teerasawad primmie Papaphat Sriprapha JitBoyS Jittana Nokngam Crws Thanamethk Mahatthananuyut has announced that head coach Thanaphat "THEELOVEFAMILY" Limpaphan will be unable to attend the Pacific Stage 1 playoffs due to a necessary surgical procedure.

“During this time, our coaching staff and team will step in to fulfill his responsibilities and ensure that the team continues to perform at its best,” the team said in the announcement. “Everyone at FULL SENSE would like to send our full support and best wishes to THEELUVFAMILY for a smooth recovery.”

THEELUVFAMILY joined FULL SENSE as the team's assistant coach back in September 2023. He was retained as the assistant coach when FULL SENSE entered VCT Pacific and signed the ex- TALON TALON Inactive Killua Tanate Teerasawad thyy Anupong Preamsak Crws Thanamethk Mahatthananuyut JitBoyS Jittana Nokngam primmie Papaphat Sriprapha roster in December 2025, and was later promoted to head coach when Crws returned to the starting roster ahead of Stage 1.

As the third seed from Group Omega, FULL SENSE is set to play their first match of the Stage 1 playoffs later today, May 7 against Kiwoom DRX. The winner will face T1 tomorrow for a spot at Masters London.
