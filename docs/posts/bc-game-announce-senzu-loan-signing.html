BC.Game announce Senzu loan signing

27-4-2026 17:48

Assistant coach ScrunK will stand in for the team for IEM Atlanta and CS Asia Championship.

BC.Game have announced the loan signing of Azbayar "⁠Senzu⁠" Munkhbold from The MongolZ to add a fourth member to their active roster, while Robin "⁠ScrunK⁠" Röpke will stand-in for the team for the next two events before the organization looks to sign a permanent IGL after the Major.

BC.Game recently parted ways with Christopher "⁠MUTiRiS⁠" Fernandes and António "⁠aragornN⁠" Barbosa after an extended period of lacklustre results, the last of which being a group-stage elimination at PGL Bucharest, while the team also convincingly missed out on a Cologne Major spot.

Read more

BC.Game bench aragornN and MUTiRiS

Senzu has turned out for Passion UA since February, but couldn't help the team find its footing despite averaging a team-high 1.21 rating.

His last appearance for the Ukrainian organization came at FRAG 20, where the Mongolian and his team finished third.

HLTV's No. 13 Player of 2025 will now link up with Oleksandr "⁠s1mple⁠" Kostyliev and company, with the team's next outings coming at IEM Atlanta on May 11, before they head to China for CS Asia Championships.

The organization also announced that assistant coach ScrunK will stand in as the team's IGL for the aforementioned tournaments, while the organization will look to permanently fill the role after the Major.
