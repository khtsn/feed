Gen.G sweeps Korean derby to make SOOP Valorant League grand final

Gen.G has defeated DRX 2-0 in the SOOP Valorant League semifinal. They will next play against NRG Esports in the event's grand final, marking Gen.G's seventh final in 2024. DRX ends the tournament in the top four, reaching playoffs after defeating Bilibili Gaming and Rapid Lofi in groups.

The match kicked off with a dominant Pearl from Gen.G, who took their map 13-3. Bind began in DRX's favor, as they ended the first half 8-4, but Gen.G returned to form in their attack, dropping only one round on their way to a 13-9 win and closing out the series 2-0.
