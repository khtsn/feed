PGL Cluj-Napoca teams, format, schedule, prizes, talent, fantasy

PGL returns to Cluj for the second year in a row

The year has barely ticked over to February, but the Counter-Strike circuit is already about to enter its third Big Event of the year.

Following PARIVISION's surprise BLAST Bounty win and Vitality's trophy lift in Krakow, the teams will now make the flight to Romania for what is quickly becoming PGL's flagship event in Cluj-Napoca.

With a much more stacked team list than previous PGL events, the scene is set for a mouth-watering week and a half of Counter-Strike in Romania.

The event is just one day away from the start, so here's everything you need to know about it:

Teams and format

In a huge shift from previous PGL events, just two top 16 teams have declined invites to the event, namely Spirit and Liquid, meaning we are set to receive some high-octane matchups throughout the tournament.

PGL Cluj-Napoca 2025 champions MOUZ are in attendance, as are Vitality and PARIVISION, the winners of the only Big Events of the year so far.
