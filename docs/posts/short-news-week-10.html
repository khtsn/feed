Short news: Week 10

Registration for the 20-team LAN will open on March 20.

Originally open to 64 teams, the WINAMAX Counter-Strike 2 Trophy tournament at the annual Gamers Assembly has been reduced to 32 teams, and the organizers have lowered the all-inclusive fee, featuring rented setups, to €1,600.

The tournament is taking place on April 4-5 in Poitiers, France, with Liquid the most notable name signed up amid a plethora of unranked teams.

Video link

Three key dates in April have been announced by ESL:

  • 6 April: Major invitations confirmed based on the VRS ranking release

  • 13 April: Deadline to confirm team details; name, logo and substitute

  • 19 April: Deadline to complete Tournament Item Submission.

The $30,000 Stockholm LAN will kick off on March 24.
