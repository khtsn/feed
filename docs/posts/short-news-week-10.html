Short news: Week 10

Three key dates in April have been announced by ESL:

  • 6 April: Major invitations confirmed based on the VRS ranking release

  • 13 April: Deadline to confirm team details; name, logo and substitute

  • 19 April: Deadline to complete Tournament Item Submission.

The $30,000 Stockholm LAN will kick off on March 24.

Thomas Hamence, CEO of paiN, has resigned from his post after a Sheep Esports report exposed the extent of the organization's mishandling of a sexual assault claim against one of their League of Legends players.
