hypex: "I'm excited to see what Vitality are like [on stage]"

GamerLegion kicked off their BLAST Rivals 2026 Season 1 in impressive fashion, scoring an upset win over FURIA that immediately booked them a place in the playoffs in Fort Worth, Texas.

While GamerLegion did not fare quite as well in their next group stage map against Natus Vincere, the European side found success in their first arena match, taking down Astralis 2-0 off the back of an impressive 4-12 comeback on Inferno.

GamerLegion move to Rivals semis over Astralis

Shortly after his team's comeback victory against Astralis, GamerLegion AWPer Milan "⁠hypex⁠" Polowiec shared his thoughts on his first notable outing in front of a crowd, the team's Inferno comeback, and the prospect of facing the titans of Vitality in the semi-finals.

Or you can listen to the interview here.

First arena game for you, what a way to get it going. What were your thoughts getting on stage in front of the crowd?

Yeah, I'm not going to lie, it was pretty awesome. I think I know why people say playing in front of a crowd is so much better. The adrenaline, the noise you can hear them making in the back, it's amazing. I'm glad the team carried through today, gave them the vibes. It was amazing, what a comeback.

Talk about coming through, that second half on Inferno was something else. Tell me how you put that together and how you were able to get such a big comeback and keep the momentum going into overtime.

Even the score, when it was so far apart, I think it was 12-4, I think we felt that the game could go either way at that point, so we just carried on with our plan, taking it round after round. I mean, it was working. I felt like they had no chance at some point. Yeah, it just worked. Then we went to T-side in overtime, we knew we were better and should have won more rounds on T, so yeah, we managed to do it.

We're going to do our best to [beat Vitality] tomorrow, and it's going to be pretty funny if it actually happens

You did an interview with Dust2.us, and you had some fighting words about going after Vitality. That's a possibility now, so how are you feeling about it?

It was just a joke at the time, people took it out of context, and people got ragebaited by it. We're going to do our best to do it tomorrow, and it's going to be pretty funny if it actually happens.

What do you think about getting that experience to play against one of the best teams in the history of Counter-Strike in an arena?

It's going to be very exciting. They play on these stages every two weeks, I would say. It's going to be exciting to see what they're like on it. Hopefully, we can pull through.

About this Astralis match, when you were down on Inferno, was there anything you said or did to rally before heading into the second half?

I mean, what we were just saying is that genuinely, it could have been way closer. We lost some rounds, if we won two in a round on T side they would have had an eco finally, but we just couldn't pull through and win two in a row, so I think that was the problem. And we started off a bit badly; they won three rounds in a row at the start of their T, and as I said, we just took it round after round after that and it worked.

Lots of excitement for tomorrow, are you going to be able to sleep tonight?

Hopefully, maybe in like four hours (laughs).
