HOTU eliminate hometown squad Legacy

n0rb3r7 led by example to guide his team onward in Rio

HOTU have sent Legacy packing from IEM Rio with a 2-0 series victory, taking the Brazilians down on Dust2 (13-7) and Ancient (16-4).

With their victory HOTU continue their lower bracket journey, but are faced with a difficult matchup with Natus Vincere in the next round.

Legacy took control early on Dust2, recovering from a force-buy loss once they got their economy in order, but slipped to a tied half in the face of a late T-side resurgence by HOTU. The second half was one-way traffic in HOTU's favor, Legacy unable to get anything going as they fell to a defeat.

HOTU bagged the lead on Ancient but struggled on their CT side once Legacy got guns up, finding themselves outclassed in early rounds and unable to push through retakes, granting the Brazilians a lead at the half.

The second half devolved into a chaotic and scrappy affair, both teams making mistakes and questionable moves as the pressure ratcheted up. The game continued into OT but it was a late multi-kill by Dmitry "⁠mizu⁠" Kondratev that sealed the deal for HOTU, sending Legacy crashing out of the tournament.
