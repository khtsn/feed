The organization now looks to develop tAk further in the upper echelons of Counter-Strike after he briefly got a taste of tier-one competition at IEM Rio, replacing Nemanja "⁠huNter-⁠" Kovač at short notice after the Bosnian captain was sidelined by an injury.

"tAk is a vocal, versatile, hungry player who is ready to take more responsibility. With him graduating from university, he is also in a position to fully focus on the next step in his career," G2 manager Petar "⁠peca⁠" Marković has told HLTV.
