MOUZ debut with narrow win over Gentle Mates

The new MOUZ have debuted their new additions of Justinas "⁠jL⁠" Lekavicius and Adrian "⁠xelex⁠" Vincze with a narrow victory over Gentle Mates.

It was far from a convincing affair, as, fresh off the two additions and Dorian "⁠xertioN⁠" Berman's move to in-game leadership, MOUZ struggled on the T sides.

It took overtime for them to convert the opening Nuke despite 9-4 and 12-10 leads, and their Mirage offense was marred with missed smokes and shoddy executions.

But the favorites finally put things together for Ancient, where they got off to an 8-1 start on the CT side and ran to a quick closing in the second half off of a second-round forcebuy win.

xelex was understandably up and down, but certainly showed up for Ancient, where he nearly pulled off a 1v5 in the second pistol round and found opening after opening in MOUZ's solid T side closing on the decider.

The other debutant, jL, was put in some hard anchor roles he hadn't been used to from NAVI, but still made a solid account of himself on the CT sides of each map.

The Lithuanian's Deagle was powerful as ever, even though it largely came in a losing effort on Mirage, where he's been parked into the difficult B anchor role.
