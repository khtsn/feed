Falcons Vega breezes past ALG GC with 2-0 win

The EMEA third seed, Falcons Vega, has defeated Ambitious Legend Gaming GC 2-0 in their opening Game Changers 2024 Campionship match.

The match began on Haven, which Falcons took 13-6 thanks in part to a dominant first half. Falcons extended their momentum on Split, with a flawless defense setting them up for a 13- win to close out the series.

Falcons will next play against Shopify Rebellion, the 2023 Game Changers Champions, while ALG will look to continue their run in the lower bracket.
