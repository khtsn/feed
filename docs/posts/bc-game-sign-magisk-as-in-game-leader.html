BC.Game sign Magisk as in-game leader

The Dane will take over as the team's captain.

BC.Game has announced the signing of Emil "⁠Magisk⁠" Reif as the team's new in-game leader.

Magisk, who was a three-time Major champion with Astralis in 2018-19, had been without a team since the end of 2025, when he left the aforementioned Danish outfit after a brief second stint in the organization.

Magisk went on to win another Major with Vitality in 2023 and was named Anchor of the Year before moving to Falcons in 2024 until September 2025.

Now, the Dane will set his sights on leading BC.Game, joining two players he knows well after facing them over the years during their storied careers, Denis "⁠electroNic⁠" Sharipov and Oleksandr "⁠s1mple⁠" Kostyliev.

This addition, which solves the issue of the team's captaincy — the Dane has previous, albeit limited, experience as a leader from a stint as Astralis' IGL in 2020 —, now puts BC.Game just one more signing away from having a starting five as the organization secured Azbayar "⁠Senzu⁠" Munkhbold on a permanent basis after loaning him from The MongolZ.
