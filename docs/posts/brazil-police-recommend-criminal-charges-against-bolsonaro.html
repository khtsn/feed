The federal police accused the former president of falsifying his Covid-19 vaccination records.

March 19, 2024, 9:50 a.m. ET

Brazil’s federal police recommended that former President Jair Bolsonaro be criminally charged in connection with a scheme to falsify his Covid-19 vaccine card, in part to travel to the United States during the pandemic.

If federal prosecutors decide to pursue the charges, it would be the first time the former president has faced criminal charges.

This is a developing story.
