Astralis send dismal FaZe home from Cluj

Astralis have eliminated FaZe from PGL Cluj-Napoca after breezing past them in a dominant two-map series.

The series started on Ancient, where Astralis ran rampant and eventually secured a dominant 13-5 win. The scoreline could have gotten even more out of hand for the FaZe side if not for two Helvijs "⁠broky⁠" Saukants 1vs3 clutches and a crisp Russel "⁠Twistzz⁠" Van Dulken ace, which allowed them to avoid a complete demolition.

While FaZe showed a better face on Mirage, they only started finding their form at 4-10 down. That was too big of a deficit for the experienced side to overcome, and Astralis did enough to close out a 13-9 victory and end their seven-match loss streak on Mirage.

2W 6L for FaZe in 2026

After the highs of the Budapest Major, FaZe have not been at the races for the early tournaments of 2026.

They haven't gotten within touching distance of a playoff appearance at any of their three tournaments this year, and have only managed to rack up two victories in eight matches (against BC.Game and HEROIC).

The alarm bells are well and truly blaring.
