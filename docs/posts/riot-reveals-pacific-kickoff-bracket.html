Riot reveals Pacific Kickoff bracket

Riot announced matchups for the 2025 Pacific Kickoff, which features a 12-team, double-elimination bracket.

Like Americas and EMEA, the draw was divided into two pools, with the first pool including teams from the region that made Champions 2024. Those teams received a bye from the first round, qualifying for the upper bracket's quarterfinals. The second pool has the region's remaining eight teams, which were drawn against each other with the winners facing teams from the first pool.

The 2025 Pacific Kickoff bracket. (Photo by Riot Games)
