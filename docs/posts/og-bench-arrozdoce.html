OG bench arrozdoce

Rafael "⁠arrozdoce⁠" Wing has been benched from OG to bring an end to the Portuguese player's nine-month tenure with the team, the organization has announced.

The move comes just two days before the team is slated to participate in BC.Game Masters Championship Season 2, which will run from May 21-24 in Villa Nova de Gaia, Portugal.

There, OG will play in a group that has been depleted just to four teams compared to the other group's six following Passion UA's withdrawal, featuring Betclic, Metizport and Rebels.
