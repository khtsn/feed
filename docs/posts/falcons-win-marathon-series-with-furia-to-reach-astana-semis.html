Falcons win marathon series with FURIA to reach Astana semis

The international side is one win away from reaching the final on its debut event with karrigan.

Falcons are through to the semi-finals at PGL Astana 2026 following a hard-fought 2-1 victory over FURIA in a high-octane series that is certainly a match-of-the-year contender.

Falcons had to come back from a map down after losing their pick of Mirage, where Danil "⁠molodoy⁠" Golubenko delivered a phenomenal performance to the delight of the Barys Arena crowd. After a quiet first game, Ilya "⁠m0NESY⁠" Osipov stepped it up on Nuke and powered his team to victory in a mouth-watering triple-overtime affair.

Dust2 turned out to be just as chaotic as its predecessor, with Maksim "⁠kyousuke⁠" Lukin and René "⁠TeSeS⁠" Madsen spearheading the effort as Falcons won the map in double overtime.

It was the sort of match that always frustratingly slipped through the fingers of Falcons in the playoffs, ultimately pushing the Saudi organization to land Finn "⁠karrigan⁠" Andersen.

"We just tried to play until the end," m0NESY said after the match. "It was a very hard game, I think one of the hardest games I've ever played in CS, in terms of overtimes.

"We played so many rounds. We just didn't give up on Nuke. It was so important to keep a good mentality. It was nice to play."

The manner in which Falcons won their first playoff series with karrigan is without a doubt a major boost for the team, who will be hoping to ride this wave of confidence into the semifinal bout with magic — a match they will be strongly favored to win.

 Match

kyousuke opened up Mirage with a pistol 4K, powering Falcons to a bright start to the game. Their lead wouldn't last, however, as FURIA responded thanks to back-to-back high-impact rounds from Yuri "⁠yuurih⁠" Santos. The Brazilian anchor and molodoy were the heroes on the CT side, combining for 26 frags as FURIA ended the first half up 8-4.

The second half started in the same vein, with a stunning pistol 4K by kyousuke, before FURIA took control of proceedings in gun rounds. Falcons still found a lifeline when Nikola "⁠NiKo⁠" Kovač got a 3K with a hero AK, but FURIA refused to let up and closed out the game in a 5v5 post-plant.

The momentum stayed with FURIA as they jumped out to a 6-2 lead on Nuke. Things were starting to look ominous for Falcons, but m0NESY stepped up to the plate with the AWP, ending the half with 15 frags and a 116 ADR.

The game became a battle of attrition for both teams in the second half as neither side truly managed to stamp their authority on proceedings. Falcons eventually reached map point at 12-10 but couldn't deal the finishing blow as FURIA forced overtime.

Individual heroics and big clutches became the norm in overtime as nerves began to show. Falcons survived series point on three occasions — one of which with a miraculous 2v4 retake — before securing the win in triple OT.

Falcons recovered from a 0-3 start on Dust2 and enjoyed a spell of dominance with some great rounds from TeSeS. FURIA refused to throw in the towel, bringing the deficit down to just two rounds before taking control of proceedings on the T side.

With the score tied 11-11, FURIA lost the round to time after a rare slip-up, only to hit back immediately to take the game to overtime. Their efforts would be for naught in the end, however, as Falcons locked down the map in double OT after surviving series point in the first set of extra rounds.
