Metizport bench Jackinho

Metizport have moved Jack "⁠Jackinho⁠" Ström Mattsson to the bench "following a mutual agreement" amidst "a longer period of inconsistent results and challenges."

The 27-year-old in-game leader lasted eight months in his second stint with the Swedish organization, which he joined alongside Liam "⁠MaiL09⁠" Tügel in July 2025.

He led the team to runner-up finishes at Svenska Cupen, Esplay De_Airport Masters, and BC.Game Masters Season 1, but early exits in online tournaments and other lackluster results meant the team never stabilized and broke through the top 60 on the VRS.

"I didn't feel happy or comfortable in the roles that I had in this lineup and I felt that another type of player would fit this lineup better," Jackinho said in a post on X.

“Given our current situation and timeline, we believe this is the right moment to begin building towards the next season," Morten Vollan Christensen, Metizport's Sports Director, said in the organization's announcement.

"Making this decision now allows us more time to develop the team and improve both structurally and tactically. This has been a mutual and respectful process throughout.”

The organization added that it is already working to finalize a replacement ahead of its appearance at ROG JOURNEY Spring from March 23-28, where the team will face UNiTY in its opening match.
