ZmjjKK reveals major shortcomings against G2 in press conference

Note: This article is a text summary of the EDward Gaming vs. G2 Esports post-match press conference. The full video press conference can be found here.

Could you sum up how the team could improve itself?

Muggle: "I think we should remember how to keep our mentality stable when we get late in rounds. We should also try to remember how to get wins when we get advantages in rounds. Additionally, when opponents change their pace, we shouldn't let them destroy our pace."

What changed from the team in your point of view from the end of map one in comparison to maps two and three and how did it cause the loss to happen?

ZmjjKK: "When we take a lead, we kind of play in a hurry to win. Everyone thought that we should hurry up. We were too hurried and in a rush to win. That's what caused our performance and actions in the server, and this includes myself too. We should be focused on the fact that when we take a lead, we need to focus on how to keep our mentality stable and not be in a rush."

Despite the loss, S1Mon had a very good performance for a rookie on the international stage. How comfortable do you feel in such a big event, and how happy are you about your individual performance today?

S1Mon: "I was ok with my performance today, but it still was a pity that we lost today."

What could you say was the most surprising thing about G2 that caught you off guard?

Muggle: "In today's matchup, I think our opponents had so much unexpected one versus ones or one versus twos that gave them wins which we didn't expect."
