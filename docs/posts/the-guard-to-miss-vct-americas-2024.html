Had The Guard met the Team Participation Agreement deadline, the organization and roster would have stay in the VCT Americas for the 2024 and 2025 seasons. Now that this year's Ascension Americas tournament has been virtually scrapped, Riot has confirmed the next season of the Americas League will only field 10 teams.
