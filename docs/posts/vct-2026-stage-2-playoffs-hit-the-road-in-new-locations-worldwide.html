VCT 2026 Stage 2 Playoffs hit the road in new locations worldwide

Riot has announced that the Valorant Champions Tour's four regions will hold their Stage 2 playoffs in new locations this upcoming season.

VCT China's multi-city, tour-like schedule was previously shared to hold the Stage 2 playoffs in Chengdu, while the group stage would be held in Changsha. The team has qualified for the playoffs of a stage just once in the franchising era, and has yet to do so in a Stage 2.

VCT EMEA will step outside of Berlin for the first time with the playoffs set to take place in Madrid. This marks the first time VCT returns to Madrid and Spain since the 2024 season, when it hosted the first Masters event of that year. In the Stage 2 playoffs, the team will be looking to qualify for their third-straight Champions event, as well as look to capture their first regional title after three runner-up finishes in the past two seasons.

VCT Americas will head to South America for the final weeks of its regional play when it heads to São Paulo. This event will mark the city's first VCT event since 2023 when it hosted LOCK//IN, the maiden event of VCT's partnership era.
