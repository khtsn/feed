xertioN keeps up superb form as MOUZ secure Astana semi-finals

xertioN has topped the board in every match so far in Astana

MOUZ have secured a spot in the PGL Astana semi-finals after beating Aurora 2-0 for the second time in four days off the back of a comeback on Dust2 and a convincing Nuke.

Dorian "⁠xertioN⁠" Berman, who had topped the board for MOUZ in every group stage match, did so once again as he led by example on both maps in his new role as the in-game leader.

T sides were no issue this time around as it was on the offense that MOUZ pulled off a superb turnaround from a 5-11 deficit on Dust2 after Aurora made all the right calls in the first half to catch the defenses on low numbers time and time again.

And it was xertioN who found several crucial openings on bombsite entries as MOUZ made the comeback happen, ending the map at nearly 9% Swing and 1.82 rating after a commendable effort to keep the map alive on CT side.

The Israeli went on to finish on top of the board for the second time after MOUZ put together an overall solid Nuke, where a seven-round T side and a successful A defense in the following pistol helped the team rack up the cushion needed to close the map on CT without too much drama.

After the 1.78-rated performance, xertioN now sits third on the tournament charts at a 1.40 rating over 13 maps, between the likes of Danil "⁠donk⁠" Kryshkovets, Danil "⁠molodoy⁠" Golubenko and Ilya "⁠m0NESY⁠" Osipov.

Moving on to the semi-finals, MOUZ will go up against the winner of G2 vs. Spirit on Saturday at 12:00 .
