G2 Gozen return to upper bracket final with 2-1 over MIBR GC

G2 Gozen have defeated MIBR GC 2-1, and are now in the Game Changers 2024 Championship upper bracket final. This is the third year in a row the organization has done so, the only team in the event's history to accomplish such a feat.

The match began with MIBR winning Bind, their map pick, 13-8. G2 took their own map, Split, 13-8, sending the series to a decider map. In the end, Gozen dominated Sunset with a 13-4, closing out the match 2-1.

MIBR remain in contention, and they will next play against ZETA Division GC in the lower bracket.
