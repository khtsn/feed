Gen.G defeat Sentinels to secure spot at grand finals

Gen.G Gen.G Korea Rank #1 Munchkin Byeon Sang-beom (변상범) t3xture Kim Na-ra (김나라) Meteor Kim Tae-O (김태오) Lakia Kim Jong-min (김종민) Karon Kim Won-tae (김원태) have advanced to the grand finals, defeating Sentinels Sentinels North America Rank #3 TenZ Tyson Ngo Zellsis Jordan Montemurro zekken Zachary Patrone johnqt Mohamed Amine Ouarid Sacy Gustavo Rossi 2-1 in the upper bracket final of the Valorant Champions Tour 2024 Masters Madrid tournament. Gen.G will move on, competing in the first global finals of the 2024 season. Sentinels were sent to the lower bracket, one loss away from elimination.

The map picks started with Sentinels picking Split and Gen.G picking Lotus, leaving Breeze as the decider.

Gen.G surprised all fans and analysts with a dominant 13-5 victory on Split, but it was followed up by a stalwart Lotus defense from Sentinels to win the map 13-7. Breeze decided who made it to the grand finals, and Gen.G stomped Sentinels 13-3.

Both t3xture and Meteor expectedly fragged well, but it was Lakia who truly stepped up on Breeze to send a Pacific team to the grand finals.
