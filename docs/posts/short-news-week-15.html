FACEIT has announced will make a return to matchmaking when Season 8 kicks off on April 22.

"Matches on Cache will count toward your Elo," the company posted on X. "Players can deselect Cache without needing to subscribe or change your other map bans."

Digital Crusade, the company behind DraculaN, has announced additional information for the upcoming DraculaN Season 7, taking place on June 23-28 in Bucharest with $150,000 on the line, split down the middle between prize pool and club share.

This time around, all teams will start from the group stage, with 14 coming from VRS invites (starting from 13th on the VRS) and two wildcard invites. The May 4 update will be used for both invites and seeding purposes.

Two eight-team double-elimination groups will give way to a six-team single-elimination playoff, with the high seeds starting in the semi-finals.

ESL has announced that the tickets for the upcoming ESL Pro League have gone on sale. The $1,000,000 tournament will take place on October 3-11, with the playoff taking place in the final three days, on October 9-11, which fans will be able to attend at the Spodek Arena.
