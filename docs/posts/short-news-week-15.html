Short: gAuLeS says g3x could return to CS

On his stream, Alexandre "⁠gAuLeS⁠" Chiqueta has announced that g3x could return to Counter-Strike.

g3x's members — known as sócios — will be able to vote until May 23 on whether they want to see the organization reopen its esports division.

g3x currently field a team in the Brazilian division of Kings League, the highly popular 7-a-side football competition.

"Today, on April 13, 2026, we announce the most important vote in our history as a community," gAuLeS said.

"It is live — the decision is in your hands, whether we should return with the esports division and the CS team. You can vote against or you can vote in favor. Today we have 874 council members, and all of them will be able to vote. The vote runs until May 23."
