Sources: Passion UA open to offers for team

Passion UA are prepared to listen to offers from organizations looking to sign their players, sources with knowledge of the matter have told HLTV.

Earlier this week, the Ukrainian organization announced that the team would be withdrawing from BC Game Masters Championship Season 2, bringing the squad's season to a premature end.
