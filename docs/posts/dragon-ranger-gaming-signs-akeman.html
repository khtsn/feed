While the China Evolution Series Act 1 saw teams who qualified for Masters Bangkok field reserve or academy teams, Act 2 serves as one of the region's qualifiers for the Esports World Cup, while also allowing teams to accrue circuit points building up to the CN EVO tournament at the end of the year.

DRG has a seven-man roster for the event, and while it is not clear if Akeman will start, their official team roster reveal suggests that he will take Cangshu 's spot in the starting five. The team has not confirmed if the signing will apply for future events.
