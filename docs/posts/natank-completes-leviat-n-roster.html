After a disappointing 2023, Leviatán made significant strides in 2024 following a complete roster overhaul. Although their season started poorly, culminating in an early exit at Masters Shanghai, the team rebounded in Stage 2, finishing with an impressive 9-1 record and placing third at Champions Seoul. Despite their growing success, the roster fragmented after the season, with Mazino leaving after his contract expired and aspas' future remaining unclear.

The nataNk joined Gentle Mates in early 2023, competing in the Challengers League: France. Despite these challenges, nataNk's individual performance shone through, finishing Stage 2 ranked seventh in ACS and among the top 15 in rating.

Following Gentle Mates' elimination, the organization released its roster, leaving nataNk a free agent before his signing with the South American organization.
