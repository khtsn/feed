By Striker

FalleN: "Happy for NiKo"

FalleN to HLTV on what went wrong: "I think there were crucial moments on every single map we played where it could have shifted our way. Mirage, we lost a forcebuy after winning a forcebuy, and that was the moment where we could tie up the match and start really feeling the game. It didn't go our way.

"There was an Inferno moment where karrigan got three of us on B and he brings the magic for a crucial round as well. So it was a difficult game. They played better, they played good. Happy for NiKo for finally winning his Major, and of course it sucks when it's against us in the finals. It is what it is. We tried our best we could and I'm proud of that."

Did the pressure get to FURIA? "Eh, I wouldn't say. Maybe there was some hesitation in some rounds to really go for space, or where guns were dropped and stuff like that. But it's more about being too careful. It's part of the game. It's an important match and people are gonna be careful, so I wouldn't say that there was something crucial."

On not getting his third Cologne before retirement: "It sucks. We had really high hopes for this tournament after we were getting better and better in the last weeks and months. It felt like we could win here. But sometimes you gotta give it to the opponent. They were better than us and their run was even more impressive, they beat a lot of top teams to win the tournament, so gotta give it to them."

MAJ3R: "We had a chance to write history as five Turkish [players], but we didn't have enough strength today"

vs.

Engin "⁠MAJ3R⁠" Küpeli, speaking to HLTV after Aurora's elimination from the Cologne Major:

On his immediate thoughts on feelings: "I'm very heartbroken because we didn't show anything today. This was the semi-final of the Major, and I had a big hope to really show a better face. Overall, I think we lost to our demons today, and the demons [beats] the angels, you know? It was not the team that I had all these matches in this match, and I'm just sad and heartbroken."

On if he knows why they didn't show up the way he expected: "No, I don't know, to be honest. We lacked a lot of things today, and when you play on the stage, in this tournament, so far, in such an important match, if we play like this, we deserve it. We didn't show good Counter-Strike at all, and that's all."

On how they move forward: "For now, I really don't know. I'm just thinking that we lost, and I'm just sad about now because we really had an opportunity with the bracket et cetera. We had a chance to write history as five Turkish [players], but we didn't have enough strength today, to be honest. I want to congratulate FURIA for how they played; they played way better. That's all."

On if there will still be a chance for a Turkish roster to make that history in the future: "Hopefully, I would love. But we will see now, I don't know. I'm just focusing on the present now we've lost, and we're going to talk about the future and we will see how the plans go."

yuurih: "Last year we had a good result because of the clutches; now we're winning them again, so let's see"

vs.

After FURIA's victory over 9z in the LANXESS Arena, HLTV spoke to Yuri "⁠yuurih⁠" Santos to get his thoughts on the game, getting back in form, and winning the kind of clutches that helped the team's trophy streak in 2025.

On winning four clutches on Mirage: "Actually, it was hard, the pistol. I just went crazy, and I think I expected a guy going stairs in the 2v1, so I knew he was close. When I killed him, I knew I would win the round. But afterward, I played bad, we lost the force-buy as well. My bad.

"But Mirage, my team played really good, they helped me a lot as well, everyone played insane. About the clutches, of course, last year we had a good result because of the clutches. We were winning everything, even against Vitality. Now, we have been winning clutches again, so let's see.

On his form: "Since the start of the year, I have been struggling on T side. For example, last year, I was the best player in the team on T side, and this year I have been struggling, so it's been so hard for me. There are some tournaments that, as a team, we play good, and with that I play good T side as well. About CT side, I have been playing good, but it's more about T side since the start of the year, and it has been hard. "

On easier side of the bracket and Aurora game: "Yeah, it's easier, the bracket. For sure. The top bracket is the best teams in the world, but even with this bracket, there are really good teams. After tomorrow, we're going to play Aurora, and they're a really good team. They are showing good CS here at the Major, so of course it will be really tough. But we know that we can win and go to the finals."

HUASOPEEK: "It's sad, but very happy to have played in the playoffs of a Major"

HUASOPEEK on the loss: "It's sad, but very happy to have played in the playoffs of a Major, also in Cologne. It's the best tournament in all of CS. I'm very sad because I think we could have won Mirage. We started with a good CT side, but we lost four rounds in a row."
