On the ground at IEM Cologne Major playoffs

Boombl4 on the loss: "They're just better today. I think we are a really young team, and to be here in the playoffs is a really big result for us. I can't be happy about how we played, but it's still a great experience and I think it's okay."

On his 0-15 start on Anubis: "(laughs) I was smiling every time because two times my teammates killed me and I was dropping AKs, so I'm really fine with it. It was a great moment, hype after my first frag, so I don't tilt about it."

On future: "I think we need to chill a little bit because in two weeks we have a tournament in China with the full lineup, I hope (laughs). I don't know what more to say. It's a great result to be at a tier-one tournament. We have a really high ranking right now. The next step is to have stability at the tier-one and to show our results, and go to every playoffs we can."
