Gen.G's mid-year facelift has come with good reason. Since enjoying one of the single-greatest Valorant seasons in 2024, it has taken a dip in form, reaching just one international event in that time. The rebuild has seen Gen.G part ways with Lakia , who was the go-to Initiator player for the team in 2024 and returned this year to take on IGL duties, and shut down ZynX for the rest of the year to address a severe wrist injury.

While Efina, born in 2001, joins as a well-traveled veteran, Raxcal is a different addition. Born in 2008, he turned 18 just last month, making him the youngest player in the world when he takes to the stage later this month at Pacific Stage 2.
