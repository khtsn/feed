Team Vitality Team Vitality Europe Rank #45 Destrian Tomas Linikas Kicks Kimmie Laasner runneR Emil Trajkovski ceNder Jokūbas Labutis have inched closer to a complete roster with the signing of Kimmie "Kicks" Laasner. The 18-year-old will be a part of the revamped Vitality team alongside runneR .

Before 2023, Kicks had played for the likes of Human Tripwires Human Tripwires Inactive Bouncy Joia Helsper r3duqwe1e jimbk Jim Boerkamp , London Esports London Esports Inactive Radski Conrad Micheal Raduchowski Dev0 Corey Brame RobbieBk Robbie Boerkamp Kicks Kimmie Laasner , and Finest Finest Inactive DESTRUCT1VEE Hakan Lekesizer Kicks Kimmie Laasner bonkar Malkolm Rench . In 2023, he participated in Challengers: Polaris as a part of bonk bonk Inactive juseu Elric Belland-Aysan . The team found a fifth-place finish in Split 1, narrowly missing out on playoffs.

He then joined Laterious Laterious Europe Rank #123 jkR in March 2023, and played with them in Challengers: DACH Evolution. Unfortunately, in Split 2, Laterious finished fifth and missed out on playoffs again. He left the organization shortly after in May 2023, and remained a free agent until now. Kicks has mostly filled the initiator-flex role on the different teams he's played for.
