A presidential candidate, Jorge Álvarez Máynez, said people were injured when a stage collapsed in a northern city after a gust of wind blew through the area.

May 23, 2024, 12:00 a.m. ET

A stage in Mexico where a presidential candidate was campaigning collapsed after a gust of wind blew through the area on Wednesday night, leaving people injured, the candidate said.

The candidate, Jorge Álvarez Máynez, said on social media that the stage had collapsed in the city of San Pedro Garza García, in northern Mexico, and that there were multiple victims.

“I am fine and in communication with state authorities to follow up on what happened,” he said.

No other details were immediately available.
