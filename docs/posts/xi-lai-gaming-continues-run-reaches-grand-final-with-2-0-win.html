Xi Lai Gaming continues run, reaches grand final with 2-0 win

With a 2-0 victory against Wolves Esports, Xi Lai Gaming has reached the VCT China Stage 1 grand final. Xi Lai Gaming continues an undefeated streak in the stage, not losing a series since the opening group stage match.

The match began on XLG's map pick, Pearl, with Xi Lai dominating the defense to win the map 13-7. Lotus continued, with Xi Lai forcing the map to overtime despite Wolves remaining in the lead throughout the series. XLG eventually won map two 14-12, taking the series 2-0.

Wolves is now in the lower final, next playing against China's final Toronto representative, either Bilibili Gaming or EDward Gaming.
