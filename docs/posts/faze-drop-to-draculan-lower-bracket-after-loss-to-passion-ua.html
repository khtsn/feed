FaZe drop to DraculaN lower bracket after loss to Passion UA

FaZe have been sent to the lower bracket of DraculaN Season 6 after losing to Passion UA in a three-map series (Anubis 10-13, Nuke 13-10, Dust2 14-16).

The series was an incredibly scrappy affair all throughout, one that was eventually decided by Azbayar "⁠Senzu⁠" Munkhbold's superstar performance on the Dust2 decider.

The Mongolian racked up 37 kills, a 1.92 rating, and almost found a contender for highlight of the year with his 1vs4 attempt featuring two jumping no-scopes.

FaZe now drop to the lower bracket to face fnatic, while Passion UA continue their upper-bracket campaign with a game against Monte later today.

The result is hugely impactful in both squads' Cologne Major aspirations. The loss plunges FaZe further away from qualification, as Finn "⁠karrigan⁠" Andersen's troops lost five VRS spots and are now predicted to miss the Major by six places following the defeat.

Conversely, Passion UA's chances have received an increase following the win, as the 41 points they accrued from the victory puts them in touching distance of an invite.
