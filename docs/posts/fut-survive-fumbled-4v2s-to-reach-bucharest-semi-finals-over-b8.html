FUT survive fumbled 4v2s to reach Bucharest semi-finals over B8

FUT have made their second consecutive semi-final after taking B8 down 2-0 at PGL Bucharest.

The András "⁠coolio⁠" Fercsák-led side made fourth at Pro League, and will go into Friday's semi-final against one of PARIVISION or The MongolZ as the underdog by ranking but fancying their chances.

Having gone 3-0 in the group stage, with a win over PARIVISION already, FUT might have been expected an easier time against B8, especially after winning both pistols on Ancient.

But their confidence was dented, with Laurențiu "⁠lauNX⁠" Țârlea acknowledging post-game that the young team still struggles with the mental game.

"After we lost to a force, we got in our head again, overthinking what they are going to do," lauNX said about Ancient to Pala "⁠Pala⁠" Gilroy Sen post-game.

The game went all 24 rounds, and all of Aulon "⁠Krabeni⁠" Fazlija's fragging in-game leader abilities were required to drag FUT over the line 13-11.

On their pick of Overpass, where B8 are 0-5 since October, FUT just needed to confirm their win and looked set to do just that with a 9-4 lead on the stronger CT side.

But, as Henry "⁠HenryG⁠" Greer said on commentary, FUT are "not known" for their closing abilities, and their struggles against pressure were a factor again here.

9-4 became 9-9 in a flash, with a 4v2 fumble in the middle of that run. Then at 10-9, when FUT seemed to be stabilizing, another 4v2 went begging with drastic economic ramifications.

lauNX again pointed to psychological issues: "Again, it's mentality. We lost some bad rounds, advantage rounds, impossible rounds. That was the biggest flaw of us today, the mental game. When we can play our game and be normal, we're good."

Down 10-12, FUT finally switched back on and recovered their form to bring it to overtime. Then, fuelled by a lauNX triple, FUT did enough to close the map 16-13 and advance out of the quarter-finals.

"It feels good but we can't be too excited about it," lauNX added post-game. "We talked about it, we expected this result, we want to take it one by one and see where we end up. After we beat PARIVISION it's pretty clear what I want from this tournament."
