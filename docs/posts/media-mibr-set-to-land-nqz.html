Media: MIBR set to land nqz

The Brazilian AWPer will continue his career in a new team after a spell on the sidelines.

MIBR are in advanced discussions with paiN over the permanent signing of Lucas "⁠nqz⁠" Soares, according to a report from Dust2 Brasil.

The 21-year-old will fill the void created by the departure of Klimentii "⁠kl1m⁠" Krivosheev, who is returning to G2 after the end of his loan spell. Earlier this month, Dust2 Brasil reported that nqz and Santino "⁠try⁠" Rigal were MIBR's main alternatives to kl1m, who averaged a 1.23 rating while playing with the Brazilian team.

nqz's signing was reportedly requested by star player Felipe "⁠insani⁠" Yuji, who recently decided against moving to Legacy in the summer. He has been offered a contract extension by MIBR, whom he joined in 2021 as an academy player before his promotion to the main roster in 2023.
