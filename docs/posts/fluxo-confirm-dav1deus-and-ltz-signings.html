Fluxo confirm dav1deuS and Ltz signings

25-5-2026 19:05

The duo brings Fluxo's roster back up to full strength.

Fluxo have announced the signings of David "⁠dav1deuS⁠" Tapia Maldonado and João "⁠Ltz⁠" Bonetti, confirming an earlier report by Dust2 Brasil.

The announcement comes two weeks after the Brazilian organization parted ways with Matheus "⁠mlhzin⁠" Marçola and Kayke "⁠kye⁠" Bertolucci following a disappointing campaign, which meant Fluxo missed out on a Major spot.

Fluxo remove duo amid dav1deuS links

dav1deuS joins the team following a short period of inactivity after RED Canids benched their entire roster in April, while Ltz comes in from UNO MILLE.

The young rifler caught the eye by averaging a 1.22 rating across 2026 in the squad, helping them make deep runs in a plethora of domestic online competitions.

The new-look Fluxo will debut at BetBoom Circuit X Base Recife 2026, where they are set to face off against BlackBelt in their opener.
