Trace remained perfect on pistols against Leviatán to tie the series at 7-7, but aspas on Neon ran through Trace to win the map 13-9.

Ascent had Leviatán go with the Deadlock and Cypher double Sentinel, while Trace went with the default Ascent comp with Killjoy.
