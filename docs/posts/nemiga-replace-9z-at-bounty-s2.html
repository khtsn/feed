Nemiga replace 9z at Bounty S2

Nemiga have accepted an invitation to replace 9z at BLAST Bounty 2026 Season 2.

9z are flying high after the end of the first season, having made it to the arenas at both PGL Astana and the IEM Cologne Major.

But they are skipping next season's July curtain raiser due to "updates to their team schedule," according to BLAST.
