Short: Aurora kick off international era by making StarSeries Fall qualifier playoffs

The new Damjan "⁠kyxsan⁠" Stoilkovski-led Aurora got off to a winning start in the StarLadder StarSeries Fall 2026 Europe Closed Qualifier with wins over K27 and Nemesis.

While they were able to show their prowess on Nuke (13-3 and 13-2 victories), they had to work for both victories after losing their opponent's map pick.

In terms of individuals, Jimi "⁠Jimpphat⁠" Salo (1.27 average) was their best performer across the two games, while Ismailcan "⁠XANTARES⁠" Dörtkardeş (0.90) finished day one bottom of the board.

The European combine now await their opponents in the playoffs, set to begin on Saturday.

Short: Luken becomes a free agent

Luca "⁠Luken⁠" Nadotti has announced he is no longer under contract with Gaimin Gladiators and is open to new opportunities. The organization benched the entirety of its roster after the Cologne Major following a "strategic review" of its Counter-Strike program, and now has only Henrique "⁠HEN1⁠" Teles left on its books.

"Unfortunately, following the changes made by Valve regarding the stickers, the organization understood that the project was no longer profitable and the team could not be sustained," Luken said on X, before adding that the organization treated the team well.

Short: Subtop de France deemed "a material risk to competitive integrity," banned from CCT

A French team called Subtop De France, which has been participating in various lower-tier events including the CCT circuit for the past four months, has been banned from the CCT circuit and its matches in the ongoing Europe 6 tournament have been forfeited.

The decision follows an "integrity assessment" by the company behind CCT, GRID, which deemed the French team "a material risk to competitive integrity."
