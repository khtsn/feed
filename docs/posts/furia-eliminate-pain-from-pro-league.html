FURIA eliminate paiN from Pro League

FURIA are one win away from advancing to the playoffs of ESL Pro League Season 23 after eliminating paiN in the 1-2 pool.

Gabriel "⁠FalleN⁠" Toledo's men were left staring elimination in the face after beginning their campaign in the online tournament with losses to B8 and 3DMAX, but have clawed back with wins over HEROIC and paiN to give themselves a chance at qualifying for the LAN finals.

It was a return to form for FURIA against their Brazilian countrymen on paiN, who are still finding their footing with new additions Vinicius "⁠vsm⁠" Moreira and Guilherme "⁠piriajr⁠" Barbosa and couldn't replicate the form that had carried them past 3DMAX, Passion UA, and G2 in Stage 1.

A FURIA win was never really in doubt during the series as they ravaged paiN on Inferno, making up for a lost pistol round with a near-lockout defense. FURIA gave up just two opening duels in the first half, with Danil "⁠molodoy⁠" Golubenko and Kaike "⁠KSCERATO⁠" Cerato going 5-0 and 3-0, respectively, and if not for a 1v4 Tec-9 clutch by Lucas "⁠nqz⁠" Soares, paiN would've been completely blown out.

Even so, they only walked away from the map with only six rounds in total despite winning both pistols and one of the conversions, and almost nothing went their way at the start of FURIA's Dust2 pick.

molodoy was on fire early doors, and insult to injury was added after KSCERATO and Yuri "⁠yuurih⁠" Santos converted a 2v5 in the first full gun round with vsm dropping out of the server mid-round.

KSCERATO and molodoy continued to shine as FURIA drummed up a 10-2 lead by the break, and though paiN made the map more competitive after winning the second pistol, molodoy set his team on map point with a saved AWP before helping convert victory with another double AWP opener in the final round.
