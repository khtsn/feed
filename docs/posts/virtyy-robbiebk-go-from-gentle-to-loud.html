LOUD, once one of the Americas region's giants, has had a disappointing start to the 2025 season. While LOUD, as a partnered organization, faces different circumstances from Gentle Mates, their poor form has also forced changes throughout the season.

The ' pANcada e amigos 2.0', or 'pANcada and friends 2.0', project hasn't worked out quite as intended yet. RobbieBk's arrival may look to address the lack of an in-game leader for LOUD. pANcada and v1nny have both been the team's IGL this year, but the Dutch Initiator player could fill that role.
