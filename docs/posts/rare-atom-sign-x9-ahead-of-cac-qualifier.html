Rare Atom sign x9 ahead of CAC qualifier

x9 follows in chengking's foosteps and signs with Rare Atom

Rare Atom have announced that they have completed the signing of FengDa benched player JiaMing "⁠x9⁠" Li, establishing a six-man lineup before the Asia-Pacific closed qualifier for the CS Asia Championships.

"x9 has competed in China's tier-2 scene for a long time, during which he has produced many impressive highlight plays, while also proving his competitive strength by winning championships at POB and PNL," Rare Atom wrote in a statement posted on social media.

The 22-year-old becomes the second player to join Rare Atom from FengDa in three months, following the December signing of Shengxuan "⁠chengking⁠" Zu.

It hasn't been an easy start to the season for Rare Atom, who were eliminated from the ZHI-TECH Elite Masters in the quarter-finals and missed out on spots at BLAST Open Rotterdam and PGL Bucharest.

The Chinese team is currently ranked sixth in the Asian VRS, outside of the qualification zone for the IEM Cologne Major. Next week, they will be fighting to keep their Major hopes alive in the CAC qualifier, a tournament that could have significant implications in the Asian ranking.
