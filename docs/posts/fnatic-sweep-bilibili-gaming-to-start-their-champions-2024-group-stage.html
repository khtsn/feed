The second match from Valorant Champions 2024 Group A was FNATIC FNATIC Europe Rank #1 hiro Emirhan Kat Derke Nikita Sirmitev Boaster Jake Howlett Chronicle Timofey Khromov Alfajer Emir Ali Beder versus Bilibili Gaming Bilibili Gaming China Rank #5 B3ar Yang Zhanxiong (杨瞻雄) whzy Wang Haozhe (王昊哲) Levius Lu Yinzhong (卢胤中) nephh Marcus Tan Flex1n Lo Rui (罗瑞) Knight Liu Yuxiang (刘宇翔) Yosemite Wang Lei (王磊) , where the EMEA team swept Bilibili 2-0. This is a rematch from the group stage of Champions 2023, where Fnatic defeated them 2-0 in-between the two BLG upset wins over NRG.

BLG started by picking Bind, leading to Fnatic picking Lotus, leaving Ascent as the decider map.

Bind began with hints of an upset for BLG, but the Fnatic attack side was impeccable as they won map one 13-9 to get ahead in the series. Fnatic started Lotus off hot and never cooled down, ending the map 13-4 to sweep the series.

Chronicle and hiro were the stars of the series, while BLG looked timid on their Bind defense and Lotus attack. With this win, Fnatic will face DRX for the first group spot in the playoff stage. As for Bilibili, they will face KRU Esports in the first elimination match of Group A.
