BetBoom to field FL4MUS and zorte as stand-ins at upcoming LANs due to visa issues

BetBoom have announced that Timur "⁠FL4MUS⁠" Marev and Aleksandr "⁠zorte⁠" Zagodyrenko will stand in for Daniil "⁠d1Ledez⁠" Kustov and Artem "⁠ArtFr0st⁠" Kharitonov due to visa issues for their run of three LANs ahead of the April 6 VRS invite cut-off for the IEM Cologne Major.

The first of those, BC.Game Masters Championship Season 1, is set to begin on March 24 in Bucharest, Romania, with BetBoom seeded directly into the single-elimination playoff bracket that kicks off on March 25.

The team will then travel back-to-back to Portugal and Spain for Roman Imperium Cup VII and Stake Ranked Episode 1, facing stiff competition across all three events with the likes of G2, HEROIC, GamerLegion, and Gentle Mates in attendance.
