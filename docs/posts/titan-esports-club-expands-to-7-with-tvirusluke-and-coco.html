TvirusLuke is "a veteran of the arena," first popping up in the Hong Kong & Taiwan First Strike qualifiers all the way back in 2020. He got his big break under DRG, who he represented since 2023, and where he fought for a place near the top of VCT China.

DRG had a successful run between 2023's China Ascension, which the team won, and China Stage 1 in 2024, where the team qualified to Masters Shanghai. At Ascension, TvirusLuke recorded the third-highest ACS average and tied second for best KPR. His team struggled to maintain their form since; January's China Kickoff was their sole top-four placement since Shanghai.

Conversely, Coco's time in the spotlight has been limited. His only five recorded matches were during last year's China Ascension, in which he played under Chosen Clique. After acing the group stage and moving to the playoffs, Coco and his team were outmatched by Xi Lai Gaming Xi Lai Gaming China Rank #3 Viva Lifan Ran (冉立凡) Rarga Arthur Churyumov happywei Deng Minwei (邓闵维) coconut Colin Patrick Chung (钟弘祖) YOU Yip Man Ho — the eventual winners — in the opening round and in the lower finals, leading to their elimination in third place.

The pair joins Titan Esports Club, who has struggled since joining the VCT. The team's best finish was a top-six placement at last year's Kickoff. TEC has exited every tournament since but one — the FGC Invitational 2024 — in last place. Their two latest additions bring about a hope to bounce back and "win every arena," as per the organization's Weibo posts.
