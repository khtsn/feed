Team Heretics qualify to first Masters with 2-0

The first EMEA team to qualify to Masters Madrid, Team Heretics have defeated NAVI 2-0. They will next play in the Kickoff grand final, against either FNATIC or Karmine Corp, for an opportunity at earning 3 Championship points.

The map began on Sunset, where Heretics was able to bring back their map pick on overtime. They went on to Breeze with the upper hand, where they were able to stomp NAVI with a 13-4.

This is the first time Team Heretics has qualified to an international VCT event. All five players are also making their international debut. Their stand-in, paTiTek, will continue to play throughout Madrid.

NAVI will miss an event for the first time since qualifying to Masters Reykjavík in 2022. They were unable to attend the LAN, but had gone to every event since.
