Round 2 matches set as Stage 2 newcomers rule

The new teams to arrive in Cologne for Stage 2 won six of the eight opening matches.

donk arrived with a chart-topping performance against BetBoom

The first round of best-of-ones in Stage 2 of the IEM Cologne Major has come to a close with favorites mostly in cruise control.

The day began with the newcomers coming out on top, as Astralis looked indomitable on Ancient against an uninspired GamerLegion Terrorist side, while 9z punished FlyQuest's egregiously slow pace on the offensive half of Dust2.

FUT then made a loud entry into the Major with a hard-fought win over B8 on Ancient, and the first newcomer fell on the other stream as paiN faltered at the hands of the warmed-up TYLOO on Mirage.

G2's aggressive Mirage CT side was too much to deal with for M80 in the third pairing of matches, which also saw Monte deny BIG on the same map off the back of a CT forcebuy win that left the German-majority team's T side lacking once again.

In the last pair of matches, Danil "⁠donk⁠" Kryshkovets arrived at the Major in style, delivering a 2.26-rated performance to make it a comfortable Mirage win against BetBoom.

In the other (somewhat) national derby, MIBR secured only the second victory of a Stage 1 team of the opening round over Legacy on Nuke off the back of a superb Klimentii "⁠kl1m⁠" Krivosheev and Felipe "⁠insani⁠" Yuji combination.
