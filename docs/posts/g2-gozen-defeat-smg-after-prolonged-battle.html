G2 Gozen defeat SMG after prolonged battle

After yesterday's quick 2-0 wins, G2 Gozen G2 Gozen Europe Rank #2 roxi Vivian Schilling Petra Petra Stoker mimi Michaela Lintrup sarah Sarah Ahmed Glance Anastasia Anisimova and Team SMG GC Team SMG GC Asia-Pacific Rank #3 Kohaibi Abigail Kong enerii Odella Abraham Alexy Alexy Francisco shirazi Kelly Jaudian Kamiyu Camille Enriquez clashed in the upper bracket today. Map one, Bind, was chosen by G2 Gozen and saw them starting on attack. SMG would find a slight lead at half-time with a 7-5 score. G2 would win a few more rounds but SMG eventually took the map 13-8.

Map two was SMG's pick and took both teams to Split. This time, G2 started on defense and found a solid lead in the first half, ending it 8-4. They would extend it further to 10-4, before SMG found seven rounds in a row to retake the lead. The game went to overtime, where G2 were able to win 14-12.

The decider map was Haven, and this was the first map where it seemed like one team held all the cards. G2 once again built up a lead in the first half, at 8-4. However, this time, there was no pushback from SMG after the sideswap and G2 won the map 13-4, netting them the series win too.

With this, team SMG will drop to the lower bracket, and face off against the winners of KRÜ BLAZE and Chao Hui EDward Gaming. G2 Gozen will face off against the winner of Shopify Rebellion GC vs BBL Queens.
