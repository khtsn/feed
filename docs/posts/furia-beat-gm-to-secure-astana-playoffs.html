FURIA beat GM to secure Astana playoffs

molodoy continues to star in Astana

FURIA became the next team to lock in their PGL Astana playoff spot after defeating Gentle Mates with a 2-1 scoreline (Nuke 13-11, Overpass 10-13, Inferno 13-6).

The win means that Danil "⁠molodoy⁠" Golubenko and company have secured a match in front of the Kazakh AWPer's home crowd in the Barys Arena, where the organization's international era truly began with a semi-final appearance just under a year ago.

The series started on Nuke, where molodoy led the way early on for FURIA to help them run away to a 6-0 lead. Despite their hot start, however, Gentle Mates proved they are a force to be reckoned with and brought it all the way back to 11-12, before FURIA's upper execute secured them the victory by the skin of their teeth.

Gentle Mates kept up the momentum of their Nuke comeback on Overpass, where they displayed a proactive and aggressive playstyle that continuously poked holes in FURIA's setups and allowed them to take a 12-4 lead. While they had some issues closing out the game on the T side, Alejandro "⁠alex⁠" Masanet and company eventually got the job done to secure a 13-10 win.

Inferno was the most one-sided map of the series, with FURIA finally showing some of the form that netted them wins over HEROIC and Monte earlier in the event.

A dominant 9-3 CT half was followed by two 1vs3 clutches by molodoy and Gabriel "⁠FalleN⁠" Toledo that ruined any chance of a Gentle Mates comeback and netted FURIA a spot in the playoffs.
