Short news: Week 22

FlyQuest are bringing back Project KOALAfication, a sustainability initiative launched during IEM Dallas 2024, ahead of the Cologne Major.

The North American organization will adopt a koala for a month for every map Vitality loses at the Major, every pistol round FlyQuest wins, and every map an Australian team wins.
