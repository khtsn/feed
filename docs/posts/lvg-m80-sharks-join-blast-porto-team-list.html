LVG, M80, Sharks join BLAST Porto team list

Lynn Vision, M80 and Sharks have secured valuable invites to BLAST Open Porto by winning their regional qualifiers.

They will be joined by Inner Circle, the winners in Europe, and 12 top-ranked VRS sides to compete for a $1.1m prize pool in one of the few Big Events before the Singapore Major invite date on November 2.
