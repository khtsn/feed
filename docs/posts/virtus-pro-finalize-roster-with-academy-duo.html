Virtus.pro finalize roster with academy duo

Virtus.pro have announced that academy duo Alexander "⁠AquaRS⁠" Kovalev and Dias "⁠F0R3VER⁠" Kutubay have been promoted to the main team, completing the roster following Nikolay "⁠mir⁠" Bityukov's return to the squad.

The move is part of a major rebuild that began with the removal of Ilya "⁠Perfecto⁠" Zalutskiy, Evgenii "⁠FL1T⁠" Lebedev and Petr "⁠fame⁠" Bolyshev from the active roster after a shocking run of results that saw the team sink to 100th in the global VRS.

While the rebuild was underway, Vladimir "⁠b1st⁠" Krasikov and Vadim "⁠tO0RO⁠" Arkov returned to VP.Prodigy. The points accrued in IZI WEX Season 1 and MPKBK CIS LAN Season 3 pushed the academy team to 77th in the global VRS — giving Virtus.pro a foundation to build upon as four players are now on the main roster.

"The decision to promote the players to the main roster was made following recent tournaments," Virtus.pro said in a statement.

"Dias and Alex showed strong performances as part of VP.Prodigy, demonstrating solid teamwork and individual skill. The club believes that working alongside an experienced captain will help the young players adapt to the main roster environment and fully unlock their potential."

The Russian organization added that the team will undergo an extended bootcamp in Serbia until the end of April to prepare for their next tournaments.

Virtus.pro are now:

Vladimir "⁠b1st⁠" Krasikov

Dmitriy "⁠ProbLeM⁠" Martinov (coach)

Petr "⁠fame⁠" Bolyshev (benched)
