Sashi bench Zyphon

Sashi have announced the benching of Rasmus "⁠Zyphon⁠" Nordfoss, ending the 22-year-old's 14-month stint on the starting roster.

Young rifler Emil "⁠Mol011⁠" Sall Würtz, who previously featured for Sashi Academy and more recently AaB, is joining the active lineup in place of Zyphon on a trial basis.

The news comes amidst a slow start to the year for Sashi, which has seen them fall short both online and at several LAN events like PLAYR Masters, Copenhagen Gaming Week Showdown and Parken Challenger Championship.

Zyphon finds himself on the chopping block despite being the team's second-highest rated player overall across 2026, only behind AWPer Frederik "⁠acoR⁠" Gyldstrand, and highest-rated player on LAN.

Replacing Zyphon on a trial basis is Mol011, a young player who made his first steps into professional Counter-Strike at the start of 2024 with Sashi Academy.

Mol011 has most recently been playing for AaB, playing lower-tier online competitions in Europe whilst averaging a 1.03 rating. The 19-year-old parted ways with AaB early in February.

Frederik "⁠acoR⁠" Gyldstrand
