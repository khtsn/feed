Zendaya, Jamie Lee Curtis and more of the best-dressed stars at the SAG Awards 2023

Best red carpet fashion at the 2023 SAG Awards

See some of the most eye-catching looks from LA's Fairmont Century Plaza, where the stars are gathering to celebrate last year's best TV and film performances.

Updated 27th February 2023

Some of Hollywood's biggest stars are gathering on the red carpet for the 29th Screen Actors Guild Awards.

This year's nominations are packed with actors known for their sense of style, with the likes of Zendaya and Viola Davis (recognized for their roles in "Euphoria" and "The Woman King," respectively) among Sunday evening's most hotly anticipated arrivals.

And if last month's Golden Globes and Critics Choice Awards are anything to go by, fashion-watchers can expect plenty of monochrome gowns, metallic textures and daring cutouts.

See below for some of the red carpet's best looks so far. This story will be updated throughout the evening.
