    Tournament organizer Exort has cancelled three of their events, namely Meteor Season 3 and Season 4, as well as Meteor South America, due to "circumstances beyond their control."

    All three events were supposed to be played online and feature a $10,000 prize pool, and were expected to start in March.

    Exort has announced registration is set to open for Exort Cataclysm Europe Season 1, a $50,000 VRS LAN in Belgrade, Serbia.

    The tournament will run May 11-14, and will cost teams an entry fee of $3,000.

    The $10,000 open LAN taking place in Latvia's capital city has begun with the best-of-one group stage, and the first group is done and dusted with FOKUS and 3DMAX advancing to the playoffs.

    The Erik "⁠ztr⁠" Gustafsson-led squad surprised the Frenchmen on Ancient and dealt them a heavy blow on the VRS, which means 3DMAX have barely improved their standing, while FOKUS jumped from #132 to #95.
