Short news: Week 12

FACEIT has announced changes to its matchmaking platform that will come into effect when Season 8 begins on April 22.

All players will have their elo temporarily hidden and be required to play 10 placement matches to receive their new starting level for the season. In the announcement and a subsequent video, FACEIT stressed that it is not a hard reset, the starting point for placement matches will be based on recent elo and activity, and explained the reasoning behind the change.

"First, some players become inactive but keep their level," the blog post says. "When they come back, they often don’t have that skill level anymore. This leads to uneven games and frustration for everyone else in the match.

"Second, level 10 is uncapped, so rank naturally stretches as the best players climb, causing the distribution of players in level 10 to move. This soft reset combats the inflation that can naturally happen at this level and ensures that level 10 stays highly competitive."
