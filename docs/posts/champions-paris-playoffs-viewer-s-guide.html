Champions Paris playoffs viewer's guide

The Champions 2025 groups are done. Eight teams are gone, and eight are through to the playoffs. The world is just a few days from crowning a fifth different Valorant world champion.

Last year's champions, EDward Gaming EDward Gaming China Rank #3 ZmjjKK Zheng Yongkang (郑永康) nobody Wang Senxu (王森旭) CHICHOO Wan Shunzhi (万顺治) Smoggy Zhang Zhao (张钊) Jieni7 Zhang Juntai (张君泰) , were eliminated in the group stage. They were sent back home alongside all their countrymen, marking the first Champions playoffs without a Chinese squad since 2023.

A number of formidable names still stand tall on the path to the Champions' cup. A new name will be etched into history in just a few days, when that cup is lifted deep in the heart of Paris. This first week of games will be hosted in the same venue where the groups took place — Les Arènes de Grand Paris Sud — but the finals weekend will move to the 20,300-seater Accor Arena.

These eight rosters were drawn into a double-elimination playoffs bracket. Each #1 seed was randomly drawn into the bracket against a #2 seed, with the only rule being that teams from the same group would have to be on opposing sides of the bracket. Regional matchups were allowed.

Every match in the playoffs will be a Bo3, barring the lower bracket final and the grand final. Those final two matches will be Bo5.
