Liquid bench ultimate

Liquid have announced that Roland "⁠ultimate⁠" Tomkowiak has been moved to the bench ahead of the upcoming season, making room for a change in AWPer.

The Pole had spent two years in Liquid since joining in July of 2024, and played 331 maps during that time with a 1.02 rating.

Liquid had been struggling since before ultimate came into the roster, and the 22-year-old was unable to help turn the tide and lift a trophy, with third-place finishes at CS Asia Championships 2025 and Birch Cup 2025 as his best results with the team.

ultimate played at four Majors during his time in Liquid, with a quarter-final finish at the PW Major in Shanghai his best finish, followed by two Stage 3 exits in Austin and Budapest before a Stage 1 exit at IEM Cologne.

Liquid is currently:

Keith "⁠NAF⁠" Markovic

Viktor "⁠flashie⁠" Tamás Bea (coach)

Roland "⁠ultimate⁠" Tomkowiak (bench)
