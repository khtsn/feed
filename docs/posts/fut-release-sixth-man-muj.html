He was brought onto FUT for Split 2 of VRL Turkey, helping the team to reach a national championship and even lift the VRL Finals trophy later that year.

Outside of Red Bull Home Ground, in which he started and helped his team qualify to the Manchester LAN, Muj made scarce appearances for FUT. He played in just two maps during the ESA Open Fire All Stars last year and was only drafted for FUT's first VCT EMEA match against Team Liquid Team Liquid Europe Rank #6 Jamppi Elias Olkkonen nAts Ayaz Akhmetshin Sayf Saif Jibraeel Redgar Igor Vlasov soulcas Dom Sulcas , remaining in the sidelines since.

Muj is not the only player on FUT with a contract set to expire this year. According to the VCT Global Contract Database, Buğra "MOJJ" Kiraz's contract is also set to expire this year. Per the VCT Roster Construction Rules, all contracts must expire on September 11.

Despite not starting with Muj, FUT were considered by many one of the surprises of the VCT EMEA. The all-Turkish roster placed third in the VCT EMEA Playoffs and, after a groups exit at Masters Tokyo, became the first Turkish team to reach an international LAN's playoffs during their Champions run.
