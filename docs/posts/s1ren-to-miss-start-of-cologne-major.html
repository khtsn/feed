S1ren to miss start of Cologne Major

BetBoom have announced that Pavel "⁠S1ren⁠" Ogloblin will not feature in the starting lineup for at least day one of IEM Cologne Major Stage 1.

The Russian organization has stated that the player will join the squad "later," though it remains unclear whether that means sometime during Stage 1 or further in the tournament should BetBoom progress.

Daniil "⁠d1Ledez⁠" Kustov has been registered as BetBoom's substitute for the Major. According to the Major's rulebook, teams are allowed to field a substitute until they choose to revert to their original lineup. After this, no further substitutions will be possible.
