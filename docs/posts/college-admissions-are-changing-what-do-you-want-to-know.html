College Admissions Are Changing. What Do You Want to Know?

We will answer your questions about applying to, and paying for, college.

June 29, 2023, 12:28 p.m. ET

More voices, better journalism. The questionnaire you are reading is just one tool we use to help ensure our work reflects the world we cover. By inviting readers to share their experiences, we get a wide range of views that often lead to a more deeply reported article. We make every effort to contact you before publishing any part of your submission, and your information is secure.

With rulings from the Supreme Court on race-conscious affirmative action and a decision expected imminently on student loans, the new college landscape might need navigating.

What does this mean if you are planning to apply to college? Send us your questions about the right strategies to use.

Nothing is too basic: What kinds of loans are available? Can you bargain for more financial aid? Should you share information about your race or ethnicity in the application process? Should you say that you plan to apply for financial aid? Does it make sense to go to community college first and then transfer to a four-year university?

What most overwhelms you? Do you think you will be affected by the end of affirmative action — either for better or for worse?

We’ll answer your questions, drawing on our own expertise and that of education experts.

A reporter may follow up with you to discuss your response. We won’t publish your name or comments without talking to you first.
