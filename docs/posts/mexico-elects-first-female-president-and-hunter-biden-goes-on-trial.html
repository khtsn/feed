With victories over Paper Rex and FUT Esports, 100 Thieves and Team Heretics round out the Masters Shanghai top four. They will next play against each other on June 7, with the winner keeping their trophy hopes alive and advancing to the top three at the event.

The first match ended with a 2-1 scoreline favoring 100 Thieves over Paper Rex. The teams took their respective map pick, with 100 Thieves winning Breeze 13-5 and Paper Rex taking Sunset 13-10. The match continued on Icebox, where Paper Rex was able to close 100 Thieves' lead and force overtime. In the end, 100 Thieves recovered and won the last two rounds, eliminating Paper Rex with a 14-12.

Match two was not as close, as Team Heretics continued their streak of defeating FUT 2-0 in VCT. Heretics opened up with a 13-7 on their opponent's map pick, Bind. Next, they closed out Sunset with a quick 13-4, sending the Turkish team home.

With these victories, 100 Thieves return to the top four at an international event for the first time since September 2021. As their second international appearance, it is Team Heretics' best performance so far. As for the losing teams, FUT are also ending with their highest placement at an international event, but this is Paper Rex's worst finish since LOCK//IN, as they made the top three in every event since.
