Fast career climbers have this pattern

I saw many profiles of fast career climbers. Someone who went from entry level SWE job to Director of Engineering in 10 years. 1. Get a job at top company at level L. 2. Within a year jump to startup as L+1 3. Within 1-2 years jump to top company at L (same level as previous job) 4. Repeat step 2.
