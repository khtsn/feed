ZywOo gunning for second MVP of 2026; career 30th

Vitality's ace, Mathieu "⁠ZywOo⁠" Herbaut, is the front-runner to take the PGL Cluj-Napoca MVP as the standalone stats leader with a 1.43 rating. Of the ten players slated to play in today's final, the nearest to him is Shahar "⁠flameZ⁠" Shushan, a long shot sitting at 1.24.

On the other side of the server, PARIVISION have only two players with even an above-average rating so far, Ivan "⁠zweih⁠" Gogin with a 1.07 and Dzhami "⁠Jame⁠" Ali with a 1.04.

This year ZywOo has already earned one MVP medal, at IEM Krakow, and will try to secure a second in the grand final against PARIVISION, which would bring his career tally up to 30.
