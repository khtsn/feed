T1 moves forward without Sylvan

T1 T1 Korea Rank #3 stax Kim Gu-taek (김구택) Meteor Kim Tae-oh (김태오) BuZz Yu Byeong-cheol (유병철) DH Kang Dong-ho (강동호) iZu Ham Woo-ju (함우주) will not field Go "Sylvan" Young-sup (고영섭) for the remainder of the season, as confirmed by the team manager Norman .

After lifting a masters trophy in Bangkok, T1 has struggled to replicate their success in Pacific. They went on a four game losing streak to go out in the first round of playoffs, missing out on Masters Toronto.

Following their elimination, T1 played in the EWC Qualifiers, debuting DH from T1 Academy T1 Academy Korea Rank #7 Pkm Park Kang-min (박강민) Moothie Yoo Seung-Woo (유승우) exa Kim Lee-hwan (김이환) GangPin Chae Jung-moo (채정무) Efina Kim Nak-yeon (김낙연) in the Controller role, replacing Sylvan. While Sylvan had been subbed in and out before, as was seen during their Kickoff and Bangkok run, this change was confirmed to be permanent.

T1 will maintain a six player active roster, including BuZz , carpe , DH, iZu , Meteor , and stax . Sylvan will remain signed to the organization, but is not expected to play.
