Pacific Ascension groups and schedule revealed

Riot Games has announced the groups and schedule for the Pacific Ascension tournament, alongside the venues where the event will take place.

Ten teams from a multitude of regions across Asia will compete in an offline event in Bangkok, Thailand to decide who will be promoted to the VCT Pacific league for the next two years beginning in 2024. This will include the victors of each respective VCL tournament in Southeast Asia, South Asia, Oceania, and East Asia.

The tournament will begin with a group stage separated into two groups of five over a week of play from June 28 to July 4 in Pullman Grande Sukhumvit Hotel.

Following the end of the group stage matches, the top three teams in each group will then go on to a single elimination playoffs bracket, with a first-round bye granted for the highest placing team in each group. Playoffs will take place from July 7-9 in Queen Sirikit National Convention Center, where all matches leading up to the finals will be BO3s, while the grand final will be a BO5.
