G2 inch past NIP into EPL Stage 2

MATYS' contributions were key in ensuring a 2-0 series

G2 have secured their spot in the next stage of ESL Pro League Season 23 by virtue of a 2-0 win over Ninjas in Pyjamas, squeaking past their opposition with close wins on Anubis (13-10) and Dust2 (16-13).

Ninjas in Pyjamas had more on the line today than mere progression in ESL Pro League. Marco "⁠Snappi⁠" Pfeiffer and company have seen their Major hopes slip away from them amidst a relatively poor start to the year, and this defeat now leaves them with a mountain to climb, based on current schedules, if they are to attend the marquee event in Cologne.

The series kicked off on Anubis with Matúš "⁠MATYS⁠" Šimko leading the way as G2 raced out to a 9-3 lead off the back of their T side, before a pistol win and conversion pushed the team to map point. Ninjas in Pyjamas put together a cosmetic comeback, but G2 found the one round needed to seal the deal.

Crazy MATYS ace reverses the 2v5 disadvantage

Dust2 was a similar story. MATYS' superlative form continued as he wrecked Ninjas in Pyjamas with numerous multi-kills to give G2 a lead off the back of their offense, but when the sides switched, Ninjas in Pyjamas began to come back into the map, largely off the back of Artem "⁠r1nkle⁠" Moroz's opening kills.

MATYS came to his team's rescue after Ninjas in Pyjamas forced overtime, re-finding his earlier form to pick up a couple of impactful multi-kills and ensure his team bagged the 2-0 victory.
