Inner Circle qualify for BLAST Open Porto

Inner Circle have secured a spot at BLAST Open Porto after winning RES Showdown 4, the Europe qualifier for the $1,000,000 tournament slated to play out from August 24 - September 6.

Viktor "⁠onic⁠" Babii's troops didn't drop a map en route to securing their spot, romping past GenOne, magic, and Virtus.pro with ease to earn a place at their first notable LAN since PGL Bucharest in April.

The victory comes weeks after the Ukrainian team impressed with a title-winning campaign at Super DraculaN Season 1, providing valuable VRS points as they inch closer towards earning future invites by being ranked 26th.

HLTV Prospect #8 David "⁠Dawy⁠" Bibik led his team's campaign, averaging a 1.38 rating over six maps even after taking a backseat in the grand final. Virtus.pro were hampered in the series as Petr "⁠fame⁠" Bolyshev had to substitute for Alexander "⁠AquaRS⁠" Kovalev on the second map, but the Russian team will likely be satisfied with a solid campaign overall — picking up wins over Ninjas in Pyjamas and B8 — after being absent from top competition for months.
