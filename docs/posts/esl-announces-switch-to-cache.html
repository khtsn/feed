ESL announces switch to Cache

ESL has updated its map pool across all ESL Pro Tour competitions to reflect Valve's removal of Overpass and the addition of Cache in the new season's Active Duty rotation.

The first ESL tournament to feature the new map will be the first European cup in the ESL Challenger League Season 52 series, taking place from July 12-14.

Cache has already seen some play at the lower tiers in CCT and the European Pro League, and it will make its tier-one debut at the XSE Pro League, which kicks off on July 1.
