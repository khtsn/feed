m0NESY, ZywOo, and donk are the three highest-rated players so far

While the fact that the trio is heading the rating charts may come as no surprise, it is quite rare we see the "Big three" in the top four of a playoff bracket.

Ilya "⁠m0NESY⁠" Osipov is coming into the semi-finals as the highest-rated player at the event, but Mathieu "⁠ZywOo⁠" Herbaut's performances in the game yesterday, where he put up a 1.92 rating against Natus Vincere, show the Frenchman is in his usual form.

Danil "⁠donk⁠" Kryshkovets, too, had a great tournament, having put up a casual 1.42 rating against MOUZ yesterday while Andrey "⁠tN1R⁠" Tatarinovich stole the show.

Spirit look to break dire Falcons streak

Live

In their last five meetings, Spirit have only beaten Falcons once.

The last time they met was at IEM Rio, and it doesn't make for good reading for Spirit fans. The majority-Russian squad mustered a total of nine rounds across two maps, while Danil "⁠donk⁠" Kryshkovets recorded the lowest-rated map of his career with a 0.30 on Mirage.

It remains to be seen if the arena could turn the tide in their favor, however.
