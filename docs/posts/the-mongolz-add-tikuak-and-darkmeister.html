The MongolZ add tikuak and DarkMeister

tikuak turned heads whilst playing for Chinggis Warriors

The MongolZ have announced the additions of Zolbayar "⁠tikuak⁠" Chimedtseren and Dugarsuren "⁠DarkMeister⁠" Ireedui to their roster, filling the gaps left by the departure of Ayush "⁠mzinho⁠" Batbold to BC.Game and the benching of Anarbileg "⁠cobrazera⁠" Uuganbayar.

The MongolZ bench cobrazera

The addition of tikuak is the highlight of the announcement, as the 17-year-old has impressed with his play regionally and been named consistently in the HLTV Prospects ranking.

The youngster has spent much of the last eight months playing for Chinggis Warriors, where he was the team's star player and averaged a 1.15 rating during his tenure. tikuak helped Chinggis Warriors to a number of top placings regionally, including LAN wins at MESA League Season 5 and ZOWIE eXTREMESLAND Asia Open.

Despite only playing regionally tikuak has shone when he has been given the chance to play against better opponents, recently posting a team-leading 1.21 rating in a defeat at the hands of TYLOO in ECL S51 Asia-Pacific Cup 4.

DarkMeister is a more unknown quantity, the 18-year-old making the step up from The MongolZ Academy with only seven HLTV-recorded maps to his name.
