kyxsan on mentality amidst rumors: "My job is to come here and play, and that's what I'm doing"

Falcons romped past Spirit to reach the playoffs of IEM Rio, getting revenge over the Russian team five months after losing to them for the first time when it mattered most in the playoffs of the StarLadder Budapest Major.

It is reported to be the swan song for the roster with Damjan "⁠kyxsan⁠" Stoilkovski as Finn "⁠karrigan⁠" Andersen is rumored to join the team after their IEM Rio campaign, but the Macedonian captain is staying focused on what is in front of him.

"When you play for a team like Falcons, there will always be rumors, especially when you're not doing good," he told HLTV after the match. "So we are used to having these kinds of rumors and having the pressure from everyone, from the community, from the fans, from basically everyone.

"So for us, it's just about staying focused on the right things, which is playing the game, and I think we are doing it well so far. We won our first two games, we are in the playoffs, so that is the most important thing."

Watch the interview here.

Congrats, through to the playoffs of Rio. A pretty dominant 2-0 over Spirit, walk me through that with how you're feeling after such a dominant win?

I'm happy, I think we played really good. The T sides were good, the CT sides were good, our communication was on point today. It felt like it was not hard to play, and the result shows it.

It was a bit of a revenge game for you. Was that part of your mentality going into it, to get revenge for that loss at the Major?

Yes. I mean, that Major was a few months ago so some time has passed already, but we obviously remember that one. We've beaten them many times in the past but we lost in the most important game at the Major, so obviously we remember that game and we definitely wanted to win more today because of that.

The last few times we played Vitality, we actually beat them more than we lost against them, so I'm pretty sure it's going to be a nice game

It has been a rough year for Falcons, so making the playoffs is showing you're on the up a bit. You're also going to play Vitality in your next match, NiKo was talking about wanting to play them, so what are your overall thoughts in terms of Falcons' trajectory and that upcoming match?

We haven't played them for a while. The last time we played them was at BLAST Bounty with a stand-in and we won. I think the last few times we played them, we actually beat them more than we lost against them, so I'm pretty sure it's going to be a nice game.

Obviously the results for us are not the best in the last few months. I would say that we are playing good most of the time, but we struggle to have the same gameplay in the playoff games, or in the more important games. So for me, it's more about the mental part because we have shown many times that we can be good, we can play good, we can beat all the teams. We've also beaten Vitality, so for me it's just about repeating that same style of playing in the more important games.

It's interesting that you bring up the mental part because coming into this event, there is the whole rumor of karrigan joining the team and replacing you afterward. How do you stay focused with that kind of rumor coming right before the event?

When you play for a team like Falcons, there will always be rumors, especially when you're not doing good. So we are used to having these kinds of rumors and having the pressure from everyone, from the community, from the fans, from basically everyone. So for us, it's just about staying focused on the right things, which is playing the game, and I think we are doing it well so far. We won our first two games, we are in the playoffs, so that is the most important thing.

Last time in Rio, I played in the playoffs when I was in HEROIC, and it was a really good experience

From a team side, there's a way to stay focused, but when the rumor is actually involving you, how do you stay focused individually?

I think it doesn't change much. My job is to come here and to play, and that's what I'm doing. So I cannot comment too much on the rumors because as I said, there are always going to be rumors when a team is not doing good, especially a team like Falcons.

Heading into the playoffs, to the arena with all the fans, how much are you looking forward to that?

I'm excited. Last time in Rio, I played in the playoffs when I was in HEROIC, and it was a really good experience. The arena here is good, the fans are good, loud, so I'm looking forward to playing in the arena again for sure.
