NAVI beat 3DMAX to march to EPL playoffs

Natus Vincere have progressed to the ESL Pro League S23 playoffs after a three-map win over 3DMAX (Dust2 13-5, Inferno 5-13, Ancient 13-5) to consign the French squad to yet another group-stage exit.

Despite their elimination, new addition Kévin "⁠misutaaa⁠" Rabier gave a good first impression by averaging a 1.23 rating in Stage 1 and a 1.13 in Stage 2, making him his team's highest-rated player in both.

The series began on Dust2, where Natus Vincere won a second-round force buy to find themselves 6-2 early on after repelling 3DMAX B-focused offense. Two clutches by Drin "⁠makazze⁠" Shaqiri and Aleksi "⁠Aleksib⁠" Virolainen then extended their lead before another second-round forebuy meant they cruised to a 13-5 win.

The French side answered on Inferno despite losing against another second-round forcebuy, marching to an 8-4 half before putting up an imperious T-side, during which they lost just one round, to return the favor with a 13-5 of their own.

The Ancient decider was another one-sided affair, as Natus Vincere dominated their defensive side to breeze to a 13-5 win and a spot in the LAN stage.
