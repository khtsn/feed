FNATIC keeps EMEA alive in Toronto, eliminates Sentinels

FNATIC has eliminated Sentinels from Masters Toronto in a close 2-1 win. Bringing EMEA into the top four, they have ensured that all four regions are represented in the final stage of the tournament.

The last time these teams faced was in Champions 2024, also fighting to break into the top four. Back then, it was Sentinels that managed to win out a close 2-1 series, but FNATIC have taken their revenge this time around.

Sentinels took the first map of Sunset 13-9. FNATIC bounced back to win Ascent 13-7, and Icebox 13-9 to take the series.

Sentinels will exit Masters Toronto with two champs points, while FNATIC moves on to the top four to face G2 Esports.
