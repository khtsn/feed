EYEBALLERS replace M80 at PGL Bucharest

PGL has announced that EYEBALLERS will replace M80 at PGL Bucharest 2026, the $1,250,000 LAN event set to run April 4-11.

The announcement comes in the wake of the news that M80 will be attending Gamers Assembly in place of Liquid, as they chase vital VRS points in their quest to qualify for IEM Cologne Major.

EYEBALLERS are set to attend their most notable LAN since their revival in 2022, benefitting from M80's decision to prioritize attending a smaller event that will confer VRS points ahead of the April 6 cut-off for Major invites.

The Swedish side are some way short of Major qualification, and so do not have the same incentive as a team like M80 to skip a bigger LAN in favour of one that concludes before the April 6 cut-off.

The withdrawal of a team to focus on the Major race is not an isolated incident for PGL Bucharest, as Imperial withdrew from the event weeks ago for that exact reason.

The tournament will see EYEBALLERS compete alongside the likes of top 10 teams PARIVISION, The MongolZ and Astralis. EYEBALLERS originally finished 3rd in the PGL Bucharest Europe closed qualifier behind Inner Circle, who also received an invite following the withdrawal of another team.
