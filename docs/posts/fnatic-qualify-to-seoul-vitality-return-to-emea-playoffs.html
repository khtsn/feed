FNATIC qualify to Seoul, Vitality return to EMEA playoffs

The second season of VCT EMEA has concluded, setting the stage for Champions Seoul qualification. Due to the inclusion of Kickoff at the beginning of the year, teams played one round-robin throughout the year, split into two stages. This allowed Stage 2 to integrate both groups into one league table, of which the top-six overall would advance to playoffs.

Playoffs entry came down to the final day of the season, with three different teams relying on the results of the last two matches, NAVI vs. Gentle Mates and Vitality vs. Karmine Corp, to punch their tickets to the knockout stage.

A Gentle Mates victory could potentially end in a five-way tie between teams with a 4-6 record, which meant the final game of the season would confirm the final two playoff teams.

NAVI needed to win the series, but were coming into the match with a five-game losing streak. The team had failed to win a single map since their Stage 1 playoffs match against Team Heretics, and their year was on the line.

The team opted for a return to their signature roles, with Shao on initiator and ANGE1 on controller. This decision helped them take their opponent's map pick, Sunset, but they lost Ascent 13-8. In the end, a close win on the decider map secured NAVI a spot in playoffs and eliminated both Gentle Mates and Liquid from Champions contention.

Due to the circuit point system for Champions qualification, the cutoff for which teams made it to the Stage 2 playoffs was determined by their overall regular season results, not just their Stage 2 performance. The top-six teams from the regular season have a chance to qualify directly to Champions 2024 via the EMEA playoffs, as well as a last opportunity to earn points and qualify as the region's fourth seed.

Due to the circuit point system, FNATIC have already qualified to Champions. They can still qualify through the knockout stage and earn a higher seed, but, should they not win any games in playoffs, no team can place outside the top three while earning more points than them.

The VCT EMEA 2024 Stage 2 playoffs begin on July 15, with the first matches being single elimination games. Those two matches are between the third to sixth seeds from the regular season, starting with:

  • Team Vitality vs. Natus Vincere - 12:00 EDT / 18:00 CEST
  • FNATIC vs. Karmine Corp - 15:00 EDT / 21:00 CEST

The two winners in the knockout round will then face off against Heretics and FUT in a four-team double-elimination format.

  • (Winner of Game 1) vs. Team Heretics - 12:00 EDT / 18:00 CEST
  • (Winner of Game 2) vs. FUT Esports - 15:00 EDT / 21:00 CEST

Team Heretics and FUT Esports received a bye due to their performance. As the top two teams in the tournament, they will need to win a single match to qualify to Champions. Should either of them fail to win a match, they will need FNATIC to place in the top three to qualify via circuit points.

With eight championship points, Karmine Corp need to either reach the top three or for the three teams above them to qualify via playoffs.

Lastly, Team Vitality and Natus Vincere need to win two matches in order to make Champions. They will face off against each other in the single elimination portion of the bracket, meaning the winner will need another victory to make Champions, while the loser would be directly eliminated.
