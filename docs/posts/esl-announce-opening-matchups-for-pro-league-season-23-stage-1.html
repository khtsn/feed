ESL announce opening matchups for Pro League Season 23 Stage 1

ESL has announced the opening matchups for ESL Pro League Season 23 Stage 1, which will be played online from March 1-5 using a Swiss format.

Seeds were based on how teams ranked each other, with PARIVISION's top billing earning them a match against the bottom-ranked SemperFi.

Headline matchups from the first round include Ninjas in Pyjamas taking on Legacy, the latter of which will debut with Andrei "⁠arT⁠" Piovezan as their new in-game leader after Lucas "⁠lux⁠" Meneghini was moved to the bench after IEM Krakow, and Passion UA playing their first match with Azbayar "⁠Senzu⁠" Munkhbold against Liquid.
