HEROIC dump Passion UA out of EPL S23

The AWP-er less squad lives to fight another day.

HEROIC have defeated Passion UA in a two-map series to send Azbayar "⁠Senzu⁠" Munkhbold and company out of the tournament with a 1-3 record.

The match was a straightforward win for HEROIC, who began the series with a 13-5 win on Passion UA's pick of Overpass. Simon "⁠yxngstxr⁠" Boije had a great game on the AWP, posting a 1.47 rating and hitting some nice AWP shots along the way to a statement victory.

Passion UA's individuals also failed to show up, as Johnny "⁠JT⁠" Theodosiou, Senzu, and Santino "⁠try⁠" Rigal all finished the map on single-digit kills.

Nuke was a closer affair, but a strong CT side by HEROIC allowed them to take control late on and secure a 13-8 victory to stay alive for the final day of Stage 1.

Senzu can still frag, but Passion UA have some work to do

Senzu has shown glimpses of his peak on some of his first maps for Passion UA despite acclimatizing to a new team and English communication, and the Mongolian's output will no doubt improve as he gets more comfortable.

He averaged a 1.18 rating across Stage 1, 0.18 ahead of his closest teammate Vladyslav "⁠Kvem⁠" Korol, but Passion UA secured just one victory across their campaign.
