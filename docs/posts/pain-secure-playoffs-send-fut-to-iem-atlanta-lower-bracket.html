paiN secure playoffs, send FUT to IEM Atlanta lower bracket

paiN kicked off their run in Atlanta with a victory over FaZe, and have now knocked FUT down to the lower bracket with a 2-1 victory to secure a spot in the playoffs.

The Rodrigo "⁠biguzera⁠" Bittencourt-led squad isn't done yet, however, as they will face Vitality's group stage upper bracket butchers, BetBoom, in the Group A upper bracket final to leapfrog the quarters and go straight into the playoffs' semi-finals.

biguzera himself started off the series in fine form, turning a 1v4 on the T side of Nuke that was crucial to get going a seven-round streak that took paiN to an 8-4 half on their pick.

After the second half, the Brazilians won four of five to hit map point, and didn't take long to secure the map with Guilherme "⁠piriajr⁠" Barbosa's +7.41% swing, 1.67-rated performance leading the charge.

FUT responded on their own map pick of Overpass, where six rounds in the first half of the T side despite losing pistols and the first gun round, put them in a good position to tie up the series.

After the switch, Nikita "⁠cmtry⁠" Samolotov and Laurențiu "⁠lauNX⁠" Țârlea — who otherwise had another subpar performance since returning from the injured list —, locked the CT side down with 1.35 and 1.29-rated defensive performances, respectively.

It all boiled down to Dust2, a map that went down to the wire, 13-11, but that paiN managed to win in the end despite a strong CT side from FUT that put the Europeans ahead 8-4 at the half.

João "⁠snow⁠" Vinicius had a big moment with four kills on a B defense in a crucial 19th round that put his team in the lead with double digits. FUT cut that lead all the way back to 11-11 after 22 rounds, but in the end could not break through in the final two attacks of the series.

FUT's run in Atlanta has not come to an end, but it has gotten much more complicated as they will be fighting for the single playoff spot available in the Group A lower bracket.
