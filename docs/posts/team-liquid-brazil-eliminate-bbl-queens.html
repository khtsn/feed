Team Liquid Brazil eliminate BBL Queens

The second game of today saw a tournament-life deciding game for BBL Queens and Team Liquid Brazil. Team Liquid had found a first round win against KRU BLAZE but had fallen in short order to Shopify Rebellion GC. Team Liquid on the other hand had been defeated in their first game by G2 Gozen, but had found a win over Evil Geniuses GC to keep their tournament hopes alive.

The series started off on Ascent, chosen by TL Brazil. They found a slight lead with a 7-5 attacking half, and capitalized on it to take the map 13-8. Map two was Haven, which saw BBL Queens start on defense. They kept the half competitive and ended it 7-5 in TL's favor. BBL Queens won seven out of the next nine rounds to get to map point. TL were able to win the next three to push it to overtime, and were able to win 14-12. With a 2-0 win, TL Brazil had eliminated the Turkish Queens.

TL Brazil will face off against Team SMG to decide who will confirm a top three finish.
