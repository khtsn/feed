IEM Atlanta teams, format, schedule, prizes, talent, fantasy

Vitality will seek to win their sixth consecutive title in Atlanta

Sixteen teams will compete in Atlanta for the $1,000,000 in prizes, with Vitality as the clear favorites for the tile after winning the last five events they attended.

Chasing them will be Natus Vincere, who have struggled against the Dan "⁠apEX⁠" Madesclaire-led squad, including in the grand final of the last event the two played, BLAST Rivals, where they lost 0-3.

The tournament, kicking off on Monday, May 11, will run through the week until Sunday, May 17.

Teams and format

IEM Atlanta will feature a classic IEM loadout, with sixteen teams divided into two best-of-three double-elimination groups, from which the winners advance to the semi-finals, and the second and third-placed teams go to the four-team quarter-finals.

The single-elimination playoffs continue with best-of-threes all the way to the third-place decider before the best-of-five grand final to crown the champion.

IEM Atlanta, clashing with PGL Astana, doesn't have all of the best teams, but it does have the top two in the ranking, Vitality and Natus Vincere.

Prizes

IEM Atlanta features a $1,000,000 prize pool, with the split between player share and club share benefitting the club, with the exception of the last-place finishers.
