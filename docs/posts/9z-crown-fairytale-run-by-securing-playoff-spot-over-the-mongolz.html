9z crown fairytale run by securing playoff spot over The MongolZ

9z secured a shock playoff appearance at the IEM Cologne Major after defeating The MongolZ with a 2-0 scoreline (Inferno 13-8, Overpass 13-8).

Maximiliano "⁠max⁠" Gonzalez's troops have now crowned their fairytale run in Germany with an appearance in the LANXESS after a Major run that started in Stage 2 and featured victories over the likes of Vitality and PARIVISION.

"This team learns very fast, and that's the important thing," max said in a post-match broadcast interview. "My guys listen to me a lot, and that gives me a lot of confidence."

The series against The MongolZ was a confident one, as 9z dominated in the entry frag department to secure a statement win on their opponent's map pick.

A cagy 6-6 first half was followed up by dominance by 9z's T side, with max and company taking seven out of the nine rounds in the second half to cruise to a 13-8 win.

9z started where they left off with a Franco "⁠dgt⁠" Garcia ace on Overpass to go up 5-1 early on, and while The MongolZ once again brought the scoreline close, 9z were a cut above them in the second half.

The MongolZ mustered a solitary round on their offense as 9z batted away anything they had to offer and cruised towards a hard-earned Major playoff appearance.

The win has also secured a Stage 3 spot for the Americas region for the Singapore Major, taking it away from Asia.
