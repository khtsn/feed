Gen.G advance to Shanghai grand final

Gen.G have reached their second international grand finals in a row after defeating G2 Esports 2-0. Including Pacific tournaments, Gen.G have participated in four out of four possible grand finals.

They will next play on Sunday, with hopes of earning Pacific's first-ever international title. G2, meanwhile, will go down to the lower bracket, where they will play a best-of-five against either 100 Thieves or Team Heretics to define the second finalist of Masters Shanghai.

The match began on G2's map pick, Lotus. After taking an early lead, Gen.G gained enough momentum to eventually close out the map 13-8. Moving on to Ascent, the Korean squad were able to prevent a comeback from G2, ending the map 13-10 and winning the series 2-0.
