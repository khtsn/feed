Wildcard replace The Huns at PGL Bucharest

PGL has announced that Wildcard will be playing at the upcoming PGL Bucharest in place of The Huns, who have backed out of the tournament as it clashes with their appearance at XSE GangKui Cup Season 2 Finals in Shanghai, running on April 1-5.

Wildcard have been invited to the event in Romania after finishing runners-up in the North American qualifier, and will kick off their run in Bucharest when the tournament gets underway on April 4.

Wildcard played their first event after signing Nemanja "⁠nEMANHA⁠" Đukić earlier in March at Urban Riga Open Season 3, where they finished in 5-8th place after falling to the eventual champions, FOKUS.

Before traveling to Bucharest, Wildcard will get another run at a second-tier event, Radio Popular Roman Imperium Cup VII, in Portugal, where they will face BESTIA, Sangal, BetBoom and NOMERCY in the group stage.
