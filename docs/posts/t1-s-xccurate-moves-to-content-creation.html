In their announcement, T1 said: "We are pleased to announce our new content creator ‘Xccurate'. He has made the decision to take on the new challenge as a T1 content creator!"

xccurate joined T1 ahead of the 2024 season as part of a roster rebuild that saw the Korean team bring in international talent. They added him, Rossy , and young Korean talent iZu , setting up a team built around a Korean core with Sayaplayer , and carpe . xccurate's time on T1 began with them narrowly missing out on qualifying for Masters Madrid by being eliminated in the playoffs of Pacific Kickoff. A 2-4 showing in Stage 1 was enough to qualify for the playoffs, and a third-place finish there was enough to qualify for Masters Shanghai. T1 was eliminated two games into the Swiss Stage.

As T1 entered the offseason, it was made clear that xccurate was not a part of their plans for 2025. It was made known that the team was shifting their focus to building a domestic roster, letting go of Rossy and filling in the rest of their roster by picking up Meteor , BuZz , and Sylvan . carpe remains on the roster as the team's substitute.
