BLAST Open Rotterdam teams, format, schedule, talent, prizes, fantasy

Teams and format

Sixteen teams will participate in BLAST Open Rotterdam after the top 12 according to the Global VRS were invited and four qualified through regional tournaments in Europe, North America, South America and Asia.

The most notable omission from the list is G2, who just narrowly missed out on a direct invite due to their 13th placing on the VRS at the time of invitation in February.

These have been distributed into two groups of eight, from where the winning side of each group will advance straight to the semi-finals, and the second and third-placed teams to the quarter-finals.

Schedule and venues

The group stage will take place over the first six days of the competition from March 18-23 in BLAST's studio on the outskirts of Copenhagen, Denmark.

For the last three days of play (March 27-29), the top-six teams will then move to Rotterdam, the Netherlands, for the playoffs played at the 15,000-seater Ahoy Arena.
