HEROIC on brink of elimination after day 1

EmiliaQAQ's late impact saved Lynn Vision from the 0-2 pool

HEROIC are the first team to go down to the 0-2 pool in Stage 1 of the IEM Cologne Major after a narrow Dust2 loss to Lynn Vision in the second round.

Earlier on the opening day of the tournament, the European side fell to one of the biggest underdogs of the stage full of Major rookies in Sharks.

With the two losses, HEROIC 's downward spiral continues. The team around Christoffer "⁠Chr1zN⁠" Storgaard has now lost seven matches in a row dating back to PGL Astana in mid-May, in most of which it was the on-paper favorite.

HEROIC now stare down the barrel of elimination in Cologne, as they enter the best-of-three matches requiring three wins in a row to move on.

For Lynn Vision, it is a sigh of relief, as they recovered a tough start on the T side of Dust2 with a superb defense, spearheaded by Zhe "⁠Westmelon⁠" Niu and Junjie "⁠EmiliaQAQ⁠" Tang.

The latter pulled off a crucial triple kill up against the ropes at 10-11, when all looked lost as he was alone on the B bomsite in a one-and-done position.
