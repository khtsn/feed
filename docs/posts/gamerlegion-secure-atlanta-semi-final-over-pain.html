GamerLegion secure Atlanta semi-final over paiN

GamerLegion have qualified for the IEM Atlanta semi-final after defeating paiN in a three-map series (Overpass 6-13, Nuke 13-6, Inferno 13-8).

Janusz "⁠Snax⁠" Pogorzelski and company will face Legacy in Saturday's semi-final.

The match began with Guilherme "⁠piriajr⁠" Barbosa absolutely dominating GamerLegion on their Overpass pick. The Brazilian rifler racked up 33 kills, 158 ADR, and an incredible 2.59 rating to help his team secure a straightforward 13-6 victory.

He wasn't able to repeat that kind of impact on Nuke, however, which allowed GamerLegion to tie up the series. Snax's troops won both pistols and had Oldřich "⁠PR⁠" Nový and Sebastian "⁠Tauson⁠" Tauson Lindelof both tally 20 kills across the map, which gave them a 13-6 win of their own and forced the Inferno decider.

GamerLegion's individual continued to dominate on Inferno. Fredrik "⁠REZ⁠" Sterner's 1vs2 post-plant clutch was one of the many plays by GamerLegion, who went up 10-3 after Milan "⁠hypex⁠" Polowiec's 1vs2 in the post before having some difficulties closing out the map on their offense.

In the end, however, it was REZ's 4k that put the final nail in the paiN coffin and secured GamerLegion a spot in the IEM Atlanta top four.
