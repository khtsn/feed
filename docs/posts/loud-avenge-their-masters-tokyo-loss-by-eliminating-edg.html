As for EDG, their run at Champions 2023 ends here, with their placement here matching their top six finish at Masters Tokyo.

The Bo3 series went the full distance. The first, Bind, saw an unorthodox Reyna pick coming out of ZmjjKK . Both teams clashed head to head, culminating in a 6-6 half while the game ended in a similarly close fashion at 13-11 after a 4K from Smoggy .

Map two was Ascent, and LOUD's choice. The battle between the teams now however took a massively different turn. LOUD found an impressive 9-3 opening half to be able to close out the game 13-5.

The decider, Haven, saw LOUD bring out their Harbor/Viper comp. The first half once again was neck-and-neck with the score tied 6-6. The second half saw LOUD slowly take control, getting to a 11-6 lead. However, EDG would win five out of the next six rounds to make the score 12-11. A 3K from cauanzin would deny all hopes of OT however and win LOUD the series.

EDG once again have achieved the same placement as Masters Tokyo- a decent top six. This runs their tournament run for the year and having achieved two playoffs out of the three events this year, have proven China's mettle despite this being their debut year internationally.

As for LOUD, they have a chance at continuing their collision course upwards, and will face off against the winner of DRX vs FNATIC.
