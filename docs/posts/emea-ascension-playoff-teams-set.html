EMEA Ascension playoff teams set

The group stage of EMEA Ascension 2025 is over, whittling eight teams down to four for playoffs.

The format for the group stage featured GSL-style double-elimination groups, dividing the eight teams into two groups of four. Each group eliminated two teams and advanced two others to playoffs.
