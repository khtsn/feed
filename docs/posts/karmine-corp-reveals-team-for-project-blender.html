Karmine Corp reveals team for Project Blender

Karmine Corp Karmine Corp Europe Rank #10 Avez Hazem Khaled SUYGETSU Dmitry Ilyushin has announced the player lineup that they will be competing with at the 2025 Project Blender tournament. The roster is Hazem "Avez" Khaled, Dmitry "SUYGETSU" Ilyushin, Burak "LewN" Alkan, Bogdan "sheydos" Naumov, and Zhumagali "dos9" Dastan, with Andrey "ENGH" Sholokhov and Ahmed "ZE1SH" El Sheikh as the coaching staff.

Karmine Corp put together an intriguing roster in 2025, pulling together a mismatch of talent from across EMEA led by Americas import saadhak . After failing to reach any international events however, only Avez, SUYGETSU, and the coaching staff remain.

New to the roster are LewN, Sheydos, and dos9, all with at least a year of VCT experience under their belt.

Both teams had their high points in the year, both coming within touching distance of reaching the international stage, but ultimately falling short at the final hurdle.

Sheydos on the other hand, is one of the oldest names in Valorant, having competed at the highest level since 2020. More recently, Sheydos has been with KOI KOI Inactive for the past three years, but was never able to find the same success.

The team will participate in the Project Blender tournament, competing with a very wide range of teams across EMEA, including seven other VCT EMEA teams. The tournament also features a unique implementation of agent bans, called Blender draft. The approach attempts to encourage agent variety by limiting the teams' abilities to play the same agents across different maps. Blender draft is explained in detail on the Project Blender website.
