FaZe trounced by paiN in IEM Atlanta opener

There were green shoots of recovery for FaZe at BLAST Rivals but that trajectory has been altered by a 2-0 defeat to paiN at IEM Atlanta.

The Brazilian side are using Rafael "⁠saffee⁠" Costa instead of star AWPer Lucas "⁠nqz⁠" Soares, and have taken a downturn of their own in 2026, but still overwhelmed their once mighty opponents with ease.

"Anubis has been our best map on practice," Rodrigo "⁠biguzera⁠" Bittencourt explained to Tres "⁠stunna⁠" Saranthus post-game, and it certainly looked like it as they won eight rounds straight on T side.

On FaZe's pick of Dust2, as well, paiN were dominant as João "⁠snow⁠" Vinicius and Guilherme "⁠piriajr⁠" Barbosa both went on to post ratings above 1.40 for the series.

The harsh reality is that even 13-6 and 13-8 scorelines flatter FaZe: They won all four pistols, with Jakub "⁠jcobbb⁠" Pietruszewski going 7-0 in the two half-starters on Dust2, and barely landed a blow in buy vs buy rounds.

"Be more focused on comms," Niclas "⁠enkay J⁠" Krumhorn was broadcast as saying at half-time on Anubis, but the damage was done; FaZe's new era has hit its first true speed bump.

FaZe will face the loser of FUT-NRG for elimination tomorrow.
