LOUD and Sentinels qualify to Madrid from Americas

LOUD LOUD Brazil Rank #1 Quick Gabriel Lima saadhak Matias Delipetro Less Felipe de Loyola tuyz Arthur Andrade cauanzin Cauan Pereira became the seventh team to qualify for Masters Madrid, taking down Evil Geniuses Evil Geniuses North America Rank #1 Derrek Derrek Ha NaturE Nicholas Garrison supamen Phat Le Apoth Vincent Le jawgemo Alexander Mor to reach the VCT Americas Kickoff grand final. EG is eliminated from the event, while LOUD will play Sentinels for the Americas first seed.

After Evil Geniuses took down LOUD on their map pick of Ascent in a comfortable 13-7 fashion with stellar performances from jawgemo and supamen , it looked as though LOUD could be in trouble. The Brazilian roster answered by allowing just 12 rounds across the next two maps to take the series and the first Americas spot at Masters Madrid.

Despite a couple strong performances from Derrek and Apoth , the EG roster in total struggled to find kills across the last two maps. Less and tuyz were the stars for LOUD on the scoreboard, but the entire team looked great in the series.

Sentinels Sentinels North America Rank #4 TenZ Tyson Ngo Zellsis Jordan Montemurro zekken Zachary Patrone johnqt Mohamed Amine Ouarid Sacy Gustavo Rossi took down NRG Esports NRG Esports North America Rank #5 crashies Austin Roberts Victor Victor Wong Marved Jimmy Nguyen Ethan Ethan Arnold Demon1 Max Mazanov in an instant classic to return to their first major event since Champions 2021. NRG is eliminated after losing their only two maps of the Kickoff tournament. Sentinels will play LOUD in a Group B rematch for the first seed for Americas at Masters Madrid.

The first map of the series saw a scrappy affair where the teams went thirty rounds on Sunset, before Sentinels survived with a 16-14 victory. Two star performances from TenZ and zekken brought them the win. Ethan answered with an elite showing of his own on Ascent to take the series to map three.

On the final map of Split, Sentinels put together retake after retake to dominate their defensive side and put themselves quite literally a couple steps away from Madrid at 11-1. johnqt converted clutches and had the right calls to lead his team, clinching an annihilating final map win.

TenZ and zekken were the star duo throughout the series, both dropping 57 kills each.
