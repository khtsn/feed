Xi Lai Gaming signs ZiXuan

Xi Lai Gaming Xi Lai Gaming China Unranked Rarga Arthur Churyumov happywei Deng Minwei (邓闵维) MrCANI Chen Tingxun (陈廷训) MarT1n Zhuo Zhengjie (卓政杰) YOU Yip Man Ho ZiXuan Liu Junwei (刘俊伟) has signed Liu "ZiXuan" Junwei (刘俊伟) to their roster.

ZiXuan last played the Controller role for Chosen Clique Gaming Chosen Clique Gaming China Unranked Maple Elvin Sun GREEN Qiu Jianzhi (邱建智) KaweNtw Martim Ferreira Coco Ding Dajian (丁达建) , the team that came in third place at China Ascension 2024. He also recently served as a stand-in for FunPlus Phoenix FunPlus Phoenix China Unranked Autumn Kale Dunne Life Qu Donghao (瞿东豪) AAAAY Zhang Yang (张杨) BerLIN Zhang Bolin (張柏霖) yosemite Wang Lei (王磊) at the TEN Asia Invitational in October.

Xi Lai Gaming is the newest addition to VCT China after winning Ascension, where they defeated Rare Atom in a dominant 3-0 in the grand final. Since then, they have parted ways with Savva "Kr1stal" Fedorov after their former in-game leader agreed in advance to depart the team due to non-resident player restrictions and added Yip "YOU" Man Ho to the team in his stead.

With this addition, XLG currently has six players. It has not been explicitly stated at this time whether ZiXuan will be replacing one of the other players on the active roster. All five of the other players are currently still listed as active in the Global Contract Database.
