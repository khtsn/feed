ESL Pro League S23 Finals playoff bracket set

The playoffs bracket for ESL Pro League Season 23 Finals has been set following the conclusion of the online stage, which saw two Swiss group stages play out from March 1-10 to whittle a pool of 24 teams down to eight.

The LAN finals will play out in the Annexet in Stockholm, Sweden from March 13-15, with Spirit and MOUZ beginning their runs from opposite sides of the bracket after going 3-0 through Stage 2.

Danil "⁠donk⁠" Kryshkovets's side will take on Astralis in their quarter-final, marking the second meeting this year between the teams after Spirit claimed a 2-0 victory in the group stage of IEM Krakow. MOUZ, meanwhile, will face FUT in a rematch of the teams' PGL Cluj-Napoca bout, where the former came back from 3-12 down on Overpass to sweep the series.

Aurora and Legacy are due for a LAN rematch of their EPL Stage 2 opener, where the Turks barely scraped victory after a quadruple overtime win on Inferno and double overtime win on the Dust2 decider. The bracket is rounded out by a match between Natus Vincere and The MongolZ, although the latter will be without coach Erdenedalai "⁠maaRaa⁠" Bayanbat as Natus Vincere look to get revenge for their defeat in Cluj.
