Pacific LCQ matchups revealed

The seven-team Pacific LCQ bracket has been released. The Pacific LCQ runs from July 18 to 23 and will qualify one team to Champions 2023.

Image by Riot Games

The Pacific LCQ's format places seven teams in a modified double-elimination bracket. Like the Americas, the Pacific LCQ will only qualify one team to Champions 2023.
