Pacific LCQ matchups revealed

4 days ago

The seven-team Pacific LCQ bracket has been released. The Pacific LCQ runs from July 18 to 23 and will qualify one team to Champions 2023.

Image by Riot Games

The Pacific LCQ's format places seven teams in a modified double-elimination bracket. Like the Americas, the Pacific LCQ will only qualify one team to Champions 2023. VCT Champions 2023 will be held in Los Angeles, between August 6 and 27. The tournament will kick off in the Shrine Auditorium, while the Kia Forum will host the final rounds of the capstone event. Tickets are still available for several days and can be found here.
