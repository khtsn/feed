VRS cut-off set closer to Major start date for PGL Singapore

PGL has announced that the VRS cut-off for teams to qualify for the PGL Singapore Major will be on November 2, making it the shortest time between the cut-off and the start date of a Major, which the TO says will ensure "that the latest competitive results are reflected in the lineup."

Previously, the VRS cut-off had been closer to two months before the start of the event. This change will also allow for more of the circuit to be used for invites.

The tournament itself will run from November 24 to December 13, with $1,250,000 on the line and 32 teams competing for their share of the prize, and this time around, there will be a third-place decider on the final day of play before the best-of-five grand final.

PGL also announced some administrative deadlines for the qualifying teams, with November 6 being the final date to confirm all team details and November 9 being the deadline for the Tournament Item Submission process.
