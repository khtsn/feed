Short news: Week 26

In a Telegram post, BetBoom CEO Artem "Pioneer" Saveliev shut down the possibility of his organization signing Timur "⁠FL4MUS⁠" Marev on a permanent deal from Virtus.pro.

FL4MUS played several events for the team on loan, most recently the IEM Cologne Major, where BetBoom reached the playoffs after coming through three Swiss stages.

"The most common question we get: 'When are you buying Flamus?'" he wrote. "My answer: not this year.

"Signing a player is always a complex decision — roles, map positions, individual skill, personal qualities, mentality, synergy with the rest of the team, and of course the buyout fee.

"The coaching staff tries to fully assess what a signing gives us now and over the long run. And at this point, buying Timur doesn't seem like an obvious option for us."
