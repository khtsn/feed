Falcons vs NAVI headlines day four of Stage 3

After squeezing past Monte, Falcons now face a tougher test against NAVI

The matchups for the fourth round of IEM Cologne's Stage 3 have been set after a third day of action that saw FURIA and Spirit lock in their playoff spots.

In the 2-1 pool, all eyes will be on the clash between Falcons and Natus Vincere. The two teams haven't faced each other since the group stage of BLAST Open Rotterdam, when NAVI ran out 2-1 winners to book their place in the playoffs.
