Liquid struggle past M80 to stay alive at EPL

Liquid have struggled past M80 in a three-map series (Anubis 13-3, Inferno 11-13, Mirage 16-13) to eliminate Elias "⁠s1n⁠" Stein and company from ESL Pro League S23.

The series was far from comfortable for Liquid, despite the fact that they started the series by romping to an 11-0 lead on Anubis before M80 finally got on the board. The German-American lineup was able to avoid a 13-0 disaster, but they could only muster three rounds on their map pick.

Inferno was more tightly contested, but Liquid kept up their momentum to cruise to an 8-4 opening half, as M80 weren't able to get their economy under control after struggling to string together more than two rounds.

s1n's troops fought back with aplomb to win the map in the final round of regulation, when Michael "⁠Swisher⁠" Schmid won a 1vs2 after Liquid allowed the round to descend into chaos after several individual mishaps.

The Mirage decider was an equally cagey affair kept in the balance by Jonathan "⁠EliGE⁠" Jablonowski's and Guy "⁠NertZ⁠" Iluz's clutches, with Liquid eventually sputtering to a 16-13 victory despite fumbling a seemingly unlosable 2vs5 situation in the penultimate round of regulation.

Liquid now require just one more victory to make Stage 2 and recover an ESL Pro League S23 campaign that got off to a terrible start with losses to Gaimin Gladiators and Passion UA and save their Cologne Major qualification chances in the process.
