IEM Cologne Major Stage 1 round 4 matchups set

GamerLegion and BIG will fight for a spot in Stage 2 while Liquid and HEROIC face off to avoid elimination.

The penultimate matchups of IEM Cologne Major Stage 1 have been set following the conclusion of day two, which saw BetBoom and B8 secure advancement while SINNERS and Gaimin Gladiators' Major hopes went up in smoke.

It's all best-of-threes for the remainder of this stage as we head into the 2-1 qualifying and 1-2 elimination games, which will see three more teams earn Stage 2 berths and three others eliminated.

The headline brawl of the 2-1 pool pits GamerLegion up against BIG, with the former looking to bounce back from a dismal match against BetBoom and the latter hoping to advance at their first opportunity after beating THUNDER dOWNUNDER in the 1-1 pool.

MIBR and Lynn Vision will also fight for a Stage 2 spot alongside M80 and NRG, while the elimination pool will kick off with an Australian derby between FlyQuest and THUNDER dOWNUNDER. TYLOO and Sharks will simultaneously play to avoid elimination before the day ends with a battle between Liquid and HEROIC, who are both looking to recover from dreary starts to their Major campaigns.
