M80 replace Liquid at Gamers Assembly amidst tight battle for Americas invite to Cologne

M80 has replaced Liquid at Gamers Assembly Festival, a $17,379 LAN in France scheduled for April 4-5. The event is among a raft of LANs slated to conclude just before the April 6 cut-off for invites to the IEM Cologne Major, with M80 also taking part in DraculaN Season 6 from March 29 - April 2.

The American organization is embroiled in a tight battle for one of the last Americas invites to Cologne, and the team's odds took a turn for the worse after they were knocked out of ROG Journey Spring by HEROIC Academy mere weeks after a group stage exit at Roman Imperium Cup VI.

Attending Gamers Assembly, alongside having a good result at DraculaN, would go a long way in boosting M80's Major hopes as they are just barely clinging on to an invite on our prediction page. But the decision also means the team will have to withdraw from PGL Bucharest, which is scheduled to run from April 4-11, in order to attend.

Liquid have withdrawn from the tournament after finishing fourth at Roman Imperium Cup VI and placing 9-12th at BLAST Open Rotterdam, but are still not guaranteed their Major spot. The team will have a chance to do so at DraculaN Season 6, where they will take on Inner Circle in their opening match of the double-elimination playoff bracket. M80 have been drawn against BASEMENT BOYS, and would play Liquid in the next round if both teams secure victory.
