BIG pause EQUIPA project

The core of 2025's team of the year has left their organization.

The BIG EQUIPA project has been "paused for the foreseeable future," the German organization has announced.

The shuttering of EQUIPA follows similar exits from NIP Impact and Imperial Valkyries at the start of the year since the end of ESL Impact.

As ESL Impact is put on hold, women's CS faces an uncertain future

When they signed Mia "⁠aiM⁠" Cooper and Sofia "⁠sosya⁠" Vasileva in January, BIG CGO Roman Reinhardt said that the org would "continue" with a women's team "as long as we can stand behind it responsibly."

But with a lack of European women's events so far this year — JB Pro League Female S1 was the only such event BIG EQUIPA attended in 2026 — the project ran out of road.

Team Manager Dominic "AlphaHirsch" said on X: "We wanted to hold on and believed that tournaments were coming, like everyone had said and promised. For five months, we waited and hoped, played male tournaments, showed ourselves, talked with all the big and small TOs… but everyone was just talking…

"To be fair to our amazing players, we have decided to pause the project so they can get different sponsors for their streams and earn more money, which they all deserve."
