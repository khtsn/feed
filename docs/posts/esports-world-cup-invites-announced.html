Esports World Cup invites announced

The 28 teams invited to Paris have been revealed following the June 1 VRS update.

Esports World Cup has revealed the 28 teams invited to Paris for the third iteration of the $2,000,000 championship on its website.

Every eligible team accepted invites to the event, including previous champions Natus Vincere and The MongolZ, with the latter receiving a regional invite alongside M80, FURIA, Legacy, TYLOO, and Wildcard.

Lynn Vision earned a spot by winning Hero Esports Asian Champions League, and four more teams are set to round out the list through the Esports World Cup Open Qualifier, set to play out on LAN in Paris from August 7-9, just days before the main event (August 12-23).

It will be the first time Esports World Cup takes place outside of Riyadh, Saudi Arabia after the $75 million festival was moved to France in response to the ongoing conflict in Iran.
