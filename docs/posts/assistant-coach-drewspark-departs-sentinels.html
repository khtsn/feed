DrewSpark first joined Sentinels as an analyst following the 2022 season, but was soon promoted to assistant coach following the dismissal of then-head coach SyykoNT . The organization had a disappointing 2023 season that saw them finish 7th in the 2023 Americas League and miss out on playoffs.

That form would continue at the Americas Last Chance Qualifier, where they were eliminated in the lower bracket after posting a 1-2 record.
