FUT move past Astralis to make EPL S23 Stage 2

FUT qualified for the ESL Pro League Season 23 Stage 2 with a 3-0 record after defeating Astralis in a two-map series (Overpass 13-3, Mirage 13-11).

Overpass was comfortable throughout for FUT, who put together a 9-3 CT side before winning the second-half pistol, which allowed them to sail to a 13-3 victory.

Mirage started in the same vein as FUT ran away to a 6-1 lead, but some choice words by Casper "⁠ruggah⁠" Due and Rasmus "⁠HooXi⁠" Nielsen during their struggles helped them win eight in a row to take a solid lead late on.

FUT rallied on their home map, however, and took control on their defensive side with the help of some hard-hitting retakes to win the map 13-11 and secure their spot in Stage 2.

Krabeni shows the talent runs deep in FUT

Much has been said about the star riflers of FUT, but the match against Astralis was Aulon "⁠Krabeni⁠" Fazlija's time to shine.

The young IGL put up a 2.12 rating on Overpass and finished the job on Mirage with a last-second defuse to put a bow on a fantastic performance.

The result means that FUT continue their hot run at the start of 2025, becoming the first team to make it through Stage 1 after moving past M80, Passion UA, and Astralis.
