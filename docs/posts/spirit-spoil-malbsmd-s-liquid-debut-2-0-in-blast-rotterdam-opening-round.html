Spirit spoil malbsMd's Liquid debut 2-0 in BLAST Rotterdam opening round

donk was at his best, topping the scoreboard with a 1.79 rating in the two-map victory.

Spirit are through to the next round of the BLAST Open Rotterdam Group B upper bracket after beating Liquid 2-0 in their debut with Mario "⁠malbsMd⁠" Samayoa.

The series kicked off on Mirage, where a 13-8 scoreline makes it look closer than it was, as Spirit's dominance was never truly put to the test in a map in which Roland "⁠ultimate⁠" Tomkowiak went missing and malbsMd could not provide any honeymoon magic.

Danil "⁠donk⁠" Kryshkovets, with a 1.99 rating, was the story on Mirage as he showed his best with 119.7 ADR and +7.87% in the effort to steal Liquid's map pick, which after a 9-3 start on the CT side was closed out 13-8.

donk continued to perform on Ancient, where he was once again the tip of the spear with a 1.63 rating, even with Liquid's efforts to keep it closer on Ancient than they did on Mirage, reaching the half 6-6 before swapping to the attack.

Spirit quickly got up to an 11-6 lead after the switch, but Liquid didn't let go and brought it all the way to within one, 11-10. That's when Boris "⁠magixx⁠" Vorobiev punished poor play in a 1v2 by Jonathan "⁠EliGE⁠" Jablonowski and Keith "⁠NAF⁠" Markovic to set up match point. Liquid hit back the following round, but it was in vain as Spirit closed it out in the final round of regulation.

ultimate, who had gone missing on Mirage, was a big part of Liquid's competitiveness on Ancient as he turned his play around with a 1.33 rating in the second map.
