Matchups set for final group stage games in Cluj

Day four of PGL Cluj-Napoca is done and dusted, seeing MOUZ, The MongolZ and FURIA earn their playoff berths whilst FaZe, B8 and HEROIC were sent home.

There is still one more day of group play left in Romania with three playoff spots on the line, and some big names are left scrapping for one of those final tickets to the next stage.

The headline game will see Natus Vincere taking on G2. Natus Vincere suffered a surprise loss to The MongolZ to end up in this position, whilst G2 have had to fight from an 0-2 record to get themselves their shot at the playoffs.

Young upstarts FUT will be facing off against another youthful side in PARIVISION, although the latter have veteran Dzhami "⁠Jame⁠" Ali to lean on, and the other game sees Aurora taking on Astralis.
