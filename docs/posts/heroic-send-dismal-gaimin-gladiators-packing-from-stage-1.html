HEROIC send dismal Gaimin Gladiators packing from Stage 1

Chr1zN's squad were in control throughout the series

HEROIC have sent Gaimin Gladiators home from IEM Cologne Major, easing past the Brazilians in comfortable 2-0 fashion. The victory snaps HEROIC's seven-series losing streak, whilst Gaimin Gladiators saw a dire Major campaign end with a whimper.

The result is not surprising considering Gaimin Gladiators form thus far this Major, and they now end their run in Cologne with a mere 16 rounds across four maps of play.

Considering the sudden removal of João "⁠felps⁠" Vasconcellos prior to the tournament perhaps this run is understandable, but the nature of their defeats was still surprising considering the calibre of some of their players.

Both maps were largely one-way traffic, Gaimin Gladiators finding themselves outclassed throughout. Their CT sides largely relied on guesswork gambles, the individual firepower was lacking, and even when they did find man-up scenarios, they managed to squander them.
