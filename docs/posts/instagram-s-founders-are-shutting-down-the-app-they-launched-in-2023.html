Instagram's founders are shutting down the app they launched in 2023

https://www.theverge.com/2024/1/12/24036539/artifact-shutting-down-kevin-systrom Artifact, the news app created by Instagram co-founders Kevin Systrom and Mike Krieger, is shutting down just a year after launch. The app used an AI-driven approach to suggest news that users might like to read.
