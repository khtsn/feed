G2 Esports adds Robert as performance coach

G2 Esports G2 Esports North America Rank #2 jawgemo Alexander Mor BABYBAY Andrej Francisty trent Trent Cairns valyn Jacob Batio leaf Nathan Orf has signed Robert "Robert" Yip as a performance coach for the 2026 season.

While he is a new face to Valorant, Robert has been well-traveled in the Esports scene, having been in coaching booths for the last 14 years in League of Legends. He most recently coached for European squad Citadel Eterna, but has also coached for Immortals and FlyQuest in the past.

G2's 2025 season was up-and-down, starting with a trophy-lifting run at Americas Kickoff and a runner-up finish at Masters Bangkok, then another regional title in Stage 1 followed by a fourth-place finish at Masters Toronto. Ahead of Stage 2, leaf stepped down from the active roster due to a medical condition, resulting in BABYBAY being signed as a stand-in. leaf returned in time for the Stage 2, which saw G2 complete a regional triple crown, sweeping all three Americas titles. The year ended at Champions Paris on a bit of a sour note, with the team finishing in the playoffs at 7th-8th.
