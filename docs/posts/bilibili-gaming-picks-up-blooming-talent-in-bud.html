BiliBili Gaming picks up blooming talent in bud

Bilibili Gaming Bilibili Gaming China Rank #3 whzy Wang Haozhe (王昊哲) Levius Lu Yinzhong (卢胤中) rushia Wang Xiaojie (王晓杰) nephh Marcus Tan Knight Liu Yuxiang (刘宇翔) bud Yang Renyu (杨韧余) has picked up Yang "bud" Renyu (杨韧余) to expand to a six-man roster ahead of China Kickoff.

bud joins BLG looking to make his debut at the VCT level. Between China Stage 2 and Champions Paris, he competed for BiliBili at China Evolution Series: Act 3. In the three sanctioned matches he's played in his career, he has played the Controller, Duelist, and Initiator roles evenly on Viper, Yoru, and Breach.

In 2025, BiliBili had arguably its most successful year in the franchising era. After missing out on the first international event of the year, a second-place finish in Stage 1 qualified the team for Masters Toronto, the first-ever Masters event in the organization's history. Returning to regional play, BiliBili set another milestone, reaching the top of the Chinese pyramid for the first time ever and lifting the Stage 2 trophy. The trophy-lifting sequence qualified BLG for its third-straight Champions event, but while the event was one to forget with a group stage elimination, it capped off a successful year.

Since the end of the season, BLG has been relatively quiet in the transfer market. On the playing front, bud's addition has been the only change.
