From a 0-2 group stage start to a blazing lower bracket playoff run, Heretics at long last redeemed their second place woes within the VCT and earned a title in their sixth grand final appearance.

Both teams traded blows in the five-map nailbiter; Vitality won Breeze 13-10, Heretics eked out Lotus 15-13, Vitality took Pearl 13-7, and Heretics won Split 13-11 and Haven 15-13.

Heretics will take a well-earned break in the form of a bye to the Masters London playoffs as the EMEA first seed. Vitality, felled at the final hurdle, will have to go through the Swiss stage.
