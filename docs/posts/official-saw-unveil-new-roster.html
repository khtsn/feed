Official: SAW unveil new roster

SAW have announced that they have completed the signing of a brand new roster featuring the core of their lineup that reached the semi-finals of IEM Cologne 2024, confirming a June 30 HLTV report.

The Portuguese organization has signed Christopher "⁠MUTiRiS⁠" Fernandes from BC.Game, João "⁠story⁠" Vieira from FlyQuest, and Michel "⁠ewjerkz⁠" Pinto from Ninjas in Pyjamas. ewjerkz has spent the last seven months on the sidelines following his December benching by NIP.

Like MUTiRiS, Adones "⁠krazy⁠" Nobre has been picked up from BC.Game. A former SAW player himself, he spent six months with the Portuguese organization before BC.Game acquired their core at the start of 2026, securing international event invites in the process.

MUTiRiS returns to SAW, his home between 2020 and 2025

Rounding out the roster is Filipe "⁠NOPEEj⁠" Dias, who had been with Rebels since September 2025. He has been a fixture of the Portuguese scene for close to a decade, notably finishing second at the 2024 IESF World Championship with Rhyno alongside krazy.

Meanwhile, SAW have announced that the lineup they have fielded since selling their core to BC.Game will remain with the organization under the name SAW Youngsters, with head coach Tiago "⁠mowzassa⁠" Rodrigues and assistant Tiago "⁠SYDOX⁠" Fonseca transitioning to the main roster.

SAW's new team will make its first appearance on Sunday in the West European Open Qualifier for BLAST Open Fall. Next month, the team will travel to Paris to compete in the Esports World Cup's Open Qualifier.

SAW are now:

Christopher "⁠MUTiRiS⁠" Fernandes

Tiago "⁠mowzassa⁠" Rodrigues (coach)
