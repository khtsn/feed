paiN send TYLOO packing from CAC

paiN have sent TYLOO packing from CS Asia Championships after a 2-1 victory in Group A's lower bracket.

The Brazilian team now has a match against MOUZ standing between them and securing playoffs for the second straight event, following their 5-6th exit at IEM Atlanta.

TYLOO began their home event with a best-of-one win over MOUZ, but couldn't qualify for the playoffs through the upper bracket after two narrow overtime losses to Legacy. Although they won a map against paiN, the elimination series was less convincing as TYLOO stumbled on their Mirage pick against Vinicius "⁠vsm⁠" Moreira and João "⁠snow⁠" Vinicius, with the Brazilians drawing first blood with a 13-5 win.

A bounce back on Nuke, which Dongkai "⁠Jee⁠" Ji closed out in style with a 1v3 clutch, extended the series and forced an Overpass decider, where paiN benefitted from their pistol win and took a slim 7-5 lead at the break. TYLOO couldn't stabilize after the swap, losing an early 1v2 to Guilherme "⁠piriajr⁠" Barbosa before Rafael "⁠saffee⁠" Costa got his team over the line with an AWP ace to send the Chinese side packing.
