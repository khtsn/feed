BuZz signed with Vision Strikers in 2021, entering the spotlight of Korean Valorant. In his first season with the team, he competed at Masters Berlin and Champions 2021. After Vision Strikers was acquired by DRX the following season, BuZz reached Masters Reykjavik and Masters Copenhagen, and then finished third at Champions 2022.

BuZz has reached the last three Champions events as part of DRX's roster, and T1 will be looking to return with him in their squad.
