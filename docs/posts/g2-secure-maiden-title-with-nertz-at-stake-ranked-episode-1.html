G2 secure maiden title with NertZ at Stake Ranked Episode 1

G2 have been crowned champions of Stake Ranked Episode 1 following a 2-1 victory over BetBoom in the grand final.

Nemanja "⁠huNter-⁠" Kovač's side went undefeated in the eight-team, double-elimination tournament bracket, beating Nemesis, GamerLegion, and BetBoom (twice) to secure their first title with Guy "⁠NertZ⁠" Iluz.

He was G2's highest-rated player heading into the final, but took more of a backseat as Matúš "⁠MATYS⁠" Šimko and Nikita "⁠HeavyGod⁠" Martynenko made the difference in a tight game. BetBoom converted Anubis with a strong offense to kick off the series, but costly mistakes on Ancient — including HeavyGod getting away with a 1v3 ninja defuse — saw G2 narrowly secure a 13-11 win to force a decider.

HeavyGod and MATYS carried that momentum into Dust2, with G2 up 7-2 before BetBoom began to stabilize. Neither team could string together rounds at the start of the second half as force-buy wars raged, with a triple from MATYS finally breaking the back-and-forth to help G2 secure the title.

BetBoom will lament the way the series ended, and frustration was evidently mounting towards the end of the match with several costly errors and squandered advantages letting a potential win slip away. Although a runner-up finish was already more than enough for the Russian team to secure an invite to the IEM Cologne Major, a win would have put them straight through to Stage 2.

Still, Kirill "⁠Boombl4⁠" Mikhailov's men can walk away more than happy after a gauntlet run of three back-to-back LANs, despite playing the events with two stand-ins, saw them go from outside Major invite range to locking in their spot.
