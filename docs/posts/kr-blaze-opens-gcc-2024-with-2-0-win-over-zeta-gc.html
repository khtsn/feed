KRÜ BLAZE opens GCC 2024 with 2-0 win over ZETA GC

KRÜ BLAZE has defeated ZETA DIVISION GC 2-0 in the inaugural Game Changers 2024 Championship match. KRÜ will next play against G2 Gozen, the 2022 Game Changers Champions and 2023 bronze medalists. ZETA, meanwhile, descends to the lower bracket, awaiting the rest of the opening matches for their opponents.

The match began on Bind, where KRÜ claimed the lead and pushed their advantage to seal the map 13-9. ZETA looked to do the same in map 2, Ascent, but KRÜ was able to overcome their round deficit, winning 13-9 and closing the series 2-0.

One of three teams in Game Changers Championship history to participate in every iteration of the event, the victory is KRÜ BLAZE's first-ever opening match win.
