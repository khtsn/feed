100 Thieves bench Ag1l

29-6-2026 18:26

The Portuguese rifler is the first player to depart the organization's new-look international roster.

100 Thieves have announced the benching of André "⁠Ag1l⁠" Gil, who moves to inactivity after six months of service.

"Roster moves are never easy, and these hit especially hard. After internal evaluation, we made the call to make changes this offseason," Sean "⁠seang@res⁠" Gares, 100 Thieves' Head of FPS, said on X.

A week ago, HLTV reported that the organization is locked in talks with Monte to sign Jack "⁠Gizmy⁠" von Spreckelsen, who would take over the IGLing while Håvard "⁠rain⁠" Nygaard transitioned to a different role within the team.
