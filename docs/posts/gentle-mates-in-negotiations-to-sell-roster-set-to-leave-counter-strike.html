Gentle Mates in negotiations to sell roster; set to leave Counter-Strike

Spain's top side is back on the market, with their AWPer the subject of interest from FaZe.

Gentle Mates are leaving Counter-Strike, according to statements by Corentin "Gotaga" Houssein, one of the organization's co-founders.

Sebastien "⁠KRL⁠" Perez, who broke the news, suggested three Spanish organizations as a possible landing spot for the team now sitting in 34th place on the VRS ranking: Heretics, KOI, and GiantX.

The French Counter-Strike streamer and commentator also posted about FaZe's interest in signing Antonio "⁠MartinezSa⁠" Martinez.

HLTV can confirm FaZe's interest in MartinezSa, but the sale of M8s' star player would affect the sale of the roster as a whole, and therefore puts his cost at a premium.

Talks with FaZe for the sniper are currently underway but at an impasse, with the American and French organizations unable to come to terms over a price.

HLTV also learned that FaZe have not made a final decision on what player or players will be replaced, and are keeping their options open depending on what opportunities they can pursue during the off-season.
