4 Killed After Minivan Crashes Into Long Island Nail Salon

New York|4 Killed After Minivan Crashes Into Long Island Nail Salon

https://www.nytimes.com/2024/06/28/nyregion/long-island-deerpark-salon-crash.html

Officials did not immediately say what caused the crash, in Deer Park. Nine people were also injured.

June 28, 2024, 8:05 p.m. ET

Four people were killed and nine were injured after a minivan crashed into a nail salon on Long Island Friday afternoon, the authorities said.

Shortly after 4:30 p.m., a minivan crashed into Hawaii Nail and Spa on Grand Boulevard in Deer Park, a hamlet in the town of Babylon, N.Y., according to according to Mark Stone, the public information officer for the Deer Park Fire Department. One injured person was in serious condition Friday evening and was transported by helicopter to Stony Brook University Hospital, he said.

The others sustained “significant injuries,” Mr. Stone said, and were taken to local hospitals.

The Suffolk County Police Department is investigating what caused the crash, he said.

Nine agencies responded to the crash, including fire departments from surrounding towns, and there were 150 firefighters and emergency medical responders on the scene, Mr. Stone said.

“We come, we pick up the pieces, we clear the scene,” he said.

Hannah Hardt, a manager at a pet store across the road from the salon, said that she saw about 10 ambulances nearby and two helicopters circling overhead. Grand Boulevard was closed for several blocks around the salon Friday evening, and around two dozen police officers were still at the scene just before 8 p.m.
