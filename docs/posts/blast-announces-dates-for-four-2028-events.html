BLAST announces dates for four 2028 events

BLAST have announced dates for four events in the first half of 2028.

It has also announced that it will host two additional events in the calendar year, with more details to be revealed "at a later stage."

The news follows the operator's reworking of its 2027 offering away from its Frequent Flyer programme, allowing teams to skip more of its events without incurring financial penalties.

That move was a hint at BLAST's declining importance for top teams after PGL put four events in the same time slot in 2027.

In 2028, too, BLAST will have to compete for top talent, as three of their four announced events overlap with competitors' events.

By committing to 12 events over 2027 and 2028, however, the Danish tournament organizers are demonstrating that their commitment to the scene will remain strong despite the increased competition.
