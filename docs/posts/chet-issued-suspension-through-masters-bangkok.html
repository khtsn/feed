Chet issued suspension through Masters Bangkok

Former NRG Head Coach Chet "Chet" Singh has been suspended from professional play until the end of Masters Bangkok on March 3, 2025, for violating the Riot Games Global Esports Code of Conduct.

According to Riot's competitive ruling, Chet breached provision 3.21, Confidential Information, by "broadcasting VoDs whose access was contingent on current status as head coach of a VCT team. His access to the content was unauthorized, and its distribution on July 31, 2024, constituted a breach of proprietary information. He did not secure permission from Riot Games, NRG, and the other teams involved prior to broadcasting publicly inaccessible VoDs and communications to his personal channels." Following section 4.10, Team Sanctions and the Role of Team Leadership, a suspension was imposed, weighing the severity and circumstances of the case.

Following the ruling's release, Chet announced that he would focus on content creation until the suspension was lifted in early March.
