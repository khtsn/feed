PGL Astana Closed Qualifier invites announced

PGL has announced the invites for their regional closed qualifiers for PGL Astana, set to get underway on March 31.

In Europe, 100 Thieves are the biggest name in attendance, receiving the invite after rising to No. 35 in the VRS. They are joined by HOTU, illwill, and Cologne Major hopefuls K27.

The four squads will be joined by four teams to progress through the two open qualifiers, the first of which will begin on March 22.

Europe invites:

HOTU

100 Thieves

In the Americas, the qualifier has been split into NA and SA, with each awarding one spot at PGL Astana.

LAG and Zomblers headline the invites for North America, while 9z and ODDIK are among the invites for the South American region.

North America invites:

LAG

FarmVille

South America invites:

9z

LP

The Asian qualifier will be played across three regions, namely Oceania, West Asia, and East Asia. Each of the qualifiers will feature four teams, two invited and two from the open qualifiers, with the winners of the three regions then facing off for the only spot on offer.

Asia invites:

SemperFi (Oceania)

Mindfreak (Oceania)
