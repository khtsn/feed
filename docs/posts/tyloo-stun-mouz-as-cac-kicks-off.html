TYLOO stun MOUZ as CAC kicks off

TYLOO have defeated MOUZ in their opening best-of-one matchup to kick off their run at CS Asia Championships 2026.

Jingxiang "⁠Mercury⁠" Wang's troops dominated MOUZ on Inferno, racking up a 10-2 lead on the offense as MOUZ struggled in their site holds and retakes. MOUZ gave themselves a lifeline with the T pistol, but they were unable to prevent TYLOO from snagging the final three rounds they needed to clinch the map 13-7.

Another notable match from the first round of the Chinese event saw B8 take down Ninjas in Pyjamas on Ancient. The Ukrainian side came back from a 0-4 deficit caused by a stunning Rasmus "⁠sjuush⁠" Beck 1v5 clutch in the pistol round, followed not long after by an ace from Kacper "⁠xKacpersky⁠" Gabara.

B8 rallied with a force-buy victory that gave them the momentum to take the half 7-5. Ninjas in Pyjamas gave themselves a foothold with a pistol win on the defense, but B8 stabilized to take the map 13-9, powered by a strong showing from Andrii "⁠npl⁠" Kukharskyi.

Other results include PARIVISION shipping a Mario "⁠malbsMd⁠" Samayoa-less Liquid to the lower bracket, MIBR taking down 3DMAX on Anubis, and The MongolZ annihilating Lynn Vision 13-3.
