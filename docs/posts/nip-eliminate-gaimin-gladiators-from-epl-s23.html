NIP eliminate Gaimin Gladiators from EPL S23

r1nkle starred for NIP to bring them within one series of advancing to Stage 2.

Ninjas in Pyjamas have continued their march towards a Stage 2 berth in ESL Pro League Season 23 at the expense of Gaimin Gladiators following a two-map series victory in the 1-2 pool.

The Brazilians came alive late in both halves of their Dust2 pick, recovering from 1-5 and 7-11 deficits, but were robbed of a win after losing to Glocks in round 13, fumbling a map point to let NIP force overtime, and giving up back-to-back 5v3s in the extra rounds.

Artem "⁠r1nkle⁠" Moroz and Artem "⁠cairne⁠" Mushynskyi topped the server for Ninjas in Pyjamas with 1.50 and 1.29 ratings, respectively, and the Ukrainian sniper took over on Nuke to ensure a blowout victory to end the series.

He decimated Gaimin Gladiators with a 24-4 K-D, seven multi-kills, and a 2.67 rating, putting NIP through to the 2-2 pool for their first and only chance to advance to Stage 2.
