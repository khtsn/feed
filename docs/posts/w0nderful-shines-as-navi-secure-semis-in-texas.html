w0nderful shines as NAVI secure semis in Texas

Natus Vincere have advanced directly to the semi-finals of BLAST Rivals Fort Worth after a confident 2-0 win over GamerLegion in the Group B seeding match (13-10 Mirage, 13-4 Ancient).

Despite the first map's scoreline looking close, a Natus Vincere victory never really looked in doubt. With Ihor "⁠w0nderful⁠" Zhdanov and Drin "⁠makazze⁠" Shaqiri both firing on all cylinders, GamerLegion had trouble finding a consistent way to win rounds, relying on a few heroic moments on map one and withering away against a menacing makazze on Ancient.

w0nderful tallied his highest-rated map of the year to kick off the series, and alongside makazze, routed GamerLegion in a lopsided end to the series. The result sets up a picture-perfect playoff bracket with Vitality and Natus Vincere both through to the semi-finals on opposite sides of the bracket, setting up the potential title decider showdown that Aleksi "⁠Aleksib⁠" Virolainen is keen for.

w0nderful was in otherworldly form on Mirage, tallying seven multi-kills in the first half alone as Natus Vincere raced to an early lead. A few heroic moments from Janusz "⁠Snax⁠" Pogorzelski and a clutch from Milan "⁠hypex⁠" Polowiec helped GamerLegion stop the rot and tie the game at 5-5, but w0nderful and makazze continued to shine in openers (ending the half 9-0) to give NAVI a lead at the break.

A pistol and conversion extended that gap to 9-5 before GamerLegion turned an eco round, and a 1v3 clutch from Sebastian "⁠Tauson⁠" Tauson Lindelof two rounds later clawed away at the deficit. But again, w0nderful stepped up with a 1v2 clutch (after Aleksib got three in 3v5), and set his team on map point a few rounds later with a flashy AWP quad-kill to help seal victory.

The makazze-w0nderful duo was even more formidable on Ancient, with the Kosovar rifler living up to his reputation on the map as he dominated GamerLegion round after round. He ended the half with 15 frags and was well ahead of his teammates for much of their offensive effort, with w0nderful catching up at the end of the half with an AK-47 ace.

The Ukrainian sniper added a triple kill with the Dualies after the swap to leave GamerLegion no chance to fight back, and Valeriy "⁠b1t⁠" Vakhovskiy tallied a few multis of his own to strike down repeated force-buy efforts to seal the victory.
