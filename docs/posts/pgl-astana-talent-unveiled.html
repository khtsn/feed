PGL Astana talent unveiled

PGL has announced the full talent lineup for the PGL Astana 2026 broadcast ahead of the event's imminent start, revealing a 15-person team of well-known faces that are set to bring the action to life from May 9-17.

Desk host duties go to two PGL regulars, Richard "⁠Richard Lewis⁠" Lewis and Sam "⁠Tech Girl⁠" Wright, whilst stage hosting duties will be taken up by James "⁠BanKs⁠" Banks.

Sudhen "⁠Bleh⁠" Wahengbam, Alex "⁠Mauisnake⁠" Ellenberg and Dustin "⁠dusT⁠" Mouret are set to serve as analysts on the broadcast and they will be joined by none other than HLTV's own Zvonimir "⁠Professeur⁠" Burazin, who has become a regular feature at PGL events.

There are four casting pairs ready to lend their voices to the games, with two operating on-site and two casting remotely. Mohan "⁠launders⁠" Govindasamy and Conner "⁠Scrawny⁠" Girvan are on-site along with Harry "⁠JustHarry⁠" Russell and Hugo "⁠Hugo⁠" Byron, whilst Jason "⁠moses⁠" O'Toole and Adam "⁠Dinko⁠" Hawthorne will be remote as well as Anders "⁠Anders⁠" Blume and Vince "⁠Vince⁠" Hill, who makes his first notable broadcast appearance in over 12 months.
