hally to miss IEM Rio and PGL Astana

Spirit head coach Sergey "⁠hally⁠" Shavaev will miss the team's next two events, IEM Rio and PGL Astana, the Russian organization announced on Thursday.

In a statement, Spirit explained that hally is temporarily stepping down from the role as he is currently "battling certain health issues."

"Therefore we have decided to give him some time off, so that hally can come back healthy and charged up for IEM Cologne Major preparations," Spirit said.

Spirit Academy coach Dmitry "⁠S0tF1k⁠" Forostyanko will be guiding the team in hally's absence. The 31-year-old worked with Danil "⁠donk⁠" Kryshkovets and Myroslav "⁠zont1x⁠" Plakhotia before the pair was promoted to Spirit's main team in July 2023.

Spirit recently attended BLAST Open Rotterdam, where they were eliminated in the group stage following losses to PARIVISION and The MongolZ.
