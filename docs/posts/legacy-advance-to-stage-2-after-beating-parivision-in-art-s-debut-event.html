Legacy advance to Stage 2 after beating PARIVISION in arT's debut event

Legacy have defeated PARIVISION with a 2-0 scoreline (Inferno 13-11, Dust2 13-10) to make the ESL Pro League Season 23 Stage 2 with a flawless 3-0 record.

The match against Dzhami "⁠Jame⁠" Ali and company was a hard-fought affair as usual, with both maps going the distance in classic PARIVISION fashion.

The Russian squad got off to a good start on their Inferno offense but fell apart on their T side, being unable to handle Bruno "⁠latto⁠" Rebelatto's multi-fragging and Andrei "⁠arT⁠" Piovezan's aggression.

A similar fate awaited them on Dust2, where they once again put together an 8-4 first half before faltering late on, with a few uncharacteristic misplays sending them to the 2-1 pool.

arT career has been a turbulent one since the Brazilian IGL departed FURIA in 2024, but he seemed to have revitalized a Legacy team after finally getting some better pieces to work with.
