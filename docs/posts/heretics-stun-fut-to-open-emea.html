Heretics stun FUT to open EMEA

The cards were stacked against Heretics from the start. They came into the year as an EMEA bottom feeder that floundered in the offseason, had to use a substitute because one of their star acquisitions is too young to play, and had one player forced to play remotely due to visa issues came into their match as massive underdogs against an internationally competitive FUT team that picked up cNed in the offseason.

In the end, Heretics stunned the entire league with a 2-0 upset. The Lithuanian Boo brothers dominated the server, with the elder Boo showing off both aim and brain to start off his redemption arc from a disastrous 2023 season.

Heretics will face the winner of Giants and Karmine Corp for a spot in the EMEA playoffs while FUT have been relegated to the losers bracket where they will have to battle just to escape into the Play-In stage.
