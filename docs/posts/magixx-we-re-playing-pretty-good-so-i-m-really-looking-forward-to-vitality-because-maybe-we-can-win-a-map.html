Spirit are bound for their first grand final in eight months, their first since Boris "⁠magixx⁠" Vorobiev took control of the captaincy at the end of 2025.

Despite missing out on the presence of coach Sergey "⁠hally⁠" Shavaev in IEM Rio, Spirit ushered in a complete turnaround from their disastrous 0-2 loss to Falcons in the group stage, securing revenge against the international side in the semi-finals with a lopsided 2-0 win.

With Spirit Academy coach Dmitry "⁠S0tF1k⁠" Forostyanko in tow, Spirit are now preparing for a best-of-five grand final against Vitality, who are on the hunt for their second ESL Grand Slam. Are you finding it hard to become that figure? You've been around a while, but you're also still pretty young.
