B8 beat Legacy 2-0 to secure a playoff spot in Bucharest

B8 have earned a playoff berth and secured a top eight finish at PGL Bucharest after taking Legacy out 2-0 in the Swiss group stage's final day of play.

B8 went down 1-2 with an opening win against Wildcard and losses to Astralis and 3DMAX before beating Inner Circle to secure a spot in the 2-2 deciders.

The Ukrainians were never in real trouble in the decider match against Legacy, even though it started on the Brazilian team's Dust2 pick. On the CT side, Andrii "⁠npl⁠" Kukharskyi and company cruised to a 9-3 CT half.

B8 hit double digits in the second pistol round, but would have to wait to seal the deal as they lost to the forcebuy in the second round, needing a whole ten rounds in the half to end the map 13-9.

The second map started in very much the same fashion, and with the same scoreline, as the Ukrainians went up 9-3, albeit this time on the attack.

"It was very easy, they played very bad," Daniil "⁠s1zzi⁠" Vinnyk told the desk after Ancient. This time around, it was Legacy who won the pistol round and B8 who won the second round forcebuy, going on to close the map 13-4 to secure the spot in the playoffs.

s1zzi, B8's 16-year-old AWPer had a day and was the second-highest rated player on the server with a 1.37 after the two maps, and he sported the highest round swing with +6.46.
