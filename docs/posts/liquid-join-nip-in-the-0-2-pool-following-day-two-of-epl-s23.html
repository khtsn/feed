Liquid join NIP in the 0-2 pool following day two of EPL S23

NRG and SemperFi have also found themselves in the elimination pool.

Day two of ESL Pro League Season 23 has come to an end with Liquid suffering a shock loss to Gaimin Gladiators, while Legacy upset G2 to move one win away from Stage 2.

Senzu impresses despite the loss

Reviews were mixed for Passion UA's signing of Azbayar "⁠Senzu⁠" Munkhbold, but the Mongolian superstar has had a fantastic debut tournament so far.

He averaged a 1.31 rating in their win against Liquid on Sunday and was the shining light in a close loss against FUT today, averaging a 1.44 rating, 0.50 ahead of his closest teammate, Santino "⁠try⁠" Rigal.

His aim was on full display throughout the series, but his Mirage performance was amazing even for his high standards. Senzu put up 28 kills, a +12.76% round swing, and a 2.09 rating to equal the scores, but the match was eventually decided by Aulon "⁠Krabeni⁠" Fazlija's 1vs2 clutch in the last round of regulation on the decider.
