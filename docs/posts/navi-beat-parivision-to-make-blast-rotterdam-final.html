NAVI beat PARIVISION to make BLAST Rotterdam final

Natus Vincere became the second finalists of BLAST Open Rotterdam after getting the better of PARIVISION in a three-map series (Dust 13-11, Inferno 8-13, Mirage 13-7).

The result continues a rich vein of form for the European combine, who recently took home the ESL Pro League S23 trophy, with Drin "⁠makazze⁠" Shaqiri adding an MVP medal to his cabinet.

The Kosovar rifler had another fantastic game on stage, taking over on the Mirage decider and earning himself a 1.60 rating, while Valeriy "⁠b1t⁠" Vakhovskiy topped the series overall with a 1.30.

Natus Vincere's individuals came to play on the series' opener, Dust2, as makazze's Deagle triple and Ihor "⁠w0nderful⁠" Zhdanov's 1vs2 clutch set the tone for the series. A 10-2 record in opening kills was only enough for a 7-5 half, however, and the close nature continued in the second half.

Mihai "⁠iM⁠" Ivan's was Natus Vincere's highest-rated player on the T-side thanks to a crazy triple-kill through the smoke in the dying moments of the game, and while Andrey "⁠BELCHONOKK⁠" Yasinskiy's 1vs2 took the game to the final round of regulation, a well choreographed B take was enough for Natus Vincere's 13-11 win.

Inferno was another close affair, one that was eventually decided by Natus Vincere's misplays. They first walked into Dzhami "⁠Jame⁠" Ali's trademark stack at 8-8 before b1t's whiff cost Natus Vincere in the next round, meaning they petered out in an 8-13 loss on their map pick.

The Mirage decider was mor more or less decided in the first half, as Natus Vincere dominated their way to a 13-7 victory and a spot in their second consecutive grand final.

NAVI earn their first stab at Vitality in 2026

Natus Vincere came into Rotterdam fresh off a title at EPL, but the Swedish tournament notably didn't have Vitality in attendance.

This time around, the juggernauts wait for Aleksi "⁠Aleksib⁠" Virolainen's troops in the grand final in what will be the team's first clash in 2026.

Since 2023, however, the organizations met six times, with Vitality winning all six with a map record of 13 wins and one loss.
