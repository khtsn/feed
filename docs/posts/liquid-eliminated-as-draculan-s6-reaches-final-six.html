Liquid eliminated as DraculaN S6 reaches final six

Day three of DraculaN Season 6 has concluded with several big names suffering significant blows to their IEM Cologne Major aspirations, while others have stoked the flame to keep embers of hope burning as the cut-off nears.

FaZe and Liquid's eliminations were by far the biggest stories of the day, with the former bowing out after back-to-back losses to Passion UA and fnatic to all but confirm their absence from the Major should their schedule not change.

Alliance, who kept themselves in the fight for a Major invite by winning ROG Journey, managed to keep hold of their slim chances by beating Inner Circle and fnatic in the lower bracket. The Swedish team sits two spots below the cut-off on our predicted list, and have been gifted an opportunity to gain an enormous swing win with an upcoming match against Aurora.

The Turkish team had romped past INFINITE and Sashi in the upper bracket, but have found themselves in the lower bracket after M80 came back from 7-10 and 6-10 deficits to reverse-sweep the series in the upper semis. The American-majority team was on the button for an Americas invite heading into DraculaN, but have gone a long way in getting out of the danger zone with their wins over Liquid and Aurora.

Monte, meanwhile, are fighting to hold on to a Stage 2 invite, and may have cemented their place by beating Alliance and Passion UA to march into the upper bracket final. The latter result is one that will sting for Johnny "⁠JT⁠" Theodosiou's troops, who were already well outside of invite range and now need a superb run of results between DraculaN and Urban Riga Open to have any chance at qualifying.
