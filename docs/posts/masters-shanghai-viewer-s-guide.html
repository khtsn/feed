Masters Shanghai viewer’s guide

The 2024 VCT season will continue with Masters Shanghai on May 23. The second international event of the year will feature 12 teams and a $1,000,000 USD prize pool, as well as three championship points for the winner of the event.

Of the twelve teams in attendance, Dragon Ranger Gaming Dragon Ranger Gaming China Rank #3 nizhaoTZH Tang Zehao (唐哲昊) vo0kashu Ilya Ushakov Shion7 Zhang Haozhe (张昊泽) Nicc Shao Yi-qun (邵逸群) TvirusLuke Chen Cheng-ching is the only squad making their international debut. Additionally, 11 of the teams have never won an international event previously, which means either a new team gets their opportunity to lift a trophy, or FNATIC FNATIC Europe Rank #1 Leo Leo Jannesson Derke Nikita Sirmitev Boaster Jake Howlett Chronicle Timofey Khromov Alfajer Emir Ali Beder become the first team in history to do so three times.

Like Masters Madrid, the opening portion of the event will be an eight team Swiss-style format. Teams that get two wins in the system advance to playoffs, while teams that lose twice are eliminated from the tournament. All matches are best-of-three.

The Swiss stage opening matches were randomly drawn, with second seeds playing against a third seed from a different region.

Two teams from each region will battle it out in the Swiss stage. Image: Riot Games

The winner of each region is seeded directly into playoffs. The knockout stage will be an eight team, double elimination bracket. Matches are best-of-three until the lower bracket final and the grand final, which are best-of-five.
