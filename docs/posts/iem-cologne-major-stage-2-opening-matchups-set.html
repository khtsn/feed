IEM Cologne Major Stage 2 opening matchups set

The likes of Spirit, Legacy and G2 join the competition from Saturday.

Spirit have been matched up against BetBoom in round 1

The first round of matches of Stage 2 at the IEM Cologne Major has been set following the conclusion of Stage 1, which saw BIG round out the list off the back of a historic comeback from 0-12 against NRG.

Read more

BIG squeeze past NRG into Stage 2 after 0-12 comeback

Eight new teams are coming in to join the competition, spearheaded by PGL Astana champions Spirit, who have been matched up against BetBoom in the opening round to set up a Russian derby.

A Brazilian one between Legacy and MIBR is another highlight match of the round 1 pairings along with Astralis vs. GamerLegion, who will open the first day of the new stage on Saturday at 12:30 .
