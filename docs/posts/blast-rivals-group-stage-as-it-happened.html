BLAST Rivals group stage as it happened

Reflecting on the hard-fought win against FUT, Vitality head coach Rémy "⁠XTQZZZ⁠" Quoniam said after the match:

"We played really badly. Not disciplined at all. We threw away many rounds on Nuke and Dust2. We didn't play together. It was too individual. It was a really bad game. It's still a win, but not happy at all."

When asked about the game plan against a team playing with a stand-in, he said:

"We know how they played. We watched them a bit. We said we needed to be careful with the discipline as they do not hesitate to take duels; they are really good individually. But we just didn't respect it. That's why it's really annoying right now."
