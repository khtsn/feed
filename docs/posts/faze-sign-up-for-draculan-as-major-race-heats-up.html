FaZe sign up for DraculaN as Major race heats up

FaZe have been confirmed as a participant at the upcoming DraculaN event, taking place right before the invite cut-off for the IEM Cologne Major. They join a strong field that also includes FUT, Liquid and Passion UA, with the final team list set to be revealed on March 15.

With only three weeks remaining before the invites to the next Major are determined, FaZe's qualification hopes are hanging by a thread. They are currently ranked 12th in Europe and will face a tall order at their next event, BLAST Open Rotterdam.

Like many other teams, FaZe are turning to tier-2 LANs hoping to pick up precious VRS points before the invite cut-off. Liquid, who are in a much more dire situation, are also attending the Roman Imperium Cup VI in Vila Nova de Gaia this week.

DraculaN Season 6 will take place in Bucharest from March 29 to April 2, with $15,000 on offer. FaZe will stay in the Romanian capital after DraculaN as they will also compete at PGL Bucharest, which will kick off on April 3.
