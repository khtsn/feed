B8 beat BESTIA to win Parken Challenger Championship Season 3

B8 have won Parken Challenger Championship Season 3 after a hard-fought reverse-sweep victory over BESTIA in the grand final (8-13 Dust2, 16-14 Mirage, 16-12 Ancient).

It is a crucial swing victory in the Major race for B8, who had their campaign on the line in the consolidation final against 3DMAX and entered invite range on our predicted list after eliminating the Frenchmen from contention.

B8 put themselves in danger after a loss to WAZABI in the group stage, from which they advanced 4-1, and dropped to the lower bracket in the playoffs after losing to 3DMAX in the upper semis. They recovered with a run that saw every series go to three maps, including that revenge game over 3DMAX, and showed tenacity in the final by recovering from 0-7 down on Mirage and denying BESTIA a comeback on Ancient to clinch victory.

The Ukrainian team now occupies the 13th spot on our predicted list and has likely secured their Cologne invite after gaining 20 spots on the global ranking from their run, although the list of Stage 1 teams remains volatile with several other LANs still underway.

BESTIA were under added pressure in the series as, though their tournament run — which included a pair of wins over 3DMAX — helped bring them into the fight for one of the last Americas invites, they need to catch a flight to Riga to make it in time for their opening match at Urban Riga Open Season 3 on Friday.

The Argentinian team has a two-point lead over Liquid and MIBR, who hold the last two invites on our predicted ranking, but a forfeit in their opening match in Latvia against BIG Academy would send them careening down the list.
