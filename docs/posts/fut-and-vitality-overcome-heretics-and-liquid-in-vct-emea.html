VCT EMEA's superweek continues until Friday, with two matches each day.

Unlike most of the squads in VCT EMEA, Heretics and FUT's new squads have already played against each other. The two met at Red Bull Home Ground 3 in December, where Heretics took a 2-0 win — their only win in groups.

The first half of Pearl was a disaster for Heretics, who had picked the map. By the time FUT were up 7-1, Mixwell had already taken two timeouts. Heretics took a second point, but FUT were unfazed and pressed on to an 11-2 lead. After Heretics bought in for a third round, MrFaliN took down three to put his team on map point. Two rounds later, FUT won Pearl 13-4.

qw1 opened the day with an absurdly strong map, clocking in a 2.4 KD while scoring four entry kills. He eclipsed everyone with his 1.63 rating, seconded only by teammates MrFaliN and qRaxs , who stood at 1.50. He also had 224 ADR, the second-highest value thus far in the VCT EMEA.

If only ranked players covered a defuse like this.

qRaxs and AtaKaptan opened up FUT's Lotus pick with a 3K and a 4K, respectively, which seemed to bode well for the squad. They were quickly shut down by Heretics' zeek , who managed a 1v1 against MrFaliN to send his team on an eight-round streak.

That streak seemed to falter in the later stages of the half, as Heretics needed three individual plays from AvovA , Mixwell and Boo to reach an 8-2 lead. qRaxs clutched against Boo and MrFaliN found a 3K that narrowed the gap to 8-4 by halftime.

qRaxs kept up his performance with a 1v4 on the second pistol round, catapulting FUT into an eight-round streak that broke through Heretics' timeout at 8-8. With FUT up 12-8, Heretics momentarily halted their streak with a strong C hold, but the Turks soon put their opponents to rest with a 13-9 victory in the second map.

AvovA was Heretics' saving grace on Lotus, being one of just two players on the server (and the only one on his team) to slay 20 or more players. Despite his strong numbers and 1.22 rating, the Dane was overshadowed by FUT's average 78% KAST. All of the team's players averaged a 0.90 rating or higher. MrFaliN led the charge with nearly 290 ACS.

FUT's win today levels the teams' head-to-head record, after Heretics had taken a 2-0 win in Red Bull Home Ground. Vitality didn't falter in the early stages of their Haven pick, ignoring a Liquid timeout for an uncontested 8-0 lead, which they capped with a 3K from Twisten 's Jett. A strong defense from Liquid finally gave them a point, prompting Vitality to take their own timeout and convert a round off of another 3K from their Jett. Liquid took the final two rounds of the half, but they were still down 9-3.

nAts attempted to take the game into his own hands, taking down three to give Liquid a fourth point. While Vitality dropped one more round, MOLSI and Destrian immediately put their team back in the game with two 3Ks in two rounds, reaching a 12-5 map point. Sayf won Liquid their sixth and final round in a 1v1 against Twisten, just before BONECOLD & co. closed out the map 13-6.

Despite taking down 17 players, Twisten clocked in just over 1.00 rating, catching most of his frags in economically weak rounds for Liquid. The highest rating belonged to IGL BONECOLD, who slew 13 and assisted in 13 other kills, averaging a 1.43 rating.

Liquid's final saving grace on Haven.

Liquid didn't let the Haven loss get to them, swiftly replying on Fracture. The Redgar -led squad took full control of the map, dropping just two rounds to Vitality — one of them off a 4K from Twisten. They pushed the series to Lotus with a quick and simple 13-2 win.

Liquid had nearly as many 3Ks as Vitality had multikills in general. Sayf, who scored three of those triples, also accounted for 23 frags (five openers) and nearly 400 ACS. His 2.25 rating is the highest of the VCT so far; the Swede also featured a 93% KAST and 261 ADR.
