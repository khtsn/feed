BBL Esports bids farewell to LewN

BBL Esports BBL Esports Europe Unranked Crewen Ali Eren Sargin Lar0k Yusuf Kaan Kanber Loita Utku Kart Rosé Eren Erzan lovers rock Umut Pekdoğan has announced the departure of Burak "LewN" Alkan.

LewN is the last member of the BBL's 2025 lineup to officially depart the team following the Turkish organization's signing of the BBL PCIFIC BBL PCIFIC Inactive roster. As BBL's affiliate team, PCIFIC stormed through Ascension, earning their spot in VCT EMEA and later moving over to BBL as a full squad in November.

LewN originally joined BBL as a rookie coming into the 2025 season, where he demonstrated an aggressive style on a wide range of Duelist agents. Over the course of the year, BBL came just shy of international qualification twice after finishing fourth in Stage 1 and third in Stage 2, where in the latter instance they did not have enough Championship points to qualify to Champions Paris over FNATIC and Team Heretics.
