1win bench HObbit, interz leaves

1win has announced the benching of Abay "⁠HObbit⁠" Khassenov and the departure of Timofey "⁠interz⁠" Yakushin, the two most experienced players on the roster who previously featured in teams like Gambit and Cloud9.

The Kazakhstani rifler had been on 1win for over a year-and-a-half, posting a 1.14 rating during his tenure, while interz had joined in August 2025 averaging an 0.97 in his time on the team.

News of the change break days after a grand final loss to Nuclear TigeRES, a team of youngsters averaging less than 20-years-old, in the GLuck StarPearl Season 1 LAN in Moscow, leaving 1win down to a three-man core.

1win is now:

Vladislav "⁠lattykk⁠" Vydrin

Ilnar "⁠cau⁠" Galeev (coach)

Abay "⁠HObbit⁠" Khassenov (benched)
