Live updates from the IEM Cologne Major quarter-finals

Aurora are playoff regulars, but have not found the winning combination since their PGL Bucharest title in 2025. Their best performance this year has been a second-place finish behind Natus Vincere at the ESL Pro League Season 23 Finals, but they've mostly dropped out in the quarter-finals.

Even at DraculaN Season 6, a tournament where they were the favorites and were invited straight into the playoffs, they lost to M80 and Alliance for a 5-6th place finish in the double-elimination bracket.

Their only non-playoff finish at an MVP event was IEM Rio, where they finished in 7-8th place after losses to Natus Vincere and MOUZ.
