BLAST Open Rotterdam semis as they happened

With the two group-winning teams joining the fray, three of their players stick out in the MVP race after dominant performances in Copenhagen.

Unsurprisingly, Vitality have two of them. Dominant as ever, Mathieu "⁠ZywOo⁠" Herbaut tops the leaderboards with a staggering 1.62 rating in groups.

Shahar "⁠flameZ⁠" Shushan may just be neck and neck with his superstar teammate, though, as he is slightly ahead in the matches outside of the opener against heavy underdogs 9z.

Third in line by some distance is Drin "⁠makazze⁠" Shaqiri, who looks to build on his MVP-winning performance in Stockholm just a few weeks ago after being the clear standout player in Natus Vincere's road to semis.

He may be closer to the Vitality duo than it appears, averaging a 1.46 rating in map wins alone, and could well continue to build on his case today.
