Aurora move closer to Rio playoffs after B8 win

Aurora moved one win away from the IEM Rio playoffs with a dominant 2-0 win over B8 (Mirage 13-7, Aurora 13-6).

"We knew they are not good enough to win against us," a happy Caner "⁠soulfly⁠" Kesici said after the game.

The result means the Turkish squad is one win away from the playoffs, and they are set to face off against Natus Vincere for a spot in the arena later on Wednesday, a team they have lost to the last six times they met.

"I don't remember how many times we lost against them, but it needs to stop somewhere. We need to stop that era, what we showed against B8 today was good, and we are really confident against NAVI," soulfly added about the upcoming match.

The series began on Mirage, where B8 remained in the game until they switched to the defensive side, thanks to a couple of clutch moments and individual plays. Once the second half started, however, Aurora took over as Ismailcan "⁠XANTARES⁠" Dörtkardeş led the way with a 1.84 T-side rating.

Overpass was an equally straightforward win, as B8 never really got close enough to trouble the Turkish side at any point before petering out of the tournament with a lacklustre offensive side.
