Shopify Rebellion overcome Team Liquid Brazil to win the Game Changers Championship

After a thrilling five-map series, Shopify Rebellion would take down Team Liquid Brazil to be crowned Game Changers Championship.

The North American squad, coming in from the upper bracket, had the advantage of two bans and picking the first map. They banned Split and Bind, and chose Haven as the first map of play. While the half saw both teams swapping rounds, Team Liquid ended the half with a 7-5 lead. Shopify would get to map point first, but TL would win the final round of regulation to push them to OT. Shopify would be able to win the next two rounds consecutively to take their map pick.

The second map of play, Lotus, was just as close-fought. Despite an early lead from SR with an 8-4 half, TL bounced back to take the map 13-11.

Map three, Breeze, once again SR's pick, saw them find a dominant 9-3 attacking half after a clean 7-0 start. Shopify Rebellion closed out the map 13-5, putting them one map away from being crowned champions.

Sunset was picked by Team Liquid, and similar to Shopify on Breeze, Team Liquid began strong with a 9-0 defensive start. Despite Shopify finding five rounds after the side swap, Team Liquid won the map 13-7, pushing the series to the decider, Ascent.

Despite a 6-6 half between both squads, once the side swap happened, Shopify Rebellion's defense looked unbreakable. Team Liquid would not be able to win a single round as SR silenced the home crowd on their way to the trophy with a 13-6 win.
