PARIVISION beat FaZe to enter 2-1 pool in Romania

PARIVISION came back from a map down to beat FaZe in three maps and move into the 2-1 pool in the Swiss stage of PGL Cluj-Napoca 2026.

It's a defeat that is likely going to sting for FaZe, who will be wondering how the series got away from them. They now find themselves staring elimination in the face after being so close to entering the progression stages in Cluj-Napoca.

FaZe looked poised to claim a statement 2-0 victory after a 13-2 rout on Mirage, where PARIVISION never found their footing. The Russian team continued to look just as out of tune on Ancient, losing several man advantages in the first half, including back-to-back 3v1s against Russel "⁠Twistzz⁠" Van Dulken.

Down 3-9, PARIVISION began to work away at the deficit on the T side and dictated the pace as FaZe floundered. With Ivan "⁠zweih⁠" Gogin leading the charge (2.62 rating and +18.08% round swing), they were able to close out the game in the last round of regulation in a decisive 1v1 battle.

The chaotic nature of the game continued on Inferno as Finn "⁠karrigan⁠" Andersen pulled off a pistol ace in a 2v5 situation before his team won a full eco a few rounds later. But these moments were mere footnotes to a strong all-around performance from PARIVISION, who calmly put the game to rest after punishing FaZe's A gamble in a 4v4 situation.
