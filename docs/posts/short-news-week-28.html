    Short: Bomb explosion interacts with smoke clouds and molotovs after new update

    Valve has shipped a new update to Counter-Strike 2 that sees the new bomb shockwave blow away smoke grenades and snuff out molotovs/incendiary grenades.

    The update also adjusted the bomb damage health preview to only show when the bomb becomes audible to players.

    Short: Subtop de France deemed "a material risk to competitive integrity," banned from CCT

    A French team called Subtop De France, which has been participating in various lower-tier events including the CCT circuit for the past four months, has been banned from the CCT circuit and its matches in the ongoing Europe 6 tournament have been forfeited.

    The decision follows an "integrity assessment" by the company behind CCT, GRID, which deemed the French team "a material risk to competitive integrity."
