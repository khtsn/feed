Short: FACEIT Season 9 to begin August 5 with soft Elo reset; open vote for half-time voice chat

FACEIT has announced details for Season 9, which will begin on August 5 with a soft Elo reset that applies to all accounts and requires 10 placement matches to regain a rank.

The reset comes after a harder reset hit players at the start of Season 8 to curb top-end elo inflation and punish inactive players who stop playing and hold on to old ranks. More details regarding the new season and reset can be found here. The platform also opened voting for the return of half-time voice chat between opposing teams for the first week of the new season.

Short: fnatic win ECL S52 Europe Cup 1
