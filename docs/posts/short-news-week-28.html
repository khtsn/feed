BLAST have commented on Liquid's Wildcard invite for their Bounty tournament after the American organization announced they will need to play with Roland "⁠ultimate⁠" Tomkowiak in place of new signing Georgi "⁠Jorko⁠" Mitev.

"Team Liquid are allowed to use 3 or more of the following players at the BLAST Premier Bounty: malbsMd, EliGE, NAF-FLY and Ultimate," BLAST's statement reads, meaning that Liquid can appear at the event with their new AWPer.

FACEIT has announced details for Season 9, which will begin on August 5 with a soft Elo reset that applies to all accounts and requires 10 placement matches to regain a rank.

The reset comes after a harder reset hit players at the start of Season 8 to curb top-end elo inflation and punish inactive players who stop playing and hold on to old ranks. More details regarding the new season and reset can be found here. The platform also opened voting for the return of half-time voice chat between opposing teams for the first week of the new season.

BLAST has revealed the draft order for Bounty Season 2, with 3DMAX getting the first pick of the litter in the 32-team event.

The live draft show will begin on Friday at 19:00 and see the bottom 16 seeds pick their opponents for the start of the online stage on July 21.
