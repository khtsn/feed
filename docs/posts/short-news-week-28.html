for people confused round swing accumulates and is rewarded on a round win based on the impact kills have to predicted winner of said round. so in donks 1v5, he gets a lot of round swing up until the point he kills teses and still gets a lot of swing (about 75% of the score zontix gets for the eventual kill on tes) but in a 1v1 with someone at 3hp, yes donk is predicted more favored to win at that point so the swing isn’t all him. I think more relevant to round swing is whether or not the predictions for round winner are accurate in 2026. when originally introduced with rating 3.0, a paper was cited as where they got their measures for a 4v5 vs 3v5 etc. but that data was in csgo. maybe winning a 4v5 in cs2 is even harder as teams have gotten better, and perhaps swing needs to be looked at with more recent data. this wouldn’t take long to accomplish
