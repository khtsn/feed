Parken Challenger Championship announce circuit expansion

The LAN tournaments will feature a larger prize pool and a 24-team field.

Parken Challenger Championship have announced an expansion to the next eight events of their open LAN circuit.

The tournaments will now feature 24 teams total, up from 18, with 20 of those teams set to battle in the open group stage while four high seeds get invited directly into the 16-team double-elimination playoffs.

The prize pool for each event has also been increased to $25,000 total per event, with the distribution looking as follows:

  • 1st: $11,000

  • 2nd: $4,000

  • 3rd: $2,000

  • 4th: $1,500

  • 5-6th: $750

  • 7-8th: $500

  • Club share: $500 for teams finishing 1st–8th ($4,000 total)

The aforementioned changes will apply to the next eight events of the circuit, set to be played on the following dates:

  • #9 - June 30-July 2

  • #10 - July 14-16

  • #11 - July 28-30

  • #12 - August 11-13

  • #13 - August 21-23

  • #14 - September 18-20

  • #15 - October 4-6

  • #16 - October 14-16

Due to the Singapore Major invite cut-off date being pushed back, all eight events will play a role in the Major race.
