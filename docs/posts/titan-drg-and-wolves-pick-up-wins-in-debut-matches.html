JDG got off to a red-hot start on Lotus with stew proving why he deserved to be re-signed with multikills aplenty. A 6-3 lead for JDG on the attack ensued before Titan began to pick up the pace.

Titan proceeded to go on a nine-round streak, completely flattening JDG to hit map point and lead 12-6. Despite stew's server topping 24 kills, JDG appeared deflated and mustered just two rounds before Titan took the lead in the series with a 13-8 map victory.

stew's heroics weren't enough for JDG.

While stew was the only player to find a positive K/D on the first map for JDG, Titan could look toward the trio of kawaii , QiuYe , and Rb to deliver results. The trio ended the game each with ACS scores greater than all of JDG aside from stew.

Bind started similarly to Lotus with JDG taking another comfortable lead in the half but this time guaranteed themselves an advantage heading onto their attack with an 8-4 scoreline. JDG's play was once again spearheaded by an excellent performance from stew.

Another late-game streak of rounds from Titan followed as Rb's squad acquired a 10-8 lead before JDG could pick up their first offensive round. Despite Titan narrowly making it to match point first at 12-11, a 1v3 clutch from Viva forced overtime.

JDG struck first before an ace from Rb evened up the map at 13-13. Both teams proceeded to trade blows before a second overtime ace, this time coming from Abo , gave Titan the necessary momentum to once again complete a comeback and close out the map 16-14 and the series 2-0.

Rb's redemption arc has begun.

Titan could thank Abo and Rb for their opening win, with the pair recording 28 and 26 kills respectively on the final map while also providing an ace each in overtime to close out Bind. Although JDG may have taken a loss, stew put up an admirable performance with 59 kills across the series, 17 ahead of runner-up Rb.

Titan will proceed to the winners match of Group B and are one series away from the playoffs, while JDG have been relegated to the elimination match.
