Riot announces finalized details for VCT Ascension Pacific

Riot has announced finalized plans for this year's VCT Ascension Pacific, which will be held in Jakarta.

The event was initially scheduled to be held in Tokyo but was relocated due to “significant event production delays and tournament quality concerns,” according to Riot. The event will now be held in Jakarta at the BritAma arena, which has a capacity of 5,000.

With the announcement, Riot also announced a finalized version of the ten-team, double-elimination format.

Riot says that teams were “grouped and drawn into brackets based on their region's results from last year's Ascension.”
