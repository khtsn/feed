Are you rich?

Source: Federal Reserve Board’s Survey of Consumer Finances 2023 data. Poor: 20th percentile, NW ~ $10,000. Middle class: 50th percentile, median household NW ~ $281,000. Wealthy: 95th percentile, NW ~ $3.2 million. The top 1%, or the 99th percentile, has a NW of $16.7 million are very wealthy.
