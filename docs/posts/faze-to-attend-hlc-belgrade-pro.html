FaZe to attend HLC Belgrade PRO

FaZe have opted to make one last-gasp attempt to qualify for the IEM Cologne Major.

The team list for HLC Belgrade PRO has been announced, and in a surprise turn FaZe have opted to attend the $30,000 LAN in Belgrade, Serbia from April 3-5.

FaZe's recent early exit from DraculaN Season 6 left the team out of the running for a spot at IEM Cologne Major, as they are scheduled to attend PGL Bucharest, which clashes with the remaining VRS LAN events.

In a last-ditch attempt to qualify for the Major in Cologne, FaZe have opted to attend HLC Belgrade Pro. As PGL Bucharest runs April 4-11, FaZe's attendance in Belgrade clashes with their commitment to PGL's event. HLTV has reached out to PGL for comment on the matter.

Also set to attend the Belgrade LAN are BIG, who are also in the running for a spot at IEM Cologne Major. The majority-German squad went on a tear over the start of the year to catapult themselves up the VRS after the addition of Benjamin "⁠blameF⁠" Bremer, but recently suffered a setback with their group stage exit at Roman Imperium Cup VII.
