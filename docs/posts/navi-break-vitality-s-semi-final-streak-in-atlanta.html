NAVI break Vitality's semi-final streak in Atlanta

apEX's troops have been stopped at 21 consecutive top-fours in a row.

Natus Vincere have defeated Vitality in the quarter-final of IEM Atlanta with a 2-1 scoreline (Dust2 11-13, Anubis 13-11, Inferno 13-3) to stop their top-four streak at 21 events in a row.

They are set to face BetBoom in Saturday's semi-final clash.

Natus Vincere came out guns blazing in the series as they dominated the early game on Dus2 outside of the pistol round. Vitality got three kills in five rounds as Natus Vincere went 5-1 up, but nearly tied up the scores at the half if not for Valeriy "⁠b1t⁠" Vakhovskiy's 1vs2.

Mathieu "⁠ZywOo⁠" Herbaut then took over after recording just two kills in the first half, allowing Vitality to run away to an 11-8 and eventually converting a 13-11 win on their map pick.

Vitality remained a force to be reckoned with on Anubis and found themselves 10-8 in the closing stages despite losing both pistols. Natus Vincere stayed in the game throughout, however, with the scales being tipped in their favor by a well-timed Mihai "⁠iM⁠" Ivan lurk that netted one map point, which they converted to force the Inferno decider.

Inferno was where it all unraveled for Vitality. Two more lost pistols consigned them to slow starts, but this time they ran out of steam for a comeback. A disjointed T side netted them just three rounds before a similar story continued on the CT side, which meant Natus Vincere took the map 13-3 in a historic victory.

A series of broken streaks

Natus Vincere's victory means a plethora of broken streaks, most notably Vitality's 21-tournament-long top-four run that began all the way back in IEM Katowice 2025, while their 28-map playoff win streak also came to an end on Anubis.

Natus Vincere also broke their unsuccessful streak against Vitality, having lost the last nine series with a 1-21 map record against ZywOo and company.

"Just the mentality," Aleksi "⁠Aleksib⁠" Virolainen named as the difference that allowed them to finally get the better of their arch nemesis. "We always worked hard for this moment, and we deserved it today for sure."
