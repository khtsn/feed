ZywOo claims the MVP at IEM Rio 2026

Mathieu "⁠ZywOo⁠" Herbaut has won his 31st career HLTV x 1XBET Most Valuable Player (MVP) award at IEM Rio 2026.

The event started with a demolition of RED Canids, but it was ZywOo's peaks against Natus Vincere, FURIA, and Spirit in playoffs that made sure the medal was his beyond any doubt.

In seven playoff maps, ZywOo was above the 1.44 mark six times, and averaged a 1.56 rating. It was a close-to-perfect arena showing from the world's best player, just in time to push his team to back-to-back ESL Grand Slam titles — the first team to do so.

That included a monstrous 2.53 versus Natus Vincere in quarters, but it was a fantastic performance throughout with a median map rating of 1.51.
