ESL announces IEM Beijing 2026

ESL has announced IEM Beijing 2026, set to run November 2-8 at the Bloomage Biotech Biohyalux ECM Arena. The 16-team tournament will feature a prizepool of $1,250,000.

The event will see fans present during all six days of play, and also feature a 360-degree stage design. "Over the past years, we’ve experienced the incredible passion and energy of Chinese fans first-hand," Marc Winther, Director of CS2 Esports at ESL-FACEIT group said. "With IEM Beijing 2026, we’re excited to take things to the next level."

The news sees ESL return to Beijing for the first time since IEM Beijing 2019, which was won by the era-defining Astralis squad.
