Roman Imperium Cup VI groups revealed

Teams like Liquid, NIP and BC.Game will be hoping to accrue precious VRS points at the Portuguese LAN.

BC.Game are currently ranked 25th in the European VRS

The tournament organizer behind the Roman Imperium Cups has revealed the groups for the next edition of the Portuguese LAN, which will take place at the SAW Esports Arena in Vila Nova de Gaia from March 12 to 15.

Liquid, Ninjas in Pyjamas and BC.Game are the tournament's headline names, with the first two teams signing up for the event last minute after watching their Major chances dwindle following poor results.
