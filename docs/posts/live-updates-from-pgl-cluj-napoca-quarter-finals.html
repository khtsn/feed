Live updates from PGL Cluj-Napoca quarter-finals

On NAVI win: "They are a really good team and they always keep you on the edge. We had a really bad T side on Ancient, but outside of that, all the other halves were pretty good."

On showing a bit more attitude against the crowd: "We are not one of the teams who interact with the crowd that much, it's true. I want to do it, but I think sometimes in games it's hard, you're really focused on the game. But I think when I see other people do it, it's cool, it's nice for the show. torzsi's doing it sometimes, so trying to enjoy the moment and adapt into it, but it's not something we talked about or anything."

On PARIVISION rematch tomorrow: "They have a really unique style. It can be really annoying to play against, especially when you lose. I think we are good at understanding what to do and how to play against them, we showed it in the group stage. But still, they are a strong team, they just beat Falcons and they are playing great CS. We were confident against NAVI and we lost T Ancient like this, so you can't underestimate anyone."

On why PARIVISION can be annoying: "Sometimes they can be really passive, sometimes they can be really aggressive, and then you have Jame, who is suddenly lurking with the AWP when you don't expect it. They can play on the last five seconds of the round. It's not as common. They're unique, yeah."
