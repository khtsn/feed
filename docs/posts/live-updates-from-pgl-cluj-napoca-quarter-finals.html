Live updates from PGL Cluj-Napoca quarter-finals

Danny "⁠zonic⁠" Sørensen stopped for a quick chat with HLTV following Falcons' elimination from PGL Cluj-Napoca at the hands of PARIVISION, emphasising his team's tendency to throw away favourable rounds.

On what went wrong: I think obviously on Dust2... we knew it was a good map for them but, in my opinion, I felt that we had it in our hands, and we just keep fucking up these rounds we are not supposed to.

It's getting a bit tiring because we work so much on it. I know that it's probably not what all people are thinking at home, but I can tell you that this is one of the main topics every time we are practicing, going through these rounds.

We do extremely well some part of the tournament, even some part of this game, I felt on Mirage T side especially we were never overextending and really trying to play as a team. It's costly, at this level you will lose games just because of that.

On if he was talking about 1v3 on Dust2: Also I think the two on one. I think there were a couple of episodes where we had to play better together, sometimes we even have to not take a fight, and it's frustrating right now.

On losing to Glocks on Ancient: We know we have an extremely good CT side on Ancient, but if you lose that second round it's going to be tough. Not just because you lose that round and get reset, but also because of the mental part. It's super frustrating and yeah, back to the drawing board.
