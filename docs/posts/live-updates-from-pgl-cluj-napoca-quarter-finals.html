Live updates from PGL Cluj-Napoca quarter-finals

A frank and damning on-stage interview with Nikola "⁠NiKo⁠" Kovač courtesy of James "⁠BanKs⁠" Banks:

"I have no idea what happened. Huge disappointment losing in such a fashion. We knew theyre a tough team to play against, no matter how well we prepare. Even if we knew what they were doing for some reason they are a very tough opponent.

"We are just bad. We are not winning the rounds we are supposed to win. If we give them the rounds this often this is the result, it's hurting us results wise and also confidence wise. It's really frustrating, no excuses, very very frustrated about how we played.

"We know we have aggressive players, who like to peek, but we have to minimise those things if you want to win. We have the team to afford mistakes, if it happens once a game, but when it's so often it's really embarrassing.

"It's noticeable that we take wrong steps, we sometimes try and finish the round too fast, sometimes we take a duel too soon, and then sometimes we don't take a duel when we shouldn't. It's a hard situation to tackle right now. Obviously we don't have the right answer."
