G2 bids farewell to malbsMd

G2 have announced the removal of Mario "⁠malbsMd⁠" Samayoa from their active roster.

The news follows consecutive group stage exits for the Samurais at PGL Cluj-Napoca and EPL Season 23.

malbsMd was crowned the 12th best player of 2024 after a scintillating start to his career in G2, but has struggled since taking on more responsibility after Nikola "⁠NiKo⁠" Kovač's departure.

He has a 1.05 rating since the start of 2025, compared to a 1.13 in 2024. In 2026, the aggressive rifler has fallen further to a 1.02 rating, and just 0.94 against top 10 opponents.

The roster change helps explain why G2 have chosen to attend BC.Game Masters, in order to play five matches with their new fifth.
