Instead, a 1.82-rated masterclass from twist led Alliance to a two-map series victory that has seen them rise to 14th on our predicted invite list. A match against M80 in the consolidation final is no longer do-or-die like Alliance's previous two, but would go a long way in helping cement their position with several other LAN events yet to conclude.

Passion UA's elimination is a severe blow to their Major campaign as they are now three spots and nearly 70 VRS points shy of the predicted Americas invite cut-off. The final spot is held by BESTIA, who are continuing to improve their standing with a run at Parken Challenger Championship, meaning JT's side would need nearly 80 points to overtake Liquid for the next spot.

Monte, meanwhile, sealed a grand final berth with a comfortable 2-0 win over M80, which has gone a long way in extending their lead over GamerLegion for a direct invite to Stage 2.
