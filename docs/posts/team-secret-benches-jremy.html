Team Secret benches Jremy

Team Secret Team Secret Asia-Pacific Rank #19 invy Adrian Jiggs Reyes n1zzy Nguyễn Tấn Thành stand-in ZesBeeW Sha Mohtar stand-in kellyS Kelly Sedillo stand-in Wild0reoo Brheyanne Christ Reyes has moved Jeremy "Jremy" Cabrera to their inactive roster.

Jremy has been with Team Secret for most of his career, joining all the way back in January 2022. Since the beginning of the partnerships era in 2023, Secret has produced solid results in VCT Pacific, coming just one win away from qualifying to Champions in both 2023 and 2024.

However, 2025 has been a particularly difficult year for the Filipino representatives thus far. After making zero roster changes coming into the year, the team has only picked up a single win out of seven matches, being against eventual Stage 1 champions Rex Regum Qeon Rex Regum Qeon Asia-Pacific Rank #3 Monyet Cahya Nugraha xffero David Monangin Jemkin Maksim Batorov Kushy Bryan Carlos Setiawan crazyguy Ngô Công Anh in week 5 of the group stage.

“This decision follows a challenging period but it most certainly does not diminish the impact he has made or the potential he continues to hold,” the team said of Jremy in their announcement. “We truly believe that he will rise to new heights in his career, with us or within a new organization.”

This change comes after 2GE was released from the team yesterday, indicating a big shift for the team. They will have to undergo a complete transformation if they still hope to earn a spot on the global stage for the first time since LOCK//IN.
