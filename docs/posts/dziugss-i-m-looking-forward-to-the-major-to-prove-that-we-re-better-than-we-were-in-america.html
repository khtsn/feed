dziugss: "I'm looking forward to the Major, to prove that we're better than we were in America"

"It feels really nice to get my name in the game," Džiugas "⁠dziugss⁠" Steponavičius says to HLTV on media day. "I really waited for this moment."

But the 17-year-old didn't have to wait for long. dziugss is the second-youngest player in Cologne, just behind Danylo "⁠s1zzi⁠" Vinnyk, and his FUT team is the youngest quintet overall.

Despite their inexperience, dziugss and FUT are not without pressure. They are ranked 13th on the world ranking, won their first title at PGL Bucharest, and boast four players at the top end of the HLTV Prospects ranking, with dziugss #1.

In their last two events, though, they suffered a blow to their confidence. Playing with András "⁠coolio⁠" Fercsák, they left BLAST Rivals in last place. When Laurențiu "⁠lauNX⁠" Țârlea returned for IEM Atlanta, they did not have time to practice and took losses to B8 and paiN.

But the mitigating circumstances mean that FUT should not overreact. After arriving in Cologne early to get used to the tournament setup, they're ready to cap an already fine season with yet another surprise.
