5 Killed in Shooting at Christian School in Nashville, Hospital Says

March 27, 2023, 12:28 p.m. ET

March 27, 2023, 12:28 p.m. ET

Three children and two adults were killed in a shooting at a private Christian school in Nashville on Monday morning, a hospital spokesman said.

The Nashville police said that officers had responded to “an active shooter event” at the Covenant School, which serves students from preschool through 6th grade, according to its website.

“The shooter was engaged by M.N.P.D. and is dead,” the Police Department said on Twitter.

John Howser, a spokesman for Vanderbilt University Medical Center, said that three pediatric patients with gunshot wounds were taken to Monroe Carell Jr. Children’s Hospital at Vanderbilt and were pronounced dead after arrival. Two adults, who were taken to an emergency room, were also pronounced dead, he said.

It was not immediately clear if the person with the gun was among the five dead.

The Nashville Fire Department said earlier on Twitter that it had responded to “an active aggressor” at the school.

“We can confirm we have multiple patients,” the department said.

A Nashville police spokeswoman said that the police had responded to a call at 10:13 a.m. for an “active shooter” at the address of the school and that there was a large police response working to assess the situation, give “patient care” and ensure safety and security.

The Covenant School, founded in 2001 as a ministry of the Covenant Presbyterian Church, has 33 teachers and has enrolled about 195 to 210 children in recent years, its website says.

The Nashville Fire Department said on Twitter that a reunification area had been set up at a nearby church for parents to meet their children.

Along the main street that leads to the school, police cars and ambulances raced up and down the street, sirens blaring as they closed off the intersections. Clusters of parents lined the roadway, several of them waiting for children in a nearby school that had been locked down as a result of the shooting.

This is a developing story.
