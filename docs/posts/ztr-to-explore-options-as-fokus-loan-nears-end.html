ztr to explore options as FOKUS loan nears end

FOKUS have announced that Erik "⁠ztr⁠" Gustafsson is exploring his options as his loan move from GamerLegion nears its completion, confirming a report from HLTV.

The Swedish captain joined FOKUS in February as the German organization entered Counter-Strike with a newly built international lineup coached by Polish legend Jakub "⁠kuben⁠" Gurczyński.

The team made a splash when they won Urban Riga Open Season 3 and qualified for PGL Bucharest, but the rest of the season was marked by inconsistency. After a second-place finish at BC Game Masters Championship Season 2, FOKUS went out of Super DraculaN Season 1 in the groups following losses to Echo and INFINITE.

French streamer Sebastien "⁠KRL⁠" Perez recently claimed that FOKUS are also exploring options to replace 17-year-old AWPer Georgi "⁠Jorko⁠" Mitev, who was ranked 25th in HLTV's latest prospects report.

A former member of Ninjas in Pyjamas's academy, ztr led GamerLegion's team for 18 months until the return of Janusz "⁠Snax⁠" Pogorzelski — the same player he had been asked to replace when he joined the team in July 2024.
