IEM Cologne Major Stage 1 opening matchups announced

ESL has announced the opening matchups for Stage 1 of the Cologne Major, set to begin on June 2.

Stage 1 and 2 will still feature best-of-ones for everything bar progression and elimination matches, while Stage 3 will be played entirely in a best-of-three format.

IEM Cologne Major to use no BO1s in Stage 3

The first stage of the Major will feature a plethora of squads from all across the world, with the team list headlined by three top-20 teams in GamerLegion, B8, and BetBoom.

The former will face ninth seed NRG, while B8 have been matched up against TYLOO, and BetBoom against Gaimin Gladiators.

Elsewhere in the bracket, BIG will meet Liquid in the standout match of the round, with Aussie duo THUNDER dOWNUNDER and FlyQuest set to play against MIBR and SINNERS in their respective openers.
