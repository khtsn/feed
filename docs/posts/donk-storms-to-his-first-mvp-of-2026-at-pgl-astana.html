donk storms to his first MVP of 2026 at PGL Astana

A 1.61-rated performance helped donk win his first medal in nine months.

Danil "⁠donk⁠" Kryshkovets has won his first HLTV x 1XBET Most Valuable Player (MVP) award of 2026 after leading Spirit to victory at PGL Astana.

Even before the final, donk had one hand on the medal after posting a 1.63 in groups, 1.42 in quarters, and 1.95 in the semi-final.

On Sunday, the destination of the special Astana design was put beyond doubt as donk made himself the highest-rated player of two of the three maps in the grand final.

Put together, he ended the event on a 1.61 rating, 0.98 KPR, and a +5.82% round swing. That's enough for the second-best rating from any MVP at a CS2 Big Event, behind his legendary debut at IEM Katowice in 2024.

He was top of the scoreboard in 71% of maps, with a peak of 2.07 and a floor of 1.22. He also got a kill in 58% of rounds, and a multi-kill in a whopping 29%.

The number two player of 2025, donk has had to endure a 2026 where Spirit rarely entered contention for the trophies that help him secure these MVP medals.

But at IEM Rio that changed, and Spirit now head to the Major having made the final of their two most recent events.

It's a frightening upshift in form at the perfect time, as Spirit and donk now set their sights on toppling the era-defining squad of Vitality in Cologne.
