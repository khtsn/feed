In the midst of a large-scale rebuild, Wolves and Satoshi disappointed in their first offseason together, finishing last or joint-last at the TEN Global Invitational 2025, China Evolution Series: Epilogue, and the China Esports Festival Super Champions Cup. Once the season began, Satoshi was replaced in the starting lineup by late addition qiutiaN , making him a sixth-man for the start of the VCT campaign.

In its rebuild, the Wolfpack parted ways with Yuicaw , Lysoar , and Juicy , three players who played a pivotal role in the team's breakout 2025 season, in favor of Satoshi, qiutiaN, and yosemite . As of Satoshi's departure, yosemite is the only offseason addition still with the team entering Stage 1. Earlier this week, the team parted ways with qiutiaN and picked up rookie player R1ckLee , and with Satoshi's departure, returns to a five-man roster.
