magixx 1v4 commemorated in-game, Falcons champion stickers released

Valve has released the IEM Cologne Major champion stickers for Falcons, as has been done since PGL Major Stockholm.

By winning the Major, Falcons are entitled to 2.85% of revenue from the Major shop and viewer passes in addition to proceeds from these champion stickers.

The post-Major update also sees a jar of tomato sauce added to Mirage, titled 'Magic: Secret Sauce' with a 4X just underneath, to honor Boris "⁠magixx⁠" Vorobiev's 1v4 spraydown from the quarter-final.

It's the first nod to a play at the Major in-game since Mihail "⁠Dosia⁠" Stolyarov's '200 IQ' grenade in 2017, with the exception of Dominik "⁠GruBy⁠" Swiderski and Damian "⁠Furlan⁠" Kislowski's Danger Zone win in 2019.

That it is magixx who earns it is particularly ironic, given his starring role in the ESL skit about ending the long wait for a graffiti just a few weeks before his now legendary play.
