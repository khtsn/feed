Snappi as NIP go 0-2 at EPL: "We are 2 different teams in practice and official"

Ninjas in Pyjamas are staring elimination in the face at ESL Pro League Season 23 after 1-2 losses to Legacy and M80 in Stage 1.

The team has looked a shadow of itself in 2026 since swapping Michel "⁠ewjerkz⁠" Pinto for Artem "⁠cairne⁠" Mushynskyi, plagued by mistakes in officials that have seen them slide down the VRS to 34th in the world.

A last-place exit from the online stage of BLAST Bounty, a solitary win over Passion UA around losses to Liquid and BC.Game in Stage 1 of IEM Krakow, and series losses to Monte and 100 Thieves at Roman Imperium Cup V have come as big blows to the team's Major hopes, with qualification to BLAST Open Rotterdam being the only solace in an otherwise dreary two months.

Marco "⁠Snappi⁠" Pfeiffer's side began their EPL campaign by trading map wins on Ancient and Nuke against Legacy before completely collapsing on the offense of the Overpass decider, and had to fight from behind to scrape a 13-11 win on Ancient against M80 around one-sided losses on Anubis (3-13) and Dust2 (8-13).

"We are 2 different teams in practice and official, it's so crazy," Snappi said in a post on X after the match. "There is nothing else to say. Either we solve it within the next few weeks or we won't go to the Major. GG."
