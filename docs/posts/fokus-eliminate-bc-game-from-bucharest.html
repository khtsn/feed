FOKUS eliminate BC.Game from Bucharest

BC.Game have been eliminated from PGL Bucharest with a 1-3 record after a loss to FOKUS.

Their only win came in a labored 2-1 victory over Voca, who did not even have their full roster.

The disparity between the tired and uninspired veterans of BC.Game and freshness of FOKUS was stark, as Mateo "⁠Matheos⁠" Prišlin and Georgi "⁠Jorko⁠" Mitev put Oleksandr "⁠s1mple⁠" Kostyliev and company to the sword.

It's a coaching win for Jakub "⁠kuben⁠" Gurczyński over old colleague Wiktor "⁠TaZ⁠" Wojtas, too, and validation for FOKUS' emphasis on young and hungry talent.

The result represents yet another setback for BC.Game, who put a heavy focus on Bucharest after failing to make the Major.

In the three weeks since they went 0-3 at Roman Imperium VI, the team promised to "fix their chemistry", but none of that was apparent in the server in another disappointing tournament.

They have a chance for redemption at IEM Atlanta and CAC in May, but hope for a miracle appears to be in short supply.
