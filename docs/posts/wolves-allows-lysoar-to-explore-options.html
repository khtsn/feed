Wolves allows Lysoar to explore options

Lysoar has been a staple on top Chinese teams since 2023. Despite never making it out of the group stage at these internationals, they were able to contend with the strong teams in their path. Their most notable performance was at Masters Shanghai, where they nearly won over both of the eventual finalists, only just coming up short.

In 2025, Lysoar moved to Wolves Esports in a conglomeration of talent across the region. The team showed strength in the off-season, and realized that strength during Stage 1, where they qualified to Masters Toronto. As the bottom seeded team from China, Wolves had some of the lowest expectations in the tournament.
