Aurora beat B8 to reach 2-2 pool in Romania

Aurora have sent B8 home from PGL Cluj-Napoca after getting the better of the Ukrainian side in a two–map series (Overpass 13-10, Mirage 16-13).

With Anubis out of the pool due to the map being B8's permaban, Aurora went for the Overpass pick in the series, and managed to narrowly close it out thanks to some masterful B defenses by Ali "⁠Wicadia⁠" Haydar Yalçın and Caner "⁠soulfly⁠" Kesici.

Mirage looked to be much more of a struggle for the Turks, but Engin "⁠MAJ3R⁠" Küpeli's troops rallied on the T side to come back from 6-11 down to secure overtime.

Aurora kept their momentum in added time, losing just one round to secure a 16-13 win and a spot in the 2-2 pool, where a victory will book them a ticket to the BT Arena.

Wicadia leads the way

While Aurora still lacked some cohesiveness at times in the match, their struggles were usually remedied by Ismailcan "⁠XANTARES⁠" Dörtkardeş, Wicadia, or soulfly bailing them out with some exceptional displays of skill.

Wicadia topped the board on Overpass (1.59) and Mirage (1.43), while the aggressive XANTARES and the measured soulfly provided ample backup for Aurora to continue their path to the playoffs.
