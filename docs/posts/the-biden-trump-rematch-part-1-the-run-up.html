ron klain

And each time, during his term, voters have gone to the polls, they’ve chosen Joe Biden and the Democrats over the Republican Party led by Donald Trump. We had a stunning historical result in 2022. And just recently, in a special election in New York, Democrats took back a seat held by the Republicans. So I think that there are polls that run in newspapers, and there’s polls we have at polling places. And the president has delivered results for the party and the polls at polling places. ron klain

But I think we have to make the argument that his age allows him to have more wisdom and perspective as president at a time of a complex and a dangerous world, that Joe Biden’s the person you want dealing with this mess we have in Europe with Putin invading Ukraine, and you want Joe Biden solving these hard problems because he’s got the experience to do that. And certainly, if it’s a choice between Joe Biden and Donald Trump, I think Biden is a better choice.

ron klain

Well, I do think that the situation in the Middle East creates a division in our party and concern in the country, as it should. And so, I think it’s another thing he has to address. It’s another problem that he has to solve as president. So I think that has changed. I think that’s another burden on him. But I think there’s no better person in the Oval Office to do that than President Biden. And I’d say the other thing that’s changed in the past year is the economy is getting better. The progress continues to be in the right direction. And I think as that continues, it’ll sink in with voters. You’re starting to see consumer confidence rise. It takes a while for people’s perceptions of the economy to come around. And I think it will, in a way, and help lift the president even further politically. So I’m positive about the economy and positive about his ability to address these foreign crises.

ron klain

ron klain

ron klain

ron klain

Nothing that I can imagine, no.

ron klain
