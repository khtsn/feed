PARIVISION sign HObbit

Abay "⁠HObbit⁠" Khassenov has signed for PARIVISION as a surprise replacement for Andrey "⁠BELCHONOKK⁠" Yasinskiy.

He will make his debut as a "stand-in" at XSE Pro League, as the team look to put their Major run behind them. The team have been asked for comment regarding the duration of HObbit's contract.

PARIVISION bench BELCHONOKK

In their welcome statement, the CEO said: "At this stage of development, it was important for us to strengthen the roster with an experienced player who will help the team progress in both the short and long term.

"We are well aware of Abay's strengths, understand the contribution he can make to the team, and are confident that his arrival will make us stronger."

A lack of experience among the team's riflers has been a constant theme this year, with Dzhami "⁠Jame⁠" Ali and Dastan "⁠dastan⁠" Akbayev both pointing to it as a weakness of the team in interviews.

32-year-old HObbit has therefore been cast as a solution to this problem, despite being out of tier one since leaving Cloud9 in 2024. He did play for 1win in a two-year spell that ended in April 2026, but did not make it to any MVP events.

The team are now:

Dzhami "⁠Jame⁠" Ali

Dastan "⁠dastan⁠" Akbayev

Andrey "⁠BELCHONOKK⁠" Yasinskiy (benched)
