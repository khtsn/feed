Global Esports Global Esports Asia-Pacific Unranked PatMen Patrick Mendoza UdoTan Go Kyung-won (고경원) Kr1stal Savva Fedorov xavi8k Xavier Juan Autumn Kale Dunne has expanded its coaching booth, welcoming in Derren "Aimix Strange" dela Cruz as an assistant coach and Sam "Fields" Greenfield as an analyst. The two well-traveled coaches will look to support newly promoted head coach Daniel "Platoon" Zhou in the coaching booth, who spent one game alone in the GE coaching booth in Pacific Stage 2.

For the second time in the past two seasons, but for different reasons, Global Esports has had a mid-season change in the head coaching position. While last year it was Elevated who went from assistant coach to head coach, this year it is Platoon, who went from analyst to head coach following a reported internal fallout between FrosT and members of the team.
