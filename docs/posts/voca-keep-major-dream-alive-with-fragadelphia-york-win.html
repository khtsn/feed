Voca keep Major dream alive with Fragadelphia York win

Voca have secured the Fragadelphia York 2026 trophy after defeating Fisher College in the grand final with a 3-1 scoreline.

Paytyn "⁠junior⁠" Johnson and company took home the $8,250 grand prize alongside a crucial four-place rise in the VRS, which currently puts them in the Cologne Major invite range according to HLTV's projection.

The American squad, which was the only attending team with a chance to make the Major, won the event by losing just one map, but can still fall short of Cologne due to other teams racking up more points.

BESTIA are close behind and could overtake them with a strong performance at the Radio Popular Roman Imperium Cup VII, where they are currently competing in the semi-finals against Ascend. Other teams, such as Azbayar "⁠Senzu⁠" Munkhbold's Passion UA, are also close enough to challenge.
