M80 performed well in the 2023 NA Challengers League. The former roster won both the Mid-Season Invitational as well as playoffs, which qualified them to Americas Ascension alongside fellow North American representative The Guard The Guard Inactive JonahP Jonah Pulice trent Trent Cairns neT Michael Bernet valyn Jacob Batio tex Ian Botsch .

M80 maintained their composure throughout Ascension playoffs and performed a lower bracket run to meet The Guard in the grand final. Unfortunately, the team stumbled when it mattered most and were sent home empty handed as their opponents claimed the Ascension trophy in a 3-1 victory.

Following the departures of Mohamed "johnqt" Amine Ouarid and Daniel "eeiu" Vucenovic, M80 were left with two vacancies. After picking up Brendan "BcJ" Jensen in November, the squad was all but set to welcome nitr0 as the missing piece of the puzzle.

Now complete, M80's new roster will make their explosive debut in the NA Challengers League. Only time will tell if the roster has retained its firepower as they duke it out against their North American rivals.
