Fisher College make history with PGL Astana qualification; 9z top South American qualifier

The squad became the first collegiate team to qualify for a tier-one event.

The PGL Astana team list has been completed with Fisher College and 9z winning their respective regional qualifiers to secure their spots at the event.

The former has made history with their qualification, becoming the first collegiate team to secure a spot at a tier-one event after beating LAG in the grand final.

Alexander "⁠AlekS⁠" Andersson and company found themselves 0-2 down in the best-of-five grand final but rallied to win Anubis (13-11), Mirage (16-13), and Overpass (13-4) to complete the reverse sweep.
