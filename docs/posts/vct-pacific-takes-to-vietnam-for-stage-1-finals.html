VCT Pacific takes to Vietnam for Stage 1 Finals

VCT 2026: Pacific Stage 1

1 day ago

Riot has revealed that Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam will host the VCT Pacific Stage 1 Finals.

This will be the first time that Vietnam's largest and most populous city will host a VCT stage final, although it has previously hosted events such as the 2024 Challengers Vietnam Split 2 Finals and the 2025 Challengers SEA Split 3 Finals.

VCT Pacific Stage 1 group play will run from April 3 to May 17. Teams will be divided into two groups of six, with the top eight advancing to playoffs to compete for the three regional slots for Masters London in June.

The Pacific 2026 schedule is:

  • Kickoff, Stage 1 group stage, Stage 2 group stage: Seoul
  • Stage 1 Finals Weekend: Ho Chi Minh
  • Stage 2 Finals Weekend: Busan
