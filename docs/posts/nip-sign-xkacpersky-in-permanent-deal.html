NIP sign xKacpersky in permanent deal

Ninjas in Pyjamas have announced that Kacper "⁠xKacpersky⁠" Gabara's transfer from ENCE has become permanent after a nine-month loan stint.

After starting his career with Young Ninjas, xKacpersky first joined Ninjas in Pyjamas' main team in June 2025 as a replacement for Rafael "⁠arrozdoce⁠" Wing and quickly became a key component of the organization's roster, ending the year as the highest-rated member on the team.

When asked by HLTV for a comment, Björn "⁠THREAT⁠" Pers said: "Thrilled we can finally announce that Kacper is fully back in NIP! What a journey it has been from joining our academy back in 2024 to becoming the hottest rookie in the world.

"We're confident with him in the team we’ll continue to improve, reaching a higher peak than we have in the recent years."
