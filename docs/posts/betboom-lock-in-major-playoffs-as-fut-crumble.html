BetBoom lock in Major playoffs as FUT crumble

BetBoom have completed their remarkable run to the Cologne Major playoffs after beating FUT 2-0.

The team started Stage 1 with no practice as a five-man lineup after Timur "⁠FL4MUS⁠" Marev substituted Pavel "⁠S1ren⁠" Ogloblin at the eleventh hour, but have succeeded in three successive Swiss groups to reach the LANXESS arena. Aleksandr "⁠zorte⁠" Zagodyrenko, too, is a stand-in on paper for Artem "⁠ArtFr0st⁠" Kharitonov.

Boombl4: "It's the most unexpected season of my career"

The result is a reminder of the undoubted quality that Kirill "⁠Boombl4⁠" Mikhailov provides to any team, and that was especially prevalent against the youngest team in the tournament in the form of FUT.

A Major winner in Stockholm in 2021, Boombl4 punished every FUT mistake en route to a 1.87 rating on map one and 1.66 on map two, topping his team both times.

He then called his team to a 7-5 lead on Dust2, pulling neat force buy tricks out of the hat to keep FUT's mentality on the floor.

"It's crazy emotions to be in playoffs with a stand-in," Boombl4 told the ESL desk post-game. "After two lost games, I understood that I was an experienced player and needed to show it in the 2-2 match. It's really stressful for everyone, FUT are a young team. I showed myself."

FUT's young guns feel the pressure

While BetBoom deserve praise for their miracle run, it must also be noted that FUT were far from their A game in the 2-2 decider.

In frequent listen-ins, we heard András "⁠coolio⁠" Fercsák try to wake up a team that were floundering under pressure.

coolio says FUT are playing like level 5s

In the first, he told the team off for playing too passive. In the second, he said they were playing "like a team of FACEIT level fives."

The effect was visible in the server too, as FUT shifted out of defaults into site hits to no effect, as they went down 1-11 on their home map of Mirage.

Then, on Dust2, he told his team to stop having "panic attacks" when they were giving information.

It was stern words from their coach, but it did not have the desired effect as pressure continued to push down on FUT's young squad with frequent missed shots.
