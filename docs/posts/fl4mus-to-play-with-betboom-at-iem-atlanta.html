FL4MUS to play with BetBoom at IEM Atlanta

The 21-year-old's loan deal from Virtus.pro continues after he previously stood in for BetBoom in their Major-qualifying campaign.

Timur "⁠FL4MUS⁠" Marev will return to the BetBoom roster for IEM Atlanta, the organization has announced on its Telegram page.

The 21-year-old will replace Daniil "⁠d1Ledez⁠" Kustov at the $1 million tournament taking place from May 11-17.

d1Ledez previously sat out the team's three European LAN appearances in their campaign to qualify for the IEM Cologne Major due to visa issues.

FL4MUS took d1Ledez's place then as well on a loan deal from Virtus.pro, which originally expired following the end of the aforementioned run.

That deal is now set to continue after the organization said it would explore an extension when d1Ledez briefly returned to the roster.
