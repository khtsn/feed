Kvem announces free agency

Vladyslav "⁠Kvem⁠" Korol has announced that his contract has come to an end with Passion UA and that he has joined the pool of free agents.

The 24-year-old joined Passion UA at the beginning of 2025, was briefly benched in August of that year when the team went from a Ukrainian roster to international, and returned to play another year.

Kvem showed that he could adapt well, averaging a 1.11 rating during his time throughout both iterations of the Passion UA roster.

He won smaller tournaments in the tier two VRS circuit, such as Glitched Masters 2025 and Urban Riga Open Season 1, but never broke out at the top tier, with 5-6th place finishes at BLAST Rivals 2025 Season 2 and StarLadder StarSeries Fall 2025 his best finishes at notable events, and 9-11th his best at a Major, StarLadder Budapest.

Kvem is yet to earn an MVP or EVP in his career, but he was given shouts in the honorable mentions sections for both IEM Atlanta 2026 and the aforementioned StarLadder Budapest Major despite limited team success.
