Gen.G continue streak to win Pacific Kickoff

Gen.G have dethroned Paper Rex with a 3-1 victory in the Pacific Kickoff. The win awards them a top seed in Masters Madrid, as well as three Championship Points for Champions qualification.

The match began on Ascent, going 13-5 in Gen.G's favor. Continuing on Split, Paper Rex were able to win 13-4. Lotus was closer, with Gen.G winning the map 13-11. Finally, they dominated Paper Rex on the final map, Bind, with a 13-4 scoreline.

Gen.G went into the tournament with a completely rebuilt roster, including a rookie pickup in Karon. Their opponents have been the most successful Pacific team in history, totalling three silver medals in VCT international events. This event marks Paper Rex's fourth grand final since the beginning of partnerships, but they have only been able to win VCT Pacific 2023.
