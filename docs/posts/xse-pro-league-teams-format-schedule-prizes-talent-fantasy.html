Teams and format

Plenty of top teams are heading to China for the chance at some valuable VRS points, including two top ten teams in the form of 9z and BetBoom.

With twelve top 30 teams attending, it's a good turnout for XSE despite Legacy, M80, and MOUZ all withdrawing after initially accepting invites.

We will also see the debuts of several new lineups, in a list that includes PARIVISION, MIBR, and FaZe.

The format is that of the Majors before IEM Cologne, with a full Swiss group featuring BO1 games in the early rounds and BO3 in progression and elimination games. Then, there will be an eight-team playoffs with a BO5 grand final.

Schedule

Matchdays will start early for Europe, from 09:00 on day one (July 1) and two before 07:00 on days three and four, and 08:00 on day five. Teams will not have to play several BO1s in one day, unlike the old Majors.

Playoffs begin on Thursday, July 9th, at 10:00 , with the same start time on Friday and Saturday before the grand final at 11:00 on Sunday.
