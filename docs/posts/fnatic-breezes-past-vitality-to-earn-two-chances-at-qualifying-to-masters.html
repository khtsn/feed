The series started on Vitality's pick of Abyss. Both teams had dominant wins in their previous showings on the map with entirely different looks, with Vitality running Waylay-Yoru and FNATIC sticking to Sova and KAY/O.

FNATIC started the map on their attack, where they dismantled Chronicle's Veto setups with slower defaults and finished off rounds with stalwart postplants. The next map was Bind. This time, FNATIC was the side playing double-Duelist, fielding a Yoru and Raze against Vitality's double-Initiator composition.

In the first half, FNATIC threatened another dominant attack side with a 5-2 start, pressuring Vitality with uncharacteristically fast site hits. Vitality instead managed to grind things back to 6-6, winning close retakes off the back of PROFEK and Derke each collecting 4Ks.

FNATIC won yet another pistol on the other side of halftime, going 4-0 in pistol rounds over the course of the series. The real impact round, however, was round 17, where Alfajer pulled off a 1v3 clutch.

Alfajer was on fire on Raze all throughout the map, spurring FNATIC on to a 13-8 win with an unreal 28/11/5 KDA, 238 ADR and 1.84 rating performance. Veqaj followed behind with 22 kills as the newest member of FNATIC had an impressive performance himself across both maps.

With this win, FNATIC remains undefeated 7-0 against Vitality all-time.
