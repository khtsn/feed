apEX: "I don't think we ever showed a good face"

Moments after Vitality's elimination from the IEM Cologne Major after a narrow loss to Falcons, HLTV managed to catch up with Dan "⁠apEX⁠" Madesclaire to ask about the elimination and what went wrong in the match.

"I don't think we ever showed a good face here, either in the group stage or today," the captain began. "That's CS sometimes. We didn't have our peak this time, and that's sad, but props also to Falcons, who played well."

"It's just tough because, when you don't show up, it's always like you're having regrets," he went on. "I think we all have regrets."

Commiserations. Can you tell me what's going through your head at the moment? You came into the Major with a clear goal, so tell me about it.

Overall, a disappointing Major, not gonna lie. I don't think we ever showed a good face here, either in the group stage or today. I don't think we played really well today at all. That's CS sometimes. We didn't have our peak this time, and that's sad, but props also to Falcons, who have played well.

It's just tough because, when you don't show up, it's always like you're having regrets, and I think we all have regrets.

You were calling your team back and playing probably your best game at this tournament so far on Dust2, and it just slipped away at the end.

Yeah. I fucked up the 12-11 call, but we lost also two ecos. That didn't help us at all. We were winning the buyrounds, but not the halfbuys, so yeah. That was our problem this tournament. We lost way too many. We gave up also advantage situations on our CT side where we had good situations. That just slipped away because we didn't deserve it with what we did.

Towards the start of the series you also lost Anubis. I spoke to NiKo after Falcons beat NAVI and he said they were even considering picking it into them, so I assume you were ready for that.

Yeah, we were ready, but once again we didn't start the side well. We had so many good opportunities where we could close the round, but we missed shots, and we had the right plays, but we don't play good. Once again, they also played well and you have to give them credit, but one thing is we didn't show up, maybe it's because they also played well.

Do you feel like individuals didn't show up, or as a team?

Actually, a bit of both. Our individuals didn't show up that much except some moments, and the teamplay was a bit off in some rounds. But that's CS. You can't always play good. We've been playing so well in a year and a half, but now we have a bit of a down, so we have to reflect and understand what happened, and just move forward.
