Spirit to face 9z for playoff spot on IEM Cologne Major day three

After two days of action, the IEM Cologne Major Stage 3 has reached elimination and progression games.

Day two was full of upsets, chief among which was 9z defeating Vitality to move one win away from the playoffs, while BetBoom also secured the upset against Falcons.

The results mean that Spirit will face 9z in one of the progression matches in a repeat of their clash in Stage 2, with the second 2-0 match happening between FURIA and BetBoom.
