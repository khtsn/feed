Bilibili Gaming looks back on 2024 season in press conference

Note: This article is a text summary of the Bilibili Gaming vs. KRU Esports post-match press conference. The full video press conference can be found here.

Can you give some reasoning behind today's loss?

bail: "I don't have much I want to say. We just came to Champions and there was so many ups and downs in our whole team such as through Stage 1 and 2. We've had a little bit of roster changes in our team, and I appreciate all of the players. They just follow us, and I think we made the decision in this super short period whereas KRU made preparation. I think the blame for the loss should be on me. Maybe the main reason is myself."

How will you put your newfound experience to use for the future?

Knight: "I think we just tried a lot of new stuff at Champions. I think we just need more time to take things step by step."

Looking back through the whole year, how was the progress on your individual and team performance, especially after bail joined?

whzy: "I think there is a lot of things. We just didn't play what we want, and during this whole year, we need to learn a lot of stuff. We just want to play our own stuff, and unfortunately we just couldn't do it."

The team has come a long way to reach the point to qualify for Champions. Due to the fact that you had to join a bit later in the year, how do you feel about the team's progress since the time you joined?

bail: "The first thing I mentioned is that there were a lot of things I can't fix. Maybe I just need to focus on our mistakes, and we need to practice and practice and make things better. I think it's been a very long time that all of these mistakes have remained. We just need to take more time to fix it. I just want them to thrive."
