B1ad3: "The way makazze is playing is amazing"

Natus Vincere secured the final playoff berth at IEM Rio after beating Aurora 2-1 in the lower bracket final of Group B, needing to get the job done "the hard way," as Andrey "⁠B1ad3⁠" Gorodenskiy put it, after they "threw the game versus FURIA" the day before.

So many situations went awry in that series that B1ad3 struggled to figure out what exactly went wrong, saying his players simply weren't killing in situations they usually would.

"Like the round versus Tec-9s that we lost, it was unbelievable," he told HLTV. "I think that somebody is doing something on the server, you know, like adjusting so that we cannot hit. Because if you rewatch this round, the positioning where we were, it's impossible [for them] to win this round."

The Ukrainian skipper also spoke about NAVI's stop-and-start run in Rio after dominant first maps and blowout losses on the second, offered some insight into why his team is finding consistency now, and how they are setting expectations following their EPL victory considering B1ad3's prior comments about needing a trophy this season to show they are "capable of becoming a team with a future."
