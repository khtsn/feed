ESL announces Cologne Major talent

ash is set to take up duties on the analyst desk

ESL has announced the broadcast talent for IEM Cologne Major, which is set to kick off June 2. 17 names familiar to our screens will help bring the action to life, with one notable name from the coaching world making an appearance.

A raft of the usual suspects will take up hosting, presenting and sideline duties, namely Freya "⁠Freya⁠" Spiers, Tres "⁠stunna⁠" Saranthus, Eefje "⁠sjokz⁠" Depoortere and fan favorite Oliver "⁠OJ Borg⁠" D'Anastasi.

It's the same story with the analysts, with Janko "⁠YNk⁠" Paunović, Martin "⁠STYKO⁠" Styk, Peter "⁠dupreeh⁠" Rasmussen and Jacob "⁠Pimp⁠" Winneche manning the desk, with the latter also listed as a presenter. Notably Ashley "⁠ash⁠" Battye, currently on the hunt for a new team after parting ways with GamerLegion, will also lend his knowledge to the desk.

Finally, the four casting pairs are Chad "⁠SPUNJ⁠" Burchill and Alex "⁠Machine⁠" Richardson, Harry "⁠JustHarry⁠" Russell and Hugo "⁠Hugo⁠" Byron, Jason "⁠moses⁠" O'Toole and Adam "⁠Dinko⁠" Hawthorne, and Conner "⁠Scrawny⁠" Girvan and Mohan "⁠launders⁠" Govindasamy.

The full talent list is as follows:
