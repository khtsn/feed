FaZe, Liquid, and Passion UA pick up wins in first round of DraculaN S6 playoffs

The first round of the DraculaN Season 6 playoffs has ended with all big names staying alive, with fnatic being the only one to find themselves in the lower bracket.

Liquid began the playoffs with a win over Inner Circle, with Major hopefuls Monte, Alliance, and M80 also all picking up victories.

The most intriguing match of the day came near the end as FaZe faced local underdogs aimclub in a bid to secure a last-minute ticket to Cologne, and Finn "⁠karrigan⁠" Andersen's troops got the job done with a comfortable 2-0 win.
