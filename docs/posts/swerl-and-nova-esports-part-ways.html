SWERL and Nova Esports part ways

Nova Esports Nova Esports China Unranked monk Wang Haoyu (王浩宇) CB Wang Qingchuan (王庆川) Ezeir Zhao Zejun (赵泽骏) GuanG Guang Honglin (光洪霖) has announced the departure of Ali "SWERL" Kobraee following a mutual termination of his contract.

The Australian SWERL joined Nova early on in the 2024 season, joining ahead of the team's first match in China Stage 1. The Duelist player's time in China began in 2023 with Royal Never Give Up Royal Never Give Up Inactive HaTo Wang Rui (王睿) 4ErWa Xiaonuo , following a long playing career in Oceania. Before moving to China as an overseas player, SWERL competed for Dire Wolves Dire Wolves Inactive , Mindfreak Mindfreak Inactive tensai Benny Phan soju Jay Jeong Dutti Sudhish Dutt Chax Chax Stipanovic , and Wildcard Gaming Wildcard Gaming Inactive InfrequeNt Jesse Barker rDeeW Riley Wilkinson Dale Dale Tang Nozz Noah McClafferty SWERL Ali Kobraee .

After helping Nova to a playoff berth in his first event with the team, Nova finished the 2024 season as a middling team in the region, ending the season at seventh. After a 7th-8th finish at China Kickoff to start 2025, SWERL was benched by Nova in favor of an all-Chinese roster for Stage 1. The new roster debuted with a trophy-lifting campaign at China Evolution Series: Act 1, qualifying for the Stage 1 playoffs but ultimately finishing 7th-8th. SWERL was reinstated into the starting lineup for Stage 2, where Nova 1-4 and failed to qualify for the playoffs.
