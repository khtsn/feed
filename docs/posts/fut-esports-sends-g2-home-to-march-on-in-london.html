The series was played out on FUT's Ascent and G2's Lotus. FUT won Ascent 13-6 and then won a thriller Lotus 15-13 to send G2 home.

G2 stayed with a familiar concept on Ascent, fielding Phoenix, Omen, Sova, and Vyse, while continuing to tinker with jawgemo 's role. After previously trying out Yoru and Neon, another fold was added, with the Duelist player on Waylay for this map. Meanwhile, FUT moved away from its Phoenix-Neon comp, going with a double-Sentinel look that split the double-Duelist pair, with s0pp on Jett and xeus on Sage.

While already boasting one of the world's most lethal Havens, FUT flexed its Ascent, enjoying some upstream success on the attack side. Early on, FUT was flawless. yetujey 's lurks on attack were big for FUT, and his clutch ability carried over onto attack. While G2 won the pistol to open its own attack, FUT turned up the heat on defense, with yetujey wrapping up a map to remember in style.

After enjoying more success on attack, both teams won their defense rounds in overtime to start, with FUT coming out on top thanks to heroics from the youngest player in London.
