FaZe ease past NRG to stay alive in Atlanta

FaZe have cast NRG out of IEM Atlanta with a comfortable 2-0 victory, removing one of the hometown sides from the competition with relative ease.

After being dominated by paiN yesterday it was important for FaZe to bounce back quickly, to continue building belief in their recent resurgence. They delivered on that task with aplomb early doors in this series by blowing NRG off the server on Ancient.

broky posts vintage 1v3 clutch

Nuke was a more interesting battle, but one FaZe had firm control of by the end of the map. NRG posted a strong defense off the back of a pistol victory and Alexander "⁠br0⁠" Bro's impact, running out to an 8-4 lead. FaZe won their own CT pistol and converted, only for NRG to pick themselves back up with guns in hand to get to 10 rounds.

FaZe's individual quality proved too much down the stretch, however, Helvijs "⁠broky⁠" Saukants coming up some some vintage multi-kills before Russel "⁠Twistzz⁠" Van Dulken took over in the last few rounds to ensure his team remain in contention in Atlanta.

br0 stands tall on the A bombsite with tidy 3K

NRG's campaign could have been a more successful one had they been able to capitalize better on their leads. They squandered a 10-3 advantage against FUT on Ancient in their opener, and were in a good position at 10-7 in this series on Nuke. Ultimately in both instances it felt like they couldn't keep up with the firepower thrown at them by their opponents.
