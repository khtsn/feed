NIP temporarily promote n0te to main roster

The Young Ninjas rifler will play for the team in the team's upcoming tournaments.

Ninjas in Pyjamas have announced the temporary promotion of Roman "⁠n0te⁠" Hamze from their Young Ninjas roster for "the upcoming events."

When contacted by HLTV for a comment, Ninjas in Pyjamas have clarified that the Belgian rifler is currently signed up for XSE Pro League and Stake Ranked Episode 3.

The promotion comes soon after the main team benched Artem "⁠cairne⁠" Mushynskyi following a disappointing season that culminated in the squad missing out on the Cologne Major.
