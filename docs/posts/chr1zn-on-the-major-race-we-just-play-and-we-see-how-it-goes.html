Chr1zN on the Major race: "We just play, and we see how it goes"

HEROIC staved off elimination from Stake Ranked Episode 1 on Thursday following a 2-0 victory against Nemesis, bouncing back from a 2-1 loss to a makeshift BetBoom roster on day one in Barcelona.

Currently ranked 13th in the Europe VRS, HEROIC are inside the qualification zone for the Cologne Major, but anything can happen until the invite cutoff on April 6. The recent losses to Tricked in the BC Game Masters Championship's Round of 16 and to Acend in the Roman Imperium Cup VII's quarter-finals were painful blows that threatened to derail their Major hopes.

"Our mental coach has done a lot of work with us, and I feel it has been better," Christoffer "⁠Chr1zN⁠" Storgaard told HLTV after the match against Nemesis.

"It has shown, I feel like, at least at the start, there were a lot more nerves than now. It's just a delicate topic, and it can come to fruition when you don't want it to, but we're trying to deal with it, and it's nice."

On Friday, HEROIC will go up against GamerLegion or Ninjas in Pyjamas in another do-or-die match. The Ninjas are also still in the race for one of the last Europe invites to the Major.

"NIP, of course, is in Major contention still, so that would be an important match for us to win," the Danish captain said.

"Hopefully, we go on and knock them out in the next one. There's nothing really much more to say about it, that's just how we'll have to deal with it."

Before you came here, you had the DraculaN win in February, then March was tough with some ups and downs. How was it coming into this important event for your Major qualification after that March?

It's been a bit tough. We've had very good chances. I feel like, against better opponents, we've been showing really good form. Then we come down and play BC Game Masters, where we fall apart. It's hard to narrow down why that was, if it was pressure from the Major or it was just an off-game, because I feel like we rebounded nicely into the first day of Roman [Imperium Cup], and then everything kind of happened against us since.

I don't know why that is, but I feel like coming into Stake now we're still confident. We know that we can play good CS, but we also know that we have very high highs and very low lows, so it's about narrowing that down so we play better on our off days.

This win here gives us a bit more confidence. It's not easy to come off of two losses, especially when there's a lot of pressure, so I hope that we can bring this forward and show a good result here. Hopefully, we'll manage to go to the Major.

You're acknowledging this pressure that a lot of teams are feeling right now because the Major race is so tight, and we're in these last days before the cut-off. How are you guys managing this? Have you had talks about how to deal with the fact that you're in this race?

Yeah, we've talked a lot about, it has been a talking point for us for a while. HEROIC didn't make the last one before I was here. They were in a similar situation, where it looked easy on paper, but it wasn't so easy when it came down to it. It's kind of the same situation we're in here now. I feel like it's natural, in a sense, because we're still a young team and it can be a bit tough.

It's so tough, now I've just given up at this point. Now we just play, and we see how it goes. If we make it we make it, and if not, then we don't

Also, including myself, it's the first time I'm playing with the pressure of having to qualify for the Major. It's not like something we strive towards because it's a nice dream to have, it's actually a reality that we should be qualifying, so I think it's very different, and it is something we have brought up a lot.

Our mental coach has done a lot of work with us, and I feel it has been better. It has shown, I feel like, at least at the start, there were a lot more nerves than now. It's just a delicate topic, and it can come to fruition when you don't want it to, but we're trying to deal with it, and it's nice.

You're really getting into the last stretch now. You had a loss yesterday and a win today in the lower bracket, and now it just depends on how far you can go in the lower bracket and what other teams do. How is it navigating this situation with the uncertainty and the need to perform?

It's so tough. Before we even started our LAN run, we were talking about how many we needed to go to and all of this, and if you ask me now, I feel like we should have only gone to one. [Laughs] Then we would have had better chances! But if you asked me before, the three that we had, I think, were very good for us.

It's so tough, now I've just given up at this point. Now we just play, and we see how it goes. If we make it we make it, and if not, then we don't. It is what it is. I feel like we had our opportunities, and if we don't go, then we probably didn't deserve it anyway.

Opening against BetBoom threw you into the lower bracket, immediately with your back against the wall. How was having to experience that?

It was tough. It wasn't the ideal scenario. Especially for that BetBoom game, both of us have been traveling for a long time, and they're playing with stand-ins, but they've been doing very well.

We won the first map, and then we went on to our pick... Maybe we were a bit too overconfident, I would say. Then maybe it started to create some pressure or something, and we didn't end up closing that game. As a match-up, it was a good one for us, but that's what it is. Now I don't even know who we're playing next...

G2 and BetBoom are on one side of the bracket, but it's the one you wouldn't get. The other side of the bracket...

It's NIP and GamerLegion, right?

Yeah, so you would play one of them.

Yeah. NIP, of course, is in Major contention still, so that would be an important match for us to win. Hopefully, we go on and knock them out in the next one. There's nothing really much more to say about it, that's just how we'll have to deal with it.
