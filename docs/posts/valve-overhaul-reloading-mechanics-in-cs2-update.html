Valve overhaul reloading mechanics in CS2 update

Valve has released a new update for CS2, headlined by changes to the reloading mechanics.

"We think the decision to reload should have higher stakes, so in today's update reloading has been redesigned," Valve's update notes say.

After the change, you will now drop the used magazine and remove all of its remaining ammo when reloading, instead of the leftover bullets being put back into your ammunition reserve.

The change also brought changes to magazine sizes for different weapons. Most will have three additional magazines, such as the M4A1-S and the AK-47, while the M4A4 will have four.

Possibly the biggest change came to the AWP, which will have just two additional magazines, meaning a total of 15 possible shots per round.

Alongside the reloading changes, the update also added Map Guides to Competitive matches, helping players pick up new tricks. These guides can be used during the first five rounds of the half in Competitive matches. An option to play Custom Games with friends has also been introduced.
