karrigan to debut for Falcons vs K27 as PGL Astana opening match-ups revealed

PGL has revealed the opening matchups for PGL Astana 2026, with the $1,600,000 LAN set to kick off soon on May 9.

All eyes will be firmly locked on Falcons as the event gets underway in Kazakhstan as veteran IGL Finn "⁠karrigan⁠" Andersen will be making his debut for his new team, and the Dane has been handed a matchup with K27 for his opening game.

The bout could be a deceptively tricky one for Falcons as K27 are not only a team who only narrowly missed out on an IEM Cologne Major berth, but they were also recently reinforced by the addition of Petr "⁠fame⁠" Bolyshev on a short-term loan.

Considering the absence of Vitality from the team list for the event, Falcons will be amongst a raft of teams who have a serious chance of taking home the trophy, the field seeming wide open as world No. 2 Natus Vincere are also not in the mix.

Elsewhere in the opening round Yasin "⁠xfl0ud⁠" Koç will have the chance to take on his former teammates in Aurora, whilst Spirit will take on The Huns in a rematch of the opening round game of BLAST Open Lisbon 2025, a game remembered for Yesuntumur "⁠nin9⁠" Gantulga's incredible 1v4 Deagle clutch.
