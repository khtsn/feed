kyousuke to miss NRG clash due to visa issues

Maksim "⁠kyousuke⁠" Lukin has been ruled out of Falcons' opening match at BLAST Open Rotterdam against NRG due to visa issues, the Saudi organization has announced. His availability for the rest of the tournament remains in doubt.

Falcons Force player Matej "⁠NucleonZ⁠" Trajkoski will be filling in for the Russian player as long as needed. It's the second time that the Macedonian has been called in as a substitute for kyousuke, who also missed the Bounty Season 1 Finals in January due to visa issues.

With NucleonZ as a stand-in, Falcons managed to reach the final in Malta, beating Liquid and Vitality before losing out to PARIVISION in three maps.

During that event, Damjan "⁠kyxsan⁠" Stoilkovski said that kyousuke's visa situation meant the team had to be selective about which tournaments they prioritized. "A lot of Schengen events are happening this year, so it was impossible to get kyousuke for all the events," the Macedonian IGL told HLTV.
