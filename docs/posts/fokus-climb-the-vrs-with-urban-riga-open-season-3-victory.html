FOKUS climb the VRS with Urban Riga Open Season 3 victory

FOKUS won the Urban Riga Open Season 3 LAN with a 2-0 victory over Phantom in two overtime maps on Ancient, 22-20, and Nuke, 16-13.

The grand final was notable for missing the tournament's favorites, 3DMAX, eliminated by Phantom in the semi-finals, making the grand final largely inconsequential in the VRS race for the IEM Cologne Major as the two finalists sit well out of reach.

Phantom knock 3DMAX out in semi-finals of Urban Riga Open

FOKUS, who have been active for just a month, did get a huge boost in the ranking, however, adding 310 points for a total of 1217 and jumping 78 positions to 53rd, while Phantom's 72 points puts them with a total of 1125 in 82nd place.

Before reaching the grand final, FOKUS beat OlyBet and Wildcard in the playoffs, while Phantom knocked out ASTRAL before their victory over the tournament's favorites in the semi-finals.

3DMAX, despite losing to weaker opposition, still came out with positive points, adding 16 to reach 1408 and jumping one spot in the ranking, to 20th place.

FOKUS passed their second LAN test with flying colors after a 7-8th finish at Parken Challenge, and will soon get to take another big step as a team when they fly to Romania for PGL Bucharest, which kicks off on April 4, after securing a spot in the tournament via the European closed qualifier.
