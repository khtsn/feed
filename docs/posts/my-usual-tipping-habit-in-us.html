My usual tipping habit in US

I have been in US since 2017. I generally tip as following - 1. Restaurant dine-in where waiter takes orders and serves food to the table - 10% (15% when I have a great experience) 2. Restaurant dine-in with self service (like McD, Burger King, etc.) - 5% 3. Ubereats home delivery - 10% (upto max
