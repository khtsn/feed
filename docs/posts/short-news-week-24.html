Graffiti incoming?

A new women's league has been announced, the Fragster Challenger EU Female Masters, with $5,000 in prizes. The league will have two divisions with promotions and relegations, and is open to cisgender and transgender women based in Europe.

The league is expected to begin in summer 2026, following open qualifiers in July, with plans already in place for two seasons in 2027.

IEM Krakow, which will feature an eight-team playoff and four arena days, will have a 360 stage setup, Director Esports for Counter-Strike at EFG, Marc Winther, posted on X. Tickets for the event in Poland will go live on July 2.
