FNATIC reaches EMEA grand final with 2-0 win

FNATIC has defeated Team Heretics 2-0 in the EMEA Stage 1 upper final. They are now in the grand final, their fourth EMEA final in the partnership era. Team Heretics remains in the event, but will descend to the lower bracket, where they will play against EMEA's final Masters: Toronto team.

The match began on FNATIC's map pick, Fracture, with a a strong finish on attack ending the map 13-6. The game continued on Icebox, where FNATIC remained in control throughout the map. In the end, a 13-8 on their opponent's map pick meant FNATIC won the series 2-0, sending them to the grand final and dropping Heretics to the lower bracket.

FNATIC will receive two bans in the grand final due to reaching the match through the upper bracket. Team Heretics, meanwhile, will play the lower final against EMEA's third Toronto team, either Team Liquid or BBL Esports.
