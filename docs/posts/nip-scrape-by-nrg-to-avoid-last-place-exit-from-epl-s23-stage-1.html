NIP scrape by NRG to avoid last-place exit from EPL S23 Stage 1

Ninjas in Pyjamas have clung on to their tournament lives in Stage 1 of ESL Pro League Season 23 after outlasting NRG in a three-map series, needing a 1.54-rated performance by Kacper "⁠xKacpersky⁠" Gabara to edge out a double overtime victory on the Anubis decider.

It wasn't a convincing win by any means and won't provide much comfort to Marco "⁠Snappi⁠" Pfeiffer, who took to X after Ninjas in Pyjamas's losses to Legacy and M80 to state how different his team plays in practice compared to officials, but it was a crucial one as the team fights to keep its narrow Major hopes alive.

NRG ended up in the 0-2 pool after failing to record a map win over HEROIC and Monte, and started the elimination game disastrously by getting blown out 4-13 on their Mirage pick. But a takeover on Ancient by Michael "⁠Grim⁠" Wince — who tallied a 2.15 rating, 27-11 K-D, an ace in round five, and two 1v2 clutches — spurred a revival for the North American side as they closed out the map 13-7 to force an Anubis decider.

There, NRG struggled despite starting on the offense, with multi-kills from Rasmus "⁠sjuush⁠" Beck and xKacpersky powering NIP to a 6-4 lead after a 0-3 start. NRG won the last two of the half and added another pistol win to regain the lead, 10-6, but lost control after NIP converted a half buy and xKacpersky completed a 3v5 recovery by clutching a 1v2 two rounds later.

The Pole continued to find high-impact multi-frags to put NIP on match point, but a clutch going NRG's way in round 24 forced overtime. Ninjas in Pyjamas then swept the first half of extra rounds, but fumbled three match points and needed double overtime to finally convert a messy series victory.

Other notable results from the third round saw Monte, 3DMAX, paiN, and G2 pick up wins over HEROIC, Gaimin Gladiators, Passion UA, and M80, respectively, with the former quartet now playing for a spot in Stage 2 and the latter fighting for their tournament lives in the 1-2 pool.
