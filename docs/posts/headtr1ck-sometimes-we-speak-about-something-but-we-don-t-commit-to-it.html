headtr1ck: "Sometimes we speak about something but we don't commit to it"

Inner Circle have suffered elimination from PGL Bucharest, their fate sealed by defeat in the all-Ukrainian derby with B8. Not only was it a national derby, it was a grudge match for Daniil "⁠headtr1ck⁠" Valitov as he was facing the team that recently benched him, something he called a "surprise."

Whilst Inner Circle are heading home early, the event was not all bad for the young squad as they scored a memorable victory over FaZe earlier in the group stage. "It is good to beat those nicknames, you know? It gives you confidence in the future," headtr1ck said of taking down FaZe.

headtr1ck spoke with HLTV following his team's exit from PGL Bucharest to discuss their run in Romania, his thoughts on their potential to improve, and his recent departure from B8.

What are your thoughts after that series?

I'm pretty sad about Dust2 because I really felt we had momentum on second side, we were winning 11-10 and I think we gave them too much space on that round. They won with Tec-9s and then they finished the game, so I think it was a crucial and important round. We also lose the next two rounds without talking, you know? It was pretty sad as I understand it could be our pick, and even if they win Mirage it would be a third map, so I think it was pretty sad the last couple of rounds.

Overall I'm happy that we managed to beat FaZe yesterday. Even though they are in a pretty hard situation right now, it is still good to beat those nicknames, you know? It gives you confidence in the future.

Overall I'm happy that we managed to beat FaZe yesterday. Even though they are in a pretty hard situation right now, it is still good to beat those nicknames

Was it harder to lose this game because that's your ex-team?

Obviously you always want to win especially if it's your ex-team, but to be fair I don't think we deserved to win this match because we weren't on the same page on Mirage. We didn't listen to each other, we were trolling some rounds I would say, and because of that they are able to close games like that.

Overall how do you feel about your team's run at this tournament? Like you say you beat FaZe, which is a bright spot.

I have mixed feelings because I feel that we need to step up in those small details and it will be way easier to play for everyone. I'm talking about just playing 100% rounds against pistols, push your economy when they have eco for example, and I have a feeling that sometimes we speak about something but we don't commit to it.

Overall, as I said, we were playing two matches first where it was 1-0 in our favour, so for me it shows that if we build the team together and if everyone will accept their mistakes and try to fix them, then we definitely have a bright future in this team. I see very good glimpses, but as I said, sometimes we don't listen to the mistakes we are trying to fix.

I wanted to ask about you joining Inner Circle. Was it a surprise to you to get benched on B8?

Yeah it was, it was a surprise because I really had a feeling that we were in a peak. At the previous Major we lost to NAVI in two close games I would say, maybe not in terms of scoreline but in terms of situations, and I really felt that we found our groove. We also had many invites, so I was pretty surprised.

Everything happens for a reason and I am full focus right now on a new team, and I want to grind with them and build new opportunities with them. That's my main goal right now.

It was a surprise because I really had a feeling that we were in a peak

What was the reason B8 gave you for your benching?

The main reason was that they tried to test different AWPers with different playstyles, but I don't know if it's a true reason or just to say something, you know?

Inner Circle is obviously a good opportunity for you. How do you feel about this team going forwards?

I feel pretty good, but as I said we need more discipline. We have young players, we have a young IGL and me also, we need to learn. It is a good opportunity for me because right now the system works that you can play one good event and climb up to top 30, and it's pretty comfortable I would say if you just trust the process and play one good event. So I have good vibes about that.

I think we could be fixing our mistakes way faster and could take some Ws at this event, because as I said we started 1-0 two series in a row, and today as well we could have started 1-0, but shit happens.
