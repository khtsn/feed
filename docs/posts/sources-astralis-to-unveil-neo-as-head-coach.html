Sources: Astralis to unveil NEO as head coach

Filip "⁠NEO⁠" Kubski will be Astralis' new head coach, sources have revealed to HLTV.

The team parted ways with Casper "⁠ruggah⁠" Due after a disastrous Stage 2 exit at the IEM Cologne Major, and CEO Jonas "⁠calc⁠" Gundersen told Pley.gg that the next coach would be an international hire.

"There’s something about the homogeneous culture of Denmark where I think we were a victim of our own success sometimes," calc said. "So, we got complacent [...] I think we’d like to try a more international performance culture, but still keeping our Nordic roots."

NEO would join after leaving FaZe in March, having been relieved of his duties as results took a nosedive this year.

While FaZe coach, NEO made it to three Major finals and lifted three trophies at IEM Sydney 2023, CAC 2023, and IEM Chengdu 2024.

The 39-year-old Polish legend's removal, which came before the invites to the Major were final, came as a surprise to FaZe. "I don't think, per se, NEO was a problem," Finn "⁠karrigan⁠" Andersen said at the time. "We as a team have not performed, and everybody is part of the problem."
