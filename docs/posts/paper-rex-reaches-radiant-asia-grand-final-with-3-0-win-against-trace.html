Paper Rex reaches Radiant Asia grand final with 3-0 win against Trace

Paper Rex has defeated Trace Esports 3-0 in the Radiant Asia lower final. They will next play against DFM in the grand final, a rematch of the upper final. Trace has been eliminated from the tournament, finishing in third place after taking out DRX earlier in the bracket.

The match began on Split, with Paper Rex taking a quick 13-5 on their first map pick. Paper Rex then won Ascent 13-4, winning the mirror match in dominant fashion. A closer Sunset followed, but the Southeast Asian squad recovered to win the map 13-11 and take the series 3-0.

Paper Rex will now need to face off against an undefeated DFM, who came out on top 2-0 in their previous upper final matchup.
