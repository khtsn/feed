FUT breeze past B8 to enter EPL 2-2 pool

dem0n led the charge as FUT outclassed B8

FUT have moved into the 2-2 pool in Stage 2 of ESL Pro League 23 with a quick 2-0 victory against B8, who have been eliminated from this stage in 12th-14th place with a 1-3 record.

After a win against FURIA and two competitive series against Spirit and Natus Vincere, B8 simply looked out of their depth against the red-hot FUT squad. They quickly flamed out on Dust2 after a strong start and couldn't even put up a fight on Anubis.

FUT's second win of this stage — after a 2-1 victory against HEROIC on Saturday — was decorated by an imperious display from Dmytro "⁠dem0n⁠" Myroshnychenko, who posted on Anubis the second-highest map rating (2.08) since he joined the Turkish organization.

"Against 3DMAX and Astralis, we started to play more passively," Aulon "⁠Krabeni⁠" Fazlija said after the match "We were not playing our usual game.

"Today, we said, 'We should just play what we were doing before. Just don't get into situations where we don't like to be.'"

For the third time this year, FUT find themselves one victory away from the playoffs. In January, they lost to FURIA in BLAST Bounty's Round of 16. Last month, they fell to PARIVISION in PGL Cluj-Napoca's 2-2 pool.

"Maybe it's the day we can go through the playoffs," Krabeni said. "I hope so."
