FaZe included in PGL Bucharest day one matchups despite HLC Belgrade attendance

The matchups for day one of PGL Bucharest have been revealed, and FaZe remain on the team list despite their last-minute signup for HLC Belgrade Pro.

Read more

FaZe to attend HLC Belgrade PRO*

They are slated to play EYEBALLERS — a rematch of the BLAST Bounty defeat that put FaZe's 2026 on the wrong foot back in January — at 18:00 on April 4, which is day two of RELOG's Belgrade tournament.

It is unclear how FaZe intend to untie this knot, but with Belgrade being crucial for their Major hopes, it seems that forfeits may be required at PGL Bucharest. PGL and FaZe were contacted for comment but did not respond before the publication of this article.

The match of the day in Romania is likely to be PARIVISION against Legacy, while high seeds Astralis and The MongolZ take on MIBR and BC.Game, respectively.

Bucharest is an important event for Oleksandr "⁠s1mple⁠" Kostyliev's BC.Game, having failed to make the Cologne Major and subsuquently withdrawn from Journey Spring "to fix" their chemistry.

Day one will also see a North American derby between NRG and Voca, FUT take on Inner Circle, and B8 against Wildcard. The final matchup pits FOKUS against 3DMAX in the sternest test yet of Jakub "⁠kuben⁠" Gurczyński's new-look lineup.
