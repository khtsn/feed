Snax on if GL can beat NAVI: "I think one of them needs to break a leg or arm, and I need to be hungry as well"

Live

Janusz "⁠Snax⁠" Pogorzelski spoke to Martin "⁠STYKO⁠" Styk on the broadcast ahead of the grand final, and as always he was relaxed and affable in his answers.

"We prepared a little bit, but I don't think you can prepare the full five maps before the BO5, it's hard to remember everything. I think we know what to do and I hope for a good final, but we might lose 0-3 and it's going to be fine, because we are in the final.

"I think the stars need to align in a good way. I think one of them needs to break a leg or arm, and I need to be hungry as well. If those three things happen, we have a chance."

Tauson on NAVI: "I think we could have won the last game we played against them"

Live

With the grand final just around the corner, it's a good time to get caught up on what the grand finalists have been saying ahead of the all-important game.

Sebastian "⁠Tauson⁠" Tauson Lindelof spoke to HLTV after his team's win in the semi-finals, which came before he knew his grand final opponent. He was clear about who he wanted to play.

"I would like to play NAVI, to be honest. You can say it's more deserved for us to win, if we win. I would like to play NAVI and get revenge against them, because I think we could have won the last game we played against them."

Aleksi "⁠Aleksib⁠" Virolainen also spoke to HLTV after the semi-finals, and whilst he was clear in expressing his confidence, he won't be taking GamerLegion lightly.

"I'm feeling confident about the best-of-five. We played them two times, we won both times. Doesn't mean we're gonna take it any less serious; I think that they just have more motivation, more hunger, to get revenge. I think they have nothing to lose"

w0nderful leads the MVP race

Live

Ihor "⁠w0nderful⁠" Zhdanov has been in fine form since the Animgraph 2 update, averaging a 1.27 rating over two events.

He leads Natus Vincere in the MVP race in Atlanta, too, after his domination of Vitality in quarter-finals.

GamerLegion are more split: Sebastian "⁠Tauson⁠" Tauson Lindelof leads them in map wins, Milan "⁠hypex⁠" Polowiec is top overall, and Oldřich "⁠PR⁠" Nový has been best against top 10 opposition.

All in all, there's plenty to play for.
