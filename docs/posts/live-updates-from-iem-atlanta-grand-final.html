Live updates from IEM Atlanta grand final

Fredrik "⁠REZ⁠" Sterner spoke to Martin "⁠STYKO⁠" Styk on stream after the loss to Natus Vincere.

On feelings after defeat: I don't think we should be disappointed at all, to be honest. They are one of the best teams in the world and we're just not there yet, that's the truth of it."

On his performance: I felt pretty comfortable against them to be honest, and I think it showed as well in my game. So yeah, I was really confident in this game. I think we lost on a lot of experience and nervousness, stuff like this, I think we respected them a bit too much and you could see it in the game, we gave them way too much space on the map.
