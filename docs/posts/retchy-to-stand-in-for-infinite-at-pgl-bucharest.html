retchy to stand in for Infinite at PGL Bucharest

Voca have announced that Sebastian "⁠retchy⁠" Tropiano will replace Gage "⁠Infinite⁠" Green for the duration of PGL Bucharest after the latter was turned away from entry into the Schengen Area for an undisclosed passport/documentation issue.

"We couldn’t find a sub willing to travel for 10 days, so we brought retchy to help as a vibes/manager," the team's coach Kory "⁠SEMPHIS⁠" Friesen said about the decision on X.

"Gage [Infinite] got denied EU entry one day out and deported, leaving us to play with me (blind in one eye, need a cornea transplant) or retchy."

retchy's last appearance on HLTV was in 2021, shortly before he was hit with a five-year ban by ESIC after having been implicated in a match-fixing case in ESEA Premier, with the ban expiring mere days ago.
