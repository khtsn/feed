FURIA prevail in late-night game against TYLOO

Day one of the BLAST Open Rotterdam group stage came to an end with FURIA edging past TYLOO in a two-map victory (Mirage 13-8, Overpass 13-11).

Despite Gabriel "⁠FalleN⁠" Toledo's troops joking about the struggles of the late games on X, they got the job done and dispatched the Chinese side to make the upper-bracket semi-final.

FURIA's next opponent will be Aurora, while TYLOO will meet FaZe in the lower bracket.

The series started on Mirage, where TYLOO overcame a lacklustre offense to bring the scores back to 7-9, but FalleN's crisp double kill at 10-7 completely killed their momentum and allowed FURIA to sail to a 13-8 victory.

Overpass was a much closer affair, as Su "⁠Zero⁠" Jingshen's crisp pistol-round ace allowed TYLOO to once again come back and take the lead at 9-8. A plethora of highlights followed, with FURIA just about getting the job done by stopping Zero's clutch in the final round of regulation.
