9z keep Astana upset run alive at expense of lacklustre Falcons

9z have kept their run at PGL Astana going after defeating Falcons in a three-map series (Mirage 13-8, Nuke 9-13, Dust2 13-1).

The South American squad is now just one win away from the playoffs after already defeating PARIVISION on Saturday, while Falcons drop down to the 1-1 pool and are set to face off against Monte on Monday.

The series started well for Falcons, who ran away to a respectable 8-4 lead on the Nuke defense. Once the sides switched, however, any semblance of cohesion by Finn "⁠karrigan⁠" Andersen's troops left the server.

They failed to win a single round on the T side and struggled due to struggling for opening kills outside of a few trademark Nikola "⁠NiKo⁠" Kovač Deagle kills.

Mirage was a better performance for the star-studded roster, as they ran away to a 12-5 lead before slightly struggling to complete the job late on. Luciano "⁠luchov⁠" Herrera found a crisp 1vs3 to extend play even further, but a 4k by NiKo's hero rifle secured the map for Falcons.

Dust2 was where it all fell apart for karrigan's troops, though. They couldn't get to grips with the CT side and needed eleven rounds to get on the board, and while they avoided a disastrous 13-0 scoreline, a lost pistol in the second half meant the map ended 13-1 in 9z's favor in what is a worrying sign for the start of karrigan's reign.
