How Can Biden Woo Moderates?

Michelle Cottle and Patrick Healy

Produced by Jillian Weinberger and Vishakha Darbha

Opinion writer Michelle Cottle watched last night’s New Hampshire Primary results come in alongside supporters of Nikki Haley. She says the battle of head versus heart has just started. In this audio interview, Cottle speaks with Opinion’s deputy editor, Patrick Healy, to break down the primary polls, voter “vibes” and the feeling of déjà vu from 2016.
