Sharks take down HEROIC to kick off Major

Sharks have opened their account at IEM Cologne Major with a victory over HEROIC, picking up the win on Nuke thanks to a strong recovery after a slow start.

The result continues HEROIC's woeful run of form, extending their losing streak to six series in a row, whilst Sharks have started positively in the organization's second-ever Major appearance.

Early doors it seemed HEROIC would cruise into the 1-0 pool as they started 5-2 up on the favored CT side, but Sharks set about a comeback. More success gaining space and opening kills Outside, powered by Danilo "⁠doc⁠" Barros, were enough to secure five rounds at the half.

The teams traded force-buys early in the second half but it was Sharks who stabilized, and once they did it was virtually one-way traffic. They needed a clutch or two, but Sharks got the job done to start their Major campaign with a win.
