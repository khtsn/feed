PARIVISION, Falcons, MOUZ among teams invited to CS Asia Championships

Perfect World has announced the 14 teams invited to the CS Asia Championships, a $1 million tournament taking place in Shanghai, China, from May 20-24.

Headlining the list are five top 10 teams, PARIVISION, Falcons, MOUZ, Aurora and The MongolZ, all of whom are slated to play back-to-back events after PGL Astana, which ends just two days before the Chinese competition.

The remainder ranges between the top 20 to top 40 teams, starting from reigning champions Legacy from the 2025 edition and ending with their compatriots MIBR.

The 14 teams that received direct invites based on the February VRS will be joined by TYLOO and Lynn Vision, who secured their place at the tournament through the Asia closed qualifier earlier this month.
