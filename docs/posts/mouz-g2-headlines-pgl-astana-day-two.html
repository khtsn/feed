MOUZ-G2 headlines PGL Astana day two

Day one of PGL Astana came to an end with FURIA getting the better of Monte in a 2-0 series, meaning the day ended with just two lower seeds securing upsets.

The results mean that the surprises of the opening day, namely 9z and HEROIC, are set to face off against Falcons and FURIA, respectively.

Elsewhere in the 1-0 pool, MOUZ will meet G2 in the standout game of the round, while Spirit lock horns with The MongolZ.
