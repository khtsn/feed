100 Thieves win Parken Challenger Championship Season 6

poiii was 100T's highest-rated player at the event with a 1.38

100 Thieves, who were runners-up at DraculaN Season 5 in February, have won their first LAN since returning to Counter-Strike in 2026 at Parken Challenger Championship Season 6.

Their year so far included a 5-8th place at Roman Imperium Cup V, and 5-6th place finishes at Parken Challenger Championship Season 2 and CCT Global Finals 2026.

Taking place outside of the VRS crunch in the race for the Major, the field at this second-tier LAN in Copenhagen was not a stacked one, with Acend (62) and BASEMENT BOYS (100), the only other two teams in the top 100 in our world ranking.

100 Thieves cruised to the upper bracket final with 2-0 victories over A Great Chaos and BASEMENT BOYS, and dropped their only map of the playoffs against Acend, beating them 2-1 on the way to the grand final.

The two HLTV Top 50 Prospects players on 100 Thieves, Alex "⁠poiii⁠" Nyholm Sundgren (11) and William "⁠sirah⁠" Kjærsgaard (14), led the way in the grand final, with 1.35 and 1.34 ratings, as well as at the whole event, finishing second and third with 1.38 and 1.33 ratings, respectively, behind Deyvid "⁠h4rn⁠" Benchev's 1.41.

100 Thieves won the rematch against BASEMENT BOYS without a hitch, and although they didn't keep the Ukrainians to five rounds in the whole series as they did earlier in the bracket, they didn't let them hit double digits on either Dust2 (13-9) or Overpass (13-6).

This victory has given 100 Thieves a 44-point boost in the VRS ranking, pushing them up to 39th, just seven below their all-time high of 32nd, which they reached in March.
