Vitality announce academy team

ZywOo's bold prediction headlines the French organization's new academy.

Vitality have announced an academy team, Vitality Academy, confirming the 'Project H' five as the inaugural roster for the project.

“Creating a Counter-Strike academy means investing in the future of our ecosystem," co-founder Neo said in a press release. "For esports to continue growing and structuring itself, it must rely on strong development pathways capable of identifying, supporting, and nurturing tomorrow’s talents. At Team Vitality, we want to perform today, but also build the champions of tomorrow and help elevate the entire scene to the next level."

The roster is headlined by 16-year-old French prospect Thiebault "⁠Katkame⁠" Ulysse. The teenage sniper was chosen as Mathieu "⁠ZywOo⁠" Herbaut's bold prediction in 2025, and is also managed by the world number one's Prodigy Agency.

Standing behind the team as coach will be Pablo "VdaK1NG" Escobar, who joined the Bees as a data analyst in September and was operating as the coach for the 'Project H' precursor team.

He said on X: "It was not originally planned for me to retain the dedicated head coach position, but the team has been working well, and I’m happy to take on a new role that will allow me to keep helping Vitality make history 3 years from now."

The team also includes Luca-Adrian "⁠lucaZ⁠" Gavrilut, previously of NAVI Junior, and 19-year-old in-game leader Kirill "⁠Dafra1D⁠" Polieiko. Finally, riflers Aleks "⁠Reqqen⁠" Frolov and Patrick "⁠patrenzo⁠" Hauer round out the roster.

As Project H, the team have already qualified for the Season 56 Finals of ESEA and taken down Metizport in the CCT Contenders qualifiers.

Thiebault "⁠Katkame⁠" Ulysse
