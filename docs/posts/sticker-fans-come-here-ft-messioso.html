Sticker fans come here ft. messioso

Danish "⁠Nohte⁠" Allana and Harry "⁠NER0⁠" Richards were joined by 100 Thieves' Head of CS Operations, Graham "⁠messioso⁠" Pitt, to discuss the new sticker system instituted by Valve for the IEM Cologne Major.

Some organizations and players are already in conflict regarding how the revenue should be divided, with pre-existing contracts already offering players a share of the total sticker pool and the new system giving players an outright cut of the total.

Valve release IEM Cologne Major stickers

The panel reviewed the estimated payouts from previous Majors for teams and players, and how the divide might shake out under the new system, which will reward performance but also see lower-ranked organizations take a hit while their players receive a bigger share.

How that has already led to conflict between some players and organizations ahead of the biggest event of the year, the pros and cons of directly purchasing stickers versus the capsule system, and community discussion around the change were also explored later in the show.
