FURIA roster to miss first Kickoff match due to visa delays

FURIA FURIA Brazil Rank #3 heat Olavo Marcelo raafa Rafael Lima mwzera Leonardo Serrati havoc Ilan Eloy Khalil Khalil Schmidt has announced that their full roster is expected to miss their first match of Americas Kickoff on Jan. 17 due to visa delays.

The team has reportedly arranged but not yet announced a substitute roster to play in their stead. As stated by the team's general manager Lucas "lukzera" Soares in the announcement, the main roster is tentatively expected to be able to make it to either their second or third match depending on the results. FURIA's second match would be on Jan. 19 if the substitute roster wins the first match and Jan. 24 if they lose.

According to sources in communication with the team, FURIA initiated the visa process on Nov. 25, following the same procedures and timeframe as the other Brazilian teams in VCT Americas. However, FURIA's application was unexpectedly met with significant delays by United States travel authorities and only received approval on Jan. 11, which does not leave enough time for the team due to remaining steps in the visa process.
