Sources: lauNX exploring options in the off-season

lauNX joined FUT in August 2025 and helped the team win PGL Bucharest earlier this year

Laurențiu "⁠lauNX⁠" Țârlea has asked FUT for permission to pursue other opportunities for the second half of the season, sources with knowledge of the matter told HLTV.

His decision is unrelated to FUT's results at the Major, the sources said, adding that the player expressed his wish for a new challenge even before the start of the team's campaign in Cologne.

FUT went out of the Major in 9th-11th place following a loss to BetBoom in the final round of Stage 3. Previously, the team had lost to Vitality and G2 and picked up wins over B8 and MOUZ.

lauNX joined FUT in August 2025 from Nexus as a replacement for Kévin "⁠misutaaa⁠" Rabier, who had signed for the Turkish organization earlier that year alongside the core of the former NAVI Junior squad.

The Romanian has averaged a 1.10 rating while with FUT, playing an important role as the team established itself as a tier-1 side in 2026. In April, FUT lifted their first notable trophy as they won PGL Bucharest.

In the aftermath of FUT's elimination from the Major, lauNX admitted that the future looked uncertain.
