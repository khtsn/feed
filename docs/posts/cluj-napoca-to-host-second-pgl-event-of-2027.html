Cluj-Napoca to host second PGL event of 2027

PGL has announced more details about the second event of its 2027 events, which will take place in Cluj-Napoca from February 11-21.

As in the 2025 and 2026 editions, PGL Cluj-Napoca 2027 will culminate in the playoffs at the BTarena, the same venue that hosted the 2015 Major by DreamHack.

PGL has six events planned for 2027 and will also run the English broadcast for the CS Asia Championships. Last month, it announced that Bucharest will host its first event of 2027, scheduled for January 17-24.

PGL Cluj-Napoca 2027 will feature a $1 million prize pool, split equally between team prize and club share. The Romanian TO will also offer $300,000 in VRS bonuses, with the top five VRS-ranked teams each receiving a $60,000 bonus for accepting their tournament invite.

Invites to the Romanian event will be sent out on November 2 to 14 teams based on the global VRS. The last two spots will be determined by a European closed qualifier, scheduled for November 15-18, featuring six invitees and two teams coming from an open qualifier.
