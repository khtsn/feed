Astralis facing elimination in Cologne after loss to TYLOO

VRS No.10 Astralis are one loss away from elimination in Stage 2 of the IEM Cologne Major 2 following a 13-6 Nuke loss to TYLOO in the 1-1 pool.

Alarm bells are certainly ringing inside the Danish organization after the team's third straight heavy loss on Nuke. Just like in Saturday's 13-5 loss to 9z, Astralis looked sluggish and off the pace on the CT side and then couldn't get much going on offense.

Love "⁠phzy⁠" Smidebrant and Jakob "⁠jabbi⁠" Nygaard struggled for impact for most of the first half, with the Swedish AWPer picking up four of his seven CT kills in the final two rounds.

A free-flowing TYLOO side romped to a 7-5 lead, and the scoreline could have been even more lopsided if it weren't for a heroic Five-SeveN play by Rasmus "⁠HooXi⁠" Nielsen and a late clutch from Gytis "⁠ryu⁠" Glušauskas.

Astralis came out swinging in the second half with a clean pistol round, but their respite proved brief. TYLOO hit back in the following round and then kept their opponents at bay for the rest of the game, with Yi "⁠JamYoung⁠" Yang dealing the finishing blow with a clean 4K in the final round.
