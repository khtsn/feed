B8 eliminate BC.Game from IEM Atlanta

B8 stayed alive at IEM Atlanta after demolishing BC.Game in a two-map series (Overpass 13-2, Mirage 13-11).

Their lower-bracket run will now continue against the loser of paiN vs. FUT on Wednesday.

Meanwhile, BC.Game suffer yet another early elimination after the loss, but their Atlanta campaign can be caveated by the fact they played with coach Robin "⁠ScrunK⁠" Röpke on the server, with the German veteran struggling for individual impact and averaging a 0.56 rating across the event.

"It feels like shit," the stand-in IGL said after the loss in a broadcast interview. "We still had higher expectations for ourselves, even with me standing in."

A better sign was Azbayar "⁠Senzu⁠" Munkhbold's debut performance, however, as the Mongolian rifler finished the event as the team's highest-rated member with only one map rated below 1.00.

The series began with B8 domination, as the Ukrainian squad won the first map 13-2 and raced away to an 8-2 lead on Mirage.

That was when BC.Game finally woke up and started a spirited comeback attempt, but the deficit proved too big to overcome despite their individuals finally starting to warm up, with the game ending 13-11 in B8's favor.
