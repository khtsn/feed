paTiTek returns to Heretics as stand-in

Team Heretics Team Heretics Europe Unranked MiniBoo Dominykas Lukaševičius Wo0t Mert Alkan RieNs Enes Ecirli benjyfishy Benjy David Fish Boo Ričardas Lukaševičius have welcomed back Patryk "paTiTek" Fabrowski for their upcoming matches at Masters Shanghai and the foreseeable future.

Having initially signed with the Spanish organization towards the start of the 2024 VCT, paTiTek and the rest of the Heretics performed a flawless run through the EMEA Kickoff group stage. The organization upset Natus Vincere Natus Vincere Europe Unranked ANGE1 Kyrylo Karasov Shao Andrey Kiprsky Zyppan Pontus Eek SUYGETSU Dmitry Ilyushin ardiis Ardis Svarenieks to qualify for Masters Madrid, all while paTiTek stood in for Mert "Wo0t" Alkan due to age restrictions.

paTiTek has been one of the most successful stand-ins in the VCT. (Photo: Colin Young-Wolff/Riot Games)

Despite exiting Spain winless, Heretics once again qualified for another Masters event by working their way through the Stage 1 playoffs of the EMEA League. Unfortunately, the grueling environment of the professional scene took its toll on a young Dominykas "MiniBoo" Lukaševičius, who had been balancing full time competing with his education.

Team Heretics announced that the young superstar duelist would be taking a break from competing while he focused on his education, leaving a vacant roster spot now filled by paTiTek as the team travels to China in hopes of leaving with a trophy.

It is unknown how long paTiTek will remain with Heretics, as there is no set date on when MiniBoo will return to competing.
