Passion UA, fnatic, and OG eliminated as BCG Masters Championship reaches playoffs

BC.Game Masters Championship Season 1 is headed to playoffs after the conclusion of its 16-team GSL group stage, which notably saw Passion UA, fnatic, and OG suffer blows to their Cologne Major hopes with early eliminations.

fnatic and OG were already well out of invite range on our predicted list, but the latter still had a slim chance, according to Mischief, before a loss to 9INE in the Group D decider match likely sealed their fate.

fnatic also had little to no chance to qualify, but are almost guaranteed out following losses to MOUZ NXT in the winners' match and a loss to aimclub in the group decider.

Passion UA, meanwhile, are in hot water after being consigned to a last-place finish by BASEMENT BOYS and Tricked.

The race for the final Americas invite for the Major was already tight, with Gaimin Gladiators currently predicted to take the spot, and now Passion UA will have to almost entirely rely on making a deep run at DraculaN Season 6 and hope the teams around them flounder at FERJEE In House 2026.

MOUZ NXT, BESTIA, Eternal Fire, and Inner Circle walked away as victors from the group stage after going undefeated, with aimclub, Tricked, HYPERSPIRIT, and 9INE advancing through the decider matches to complete the single-elimination playoff bracket.

The eight highest-ranked teams that registered for the event were seeded directly into the playoffs and will face the advancing group stage teams, with the highlight match of the Round of 16 seeing Guy "⁠NertZ⁠" Iluz make his debut for G2 against aimclub.
