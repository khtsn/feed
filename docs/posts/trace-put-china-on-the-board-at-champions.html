Trace put China on the board at Champions

The second match of the day was a third straight EMEA vs. China duel in the opening round of Champions 2024. After FPX and BLG fell to TH and FNC, Trace was looking to secure China's first win, up against Vitality.

A back and forth battle led to a massive upset as Trace finally got China their first win of the event. Across all three maps LuoK1ng was a standout performer, putting up 64 kills to 44 deaths.

Trace moved into the winners match of group C to face LEV for a spot in the playoffs. Vitality were dropped to the losers match where they will have to get past Talon to stay alive.
