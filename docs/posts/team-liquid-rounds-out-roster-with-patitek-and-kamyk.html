Later, he returned to the team in place of MiniBoo for Masters Shanghai after the Duelist stepped back to focus on completing his education. As the team's flex, kamyk played six different agents in Split 2 and achieved a 1.14 overall rating. Joblife claimed first prize in France to qualify for EMEA Ascension, where they were narrowly eliminated in the group stage with a 2-2 record.

Team Liquid built their roster coming into 2024 around the 2023 Apeks core of Georgio "Keiko" Sanassy, Enzo "Enzo" Mestari, and James "Mistic" Orfila. They eventually ended the year at eighth place in VCT EMEA with a 4-6 regular season record, failing to qualify to any international events over the course of the year.

Following this result, Liquid parted ways with Elias "Jamppi" Olkkonen, Mistic, Enzo, and former head coach Emil "eMIL" Sandgren. They retained Keiko and Ayaz "nAts" Akhmetshin and announced the signings of Kamil "kamo" Frąckowiak and new coaches LohaN and Daniel "yaotziN." Roczniak in October. With the additions of paTiTek and kamyk, Liquid has finalized their starting roster for the 2025 season.

Liquid will next play on Nov. 30 against GIANTX as part of the Monsters Reloaded 2024 main event.
