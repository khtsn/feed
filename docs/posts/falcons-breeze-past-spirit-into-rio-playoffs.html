Falcons breeze past Spirit into Rio playoffs

NiKo and company become the second team to confirm their attendance in the Farmasi Arena.

Falcons became the next team to qualify for the playoffs after destroying Spirit in a two-map series (Anubis 13-4, Mirage 13-5).

The series was one-way traffic throughout, but the result could have been much worse if Spirit hadn't won three out of the four pistol rounds across the series.

Falcons' individuals poked holes into Spirit's setup time after time again, while the Russian squad's usual savior Danil "⁠donk⁠" Kryshkovets went completely missing on Mirage and posted a 0.30 rating.

Falcons will now meet the final boss, Vitality, in a battle for a semi-final spot, while Spirit drop down to the lower bracket to face RED Canids.
