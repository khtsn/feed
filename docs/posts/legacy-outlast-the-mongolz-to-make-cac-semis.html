Legacy outlast The MongolZ to make CAC semis

Legacy are still on the road to defend their CS Asia Championships title thanks to a 2-1 victory over The MongolZ, outlasting their opponents down the stretch by virtue of some individual brilliance.

In a series characterized by streaks of dominance interspersed with messy, brawly halves of CS, special moments of individual playmaking ended up being the difference. Legacy have Bruno "⁠latto⁠" Rebelatto to thank for their victory as the rifler was immense on both Inferno and Nuke, the two maps they won.

As a result Legacy move on to the semi-finals, where a Brazilian derby against MIBR awaits them.

The MongolZ will be disappointed with the result as they had the series in their hands, leading on the favored CT side of Nuke, only to let it slip. Both teams had been guilty of disjointed play throughout the series but The MongolZ suffered when it mattered most, finding themselves disconnected in a 3v1 against latto and allowing him to clutch the series.

As mentioned before latto was the man of the hour on Inferno, the rifler virtually single-handedly carrying Legacy to the victory. The MongolZ were in control on their offense, finding both bombsites at their mercy in the majority of rounds, but latto bagged a couple of important multi-kills to give his team something to work with in the second half.

bLitz runs through the flames for round-saving AWP 3K

With the sides switched Legacy set about running the map back from a 3-9 deficit, and they did so with aplomb. latto only got better as Andrei "⁠arT⁠" Piovezan's high tempo, relentless offensive approach worked perfectly with the momentum Legacy generated, and The MongolZ could not recover, granting the Brazilians the lead in the series.

The MongolZ levelled the game on Mirage, taking advantage of a disjointed performance by Legacy. A second round force-buy win set the Mongolians on their way on their CT side and, despite the best efforts of a sterling Vinicius "⁠n1ssim⁠" Pereira, they converted the map in the second half by pouncing upon isolated defenders time and time again.

latto closes the series with sick ace clutch

Both sides suffered from scrappy play early on Nuke, low-buys being traded back and forth before Legacy eventually managed to assert control on the CT side, aided by a armorless pistol 4K by Guilherme "⁠saadzin⁠" Pacheco and some high-impact multi-kills from arT.

The MongolZ put together a streak of rounds at the start of their defense to race out into the lead, but Legacy slowed their offense down and began to find success in drawn-out mid-round situations. arT tried to switch things up, but a Ramp rush was dispelled by The MongolZ to keep the series going, only for latto to come up huge with everything on the line, a majestic ace clutch giving his team a semi-final berth.
