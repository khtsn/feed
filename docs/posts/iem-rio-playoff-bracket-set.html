IEM Rio playoff bracket set

The six-team playoff bracket of IEM Rio 2026 has been set, following the conclusion of the event's group stage.

Kicking off the quarter-finals is a series between MOUZ and Spirit, the second meeting between the two sides this year. The first time they clashed in 2026, in the third-place decider of IEM Krakow, Spirit came out on top in a straightforward two-map affair.

The winner of this quarter-final will take on Falcons in their semi-final match.

The other quarter-final features a clash between Natus Vincere and Vitality. The two sides last met in the grand final of BLAST Open Rotterdam, where Dan "⁠apEX⁠" Madesclaire's troops took down NAVI in a straightforward 3-0 affair to secure the trophy. Now, Natus Vincere will have to secure revenge against Vitality if they wish to have a chance of extending their grand final streak to three in a row.

The victor of this quarter-final will then move on to face hometown heroes FURIA in the semi-finals.
