Vitality march past BetBoom to reach playoffs

Vitality are the fourth team to earn a place in the playoffs of the IEM Cologne Major, setting aside BetBoom for the honor of joining the likes of Spirit, FURIA, and Aurora in the LANXESS Arena.

With this result, Vitality have also claimed revenge against Kirill "⁠Boombl4⁠" Mikhailov's men after BetBoom bested them 2-1 in their final pre-Major outing at IEM Atlanta.

Vitality had a hard-fought run to the playoffs, faltering to South American underdogs 9z while also requiring all three maps in their wins over FUT and MOUZ. Though Vitality managed to take down BetBoom in two maps, Vitality had a labored finish on the second map of Nuke, the result of a lackluster T-side showing.

"We are facing great teams at this Major, but we are also not playing the best CS," Mathieu "⁠ZywOo⁠" Herbaut shared after his team's win. "We are making too many individual mistakes, not refragging, not playing together, so sometimes we're making it a rough game for ourselves.

"We need to find out the way we want to play, and the way we used to play before, to find our playstyle again. I also think in playoffs we're going to play different, because we like to play on stage."

The series kicked off on Vitality's choice of Overpass, where Dan "⁠apEX⁠" Madesclaire's troops hit the ground running on the offense after Robin "⁠ropz⁠" Kool and Shahar "⁠flameZ⁠" Shushan pieced together a 2v4 win in the pistol round. BetBoom found their footing on the CT side once they got guns in their hands, leading to a dead-even scoreline of 6-6 at the half.

BetBoom looked to take the lead after the side swap as a Timur "⁠FL4MUS⁠" Marev quad kill netted them the second pistol, but the Russian side struggled to find much more than that on the offense as they succumbed to a Vitality force buy. BetBoom would manage to scrounge together just one more round in the second half as Vitality cruised to a 13-8 win.

Despite faltering in the opening pistol, Vitality were in the driver's seat on BetBoom's pick of Nuke, with ZywOo, ropz, and apEX all contributing to their eventual 8-4 scoreline. Outside of the T-side pistol and subsequent conversion, Vitality looked like a shell of themselves in the second half, struggling to find success as BetBoom began to crawl back into the map. In the end, a hero buy and a hectic last-second site hit netted Vitality the final two rounds they needed to take the map 13-11 and the series 2-1.
