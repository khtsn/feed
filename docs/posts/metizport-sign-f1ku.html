Metizport sign F1KU

Metizport have announced the signing of Maciej "⁠F1KU⁠" Miklas, bringing the Polish rifler onboard after he was recently benched by ENCE.

The move reunites F1KU with former Wildcard teammate Peter "⁠stanislaw⁠" Jarguz, who was recently installed as Metizport's IGL, and coach Vincent "⁠vinS⁠" Jozefiak.

"We’ve needed someone like Maciej for a while now," Metizport's Sports Director Morten Vollan Christensen said in a statement. "Both in terms of what he brings in-game and the experience he carries. We’re in the middle of building something meant to last, and he’s exactly the kind of player we want in that project."

F1KU has already featured in several matches for Metizport, helping the team take first place in European Pro League Series 6 in April. The Pole put up good numbers during his team's successful campaign, averaging a 1.18 rating over 11 maps.

The transfer represents the latest step in Metizport's ongoing rebuild, which commenced in late March following a disappointing start to the year which saw the team slide down the VRS. Jack "⁠Jackinho⁠" Ström Mattsson and Nikola "⁠Dragon⁠" Boskovic were both benched, making room for stanislaw and now F1KU, and recent results suggest the moves are already bearing fruit.

It was also suggested by the organization, at the time of stanislaw's signing, that Liam "⁠MaiL09⁠" Tügel would return to the roster following the conclusion of his loan at Alliance. "His loan agreement runs until June 30, but includes a recall option," the organization said. "We will engage in a dialogue with Alliance, Liam and his representatives to determine the best solution for all parties."
