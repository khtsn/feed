Valve release Major-deciding April VRS update

After a grueling month of tier-two LANs, the ranking used for invites to the IEM Cologne Major is now set in stone.

Valve has released the monthly Valve Regional Standings update for April, which will be used to send out invites to the IEM Cologne Major, LORGAR RANKINGS Season 1, and Stake Ranked Episode 2.

An intense month of competition saw a wealth of tier-two LANs play out as teams duked it out for a spot at the German Major, with FaZe's hopes going up in smoke at HLC Belgrade PRO to finalize the list.

Below you can find a list of how tournaments will allocate slots from the ranking and its relevant regional lists:

IEM Cologne Major (June 2-21)

  • 17 EU spots (5 to Stage 3, 5 to Stage 2, 6 to Stage 1)

  • 10 Americas spots (1 to Stage 3, 3 to Stage 2, 6 to Stage 1)

  • 5 Asia spots (1 to Stage 3, 4 to Stage 1)

LORGAR RANKINGS Season 1 (April 25-26)

  • 4 EU spots to main event

Stake Ranked Episode 2 (May 27-30)

  • 6 Global spots to main event

  • 14 Global spots to closed qualifier
