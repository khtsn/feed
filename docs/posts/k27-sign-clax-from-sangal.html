K27 sign clax from Sangal

K27 have signed Timur "⁠clax⁠" Sabirov from Sangal, bringing their starting roster back up to full strength after Petr "⁠fame⁠" Bolyshev's loan from Virtus.pro recently ended.

The transfer ends a short stint on the sidelines for clax, who hasn't played a HLTV-recorded official since April after he was benched by Sangal amidst their steady transition away from a Russian roster.

clax arrives in K27 having been one of Sangal's best performers during his tenure, averaging a 1.09 rating across 12 months. His last act as a Sangal player was to help the team secure the Urban Riga Open Season 4 trophy, posting a team-leading 1.24 rating during the title-winning campaign.

K27 will be hoping the addition of clax can help the team push on, following a season where they narrowly missed out on qualification for IEM Cologne Major. The team will enjoy their first LAN outing with their new signing in the near future, as they are set to participate in Stake Ranked Episode 3 from July 15.
