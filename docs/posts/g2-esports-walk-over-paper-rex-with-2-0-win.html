G2 Esports walk over Paper Rex with 2-0 win

G2 Esports have defeated Paper Rex 2-0 to open their Champions run. They advance to the Group D winner's match, where they will play against either FUT Esports or EDward Gaming for a spot in playoffs. Paper Rex are now in the lower bracket, where they will play against the losing team of the next series, EDward Gaming vs. FUT Esports.

G2 took their opponent's map, Icebox, with a comfortable 13-8. They took an early lead in Lotus, and were able to deny a comeback, closing out the map 13-9 and sealing the series 2-0.

This is the second time both teams have faced each other, with G2 sending Paper Rex to the lower bracket in the Masters Shanghai playoffs. It is also Paper Rex's first-ever opening series loss at Champions.
