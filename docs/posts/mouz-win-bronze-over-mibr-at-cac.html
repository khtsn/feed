MOUZ win bronze over MIBR at CAC

xertioN has led his new-look side to back-to-back third place finishes.

Dorian "⁠xertioN⁠" Berman was the star once again as MOUZ secured third place over MIBR in Shanghai.

A 1.94 series rating puts the newly christened in-game leader on a 1.28 for the event, and a 1.31 since taking charge of MOUZ last month with a 1.55 on CT side.

The match was Justinas "⁠jL⁠" Lekavicius's final one with the team before he returns to Natus Vincere as their substitute for the Major, and ensures the team leave on a high after two promising results with their new lineup.

jL made sure he left on a personal high too, with 133 ADR in a dominant T side first half on Overpass to start the third-place decider.

"It's still unclear what will happen," Ádám "⁠torzsi⁠" Torzsás told HLTV post-game about the roster. "I would say that I'm really happy with what we've shown, with what Justin [jL] and Adrian [xelex] showed. I think they are great players."

The win keeps MOUZ fifth in VRS, and nets them an extra $40,000 over fourth-place MIBR — who can also be pleased with their result in Shanghai.

While it ended in a whimper here, CAC was a reminder to the scene ahead of the Major of the danger Felipe "⁠insani⁠" Yuji can deal, and how the team finally have a core around him ready to compete.
