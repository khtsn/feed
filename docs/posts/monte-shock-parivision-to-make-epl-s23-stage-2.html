Monte shock PARIVISION to make EPL S23 Stage 2

Monte have shocked PARIVISION 2-1 to advance out of ESL Pro League Season 23's first stage with a 3-1 record.

The Jack "⁠Gizmy⁠" von Spreckelsen-led outfit picked up an additional 39 VRS points for their troubles, and have increased their already good odds of making the Cologne Major further.

While alarm bells will ring for a weary and disjointed PARIVISION side, credit must also go to Monte for enacting a clear game plan to throw the Russians off, best illustrated by Gizmy's 512 points of utility damage in the first ten rounds of Dust2.

Fortune played its part: Gizmy won a technical 1v5 after a late PARIVISION retake attempt on Mirage, while Aurélien "⁠afro⁠" Drapier scored a collateral with the Scout to kickstart the 6-10 comeback to 13-10.

At 6-12 on map two, and with the pressure off, PARIVISION finally started to look like themselves and Monte stumbled, allowing PARIVISION to force overtime and take the map, 19-15.

The Inferno decider was Monte's first match on the map this year, but the European combine showed no signs of rustiness.
