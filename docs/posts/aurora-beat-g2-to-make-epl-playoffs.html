Aurora beat G2 to make EPL playoffs

Aurora have advanced to the ESL Pro League Season 23 playoffs with a 3-1 record after beating G2 in a two-map series.

The match was a close affair, eventually decided by Aurora edging out their T halves on both maps, with their rifling trio of Ali "⁠Wicadia⁠" Haydar Yalçın, Ismailcan "⁠XANTARES⁠" Dörtkardeş, and Caner "⁠soulfly⁠" Kesici all finding impact on their two offensive halves.

The latter was especially impactful on Anubis, first stopping Nikita "⁠HeavyGod⁠" Martynenko's 1vs5 attempt before hitting a crucial headshot to help his team turn a 2vs4 alongside Özgür "⁠woxic⁠" Eker.

The result means that G2 will face FUT in the final round of Stage 2, with the entire schedule looking as follows:
