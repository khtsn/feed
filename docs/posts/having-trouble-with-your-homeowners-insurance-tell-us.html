Having Trouble With Your Homeowners Insurance? Tell Us.

Climate|Having Trouble With Your Homeowners Insurance? Tell Us.

https://www.nytimes.com/2024/03/28/climate/homeowners-insurance-personal-stories.html

Bills are soaring. Insurance companies are struggling. We want to hear how it’s affecting you.

We’re reporters who cover climate change for The Times and we want to hear about experiences with home insurance. The Times has reported that home insurance bills are rising and we want to learn about what that means for you and your community.

Here are a few topics we’d like to hear about, though we also hope you’ll share anything else that’s important for us to know:

  • Have your costs changed and, if so, how?

  • If your home suffered damage, how did the claims process work?

  • Has your access to coverage changed?

We will not publish any part of your submission without contacting you first. We may use your contact information to follow up with you. Thanks!
