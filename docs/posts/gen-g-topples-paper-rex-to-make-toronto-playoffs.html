Gen.G topples Paper Rex to make Toronto playoffs

With a 2-0 win, Gen.G has defeated Paper Rex, advancing to playoffs without dropping a single map. Having reached the knockout via the Swiss format, they will next play against one of the regional top seeds. Paper Rex remains in the tournament, awaiting the results of the next round of the Swiss stage for their opponent.

The match began with a close Ascent, in which Gen.G took an early lead, keeping Paper Rex's attempted comeback at bay to win the map 13-11. The Korean team's map pick followed, with a quick 13-3 on Icebox closing out the series with a 2-0 scoreline.

Master Toronto will continue with the group stage, now with elimination on the line for the remaining hopefuls.
