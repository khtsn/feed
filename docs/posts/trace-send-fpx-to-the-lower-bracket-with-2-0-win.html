Trace send FPX to the lower bracket with 2-0 win

Trace Esports defeat FunPlus Phoenix 2-0 in the VCT China 2024 playoffs. They are now in the upper bracket final, guaranteeing at least a top-three placement in the tournament. Meanwhile, FPX descend to the lower bracket, where they will play against either Bilibili or EDward Gaming for a chance to join Trace in the top three.

The match began on Ascent, where Trace breezed past FPX with a 13-3 scoreline. They then denied a comeback on FPX's map pick, taking Haven 13-11.

This victory extends Trace's winstreak to four series, and will be the first time in VCT China that Trace breaks out of the top-four, as they went out in the Kickoff semifinals and finished fourth in Stage 1.
