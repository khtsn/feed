MIBR leaps past Leviatán to reach Kickoff top four

With a 2-1 win, MIBR has eliminated Leviatán from Kickoff Americas. Now in the top four, they will next play against the winner of today's second elimination match, KRÜ Esports vs. LOUD. The loss means Leviatán will not earn Championship points, and will return to the server until the conclusion of Masters: Bangkok.

The series began on Leviatán's selection, Lotus, which they took 13-7. MIBR bounced back on Pearl, a counter pick against Leviatán, which paid off with a 13-9. The decider went MIBR's way, with a 14-12 victory propelling them to a historic top four at Kickoff Americas.
