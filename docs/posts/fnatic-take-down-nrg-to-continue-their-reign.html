FNATIC take down NRG to continue their reign

Defending international title holders FNATIC dominated NRG in a historic first matchup between two of the best cores in Valorant's history.

For what seems like ages the core rosters of FNATIC FNATIC Europe Rank #1 Leo Leo Jannesson Derke Nikita Sirmitev Boaster Jake Howlett Chronicle Timofey Khromov Alfajer Emir Ali Beder and NRG Esports NRG Esports North America Rank #2 FiNESSE Pujan Mehta s0m Sam Oh crashies Austin Roberts ethos Alan Ruan ardiis Ardis Svarenieks Victor Victor Wong have fought battles at the highest levels of international Valorant, but at every turn, they have avoided the head to head confrontation.

An even 6-6 first half on Fracture turned into a complete rout as NRG managed just seven round wins across the following 27 rounds of play.

Every member of FNATIC finished with more kills than deaths and vice versa for NRG as the boys in orange and black secured EMEA's second team in the upper semifinals.
