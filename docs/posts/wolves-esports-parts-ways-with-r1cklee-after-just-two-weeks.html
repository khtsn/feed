Wolves Esports parts ways with R1ckLee after just two weeks

Wolves Esports Wolves Esports China Unranked glacier Liang Zhekai (梁哲恺) SiuFatBB Pong Gaa Hei (庞加曦) jowa Jordan Wang yosemite Wang Lei (王磊) Spring Liu Jiunting (刘俊霆) has parted ways with Li "R1ckLee" Xu (李旭), who was signed by the organization about two weeks earlier.

R1ckLee joined Wolves in the midst of a busy midseason rebuild that took place between China Kickoff and the start of Stage 1. The team has been in rebuild mode since the end of its 2025 season, which ended with the team missing out on Champions Paris. A disappointing Stage 2 meant that, despite finishing third at Masters Toronto, they would not be in France for the biggest event of the year.

At the end of the 2025 season, Wolves parted ways with the likes of Juicy , Yuicaw , and Lysoar . In their place, they signed qiutiaN , jowa , and yosemite while adding Satoshi as a sixth man. Since the end of Kickoff, Wolves have signed glacier and parted ways with qiutiaN and Satoshi, and of course have now signed and released R1ckLee. It is believed that R1ckLee occupied a spot in the team's active roster that needed to be filled while glacier was ineligible to play.
