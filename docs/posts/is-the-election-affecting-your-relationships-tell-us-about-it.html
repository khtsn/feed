If this political season is influencing your marriage, friendships or dating life, for better or worse, we want to hear about it.

Oct. 7, 2024, 12:16 p.m. ET

The 2024 presidential election is less than a month away, and the strong opinions that come with it can shape our dynamics with friends, family, co-workers and romantic partners.

Perhaps you and your spouse have squabbled over who won a debate. Maybe you’ve reconnected with a friend by talking politics together. Or it could be that you’re planning to skip family Thanksgiving to avoid post-election skirmishes.

We want to know: Is the upcoming election affecting your relationships, or even your dating decisions? If so, how?

Please share your story in the form below, and a reporter may be in touch. (We will not publish your submission without asking for your permission first.)
