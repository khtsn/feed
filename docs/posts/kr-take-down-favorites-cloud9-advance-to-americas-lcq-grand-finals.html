KRÜ take down favorites Cloud9, advance to Americas LCQ grand finals

Underdogs KRÜ Esports KRÜ Esports Latin America South Rank #3 keznit Angelo Mori NagZ Juan Pablo Lopez Klaus Nicolas Ferrari Melser Marco Eliot Machuca Amaro DaveeyS Santiago Galvis Ruiz axeddy Carlos Alberto defeated long-time scrim partner Cloud9 Cloud9 North America Rank #5 Xeppaa Erick Bach leaf Nathan Orf Zellsis Jordan Montemurro runi Dylan Cade jakee Jake Anderson 2-1 in the upper bracket final of the VCT Americas Last Chance Qualifier. KRÜ will advance to the grand final where they will face the winner of the lower bracket final for a chance to make Champions 2023. Meanwhile, Cloud9 have dropped down to the lower bracket final where they will face off against the winner of Sentinels vs. Leviatán.

During the map selection, Cloud9 banned Bind and Split, spelling trouble for KRÜ, a team that so far relied on Raze-favored maps to gain an advantage on their opponents. Cloud9 proceeded to dominate KRÜ 13-4 on KRÜ's comfort pick Ascent, but KRÜ came back with a 13-4 demolishing of their own on Cloud9's map pick Pearl. In the deciding map of the series, Haven, KRÜ took the map 13-6. This marked the first time KRÜ won Haven in VCT Americas.
