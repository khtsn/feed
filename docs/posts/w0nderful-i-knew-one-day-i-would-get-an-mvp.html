w0nderful claims first career MVP at IEM Atlanta

Following his team's victory in Atlanta, w0nderful stepped aside to chat with HLTV regarding the feeling of earning his first MVP award, the 3-0 win over GamerLegion in the grand final, and his uptick in form after a slow start to 2026.

w0nderful, champion of IEM Atlanta, your second trophy of the year. Tell me how you're feeling right now.

I'm feeling amazing.

This is your first MVP; you've been with NAVI for two-and-a-half years and it's taken this long. How does it feel to get the MVP?

The feeling is very good, because there were a lot of chances for me to get it, but every time I was playing bad in finals or we are losing. This is my first time, and I am super happy.

iM, b1t, and makazze, all got MVPs before you. Was it a little disappointing to go so long without an MVP while your teammates got one?

A little bit, yes, but I knew one day I would get it.

Tell me about that game. The last Mirages you've played against them have been very close, but this one was just a stomp. What was going your way this time around?

I don't know. On Mirage, we just felt amazing, because every round was working. CT-side, we were just destroying them, we understood what we were doing and just killing them. Just a good CT-side for us.

On Nuke, it looked like they had you guys figured out in the second half, but you pieced together the comeback. What was the problem, and how did you manage to get the comeback going?

We just couldn't win one round, this one round means a lot. As soon as won it, the game started to be different. We started to do good rounds, do crucial kills, and that's how we come back.

This is your highest-rated MVP-eligible event of your career. Rivals, right before this, is your second-highest rated MVP event. You're in a very good period now, but the start of the year was very slow. Why do you think things are going so well now? Did you just need some time to warm up?

I don't know. After the Budapest Major, I was feeling really good, I was so confident. But the first tournaments were kinda disappointing for me, but I didn't change anything, I was playing the same game. Things were just all wrong. Now it's finally, I just came back.

How are you feeling about the team's chances heading into the IEM Cologne Major?

I think it's high chances.
