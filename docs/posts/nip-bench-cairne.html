NIP bench cairne

Ninjas in Pyjamas have announced the benching of Artem "⁠cairne⁠" Mushynskyi to bring an end to the Ukrainian's tenure that began in January 2026.

"Unfortunately, we came to realize that our fundamental approach to the game differs in some important ways and in the end, the team needs something different from what I naturally bring as a player, and I respect that decision," the Ukrainian rifler said in an X statement.

The 23-year-old was signed from Inner Circle at the start of the year and averaged a 1.03 during his time on the team, with his best performance coming at HyperX Roman Imperium Cup VI — one of two trophies he won with the organization alongside Stake Ranked Episode 2.

Despite winning the two VRS LANs, however, Ninjas in Pyjamas failed to qualify for the Cologne Major due to their lack of tier-one success. That already cost Artem "⁠r1nkle⁠" Moroz his spot on the roster after he was replaced by Martin "⁠stavn⁠" Lund in April, with cairne following suit less than two months later.
