Falcons scrape past BC Game to end round one of CS Asia Championships

The best-of-ones are behind us in Shanghai.

electroNic posted a 1.73 rating in the BO1 against Falcons

Falcons survived a scare against BC.Game to close round one of CAC 2026, after they lost both pistols and needed all 24 rounds to defeat the world number 49.

Had BCG been more disciplined on a post-plant or two, they might have been able to make more of rounds like Oleksandr "⁠s1mple⁠" Kostyliev's Deagle 3K or an Denis "⁠electroNic⁠" Sharipov 4K and converted the win.

Instead, Falcons wore their opponents down to survive their first game 13-11, countering their weariness having just arrived from PGL Astana's grand final.
