The best-of-five series played out on Split, Pearl, Abyss, Fracture, and Haven, Split and Abyss being G2's picks and Pearl and Fracture being Sentinels' picks. Sentinels won Split 13-4, G2 won Pearl 13-8, Sentinels took Abyss 13-9, and G2 won Fracture 13-5 and Haven 13-10.

These teams matched up once already in the upper final three days ago, where G2 breezed past Sentinels in a comfortable 2-0 to continue their undefeated 6-0 map streak in Kickoff. Although closer this time, G2 ultimately maintains their winning streak over SEN City and will fly to Thailand as one of the favorites to win the first international event of 2025.
