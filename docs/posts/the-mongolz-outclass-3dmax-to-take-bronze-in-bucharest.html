The MongolZ outclass 3DMAX to take bronze in Bucharest

The MongolZ beat 3DMAX 2-0 on Saturday in the third-place decider of PGL Bucharest 2026, claiming a spot on the podium ahead of the grand final between Astralis and FUT.

The Asian team hardly had to break a sweat to establish its dominance in the series. 3DMAX they looked second best in every area, unable to cope with their opponents' intensity and firepower.

The bronze medal is a small consolation prize for Garidmagnai "⁠bLitz⁠" Byambasuren's charges, who will be kicking themselves for wasting the chance to reach their first grand final with Anarbileg "⁠cobrazera⁠" Uuganbayar. They will now have a full month to prepare for PGL Astana, which will feature a much stronger field.

Inferno was a one-way street almost from the get-go. 3DMAX won both pistols but lost the conversions, and were never able to get into any sort of rhythm in gun rounds as The MongolZ calmly put the game to rest, with Sodbayar "⁠Techno⁠" Munkhbold leading the charge with a 1.86 rating.

More misery was to come for 3DMAX, who started Ancient on the back foot by wasting a 5v3 advantage in the pistol round. The MongolZ went into half-time with a comfortable 10-2 lead, and then bLitz hammered the final nails into 3DMAX's coffin with a 3K in the pistol round and a knife kill to close out the series.
