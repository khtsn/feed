Erv joins TYLOO, looking to make his debut at the VCT level after spending the 2025 season with LANDODGERS LANDODGERS Asia-Pacific Rank #35 Ladi xuan Bea2 yTao solo88 Vadim Solovyev . He competed in the CN Nationals scene with the squad, primarily playing the Initiator and Duelist roles.

TYLOO had an up-and-down year in VCT China, finding just one win across Kickoff and Stage 1, but the team appeared to figure things out in Stage 2. They went 3-2 in the group stage, and followed that up in the playoffs with one win to finish 5th-6th. After not making the playoffs at all in 2024, the finish was an achievement for a TYLOO squad that has been at the bottom of the region since the beginning of its partnership era.

Since the end of the 2025 season, TYLOO has parted ways with Ninebody and waituu . Erv's arrival expands the team's roster size back up to six.
