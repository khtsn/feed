Another close win gets Falcons into CAC playoffs

Falcons are 3-0 in maps at CAC with scorelines of 13-11, 19-15, and 13-11.

Tournament favorites Falcons have made it to the playoffs of CS Asia Championship by beating M80, as they look to end their 13-month trophy drought in China.

After a strong debut with Finn "⁠karrigan⁠" Andersen in Astana, Falcons are looking for a confidence boost ahead of the Major, but instead had it knocked in close opening games against BC.Game and M80.

Clip: Falcons fumble a 3v1 against Josh "⁠JBa⁠" Barutt

The important part is that they emerged unscathed from those tests, 13-11 against BCG and successfully returning from 0-6 and 0-4 starts to win both maps against a well-playing M80. It's a performance more reminiscent of karrigan's old team, where the job gets done but without the comfort that Falcons often had in group stages in the last year.

Inferno was particularly tetchy as Falcons trailed 6-0, 7-1, and 10-9 before sweeping the second set of overtime rounds 4-0. The B defence between karrigan and Maksim "⁠kyousuke⁠" Lukin is a new partnership, and is clearly a work in progress.

Ancient began in a similar vein, with Ilya "⁠m0NESY⁠" Osipov fragging well but rounds being hard to come by as JBa stole the pistol and Elias "⁠s1n⁠" Stein led M80 to an 8-4 half. On CT side, Falcons worked their way back in, and were impressive as they won five on the bounce to turn 8-11 into 13-11 in their favor.

Some of these struggles can be put down to a lack of preparation time between Astana and now, so Danny "⁠zonic⁠" Sørensen and karrigan will be happy that they have secured playoffs early with a rest day tomorrow before their upper bracket final.
