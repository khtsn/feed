DraculaN Season 5 reaches playoffs

The group stage of DraculaN Season 5 has concluded with eight teams advancing to the double-elimination playoff bracket through four GSL groups.

100 Thieves eased through Group B with 2.01 and 2.09-rated maps from André "⁠Ag1l⁠" Gil and William "⁠sirah⁠" Kjærsgaard leading them to comfortable victories over MANA and BRUTE, with the former win earning the North American organization its entry on the VRS at #159.

Nexus, ASTRAL, and Eternal Fire topped the other groups to make the playoffs, while the decider matches were won by aimclub, MANA, INFINITE, and HYPERSPIRIT.

The eight teams from the group stage will now face the seven high-seeded teams in the playoffs, a pool which includes HEROIC, FlyQuest, and ENCE. The eighth high seed, GenOne, notably withdrew from the event after the departure of a trio of its members, meaning Nexus were awarded a forfeit and will skip to the upper bracket quarter-finals.
