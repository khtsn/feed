XSE Pro League to feature Cache

Cache will be part of the map pool for the XSE Pro League, the tournament organizer has confirmed, making it the first big LAN event to feature the map since it replaced Overpass in the Active Duty pool.

The XSE Pro League will take place between July 1-12 in Guangzhou, China, with $1 million on offer. The tournament will mark the debut of several new rosters, most notably PARIVISION, FaZe and MIBR, and feature two Major quarter-finalists in 9z and BetBoom.
