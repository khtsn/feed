BC.Game bid farewell to Senzu

Azbayar "⁠Senzu⁠" Munkhbold has left BC.Game's roster following the end of his loan deal from The MongolZ, it has been announced.

The Mongolian star, who ranked 13th among HLTV's top 20 players of 2025, joined BC.Game at the end of April for the final stretch of the season after his Passion UA loan was abruptly cut short.

He played only four matches with BC.Game, averaging a 1.03 rating as the team finished last at IEM Atlanta and the CS Asia Championships. The team attended both events with analyst Robin "⁠ScrunK⁠" Röpke as a stand-in.

Senzu's departure leaves BC.Game with the task of rebuilding again around Oleksandr "⁠s1mple⁠" Kostyliev and Denis "⁠electroNic⁠" Sharipov following the benching of the former SAW core. They will have to grind their way into the VRS unless BC.Game manage to sign another core that is already ranked.

BC.Game are now:

Oleksandr "⁠s1mple⁠" Kostyliev

Wiktor "⁠TaZ⁠" Wojtas (coach)

Christopher "⁠MUTiRiS⁠" Fernandes (benched)
