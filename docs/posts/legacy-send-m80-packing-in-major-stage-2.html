Legacy send M80 packing in Major Stage 2

Legacy are one victory away from Stage 3 of the IEM Cologne Major after moving past M80, who have been eliminated with a 1-3 record.

The Brazilians entered Stage 2 as one of the favorites but have struggled to replicate the form that saw them win the CS Asia Championships over Falcons last month.

With no room for error after losing to MIBR and Monte in BO1 games, Legacy put in a positive performance against M80 in their first elimination battle in Cologne. Next up is a clash against TYLOO, whom they beat at CAC in two extremely close maps.

Andrei "⁠arT⁠" Piovezan's troops came out on Dust2 with fire in their belly, strolling to a 10-2 lead on the T side as M80 looked helpless to stop the onslaught.

The North Americans had a mountain to climb but refused to throw in the towel, getting their T side off to a good start with an ace from Mason "⁠Lake⁠" Sanderson.

Legacy never looked too comfortable on the CT side but eventually closed out the game in the 21st round after hitting map point thanks to individual heroics from star duo Bruno "⁠latto⁠" Rebelatto and Eduardo "⁠dumau⁠" Wolkmer.

Legacy lacked the same spark in the early stages of Inferno, ceding control to M80 before making a late recovery as the half ended in a 6-6 stalemate.

The rest of the game was a scrappy, chaotic affair as nerves set in. M80 came back from match point down to take the map to extra rounds, but they were unable to prevent Legacy from closing it out in overtime, with latto dealing the finishing blow with a 1v2 clutch.
