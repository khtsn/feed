KRÜ Esports KRÜ Esports Latin America South Rank #2 saadhak Matias Delipetro mwzera Leonardo Serrati silentzz Caio Morita Less Felipe de Loyola Basso Dantedeu5 Jesús Federico Larrosa has announced their new roster for the 2026 VCT season. The lineup consists of Jesús "Dantedeu5" Federico Larrosa, Leonardo "mwzera" Serrati, Matias "saadhak" Delipetro, Caio "silentzz" Morita, and Felipe "Less" de Loyola Basso.

The biggest names on this new roster are the world champion duo of saadhak and Less. The two were on LOUD LOUD Brazil Rank #5 lukxo Lucca Travaioli Darker Sebastián Castro Cicuamia Virtyy Leandro Moreno pANcada Bryan Luna cauanzin Cauan Pereira together for three years, earning a wealth of accolades in that time, most notably including a Champions win in 2022. However, after LOUD crumbled at the end of 2024, both players crossed the ocean to play in EMEA in 2025. Less started off hot with Team Vitality Team Vitality Europe Rank #14 Jamppi Elias Olkkonen PROFEK Dawid Święć Derke Nikita Sirmitev Chronicle Timofey Khromov Sayonara Ștefan Mîtcu , winning Kickoff and coming fourth at Masters Bangkok. After Bangkok, however, the team fizzled out into a middle-of-the-pack team for the rest of the year. For saadhak, Karmine Corp Karmine Corp Europe Rank #24 Avez Hazem Khaled SUYGETSU Dmitry Ilyushin was his new home in 2025 as he aimed to lead a band of misfits through EMEA. The team showed potential throughout the year, but ultimately struggled to pull through when it mattered most, being eliminated in the first round of playoffs in both Stage 1 and 2. The LOUD duo now returns to Americas together to don the KRÜ pink.

The other two additions are silentzz and mwzera. silentzz joins from 2Game Esports 2Game Esports Brazil Rank #7 ambitioN Gabriel Lima frz Leandro Gomes Yushi Luiz Felipe Coelho , the 2024 Americas Ascension winners. While they were able to win it all in tier 2, VCT was a whole different beast for 2Game, fittingly only winning 2 games all season. mwzera, on the other hand, returns to tier 1 after a brief hiatus in 2025. He had been with FURIA FURIA Brazil Rank #39 nerve Michael Yerrow artzin Arthur Araujo eeiu Daniel Vucenovic koalanoob Gianfranco Potestio alym Torogul Baidyldaev since the beginning of the partnership leagues, but found little success. Health concerns put him out of commission after Kickoff, and he spent the rest of the year in tier two with Los Grandes Los Grandes Inactive mwzera Leonardo Serrati ambitioN Gabriel Lima mazin Matheus Araújo jzz João Pedro GuhRVN Gustavo Ferreira . The team came close to reaching Ascension, but fell short in a five-map bout with Team Solid Team Solid Brazil Rank #2 raafa Rafael Lima havoc Ilan Eloy PxS Josh Brunelli kon4n Vitor Hugo .

Finally, Dantedeu5 returns as the only remaining player from KRÜ's 2025 roster. Dantedeu5 made waves at Ascension in 2024 with his explosive Neon gameplay. When he became old enough to play in VCT in 2025, KRÜ seized the opportunity and picked him up for Stage 2. Unfortunately, he and KRÜ as a whole underwhelmed, missing out in Champions for the first time in the team's history.

KRÜ looks to return to their former glory with saadhak and their new coaching staff leading their fresh crop of talent. The new roster will debut in Americas Kickoff, beginning on Jan 15.
