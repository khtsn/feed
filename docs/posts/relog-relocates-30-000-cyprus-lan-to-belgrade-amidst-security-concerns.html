Relog relocates $30,000 Cyprus LAN to Belgrade amidst security concerns

Relog Media has relocated its $30,000 LAN, scheduled for April 3-5, from Limassol, Cyprus to Belgrade, Serbia.

"Due to the recent security situation, the closing of the airspace in light of attacks targeting a military base in Cyprus and in order to ensure the safety of all players, staff and partners, the HLC CYPRUS PRO event will be relocated to Belgrade, Serbia," the Serbian tournament organizer shared in a statement.

"All tournament dates and the overall format will remain the same. Updated venue and travel information will be shared with participating teams shortly. We appreciate everyone’s understanding and continued support, and we look forward to hosting the event in Belgrade."
