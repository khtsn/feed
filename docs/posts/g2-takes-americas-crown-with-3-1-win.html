G2 takes Americas crown with 3-1 win

G2 Esports has defeated Sentinels 3-1 in the VCT Americas Stage 1 grand final. G2 is now the first-ever back-to-back Americas champions, as well as the only team to win two Americas titles in the partnership era.

The eighth encounter between the two teams began on Haven, with G2 taking the map 13-9 despite a strong first half from Sentinels. Split mirrored the first map, another 13-9 for G2 with a dominant second half. Sentinels bounced back on Pearl, continuing an undefeated streak on the map after denying G2 a comeback. Defense-sided Ascent was the final map, a 13-10 going G2's way thanks in part to taking both pistol rounds and having a better attack than their opponents.

The win allows G2 to skip the Masters: Toronto swiss stage, seeding them directly into the playoffs. G2 also earns five Championship points, setting them well on track for Champions: Paris qualification. Sentinels, meanwhile, will go to Masters as the second Americas seed, and only earning three Championship points for their efforts.
