100 Thieves return to Red Bull Home Ground

1 day ago

Red Bull Home Ground, one of the biggest OFF//SEASON events of 2022, is back again this year. The tournament will take place between November 3-5 and will be held at the historic Sumo arena Ryogoku Kokugikan in Tokyo, Japan.

The first invited team is 100 Thieves 100 Thieves North America Rank #7 bang Sean Bezerra Cryocells Matthew Panganiban stellar Brenden McGrath Derrek Derrek Ha Asuna Peter Mazuryk , who won the event last year in Manchester after beating the likes of Cloud9 Cloud9 North America Rank #5 Xeppaa Erick Bach leaf Nathan Orf Zellsis Jordan Montemurro runi Dylan Cade jakee Jake Anderson , KRÜ Esports KRÜ Esports Latin America South Rank #3 keznit Angelo Mori NagZ Juan Pablo Lopez Klaus Nicolas Ferrari Melser Marco Eliot Machuca Amaro DaveeyS Santiago Galvis Ruiz axeddy Carlos Alberto , and Team Vitality Team Vitality Europe Rank #42 MOLSI Michał Łącki Destrian Tomas Linikas ceNder Jokūbas Labutis . 100 Thieves won the event without dropping a single series, only losing two maps in the group stage overall.

Last year, Red Bull held qualifiers for EMEA and Turkey while this year, open and closed qualifiers will be held in EMEA and Japan. The two winning teams will join six invited teams from across the world at the Red Bull Home Ground finals in Japan.
