3DMAX, The MongolZ, and MIBR secure playoffs on PGL Bucharest day four

Day four of PGL Bucharest came to an end with 3DMAX progressing through the Swiss system as the final 3-1 team after their 2-0 win over PARIVISION (Inferno 13-9, Dust2 13-9).

The French squad makes the playoffs alongside The MongolZ, who beat Wildcard, and MIBR after the Brazilians defeated EYEBALLERS in the first match of the day.
