HOTU complete roster with dwushka

HOTU have announced Aleksandr "⁠dwushka⁠" Pakhnevich as the final member of their starting five, completing the rebuild that began in April with the removal of Suleımen "⁠dukefissura⁠" Orazaly and Altair "⁠kAlash⁠" Askarov.

The majority-Russian side have already enjoyed success with their newewst member dwushka, as he featured for the team during their winning run at the regional LAN 1xBet FRAG Season 17.

HOTU opted to commence their rebuild in the wake of their appearance at IEM Rio 2026, only the third Big Event appearance in the organization's history, where they exited the tournament with a single series win over Legacy to their name.

The Russian organization opted to drop dukefissura and kAlash from the lineup and bring Aidos "⁠kade0⁠" Khairzhan back into the fold, who previously represented the team across the majority of 2025.

To fill the final roster slot HOTU have turned to dwushka, a 21-year-old who has previously featured for academy sides VP.Prodigy and FORZE Reload, as well as Insilio and most-recently Bebop.

The young rifler impressed during his recent stint with Bebop, averaging a team-leading 1.20 rating across the year thus far whilst playing in European online competitions like CCT Europe Series.
