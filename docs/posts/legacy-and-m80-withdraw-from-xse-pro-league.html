Legacy and M80 withdraw from XSE Pro League

CAC winners Legacy will not return to China for the XSE Pro League

Legacy and M80 have been forced out of the XSE Pro League "due to passport-related issues and jury duty obligations," the Chinese tournament organizer announced on Tuesday.

The Americas pair were due to meet in the first round of the $1 million LAN in a rematch of Monday's IEM Cologne Stage 2 match. Legacy ran out 2-0 winners to progress to the 2-2 pool, eliminating M80 from the event.

Replacing them in the tournament will be TYLOO and Lynn Vision, the highest-ranked eligible teams from Asia in the VRS standings. TYLOO are still alive at the Major heading into the last day of Stage 2, while Lynn Vision were eliminated in Stage 1 with a 2-3 record.

The XSE Pro League will take place in Guangzhou between July 1-12, during the player break. Earlier this month, the tournament organizer announced that SINNERS had been invited as a replacement for MOUZ, who could not field a full roster.
