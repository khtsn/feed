The best highlights from BLAST Open Rotterdam group stage

Qianhao "⁠Moseyuh⁠" Chen was over the moon with TYLOO's 2-1 victory against FaZe, which put his team back in the invite range for the Cologne Major as they are now ranked fifth in Asia.

On beating FaZe: "We're just so excited to get this win. It was a really important win for us."

On gaining 100 points and moving back into the invite range for the Major: "It's still not 100% confirmed that we will get invited. So we will have to keep working."

On their chances against Falcons/NAVI: "We will try our best not to lose in an ugly fashion."

Andrey "⁠B1ad3⁠" Gorodenskiy, speaking on the analyst desk after Natus Vincere's win over B8, said he is looking forward to seeing how his team performs at BLAST Rotterdam with the biggest teams in attendance after lifting the ESL Pro League trophy.

"I'm actually looking forward to this event to check it," he said with a smile. "Because the big boss is here, and others, like donk, want to show that it was not a random tournament for them or random play. That they still can prove they can do something. Falcons are also hungry because they missed EPL."

Matej "⁠NucleonZ⁠" Trajkoski stopped by HLTV's microphone to discuss his return to Falcons' lineup as a stand-in for Maksim "⁠kyousuke⁠" Lukin.

The Macedonian player put in a 1.17 rating as Falcons beat NRG 2-0.

On his performance:  It was a bit rough on the T side [on Dust2]. I will try to fix my mistakes for next time. But on the CT side, I think it was very good.

On being back on the team: It feels very good, and I think I've improved a lot from last time. I just think I can learn even more and become an even better player with time, of course.

On the preparation for this event: We had only one day of practice, and it was really quick. It was like five days before the tournament. I had to fly out and come here.

On his individual goals for this event: I want to improve from last time. I think that would be my goal, to improve for the future. And hopefully, I can be more in this scene.

Danny "⁠zonic⁠" Sørensen has expanded on his HLTV interview with the BLAST desk:

"I like to involve my players, give them a sense that they are a part of decision making. But with this team, it could also be a cultural thing, I need to just say, 'this is how I want it, and this is what's going to happen.'

"I've not done a good enough job with that, I think I've been too inclusive."
