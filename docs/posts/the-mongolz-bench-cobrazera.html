The MongolZ bench cobrazera

Anarbileg "⁠cobrazera⁠" Uuganbayar has been moved to the bench on The MongolZ' Counter-Strike roster, the organization announced on Sunday.

His removal from the active lineup marks the second change in the Mongolian squad in the off-season, following Ayush "⁠mzinho⁠" Batbold's departure to BC.Game.
