Media: Legacy target tomaszin

The Brazilian team is looking to sign two Argentines in the off-season.

Legacy are locked in talks with BESTIA over the acquisition of Tomas "⁠tomaszin⁠" Corna, according to Dust2.com.br.

Should the move come to fruition, the 22-year-old Argentine would replace Vinicius "⁠n1ssim⁠" Pereira, who stepped down from Legacy following their Stage 3 elimination from the IEM Cologne Major.

This comes after the organization failed its approach for Felipe "⁠insani⁠" Yuji, who chose to remain at MIBR despite advanced talks.

Talks between BESTIA and Legacy were described as being "in the initial stages" and that they are expected to be difficult due to tomaszin being a key player for the Argentine organization as well as due to the controversy that saw Legacy replace BESTIA at the BLAST.tv Austin Major at the last minute.

A previous Dust2 Brasil report also revealed that the organization looks to make another change, targeting another Argentine player, Santino "⁠try⁠" Rigal, as a replacement for Guilherme "⁠saadzin⁠" Pacheco.
