K27 win CCT Europe S18 to strengthen Cologne Major chances

K27 have defeated BIG in the CCT Season 3 Europe Series 18 grand final with a 2-1 scoreline (Nuke 13-8, Overpass 11-13, Anubis 13-8) to further strengthen their grip on a Cologne Major invite.

The majority-Russian squad earned a total of 27 VRS points during its run to the title and is currently predicted to receive a Stage 1 invite to the Cologne Major as per HLTV's prediction.

Despite their high ranking, K27 are expected to drop out of Major contention over the next couple of weeks as they are currently not signed up to any of the open LANs happening before the invite cut-off, giving several teams a chance to leapfrog them in the standings.

Their chances would rise massively if they were to sign up to one of those tournaments, however, as they are currently only playing in the online ESL Challenger League Season 51 Europe Cup 2.

For BIG, the tournament was still positive despite the grand final loss. They earned a total of 33 points throughout the event and rose four places in the VRS, putting themselves in a prime position to appear in the organization's home Major.

The Benjamin "⁠blameF⁠" Bremer-led team is also on the other side of the open-LAN spectrum, having signed up to BC Game Masters Championship Season 1, Roman Imperium Cup VII, and Urban Riga Open 4.
