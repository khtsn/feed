kl1m on beating PV: "We're now getting into every series very confidently"

MIBR locked up a spot in the semi-final stage of the CS Asia Championships with a 2-1 victory over B8, extending their hot run in Shanghai after previously beating 3DMAX and PARIVISION.

In a brief interview after the group's upper bracket final, Klimentii "⁠kl1m⁠" Krivosheev admitted that claiming the scalp of a team like PARIVISION has provided an injection of confidence for the team.

The Russian AWPer, who is averaging a 1.27 rating in the tournament, said he believes MIBR can go all the way in Shanghai and ride that wave of momentum into the Major.

"We will start from Stage 1 in the Major, we can be in really good shape at the Major and get to Stage 3 or playoffs," he said. "I think we can do it."

You're through to the semi-finals after a 2-1 win against B8. Give me your thoughts on the match.

It was a good series. They're a good team, but I think on Inferno and Ancient we were ready for everything they did. We destroyed them, no chance. On Mirage, we struggled a bit on the T side, but it was a good series.

Before this, you beat PARIVISION, a very good team and a favorite here in this tournament. How important was it to get a win like that?

It was very important because we had a long botcamp, we worked well and we improved a lot of things. But we needed to see if they worked. And when you beat such a team, you gain a lot of confidence. And now we're getting into every series very confidently.

And now you're going to play in the semi-finals in front of a crowd. How big of a boost, confidence-wise, is this, before the Major?

It's very good for the Major. We're on a good run now already. We will start from Stage 1 in the Major, we can be in really good shape at the Major and get to Stage 3 or playoffs. I think we can do it.

What about your goals for this event? You're already top four here.

Now that we're this deep, we want to win it. But there's Falcons, a very good team. They're on the other side of the bracket. If we win our match and the final is against them, it will be a hard game. But I think we have a chance.
