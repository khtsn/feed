adverso calls it a career after six years in competitive Valorant

After competing for six years for some of the most notable squads in LATAM and Brazilian Valorant, Benjamín "adverso" Poblete has called it a career.

“This has undoubtedly been the best six years of my life,” he said. “I achieved things I could only imagine before: playing in a world championship, living abroad, traveling the world, representing an entire region, and making a living from it.”

He cited a gradual decline in passion for the game, one that has been lingering for a while, but only from a playing perspective.

“This isn't a definitive goodbye to esports,” he said. “My intention is to keep making an impact from another position, so I'm open to new projects and opportunities.”
