2025 Oscars Red Carpet Photos: Ariana Grande, Lupita Nyong’o, Zoe Saldana

The carpet outside the Dolby Theater in Los Angeles was classic red. The stars who appeared at the 97th Academy Awards wore colors from all across the spectrum.

The Oscars campaign season has been a long but fashionable slog for Timothée Chalamet, Colman Domingo, Cynthia Erivo, Ariane Grande, Mikey Madison and other luminaries. By the time they arrived for the annual ceremony on Sunday afternoon, they had settled into their sartorial best.

More on the Oscars

  • ‘Black Box Diaries’: Despite being the first full-length documentary made by a Japanese director ever nominated for an Oscar, Shiori Ito’s searing indictment of Japan’s justice system in handling sexual assault cases cannot be seen in her home country.

  • ‘Emilia Pérez’: Instead of signaling a new stage in the fashion-film relationship, the film — from a new initiative, Saint Laurent Productions — became yet another example of the risks that can accrue to a brand when it gets too close to a (fallible) star.

  • Is Hollywood Still Culturally Relevant?: Worries about the award show’s TV viewership, a barometer of the industry’s influence, are rumbling through the movie capital.

  • Best Picture Nominees: This year’s 10 best picture nominees are weird. They take weird forms. The people in them do weird stuff. What seems clear is that Hollywood is celebrating the kinds of movies it doesn’t make. (Weird, right?)
