Team Liquid Brazil cement top three finish after eliminating Team SMG GC

The first game of the day saw an elimination series between home favorites TL Brazil and APAC reps Team SMG. The winner of this series would confirm a top three finish at the event.

The first map was Haven, chosen by Team SMG. It saw them start on defense and after a close-run first half, SMG would find a slight lead of 7-5. In the second half, SMG found four rounds in a row before TL punched back with some wins of their own. SMG refused to be denied however, and claimed the map 13-9.

Map two saw Bind on the table, and despite an initial pistol win and antieco conversion for SMG, TL built up momentum with five round wins in a row. SMG re-stabilized to end the half at 7-5, in TL's favor. TL, after swapping to defense, would only let SMG win two more rounds before closing out Bind 13-7.

The decider was Ascent, a map where statistically, SMG had a higher winrate. However, TL found a dominant attacking half, where they built up a scoreline of 8-4. After the side swap, they got to match and series point at 12-7. SMG still fought, finding three rounds in a row, but in round 23 of regulation, TL were able to win 13-10 and guarantee themselves a top three finish for the second year in a row.

TL Brazil will play against G2 Gozen, and the winner will meet Shopify Rebellion in the grand final.
