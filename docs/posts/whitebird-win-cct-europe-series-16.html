WHITEBIRD win CCT Europe Series 16

WHITEBIRD have come out on top of CCT Europe Series to claim a $22,000 grand prize.

The Russian side beat magic, Alliance, SINNERS and Vladislav "⁠nafany⁠" Gorshkov's TDK in the playoffs en route to victory, with Aleksandr "⁠TRAVIS⁠" Timkiv and Viktor "⁠Lack1⁠" Boldyrev leading the way.

It marks the first victory of the team around Aleksei "⁠NickelBack⁠" Trofimov since it was formed at the end of last year out of a core of players that have played together on and off for the past five years, under banners like EPG Family, Entropiq, 1win and AMKAL.

Off the back of the long run through the Swiss stage and playoffs, the squad rose by over 20 spots to now #50 on the Global VRS.

That should put them among regular invites for similar competitions and likely seeded straight into the playoffs in the future.

The final standings of CCT Season 3 Europe Series 16 are:

1. WHITEBIRD - $22,000
