NIP replace Aurora at CS Asia Championships

The Turkish side is unable to participate in the Chinese event due to visa issues.

Ninjas in Pyjamas have been invited to replace Aurora at the CS Asia Championships after the Turkish side was unable to secure visas for the Chinese tournament, Perfect World has announced on the official website.

The newly Danish-majority squad was invited as the team next in line for an invitation based on the February Valve Regional Standings, in which Ninjas in Pyjamas was placed 24th.

The unexpected invite comes as a significant boon for the Marco "⁠Snappi⁠" Pfeiffer-led side, who would otherwise only have Stake Ranked Episode 2 to play before the end of the season.

Without it, Ninjas in Pyjamas, who have just debuted Martin "⁠stavn⁠" Lund in his new role as AWPer with third place at CCT Global Finals, were certain to tumble down the ranking as many around them have more events on their calendar, including the IEM Cologne Major.

CS Asia Championships is taking place from May 20-24 in Shanghai, China. Four top 10 teams headline a diverse participants' list for the $1 million competition.
