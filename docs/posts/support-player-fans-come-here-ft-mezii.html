Support player fans come here ft. mezii

William "⁠mezii⁠" Merriman was the guest on this week's Come Here podcast, discussing what it's like to play as a supportive rifler in modern Counter-Strike alongside Danish "⁠Nohte⁠" Allana and Harry "⁠NER0⁠" Richards.

2025 anchor of the year mezii has played a host of roles across his career but has settled down as a flexible rifler in the imperious Vitality side.

All that made him the perfect guest to discuss the support role itself, and the various ways of defining it. An old-school support might have been the team's grenadier, but given how often AWPers are the ones with the flashbangs it is not as simple as that in 2026.

With the help of mezii then, we were able to evaluate better ways to define a support, from tough CT positions, who has the weakest weaponry, or even who gets traded the most (compared to those who get to bait).
