PARIVISION, B8, Monte to attend XSE Pro League

The organizers of XSE Pro League have announced three further invites for the $1 million tournament taking place on July 1-12: PARIVISION, B8 and Monte.

The aforementioned trio joins the likes of Legacy, FaZe and BetBoom to form a surprisingly competitive participants' list considering the tournament is taking place during the time tier-one teams usually take off to recharge batteries and make changes.

They have done so because many of them are fighting for regular appearances at tier-one competitions, being ranked around 15th-20th place on the Valve Regional Standings.

Four more teams will join the team list and will be announced by Tuesday, the organizers have told HLTV.
