SportsBetExpert unveil new lineup

North American organization SportsBetExpert have announced the signing of their new five-man lineup, featuring the likes of Jaxon "⁠Peeping⁠" Cornwell, Adam "⁠WolfY⁠" Andersson, and Ian "⁠motm⁠" Hardy.

SportsBetExpert previously fielded a North American side up until late March, when the organization opted to part ways with the roster following a disappointing loss in ESL Challenger League Season 51 North America Cup 2.

The only player from the team's previous lineup that has carried over to the new roster is Samuel "⁠sml⁠" Oliveira, who averaged an impressive 1.20 rating against domestic competition during his time with the team. The Portuguese rifler is joined on SportsBetExpert's new lineup by former Marsborne duo WolfY and motm, and Wildcard rifler Peeping. The team is rounded out by Derek "⁠dare⁠" Brown, who is most well known for his tenure with timbermen.
