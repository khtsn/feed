3DMAX out of Cluj after 0-6 run: "It sucks, we cannot play properly, we are losing our feeling"

HEROIC have sent 3DMAX home from PGL Cluj-Napoca after getting the better of the French squad in a two-map series (13-5 Nuke, 13-10 Inferno).

3DMAX were nowhere to be seen on HEROIC's Nuke pick before putting up a better fight on Inferno after a lengthy delay to the start of the map, but eventually ran out of steam and dropped an 8-4 lead on the CT side.

"Obviously, we're trying to play a style that takes time to put into place and to get all the right mechanisms, to understand them and to put them into action," Alexandre "⁠bodyy⁠" Pianaro told HLTV after the loss.

"This tournament serves as experience, but we are playing in a way that obviously isn't looking like the regular 3DMAX. Right now, it sucks, not gonna lie, it fucking sucks. We cannot play properly, we are losing our feeling, and that's just how it is right now after not winning a single map at this tournament."

The loss eliminates 3DMAX from the tournament, having failed to win a single map against Falcons, Astralis, and HEROIC.

3DMAX's post-Krakow struggles carry on

The French squad bounced back from their online struggles at Bounty well at IEM Krakow, beating FUT and BC.Game to qualify for Stage 2. There, they began with a victory over FaZe, but were eventually eliminated after facing a tough draw in Vitality and Falcons.

Since then, however, the team has looked like a shell of itself. They were thrown out of RES Showdown 3 by the VRS-unranked M1X KS before tumbling out of PGL Cluj-Napoca to further compound their misery.
