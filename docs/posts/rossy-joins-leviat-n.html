Rossy joins LEVIATÁN

LEVIATÁN LEVIATÁN Latin America South Rank #1 Demon1 Max Mazanov tex Ian Botsch Rossy Daniel Abedrabbo kiNgg Francisco Aravena C0M Corbin Lee has added Daniel "Rossy" Abedrabbo to their roster. He will join the starting roster in place of nataNk , who was benched after only three official matches with the team.

After a great 2024 season from Leviatán that saw them win the Americas league and place third at Champions, their top-six placement at 2025's Kickoff came as a relative disappointment. This led to the benching of their European import nataNk , who, according to head coach Itopata , was having trouble fitting into the team system.
