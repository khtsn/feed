Aurora defeat FURIA to secure BLAST Rotterdam playoff spot

Aurora locked in their BLAST Open Rotterdam playoff spot after getting the better of FURIA in a two-map series (Dust2 16-14, Inferno 13-6).

Gabriel "⁠FalleN⁠" Toledo's squad once again joked on X after playing a second consecutive game that ended past midnight after besting TYLOO in another late affair on Wednesday.

This time around, FURIA were once again sleepy out of the blocks and lost the first five rounds of Dust2, only to come back to even keel thanks to some individual brilliance coupled with a smidge of luck, such as FalleN's blind spraydown.

They never let go of the gas in the second half to find six map points, but couldn't convert a single one as Aurora secured overtime and eventually took the map 16-14.

Inferno was a completely different story, as Aurora bulldozed their way to an incredibly dominant 10-2 half by not giving FURIA a chance.

Kaike "⁠KSCERATO⁠" Cerato then replied with a bonkers quad-kill in the pistol to start FURIA's comeback, but their feel-good moment was cut short as they could only muster four rounds before succumbing to a 6-13 loss.
