BOROS leaves JiJieHao

Mohammad "⁠BOROS⁠" Malhas has announced that his contract with JiJieHao has been terminated following mutual agreement.

The departure marks the end of the Jordanian's second stint with the organization. After buying himself out of his contract with the Chinese side last July, he rejoined mere months later as a replacement for his compatriot Issa "⁠ISSAA⁠" Murad.

He is now once again out despite JiJieHao's promising results, which saw them place second at two Chinese events, ZOWIE eXTREMESLAND Asia Open late last year and at ZHI-TECH Elite Masters in late July, most notably beating Lynn Vision each time.

"I am a free agent [after] we just terminated the contract together with JiJieHao," BOROS said on X. "I am looking for a team. I am so motivated, it was a good two months, we reached two finals, a great experience."

BOROS' departure from JiJieHao comes just days before their next outing at IstanbuLAN, a $25,000 open tournament taking place from February 12-14 in Istanbul, Turkey.

The team will temporarily bring in Flatron "⁠juanflatroo⁠" Halimi for the event, Dionis "⁠sinnopsyy⁠" Budeci has told HLTV.
