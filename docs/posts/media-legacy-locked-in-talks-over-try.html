Media: Legacy locked in talks over try

Legacy are locked in talks with Imperial over the acquisition of Santino "⁠try⁠" Rigal, according to Dust2.com.br.

According to the information, try and Legacy have already come to an agreement over the individual deal, leaving the move down to negotiations between the two organizations.

Should the move come to fruition, the Argentine AWPer would replace Guilherme "⁠saadzin⁠" Pacheco on the roster in time for the new season.

That would mark the second change in the team in the off-season, after Legacy also benched Vinicius "⁠n1ssim⁠" Pereira in the aftermath of their 12-14th placing at the IEM Cologne Major.

Their original target for the anchor's replacement was MIBR star Felipe "⁠insani⁠" Yuji, but the sale did not come to pass in the end, another Dust2 Brasil report says.
