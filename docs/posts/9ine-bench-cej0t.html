9INE bench cej0t

9INE have announced they have moved Jan "⁠cej0t⁠" Dyl to the inactive roster effective immediately, ending the Pole's 10-month stint on the starting lineup.

"We thank Jan for his hard work during his time with us," 9INE's statement read. "You have a bright future ahead of you!"

cej0t joined 9INE in June of last year as part of an overhaul that saw the removal of Jonatan "⁠bobeksde⁠" Persson and Mateusz "⁠mantuu⁠" Wilczewski. The 19-year-old went on to help the team cement themselves as one of the better squads in tier-two as the year wore on, the peak coming in a run to first place at Birch Cup in September.

The team came very close to qualifying for StarLadder Budapest Major at the end of the year, thanks to their strong results such as the win at Birch Cup, but narrowly missed out at the last gasp after losing to fnatic in the grand final of Fragadelphia Blocktober.
