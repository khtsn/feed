The 2006-born KiTae joins Gen.G not just looking to replace a spot in the starting lineup, but may also be taking over shot-calling duties from first-year IGL Lakia for the Tiger Nation. He was the IGL for GGA through Challengers Korea Split 1, helping the team to a runner-up finish. He primarily played the Initiator role, while also playing Omen on some maps for the academy squad.

Life has been moving fast for KiTae, who just a few months ago was a university student studying esports at Osan University. Should Gen.G make KiTae its IGL, another shift of roles may be on the cards. With Lakia being a lifelong Initiator player, KiTae may take over Controller duties from Ash.
