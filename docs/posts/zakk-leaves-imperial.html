zakk leaves Imperial

Rafael "⁠zakk⁠" Fernandes has departed Imperial, bringing an end to his three-and-a-half year stewardship of the team.

A Major finalist with Immortals in 2017, zakk took Imperial to four Majors and a top-four finish at BLAST Spring Final 2023 during his time in charge.

But times have been tougher in 2026; Imperial sit at 81st on the VRS after missing the Cologne Major, a significant fall from their peak of 11th in April 2024.

The double signing of Lucas "⁠decenty⁠" Bacelar and Guilherme "⁠levi⁠" Gustavo in February was designed to arrest that decline, only for Imperial to continue their tumble down the rankings.

"It was a very important stage in my career, marked by many lessons, challenges, and special moments," zakk said on X.

"The constant roster changes made the journey very exhausting, but I appreciate the dedication of everyone who went through it with me."
