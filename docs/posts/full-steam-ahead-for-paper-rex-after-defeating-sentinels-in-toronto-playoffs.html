Full steam ahead for Paper Rex after defeating Sentinels in Toronto playoffs

With a strong 2-0 win, Paper Rex has defeated Sentinels in the Masters: Toronto upper bracket. Now in the top three, they will next play against Wolves Esports for a spot in the grand final. Sentinels descends to the lower bracket, facing off against FNATIC in an elimination series.

The match began on Split, where Paper Rex dominated the attack to end 13-3. Sentinels had a strong start on their map pick, Sunset, forcing a comeback from the Southeast Asian squad, who ultimately won 13-11 to end the series.

The victory puts Paper Rex on path to qualify to Champions: Paris, as they are now tied for third place in the Pacific Championship Points table.
