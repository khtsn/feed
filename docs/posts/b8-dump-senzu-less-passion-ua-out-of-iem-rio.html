B8 dump Senzu-less Passion UA out of IEM Rio

B8 stayed alive in IEM Rio after breezing past Passion UA in the lower bracket with a 2-0 scoreline (Overpass 13-9, Mirage 13-3).

The worldwide squad, which played the event without Azbayar "⁠Senzu⁠" Munkhbold due to roster registration rules, decided to pick Overpass in the series, a map that B8 were yet to win in 2026.

The first half went according to expectations as Johnny "⁠JT⁠" Theodosiou's troops ran away to an 8-4 lead on the offense before completely falling apart in the second half, winning just one round and eventually dropping the map 9-13.

B8's pick of Mirage was a straightforward affair for the Ukrainian side, as Dmytro "⁠esenthial⁠" Tsvir and Daniil "⁠s1zzi⁠" Vinnyk led the way to a 13-3 victory.
