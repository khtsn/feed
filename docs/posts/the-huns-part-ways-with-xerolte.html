The Huns have made their second change in as many days.

sk0R departs The Huns

xerolte had featured for The Huns across two stints since 2024, helping the team establish themselves as one of the best in their region and qualify for several notable international events.

The Huns failed to make much headway in said international events, however, exiting CAC 2025, StarLadder Budapest Major and, most-recently, PGL Astana 2026 with only two wins combined across all three tournaments.

Never one of the team's better performers, xerolte struggled alongside the rest of his team when playing internationally, posting a 0.90 rating against top-30 opponents compared to a 1.07 average rating overall.
