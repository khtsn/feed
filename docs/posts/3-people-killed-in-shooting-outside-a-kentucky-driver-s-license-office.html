3 People Killed in Shooting Outside a Kentucky Driver’s License Office

Louisville police said they were still investigating and had not yet identified any suspects.

Feb. 21, 2025, 2:07 p.m. ET

Three people were fatally shot outside a driver’s license office in Louisville, Ky., on Friday, and several people fled the scene in a vehicle, the police said.

The Louisville Metro Police Department told residents to stay away from the scene at the Driver Licensing Regional Office at 6202 Willismore Drive, near Dixie Highway, where a large group of law enforcement personnel had responded to the shooting at noon.

The victims were found in the parking lot, the police said in a statement posted to social media. One man died at the scene and two women were taken to a hospital, where they died, the police said.

There were “no known suspects,” the police said in the statement. When asked if the shooting was a murder-suicide, an officer said no.

A police officer said at a news conference that aired on local television stations that officials were still investigating, collecting video surveillance and interviewing witnesses. The police did not have information about a motive or whether the three victims were connected.

This is a developing story.
