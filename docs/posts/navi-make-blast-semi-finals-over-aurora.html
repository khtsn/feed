NAVI make BLAST semi-finals over Aurora

Natus Vincere have comfortably secured a semi-final berth in BLAST Open Rotterdam, cruising past Aurora 2-0 with wins on Anubis (13-8) and Nuke (13-10).

The game was a rematch of the recent ESL Pro League final, which Natus Vincere triumphed in 3-1, and the result here was even more decisive. Aurora had bright spells but were comfortably second best across the series.

Natus Vincere raced out to a lead on the T side of Anubis, finding success with a mid-centric approach, before Ismailcan "⁠XANTARES⁠" Dörtkardeş and Ali "⁠Wicadia⁠" Haydar Yalçın came online to help Aurora wrestle back control before halftime.

When the sides switched Natus Vincere once again started hot, winning the pistol and rolling with that momentum. Aurora eventually got on the board but lost the very next round, scuppering their economy and giving Natus Vincere a clean runway to a 1-0 series lead.

Özgür "⁠woxic⁠" Eker and Caner "⁠soulfly⁠" Kesici started Nuke in good form to help Aurora recover from an 0-3 deficit on their CT side, guiding the Turk to a 7-5 halftime score, but the joy soon dried up. Aurora got to 10 rounds off the back of a pistol win, only for Natus Vincere to take full control once guns came up, picking up seven rounds in a row to seal the series.
