MOUZ and Jimpphat part ways

MOUZ has announced that Jimi "⁠Jimpphat⁠" Salo, who got his start in MOUZ NXT in May 2022 before being promoted to the main squad in July 2023, is no longer contracted to the organization.

The Finn had been on MOUZ's bench since April, when the team went through a shakeup that also saw Ludvig "⁠Brollan⁠" Brolin removed from the starting five as the team failed to reach finals and contend for trophies during the first months of the year.

During his time in the Hamburg-based organization, Jimpphat won a WePlay Academy League trophy during his days in NXT, followed by two Pro Leagues, BB Dacha, and PGL Cluj-Napoca with the main team.

Jimpphat also became a Top 20 Player of the Year for the first time with MOUZ in 2024, finishing 17th in the end-of-year ranking, and averaged a 1.10 rating during his time with the main squad.
