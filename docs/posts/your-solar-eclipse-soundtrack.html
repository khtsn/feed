Songs to put you in the mood.

April 6, 2024

John White

Edited by Wendy Dorr

Engineered by Sonia Herrero

Featuring Wesley Morris, Jon Pareles and Lindsay Zoladz

This Monday a total solar eclipse will appear across much of North America. Our critics Wesley Morris, Jon Pareles and Lindsay Zoladz put together a music playlist that’s a perfect accompaniment for watching the event.

Featured songs:

  • R.E.M., “Near Wild Heaven”

  • Nina Simone, Everyone’s Gone to the Moon”

  • The Youngbloods, “Darkness, Darkness”

  • Keith Jarrett, “Sundance”

  • Bonnie “Prince” Billy, “I See A Darkness”

  • Brian Eno, “An Ending (Ascent)”

On today’s episode

Lindsay Zoladz writes The Amplifier newsletter for The Times

Jon Pareles is the chief pop music critic for The Times.

Wesley Morris is a Times critic who writes about art and popular culture.
