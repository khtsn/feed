Just like the other upper bracket match between Karmine Corp GC and Xipto Esports GC, the series was one-sided as TL Brazil started its GC Champs run as best as the team could have.

The series was played on Corrode and Bind, with Team Liquid Brazil taking both 13-5 and 13-6, respectively. On Corrode, daiki was the star on KAY/O, ending the map with 22 kills, a 1.81 map rating, and 306 ACS. Then, on Bind, Bizerra stepped up with the best Veto performance of the event so far, with 22 kills of their own, a 1.67 map rating, 331 ACS, and 227 ADR.

With this win, Team Liquid Brazil moves on in the upper bracket and will face the victor of the G2 Gozen and Nova Esports GC match. As for KRÜ BLAZE, the team has to fight through the lower bracket and will face GIANTX GC to start its potential lower bracket run.
