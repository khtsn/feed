poiii hypercarry not enough as AM eliminate 100 Thieves from PCC S2

100 Thieves have been eliminated from Parken Challenger Championship Season 2 after losses to Johnny Speeds and AM in the double-elimination playoffs bracket.

Håvard "⁠rain⁠" Nygaard's men advanced through the round-robin group stage undefeated and beat FOKUS to begin their playoff campaign at the Danish LAN, but were cast down to the lower bracket after a red-hot showing from Jacob "⁠jocab⁠" Nerheden carried Johnny Speeds to victories on Overpass and Dust2 on either side of an overtime defeat on Inferno.

100 Thieves then failed to convert on a transcendent series by Alex "⁠poiii⁠" Nyholm Sundgren in their lower bracket game against AM, suffering elimination after losses on Dust2 (6-13) and Anubis (17-19).

The Swede finished the 55-round series with a 1.99 rating (1.05 clear of his nearest teammate, André "⁠Ag1l⁠" Gil), 72-39 K-D, +7.58% Round Swing, and 119.2 ADR, but all of that heavy lifting went to waste as the rest of his team struggled to come online.

Credit for 100 Thieves even getting to overtime on Anubis falls almost entirely on poiii, who fell a single kill shy of tying Danil "⁠donk⁠" Kryshkovets' LAN regulation kill record of 38 and tallied 14 multi-kills — including a quad-kill with a FAMAS to power a comeback from 8-12 down — despite the eventual defeat.

100 Thieves had a 2% chance to qualify for the IEM Cologne Major as of Sunday, according to Mischief, and their exit from the Danish LAN has likely sealed their fate. The team is not currently signed up for any upcoming LANs.
