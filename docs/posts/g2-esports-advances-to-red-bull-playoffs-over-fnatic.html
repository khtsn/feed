The match kicked off on FNATIC's map pick of Haven, where they dominated FOKUS 13-4 yesterday. They opted for their signature Yoru-Iso composition, while G2 brought out a standard Neon-Breach.

A second pistol win for G2 seemed to set them up for a simple win. However, reality was more complicated, as the next three rounds saw three back-and-forth round wins by the team with worse weaponry.
