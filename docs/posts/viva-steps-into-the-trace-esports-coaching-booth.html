Viva steps into the Trace Esports coaching booth

Trace Esports Trace Esports China Unranked LuoK1ng Zhang Zhanpeng (张展鹏) Kai Lu Zhinan (卢智楠) Xlele Zhao Yanbo (赵炎博) Abo Han Bohao (韩博皓) deLb Delbert Tanoto has announced that in-game leader Lifan "Viva" Ran (冉立凡) will be moving to the coaching booth, seemingly marking an end to his playing career.

Viva, an Initiator player throughout most of his career, was midway through his first season with Trace. He joined at the end of the 2025 season as part of a shift for the organization that moved away from the core centered around heybay .

In 2023, he came up just short of ascending with RA, finishing second at China Ascension alongside stew , MarT1n , yoman , YiHao — four players who have all since competed on the VCT stage. Viva made his VCT debut when JDG acquired the RA roster the following offseason. He stayed with JDG through the 2024 season and joined the newly ascended XLG shortly after, reaching Masters Toronto and Champions Paris in his lone year with the squad.

Viva will step into a Trace coaching booth that has been led by destroyeR since 2023. Over the years, destroyeR has brought Trace up from one of China's middling teams to one of its strongest, entering Champions Seoul in strong form. Trace remained one of the top Chinese teams to start 2025, qualifying for Masters Bangkok, but has failed to qualify for another international event since.

In its quest to return to being one of China's top teams, Trace has been in constant motion, making changes to its roster. Viva's signing was one of the first in the org's first this past offseason, joined with deLb 's addition and the departures of MarT1n and Biank . Viva's move into the coaching booth, paired with FengF 's return to play after a half-year spell on the sidelines, could see Trace return to a five-man roster, but also opens the door for a continued six-man roster. LuoK1ng , who also played during Kickoff, competed during the team's post-Stage 1 EVO Series matches alongside FengF, which may hint at what the team's roster come Stage 2 might look like.
