Shopify confirms top three after defeating Xipto Esports 2-0

After defeating Xipto Esports 2-0, defending champions Shopify Rebellion have made the top three in the 2024 Game Changers Championship.

The series began on Xipto's map, Pearl, which Shopify took 13-8 after a comfortable defense half. Xipto put up a fight on their Sunset attack, but a late back-and-forth went Shopify's way, ending their map pick 13-11 and the series 2-0.
