Vitality

Quote: William "⁠mezii⁠" Merriman in Atlanta: "It would have been crazy to win here. I think it's still fine as well; now we've got the time to prepare for the Major. That's the main goal. We know we're gonna come back even more hungry, and even more motivated to make sure that no team can beat us again."

Read more

mezii: "We had to expect that we weren't going to keep winning events with no preparation"

Stat: Vitality's CT sides, at a 64% win-rate, are beyond reason so far this year. They average 1.01 multi-kills per CT round, with 37.6 utility ADR and a ridiculous 36.8% 4v5 win-rate.

Fact: Dan "⁠apEX⁠" Madesclaire goes into the Major looking to tie Peter "⁠dupreeh⁠" Rasmussen on five titles as the most successful player in Major history. At 22 appearances, only Finn "⁠karrigan⁠" Andersen can rival apEX's longevity, and it's worth noting that the Frenchman posted a 1.38 rating 1.0 at Cologne's first Major in 2014.

Natus Vincere

Quote: Andrey "⁠B1ad3⁠" Gorodenskiy: "We ask two months in advance what the schedule is, how we can organize everything. We will do a bootcamp, and we will also come to the Major three days ahead, to get used to the PCs. ESL can organize this if some teams want to do it."

Read more

B1ad3: "Beating Vitality in any condition benefits us hugely"

Stat: At MVP events, Ihor "⁠w0nderful⁠" Zhdanov has improved the most out of anyone since animgraph 2's release. He has gone from a 1.02 rating in the first three months of the year to a 1.25 since (+0.22). He's also a Major specialist, with a 1.20 rating in them in CS2.

Fact: Natus Vincere remain the only organization to attend all 24 Majors, opening up a huge gap to fnatic in second on 18. They're also the only club that attended all four of Cologne's Majors in 2014, 2015, 2016, and 2026.

zonic on karrigan: "My main goal has really been to give him the keys to the ship"

Stat: In Maksim "⁠kyousuke⁠" Lukin's first 34 playoff maps, he recorded a rating above 1.00 just 14 times. But since karrigan's arrival, he has posted ratings above that mark 13 times, taking his overall rating from 1.03 to 1.25.

Fact: karrigan's 0.80 rating at MVP events this year is rock bottom, with a 0.03 gap to Janusz "⁠Snax⁠" Pogorzelski. His all-time kill differential at Majors is -608, more than double Andrei "⁠arT⁠" Piovezan's -254 in second place.
