Shortly after his team's semi-final win over BetBoom, Natus Vincere captain Aleksi "⁠Aleksib⁠" Virolainen spoke with HLTV regarding the series against the Russian side, their impending grand final match against GamerLegion, and the importance of securing a victory over Vitality so close to the IEM Cologne Major.

Or you can listen to the interview here.

Aleksib, you're headed to the grand finals of IEM Atlanta, 2-0 over BetBoom. How are you feeling?

Feeling great. I think they started the game strong, we had some struggles on the T-side of Ancient, but we won some very crucial rounds, clutch situations, 1v2s, by me and makazze, and these rounds were very important for the CT comeback. I think that after we got back to the CT side, it was just getting into our groove, and we took that momentum into Nuke as well.

What were those difficulties with the Ancient T-side?

I just think that when we won the first buy round on the T-side, we won it with one person alive, if I'm not mistaken. I felt like every gun round we tried to string, we were playing with Galils; sometimes I was dropping AK and received a Tec-9. It was not pretty at all, and Ancient can be a frustrating map to play, because you need utility to take space, and there are only so many gimmick rounds. It was rough for us. But we ended up still managing to play a decent T-side.

I spoke to B1ad3 before the semi-final, and he said that BetBoom could be a pretty dangerous team. Did you feel that when playing against them?

Yeah, I do think so. To be fair, Vitality lost against us, but they also lost against BetBoom. We knew that they were in the semi-finals deservedly. They have some maps that they have lately shown great results, like Anubis, Nuke, Dust2, they didn't have too many losses on those maps. Basically, we understood that this is a strong team, we need to take them seriously, and they were pushing us back as soon as the game started. But, in the end, we showed that we were the stronger team.

I think that they just have more motivation, more hunger, to get revenge

Aleksi "⁠Aleksib⁠" Virolainen on GamerLegion

You had the crowd with you against Vitality yesterday, and that seems to have carried into today. That help out a little bit?

Yeah. To be fair, we've had the crowd's support a couple more times this season, and Vitality have just been smashing us. It's good that we changed it.

You've got a rematch against GamerLegion, a best-of-five grand final this time. How are you feeling about that matchup?

I'm feeling great. I'm feeling confident about the best-of-five. We played them two times, we won both times. Doesn't mean we're gonna take it any less serious; I think that they just have more motivation, more hunger, to get revenge. I think they have nothing to lose, they already made it this far, and they played some solid CS. We're just gonna go, we're gonna prep, we're gonna try and rest, and tomorrow I hope we're gonna play some great CS.

How important is it to take down Vitality and get a deep run like this right before the Major?

It is important, and I think the key here is that we beat Vitality, and we didn't lose the next game straight up. We're in the grand finals, we're here to show that we deserve this trophy, and I'm hoping that we do our best tomorrow.
