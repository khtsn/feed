FL4MUS returns to GamerLegion

GamerLegion have announced the return of Timur "⁠FL4MUS⁠" Marev, bringing the Russian rifler back into the fold 18 months after he last played for the team before departing for Virtus.pro.

"We are really excited to welcome FL4MUS back indefinitely as we begin the next chapter and head into the new season," GamerLegion's statement read. "Together we've shared some unforgettable moments, and we're ready to create many more."

With this news FL4MUS has finally found a permanent new home after a string of loan moves following his benching by Virtus.pro in November 2025. Since that time he has turned out for OG and more recently BetBoom, both on loan deals.

During his time with BetBoom FL4MUS enjoyed several deep runs in tier-two European LANs, notably winning Roman Imperium Cup VII, a top-four finish at IEM Atlanta, and most impressively made it all the way from Stage 1 to the playoffs of IEM Cologne Major.

FL4MUS is already familiar with GamerLegion having turned out for the organization over the latter half of 2024, impressing upon his step into the international roster with his aggressive rifling presence.

The GamerLegion squad FL4MUS joins looks much different to the one he left, however, with only Sebastian "⁠Tauson⁠" Tauson Lindelof and Janusz "⁠Snax⁠" Pogorzelski remaining from the lineup that featured alongside FL4MUS in his first stint.
