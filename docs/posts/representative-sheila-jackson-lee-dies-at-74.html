Representative Sheila Jackson Lee Dies at 74

Ms. Lee, who represented a district in the Houston area for nearly three decades, said in June that she had pancreatic cancer.

July 19, 2024, 11:26 p.m. ET

Representative Sheila Jackson Lee, a Democrat from Texas who served three decades in the House, died on Friday. She was 74.

Her death was announced in a statement from her family. She said in June that she had been diagnosed with pancreatic cancer but would continue her work in Congress as she underwent treatment.

“By God’s grace, I will be back at full strength soon,” she told constituents.
