Gen.G reach upper final with 2-0 win

Gen.G advance to the Masters Madrid upper final after defeating Paper Rex 2-0. Their eighth win in a row, they are now the third Korean team to make top 3 at an international event.

The match began on Split, Paper Rex's map pick. Gen.G were quick to establish their superiority with a 13-8 win. They then won 13-9 on Lotus, completing the 2-0 victory and sending their opponents to the lower bracket.

Gen.G will next play on March 22 with hopes of securing a spot in the grand final.
