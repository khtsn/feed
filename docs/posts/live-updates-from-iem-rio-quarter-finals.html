Andrey "⁠tN1R⁠" Tatarinovich averaged a 1.01 rating so far in 2026, but his performances on Dust2 usually hit a much higher level.

His Dust2 average before this game was 1.17, one that increased to quite a bit following a 2.15 masterclass against MOUZ.

While Mirage might prove to be a different game for the Russian squad, a red-hot tN1R is certainly a good sign for the rest of the series.

Jimi "⁠Jimpphat⁠" Salo spoke to Freya "⁠Freya⁠" Spiers on the broadcast ahead of his team's playoff campaign, and spoke about the confidence of MOUZ at the moment.

"I would say like eight maybe," he said when ranking his team's confidence level. "I think we still have some way to go, but at the same time, coming into playoffs with this form is good on a way for us. We're not expected to win dominantly against the top-tier teams.

"Our expectations of course are the standard, we still know we are a really good team. But of course maybe not the most confident at the moment, speaking truthfully, but we know we are really good. When we are playing a good game, we can beat any of the teams."
