B3ar began his career with NTER in 2022 when the Chinese scene was still finding its footing. Even in the fledgling scene, NTER were one of the first ones to make a mark, with third place at FGC Invitational 2022 Act 2, several top two finishers at Xyper Arena Weekly Showdowns, and another third-place showing at FGC Invitational Epilogue at the end of the year. However, in 2023, NTER could not replicate the same momentum as they were consistently eliminated in open qualifiers.

The storied Bilibili organization entered the scene in February 2023 with the debut of FGC Invitational Act 1 — where they came ninth place. Through a series of roster changes, BLG later solidified their position in the Chinese hierarchy as a top-three team, placing second in both FGC Act 2 and the VCT Champions China Qualifier.
