HEROIC bow out of PGL Astana to magic

Chr1zn and company failed to build on a day one win against Aurora.

HEROIC are out of PGL Astana 2026 after a humbling 2-1 defeat to magic.

The team went into the tournament, their first Big Event since Cluj-Napoca, with high hopes after qualifying for the Major and stabilizing with Simon "⁠yxngstxr⁠" Boije as the AWPer. That was validated on day one, as they took down Aurora 2-0.

"We weren't... I'd say, ready for [Cluj]," Christoffer "⁠Chr1zN⁠" Storgaard told HLTV on day one. "There was too much change, and a lot of stuff was uncertain with the team and so on.

"Now that there is a bit more calm and everyone has gotten the month to practice and actually feel each other out, it's going to be much better."

But that confidence has been washed away after losses to FURIA, Gentle Mates, and now magic.

The first defeat is forgivable, but only picking up ten rounds in two maps against Gentle Mates was not. Neither is a 6-13 showing on the decider against world number 40 magic.

Their struggles are illustrated by a 39.3% win-rate on T side, and the fact that none of their players exceeded a 1.00 rating on CT side.

With the Major just around the corner, and Alimzhan "⁠Alkaren⁠" Bitimbay still waiting in the wings, HEROIC have more work to do in order to make the season a success.
