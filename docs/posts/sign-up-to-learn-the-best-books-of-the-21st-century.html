July 5, 2024, 5:02 a.m. ET

Good news, book lovers! We’ve arrived at a list of the 100 Best Books of the 21st Century, which is sure to be endlessly dissected and debated, pored over and relished.

To create our list, we asked hundreds of people — writers, historians, librarians, editors and book lovers — to pick their 10 best books of the century. Our respondents defined “best” in their own way: Some interpreted it to mean “favorite.” For others, it meant books they thought would stand the test of time.

And now we have our ranking.

Each day, from July 8-12, we’ll reveal 20 books on the list. You won’t want to miss an update, so sign up for our Books newsletter to make sure you’re among the first to know. After the list is announced, you will receive our newsletter (featuring book recommendations, publishing news and more) every week.

If you already receive the Books newsletter, you will automatically receive the updates, and will not see a way to sign up below. You can find out which newsletters you are signed up for here.
