TYLOO and Lynn Vision qualify for CS Asia Championships

The Chinese teams advanced through the Asia qualifier to secure spots at their home event.

TYLOO and Lynn Vision have secured the only non-invite spots at CS Asia Championships after topping the Asia Closed Qualifier.

TYLOO romped through the upper bracket without dropping a map, beating Morningstar, The Huns, and Chinggis Warriors in series play to earn a place at their home event in Shanghai, China.

Lynn Vision endured a grueling run after dropping their best-of-one opener to Alter Ego in overtime, but stabilized spectacularly with confident 2-0 wins over Ground Zero, NEXVOID, Morningstar, and The Huns to earn a rematch against Alter Ego in the lower bracket final.

Lynn Vision were tested again, but battled back from an opening map loss with a win on Dust2 and overtime victory on Nuke to set up a qualifying bout against Chinggis Warriors. The Mongolian team missed their first chance to advance against TYLOO, and faltered on their second opportunity too as Lynn Vision swept the series to earn their LAN berth.

Alongside earning a spot at the $1,000,000 event, scheduled for May 19-24, the qualifier offered valuable VRS points for teams amidst the race for spots at the IEM Cologne Major.
