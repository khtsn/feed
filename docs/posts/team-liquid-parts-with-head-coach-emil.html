Team Liquid parts with head coach eMIL

Team Liquid Team Liquid Europe Rank #24 nAts Ayaz Akhmetshin Keiko Georgio Sanassy will be allowing their head coach Emil "eMIL" Sandgren to explore his options for the 2025 season. His run as head coach for the team comes to an end after two years together.

eMIL first joined Liquid towards the tail end of 2022. Liquid were feeling the effects of the loss of their previous coach Sliggy , as they had failed to qualify for Masters Copenhagen. eMIL would step in for Liquid right before LCQ, where he would lead Liquid to win the tournament and secure a spot at Champions, where they would finish in the top eight.

With a new roster in 2023, Liquid started out slow, being dominated by Team Secret Team Secret Asia-Pacific Rank #8 invy Adrian Jiggs Reyes JessieVash Jessie Cuyco Wild0reoo Brheyanne Christ Reyes 2ge James Goopio Jremy Jeremy Cabrera at LOCK//IN. However, they would steadily improve throughout the season, and would eventually come out as the champions of EMEA, upsetting FNATIC FNATIC Europe Rank #2 Boaster Jake Howlett Chronicle Timofey Khromov Alfajer Emir Ali Beder in the grand finals to hand them their only loss of the year at that point. This result would qualify themselves for Masters Tokyo and Champions, but their regional success would not transfer to the international stage. Across both tournaments, they only found one win, placing in the top six at Tokyo and in the bottom four at Champions.

With another new roster in 2024, Liquid would once again show signs of early struggles, as they failed to make it out of the group stage at Kickoff. Unlike the previous year, however, their troubles continued throughout the year, as they would only find four wins through both stages of EMEA. With these results, they failed to qualify to any international events throughout the year, a first for the Liquid organization.

Team Liquid are now:

  • Ayaz "nAts" Akhmetshin
  • Georgio "Keiko" Sanassy
  • Nico "Harmii" Harms
  • Eamon "cauzed" Drea (Manager)
  • Jake "Bacon9" Lloyd (Analyst)
