Now on the back foot, Talon played a near-perfect defensive half of their own to come back into the map. Dropping just two rounds, Talon demolished the offence of Gen.G. garnetS on the Jett was too much to overcome. The second map of Split started off as a much closer affair compared to the first. Despite Gen.G taking five of the first seven rounds, Talon stabilized late in the half to keep things close. The Koreans narrowly took the lead going into halftime at 7-5.

Ultimately this return to high individual form and a clear comfortability on their map pick gave Gen.G enough momentum to push things over the line and close out the map 13-10.

The second half contained a mismatched bundle of traded rounds, however, it was Gen.G who would match Talon's offensive half with an 8-4 half of their own. The Thai hopefuls hit 12 first and held a lead of 12-10 before Gen.G forced overtime with two perfect site executes. Gen.G finally hit their stride in overtime, taking three rounds in a row to secure the map 15-13 and the series 2-1.
