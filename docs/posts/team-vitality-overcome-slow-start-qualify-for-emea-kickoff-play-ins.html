With this loss, Gentle Mates were eliminated from the EMEA Kickoff as well as Masters Madrid.

The maps went as follows: Gentle Mates picked Breeze, Team Vitality picked Lotus, leaving the third map as Sunset, if needed. Gentle Mates took their map pick in convincing fashion, winning Breeze 13-4 off the back of a great Yoru map by Jonathan " TakaS " Paupard.

After that, Vitality had to win Lotus to keep the series going, and did just that with a nail-biting 15-13 OT win. The standout player for Vitality on Lotus was Kimmie " Kicks " Laasner, who led the map in KAST and dropped 26 kills in the map win.

Sunset started off entirely in Team Vitality's favor and never changed, ending 13-3 and completing the series comeback. Saif " Sayf " Jibraeel was the top performer, reading Gentle Mates and denying any aggression with his Viper play.

It looked bad for Team Vitality to start, but they won overtime on Lotus and steamrolled on Sunset to earn their spot in the EMEA Kickoff play-ins, where they'll be a couple of wins away from the playoff section of the tournament.
