Take Your Vitamins

On Thursday, President-elect Donald J. Trump said he would nominate Robert F. Kennedy Jr., a vaccine skeptic known for some strange encounters with animals, to be his secretary of health and human services.

Stephen Colbert advised his booing audience to keep an open mind, “because that’s how the worm gets in.”

Some say he’s doing this strategically to weaken certain sections of the government. Or, and this is the theory that I believe, he’s dumb.” — JIMMY KIMMEL
