Liquid survive in EPL with labored win over SemperFi

EliGE was a key factor in getting Liquid over the line

Liquid have kept their campaign in ESL Pro League Season 23 alive with a labored 2-1 victory over SemperFi, eliminating the Oceanic/European combine with wins Dust2 (13-6) and Inferno (13-9) either side of a loss on Mirage (7-13).

The much-maligned Liquid squad have been in poor form as of late, exiting IEM Krakow early doors before slipping to defeats to Passion UA and Gaimin Gladiators to open their Pro League campaign.

SemperFi was a fortunate matchup for Liquid to be dealt as it seemed, at least on paper, like a chance to grab a comfortable win and build some confidence. Instead Liquid struggled, barely getting themselves over the line in a three-map affair.

Liquid's shaky form continues

They may have won this series, but Liquid will not be happy with the way they played. Individual output was patchy from everyone except EliGE, and their T sides were troublesome apart from on Dust2.

Due to their recent results, Liquid's qualification for IEM Cologne Major is very much in doubt. They currently seem a long way off the level required to rectify that situation.
