DRX claim first playoff berth of Group A

DRX have taken first place in Valorant Champions' Group A, securing a playoffs spot in a 2-0 victory over FNATIC.

The match kicked off on DRX's Abyss pick. Despite hiro opening it up with a 4K, the map was kept tight throughout and the two teams were rarely more than two rounds apart. BuzZ clutched the final round of the map with a Bladestorm knife through a smoke, gifting his team Abyss 13-10.

Lotus was a more relaxed endeavor for the Korean squad once they got into the second half. The first half was equally close, edged out by DRX 7-5, but they ran away with the map on defense to claim a 13-6 victory that booked their playoffs slot.
