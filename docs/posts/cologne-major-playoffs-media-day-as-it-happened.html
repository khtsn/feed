Cologne Major playoffs media day as it happened

Here's our first interview from the media day with Caner "⁠soulfly⁠" Kesici, on making Major playoffs as a rookie, his improved performance, BetBoom in the quarter-final and mentality being on the easier side of the bracket.

soulfly on Major playoffs as a rookie: "I've never tasted this feeling before. I was always imagining playing at a Major, but I never imagined playing in front of the crowd in the arena. But now I'm here, achieving my dreams, and I hope I'm gonna achieve more."

On his improved performance: "Recently I'm more confident. My teammates are helping and our doctor is helping me a lot to get my confidence back. That is the secret that I'm performing right now."

On BetBoom quarter-final: "They look really scary. Coming to playoffs is really hard, and BetBoom have been playing tier-two and coming to tier-one teams and destroying tier-one teams. We don't underestimate them because I know what Boombl4 is capable of, and I faced his teammates and teams on FACEIT, they're shooting good and Boombl4 is calling really well. But I believe we're gonna end up winning, hopefully."

On not getting ahead of themselves being on the easier side: "If we wanna be champions, we have to beat the [upper] team anyway. Winning against Vitality and Spirit in BO3 is possible, but BO5 is really hard. We have to be sharp and we have to have a good mentality."
