Dominican Republic Nightclub Roof Collapse: At Least 13 People Killed

The authorities said that search and rescue operations were continuing at the Jet Set nightclub in Santo Domingo, the capital.

April 8, 2025, 7:29 a.m. ET

At least 13 people were killed and dozens were injured when the roof of a nightclub collapsed early Tuesday in the Dominican Republic, according to the police.

In a series of social media posts, the national police said that 93 people had been taken to hospitals after the roof collapsed at the Jet Set nightclub in Santo Domingo, the capital.

Search and rescue operations were continuing, according to the authorities.
