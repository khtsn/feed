Falcons the only top team to skip PGL Masters Bucharest as invites are announced

A stacked lineup of teams will compete at the $1,250,000 event in Romania.

PGL has announced the 12 invited teams for PGL Masters Bucharest, which is scheduled to run from October 24-31 in Bucharest, Romania and concludes just before the November 2 VRS invite cut-off for the PGL Singapore Major.

Falcons were the only top team, based on the July 6 VRS, to not accept an invite, allowing The MongolZ in 13th to snag the final spot.

That leaves the $1,250,000 studio event with a stacked field of teams, including Spirit, Vitality, Natus Vincere, FURIA, G2, and reigning PGL Masters Bucharest champions Aurora, who will defend their crown with an international lineup after pulling the trigger on changes during the off-season.
