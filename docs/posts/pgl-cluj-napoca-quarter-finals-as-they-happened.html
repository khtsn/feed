PGL Cluj-Napoca quarter-finals as they happened

YEKINDAR on what went wrong and slow first halves: "Many factors. I think, obviously, The MongolZ are very hungry and prepared with new stuff very well, shoot hard.

"At the same time, for us, we were on a little bit of a downturn after the Krakow final in terms of being out of it energy-wise. It's very hard to keep the energy going back-to-back three tournaments.

"Today I got food poisoning as well, so it was very unfortunate and very hard to find the energy to perform, to play. Many, many of the duels, I take responsibility myself because there were many rounds where I could have impact, and I just couldn't.

"Props for them, honestly, they're a great young team and they found a great player for themselves. Hopefully, we're gonna see them at the very, very top this year and further."
