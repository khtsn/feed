T1 signs Sylvan

T1 T1 Korea Rank #4 stax Kim Gu-taek (김구택) Sylvan Ko Young-sub (고영섭) Carpe Lee Jae-hyeok (이재혁) iZu Ham Woo-ju (함우주) xccurate Kevin Susanto has announced the signing of Ko "Sylvan" Young-sub (고영섭), adding the talented Korean initiator to their roster as a part of their domestic rebuild.

T1's 2024 roster mixed players from different regions of the world, bringing in Rossy as the lone import, while also signing Indonesian player xccurate . Carpe is the only player remaining from the 2023 roster, and preparations for the 2024 season rounded out the team with veteran Sayaplayer and Japanese Challengers player iZu .

Sylvan joins after spending the 2024 season unattached to a VCT roster. In the 2023 season, he played for Gen.G Gen.G Korea Rank #1 t3xture Kim Na-ra (김나라) Karon Kim Won-tae (김원태) before being released at the end of the VCT season. He signed with Chinese Challengers side TOP Esports TOP Esports Inactive MrCANI Chen Tingxun (陈廷训) , but was soon again a free agent following their elimination at China Evolution Series Act 3: Heritability.

With former coach Daeda revealing that the organization was looking to build a roster with a more domestic focus, they have done just that, signing one of the most valuable Korean duelists on the market and a Controller player who looks to fill in the missing gaps.

T1 is now:

  • Kim "stax" Gu-taek (김구택)
  • Lee "Carpe" Jae-hyeok (이재혁)
  • Ham "iZu" Woo-ju (함우주)
  • Kevin "xccurate" Susanto
  • Ko "Sylvan" Young-sub (고영섭)
  • Junho "Norman" Shin (Manager)
  • Yoon "Autumn" Eu-ddeum (윤으뜸) (Head coach)
