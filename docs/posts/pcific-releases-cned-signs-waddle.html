After hoisting one of Ascension's trophies last year, PCIFIC has yet to win a series. Currently, the organization is 0-8 within the EMEA League with a map record of 4-16. The team also bombed out of the EWC EMEA qualifiers without a win.

After finishing dead last at Kickoff and Stage 1, a much-needed change was in order. cNed, who was shuffled around Duelists following the Yoru nerf, had seen many rough patches throughout the year. The signing of waddle, a Recon and Viper flex player, may allude to a role revamp for the PCIFIC squad.

In an ironic twist of fate, the Brit now joins the very organization that prevented him from ascending to the EMEA League last year.
