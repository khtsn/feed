AM Gaming squad find new home

AM have let go of their squad ahead of a move to a new organization.

The team, an arm of Fabian Broich's Achieve Minds agency, sit at 82nd in the VRS and placed 9-12th at Super DraculaN in their only LAN appearance in the last three months.
