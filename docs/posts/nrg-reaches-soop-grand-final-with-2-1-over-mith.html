NRG reaches SOOP grand final with 2-1 over MiTH

NRG Esports has defeated Made in Thailand 2-1 in the SOOP Valorant League semifinal. NRG will next play against either DRX or Gen.G in their second off-season grand final. MiTH has been eliminated from the tournament, going out in the top four after taking wins against Sentinels and Talon Esports.

The match began with a MiTH map win, taking Haven 13-10. Pearl went NRG's way, another 13-10 win to send the series to the decider. A quick Ascent concluded the match, with NRG winning 13-5 after a dominant defense.
