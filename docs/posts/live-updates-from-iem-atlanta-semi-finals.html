Live updates from IEM Atlanta semi-finals

Talking to STYKO, on nerves: "Its' got better. On the stage, I can feel the difference from last year. I'm just happy that I can feel the experience this young, it's going to help me.'

On Legacy: "We can smell a bit of the Brazilian vibe. I love Brazil, but they have a very specific playstyle. paiN, and FURIA, are the only teams playing a bit different."

On his own form: "I always have a hard start to events, I'm figuring out why. After the first map, I get into the zone and feel way better."

On the crowd: "It's the Snax effect. Thanks guys, for cheering for us."
