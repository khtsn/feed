Legacy have won titles before

vs.

Legacy beat Natus Vincere in groups to secure this semi-final spot, and go into their clash with GamerLegion safe in the knowledge that they have won titles before.

Of course, CAC in 2025 is a tier below this IEM, but Eduardo "⁠dumau⁠" Wolkmer and company have got their mojo back after struggling at IEM Rio and swapping IGLs earlier this year.

"We talked a lot, and we sat together, and we just managed to get the trust of everybody with the team," Andrei "⁠arT⁠" Piovezan explained. "We just believed that we could do a whole lot better, and trust each other more, and just play the rounds. [...] I think the mentality was way better after that."

Wait, where are Vitality?

vs.

Before Atlanta, Vitality had won five events in a row, made top four at 21 events in a row, and won a whopping 28 playoff maps in a row.

But after taking some well-earned time off, a weakened Vitality could not keep up any of those three streaks at IEM Atlanta, and lost their quarter-final against NAVI yesterday.

With a look at the bracket, you'd think that makes NAVI's path to the trophy straightforward but their three rivals are here for a reason. You just might not have predicted it.
