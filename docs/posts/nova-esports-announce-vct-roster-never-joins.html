Nova Esports announce VCT roster, neveR joins

21 hours ago

UPDATE: As of January 15, 2023, Nova Esports have announced their VCT CN roster. neveR joined the team in December 20, 2023. This article has been updated to reflect the current roster.

Nova Esports Nova Esports China Rank #28 neveR Vsevolod Suslov PangH Song Wenhao (宋文浩) o0o0o Ye Xiaodong (叶晓东) OBONE Chen Yijie (陈怡洁) CB Wang Qingchuan (王庆川) GuanG Guang Honglin (光洪霖) have announced their VCT China roster including Russian import Vsevolod "neveR" Suslov.

neveR joined his first-ever professional team, Team RA'AD Team RA'AD MENA Rank #1 Shniider Yousef Osama Sand Mark Emad Tuna Ayman Mosaad phaze Karim Fouad , in January 2023. RA'AD found immediate success and dominated their regional competition, winning VCT MENA North Africa Split 1. They continued to be a powerhouse, with a back-to-back in Split 2. However, neveR was subsequently moved to the substitute role and later left in July.

Nova Esports entered the Chinese scene in March 2023. Their first competition was qualifying for FGC Invitational Act 1, which they could not do. Nova found little success throughout the year; they were consistently eliminated in open qualifiers in all competitions, including the VCT Champions China Qualifier, save for a win against Invincible Gaming Invincible Gaming China Rank #30 Penge Artill .

On December 10, 2023, Nova were announced as one of 11 teams participating in the upcoming VCT China League. They made their first roster move on December 20, 2023, by signing neveR, then added Guang "GuanG" Honglin (光洪霖) from Royal Never Give Up Royal Never Give Up China Rank #12 HaTo 4ErWa Xiaonuo on January 14, 2024.

Nova Esports Nova Esports China Rank #28 neveR Vsevolod Suslov PangH Song Wenhao (宋文浩) o0o0o Ye Xiaodong (叶晓东) OBONE Chen Yijie (陈怡洁) CB Wang Qingchuan (王庆川) GuanG Guang Honglin (光洪霖) are now:

  • Vsevolod "neveR" Suslov
  • Song "PangH" Wenhao (宋文浩)
  • Ye "o0o0o" Xiaodong (叶晓东)
  • Chen "OBONE" Yijie (陈怡洁)
  • Wang "CB" Qingchuan (王庆川)
  • Guang "GuanG" Honglin (光洪霖)
  • "24K" (Coach)
