Astralis cruise past Liquid into EPL semis

In the VCT Americas Stage 1 playoffs, the upper final saw 100 Thieves take down G2 Esports 2-1. This pushes 100 Thieves into the grand finals, while G2 is in the lower final.

The second game saw Leviatán go up against KRÜ, where the winning team qualified for Masters Shanghai while the loser would be eliminated. The series went to all three maps, and Leviatán came out on top, making them the third Americas representative at Shanghai.
