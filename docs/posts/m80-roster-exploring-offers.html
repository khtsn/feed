M80 roster exploring offers

All of M80 M80 North America Rank #4 zander Alexander Dituri eeiu Daniel Vucenovic NiSMO Marc-Andre Tayar johnqt Mohamed Amine Ouarid koalanoob Gianfranco Potestio are looking for offers, the players have announced, following the team's second-place finish at VCT Ascension Americas.

Photo by Bruno Alvares/Riot Games Brasil

Gianfranco "koalanoob" Potestio was the first to advertise his unrestricted free agency, with three of his teammates following soon after. Daniel "eeiu" Vucenovic is the only player who has not revealed his current status, however, Prodigy Agency CEO Jérôme Coupez confirmed on Twitter eeiu will also be seeking offers. Coaches Vincent "Happy" Schopenhauer and GUNTER have not made their status public either.

M80 CEO Marco Mereu stated the organization hopes to return to 2024 with this team "as a unit," but that the current roster has "a right to explore any opportunities they feel would be best for them if not with M80."

M80 ended 2023 as one of the best teams in the Americas, coming into VCT Ascension Americas with two titles under their belt. The roster suffered just two losses in the North American Challengers League's group stages, scoring near-perfect records across both the Mid-Season Face Off and the second split's Playoffs.

However, in Ascension, M80 placed second, just two maps away from claiming the coveted spot in the VCT Americas League for the 2024 and 2025 seasons. The Guard The Guard North America Rank #1 JonahP Jonah Pulice trent Trent Cairns neT Michael Bernet valyn Jacob Batio tex Ian Botsch outlasted the team in the Bo5 grand final, where M80 won just one map (Ascent, 13-11) before dropping the next three and losing the grand final 3-1.
