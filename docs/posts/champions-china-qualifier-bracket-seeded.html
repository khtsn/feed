Champions China Qualifier bracket seeded

China will see its first LAN event just ahead of the official launch for Valorant in the region with twelve teams competing for the coveted three slots at Champions later this August. The play-in and double elimination bracket will be played between July 3 and 16 in Shanghai.

The Champions China qualifier bracket. Image from @AlanGai

The bracket features a single-elimination knockout round which feeds into a standard eight-team double-elimination bracket.

The knockout round, upper bracket semifinals, and first two lower bracket rounds will be Bo3, while the rest of the matches will be Bo5.
