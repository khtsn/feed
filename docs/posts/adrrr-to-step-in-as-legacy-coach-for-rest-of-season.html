adrrr to step in as Legacy coach for rest of season

RiVAS remains contracted to Legacy during his absence

Legacy have announced that assistant coach Alan "⁠adrrr⁠" Riveros will stand behind the team in the upcoming tournaments in place of head coach Bruno "⁠RiVAS⁠" Rivetti.

adrrr will coach the team at BetBoom Rush B and has been registered for all Legacy's tournaments this season, including the Cologne Major.

Despite RiVAS' absence, the organization stated that the Brazilian coach remains under contract, but has given no indication whether he is set to return to the role following the Major.

The change continues an unstable period for the Brazilian team, who replaced ex-coach Olavo "⁠chucky⁠" Napoleão with RiVAS at the start of the year, while also changing in-game leaders in February as Andrei "⁠arT⁠" Piovezan came in for Lucas "⁠lux⁠" Meneghini.

Legacy will play the rest of the season with the following roster:

Eduardo "⁠dumau⁠" Wolkmer
