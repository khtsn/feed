BLAST Open Rotterdam talent announced

BLAST has announced its talent lineup for the Open stop in Rotterdam, which will run on March 18-29.

Most notable is the addition of Natalie "NatTea" Mahoney, known for her work in Dota2, including last year's The International, as the desk host and interviewer. Along with her on the desk will be Mike "⁠DarfMike⁠" Winnick. Another new face is Iain Chambers, a regular in the Rainbow 6 circuit, who will be hosting on stage.

The desk will feature four BLAST regulars in Peter "⁠dupreeh⁠" Rasmussen, Jacob "⁠Pimp⁠" Winneche, Alex "⁠Mauisnake⁠" Ellenberg, and Chayut "⁠voo⁠" Changtongkum.

Conner "⁠Scrawny⁠" Girvan and Mohan "⁠launders⁠" Govindasamy make up one of the casting duos alongside Anders "⁠Anders⁠" Blume and Henry "⁠HenryG⁠" Greer.

Natalie "NatTea" Mahoney - Desk host & interviewer
