In her statement, florescent provided some insight into the change. She cited problems such as personal and family emergencies, as well as struggling to adapt to her relocation to EMEA. She had also previously vocalized some of her struggles in other posts and interviews.

Apeks corroborated many of these troubles in their own official statement. Despite doing the best they could to accommodate and help florescent throughout the year, both parties ultimately decided it was best for her to step down for the time being.

Apeks' signing of florescent back in December was a major milestone for VCT. As the first Game Changers player to ever step foot on the main VCT stage, her debut was raked with anticipation and expectation. While Apeks has yet to win a match in 2025, florescent has proven that she can compete with the best. She was the third-highest rated player in Kickoff, and had her share of incredible moments and performances throughout Stage 1.

florescent has definitely made her mark on EMEA.

florescent has suggested the possibility of returning to competition in the future, but for now will take the time to focus on her own well-being.
