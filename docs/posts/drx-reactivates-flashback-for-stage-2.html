DRX reactivates Flashback for Stage 2

2 days ago

DRX DRX Korea Rank #2 Flashback Cho Min-hyuk (조민혁) BuZz Yu Byung-chul (유병철) MaKo Kim Myeong-gwan (김명관) Foxy9 Jung Jae-sung (정재성) BeYN Kang Ha-bin (강하빈) have brought back Cho "Flashback" Min-hyuk (조민혁) from his reserve role after an absence that spanned the entirety of Stage 1. The move comes after the benching of former in-game leader Kim "stax" Gu-taek (김구택) just days prior.

Flashback was touted as one of the team's rising rookies after being promoted from DRX Prospects DRX Prospects Korea Unranked Leviathan in December last year, and participated in several OFF//SEASON events including TEN Global Invitational and Red Bull Home Ground 4. Heading into Kickoff, DRX was anticipated to qualify for Masters Madrid. However, they failed to do so after losing in the playoffs to Gen.G Gen.G Korea Rank #1 Munchkin Byeon Sang-beom (변상범) t3xture Kim Na-ra (김나라) Meteor Kim Tae-O (김태오) Lakia Kim Jong-min (김종민) Karon Kim Won-tae (김원태) , the eventual Kickoff winner and Madrid grand-finalist.

After Kickoff, Flashback stepped back from the active roster due to personal reasons. Kang "BeYN" Ha-bin (강하빈), another member of the DRX Prospects team, was called up as his replacement for Stage 1. DRX went flawless during the group stage before being handed a defeat at the hands of Paper Rex Paper Rex Asia-Pacific Rank #1 mindfreak Aaron Leonhart Jinggg Wang Jing Jie f0rsakeN Jason Susanto d4v41 Khalish Rusyaidee something Ilya Petrov and Gen.G, losing their chance at a Shanghai berth.

DRX's next appearance will be in Stage 2, with their opening match against ZETA DIVISION ZETA DIVISION Japan Rank #9 Laz Ushida Koji yuran Yushin Hato hiroronn Hiroki Yanai Dep Yuma Hashimoto SugarZ3ro Shota Watanabe scheduled for June 15.
