The Korean player played duelist/Killjoy for GE. His departure leaves GE with only three active players on the roster.

He was their duelist main but switched between the duelist and sentinel role as a part of GE.

Global Esports had a disappointing debut as a partnered VCT team. They were knocked out in the first round of LOCK//IN by Team Vitality Team Vitality Europe Rank #43 Destrian Tomas Linikas ceNder Jokūbas Labutis . The team placed eighth in the Pacific League regular season and failed to make playoffs, denying them a chance to make it to Masters Tokyo.
