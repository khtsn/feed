3DMAX make PGL Bucharest semi-final over MIBR

3DMAX became the first semi-finalists of PGL Bucharest after defeating MIBR with a 2-1 scoreline (Inferno 9-13, Ancient 16-13, Nuke 13-9).

The French squad completed an impressive comeback from 6-11 down on the second map to help secure their spot in the top-four stage, where they will face the winner of EYEBALLERS vs. Astralis.

Inferno was the first battleground, and the French side got off to a solid start after overcoming a lost pistol round to take the lead early on. That was as good as it got for 3DMAX, however, as they went completely missing after that to find themselves 4-11 down and drop the map 9-13.

That means 3DMAX needed to save themselves on Ancient, a map they had a 31% win-rate in the last three months before the quarter-final. It looked like their rotten streak was going to continue as MIBR took an 11-6 lead, but a pair of 1vs2 clutches by Bryan "⁠Maka⁠" Canda and Lucas "⁠Lucky⁠" Chastang eventually netted the French squad an overtime victory.

The decider of Nuke was a scrappy affair in the first half, but 3DMAX took over the game once on the defensive side. Lucky (1.94 CT-side rating) and Maka (1.76) locked down the outside area of the map with aplomb and gave their team a platform for success, meaning they made the semi-final after a 13-9 win.
