BLAST Rivals S1 talent revealed

sjokz returns to Texas as the desk host of BLAST Rivals Fort Worth

BLAST has unveiled its talent lineup for the upcoming Rivals event in Fort Worth, which will take place from April 29 through May 3.

Mike "⁠DarfMike⁠" Winnick will be the arena host for the North American event, while Eefje "⁠sjokz⁠" Depoortere will be the desk host, making her second appearance at a CS event in 2026 after IEM Kraków. Iain Chambers, who was the stage host at BLAST Open Rotterdam, will combine the duties of show host and interviewer this time around.

The desk will feature two North American analysts in Alex "⁠Mauisnake⁠" Ellenberg and Dustin "⁠dusT⁠" Mouret, while BLAST's regulars Mohan "⁠launders⁠" Govindasamy and Conner "⁠Scrawny⁠" Girvan make up one of the casting duos, along with Jason "⁠moses⁠" O'Toole and Adam "⁠Dinko⁠" Hawthorne.

BLAST Rivals S1 groups and opening matchups revealed

Headlined by Vitality, Natus Vincere, FURIA and FUT, BLAST Rivals will begin with two double-elimination groups. The top three teams from each group will advance to the single-elimination playoffs, which will culminate in a best-of-five final.
