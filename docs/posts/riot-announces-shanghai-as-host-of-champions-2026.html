Riot announces Shanghai as host of Champions 2026

2 days ago

Riot has announced that Champions 2026 will be held in Shanghai next year.

Shanghai will host a VCT international event for the second time ever.

The event will be the second international VCT event to be held in Shanghai following Masters Shanghai in 2024. With the announcement, Riot also announced that Champions 2027 will be held in the Americas as VCT looks to rotate through hotspots of each of the four regions.

Since VCT China's adoption of the franchising system in 2024, the region has made great strides. Locations for the earlier two Masters events in the 2026 season have yet to be unveiled, but with China hosting Champions, Americas, EMEA, and Pacific can expect to be in the running for the two events.
