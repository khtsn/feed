Short news: Week 23

Jonas "⁠calc⁠" Gundersen, and then Astralis themselves have criticised the online abuse directed at his Astralis players after their defeat yesterday.

"Disappointment is something we all feel, especially them," calc said on X, "but please stop this insanity, and show respect for others, and if you partake in online harassment seriously re-evaluate your life. No matter the results, we are all just humans trying our best."
