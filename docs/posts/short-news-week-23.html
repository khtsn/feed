Short news: Week 23

Metizport sports director Morten "⁠zEVES⁠" Vollan Christensen has conducted an interview with Fragbite.se where he spoke about the future of Liam "⁠MaiL09⁠" Tügel.

He says the team have plans in place whether MaiL09 is re-integrated or if a team meets their asking price, something Alliance felt they could not do. The only thing ruled out is a loan deal.

"Liam has a contract with us until the summer next year," zEVES explained, in comments translated by Fragbite staff. "So if he goes on loan for another season, [...] there'd be a much smaller chance to get something economically beneficial."

He went on: "I can understand him [wanting to stay at Alliance], of course, but reality is what it is. He has a contract with Metizport, we tried to find a solution [with Alliance] but we just didn't get there. If there are no more offers, he's going to be a Metizport player."

Movistar KOI co-owner Ibai "Ibai" Llanos said on his stream that his organization will "definitely" try to put together a CS team again.

KOI have been without a team since the end of 2024, when they parted ways with the roster that is now playing under Gentle Mates.

"We will try to be in Counter-Strike without a doubt," he said. "Without a doubt. Hopefully, during this summer or afterwards, we will be in Counter-Strike and Valorant. We will fight to make it happen, without a doubt."
