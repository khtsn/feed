TYLOO was global rank 34 on May 4, so it's the first Asian team after the lowest ranked team already in the event (SINNERS, global rank 30) And I think these replacement rules are something where Valve is a bit more open, because what do you want them to do? Invite a team that can't get a visa in time? The event is in 3 weeks, that's not enough time to get visas, which is why the rulebook says that any replacement team has 24 hours to prove they can enter the country, which is easy for Chinese teams to do, but not really anyone else. Note that if Legacy withdrew a month before the event or earlier, the replacement would be from global VRS, precisely because then you'd actually have enough time to get visas.
