Teams and format

Just three top-ten teams from the VRS Ranking are in attendance in China, namely Falcons, The MongolZ, and MOUZ, alongside a plethora of lower-ranked names.

Last year's winners Legacy are also in attendance, as are Ninjas in Pyjamas after they replaced Aurora.

NIP replace Aurora at CS Asia Championships

The tournament will begin with best-of-one opening matches followed by the rest of the double-elimination group stage in a best-of-three format. Each group will feed three teams into the playoffs, set to culminate with a best-of-five final, with the winners of each group securing a direct path to the semi-final.
