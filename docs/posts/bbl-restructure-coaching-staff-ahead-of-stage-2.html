BBL restructure coaching staff ahead of Stage 2

BBL Esports BBL Esports Europe Unranked Elite Efe Teber pAura Melih Karaduran Brave Eren Kasırga reazy Kaan Ürpek QutionerX Doğukan Dural have announced the departures of former head and assistant coaches Arda "Epic" Yıldız and Adil "elemeNt" Maufroid. Subsequently, Salih "marqnue" Onaran has been onboarded as the new head coach while Juan "McJuankar" Carlos Mesa Ciruelo has been promoted from analyst to assistant coach.

The change in staff took place shortly after BBL narrowly missed out on the EMEA Stage 1 playoffs through a five-way tiebreaker. The Turkish organization had yet to break past the group stage in both last and this year's VCT despite multiple roster changes.
