karrigan interview: Leaving FaZe, joining Falcons, and first impressions

The Danish IGL spoke at length about his move to Falcons ahead of his debut in Astana.

karrigan gave his first interview as Falcons' new IGL (Photo by John Lindholm / Team Falcons)

PGL Astana will officially mark the start of a new era for Falcons, who signed Finn "⁠karrigan⁠" Andersen as the new IGL in the hopes that the veteran IGL will end a title drought that stretches back to April 2025, when the team won PGL Bucharest 2025.

Speaking to HLTV ahead of his first appearance under the Saudi organization, karrigan explained the circumstances of his departure and what motivated him to embark on this new challenge.

"When this offer came, I could feel something I hadn't felt in my stomach for a long time," he said. "We're talking when ropz joined FaZe Clan, when I heard that he was coming. Because I know there is a big potential for this being insanely good, and having that feeling and that happiness going into a project is something that I could not say no to."
