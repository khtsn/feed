K27 to attend PGL Astana as FUT withdraw

FUT expected to attend IEM Atlanta as they withdraw from PGL Astana

PGL has announced that FUT have decided against attending its May event in Astana, with K27 set to fill the vacant spot in the $1.6 million tournament.

FUT's withdrawal suggests that the team has secured US visas to compete at IEM Atlanta, which will run concurrently with PGL Astana. In a statement to HLTV on March 3, the Turkish organization explained that PGL had "allowed every team to withdraw from the event until a certain date, which is after the deadline visa grant for IEM Atlanta."

K27 were selected as a replacement as the highest-ranked runner-up from the closed qualifiers.

The Russian side lost to magic 3-2 on Friday in the Europe Closed Qualifier, which put a major dent in their Cologne qualification hopes. They need to win the MPKBK CIS LAN alongside favorable results at HLC Belgrade PRO to reach the Major.
