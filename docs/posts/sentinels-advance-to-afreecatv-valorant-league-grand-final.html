Sentinels advance to AfreecaTV Valorant League grand final

Sentinels beat Team Liquid 2-1, securing an appearance at the AfreecaTV Valorant League grand final. Their opponent will be either Paper Rex, who they defeated 2-1 in the group stage, or T1, the first seed from Group B.

The match began with their map pick, Split, which they won 13-8. Liquid answered with a victory of their own via a 13-5 on Haven. The series continued to the decider, Sunset. Team Liquid had never played on the new map, while Sentinels already showed they were comfortable, with a 5-1 record. The experience was evident, as Sentinels scored a 13-5 of their own, winning the series.

Just over a month after announcing their 2024 roster, Team Liquid concludes their debut with a top four finish. Sentinels, on the other hand, will feature in their second grand final of the off-season, previously winning the Sentinels Invitational with a 2-0 against Oxygen Esports.
