SINNERS clinch victory at LORGAR RANKINGS S1

SINNERS won LORGAR RANKINGS Season 1 with a 2-1 victory over Gentle Mates, reverse-sweeping the Spaniards on home turf in Barcelona to lift their second trophy of the year.

The Czech organization earned a Stage 1 spot at the IEM Cologne Major after stringing together a series of solid LAN results before the April 6 cut-off, including a third-place finish at Parken Challenger Championship Season 1, quarter-finals at Roman Imperium Cup VII, and a trophy win at BC Game Masters Championship.

The latter was only the second LAN title for SINNERS outside of Czechia in its history, with their previous win dating back nearly six years, and it took just one month for them to add a third trophy to the cabinet in Spain.

The eight-team, single-elimination bracket left no margin for error at the $50,000 LAN, and Urban Riga Open Season 3 champions FOKUS learned that the hard way with a quarter-final loss to KOLESIE.

SINNERS, meanwhile, romped past Inner Circle Academy in their opener and swept KOLESIE to reach the grand final, where Gentle Mates awaited after beating ASTRAL and 9INE.

The Spaniards drew first blood in the series after SINNERS collapsed on the offense of Infern, squandering an 11-3 lead with just one gun round win in the second half that ultimately let Gentle Mates emerge the victors, 19-17. But rather than collapse mentally on Mirage, SINNERS were tenacious and recovered from 2-7 and 6-10 deficits to clinch a 13-11 win, before early momentum in both halves of the Nuke decider helped seal the series win.
