BLAST announce Open Singapore for March 2027

The location of BLAST Open 2027 Season 1 will be Singapore, BLAST have announced.

The small country hosted the "sold-out" World Final in 2024, and is also set to welcome a CS2 Major run by PGL this November.

Strike #10: Death, taxes and the TO war

Open Singapore will run concurrently with a March PGL event, like many of BLAST's offerings in 2027.

To incentivize teams, BLAST have cancelled their frequent flyer programme in favor of acceptance fees from 2027 onwards. Open Singapore will feature a $400,000 acceptance pool for the event, $25,000 per team if split equally.

BLAST announces revamped 2027 circuit and first location

BLAST's 2027 will also see a revamped Bounty, three Open tournaments (rather than two), with a total of $10 million of investment. That compares to $11m for PGL, and $10.45m for ESL excluding the $1m Grand Slam.

Open Season 3's location has already been announced as Rio de Janeiro, while Bounty S1 will continue in Malta, but the remaining cities are yet to be confirmed.
