BC.Game out of Parken Challenger Championship Season 1 after losses to ECSTATIC and illwill

The team advanced from the group stage with a 3-2 record, but only picked up a solitary win over MASONIC in the playoffs before bowing out.

BC.Game have had their campaign at the inaugural Parken Challenger Championship cut short in the double-elimination playoffs bracket following losses to ECSTATIC and illwill.

Oleksandr "⁠s1mple⁠" Kostyliev's side advanced through Group B of the best-of-one round-robin stage with a 3-2 record, losing games to Sashi and illwill, and the latter played spoiler again in the lower bracket of the playoffs with a landslide win on Nuke (13-2) and a strong offense on Dust2 (13-9) to send BC.Game packing early from the Danish LAN.
