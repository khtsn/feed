Astralis reach Bucharest grand final over 3DMAX

Astralis beat 3DMAX 2-0 to secure their spot in the grand final, with Love "⁠phzy⁠" Smidebrant leading the way with his AWP. The Swedish sniper posted a 1.47 rating and was followed closely by Rasmus "⁠HooXi⁠" Nielsen with a 1.45 as the two highest-rated players on the server.

The series kicked off on 3DMAX's Dust2 pick, but it was Astralis who were quick to take control on the CT side despite losing the first pistol round. After guns came into play, the HooXi-led squad won nine of the final ten rounds to lead 9-3 at the switch.

3DMAX won the second pistol round and immediately made a bid to start a comeback as they won six rounds in a row, but once Astralis managed to crack open the defense, they took the edge and stole the map in the final round of regulation, 13-11.

It was Astralis' game to win as the series moved to Overpass, their map pick, but 3DMAX were not done trying to turn around a tournament in which they have gotten better and better after an opening loss to FOKUS was followed up with four victories.

The Frenchmen started off 7-3 on the CT side, but a triple by HooXi with a Tec9 to help turn a 2v4 got Astralis right back in it as they won the final two rounds before the half.

phzy had several big moments as Astralis brought it back from a 9-5 deficit, including a triple A defense with the AWP to start bridging the gap and a vital 1v2 clutch in the 21st round to give his team the 11-10 lead.

From there, the HooXi-led squad went on to close it out, 13-10, to secure a spot in the grand final on Saturday, where they will play the victor of FUT vs. The MongolZ.
