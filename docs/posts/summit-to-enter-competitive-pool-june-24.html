Summit to enter competitive pool June 24

Riot has revealed Summit, a new map set to join the competitive map pool on Wednesday, June 24 as part of the Season 2026 // Act 4 update. Summit is set in a Radiant training academy within China's mountains and will feature two Spike Sites, a three-lane layout, and a new mechanic in droppable walls. Summit Overhead (Image: Riot Games)

For the first two weeks following its release, players will lose 50% less RR on Summit while still gaining 100% for wins as usual.

Additionally, a Summit Only queue will be available for seven days in a Swiftplay mode format.

Three-Lane Overhead View

Three droppable doors seen at B Site (Left), Mid (Middle), and A Site (Right) can be used to cut off sightlines and rotation routes for the rest of the round. Summit Lanes (Image: Riot Games)
