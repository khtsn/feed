Wildcard confirm nEMANHA signing

nEMANHA leaves illwill to join Wildcard

Wildcard have completed the signing of 28-year-old IGL Nemanja "⁠nEMANHA⁠" Đukić, finalizing their roster following the departure of Anthony "⁠CLASIA⁠" Kearney.

nEMANHA spent over four years on Zero Tenacity before the Serbian organization released their team in December. He went on to join other free players on illwill, a team that has broken into the top 30 in the global VRS after a series of deep runs, both online and on LAN.

The Serbian's addition is expected to provide more structure to a team that has lacked a proper IGL since Peter "⁠stanislaw⁠" Jarguz's benching in November. Wildcard recently missed out on a spot at PGL Bucharest after losing to Voca in the grand final of the North American closed qualifier.

"nEMANHA has been on our scouting list for years at this point," Wildcard co-owner John "Griff" Griffin told Dust2.us. "We were one of the first organizations to adopt [Skybox] EDGE as a Scouting Tool, and believe firmly in its ability to find talent.

"nEMANHA has fit our outlier datasets for nearly two years, and the ability to showcase it on LAN events over the past 12 months pushed us to move quickly and decisively towards his signing.

"We are extremely excited about adding such an advanced mind, and sharp aim, to the server in our strive towards Cologne and beyond."

Currently ranked 25th in the Americas VRS, Wildcard will be hoping to make up some ground in the coming two months as they will attend the next two Urban Riga Open events, Roman Imperium Cup VII and FRAG 20 X LVLUP EXPO.
