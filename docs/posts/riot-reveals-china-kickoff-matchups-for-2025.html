Riot reveals China Kickoff matchups for 2025

Following a series of showmatches and year-end awards show, Riot revealed the results of the China Kickoff opening matchup draw. The event will have a 12 team, double-elimination bracket, with the best performing 2024 VCT teams earning a bye into the second round.

The top two teams will qualify to Masters Bangkok and earn Championship Points, while the teams that round out the top four will earn 1 Championship Point. This year, two teams will qualify to Champions Paris off of Championship Points, further rewarding year-long consistency.

Currently, specific times and dates are still to be announced, and the specific order of the matches is not final.
