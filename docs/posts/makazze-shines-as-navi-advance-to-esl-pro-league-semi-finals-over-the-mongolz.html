makazze shines as NAVI advance to ESL Pro League semi-finals over The MongolZ

The Kosovan rifler had a day with a 1.87 rating and +8.95% round swing in the quarter-final victory.

Natus Vincere have punched their ticket to the ESL Pro League Season 23 semi-finals with a 2-0 victory over The MongolZ, who made the trip to Sweden without their coach, Erdenedalai "⁠maaRaa⁠" Bayanbat.

The series kicked off on Natus Vincere's Ancient pick, although The MongolZ held their own on the CT side, Despite falling behind 3-4 early on, they regained the two-round buffer they had after winning the pistol round by the end of the 7-5 half.

Drin "⁠makazze⁠" Shaqiri, Natus Vincere's Ancient specialist, had a big quad-kill round to keep his team ahead after tying the series 8-8, and led the charge with a 1.55 rating and +5.90% swing in the 13-11 victory.

makazze didn't slow down after the map changed to Mirage; if anything, he was just ramping up, downing three in the first gun round to get Natus Vincere off to a flying start on the CT side after winning the pistols.

makazze twisted the knife in the 10th round, which was a 1v5 mere seconds into it, slicing Sodbayar "⁠Techno⁠" Munkhbold from the back to go 9-1. The Kosovan rifler ended the first half with 17 kills against a The MongolZ side lacking ideas and getting outplayed in the tactical department.

An Anarbileg "⁠cobrazera⁠" Uuganbayar triple with the USP in the second pistol round kept The MongolZ alive, but only momentarily as Natus Vincere made sure to not let such a dominant 10-2 half slip away and closed it out once guns came into play, 13-4.

The MongolZ are now out of contention in the single-elimination bracket, while Natus Vincere will play the winner of FUT vs. MOUZ in what could be the first time makazze plays his former NAVI Junior teammates if they manage to get past Ludvig "⁠Brollan⁠" Brolin and his men.
