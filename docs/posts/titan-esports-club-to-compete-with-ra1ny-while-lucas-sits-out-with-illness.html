Titan Esports Club to compete with Ra1ny while lucas sits out with illness

Titan Esports Club Titan Esports Club China Unranked Haodong Guo Haodong (郭浩东) ra1ny Chen Baohuan (陈葆桓) Spitfires Xin Mingyan (辛明阳) Dynamite Zhao Zihao (赵梓浩) Coco Ding Dajian (丁达建) has announced that Initiator player Yunkun "lucas" Lu (路昀昆) will be out for the time being with an illness, and that Chen "ra1ny" Baohuan (陈葆桓) will be taking his spot in the starting lineup for the team's EWC Qualifier matches.

"Lucas experienced discomfort during yesterday's match but still insisted on completing it," Titan said. "His condition did not improve after the match, and his symptoms continued to worsen. The club prioritizes the health of its players and immediately arranged for someone to accompany him to the hospital for a comprehensive examination and treatment. To avoid exacerbating his condition with high-intensity matches, the club has decided that ra1ny will substitute for him in today's match."
