EDG continue dynasty with 3-2 over FPX

EDward Gaming have defeated FunPlus Phoenix in the VCT 2024 grand final, confirming once again their dominance in the region. The victory rewards them with a top seed at Champions Seoul, while FPX will be second seed.

The match began with an EDG win on Sunset, 13-9. FPX defended their map, Abyss, with a 13-9, but were unable to find their footing on Haven, which ended 13-7 in EDG's favor. FPX recovered their stride with a six round streak on Icebox to win 13-9. The decider map, Bind, began with a dominant EDG, denying a comeback to end the close out the map 13-8.

The ninth match between the two organizations, it is the seventh time EDward Gaming come out on top. It is also EDG's fourth win out of four in best-of-five series against FPX. The victory is EDG's eighth domestic title in a row, going back to FGC Act 2 in 2023.
