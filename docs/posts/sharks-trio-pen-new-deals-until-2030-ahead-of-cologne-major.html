Sharks trio pen new deals until 2030 ahead of Cologne Major

The Portuguese organization has tied three players down to long-term contracts.

Danilo "⁠doc⁠" Barros, Victor "⁠gafolo⁠" Andrade and Daniel "⁠rdnzao⁠" Monteiro have signed new deals with Sharks, tying them to the Portuguese organization until 2030.

The trio signed new contracts after the team qualified for the IEM Cologne Major's Stage 1 as the seventh-highest-ranked team in the Americas. It will be Sharks' second appearance at a Major after competing at PGL Stockholm 2021.

According to Dust2 Brasil, João "⁠koala⁠" Pfeffer and Máximo "⁠maxxkor⁠" Cortina are also tied down to long-term contracts with the organization. The former has been a regular presence in HLTV's prospects reports, featuring at No.11 in the latest edition.

“Our focus is on stability, the development of our athletes, and a rigorous training methodology, in which sustainable growth takes precedence over immediate results," Sharks coach Hélder "⁠coachi⁠" Sancho told Dust2 Brasil.

"We believe in processes and in the daily improvement of our players."

Sharks will go up against HEROIC in the opening round of the Major. Before the trip to Cologne, the team will attend the BC Game Masters Championship Season 2 and Stake Ranked Episode 2.
