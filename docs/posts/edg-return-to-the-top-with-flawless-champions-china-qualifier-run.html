The Champions China Qualifier was a 12-team LAN held in Shanghai that qualified three teams to Valorant Champions 2023. Of the 12 teams, four were selected based on their results in the FGC Valorant Invitational: Act 1 and Act 2, while the other eight qualified via a preceding Swiss stage in June.

The China Qualifier's playoffs used a modified double-elimination bracket that gave the four FGC teams a first-round bye. The other eight first played a knockout round, which eliminated the losers from the tournament. From thereon, the remaining eight squads played the double-elimination bracket. The upper quarterfinals and first two rounds of the lower bracket were Bo3, and the remaining matches were all Bo5. The top three teams were selected to Champions — the two finalists and the lower final's losers.
