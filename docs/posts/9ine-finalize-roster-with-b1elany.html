9INE finalize roster with b1elany

b1elany is the last piece of the puzzle for 9INE

9INE have completed the signing of KOLESIE star Maciej "⁠b1elany⁠" Biliński, who will reunite with his former teammate Alan "⁠flayy⁠" Krupa on the international team.

b1elany becomes the second player to join 9INE in the summer following the signing of Emir "⁠rim3⁠" Dönmez from Eternal Fire last month. The pair will fill the vacancies left by the double-benching of Jan "⁠cej0t⁠" Dyl and Bartosz "⁠bnox⁠" Niebisz.

b1elany played for 9INE as a stand-in last week at Super DraculaN (which rim3 missed due to visa issues). He averaged a 1.08 rating as 9INE, who also fielded Vladyslav "⁠St0m4k⁠" Lykhoshva as a stand-in, finished 9th-12th, beating GamerLegion in between losses against Sashi and Inner Circle.

"I'm very happy to welcome b1elany to the team," head coach Danny "⁠BERRY⁠" Krüger told HLTV. "He's a versatile player that brings the flexibility we have been looking for. I feel we have a great balance of firepower and experience, and I'm excited to see what this lineup can do.

"I was quite surprised that a lot of teams were sleeping during this transfer season."
