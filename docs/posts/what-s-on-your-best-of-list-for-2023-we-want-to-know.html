Best line from a movie? Best pasta recipe? Best advice? Tell us your highly subjective, super-specific favorites from the past year.

Melissa Kirsch

Dec. 9, 2023, 5:01 a.m. ET

What’s on your own best-of list for 2023? Think about everything you consumed, regardless of genre: yes, the best thing you watched, but also the best thing you ate, the best advice you received, the best app you discovered, the best line you encountered in a movie, the best book you read that’s been sitting on your shelf for over a decade, the best change you made to your evening routine. (For inspiration, check out reader favorites from last year.)

We read every submission, and we plan to use some in upcoming newsletters that highlight readers’ year-end picks and favorite advice. We won’t share your response with anyone outside our newsroom or use your contact information beyond following up with you. And we will not publish your submission without first contacting you to verify that your information and response are accurate.

Melissa Kirsch is the deputy editor of Culture and Lifestyle at The Times and writes The Morning newsletter on Saturdays. More about Melissa Kirsch
