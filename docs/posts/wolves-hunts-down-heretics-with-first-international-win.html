Wolves hunts down Heretics with first international win

Wolves Esports has eliminated Team Heretics from Masters: Toronto with a 2-0 sweep. They remain in the Swiss stage, needing one more win to advance to playoffs.

The match opened on Pearl, picked by Wolves. A strong start set them up to take the first map 13-10, and momentum continued going into Lotus. Wolves closed out the series with a 13-9 win, sending Heretics home without letting them win a single map.

Wolves will next play against one of the remaining 1-1 teams, with hopes of qualifying to the playoffs in their first international appearance.
