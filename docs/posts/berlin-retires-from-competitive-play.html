BerLIN retires from competitive play

Zhang "BerLIN" Bolin (張柏霖) has officially retired after more than four years of competitive play.

In a statement on his personal Weibo, BerLIN cited health issues as the main reason for his retirement. He explained that he did not want these issues to affect his teammates and expressed a desire to return once his condition improves.
