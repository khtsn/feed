Short news: Week 21

In a reddit post, ESL's Director Esports for Counter-Strike, Marc "⁠Nix0n⁠" Winther Kristensen, has revealed some of the tournament organizer's plans for the upcoming IEM Cologne Major.

As a part of its content roadmap, ESL has created "three deep-dive player profiles on the stars of the tournament," which will be released during the Major.

The organizer has also created a documentary for what it refers to as "the greatest series in CS history," the IEM Cologne 2022 grand final between Natus Vincere and FaZe, and will additionally release a 10-minute trailer of the best narratives from the season on Monday.

The full post, which can be found here, also details some upgrades and additions to the fan experience side of the event, including LED wristbands, an easier way to secure merch, and more.
