VCT Pacific Stage 1 groups unveiled following Kickoff

After the conclusion of the VCT Pacific Kickoff Lower Final, an on-stage draw was held to unveil the groups for VCT Pacific Stage 1.

Like the Americas draw, the region's 12 teams were split into six pools sorted by their Kickoff placement, with one team from each pool drawn into Group Alpha and the other into Group Omega.

The draw brings forward a handful of eye-catching matchups. In Group Alpha, a grudge match between head coaches is promised when DRX and Global Esports meet in a group that will have four different Pacific champions competing. In Group Omega, all three of the region's Japan-based orgs will clash against each other, while T1 and RRQ will meet in a rematch of the Middle Final.

Stage 1 is set to begin on April 3, with the group stage set to run until May 17. Finals weekend is set to take place in Ho Chi Minh, Vietnam, bringing a VCT event to the country for the first time ever.
