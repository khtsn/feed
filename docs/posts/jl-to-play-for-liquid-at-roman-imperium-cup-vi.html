jL to play for Liquid at Roman Imperium Cup VI

Natus Vincere benched player Justinas "⁠jL⁠" Lekavicius will play for Liquid as a stand-in at Roman Imperium Cup VI, HLTV.org has confirmed. The Lithuanian will fill in for Guy "⁠NertZ⁠" Iluz, who is unable to attend the LAN due to travel issues.

The Israeli star is expected to be back in time for BLAST Open Rotterdam, where Liquid will face Spirit in the opening round.

jL has been out of action since July, when he announced that he was taking a break from competition to focus on his well-being. Earlier this season, he was part of IEM Krakow's on-air team as an analyst.

On Liquid, jL will reunite with head coach Viktor "⁠flashie⁠" Tamás Bea, who was part of Natus Vincere's backroom staff as an analyst between August 2023 and July 2025.

"I know how important VRS points are for them right now and I'll do my absolute best to help my best friend out, flashie," jL wrote on X.

It's the second time this year that Liquid are unable to field a full lineup for an event. The team attended IEM Krakow with Karim "⁠Krimbo⁠" Moussa as a stand-in as Keith "⁠NAF⁠" Markovic missed the tournament because of personal reasons.

Liquid signed up for Roman Imperium Cup VI and DraculaN Season 6 after watching their Major chances dwindle following a Stage 1 exit from ESL Pro League 23. According to Finn "Mischief" Farrer, the team currently has a 60.5 percent chance of qualifying for the Major.

The Roman Imperium Cup will take place from March 12-15 at the SAW Esports Arena in Vila Nova de Gaia. Liquid will take on BESTIA, Gaimin Gladiators, Famalicão and OlyBet in the best-of-one round-robin group stage.
