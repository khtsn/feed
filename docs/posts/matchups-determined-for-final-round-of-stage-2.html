Matchups determined for final round of Stage 2

The matchups for the final round of IEM Cologne's Stage 2 have been determined, with six teams remaining in contention for the final three spots in Stage 3.

The fifth round will begin with a clash between Monte and paiN, who are separated by just two places in the global VRS. The European team will be playing its first elimination match in Cologne, while the Brazilians have already seen off FlyQuest and Astralis.

VRS No.8 Legacy will go up against TYLOO in a rematch of their CS Asia Championships group upper bracket semi-final. The Brazilians ran out 2-0 winners in a highly contested series with two overtimes on Overpass and one on Ancient.

Just like TYLOO, BIG once again find themselves in a 2-2 pool in Cologne. After losing to G2, the hometown heroes will take on B8, who will be looking to make it three wins in a row after staving off elimination against GamerLegion and MIBR.
