Two Astronauts on an 8-Day Mission That Turned Into a 9-Month Odyssey

An interview with Suni Williams and Butch Wilmore, whose planned short mission to space turned into an adventure lasting much, much longer.

March 14, 2025Updated 10:42 a.m. ET

Listen and follow ‘The Daily’

A spacecraft malfunction in June kept the NASA astronauts Suni Williams and Butch Wilmore on the International Space Station for more than nine months. They are scheduled to return home in the next few days.

Before they do, Michael Barbaro spoke to them about their extended stay in orbit.
