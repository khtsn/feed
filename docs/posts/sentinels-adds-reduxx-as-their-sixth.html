Sentinels Sentinels North America Rank #2 N4RRATE Marshall Massey bang Sean Bezerra Zellsis Jordan Montemurro zekken Zachary Patrone johnqt Mohamed Amine Ouarid has signed Yassin "Reduxx" Aboulalazm as their sixth player for the 2025 season. This addition comes after their previous sub, curry , was removed from the roster.

Before joining Sentinels, Reduxx spent most of his career with Oxygen Esports Oxygen Esports Inactive skuba Logan Jenkins mitch Mitch Semago Verno Andrew Maust Reduxx Yassin Aboulalazm dapr Michael Gulino in the tier two scene. When he became old enough to compete in NA Challengers in 2023, he stepped into the starting roster and placed fourth in the playoffs. In the following year, Oxygen were strong contenders for a shot at Ascension, but they once again fell short at fourth place. Following this result, Oxygen disbanded, leaving Reduxx free to explore his options.

Prior to announcing their two newest additions to their team, Sentinels played in the Red Bull Home Ground NA play-ins. For this tournament, Reduxx unofficially debuted under the org along with curry. In this tournament, Sentinels narrowly beat YFP Gaming YFP Gaming North America Rank #12 jakee Jake Anderson bdog Brandon Sanders penny Erik Penny thief Brady Dever fiziq Lucas Blow 2-1 before being defeated by Cloud9 Cloud9 North America Rank #4 Xeppaa Erick Bach Rossy Daniel Abedrabbo v1c Victor Truong moose Kaleb Jayne OXY Francis Hoang in the grand finals.
