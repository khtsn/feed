BME to host three tier 2 LANs in Stockholm

Black Molly Entertainment has announced a new LAN series dubbed the "Stake Pulse Beats," which will run as three separate tournaments in Stockholm, Sweden, in August, September and November.

Details for the first event, taking place from August 4-9, have been unveiled.

It will boast a $60,000 prizepool and feature 16 teams, two of which will come from open qualifiers (June 23-24) and 14 from open signups on a first-come, first-served basis, with a $2,000 fee attached.

Registrations will open on June 18 at 12:00 on the Stake Pulse website.

The following two events will take place on September 21-27 and October 12-17 and also feature 16 teams.
