Champions Paris playoff draw revealed

Valorant Champions 2025

1 day ago

With the group stage coming to an end, the playoff matches for Champions Paris have been revealed with an on-stage draw show by Riot.

While inter-regional matchups were allowed, teams could not be drawn to play on the same side of the eight-team bracket as another team from the same group.

The matches are as follows:

  • Team Heretics Team Heretics Europe Rank #1 MiniBoo Dominykas Lukaševičius Wo0t Mert Alkan RieNs Enes Ecirli benjyfishy Benjamin David Fish Boo Ričardas Lukaševičius vs. MIBR MIBR Brazil Rank #1 artzin Arthur Araujo xenom Eduardo Soeiro cortezia Gabriel Cortez Verno Andrew Maust aspas Erick Santos (Group D #1 vs. Group B #2)
  • NRG NRG North America Rank #2 s0m Sam Oh brawk Brock Somerhalder mada Adam Pampuch skuba Logan Jenkins Ethan Ethan Arnold vs. GIANTX GIANTX Europe Rank #9 Cloud Kirill Nehozhin grubinho Grzegorz Ryczko westside Miłosz Duda Flickless Karel Maeckelbergh ara Hanceriuc Eduard-George (Group C #1 vs. Group A #2)

The playoffs will start on the 25th, with matches being played in the Les Arènes de Grand Paris Sud (Grand Arena of South Paris), while finals weekend will be hosted at the 20,300-seat Accor Arena. The match schedule for playoffs is set to be revealed in the coming days.
