Vitality defeat Aurora in two to reach PGL Cluj-Napoca semi-finals

The French star posted a 1.66 rating in the quarter-final bout

Vitality are through to the PGL Cluj-Napoca semi-finals after a resounding first-round playoff victory over Aurora. "We showed our A game even though we had a lot of people sick," Shahar "⁠flameZ⁠" Shushan, the second-highest rated player for Vitality in the series, said on the broadcast after the match.

"No one played for three days, so we needed to get back into things," he added, "but we're very confident going into the semis. Most of the time, if we play our A game, it's gonna be a trophy in the bag."

The series kicked off on Inferno, where Robin "⁠ropz⁠" Kool was the pointman for Vitality on the CT side. With Aurora gunning to make it even at the half, the Estonian's best play came in the final round of the half, when he single-handedly turned a 2v4 situation with four kills to keep his team in the lead at the switch, 7-5.

Mathieu "⁠ZywOo⁠" Herbaut, who didn't have much impact in the first half, came alive on the attack, with 13 kills, 130 ADR, and +12.47% swing to lead Vitality to a 13-8 victory on Aurora's map pick.

The series moved to Overpass, and the ZywOo show continued as Vitality's star went 16-0 in the first eight rounds of play with around 200 ADR before Caner "⁠soulfly⁠" Kesici managed to kill him for the first time. And it wasn't until the following round that Aurora put together a successful defense in what was quickly looking like a lost cause.

Trailing by six after the two-man show put together by ZywOo and flameZ, Aurora won the second pistol round, but Vitality hit right back by winning the forcebuy. The Turks didn't give up, and finally put up some resistance, winning four in a row until a botched A hit against a forcebuy put Vitality up 12-8.

Aurora were not going to go gently into that good night, however, and a double entry by Ismailcan "⁠XANTARES⁠" Dörtkardeş in the 22nd round put his team on double digits and within range of the comeback.

Who else but ZywOo, who had gone somewhat quiet on the CT side after his heroic T side, was the one to put an end to it, clutching out a 1v2 to secure the victory for Vitality.

Aurora's journey in Cluj-Napoca is now over, while Vitality's next rival will come from the final game of the quarter-finals, pitting The MongolZ and FURIA.

This match also helps ZywOo cement his spot at the top of the stats leaderboard, which he is spearheading with a 1.44 rating, .11 above the eliminated Ilya "⁠m0NESY⁠" Osipov and .20 ahead of his teammate flameZ.
