Shortly after his team's win over FaZe, saffee spoke with HLTV regarding returning to the server after his lengthy time away, how he spent his time on MIBR's bench, and team's plans as the IEM Cologne Major rapidly approaches.

You can listen to the interview here.

Big win over FaZe to kick things off in Atlanta, how are you feeling about the team's performance?

It feels good, right, to have such a good victory against such a good team. It just feels like we're on the right path. We've been practicing for two weeks now, and we're just trying to figure things out. I believe we felt good in the server.

This is your first official in nine months; how does it feel to be back in the server?

It feels really good, I missed it. Also, being back in this jersey feels nice. I really missed it; I missed competing and travelling.

Obviously, this isn't your first time in a paiN jersey, but the team is very different from when you first played for them. What's it like working with these guys, especially biguzera again?

It feels nice because it's such a young team, if you take a look. With biguzera, I know the way that he likes to call because, the system, it's not the same, but it's how they think about the game and things like this.

I know how important it is for me, and for the people around me, for me to be here

You were riding MIBR's bench for a few months. What was that process like? Were you getting offers and just waiting for the right one? How were you staying sharp in the meantime?

At first, I believe it was early September it happened, I just wanted to spend time with my family and the people that I love, and I didn't even want to focus on CS that much. When it got to November, I started to keep in shape, look at games, and things like this, because I had a few offers for January, and I just wanted to be sharp. Things didn't work out, and I just tried to keep working, and now I'm here.

You're taking the spot of nqz who stepped aside for personal reasons. Do you have any idea about what the situation would look like if he wanted to return?

Not really, I just try to focus on my game and try to help those guys, and give the best that I can. I know how important it is for me, and for the people around me, for me to be here.

After this event, you're headed over to China for CAC, a little bit of a break, and then the Major. What are the pre-Major plans for you guys?

I believe we're going to bootcamp after China, I believe we're going to Belgrade. We're going to stay there and try to get better every day.
