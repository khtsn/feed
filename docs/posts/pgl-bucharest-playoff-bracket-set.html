PGL Bucharest playoff bracket set

PGL Bucharest has reached the bracket stage after five rounds of Swiss group play, which saw FUT and Astralis go through undefeated and PARIVISION just barely advance through the final 2-2 round.

Dzhami "⁠Jame⁠" Ali's men were one of the pre-tournament favorites for the title, but squandering two chances to qualify for the playoffs means have been matched up against the other highest-ranked team, The MongolZ, in the quarter-finals. The teams will face each other for the first time in their history, with Jame's last match against them dating back to IEM Rio 2024.

FUT, who beat PARIVISION in the 2-0 pool, are on the same side of the bracket and will take on B8 in their quarter-final, while the other 3-0 team, Astralis, will take on EYEBALLERS on the opposite end and MIBR will do battle against 3DMAX.
