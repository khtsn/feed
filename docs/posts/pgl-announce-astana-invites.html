PGL announce Astana invites*

Update 12:11

HLTV reached out to FUT regarding the fact they accepted invites for both IEM Atlanta and PGL Astana, with a team representative sharing the following statement:

"It was on purpose to cover the possibility that the team wouldn’t be able to get visas. PGL allowed every team to withdraw from the event until a certain date, which is after the deadline visa grant for IEM Atlanta."

PGL has announced the invites for the upcoming Astana tournament, set to be played from May 9-17 in Kazakhstan's capital.

Most top ten teams have accepted invites, with notable emissions from the team list coming from Vitality and Natus Vincere. Last year's finalists Astralis have also declined the invite, as have FaZe, GamerLegion, and NRG. All six rosters will be attending IEM Atlanta at time of the Kazakh tournament.

FUT, who have accepted an invite to Astana, are also currently confirmed to be playing IEM Atlanta during the same timeframe.

The remaining four spots for the event will come from regional qualifiers, set to be begin at the end of March.
