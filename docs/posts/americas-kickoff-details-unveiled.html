Americas Kickoff details unveiled

Following the announcements for EMEA and Pacific, Riot have announced the schedule and groups for the 2024 VCT Americas Kickoff tournament. Beginning on February 16, Kickoff will determine which teams will participate in Masters Madrid and offer points for Champions qualification.

The 11 team event will begin with a GSL format involving three groups, two groups of four teams and one with only three. Having won Champions 2023, Evil Geniuses Evil Geniuses North America Rank #1 Derrek Derrek Ha NaturE Nicholas Garrison supamen Phat Le Apoth Vincent Le jawgemo Alexander Mor are automatically placed in the latter group, receiving a first round bye. The remaining teams were split into pools and drawn into their respective group. All matches will be best-of-three.

Each group's second place team will play in a single round-robin, with the winner earning a spot in Playoffs. All matches will be best-of-three.

The three best teams of each group and the winner of the Play-In stage will compete in a single elimination bracket. The Play-In team will be placed opposite of the team in their original group. Both finalists will qualify to Masters Madrid, with the winner receiving three Championship Points for Champions qualification and preferential seeding at Madrid. The semifinals will be best-of-three, while the grand final is a best-of-five match.
