Inner Circle sign headtr1ck

Inner Circle have announced the signing of Daniil "⁠headtr1ck⁠" Valitov to fill the void left by Artem "⁠Flierax⁠" Khioara's benching earlier in March.

Inner Circle bench and transfer-list Flierax

headtr1ck joins the team after a three-month stint on the B8 bench, which ended at the start of March when the AWPer was released from his contract.

The 21-year-old averaged a 1.05 rating across 2025 in the team, but his form dropped off from a 1.12 in the first half of the year to 0.94 in the second, leading to his eventual removal in the off-season.

Inner Circle will hope that the addition of the experienced AWPer will provide an upturn in form after the team was eliminated in the first playoff round in three of their last four tournaments.

headtr1ck's debut with Inner Circle is expected to come in CCT Season 3 Europe Series 18, where they have received an invite to the playoff stage.
