Golden Globes 2025 Red Carpet: See Looks From Emma Stone, Melissa McCarthy and More

Stars have begun arriving for the 82nd Golden Globes, an awards season opener of sorts celebrating the biggest stars in film and television.

Jan. 5, 2025Updated 8:30 p.m. ET

By some measures, awards season dressing has already been underway for weeks: Festival juries and film critics associations hand out trophies, and if stars deign to collect them in person, propriety usually demands that they be clothed.

With social media, stars have a platform to show off their looks even when network TV cameras are nowhere to be found. But with the Golden Globes on Sunday, the televised portion of the horse race for hardware begins in earnest. Here’s what some of Hollywood’s buzziest performers wore to make their entrance at the Beverly Hilton Hotel in Los Angeles.
