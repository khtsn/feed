Aurora send PARIVISION to 1-2 bracket in Astana

Superior individual impact ensured Aurora put PARIVISION one match from elimination.

Aurora are a single match away from the playoffs of PGL Astana after securing a 2-0 victory over PARIVISION, beating their opponents on Anubis (13-7) and Dust2 (16-14).

Anubis kicked off the series and it was a dire map for PARIVISION. Aurora could do no wrong on their CT side, winning several man-down scenarios including a ridiculous 1v4 by Ali "⁠Wicadia⁠" Haydar Yalçın, securing a dominant score on the unfavored half.

Wicadia brings home a ridiculous 1v4 by switching to the Dualies

A pistol win and conversion on the T side virtually ensured the result for Aurora, and although PARIVISION picked up a few rounds off the back of a Vladislav "⁠xiELO⁠" Lysov and Andrey "⁠BELCHONOKK⁠" Yasinskiy 2v4, the result was inevitable.

Dust2 was a scrappy and back-and-forth affair, bizarre situations falling in favour of both teams across the map. PARIVISION started strong on their T side before Caner "⁠soulfly⁠" Kesici's impact hauled Aurora back into contention, including a 1v3 clutch at the end of a bizarre round.

soulfly kills three on his own at Long while his team hits B

PARIVISION seemed set to force a decider as Aurora seemed short of ideas and firepower at the start of their offensive half, but once PARIVISION hit 11 rounds, the Turks found their footing. The entry frags started flowing to help force overtime, before immense impact by soulfly and Wicadia ensured a 2-0 series victory for their side.
