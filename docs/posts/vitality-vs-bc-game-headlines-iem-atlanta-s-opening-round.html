Vitality vs BC.Game headlines IEM Atlanta's opening round

ESL has revealed the match-ups for the first round of the group stage of IEM Atlanta. The North American tournament will take place from May 11-17 and feature a $1 million prize pool.

The 16 participant teams have been split into two double-elimination, best-of-three groups, with the top three teams from each group advancing to the single-elimination playoffs.

In the opening round, all eyes will be on the clash between Vitality and BC.Game. The two teams met in Stage 2 of IEM Kraków back in January, with Dan "⁠apEX⁠" Madesclaire's charges running out 2-0 winners.

BC.Game will be fielding a different lineup this time around, however, following the double benching of Christopher "⁠MUTiRiS⁠" Fernandes and António "⁠aragornN⁠" Barbosa.

Azbayar "⁠Senzu⁠" Munkhbold was signed on loan from The MongolZ, while analyst Robin "⁠ScrunK⁠" Röpke will be playing for the team as a stand-in in this tournament and at the CS Asia Championships.
