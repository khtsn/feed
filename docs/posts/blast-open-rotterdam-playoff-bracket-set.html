BLAST Open Rotterdam playoff bracket set

The playoff bracket for BLAST Open Rotterdam has been set following the conclusion of the group stage at BLAST's studios in Copenhagen, with six teams now heading to the Netherlands to play in the Ahoy Arena.

The MongolZ sealed the final spot after an Ayush "⁠mzinho⁠" Batbold masterclass carried them past Spirit, and the Mongolian team will be the first into the arena as they take on Aurora in the quarter-finals.

The clear headliner of the opening pair of matches is a rematch of the PGL Cluj-Napoca quarter-final between Falcons and PARIVISION, with the former on the hunt for revenge after being trumped by Dzhami "⁠Jame⁠" Ali's side, 2-1, in that meeting.

Vitality and Natus Vincere earned byes to the semi-finals after topping their groups, with Dan "⁠apEX⁠" Madesclaire's men awaiting the winner of The MongolZ and Aurora while Natus Vincere take on the winner of the Cluj-Napoca rematch.
