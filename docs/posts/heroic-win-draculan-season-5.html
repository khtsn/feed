HEROIC win DraculaN Season 5

HEROIC have come out on top in DraculaN Season 5, taking down the newly assembled 100 Thieves lineup in the grand final 2-1 to claim the Romanian event's $7,500 grand prize.

HEROIC, coming off a lackluster 12-14th finish at PGL Cluj-Napoca and still without the accompaniment of AWPer Alimzhan "⁠Alkaren⁠" Bitimbay, battled past much of their opposition with ease. The international side reached the grand final through the upper-bracket, taking down INFINITE, ASTRAL, CYBERSHOKE, and FlyQuest along the way, all without dropping a map.

100 Thieves, who entered the event without a place on the Valve Regional Standings (VRS), gave HEROIC a tougher fight, locking down their pick of Inferno 13-10 before HEROIC rallied with wins on Nuke (13-10) and Ancient (13-9).

HEROIC started the event at #28 in the global VRS, but have now climbed to #15 following their DraculaN victory, putting them in the running for invites to PGL Astana and FISSURE Playground 3 as the March 2 invitation deadline fast approaches. In the meantime, HEROIC will be preparing for their next outing at ESL Pro League Season 23, set to begin tomorrow with an opening match against NRG.

Many teams other than HEROIC enjoyed VRS boons from DraculaN Season 5. Notably, 100 Thieves went from being unranked to finishing at #32 in the world, while Eternal Fire climbed 86 spots to #76.
