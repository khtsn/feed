Cast your vote for the Major MVP medal design

The community has the chance to influence a piece of Counter-Strike history once again, thanks to HLTV and 1xBET.

Continuing HLTV's newest MVP tradition, where the biggest and best events are honoured with special commemorative MVP medals, together with 1xBET we are once again asking for the community's contribution for the IEM Cologne Major medal.

Featuring one more option than the Budapest Major MVP medal voting late last year, there are four designs competing for your votes and the one that wins will be presented to the MVP of IEM Cologne Major 2026.

Each design features references to the host city as well as Counter-Strike elements. Check out all four options in the carousel and find your favorite, and then cast your vote in the widget below:
