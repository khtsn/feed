Elon Musk Leads Bid to Buy OpenAI for $97.4 Billion

The billionaire is leading a group of investors in the unsolicited offer, which complicates the start-up’s plan to change its nonprofit status.

Feb. 10, 2025, 4:22 p.m. ET

A group of investors led by Elon Musk has made a $97.4 billion bid to buy the nonprofit that controls OpenAI, according to two people familiar with the bid, escalating a yearslong tussle between Mr. Musk and OpenAI’s chief executive, Sam Altman.

The consortium includes Vy Capital and Xai, Mr. Musk’s A.I. company, and other investors, said the people, who spoke on condition of anonymity because the discussions are ongoing.
