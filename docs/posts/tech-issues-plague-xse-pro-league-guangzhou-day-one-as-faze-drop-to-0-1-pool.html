Tech issues plague XSE Pro League Guangzhou day one as FaZe drop to 0-1 pool

Day one of XSE Pro League Guangzhou 2026 came to an end with B8 taking down MIBR after lengthy tech issues prolonged the opening round of the Swiss bracket.

The tournament is the first Big Event to feature Cache in the map pool since it was re-added to the game, but the map failed to see any action on the first day.

The tournament began with TYLOO getting the better of FaZe on Jason "⁠JBOEN⁠" Boe Nielsen's debut for the squad, despite the Danish sniper topping the board with a 1.09 rating.

More debuts followed, as 3DMAX began their time with Jeremy "⁠Kursy⁠" Gast with a win over Ninjas in Pyjamas, while the new-look PARIVISION demolished Alliance.

Luminosity's return to Counter-Strike wasn't as successful, however, as they were bested by Nemesis in the organization's first official in almost seven years.
