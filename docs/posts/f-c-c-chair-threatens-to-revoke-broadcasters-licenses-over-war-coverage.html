F.C.C. Chair Threatens to Revoke Broadcasters’ Licenses Over War Coverage

FUT and Astralis are playing their third match at ESL Pro League in the bronze medal match. The two teams, in 9th and 10th place in the VRS standings, met in Stage 1 and Stage 2 of the Stockholm event, trading 2-0 series ahead of their third meeting in the third-place decider.

After the bronze match, Aurora and Natus Vincere will take the stage to crown a new ESL Pro League champion as the two teams chase their first trophy of 2026.
