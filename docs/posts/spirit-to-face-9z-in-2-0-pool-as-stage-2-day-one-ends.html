Spirit to face 9z in 2-0 pool as Stage 2 day one ends

donk is sitting on a 2.01 rating after two easy BO1 games

The opening day of the IEM Cologne Major's Stage 2 has come to an end, with four teams being one win away from the next stage in the 2-0 pool and four staring elimination in the face in the 0-2 pool.

After a rather straightforward first round, there were a few surprises in the second round, most notably M80's win against B8, who are now in a do-or-die situation.

Favorites prevail in 1-0 pool

The biggest surprise in the 1-0 pool was how competitive the Mirage game between G2 and Monte turned out to be. Nemanja "⁠huNter-⁠" Kovač's troops managed a triple-overtime win after surviving four match points (and wasting two of their own in regulation).

VRS No.2 Spirit, one of the favorites to go 3-0 in this stage, made light work of MIBR on Mirage, with Danil "⁠donk⁠" Kryshkovets leading the way with a 1.75 rating.
