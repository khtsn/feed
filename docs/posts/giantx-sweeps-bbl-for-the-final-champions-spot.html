Just one day earlier, BBL were only one round away from qualifying to Champions, but a nAts ace denied them that opportunity. Just like in Kickoff and Stage 1, Team Liquid were the ones to gatekeep BBL from the international stage, forcing them down to the lower bracket to face GIANTX.

Few believed in GIANTX's chances at Champions. They had a rough Kickoff and Stage 1, but the additions of ara and Flickless breathed new life into the team, and they came out strong in Stage 2. Even so, with only one championship point before Stage 2, they would have to make the top two to qualify to Champions, an impossible-looking task with the top three of EMEA seeming so far ahead of the pack. Nevertheless, GIANTX leveled up in playoffs and completed the run all the way to the grand final, earning not only a spot at Champions, but a top two seed from EMEA.

In three out of four of GIANTX's playoff matches, they got absolutely stomped on the first map. So, when GIANTX came out and dominated BBL on map one with a 13-2 scoreline, it was clear they were a different beast today. Their Ascent defense was completely impregnable, with westside and Cloud holding down the B site, while ara's Operator shut down any A hits.

The confidence was flowing out of GIANTX

Map two of Sunset hardly went any better for BBL, as the first half ended with them down 3-9. Out of the five rounds they had won at that point, none of them had been consecutive, meaning they were never able to build any sort of momentum. All of GIANTX was firing on all cylinders, while BBL was constantly making costly mistakes.

Finally, in the second half of the map, BBL started showing some life. With their first pistol round of the series, they began making the comeback. A 2v4 from Jamppi and sociablEE helped close the gap down to 8-10, but that was the last round they would see before GIANTX closed out the map 13-8.

Jamppi and sociablEE give BBL some fleeting hope.

The last map of the series was Bind, where it finally looked like BBL was going to keep up with GIANTX in the first half. While they still lost the pistol, they managed to keep it close, and were even the better team on buy rounds in the half. However, two eco rounds kept GIANTX in the lead, ending the half with a 7-5 scoreline.

westside's Sheriff is demonic.

When GIANTX moved to the attack side, BBL were helpless, as Bind turned into ara's playground. In the end, the rookie that came in to revitalize GIANTX's season was the one to deliver the death blow to BBL, and send his team to Champions. With a 4k to cap off an incredible series and playoffs run, ara brought his team over the line to win map three 13-6, completing the 3-0 sweep.

Elation for GIANTX, heartbreak for BBL.

For the first time since 2023, GIANTX will be featured at a global event, soon to take the stage at Champions Paris. Tomorrow, GIANTX will face Team Liquid in the grand final to decide who will win first seed and lift the EMEA Stage 2 trophy.
