Media: Legacy to bench n1ssim; target insani as replacement

Vinicius "⁠n1ssim⁠" Pereira has stepped down from Legacy's starting lineup, according to a report from Dust2 Brasil. The news comes just a day after the Brazilian team was eliminated from the Cologne Major in Stage 3 with a 1-3 record.

Dust2 Brasil adds that Felipe "⁠insani⁠" Yuji is Legacy's main target to fill the vacancy on their roster, though they face a big task to convince MIBR to let go of their most prized asset.

This gives MIBR's hierarchy another headache to deal with. Klimentii "⁠kl1m⁠" Krivosheev's loan deal expired after the Cologne Major, and his future remains subject to conjecture as G2 are prepared to listen to offers for the Russian AWPer.

n1ssim has been on Legacy's books since January 2025, when he was picked up on a free transfer following the expiry of his Sharks contract. He replaced Bruno "⁠b4rtiN⁠" Câmara in the starting lineup.

With n1ssim on the roster, Legacy won back-to-back CS Asia Championship trophies. The Brazilian team was unable to ride the momentum from its second CAC title into the Major, where it made it through Stage 2 only in the last round before exiting Stage 3 in 12th-14th place, beating PARIVISION and losing to MOUZ, Natus Vincere and G2.
