FaZe and NIP stave off elimination as XSE Pro League day three ends

FaZe brushed aside SINNERS in the 0-2 pool

FaZe and Ninjas in Pyjamas survived elimination on day three of the XSE Pro League, which, like the first two days, ran deep into the early hours due to severe delays, with the last match ending past 4 am local time.

FaZe made light work of SINNERS in a 0-2 pool match, while Ninjas in Pyjamas had to come back from a map down to defeat the new Luminosity team in the other elimination series.

TYLOO became the first team to book a playoff spot in Guangzhou with a convincing 2-0 win over EYEBALLERS at the start of day three. The other progression series was a keenly contested Russian derby between BetBoom and Nemesis that went the way of Kirill "⁠Boombl4⁠" Mikhailov's troops after overtime on the decider map.
