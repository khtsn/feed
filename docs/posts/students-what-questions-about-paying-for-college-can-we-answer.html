Students, What Questions About Paying for College Can We Answer?

We hope to tackle some essential queries — but we need your help.

The college admissions process is a fraught one, to put it mildly. But there is one subject that often dominates the discussion: paying for college.

Are you a student in the United States applying — or even just considering it? If so, what questions do you have about paying for it? No question is too basic: What type of school is right for me? What kinds of financial aid are available? Are work-study programs worth it?

We may use your submissions to help guide The Times’s coverage. (Personal information of respondents will not be published.)
