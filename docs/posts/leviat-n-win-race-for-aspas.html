The Latin American org has signed aspas , arguably the most sought-after Americas players of the off season. The announcement follows the expiration of Tacolilla 's contract, with the Brazilian duelist joining as a direct replacement.

Due to a fairly good international debut and the acquisition of their direct rival's best players, Leviatán started 2023 with sky-high expectations. Most considered that they fell short. In spite of top placements in every tournament they participated in, they failed to qualify to an international event.
