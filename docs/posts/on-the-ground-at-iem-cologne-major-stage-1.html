On the ground at IEM Cologne Major Stage 1

Sebastian "⁠beastik⁠" Daňo didn't mince his words after SINNERS' elimination in Cologne. "A disaster, I would say, we did not come here for 0-3," he told HLTV after the elimination.

"I'm not happy with it. Even though today we kind of played okay-ish, I would say, but I would still expect more from us. What we have shown in the past, I think we have more [to show], so I'm very disappointed."

On how FL4MUS is adapting to s1ren's positions: "He's ready to push the smoke, and he's ready to peek, and he's very proactive inside the game, and I think it's much easier to play with him.

"Especially on the stage, I just feel, for example, if you don't know what to do on the map, you just go like, okay, I'm pushing the smoke. Okay, and then he gets some important spots on the map, and it's easier to play with."

The highest-rated player of day one wants to keep it up.

On getting the best out of insani and kl1m: "It's maybe not the focus, like how to get the most out of him and both at the same time. But we try to always put them both in situations where they can thrive. But not specifically them, but just in general.

"And I think today was maybe more him just taking the game in his own hands and maybe not me actually doing so much for him. Which was really nice to see. I'm really happy for him that he could play so well today."
