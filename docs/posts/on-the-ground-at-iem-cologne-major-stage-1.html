On JBa's spike in form: "The biggest thing for him has been trying to get him into our system and, like, into the team play system. Sometimes he gets, I think we saw it after player break, or we had like a three-week break after Dracula or something, where it's like the bad habits come back a little bit from the spam pugs.

"So it's like, we're trying to get him to be like, 'like, yeah, like, this is the pug mentality, but, but come on, like, get back into this for a match.' Like, 'don't repeat this.' And things like that. But he always pugs. He's like almost pugging with slaxz- 24-7 every day."

On making Stage 2: "I think I can say I feel some relief inside of my body. I'm happy that we proved that we can go to the next stage. Hopefully we're not done here, we want to go as far as possible, but this was a hard game, personally for me as well, and I'm just happy we did it.

koala after elimination: "It's good to play the Major, of course, but how it ends, for me right now it's a bit sad because we didn't play one match well. At least the first map against TYLOO, it was a good map. When we won HEROIC we didn't play well on Nuke, but we won because our Nuke is strong, but we only played one map well, in my opinion, in the entire Major.

"So it's sad because we put so much expectation, we wanted to play well and prove ourselves to the world. We are not the tier-S team yet, but it's like a tier 2, challenging the best teams in the scene. But it didn't happen, so it's really frustrating after all."

Declan "⁠Vexite⁠" Portelli stopped in the mixed zone to give his thoughts on FlyQuest's survival over THUNDER dOWNUNDER.

After beating TDU 2-0: "I was definitely having fun. Domestic CS is a special thing, and I feel like we don't always get to play it. It was a special game, really fun to play.

On it being the first Oceanic derby at a Major: "I don't know, the Major is a prestigous tournament, and with it being an elimination match it definitely mattered a lot to us. But the special part was us versing our old teammates. We haven't played Australians for a while and we wanted to prove that we are still the best Australian team. I guess this game solidifies that.

On TDU overtaking them in VRS: "They definitely deserve the VRS points they have, they won the games and went further than us in some tournaments. At the end of the day we played different competitions, and never really matched up against each other. That's why it made it special."
