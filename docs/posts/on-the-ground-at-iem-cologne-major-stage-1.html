GUM: "HEROIC has been on a downfall recently; we thought we should beat them easily on Dust2"

vs.

Yifei "⁠GUM⁠" Dai was thrilled when the veto went to Lynn Vision's home map of Dust2 against HEROIC:

"Heroic is actually been on a downfall recently, they lost so many matches in a row. And for best of one today, we had the advantage because of the pick-ban, we can almost see the map will be Dust 2, which is our best map. Actually, we think we should beat them easily on Dust 2, but we made a mistake from the start, like the Inferno [vs M80]. So today, actually, both maps, we had a slow start. But fortunately, we won this Dust 2, so great."

NAF: "Team Liquid is a fantastic organization so it's super disappointing to be letting them down"

vs.

Speaking to HLTV: "Team Liquid is a fantastic organization, you know, so it's super disappointing to be kind of letting them down with how bad of a string of events in the past year have been, you know, so they. Definitely, the org, the name deserves to be on top, and we obviously are all of course very disappointed, especially myself. It's no fun to be in this position and just letting everybody around you down, it's not a good thing.

"But I think we're slowly getting there, hopefully striving for greatness, and yeah. The goal is just to get as many points as we possibly can, I don't care what stage or whatever we get, we just need to keep winning, keep our heads down and work harder than everybody else, because that's the only way we can get back to the top."
