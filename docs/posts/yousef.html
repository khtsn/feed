Yousef

Can he find his family a safe place to stay in Gaza?

March 2, 2024, 7:30 a.m. ET

On This Week’s Episode:

Yousef Hammash has been moving his family from one place to another in Gaza for five months. He always finds options. Now his youngest sister is about to have a baby, and everyone may need to move again. They’re all looking to Yousef to find one more option.
