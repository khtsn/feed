BLAST unveil opening matchups of Open Rotterdam

BLAST has revealed the opening matchups for BLAST Open Rotterdam, which is set to take place March 18-29. The group stage of the event will take place in the BLAST studio in Denmark, whilst the playoffs will go down in the Ahoy Arena in Rotterdam.

The 16 teams attending the event will be split across two groups of eight and compete in a double-elimination BO3 format, with the group winners advancing to the semi-finals whilst second and third head to the quarter-finals. A single-elimination BO3 format will take over for the playoffs, culminating in a BO5 final.

Some of the more compelling opening matchups see FaZe take on Aurora, with both teams looking to prove something coming into the event, and Natus Vincere facing off against B8, pitting Ukraine's premiere organization against an all-Ukrainian lineup.
