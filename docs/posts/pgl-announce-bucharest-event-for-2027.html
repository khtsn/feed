PGL announce Bucharest event for 2027

PGL have shared the details regarding the first of their 2027 events, PGL Bucharest, set to run from January 17-24.

Originally planned to take place in Krakow, PGL opted to shift locations for their first event of the year after the Polish city's TAURON Arena cancelled their booking for the event. The Romanian tournament organizer is already well acquainted with Bucharest, having hosted two events in the city in 2025, with two more scheduled for 2026.

PGL's first event of the 2027 calendar is notable for being almost entirely overlapping with BLAST Bounty Season 1, with the Maltese event running from January 11-24, starting just days earlier.

The event will feature 16 teams, with 14 spots coming via global VRS invites, issued based on the rankings on October 5. The remaining two spots will be filled via a European closed qualifier, which will itself be filled with six teams from the European VRS and two teams from open qualifiers.

PGL Bucharest 2027 is set to feature a $1,000,000 traditional prize pool and a $400,000 Valve Regional Standings (VRS) invite bonus pool, offering an $80,000 bonus to the top five-ranked teams in the VRS that accept an invitation to the event, limited to the global top 14.
