Valve release March VRS update

The penultimate ranking update before the Cologne Major will send out invites to three notable LANs.

Valve has released the monthly Valve Regional Standings update for March, which will be used to send out invites to PGL Astana, BLAST Rivals, and CCT Global Finals.

FISSURE Playground 3 is also slated to use the March ranking for its invites, but it remains unclear whether the event will happen as scheduled amidst rumors of the tournament organizer facing bankruptcy and losing its primary sponsor.

Below you can find a list of how those tournaments will allocate slots from the ranking and their relevant regional lists:

PGL Astana (May 9-17)

  • 12 Global spots to main event

  • 4 EU spots to EU qualifier

  • 4 NA spots to NA qualifier

  • 4 SA spots to SA qualifier

  • 2 East Asia, 2 West Asia, 2 Oceania spots to Asia qualifiers

BLAST Rivals 2026 Season 1 (April 27 - May 3)

  • 8 Global spots to main event

CCT Global Finals (April 23-26)

  • 6 Global spots to main event

  • 2 Wildcard spots to winners of Parken Challenger Championship S1 & S2

FISSURE Playground 3 (April 20-26)

  • 15 Global spots to main event

  • 5 Asia spots to Asia qualifier
