Jimpphat signs with Aurora

3-7-2026 20:00

The Finnish anchor has found a new home after parting ways with MOUZ.

Aurora have announced that they have completed the signing of Jimi "⁠Jimpphat⁠" Salo, who embarks on a new challenge after over four years with MOUZ.

The Finnish player has been inactive since April, when MOUZ decided to shake things up in the lead-up to the IEM Cologne Major by making a double change.

Jimpphat's signing comes as part of an international pivot for Aurora, who have benched Caner "⁠soulfly⁠" Kesici and parted ways with IGL Engin "⁠MAJ3R⁠" Küpeli and head coach Sezgin "⁠Fabre⁠" Kalaycı in the off-season.

Earlier on Friday, Aurora unveiled Ashley "⁠ash⁠" Battye as their new head coach, handing the Brit his first coaching job since he stepped down from GamerLegion in October 2025 due to burnout.
