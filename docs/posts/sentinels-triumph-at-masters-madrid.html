Sentinels triumph at Masters Madrid

It's been a long time since Sentinels have graced the biggest stages in the world. Nearly three years after lifting the first international trophy in VCT history, they finally returned to lift a trophy once more.

In one of the best finals the VCT has yet seen, SEN and GENG battled back and forth for five hours on five maps. Close fought affairs on Breeze, Bind, Split, and Ascent finally yielded to SEN's coronation on Icebox.

Sentinels have become just the second organization to hoist multiple VCT trophies.

They rise once more.
