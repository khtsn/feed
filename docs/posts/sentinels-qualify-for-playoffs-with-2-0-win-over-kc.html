Sentinels qualify for playoffs with 2-0 win over KC

Sentinels Sentinels North America Rank #2 TenZ Tyson Ngo Zellsis Jordan Montemurro zekken Zachary Patrone johnqt Mohamed Amine Ouarid Sacy Gustavo Rossi took down Karmine Corp Karmine Corp Europe Rank #9 MAGNUM Martin Penkov tomaszy Tomás Machado N4RRATE Marshall Massey marteen Martin Pátek sh1n Ryad Ensaad in a matchup of 1-0 teams to become the first team to qualify for the Champions Tour 2024:Masters Madrid Playoffs. In a matchup of top ranked teams from their respective regions, SEN was able to outlast KC to win 2-0.

Karmine Corp let split through in the Map veto, and Sentinels showed why they are the most dominant team in the world on that map with a 13-8 victory. TenZ and zekken once again led the way on split with 20 and 26 kills respectively.

After falling behind 1-0 in the series, KC fell behind early on Lotus. After cutting the deficit to 7-5 at the half, it was a close map the rest of the way. A thrifty in the in the penultimate round gave Sentinels a 12-11 lead and they were able to close the series out in the next round.

Sentinels were led by a vintage Tenz performance. He posted 42 kills and a 1.45 rating across the two maps. N4RRATE once again led the way for KC with 30 kills in a losing record.

Sentinels have a few days to rest and get ready for the playoffs, while KC will play an elimination match on monday against a team to be determined.
