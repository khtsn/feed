The map veto began with FUT picking Icebox and 100 Thieves picking Bind, leaving Ascent as the potential third map.

100 Thieves were explosive on Icebox, their stalwart defense preventing FUT from even planting the spike until round nine. Winning the pistol and getting the Operator for Cryocells on their bonus round put 100 Thieves in a lead that they never let go of. The half ended 11-1 for 100 Thieves, who lost the second pistol before converting a thrifty eco win to end the map 13-2.

Both bang and eeiu stood out on Icebox, but moving Cryo onto Brimstone unlocked his true potential on Bind. 100 Thieves won the first pistol round thanks to an Asuna flank, then played a different defensive style to their Icebox. Instead of preventing spike plants, they coordinated utility on retakes to make post-plants tough for FUT. That was where Cryo showed upon Brimstone, landing crisp flicks to plow through the third seed from EMEA.

The half ended 8-4, and 100 Thieves won their second pistol round on Bind to keep the pressure on FUT. When the Thieves converted their bonus, the chance of a comeback was over. Bind ended 13-5, and 100 Thieves moved on in the upper bracket.

Cryocells was the star of the show, ending the series with 46 kills and a K/D differential of +26.
