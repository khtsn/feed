He started the year as the team's starting Controller player, but was benched three games into the team's Pacific Kickoff campaign before being benched for longtime player carpe . His spot in the starting five was reinstated in the team's second match at Masters Bangkok, where he helped the team lift its first-ever VCT trophy. T1 and Sylvan's return to regional play disappointed, though. After going 3-2 in the group stage of Stage 1, T1 was eliminated two matches into its playoff run. Since Sylvan's slide to the inactive roster, T1 achieved a fourth-place finish in Stage 2, qualified for Champions Paris through Championship Points, and was then eliminated in the group stage in Paris.

Team Secret will be Sylvan's first squad outside of China and Korea. The Filipino squad spent much of the season sitting at the bottom of the Pacific standings, managing just two wins in VCT play. Since the end of its season, Team Secret has cleared the books for a rebuild, letting go of Jremy , ZesBeeW , n1zzy , and Wild0reoo . Sylvan will team up with Korean head coach Meow , who joined Team Secret before the 2025 season began.
