NIP reach upper final at Stake Ranked; 3DMAX and HEROIC eliminated

The second day of Stake Ranked Episode 2 wrapped up just past midnight in Barcelona, Spain with Ninjas in Pyjamas making quick work of Sharks in the upper bracket (13-7 Ancient, 13-5 Nuke).

Rasmus "⁠sjuush⁠" Beck, when asked about Sharks after the game by Martin "⁠STYKO⁠" Styk, said the Brazilian team had good executes, but that gave Ninjas in Pyjamas material to counter and anti-strat.

That was on display throughout the series as Marco "⁠Snappi⁠" Pfeiffer had a handle on Sharks' strategy, and after sjuush's 24-12 K-D and 1.68 rating ensured a swift victory on Ancient, Kacper "⁠xKacpersky⁠" Gabara took over on Nuke with a career-best 2.81 rating and 26-10 K-D to ease through to the upper bracket final and a match against magic.
