NAVI to face Vitality in quarters as IEM Atlanta playoff bracket is set

Vitality have a clean record against NAVI this year

The IEM Atlanta group stage has come to an end, and with Vitality dropping to the lower bracket early in Group A and Natus Vincere failing to win Group B in the upper bracket final against Legacy, the tournament's two best squads will meet in the quarter-finals.

The odds are not very good for Natus Vincere, who are going into the match after having lost three series — including two best-of-five finals — this year against the Dan "⁠apEX⁠" Madesclaire-led squad without winning a map.

The winner of that series will go up against BetBoom, Vitality's butchers in Group A. On the other side of the bracket, paiN will face GamerLegion in the quarter-final, where the Brazilians will hope to move on to play a derby against Legacy in the semis.

The playoffs will kick off on Friday, May 15, with the quarter-finals, followed by the semi-finals on Saturday, and the third-place decider and grand final on Sunday.
