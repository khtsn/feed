Ascent was a complete reversal of Breeze as Shopify quickly re-established themselves as a contender in the series with a blistering 6-0 start. The North Americans dropped just two rounds in the first half and evened up the series with a 13-3 map victory.

The entire squad was firing on all cylinders with four players recording greater than 150 ADR, and with alexis and meL earning 100% KASTs. The clutch gene was also present for NA's last hope as all four 1v1 scenarios presented were won by Shopify.

Petra's heroics proved not enough for G2 Gozen.

Both teams played Sunset for the first time within the Game Changers circuit at this event with G2 recording a 14-12 win over Team Liquid Brazil and Shopify dominating Chao Hui EDward Gaming Chao Hui EDward Gaming China Rank #27 Blue WawaLee XiMiLu Lizhi Yusin Clem 13-2. Only one of them would turn a 1-0 map record into a 2-0 record today.

An initial pistol round win for G2 kept hopes high as they trailed by a narrow 7-5 scoreline coming into halftime. Petra continued to be the MVP for G2, while the entirety of Shopify continued to perform.

A four-round streak from Shopify to start the second half quickly dwindled any hopes for the reigning Game Changers champions as the North Americans led 11-5. A few stumbles on the road were all that stood between Shopify and a grand finals appearance as the lead ultimately proved too much for G2. While Petra topped the scoreboard in terms of ACS with a score of 333 and a +7 K/D differential, Shopify took the series 2-1 with a 13-8 map win.

The duo of florescent and meL proved pivotal in the final map as florescent's entrying capabilities were on full display. Both players recorded greater than 1.30 ratings, with florescent racking up 207 ADR.
