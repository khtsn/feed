B8 send FUT packing in Atlanta

s1zzi and npl were the stars as B8 defeated FUT

VRS No.7 FUT have been eliminated from IEM Atlanta in 9th-12th place following a 0-2 loss to B8, who will take on the winner of Vitality vs FaZe in a few hours for a spot in the playoffs.

It's a disappointing ending for FUT, who were hoping to build on their PGL Bucharest title after attending BLAST Rivals without Laurențiu "⁠lauNX⁠" Țârlea.

After a hard-fought win against NRG and a close loss to paiN, they turned in a beige performance against the Ukrainian team, and will now have three weeks to address their shortcomings before their Cologne Major campaign begins in Stage 2.

FUT were in control for the majority of the first half of Ancient before B8 secured the final two rounds of the half to keep the scoreline competitive. The second half was all about B8's tight defense as the Ukrainian team went on a flawless 8-0 run, during which Andrii "⁠npl⁠" Kukharskyi and Danylo "⁠s1zzi⁠" Vinnyk combined for 24 frags.

Overpass was meant to be a punish pick by FUT, but it was B8 who came out swinging as they cruised to an 8-4 lead on the CT side after keeping their opponents from stringing rounds together.

FUT showed some fight after switching to the CT side and eventually took the lead, but then s1zzi dragged his team over the line with some great plays in the dying stages, including a vital 1v2 clutch.
