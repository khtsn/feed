Rex Regum Qeon and Emman part ways

Rex Regum Qeon Rex Regum Qeon Asia-Pacific Rank #23 2ge James Goopio EJAY Eroll Jule Delfin xffero David Monangin Lmemore Hagai Kristen Yesyurun Estrella Park Gun (박건) fl1pzjder Saibani Rahmad have parted ways with Emmanuel "Emman" Morales, the organization announced on X. The player is seeking offers in any region and role but sentinel.

Emman joined RRQ midway through 2022. He was a mainstay of the starting roster in the second half of the year, having joined in July. Through RRQ's run at LOCK//IN, VCT Pacific, and the Pacific LCQ, Emman pivoted to a sixth man position.

Despite playing at LOCK//IN, he was absent for the first three weeks of play in the VCT Pacific, during which RRQ only played with five members. In the remaining five weeks, Emman mostly appeared for one map per match playing Skye or KAY/O. He was also present for RRQ's first two matches in the Pacific LCQ, where he exclusively played Raze, but was not drafted for the team's final match in the LCQ.

Rex Regum Qeon had a muddy 2023, which they kicked off with a first-round exit at LOCK//IN. Despite accruing four wins at VCT Pacific in the following months, RRQ placed seventh — under Gen.G Gen.G Korea Rank #4 Meteor Kim Tae-O (김태오) — at just missed out on the playoffs.
