d1Ledez returns to BetBoom's starting roster as zorte stays for the season over ArtFr0st

The team is still working to finalize its roster for the Major.

BetBoom have moved Artem "⁠ArtFr0st⁠" Kharitonov to the bench amidst continuing visa issues and announced the return of Aleksandr "⁠zorte⁠" Zagodyrenko for the remainder of the season.

"This decision was due to the complicated visa situation we found ourselves in," the organization stated in a post on Telegram. "Artyom [ArtFr0st] needed to apply for a US visa from Europe, but without a Schengen visa, he automatically lost the opportunity to participate in the Atlanta tournament."

BetBoom added that Daniil "⁠d1Ledez⁠" Kustov has also returned to the lineup while it engages in a "productive dialogue" with Virtus.pro to potentially extend Timur "⁠FL4MUS⁠" Marev's loan, which expired after the team's runner-up finish at Stake Ranked Episode 1.

That was the third LAN in a row the Russian team played with zorte and FL4MUS as stand-ins, with a trophy finish at Roman Imperium Cup VII helping the team rise up the rankings and secure an invite to the IEM Cologne Major.
