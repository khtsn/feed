Team Liquid Team Liquid Europe Rank #10 paTiTek Patryk Fabrowski nAts Ayaz Akhmetshin trexx Nikita Cherednichenko Keiko Georgio Sanassy kamo Kamil Frąckowiak has parted ways with Maikls "Serial" Zdanovs, only five days ahead of Stage 2.

Serial first joined the team following Masters: Bangkok. He helped the team reach the top three in his rookie VCT stage and qualify to Masters: Toronto. Due to visa issues with their captain, nAts , Liquid announced penny as an emergency substitute, rotating him with Serial throughout their matches at the international event. The team failed to make it past the Swiss stage, losing their final match to eventual champions, Paper Rex Paper Rex Asia-Pacific Rank #1 PatMen Patrick Mendoza Jinggg Wang Jing Jie f0rsakeN Jason Susanto d4v41 Khalish Rusyaidee something Ilya Petrov .

Team Liquid has yet to announce a replacement for the Latvian player, with Toronto substitute penny having joined Apeks as their fifth.
