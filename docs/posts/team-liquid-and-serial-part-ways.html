Team Liquid and Serial part ways

Serial first joined the team following Masters: Bangkok. He helped the team reach the top three in his rookie VCT stage and qualify to Masters: Toronto. Due to visa issues with their captain, nAts , Liquid announced penny as an emergency substitute, rotating him with Serial throughout their matches at the international event.
