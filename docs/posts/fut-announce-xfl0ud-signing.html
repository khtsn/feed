FUT announce xfl0ud signing

FUT have announced the signing of Yasin "⁠xfl0ud⁠" Koç, bringing the Turkish rifler onboard to complete their roster after the benching of Laurențiu "⁠lauNX⁠" Țârlea earlier in the day.

Official: FUT bench lauNX

"I'm thrilled to join the FUT family," xfl0ud said of joining the Turkish organization in a post on X. "Up to this day, I've done my absolute best to represent our country in the best possible way on the global stage. Now, the prospect of fighting together with one of our country's teams on this path fills me with immense excitement."

xfl0ud arrives at his new home having spent the past 18 months with HEROIC, whom he joined in January 2025. The aggressive rifler was a solid performer for the team during his tenure, averaging a 1.08 rating whilst helping the squad to Major appearances at BLAST.tv Austin Major and IEM Cologne Major.

HEROIC were consistently in and around the top 20 during xfl0ud's time with the team, even managing to peak just inside the top 10, and won a couple of LAN events, namely DraculaN Season 5 and MESA Nomadic Masters Spring 2025.

Things took a turn for the worse at the end of xfl0ud's tenure, however, with HEROIC exiting PGL Astana, Stank Ranked Episode 2 and IEM Cologne Major early doors, with only two wins in 10 matches across both events.
