paTiTek to explore options outside of Team Liquid

2 days ago

Team Liquid Team Liquid Europe Rank #7 nAts Ayaz Akhmetshin trexx Nikita Cherednichenko keiko Georgio Sanassy kamo Kamil Frąckowiak has allowed Patryk "paTiTek" Fabrowski to search for another team following his one-year stint with the organization. He is the first and only member of Liquid's 2025 roster that has been allowed to explore his options thus far.

Team Liquid was coming into the 2025 season off a disappointing 2024, where they missed every international event. The team's 2025 rebuild, which included the addition of paTiTek, went under the radar compared to the more major roster shuffles across EMEA. However, Liquid defied expectations and proved themself as a top EMEA team, qualifying to every international event, and even coming out on top as the champions of EMEA in Stage 2.

On the international stage, however, Liquid hit a roadblock, as they were never able to make it past the group stage. They were eliminated from each event with a 1-2 record, even at Champions, where they were one of the favorites to make a deep run.

This is now paTiTek's second consecutive season as an international mainstay, having also had a successful 2024 as a stand-in for Team Heretics Team Heretics Europe Rank #2 Wo0t Mert Alkan RieNs Enes Ecirli benjyfishy Benjamin David Fish Boo Ričardas Lukaševičius . He reached his first international event at Masters Madrid, and was also part of Heretics' Masters Shanghai run to the grand final, where they came up just short against Gen.G Gen.G Korea Rank #2 Foxy9 Jung Jae-sung (정재성) Ash Ha Hyun-cheol (하현철) t3xture Kim Na-ra (김나라) Munchkin Byeon Sang-beom (변상범) Lakia Kim Jong-min (김종민) Karon Kim Won-tae (김원태) .
