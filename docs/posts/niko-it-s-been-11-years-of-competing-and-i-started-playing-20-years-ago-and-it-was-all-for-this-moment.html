NiKo: "It's been 11 years of competing and I started playing 20 years ago, and it was all for this moment"

"I said it many times that I wanna leave my name in the game, I wanna create legacy, and it wasn't possible without a Major. I hope this is just the beginning," NiKo told HLTV after winning his first Major title.

Today was the day for NiKo

It took 17 Majors, but Nikola "⁠NiKo⁠" Kovač finally got his Major title as Falcons beat FURIA in a convincing 3-0 grand final affair, a cherry on top of one of the hardest Major runs to date after the team also beat the world's top-three teams beforehand.

And it was in no small part thanks to the Bosnian himself, who got over his grand final jitters just when it mattered most with a scoreboard-topping 1.43 rating after standout performances on all three maps.

HLTV briefly spoke to the Bosnian shortly after he and the rest of the team lifted the Major trophy about ending the "haunting" streak and what was different about this grand final.

For some reason, I was very confident for this Major from the beginning to the end

Nikola "⁠NiKo⁠" Kovač

"A lot of these losses were lessons, huge lessons, and I took away a lot of things, especially from the last two finals that I played," NiKo began.

"For some reason, I was very confident for this Major from the beginning to the end. I could just feel the team all the time and I could feel the trust that they had in me and I had in them. Everything was [flowing]."

+13

"I just decided not to care about losing. I constantly focused on winning, on the solutions, on how I can be there for the team no matter if I play good or bad. Just happy I could get over this edge because it has been haunting me for a long time. I'm just having huge relief right now."

But does it feel like life completed? NiKo says not quite.

"I dedicated almost my whole life to this. It's been 11 years of competing and I started playing 20 years ago, and it was all for this moment," he said.

"It definitely feels like a huge milestone achieved, but I truly hope this is just the beginning. I said it many times that I wanna leave my name in the game, I wanna create legacy, and it wasn't possible without a Major. I hope this is just the beginning."
