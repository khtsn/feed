All Gamers sends Gentle Mates home after three maps

All Gamers, the top China seeds, has defeated Gentle Mates with a 2-1 victory in the Masters Santiago lower bracket. The last hope for their region, All Gamers not only won China's first series of the event, they also took China's only map wins in the tournament so far, after compatriots EDward Gaming and Xi Lai Gaming went home without a single map win.

Gentle Mates opened with a dominant attack on Pearl, setting them up to take the first map 13-5. All Gamers responded in kind the following map, a strong first half and five-round streak on attack securing a 13-4 win to send the match to the decider. Gentle Mates looked to end the day early, but All Gamers refused, taking 11 wins in a row to send their opponent back to Europe with a 13-8 on the third map.

All Gamers awaits their next opponent in the lower bracket, looking to go on a run on their international debut.
