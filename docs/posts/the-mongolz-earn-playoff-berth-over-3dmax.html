The MongolZ earn playoff berth over 3DMAX

3DMAX looked the better team on Inferno, but lost to multiple bad buys

The MongolZ have bagged a spot in the ESL Pro League playoffs thanks to a 2-1 victory over 3DMAX, winning Dust2 (13-10) and losing Ancient (4-13) before taking advantage of the French side's profligacy on Inferno (13-10).

3DMAX will be left regretting their mistakes on the decider as the series was in their hands. The Frenchmen recovered from a loss on Dust2 with a convincing win on Ancient, and looked the better side for much of Inferno. Instead of capitalizing, however, they threw away several key rounds against low buys to slip to the 2-2 bracket.

Dust2 was a hard-fought brawl, both sides bringing aggressive approaches on either side of the map. Ultimately The MongolZ's better success at finding opening kills told, helping them get over the line on their T side.

3DMAX fumble 2v2 against armorless USPs

3DMAX levelled the series with a convincing performance on Ancient, Lucas "⁠Lucky⁠" Chastang's efforts ensuring a dominant CT half before Pierre "⁠Ex3rcice⁠" Bulinge took over to seal the deal on offense.

On the decider of Inferno The MongolZ bagged the lead in the first half due in large part to powerful Deagle work in a couple of low-buy rounds, and then benefitted from 3DMAX fumbling a 2v2 against armorless USPs in the second half, securing a playoff berth for the Mongolians.
