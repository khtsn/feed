StarSeries Fall closed qualifier invites announced

StarLadder have announced the direct invites for the upcoming StarSeries Fall 2026 closed qualifiers.

The qualification events will be held in three regions, namely Europe, South America, and North America, with the latter two featuring eight teams, while 16 squads will contest the European tournament.

The highest-ranked out of those 16 is the new-look Aurora squad, who will also be the only top-20 team attending the qualifier. Joining them are the likes of Astralis, Ninjas in Pyjamas, and 3DMAX, while 100 Thieves have also sneaked into the invite list thanks to a plethora of declines.
