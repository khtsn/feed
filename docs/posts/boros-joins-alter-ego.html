BOROS joins Alter Ego

10-3-2026 12:04

The Jordanian has landed on a team soon after departing JiJieHao.

Mohammad "⁠BOROS⁠" Malhas has announced he has linked up with the Alter Ego squad after a month on the sidelines.

"We have a big chance to qualify for the next 2 Majors, we are aiming to go to the Singapore one. Thanks for inviting me to join the team," the Jordanian said on X.

BOROS left JiJieHao for the second time in February and linked up with the experienced squad shortly after.

Alter Ego are currently playing the CS Asia Championship closed qualifier, where they have qualified for the upper-bracket semi-final after beating Lynn Vision and Rare Atom.

Their next test will come against Chinggis Warriors on Wednesday.
