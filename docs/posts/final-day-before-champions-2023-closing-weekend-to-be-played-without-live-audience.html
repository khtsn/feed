Final day before Champions 2023 closing weekend to be played without live audience

Riot has announced that Champions 2023's matches for Aug. 20 will be played without a live audience due to severe weather in the Los Angeles area.

Hurricane Hilary's landfall is expected to cause "dangerous to catastrophic flooding" across southern California over the next couple of days, according to the U.S. National Weather Service.

Currently, Riot anticipates the two lower quarterfinal matches planned for Sunday will be broadcast as scheduled.
