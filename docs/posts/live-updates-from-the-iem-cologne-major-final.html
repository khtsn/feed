Regardless of who wins the grand final today, we will have a new oldest Major champion, with Gabriel "⁠FalleN⁠" Toledo vying for it on one side and Finn "⁠karrigan⁠" Andersen on the other.

karrigan had held the title at one point, when he won PGL Antwerp at 32 years and 38 days of age. At that time, he dethroned Danylo "⁠Zeus⁠" Teslenko, who lifted the PGL Major Kraków 2017 trophy at 29 years and 289 days old.

The current holder is Dan "⁠apEX⁠" Madesclaire, who became the oldest Major winner at BLAST.tv Austin at 32 years and 121 days old and then beat his own record at StarLadder Budapest at 32 years and 293 days.

If karrigan were to break the record now, he would do it at 36 years and 68 days old, while FalleN would do it at 35 years and 22 days.
