Just months removed from seemingly being relegated back to the Challengers level, Gentle Mates have come back and taken EMEA by storm. The team underwent a large-scale rebuild, retaining just Minny from 2025, but has looked better than ever. In a 2-0 sweep over NAVI, the Mates never looked uncomfortable.

The series started on M8's pick of Abyss, where the Mates' double-Duelist comp was tested against NAVI's util-heavy double-Initiator comp. From the get-go, Gentle Mates had all of the momentum. A 4-0 run to start the map was promptly responded to with a 3-0 NAVI run, and by halftime, the Mates had a 7-5 lead.

All alone on site, marteen makes it look easy.

The former Ascension squad looked to make the map a quick one by winning the pistol of its defense half, but an anti-eco from NAVI threatened to flip the script. After giving up the thrifty and losing its own anti-eco, Gentle Mates responded in a big way, winning five straight to close out the map 13-7.

Duelist players marteen and bipo formed the perfect one-two punch on the map, finishing with 20 and 18 kills, respectively, as part of the 'mosquito comp' duo that is Yoru and Waylay. Despite the best efforts of hiro , who was on Tejo, NAVI could not claw back on Abyss.

Moving onto defense, BBL claimed its second pistol round of the half and third of the series to en route to hitting double-digit rounds, but Vitality did not go down without a fight.
