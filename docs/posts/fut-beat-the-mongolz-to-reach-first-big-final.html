FUT beat The MongolZ to reach first big final

FUT are through to the grand final of PGL Bucharest 2026 following a 2-0 victory over The MongolZ, stealing their opponents' map pick thanks to a great offensive showing before winning Mirage in a high-octane game that went to overtime.

It's the first grand final of a notable event for the young FUT roster, which achieves yet another milestone after a fantastic start to the year. The win pushes them to sixth on the global VRS ahead of Saturday's final against Astralis, whom they faced three times in ESL Pro League 23 last month.

"It feels amazing, especially after all the games we've been through, after all the close calls we've had, especially last time in Pro League," Laurențiu "⁠lauNX⁠" Țârlea said after the match.

"It's like a dream, especially because it happened to be in Romania. It makes it even better for me. I have even more hunger."

Looking ahead to Saturday's grand final, the Romanian said: "I think it will be a blast. Every time we faced them, it was not a fast game. It was always back and forth. We threw some rounds, they threw some rounds. They threw a map, we threw a map.

"I'm looking forward to it, especially because I'm playing my ex-teammate Staehr. It's always nice to see him play. I feel great playing against him and having good vibes before and after the game. I think it will be a very good game."
