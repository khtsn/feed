Image by Lance Skundrich/Riot Games

All three players were core members of the roster that formed at the start of the partnership program heading into the 2023 season. Despite sporadic wins, that season was mostly characterized by loss and dysfunction, headlined by the benching of their star duelist to play the remainder of their games with an assistant coach. They finished second to last in the Americas League, and dead last in the Americas LCQ.

Despite the poor results, they kept the core roster intact, bringing in Matheus "mazin" Araújo as the IGL and Arthur "artzin" Araujo as the new duelist.
