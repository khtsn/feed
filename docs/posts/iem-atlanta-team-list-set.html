IEM Atlanta team list set

The IEM Atlanta team list is complete after Passion UA secured the final berth with a win in the North America closed qualifier.

The Johnny "⁠JT⁠" Theodosiou-led squad beat mouse and SportsBetExpert before back-to-back victories over Wildcard in the upper bracket and grand final secured their spot at the event in Atlanta.

Before that, two Russian squads secured passage through the global qualifier, BetBoom and BET-M, beating SINNERS and fnatic, respectively, in the semi-finals. SINNERS then won the lower bracket final, but were taken out in the consolidation final by the qualifier's breakout team, BET-M.

Running alongside PGL Astana, IEM Atlanta will be one of the weaker IEMs on the calendar, but will still count with teams like Vitality, FaZe, and Natus Vincere on the team list.
