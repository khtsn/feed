Team Heretics reach top four with 2-1 over DRX

Team Heretics have eliminated DRX 2-1 in the Champions 2024 lower bracket, advancing to the top four in the event. They will next play against Sentinels, who took the win against FNATIC earlier in the day.

The series began on Abyss, DRX's map pick. They were able to take the map comfortably, a flawless defense winning the opener 13-5. Heretics answered in kind, switching their composition ahead of time to take Sunset 13-5. They then won the decider, Icebox, with a 13-10 score.

Now with their complete lineup, Team Heretics have made the top four at their first Champions appearance. DRX, meanwhile, are eliminated from Champions Seoul, ending their home event in the top six.
