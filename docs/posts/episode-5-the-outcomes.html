Episode 5: The Outcomes

https://www.nytimes.com/2023/08/03/podcasts/serial-the-retrievals-yale-fertility-clinic.html

From Serial Productions. The patients wrestle with the lasting effects of their experiences at the clinic.

Aug. 3, 2023, 6:00 a.m. ET

Listen and follow ‘The Retrievals’

Outcomes of fertility treatment are typically measured by whether or not the patient has a baby. The outcomes in this case are a lot more complicated.

Women at the clinic lost trust in the medical system. Now many of them have to reckon with that unease as they progress through pregnancies and childbirth.

They are also trying to hold Yale accountable for its failures, but how do you bring a lawsuit for ignoring pain?

We also find out what happens to the nurse when she seeks a return to normalcy.
