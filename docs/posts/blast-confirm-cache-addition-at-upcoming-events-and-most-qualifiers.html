BLAST confirm Cache addition at upcoming events and most qualifiers

BLAST Open Porto qualifiers will be played with Cache in the pool with the exception of the North American open qualifier.

BLAST has announced that Cache will be added to the pool in place of Overpass for its upcoming Bounty, Open Porto, and Rivals Hong Kong events, as expected after Valve announced the map's return to the Active Duty pool on June 22.

Most open qualifiers for BLAST Open Porto will already feature Cache. Only the open qualifier in North America will continue with the old map pool as it kicks off on June 27, while the open qualifiers in Asia, Europe, and South America will get underway in July with the new pool.
