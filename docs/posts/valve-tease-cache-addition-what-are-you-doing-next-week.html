Valve tease Cache addition: "What are you doing next week?"

Valve has teased the release of Cache by posting a short X video of the map's best highlights throughout the years, titled "What are you doing next week?"

The news comes almost a year after the map's creator, Shawn "FMPONE" Snelling, confirmed Valve "reached out to buy it on day one of release."

FMPONE on Cache: "[Valve] reached out to buy it on day one of release"

Recently, FACEIT introduced the map to their matchmaking, a decision that gave the platform an unprecedented number of players and even crashed the queues, while Valve have already teased the map's introduction by changing their Twitter header to a screenshot of the map earlier this week.

Originally created for CS:Source by Salvatore "⁠Volcano⁠" Garozzo, Cache was added to the Active Duty pool in 2014 and remained there until 2019, when it was removed in favor of Vertigo. It now seems set for a return to competitive play after seven years.
