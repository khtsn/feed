The LAN in Barcelona, Spain is crucial in the fight for Stage 1 invites to the IEM Cologne Major, both for several of its attendees and for other teams on the cusp of securing berths on our predicted invites page.

BetBoom are playing their last of three LANs in a row with two stand-ins, having bolstered their Major hopes with a trophy lift at Roman Imperium Cup VII. A win over HEROIC at Stake, their fifth in a row, has helped increase that gap further, but they are by no means safe with only 20 points separating the final five and teams below them catching up.

Among those on the climb is Ninjas in Pyjamas, who emerged victorious in a pivotal win over Gentle Mates to end the day. Two points separated the teams before the series and both still remain outside of the invite cut-off range, but Marco "⁠Snappi⁠" Pfeiffer's side has closed the gap to SINNERS from 57 points to 26 after the win.

Gentle Mates will now have to rely on a lower bracket run to salvage their Major campaign, and Ninjas in Pyjamas are still in hot water with continued wins needed to climb into contention. GamerLegion and G2 are the only teams that aren't in danger of losing their invites, although the former remains in a battle against DraculaN Season 6 finalists Monte for a Stage 2 invite.
