Sentinels add curry

2 days ago

Sentinels Sentinels North America Rank #3 TenZ Tyson Ngo Zellsis Jordan Montemurro zekken Zachary Patrone johnqt Mohamed Amine Ouarid Sacy Gustavo Rossi have signed curry to their roster for the Valorant Champions Tour 2024 season. This pickup looks to replace the sixth man role on the team that pANcada fills, as the Brazilian looks to be on his way out to another VCT team.

Previously, curry has competed in the Valorant scene, but is primarily known as a content creator for Cloud9 Cloud9 North America Rank #9 Xeppaa Erick Bach jakee Jake Anderson vanity Anthony Malaspina OXY Francis Hoang . He signed with them as a player back in early 2022, competing in the VCT for four months. Afterward, he was moved to the inactive roster, transitioning from a player to a content creator. He remained with the org for just over two years, streaming and reacting to VCT games alongside ranked play.

In the 2023-24 offseason, curry filled in on the C9 roster, showing his capabilities against other VCT competition at the Red Bull Home Ground. The curry version of C9 almost beat FNATIC FNATIC Europe Rank #1 Leo Leo Jannesson Derke Nikita Sirmitev Boaster Jake Howlett Chronicle Timofey Khromov Alfajer Emir Ali Beder in the finals, losing to them in a close 3-2 series.

With Masters Madrid approaching, curry confirmed that he will not be traveling with the team, but will be the substitute if needed.
