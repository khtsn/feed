BLAST Bounty 2026 Season 2 teams revealed

BLAST has announced the 32 teams invited to BLAST Bounty 2026 Season 2, which will kick off with the online portion on July 21-26 before continuing on LAN in Malta with the top eight teams from July 30-August 2.

The tournament will be a single-elimination best-of-three bracket, with some notable teams missing, including Natus Vincere, Legacy, BetBoom, and B8.

Four wildcard invites were given out at the 32-team tournament, and it was Liquid, 100 Thieves, EYEBALLERS, and OG who received them.
