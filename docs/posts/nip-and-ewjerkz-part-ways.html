NIP and ewjerkz part ways

Ninjas in Pyjamas have announced that they have parted ways with Michel "⁠ewjerkz⁠" Pinto, who had been on the team's bench since December 2025, by mutual accord.

ewjerkz rose to prominence during a golden period with SAW in 2024, which included his Major debut at PGL Copenhagen and a semi-final run at IEM Cologne, where he posted a 1.11 rating.

Not long after, at the start of 2025, he made the jump to Ninjas in Pyjamas, where he played another IEM Cologne among many other big and small events, as well as participating in his second Major, StarLadder Budapest.

Despite holding down a respectable 1.08 rating during his time with the Ninjas, the team was not getting results, and ewjerkz was moved to the bench at the end of the year.

Now, after half a year on Ninjas in Pyjamas's bench, the 25-year-old is free from his contractual obligations with the organization as he enters free agency.
