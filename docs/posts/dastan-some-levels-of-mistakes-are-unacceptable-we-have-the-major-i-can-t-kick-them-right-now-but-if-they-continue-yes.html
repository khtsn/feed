PARIVISION struggled through the group stage of PGL Bucharest despite going up 2-0 in the Swiss pool, needing all three tries and a labored victory over Wildcard to get the job done.

It is the latest illustration in a picture of inconsistency the team has displayed since the start of the year, with a trophy win at BLAST Bounty and runners-up finish at PGL Cluj-Napoca being superb highlights surrounding early exits at IEM Krakow and ESL Pro League Season 23.

The team has enjoyed a swift rise up the rankings and the results have been a redemption arc for Dzhami "⁠Jame⁠" Ali, but bringing a trio of young, inexperienced players into tier-one has its own set of issues. Repeated mistakes and errors in their event runs has started taking its toll on Dastan "⁠dastan⁠" Akbayev, who admitted in a post-match interview on broadcast that he was upset with how his team played in their qualifying match against Wildcard despite him doing significant preparation.

"A lot of rounds we're losing like noobs," he said. "I have questions to nota, to xiELO, to BELCHONOKK. To Jame and zweih, less questions. I know you're also making some podcast, they're making some mistakes, and some pauses I take, 'What the fuck are you doing? What's wrong with you?'"

Limited practice time between events has also played a factor, with the team remaining inside the Schengen zone after reaching the playoffs at BLAST Open Rotterdam, so a month-long break after Bucharest before the team heads to PGL Astana will be much-needed.

But that time has to be used well, as the Kazakhstani coach is growing impatient with the same errors surfacing, and his players may be in danger after the IEM Cologne Major if their general level doesn't improve.

"Today we managed to win and it's nice that we qualified, but I have questions for my players," dastan said. "I want to see how they will perform next month, because some levels of mistakes are unacceptable. We have the Major, I can't kick them right now, but if they continue, yes, [they might be kicked].

"I can tell it openly because it's not okay. Some situations is too much, too much of the same mistakes, and I'm tired of saying again and again the same mistakes. I hope they will learn and be better players, and in tier-one elite games, we need really strong guys who play comfortably, like makazze and b1t in NAVI, ropz and ZywOo, donk, and my guys... at least two of them should play like 1.1, 1.2 ratings, please. We're all 1.0 rating. Here is zweih, and Jame. Jame is good, always plays comfortably."
