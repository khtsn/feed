Legacy destroy BetBoom to claim third in Atlanta

latto put up a 2.11 rating across the series.

Legacy claimed third place at IEM Atlanta after breezing past BetBoom in a two-map series (Mirage 13-4, Nuke 13-3).

The series was a very straightforward victory for the Brazilians. Bruno "⁠latto⁠" Rebelatto first spearheaded their dominant Mirage victory with a 1.96 overall rating before topping that on Nuke, where he starred with a 2.21 rating.

"We came in hungry today after yesterday," Andrei "⁠arT⁠" Piovezan said about the victory. "The last tournaments weren't as good, so this result will give us a lot of confidence and a lot of maps to watch."

For BetBoom, this is a disappointing end to an otherwise very encouraging campaign that included an upset win over Vitality.
