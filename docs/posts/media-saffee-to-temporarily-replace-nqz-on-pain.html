Media: saffee to temporarily replace nqz on paiN

paiN have announced that Lucas "⁠nqz⁠" Soares is set to step away from competition due to personal reasons, with a Dust2.com.br report stating that the Brazilian AWPer will be replaced by Rafael "⁠saffee⁠" Costa for the foreseeable future.

"nqz is temporarily stepping away from his competitive activities for personal reasons," paiN's X statement reads.

"The decision was made by the player with the organization's support, prioritizing his well-being and full recovery, with the necessary assistance throughout this period."

nqz, who has been part of paiN for two and a half years, also spoke in the announcement video, stating that he has been suffering from stress and that he is looking to return to the server as soon as possible.

paiN gave no indication on when nqz is set to return to activity, but Dust2.com.br's report states that saffee will play for the team at IEM Atlanta and CS Asia Championship at a minimum.
