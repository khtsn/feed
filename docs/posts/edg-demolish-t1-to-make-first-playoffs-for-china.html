EDG demolish T1 to make first playoffs for China

EDward Gaming EDward Gaming China Rank #1 ZmjjKK Zheng Yongkang (郑永康) nobody Wang Senxu (王森旭) Muggle Tang Shijun (唐时俊) CHICHOO Wan Shunji (万顺治) Smoggy Zhang Zhao (张钊) Haodong Guo Haodong (郭浩东) demolish T1 T1 Korea Rank #3 iNTRO Kang Seung-gyun (강승균) xeta Son Seon-ho (손선호) Sayaplayer Ha Jeong-woo (하정우) ban Joseph Seungmin Oh Munchkin Byeon Sang-beom (변상범) Carpe Lee Jae-hyeok (이재혁) 2-0 on their way to become the first Chinese team to make the playoffs at an international event, let alone take a single win. T1 have been eliminated from Masters: Tokyo and now look forward to Champions 2023 in August.

T1 had previously taken the win over EDG on the first map of Fracture, however, things would turn out differently this time around. The Chinese squad looked to be firing on all cylinders coming into the series and the whole team succeeded as individuals. An aspect that was commonly missing from EDG's previous matchups against T1 and NAVI.

After taking an 8-4 first half it was a quick path to the finish line for EDG as they refused to drop a single defensive round with a Smoggy 4K in the second pistol. EDG took the map 13-4 and had all eyes set on the playoffs.

Coming into their map pick of Haven, a map they had previously found success on against T1, EDG continued their impressive form. T1's defenses crumbled as ZmjjKK continued his star level performance. Dropping 18 kills in just the first half, EDG played a near perfect half, only dropping two rounds.

T1 gave themselves a chance in the map with a strong start after swapping sides, taking both the pistol and bonus rounds to reach four in a row. Unfortunately for the Koreans this streak ended after a failed push into A Link led to EDG's first defensive round. The kings of China quickly re-established themselves by taking map and series point with a 12-6 lead. A lead that proved too much for T1 as EDG cruised to a playoffs appearance and 2-0 series victory.

With the win EDG have qualified for Masters: Tokyo playoffs while T1 have been eliminated.
