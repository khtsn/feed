NRG bench br0

NRG has announced that Alexander "⁠br0⁠" Bro, who had been on the team for nearly a year and a half since joining in early 2025, has been moved to the bench.

The Dane's removal from the starting five comes after a poor showing from the team at the IEM Cologne Major's Stage 1, where NRG were eliminated in 9-11th place.

br0 averaged a 1.13 rating during his tenure in NRG — the highest on the team —, and played at three Majors, including the aforementioned IEM Cologne as well as BLAST.tv Austin and StarLadder Budapest, and a scattering of big and small events without notable results on the big stages.
