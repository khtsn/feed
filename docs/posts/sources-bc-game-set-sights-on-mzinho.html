Sources: BC.Game set sights on mzinho

If mzinho moves to BC.Game, he will reunite with Senzu

The MongolZ star Ayush "⁠mzinho⁠" Batbold is high on BC.Game's list of targets for the final roster spot, multiple sources told HLTV.

BC.Game remain in the market for a player to finalize their roster after already landing four-time Major winner Emil "⁠Magisk⁠" Reif as the IGL and signing Azbayar "⁠Senzu⁠" Munkhbold on a permanent deal from The MongolZ following a loan spell.

If completed, the move would reunite two of Mongolia's brightest talents. In October 2025, when The MongolZ moved Senzu to the bench in a shocking move, mzinho spoke of his teammate as his "lifetime friend."

"He’s been with me since day one," mzinho wrote at the time. "We were talking about [having] our own stickers in the game at the age of 12, 13. I'm so happy we made it to the Major final."

The MongolZ and BC.Game did not immediately respond to a request for comment.

mzinho's signing would be a significant step toward BC.Game CEO Ali Muhanned's wish of seeing his team break into the top-5 in the world by the end of the year. The 19-year-old has established himself as a key player for The MongolZ, who made several deep runs last year, most notably finishing second at the BLAST.tv Austin Major and winning the Esports World Cup.
