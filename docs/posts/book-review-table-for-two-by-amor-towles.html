Book Review: ‘Table for Two,’ by Amor Towles

As confirmed by SWERL, he is expected to replace PangH on the starting lineup. Their previous import player, Vsevolod "neveR" Suslov, has been removed from the Global Contract Database, but neither he nor the org have made an announcement regarding his status. The move follows Nova's exit in the Kickoff group stage, which they ended with a 1-2 record after a surprise debut against FunPlus Phoenix.

SWERL spent most of his career in the Oceania scene, playing for notable regional orgs such as Dire Wolves, Mindfreak, and Wildcard. He was able to reach multiple semifinals throughout the years, and win a handful of local events, but Oceania's lack of integration into the greater VCT circuit hindered the development of the region.

By 2023, many of the top Oceania players began moving onto international teams. WRONSKI's move to Global Esports kicked off an exodus, and China's increasing investment offered a prime opportunity for aspiring prospects. Among the new organizations was Royal Never Give Up, returning to Valorant after previously fielding a team in 2020-2021.

The org acquired SWERL and signed as part of their new roster. They were able to qualify to events such as the FGC Invitational and the Champions 2023 China Qualifier, but did not advance past the early stage of those events. Ahead of the China Evolution Series, RNG picked up GuanG , who is currently on Nova.

The team maintained a similar level, advancing to the Act 2 and Act 3 main events, but failing to make it past the group stage. After not being invited to VCT China, and despite having enough points to participate in Ascension, RNG exited the scene in November.

Nova Esports look to reinforce their roster after an early exit from Kickoff. They defied low community expectations with a competitive opening map against FunPlus Phoenix and a 2-0 victory against TYLOO, but were unable to make it past the group stage. The team seeks to continue improving with their focus on SWERL as their new duelist and his past synergy with GuanG. He has struggled with wrist pain in the past, but Nova have made an effort to assist him as they further integrate him into the roster.
