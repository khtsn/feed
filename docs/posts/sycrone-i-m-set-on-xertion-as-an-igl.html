sycrone: "I'm set on xertioN as an IGL"

sycrone and xertioN have developed a "good relationship," according to the coach

HLTV spoke to Dennis "⁠sycrone⁠" Nielsen in the aftermath of MOUZ's 1-3 elimination from the IEM Cologne Major.

The Danish coach reflected on the result, admitting it fell short of their goal as a result but that it still served its purpose.

"Of course not what we wanted," sycrone said. "We're still here to make playoffs. A bit disappointed on that end, but we also got what we wanted. We wanted to test out a couple of players, and we put ourselves in a position where the Major was going to be difficult."

sycrone gave an honest estimate of Adrian "⁠xelex⁠" Vincze in his first few months at tier-one. "There's still some ways to go for him, but he's so young and I think he has a bright future ahead of him," he said about the MOUZ NXT prospect.

We'll look at the market and see what we think makes sense for our team

sycrone however remained vague when asked about xelex remaining on the roster. MOUZ are leaving their options very much open, having even said that Ludvig "⁠Brollan⁠" Brolin may still have a future with the team despite being moved off of the lineup while Justinas "⁠jL⁠" Lekavicius was being trialed.

"We will go home and evaluate what we have and what we saw at the end of this season. Like we do most post-seasons, we'll look at the market and see what we think makes sense for our team," sycrone explained.

Commiserations. Tell me about your first impressions of the result overall and the loss to FUT.

Let's separate the question because I think on a playing side, playing the teams that we played, I'm quite satisfied with the output xertioN had in terms of leading and IGLing. We pushed a couple of good teams. It's clear we didn't have the map pool to go against all of the teams. We didn't have the foundation to win three maps against either FUT today or, before that, FURIA and Vitality.

In terms of the result, of course not what we wanted. We're still here to make playoffs. It was a goal of ours to do that. A bit disappointed on that end, but we also got what we wanted. We wanted to test out a couple of players and we put ourselves in a position where the Major was going to be difficult.

You speak of testing a couple of your players. Can you tell me about your impressions of xelex throughout this tournament and the time he's shown so far on MOUZ?

I think he's obviously a very good talent. Even on NXT you saw him fragging hard. One of the things he has naturally that maybe other players don't have is that he's quite composed under pressure. He has a go-karting background and maybe he's used to pushing it on the edge, the adrenaline, so he manages that fairly well, I think.

He's still very young. He has a way to go, and I also think the current iteration of NXT, he was the one guy who could have a chance, and the main team was in a position where maybe it was fair to test out things and give him a shot even if I think the jump was the biggest we've had so far from academy to main team. There's still some ways to go for him, but he's so young and I think he has a bright future ahead of him.

xelex's future on the team is up in the air

You had to put him in some positions he wasn't familiar with in the past. Do you feel like he has progressed to your satisfaction in some of the anchor roles, like on B site on Dust2 where he had a couple of big moments that he was missing in the past?

Yeah, he handled that pretty well. Some of it was positions he played before, mostly on T sides. I think at this level there's way more anti-strat on the map control positions and people are way more bullying you, annoying you, and there are more steps to the game. He has to learn a bit of that. I think xertioN was a really good mentor in that sense, he used to play that role.

On CT side he perhaps has a harder time. It's not elegant for us right now. He's playing positions he didn't play before and it will take him some time, and it was also positions that are quite heavily anti-stratted. If you just have a week of practice for a position, or just a couple of tournaments, it's tough to be super confident in it yet.

Heading into the off-season, there's a lot of uncertainty around MOUZ at the moment with the whole Brollan situation coming back in. You've both spoken about it a bit, that there's still some possibility of him remaining with the team. Can you give us an update in general about the roster and whether xelex will continue with the team past this point?

What I will say is that we will go home and evaluate what we have and what we saw at the end of this season. Like we do most post-seasons, we'll look at the market and see what we think makes sense for our team.

I think even if we were to continue with the five we saw at this tournament, just having a fresh start and knowing that this will be the five, working on the foundation, we will have a better look at the start of the next season.

sycrone still believes in Brollan as a role player

For Brollan, I think he's an incredible asset to any team. We're gonna have to see there. Even when he stepped up to be a leader for us, he did that very well, but especially as a role player, I think he's incredible.

He's able to play-make, he's able to anchor, he's very unselfish, and a super-hard worker. I think even if I tell myself that I work hard, maybe xertioN works hard, torzsi as well... it's tough to match Brollan. He has immense drive even if he started his career at 15, he's in his early 20s now and he still has this drive. I would love to see him on a team, if it's here or somewhere else.

Can you say whether you're set on anything at all at this point? xertioN becoming a new in-game leader, is that going to keep going at least?

Yeah. I'm set on xertioN as an IGL. I think he has a very good understanding of what it means to facilitate and communicate. I think in this game you need to be able to instantly micro-manage things. I'm really big on communication, and it's one of his fortes.

We have a good relationship. In some way he likes to say he's an extension of me on the server; I think he's more capable than that. But it will be him for the future.
