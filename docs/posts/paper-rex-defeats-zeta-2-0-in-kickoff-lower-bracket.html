Paper Rex defeats ZETA 2-0 in Kickoff lower bracket

Paper Rex has defeated ZETA Division 2-0 in the second Pacific Kickoff lower bracket match. They will next play against either Global Esports or DetonatioN FocusMe, while ZETA is eliminated from the event.

The match began on ZETA's map pick, Lotus, which PRX won 13-5. Map two, Fracture, went Paper Rex's way, with a 13-8 ending the series 2-0.

Paper Rex remains in Kickoff contention, looking to earn Championship points and qualify to Masters: Bangkok should they reach the final. ZETA will need to wait until Stage 1 to return to the server.
