Game Changers Championship São Paulo viewer's guide

The second international LAN event of the Game Changers circuit is creeping closer by the day. Riot have revealed the bracket for the upcoming Game Changers Championship: São Paulo event nearly a week prior to its commencement.

The event will run from Nov. 28 to Dec. 3 and will see eight of the world's finest Game Changers rosters battle it out for the honor of hoisting the second international trophy the circuit has ever offered. Teams from each region have all earned their place under the golden lights by either accumulating enough circuit points or winning their regions' respective qualifiers.

Mirroring the same format used in last year's championship, Riot have retained the use of a double-elimination bracket utilizing Bo3 matches all the way up to the grand final, which will feature a Bo5 between the two remaining teams. Teams from the same region will be seeded on opposite sides of the bracket to ensure diverse matchups.

Game Changers Championship: São Paulo will have broadcast drops, which means that viewers can earn in-game rewards from watching official streams. If viewers connect their Valorant accounts with their Twitch or YouTube accounts, they may be awarded a title and player card.

The “Louder” title can be earned by watching any match from November 28 to December 2. The “Game Changers Trophy” gun buddy can only be earned by watching the grand final on December 3.

Riot have not yet announced the broadcast talent.

Riot will stream the event on their Twitch and YouTube channels. There has been no information pertaining to watch parties and streams in different languages yet.
