NAVI-G2 headlines Stage 3 final day

The world number two will face a tricky opponent in the 2-2 pool.

Natus Vincere and G2 will face off for a Major playoff spot on the final day of IEM Cologne's third stage.

BetBoom and 9z, meanwhile, are trying to avoid the unceremonious fate of going from the 2-0 pool to a 2-3 finish, but both teams will go into their third chance as the on-paper underdog against FUT and The MongolZ respectively.

All possible outcomes of the 2-2 matchups will lead to Spirit and Vitality starting their playoff runs from the same side of the bracket.
