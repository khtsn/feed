Who’s the Boss?

Former President Donald Trump held a campaign rally in Charlotte, N.C., on Wednesday.

Stephen Colbert said Trump spent most of the evening “focused on the real issue gripping the country: desperately workshopping a new nickname for Kamala Harris.”

“[imitating Trump] K as in Kamala, A as in Amala, M as in Malala, A as in Ah, L as in Lyin’ Kamala — L-Y-I-N-apostrophe — oh God, I’m back at the beginning again.” — STEPHEN COLBERT

Colbert blasted Trump for resorting to his old “Apprentice” tagline, “You’re fired.”

“That’s a 10-year-old reference!” — STEPHEN COLBERT
