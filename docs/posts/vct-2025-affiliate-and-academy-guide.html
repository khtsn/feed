VCT 2025 affiliate and academy guide

2 days ago

Last year, Riot announced the introduction of affiliate partnerships regionally, allowing for Challengers and Game Changers teams to unlock benefits such as Two-Way Players with VCT-partnered teams.

Nearly a year later, a multitude of teams have taken advantage of not just affiliate partnerships, but have also eyed the academy team system as well. Teams that field academy teams may participate in Challengers without fear of relegation in their first year, but cannot compete in VCT Ascension.
