Astralis ease past 3DMAX into EPL 23 Stage 2

Astralis have punched their ticket to ESL Pro League Season 23 Stage 2 with a 3-1 record after beating 3DMAX amply 2-0 on Dust2 and Overpass. This victory comes after the Danish-majority squad beat Monte and HEROIC in their first two matches before their only Stage 1 loss to FUT.

The round four series kicked off on 3DMAX's Dust2 pick, but the Frenchmen were immediately in hot water as they fell 1-7 out of the gate and only managed three scattered rounds on the attack before the switch.

Astralis' Danish core led the way to the 13-6 victory with Victor "⁠Staehr⁠" Staehr posting a 1.67 rating follwed by Rasmus "⁠HooXi⁠" Nielsen's 1.61 and Jakob "⁠jabbi⁠" Nygaard's 1.54.

Astralis were just as dominant moving to Overpass, where starting on the T side was no inconvenience as they got off to a 7-1 start and another 9-3 half. Once again, 3DMAX started the second half with a cosmetic comeback, but Astralis were quick to the finish line with guns in play, shutting any momentum down to win out 13-5.

3DMAX aren't out of the running yet. After an opening loss to paiN was followed up by victories over SemperFi and Gaimin Gladiators, the Frenchmen are now going into the final day of Stage 1 in the do-or-die 2-2 pool of the Swiss group.
