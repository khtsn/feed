Red Bull Home Ground #5 arrives in November

Red Bull Home Ground's fifth annual tournament is set to return in November at the Arena Berlin in Germany. The three-day event will run from November 20 to 23.

Red Bull will continue to have teams compete in-person this year as opposed to online.

Furthermore, the North American qualifiers will take place in the form of a LAN event in Seattle, Washington beginning September 29. Cloud9 and Sentinels Sentinels North America Rank #5 TenZ Tyson Ngo Zellsis Jordan Montemurro zekken Zachary Patrone johnqt Mohamed Amine Ouarid Sacy Gustavo Rossi are among some of the invited teams that will not have to partake in the open qualifiers. The winning team will claim the biggest portion of the $20,000 prize pool as well as an appearance at the World Finals in November.

The Arena Berlin is a 17,000-seat venue located in Berlin, Germany. Last year's venue in Japan could hold up to 11,000 spectators, meaning that this year's crowd could be considerably bigger.

Red Bull will unveil more invited teams for all qualifiers as the tournaments loom closer. Home Ground #5 will serve as a great way for teams to hone their skills and strategies against both regional and international competition.
