Nexus go international with new roster

Former OG player Nexius is now on Nexus

Nexus, known for fostering Romanian teams for the past decade, have taken a turn by signing three international players alongside two Romanians under the orders of coach Cătălin "⁠H4rder⁠" Hardulea.

The changes come after a disappointing year, as the organization sat just outside the top 20 in the VRS at the beginning of 2025 and plummeted to outside the top 100 by March 2026, when the team finished 21st-24th at DraculaN Season 6.

The two local players are Alexandru "⁠s0und⁠" Ștefan, who has averaged a 1.07 rating on the team since the beginning of 2025, and in-game leader Mihai "⁠fNk⁠" Costache, who joined the team at the turn of the year alongside H4rder.

The international contingent is made up of 24-year-old Sébastien "⁠SBT⁠" Sabbadin from France, who carries a 0.93 career rating; former MOUZ NXT, OG, and Nemesis rifler Bram "⁠Nexius⁠" Campana; and 1.08-career rated AWPer Roberts "⁠shield⁠" Tipsis, who was acquired from 9INE, where he had been benched in March when the organization signed HLTV's current #22 prospect, Alan "⁠flayy⁠" Krupa.
