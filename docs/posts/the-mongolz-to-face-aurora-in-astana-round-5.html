The MongolZ to face Aurora in Astana round 5

Day four of the PGL Astana group stage has concluded with FURIA, Falcons, and MOUZ securing their playoff spots, while HEROIC, The Huns, and PARIVISION suffered early eliminations.

The results mean that six more teams remain in the running for the three leftover playoff spots. magic will face Gentle Mates in the first game of the day at 07:00 before G2 take on Monte.

The MongolZ will then face Aurora in what will be the standout match of the 2-2 pool and the third time these two teams face off against each other, with the current record standing at one victory apiece.
