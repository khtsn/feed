TL Jamppi, Mistic, and Enzo enter restricted free agency

In a move to rebuild their roster, Team Liquid Team Liquid Europe Unranked Jamppi Elias Olkkonen nAts Ayaz Akhmetshin Enzo Enzo Mestari Mistic James Orfila Keiko Georgio Sanassy have announced that Enzo , Mistic and Jamppi will be allowed to explore other options as restricted free agents.

Enzo and Mistic, the former of whom was the team's in-game leader this past season, have been with the organization since November 2023.

Both of their Valorant careers started in 2020, but their paths would not cross until 2022 when they found themselves as teammates on FNATIC FNATIC Europe Unranked Boaster Jake Howlett Chronicle Timofey Khromov Alfajer Emir Ali Beder . Their tenures as players on the team's active roster ended in September 2022, and they would become teammates once again when Team Liquid signed them both ahead of the 2024 season.

“Willing to IGL, lead and build a team! Can also flex and second IGL,” Enzo said following the announcement. The frenchman also has his sights set on great heights in 2025. “Let's make it to Champs Paris.”

Team Liquid's roster overhaul comes following less-than-desirable results in their 2024 season. Their performance was peaked by a 5th-6th place finish at EMEA Stage 1. It was also the first season that saw the team fail to qualify for an international event.

“Tough year for us so Liquid have allowed me to explore my options as a restricted F/A,” Mistic said following the announcement.

Jamppi was on the Team Liquid team that won the 2023 EMEA League. Most known for his initiator play, his 1.16 rating across four maps in the grand final against FNATIC will be fresh in the minds of Liquid fans.

“I'll never ever forget all the support you all have given me,” Jamppi said following the announcement. “It is still my goal to bring this organization big success.”

Both Mistic and Jamppi stated that they are open to opportunities in NA, EMEA and APAC.

Team Liquid is now:

  • Ayaz "nAts" Akhmetshin
  • Georgio "Keiko" Sanassy
  • Nico "Harmii" Harms
  • Eamon "cauzed" Drea (Manager)
  • Emil "eMIL" Sandgren (Head coach)
  • Jake "Bacon9" Lloyd (Analyst)
