NIP's Cologne hopes stamped out by HEROIC

Ninjas in Pyjamas's slim hopes to qualify for the IEM Cologne Major are all but extinguished after a loss to HEROIC put them out of Stake Ranked in 5-6th place.

For HEROIC, the win is a huge step towards their already likely attendance in Cologne, and they can now turn their gaze towards a potential Stage 2 spot should they win the $100k event outright.

After a tough start to 2026 with early exits at BLAST Bounty, IEM Krakow and BLAST Rotterdam, Ninjas in Pyjamas needed to make at least the top three at Stake Ranked to save their season and make the Major, according to VRS expert Mischief.

A big day one win over Gentle Mates opened the door, but losses to GamerLegion and now HEROIC have now ended that dream.
