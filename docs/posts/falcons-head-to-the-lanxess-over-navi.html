Falcons head to the LANXESS over NAVI

Falcons have laid claim to their place in the playoffs of the IEM Cologne Major, taking down Natus Vincere in a three-map affair that culminated in a double-overtime brawl on Anubis. Finn "⁠karrigan⁠" Andersen's men now join Vitality and Aurora among the top eight, who similarly qualified with 3-1 records, as well as FURIA and Spirit, who qualified without dropping a series in the group stage.

"It feels good," karrigan said of securing a place in the LANXESS. "Obviously, I've played a lot of Majors in there, and I think the LANXESS Arena is one of them we're I've always had one grand final, but I've always been in the quarters and semis, many times, and I think the only player to play all Colognes.

"I'm super grateful for this opportunity, three games away from winning the Major in Cologne. A dream come true. Few days off now; super proud of the way that we beat NAVI. I think everything can be possible, but our consistency has been the biggest issue. Maybe we play up to our enemies, because we played way better today than we did against Monte."

Falcons faced hardly any trouble during the first half of their pick of Dust2, with a quad kill from Nikola "⁠NiKo⁠" Kovač in the pistol round laying the ground work for a successful T side. With René "⁠TeSeS⁠" Madsen and Ilya "⁠m0NESY⁠" Osipov each racking up three multikills on the offense, Falcons managed to march ahead to a comfortable 9-3 lead before swapping sides.

A CT-side pistol victory, followed up by a well-played anti-eco retake and a triple kill from m0NESY led to Falcons quickly wrapping things up on their pick 13-3.

NAVI's pick of Mirage featured a back-and-forth start in the first half, with Aleksi "⁠Aleksib⁠" Virolainen's men overcoming a lost pistol round to build up a 6-6 scoreline as they wrapped up their T-side. Natus Vincere laid claim to their first pistol round win of the series thanks to a triple kill from Valeriy "⁠b1t⁠" Vakhovskiy, putting them in the driver's seat for the remainder of the map.

Though Natus Vincere let Russian duo Maksim "⁠kyousuke⁠" Lukin and m0NESY steal away a 3v5, that was the only round they would let slip from their grasp in the second half, marching ahead to a 13-7 scoreline after shaking off their dismal Dust2 performance.

Falcons claimed yet another pistol round win to kick off the Anubis decider, but NAVI were the ones to come out of the first half victorious, as impact from Ihor "⁠w0nderful⁠" Zhdanov allowed the team to edge out a 7-5 lead on the offense. Natus Vincere inched closer to a playoff berth thanks to a CT-side pistol win and a triple kill from Drin "⁠makazze⁠" Shaqiri, but ultimately failed to close things out in regulation, squandering man-advantage situations while TeSeS provided stellar output for Falcons.

Down 6-11, Falcons managed to go on a six-round streak that netted them series point, with Natus Vincere finally breaking their dry spell to force overtime. Falcons were unable to finish off Anubis in the first set of extra rounds despite impressive multikills from m0NESY and TeSeS, but managed to get it over the line the second time around, winning out the map 19-16 and the series 2-1.
