Bilibili is eliminated from the event, earning 1 Championship point but missing out from their first-ever Masters appearance.

The matchup is one EDward Gaming has dominated in the past, having won every single encounter and only losing three maps in eight series. The teams have only played each other twice since the beginning of VCT China, with their most recent encounter taking place in the Stage 2 playoffs.

In the map veto, the teams banned their respective permabans, with Bilibili taking away Fracture and EDG removing Abyss. Bilibili chose Pearl to open the series, while EDG picked their favored map for this event, Haven. Split remained as the fifth and final map, should the series have gone the distance.

Starting off on Pearl, EDG won the pistol round, but it was Bilibili who took an early lead off the back of multiple rushia triple kills. EDward Gaming's timeout let the Champions get a couple of rounds, but whzy matched the opposing Neon and found a 3K of his own to set the score 5-3. ZmjjKK continued exploiting Neon's agility to find early advantages for his team, ending the half with 4 kills and 15 eliminations.
