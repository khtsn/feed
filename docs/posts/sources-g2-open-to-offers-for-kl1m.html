Sources: G2 open to offers for kl1m

kl1m is available for transfer this summer

G2 are prepared to listen to offers for Russian AWPer Klimentii "⁠kl1m⁠" Krivosheev, whose loan to MIBR will expire after the IEM Cologne Major, sources with knowledge of the matter told HLTV.

kl1m is widely regarded as one of the brightest young talents in the scene, featuring at No.5 in HLTV's latest prospects report. He has averaged a 1.24 rating since joining MIBR on a loan deal from G2 in August 2025, when the Brazilian team followed in FURIA's footsteps and went international.

Despite kl1m's promising start to life in the tier-1 space, HLTV knows that G2 aren't considering promoting the 20-year-old to the main roster. As things stand, G2 are unlikely to entertain further loan offers for the player.

In an interview with Dust2 Brasil on the first day of the Major, kl1m said that his future beyond the Major was unclear.

"I don't know what is going to happen in the future," he said. "It's uncertain. We need to talk to MIBR, we need to talk to G2, because I'm still under contract with them. I don't know.

"I like it here. But it depends."
