Live updates from IEM Rio grand final

Vitality can further their claim as the greatest team of all time should they win today, not only because they would be adding another trophy to a cabinet already overflowing, but also because they would win a second ESL Grand Slam.

"We'll have pressure for sure. It's the Grand Slam," Shahar "⁠flameZ⁠" Shushan told HLTV in an interview yesterday. "Tomorrow is a great opportunity for us, we'll come and give a lot of energy. It's just about ourselves, if we don't lose to ourselves, it's going to go well for us."

Should they claim the Grand Slam, Vitality would be the only team to have done so twice, and be the only back-to-back Grand Slam winners. It would be yet another accolade for the squad to add to their resume.

And remember, Spirit can claim $100k if they beat them tonight.

To Freya "⁠Freya⁠" Spiers on a possible third grand slam: "It will be a prestigious achievement, one of a kind, my first time that I have such an achievement. We have some chances left, but hopefully we get it done here.

"It would be nice to do it before the Major. The sooner the better, as always with the grand slam, to ease the pressure."

On Spirit: "I haven't looked too much into them specifically. Big change with the IGL, tN1R has been playing really well. That's the biggest difference maker for them if the others can step up to help donk. donk is always there, if the others play well it will be tough for us."

No Ancient today, we expect, but there's still some intrigue in the veto with both teams on a hot streak on Dust2.

Hard to see Spirit taking the series without a win on their home map...

Talking to Martin "⁠STYKO⁠" Styk, on the crowd: "I think nobody wants Vitality to win again. Probably, fans on our side, it's more enjoyable to play when they cheer for you but it's still a game.

"We have a good opportunity to beat them. They are in good shape as a team. We can make some damage. I do not think about me beating ZywOo, it's about Spirit beating Vitality."

On BO5: "Yeah, we have a map pool ready. We had more time to prepare, we played first. We are ready. It's easier to play BO5 because you know all the maps.'

Underdog: "We are not chasing the trophy, we are chasing a good game. We want to be a better team; we don't think about lifting the trophy. Vitality is a great team, maybe they are better than us. It's interesting to see what damage you can do to the best team in the world."
