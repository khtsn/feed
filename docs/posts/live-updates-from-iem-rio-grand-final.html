Vitality can further their claim as the greatest team of all time should they win today, not only because they would be adding another trophy to a cabinet already overflowing, but also because they would win a second ESL Grand Slam.

"We'll have pressure for sure. It's the Grand Slam," Shahar "⁠flameZ⁠" Shushan told HLTV in an interview yesterday. "Tomorrow is a great opportunity for us, we'll come and give a lot of energy. It's just about ourselves, if we don't lose to ourselves, it's going to go well for us."

Should they claim the Grand Slam, Vitality would be the only team to have done so twice, and be the only back-to-back Grand Slam winners. It would be yet another accolade for the squad to add to their resume.

And remember, Spirit can claim $100k if they beat them tonight.
