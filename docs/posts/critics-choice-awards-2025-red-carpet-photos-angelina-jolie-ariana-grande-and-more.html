Critics Choice Awards 2025 Red Carpet Photos: Angelina Jolie, Ariana Grande and More

See what the biggest stars of film and television wore to make their entrances at the Critics Choice Awards, hosted by Chelsea Handler in Santa Monica, Calif.

Feb. 7, 2025Updated 8:20 p.m. ET

See the full list of 2025 Critics Choice Award winners.

If the timing of this year’s Critics Choice Awards strikes you as odd, you’re right: Most major awards shows are held on Sunday evenings. But after two postponements because of the Los Angeles wildfires, the ceremony finally came to rest on a Friday in early February.

Despite the rescheduling, the main details held firm: Chelsea Handler hosted the event as planned from the Barker Hangar in Santa Monica, Calif., with many of the year’s biggest stars of film and television in attendance. But, in what may be a sign of the organizers’ downsized ambitions for the show, a planned two-hour special of red carpet coverage before the ceremony was scrapped with the most recent rescheduling.

Not to fear — The New York Times has all the must-see looks from stars and presenters at the 30th annual Critics Choice Awards.
