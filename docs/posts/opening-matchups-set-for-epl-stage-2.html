Opening matchups set for EPL Stage 2

FaZe are on the hunt for their first playoff berth of the year

ESL Pro League Stage 1 has drawn to a close, with eight teams securing their spot in the next stage of the competition. They will now join a raft of top teams in vying for a playoff berth, including the likes of Spirit and FURIA.

The opening matchups for Stage 2 are now known, the teams having seeded each other following the conclusion of Stage 1.

Perhaps the biggest opening match on paper, at least in terms of name value, sees G2 taking on FaZe, although neither team has been in convincing form as of late. G2 made the playoffs in Katowice but have been inconsistent since, whilst FaZe have only two series wins to their name in 2026.

The matchup between FUT and Astralis has become a staple as of late, the teams having met three times previously in recent events. Astralis took the first bout, eliminating FUT from IEM Krakow, but FUT have had revenge twice since.

Legacy meanwhile will be out for revenge as they take on Aurora, the two having contested a BO5 final late last year at PGL Masters Bucharest. Aurora clinched that series 3-2 to bag their first-ever notable trophy.
