Trial to Begin for N.Y.P.D. Sergeant Who Threw Cooler at Fleeing Man

Bilibili Gaming Bilibili Gaming China Rank #3 whzy Wang Haozhe (王昊哲) rushia Wang Xiaojie (王晓杰) nephh Marcus Tan Knight Liu Yuxiang (刘宇翔) bud Yang Renyu (杨韧余) player Lu "Levius" Yinzhong (卢胤中) has shared that he will be taking a temporary leave of absence from competitive play.

"I have encountered some problems in my psychological and competitive state recently," he shared. "To better prepare for future competition, and to live up to the team and those who support me, I'm choosing to press the pause button at this stage, hoping to find my own answer in this period of time. I believe this will not be the end of my career. I hope that after the phased adjustment, I can return to the game in a better state."

Levius has spent his entire professional Valorant career with BiliBili, having joined the team at the end of the 2023 season as a rookie. While he has played other roles before in a limited capacity, he has primarily been BLG's go-to Sentinel player. In recent offseason events, the team has tried using him as a Controller player.

In 2025, Levius helped BiliBili reach the international stage on two different occasions. A second-place finish at Stage 1 saw the team just miss the mark on lifting its first VCT trophy, but helped it qualify for its first-ever Masters event. He helped BLG break past that barrier in Stage 2, which ended with the team not only qualifying for a second-straight Champions event, but lifting its first-ever trophy in VCT.

Since the end of the season, BLG has been relatively quiet in the transfer market.
