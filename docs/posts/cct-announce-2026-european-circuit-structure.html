CCT announce 2026 European circuit structure

CCT have announced their circuit structure for 2026, featuring a progression system that gives a chance to up-and-coming teams for a shot at the CCT Europe trophy.

The tournament organizer will hold a total of 34 across three circuits, namely the Regular CCT Europe, Challengers Europe, and Contenders Europe.

The lowest circuit in the food chain, Contenders, will feature a 16-strong team list which will come entirely from two open qualifiers for the first four events, and feature a $2,500 prize pool alongside two spots in the next Challengers event.

From Series 5-10, two open qualifier teams will join 14 VRS invited squads.

Each Challengers event will award $5,000 and two spots at the CCT Europe circuit, with all teams set to receive their invites either through the VRS ranking or qualifying through the Contenders circuit.

The main CCT Europe circuit remains largely unchanged, with each of the 16 iterations across the year featuring a $25,000 prize pool, with a total of 36 invites spread across the Play-In, group stage, and playoffs.
