TYLOO win XSE GangKui Cup S2 over FlyQuest

JamYoung posted a 1.29 rating at the $15k LAN

TYLOO have become champions of XSE GangKui Cup Season 2 by beating FlyQuest in the grand final.

It's the fourth win in a row for TYLOO over FlyQuest, albeit their first in 2026, as they asserted their dominance as the best of the rest in Asia underneath The MongolZ.

China's number one side is set to play another grand final today at the Yuqilin Pinnacle of Battle, but have already qualified comfortably for Stage 1 of the Cologne Major after a 9-12th finish at BLAST Rotterdam and the win here.

FlyQuest have also done enough, and will join compatriots THUNDER dOWNUNDER at the Major in Germany.

GangKui's $15,000 Cup had a crucial role in deciding the final Asian spot for the Cologne Major, as The Huns and SemperFi were both handed early eliminations to allow Lynn Vision, via their run at PoB, to keep their spot in the top five of the ranking.

The event then became about maximising points for Major seeding and invitations to other events, with TYLOO pocketing 36 points from the final and climbing from 108th in the March 16 VRS to 31st now.
