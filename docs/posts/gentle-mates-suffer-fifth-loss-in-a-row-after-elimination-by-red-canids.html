Gentle Mates suffer fifth loss in a row after elimination by RED Canids

Young sniper reNTU starred on Mirage

Gentle Mates have been eliminated from IEM Rio by RED Canids, extending their streak of match losses to five.

The Spanish side did not make the Cologne Major despite being in a strong position earlier in the year, and have taken a nosedive since being eliminated from BCG Masters late in March.

Their tournament started so well too, with a map one win over G2 fuelled by some fantastic AWP play from Antonio "⁠MartinezSa⁠" Martinez. But that form was nowhere to be found against RED, as the former 9z sniper recorded a team-low 0.91 rating.

New boy Renan "⁠reNTU⁠" Cardoso only has 23 maps on record but was the star on Mirage as the 20-year-old rookie posted a 1.54 rating to start the series with a bang.

Ancient was convincing in the other direction, as Gentle Mates pushed the series to an Overpass decider.

But there, Richard "⁠chayJESUS⁠" Seidy took over. He played with his food en route to a 1.84 rating and 120.0 ADR on the crucial T side, as RED Canids took control from 10-10 to win the map 13-10 and series 2-1.
