The Huns qualify for PGL Astana

controlez set to attend his first Big Event with The Huns

The Huns have qualified for PGL Astana 2026 by beating DEPO 3-0 in the final round of the Asia Closed Qualifier. They become the 13th team confirmed at the $800,000 event, which will take place between May 7-17.

The Asia Closed Qualifier featured only three teams, determined by qualifiers held in East Asia, West Asia, and Oceania. DEPO moved past Ground Zero in the first round but couldn't make a dent against The Huns in the best-of-five final.

PGL Astana will be The Huns' first Big Event since signing Unudelger "⁠controlez⁠" Baasanjargal, who was recruited in January to replace Anarbileg "⁠cobrazera⁠" Uuganbayar.

The Huns are still in the race for an Asian spot at the Cologne Major. They are currently two spots below the qualification zone but remain alive in the XSE GangKui Cup Season 2 Finals, in Shanghai. Their next match will see them take on TYLOO, another team that is battling for a Major spot.
