TSM parts with tdawgg and XXiF

2 days ago

TSM TSM North America Rank #14 seven Johann Hernandez gMd Anthony Guimond sym Tyler Porter has announced the release of both Travis "tdawgg" Regan and Damion "XXiF" Cook. This change comes only a month after their signing in November.

The pair of tdawgg and XXiF came about in 2024 when both joined QoR QoR North America Rank #26 ALEKSANDAR Aleksandar Hinojosa nerve Michael Yerrow snarly Boni Logan Logan Coffey to compete in NA Challengers. QoR placed in the top eight of Split 1, but missed the playoffs of Split 2, ending the team's chance for Ascension. After their elimination, tdawgg and XXiF opted not to re-sign with the organization for 2025.

TSM, after a trip to Ascension, brought in the QoR duo as they rebuilt their roster for Split 3 of Challengers. This new roster played in two tournaments together. They emerged victorious at For Those Who Dare 2024, an invitational featuring 100 Thieves 100 Thieves North America Rank #6 zander Alexander Dituri Cryocells Matthew Panganiban eeiu Daniel Vucenovic Boostio Kelden Pupello Asuna Peter Mazuryk , and then placed in the top eight of Challengers Split 3.

tdawgg has clarified that he was not dropped by TSM, but left in order to pursue an offer from a different team. No statement has been made thus far from XXiF.

TSM will need to re-complete their roster before Stage 1 of Challengers 2025, starting on Jan 29.

TSM TSM North America Rank #14 seven Johann Hernandez gMd Anthony Guimond sym Tyler Porter are now:

  • Johann "seven" Hernandez
  • Anthony "gMd" Guimond
  • Tyler "sym" Porter
  • "Recoil"
  • Laurynas "Nbs" Kisielius (Head coach)
