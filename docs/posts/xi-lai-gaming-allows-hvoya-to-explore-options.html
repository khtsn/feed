Xi Lai Gaming Xi Lai Gaming China Rank #2 Viva Lifan Ran (冉立凡) NoMan James Man Rarga Arthur Churyumov happywei Deng Minwei (邓闵维) coconut Colin Patrick Chung (钟弘祖) head coach Aleksandr "hvoya" Eremin has announced that he has been allowed to explore his options ahead of the offseason.

"My contract is expiring soon and I am still in talks with XLG, but I am allowed to explore other options," he said. "LFT for VCT 2026 as a Head coach/assistant for any region."

hvoya has been XLG's head coach for the past two seasons, leading them to trophy-winning campaigns at the China National Competition: Season 2 and eventually China Ascension in 2024. Led by hvoya in their debut season in the VCT, XLG hit the ground running. After a 5th-6th finish at China Kickoff, XLG went 4-1 in the Stage 1 group stage, and then went nearly perfect in the playoffs, dropping just one map en route to lifting the trophy and qualifying for Masters Toronto.

After facing a swift elimination two games into their campaign at Toronto, XLG went 4-1 in the Stage 2 group stage. They finished fourth in the playoffs, but ended the year with enough championship points to qualify for Champions Paris.
