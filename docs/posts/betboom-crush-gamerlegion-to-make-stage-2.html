BetBoom crush GamerLegion to make Stage 2

BetBoom have advanced to Stage 2 at the IEM Cologne Major after dominating GamerLegion in a two-map series in the 2-0 pool (13-2 Nuke, 13-8 Ancient).

It was a flat showing out of Janusz "⁠Snax⁠" Pogorzelski's men, who gave up a wealth of man and multi-man advantages round after round and were outgunned by sharp shooting out of BetBoom's individuals.

That isn't to take away from the calling out of Kirill "⁠Boombl4⁠" Mikhailov, who ran circles around GamerLegion in a masterclass 11-round T side run to kick off the series on Nuke — a somewhat unexpected choice as the analyst desk predicted BetBoom to pick into their home turf of Anubis.

The Russian captain also ended the map with the highest rating (1.80), Round Swing (+12.67%), and ADR (98.8) despite only a 9-9 K-D, garnering several assists alongside a 17-10 showing out of Timur "⁠FL4MUS⁠" Marev.

GamerLegion had a better start to Ancient, and looked set to make it a more competitive series after going up 5-0 after winning the pistol and turning a few disadvantages of their own. But it didn't take long for them to collapse in the same fashion as they had on Nuke, with Kirill "⁠Magnojez⁠" Rodnov dominating from Cave and Boombl4 and FL4MUS again finding high-impact multi-frags.

The nail was put in the coffin after GamerLegion's first two gun round wins in the second half were followed by them giving up a 4v3 to set BetBoom on match point, an opportunity the Russian team didn't waste to close out the series dominantly.
