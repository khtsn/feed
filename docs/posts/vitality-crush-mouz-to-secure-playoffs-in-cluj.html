Vitality crush MOUZ to secure playoffs in Cluj

Vitality are the first team through to the playoffs of PGL Cluj-Napoca after trouncing MOUZ in a two-map series (13-5 Dust2, 13-8 Inferno).

It was a near-identical bashing to the teams' semi-final bout at IEM Krakow, where Vitality had secured 13-7 and 13-6 wins on Nuke and Dust2, respectively, and brought Vitality's head-to-head against MOUZ to 11-2 since bringing on Robin "⁠ropz⁠" Kool at the start of 2025.

Mathieu "⁠ZywOo⁠" Herbaut was indomitable across the series, pairing with Shahar "⁠flameZ⁠" Shushan on Dust2 and ropz on Inferno to offer MOUZ no chance at getting their foot in.

The scoreline on the former was even more one-sided than it appears as MOUZ tallied three early rounds off a pistol victory, and managed just two more in the half as flameZ proved lethal in entries and Dan "⁠apEX⁠" Madesclaire called circles around his opponents.

Vitality's Dust2 record after that victory now sits at 38-4 since the start of 2025, and their form carried into Inferno with MOUZ looking flustered as the map wore on. apEX was in Lotan "⁠Spinx⁠" Giladi's head, calling him out for baiting on both maps, and Vitality stormed to a 9-3 lead on the defense between ropz's solid anchoring and ZywOo's lethal multi-kills.

No opening duels went MOUZ's way for the first nine rounds in a row, and it took a Ádám "⁠torzsi⁠" Torzsás 1v3 ace clutch for them to tally their first.

Another quad-kill from the Hungarian in the second pistol round offered his team a small lifeline, but it only served to make the scoreline look slightly more respectable before Vitality sealed victory, 13-8.
