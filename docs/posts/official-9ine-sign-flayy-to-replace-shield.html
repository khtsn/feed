Official: 9INE sign flayy to replace shield

9INE have announced the signing of Polish sniper Alan "⁠flayy⁠" Krupa, confirming a previous report by HLTV. The 20-year-old replaces Roberts "⁠shield⁠" Tipsis, who has been moved to the inactive roster.

Sources: 9INE close in on flayy

It will be the first international roster for flayy, who was transfer-listed by Venom in January when the organization chose to disband its roster. He has averaged a 1.14 rating in the last 12 months and made appearances in the top 20 of all four HLTV Prospects reports, with 9INE hoping he can deliver on that promise to help curb their slide down the rankings.

The team, which is now composed of three Poles and three Danes, is currently ranked 42nd on the live VRS. They are signed up for a multitude of LANs ahead of the Cologne Major cut-off, and will make their debut with flayy in the Stake Ranked Episode 1 Closed Qualifier against Tricked on Thursday.
