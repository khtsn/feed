BBL Esports signs LewN

BBL Esports BBL Esports Europe Rank #39 Elite Efe Teber Brave Eren Kasırga LewN Burak Alkan reazy Kaan Ürpek QutionerX Doğukan Dural has signed Turkish duelist player Burak "LewN" Alkan from Galatasaray Esports Galatasaray Esports Europe Rank #50 CyderX Caner Demir Padisah Onur Dönmez Docent Kadri Vatandaş Touven Can Eryıldız .

He is seen most often on Jett and Raze, but has also shown an ability to play Iso and Neon this past season.

According to BBL's announcement, he joins on a three-year contract pending approval from Riot, whose minimum age requirement for VCT is 18. LewN will turn 18 in November. According to an announcement by his former team, LewN's move from Galatasaray to BBL includes a transfer fee and bonuses.

LewN joined Galatasaray ahead of the 2024 season, and previously played for PCIFIC Espor PCIFIC Espor Europe Rank #5 ComeBack Rosé Eren Erzan Celasun Emir Celasun Loita Utku Kart Lar0k Yusuf Kaan Kanber . Competing in the Turkish VCL this past year, he helped Galatasaray to a fourth-place finish, missing out on Turkey's lone EMEA Ascension slot.

In 2024, BBL failed to qualify for the knockout rounds of any competitions they were in. A 3-3 start in EMEA Stage 1 saw them narrowly miss out on the playoffs due to a five-way tie in Group Alpha placing them in fourth. In Stage 2, they went 1-3 and again narrowly missed out on playoffs, finishing with the same overall record as Karmine Corp Karmine Corp Europe Rank #31 tomaszy Tomás Machado marteen Martin Pátek but finishing behind them by two maps. BBL ended their 2024 season ranked seventh in Europe.
