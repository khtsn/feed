Founding member AsLanM4shadoW leaves BBL

BBL Esports BBL Esports Europe Unranked Elite Efe Teber pAura Melih Karaduran Brave Eren Kasırga reazy Kaan Ürpek QutionerX Doğukan Dural have bid farewell to Ali "AsLanM4shadoW" Osman Balta, one of the team's founding members, having joined in the summer of 2020. AsLanM4shadoW leaves BBL after 1268 days together.

When AsLanM4shadoW joined BBL, the budding Turkish roster was still fighting for their presence in Europe, while simultaneously dominating regional competition. That became the standard for BBL, who only solidified their spot in EMEA in 2022, with that year's format changes and with 2023's kickoff of the partnered era.

During the time leading up to the partnered era, AsLanM4shadoW survived numerous rebuilds. He was the only player from BBL's founding roster to reach the partnered VCT EMEA, and the most tenured member in the squad by over a year at that time; QutionerX was the second-most tenured member, having joined in December 2021.

In AsLanM4shadoW's final year under BBL, the roster entered VCT EMEA as one of two Turkish representatives (alongside FUT Esports FUT Esports Europe Unranked MrFaliN Furkan Yeğen yetujey Eray Budak AtaKaptan Ata Tan cNed Mehmet Yağız İpek qRaxs Doğukan Balaban ).
