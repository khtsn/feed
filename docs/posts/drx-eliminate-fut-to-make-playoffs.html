FUT's map pick of Bind began with a chaotic back and forth of rounds with both teams continuously trading thrifty wins. Ultimately, FUT would be the first to stabilize with seven rounds in a row to finish their defensive half. Powered by a 16 kill performance from qw1 , FUT looked poised to take the map.

DRX were unable to muster up a satisfactory defense to start the half as FUT strolled to the finish line after winning ten of the last eleven rounds to hit map point. Despite BuZz beginning to come into form, DRX were unable to take back control of the map and conceded Bind to FUT 13-9 with qw1 ending on 28 kills.

DRX's playoff hopes continued to be crushed by a strong start from FUT. After losing the pistol and following eco, FUT won four in a row to establish themselves on the offense. Late into the half, DRX's individuals began to show, with a hero round from BuZz with the Bucky imbuing confidence into the DRX side. This was enough to keep DRX in the series as they ended the half tying up the score 6-6.

DRX looked reinvigorated coming onto the offense, winning both the pistol and bonus round to reach nine. A 1v3 from Zest prevented FUT from coming back into the map after two successful retakes from the Turkish team brought the map to within a single round. Four rounds in a row concluded the map for DRX as they forced a decider in the series with a 13-8 victory after a dominant offensive half.

DRX began the decider of Haven in commanding fashion, taking the first pistol and bonus round to reach a 4-0 lead. A blistering hot performance from the entire squad crushed any hopes that may have been left after FUT took map one. The Turks seemed deflated as DRX ended the half with a near flawless record, 11-1. Little could be said about the second half as DRX's lead was too strong for FUT to overcome. The map was closed out by DRX in an incredibly one-sided final map 13-4.
