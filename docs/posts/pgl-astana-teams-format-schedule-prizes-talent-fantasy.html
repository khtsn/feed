Here's everything you need to know about the second edition of the Kazakh tournament.

Teams and format

Six top-ten teams are in attendance in Kazakhstan due to IEM Atlanta taking place at the same time, with Falcons and Spirit having the highest VRS ranks.

The event will be an exciting one in terms of debuts, as Finn "⁠karrigan⁠" Andersen will play his first event in a Falcons jersey, while Justinas "⁠jL⁠" Lekavicius and Adrian "⁠xelex⁠" Vincze play for the new-look MOUZ.

The tournament's format will be the usual for PGL events, as the group stage will be played in a Swiss system. The eight advancing teams will all start their playoff run in the quarter-finals, with the two finalists set to meet in a best-of-five trophy clash after a third-place decider opens up proceedings on Sunday.
