BCG Masters Championship 2 to be held in Vila Nova de Gaia

Vanta, the tournament operator behind the JB Pro League, has announced that it will run the second edition of the BC Game Masters Championship at the SAW Esports Arena in Vila Nova de Gaia between May 21-24.

The event will be a fully open 16-team LAN with a €2,000 sign-up fee and a $50,000 prize fund. The top four VRS-ranked teams will go straight to the playoffs, while the remaining 12 will be split into two round-robin, best-of-one groups.

The announcement came as a surprise as the event had not featured in last month's roadmap for the remainder of the year. It's unclear whether this tournament will be held in addition to the Championship LAN planned for Q4 or in its place.
