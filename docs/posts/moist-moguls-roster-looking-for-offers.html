Moist Moguls roster looking for offers

4 days ago

Moist Moguls Moist Moguls North America Rank #6 aproto Alex Protopapas sym Tyler Porter brawk Brock Somerhalder flyuh Xavier Carlson thief Brady Dever are now looking for offers to join the VCT ecosystem after their third-place finish in the Split 2 playoffs.

The head coach, Tanishq "Tanizhq" Sabharwal announced on social media that he and the rest of the team are looking for potential VCT offers. He also mentioned that the team does want to continue together next year but also that each of his players wants to compete at the highest level and deserves to shine in VCT.

Their spot in VCL North America is still guaranteed after their placement and performance this year. The roster showed consistent improvement over the course of the Challenger season. During Split 1 and the Mid-Season Face-Off, they came in the top six. In Split 2, they managed a flawless group stage to qualify for the playoffs.

Moist Moguls ended up placing third during the playoffs stage, narrowly missing out on qualifying for the Ascension tournament in Brazil. Their 3-0 loss to The Guard The Guard North America Rank #1 JonahP Jonah Pulice trent Trent Cairns neT Michael Bernet valyn Jacob Batio tex Ian Botsch ended their 2023 competitive season. The two finalists of the playoffs — M80 M80 North America Rank #4 zander Alexander Dituri eeiu Daniel Vucenovic NiSMO Marc-Andre Tayar johnqt Mohamed Amine Ouarid koalanoob Gianfranco Potestio and The Guard — ended up fighting for the Ascension spot at VCT NA 2024 with The Guard walking away victorious.

M80 is in a similar position — with their Ascension bid foiled this year, the team is willing to consider individual offers to join the VCT ecosystem. MxM, like M80, in the absence of such offers, will participate in VCL NA 2024.
