Falcons have advanced for the PGL Astana playoffs after surviving a 4-10 deficit on the deciding Mirage against The MongolZ.

Ilya "⁠m0NESY⁠" Osipov and Maksim "⁠kyousuke⁠" Lukin were the keys to victory, combining for 38 kills in the last 14 rounds as they pulled the decider back to overtime and won it cleanly from there.

Before that, the Russian pair also led the way to Falcons' comfortable opening win on Dust2 and were on top of the board in a narrow Inferno loss. By the end, they averaged 1.36 and 1.45 ratings, respectively, for the series.

The win puts Falcons through to the Berys Arena after they went 3-1 in the Swiss group stage.

"We struggled in playoffs and the reason we brought Finn is to get over that edge, and I'm excited to get on the stage with him after eight years," NiKo said. "He's a big showman, and I'm gonna enjoy it."
