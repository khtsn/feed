Vitality reach IEM Atlanta playoffs through lower bracket; BetBoom secure semi-final seed

Vitality punched their ticket to the IEM Atlanta playoffs with a resounding victory 2-0 over B8 on Mirage and Dust2, while BetBoom beat paiN to skip the quarter-finals and go straight to the semis.

The Vitality series started off as a nightmare for B8, who didn't get on the board until the sixth round on the attacking side of Mirage, and scored just one more round as the Dan "⁠apEX⁠" Madesclaire-led squad hit double digits at the half.

Danylo "⁠s1zzi⁠" Vinnyk had a great game despite B8's struggles, posting a server-high +13.97% swing and 1.92 rating in the 13-5 loss, but nobody else on his team followed suit with everyone else below a .60 rating by the end of it. On the other side of the spectrum, Alexey "⁠alex666⁠" Yarmoshchuk got the Fort Minor treatment for his 1-16 showing.

The second map was just as much of a walkover at first, with Vitality once again getting the first five before B8 got on the board, this time on the CT side of Dust2. This time around, however, the Ukrainians had a late rally to at least put four on the board before the switch.

After the half, back-and-forth rounds didn't let any team settle, but it favored Vitality as they kept their lead all the way to match point, 12-7. Two rounds later, with a jumping Five-seveN shot from Robin "⁠ropz⁠" Kool on s1zzi in a 1v1, it was over.

BetBoom and paiN played for seeding in the playoffs over on the B stream, with the advantage of winning the series being that of starting in the bracket's semi-finals.

Dust2, BetBoom's pick, was close as can be, as the two teams went to the half with a 6-6 scoreline and after the half went the distance, to the 24th round, before the Russian squad could lay claim to their pick. It was in large part thanks to Aleksandr "⁠zorte⁠" Zagodyrenko's 1.71-rated performance.

paiN struck back in their own map, only losing a single round in the second half after putting up seven rounds on the attack to lead 7-5 at the half. Rodrigo "⁠biguzera⁠" Bittencourt was outstanding for his team in the second map, posting a 1.82 rating and +7.15% swing to force the rubber map.

paiN deflated on Anubis, however, as BetBoom ran circles around them on the T side all the way to an 11-1 half with Kirill "⁠Magnojez⁠" Rodnov reaching 14 kills. paiN put up a bit of a fight on the T side, winning four in a row, but the comeback was nothing more than cosmetic as BB ended it 13-5.
