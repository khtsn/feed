In 2023, the first year of Valorant's partnership era, FUT ended the year third in the EMEA League, qualifying for Masters Tokyo as well as Champions Los Angeles. In 2024, with Turkish legend cNed , FUT started the year by watching the year's first Masters event from home, but qualified for Masters Shanghai, finishing 5th-6th.
