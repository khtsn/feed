"My contract has ended with Wolves and since they have decided to go into a different direction, I am now looking for a new team for the remainder of the 2026 season," he said. Maark shared that he is open to assistant coaching roles in Tier 1 as well as head coaching positions in Tier 2.

Maark joined Wolves last June midway through the team's run at Masters Toronto, where the team went on a Cinderella run to finish third.
