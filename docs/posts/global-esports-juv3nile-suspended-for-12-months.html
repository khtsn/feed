Global Esports' Juv3nile suspended for 12 months

Riot Games has suspended Global Esports Global Esports Asia-Pacific Rank #47 patrickWHO Mark Musni UdoTan Go Kyung-won (고경원) Kr1stal Savva Fedorov kellyS Kelly Sedillo Papi Federico Evangelista Deryeon Derrick Yee head coach Preston "Juv3nile" Dornon for 12 months beginning April 4, 2025 for potential breaches of its Esports Global Code of Conduct.

The announcement stated that Juv3nile will "be suspended from all Riot-sanctioned esport competitions for 12 months, and he will be required to complete an executive training focused on conduct, respect, and professionalism."

A statement from Global Esports said: "We respect and support Riot's decision fully. At Global Esports, we believe that accountability and integrity are non-negotiable. No matter how difficult or unfortunate a situation may be, the standards of conduct and professionalism — both inside and outside the game — must be upheld at all times, by coaches, staff & players.

With Juv3nile out of Global's coaching booth for the next twelve months, their coaching staff has been reduced to assistant coach Elevated , and performance coach Patathor8 . Should no head coach be signed before the team's next match, it is believed that Elevated will assume head coaching duties.
