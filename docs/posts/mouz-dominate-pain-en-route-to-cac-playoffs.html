MOUZ dominate paiN en route to CAC playoffs

xelex produced a monstrous 2.53 rating on Overpass

MOUZ have secured their place in the CS Asia Championships playoffs by virtue of a comfortable 2-0 victory over paiN, besting the Brazilians with ease on Nuke and Overpass.

It was a straightforward series for MOUZ who were simply better than paiN across the board, the individual performances of the likes of Dorian "⁠xertioN⁠" Berman and Adrian "⁠xelex⁠" Vincze putting the Brazilians on the backfoot. paiN were also guilty of some sloppy coordination at times, aiding their own downfall.

With their victory MOUZ have now claimed back-to-back playoff berths with their new lineup, and things are looking good for the latest iteration of the team. xertioN's fine form continues, xelex is growing with each passing game, and B8 are a very beatable team as MOUZ look to earn a semi-final berth later today.

Nuke was an entirely one-sided affair, MOUZ picking up a second round force-buy win on their CT side and never looking back, xertioN and Ádám "⁠torzsi⁠" Torzsás having a field day as their team comfortably secured the opening map.

xertioN puts up insane 4K hold of the A bombsite

paiN made things a little bit closer on Overpass, but xelex came through with an absolute masterclass to ensure relatively smooth sailing for MOUZ. The young gun was in sublime form throughout the map, putting up opening kills, clutches and multi-kills across both halves of the map.

A string of rounds towards the middle of the map gave paiN a glimmer of hope that they might be able to make things close, but a xelex and torzsi 2v5 crushed the Brazilian's hopes and secured a playoff spot for their side.
