Official: Gizmy takes over as 100 Thieves IGL

100 Thieves have announced the signing of Jack "⁠Gizmy⁠" von Spreckelsen from Monte, confirming an earlier report by HLTV.

The news comes after the organization removed André "⁠Ag1l⁠" Gil from their active roster on Monday.

Gizmy joins the team from Monte, with whom he played at the IEM Cologne Major and helped the international squad reach Stage 3.

Following their exploits in Germany, Gizmy's four teammates made the switch to Luminosity, prompting the Brit to say on X that his teammates signing contracts pre-Major without telling him was a "pretty sad ending."

The 22-year-old will take over in-game-leadership duties from Håvard "⁠rain⁠" Nygaard, with the Norwegian transitioning to a different role on the roster.

Gizmy is expected to make his 100 Thieves debut at BLAST Bounty 2026 S2, set to start on July 21 with the online stage.
