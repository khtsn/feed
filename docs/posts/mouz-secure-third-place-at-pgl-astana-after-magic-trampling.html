MOUZ secure third place at PGL Astana after magic trampling

jL ended his MOUZ debut with his best map in the new jersey

MOUZ have secured bronze at PGL Astana after trampling magic 13-2 on Mirage and 13-7 on Inferno in the third-place decider.

Lotan "⁠Spinx⁠" Giladi put on a show on the opening Mirage with a 20-4 score and a 2.24 rating, before Justinas "⁠jL⁠" Lekavicius took over on Inferno with his best map yet in the MOUZ jersey at 23-7 and 2.12.

The one-sided victory brings an end to MOUZ's debut tournament with their new lineup following Dorian "⁠xertioN⁠" Berman's transition to an in-game leader and the arrival of Adrian "⁠xelex⁠" Vincze and jL.

We definitely reached our goals as a team

On the whole, the new IGL deemed the event a success after they beat the likes of G2 and Aurora twice on the way to the top-three finish.

"I think it was a successful first event. We definitely reached our goals as a team, so I'm happy about that," xertioN said on the official broadcast after the match.

"There are a lot of learnings. I still need to develop my leadership more. For now, we're enjoying the victory and the third place here. Overall, we're pretty happy."

Next up, MOUZ will travel to Shanghai in time for the start of the CS Asia Championships on Wednesday, which features the likes of Falcons, PARIVISION and The MongolZ.
