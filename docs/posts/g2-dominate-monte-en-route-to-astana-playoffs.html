G2 dominate Monte en route to Astana playoffs

NertZ ended the series 2.06-rated

G2 are the penultimate team qualified for PGL Astana playoffs after breaking no sweat in their 2-2 group stage deciding match against Monte.

The squad around Nemanja "⁠huNter-⁠" Kovač thoroughly dominated the opposition on Ancient T side, where a Guy "⁠NertZ⁠" Iluz pistol round 1v3 kicked off a dominant run for the G2 side, who closed it 13-2.

And Dust2 was more of the same with Monte winning the first three rounds, only for G2 to completely take over with an active CT side, spearheaded by some early opening moves from Álvaro "⁠SunPayus⁠" García.

NertZ then ensured a quick closing once on the T side to end the series 2.06-rated, his highest-rated performance in a G2 jersey to date.

The daunting loss brings an end to an underwhelming run from Monte, who hoped for a playoffs showing off the back of their recent success in the tier two.

They grabbed wins over magic and The Huns but had a hard time in matches against tier-one opposition in FURIA, Falcons and G2, never feeling like they had a chance to take a map off the big dogs.
