KRÜ Esports stay alive with 2-0 over Bilibili

KRÜ Esports have defeated Bilibili Gaming in the Champions 2024 group stage. They will next play against FNATIC for a spot in playoffs. Bilibili are now eliminated from the tournament, the second team to end their run.

The match started on KRÜ's map, Lotus, which they won 13-9. Icebox followed with a dominant performance from the Americas team, winning 13-4 and sending Bilibili home.

The victory means KRÜ will stay alive in the event, playing FNATIC in the Group A decider, a rematch of KRÜ's iconic quarterfinal win in Champions 2021.
