NAVI beat Falcons to qualify for Rotterdam playoffs

Natus Vincere became the first team to secure a spot in the playoffs of BLAST Open Rotterdam after a 2-1 victory over Falcons in the Group A upper bracket semi-finals.

It had been up in the air whether they would face a full-force Falcons in the match, but Maksim "⁠kyousuke⁠" Lukin arrived just in time after finally securing his visa.

Doubts of whether he'd be up to his best after coming in at the last minute were temporarily quelled as the young Russian kicked off the series with a quad-kill in the first pistol of Mirage before he led the way to a comfortable victory.

But he then disappeared as Natus Vincere equalized the series on Inferno off the back of a strong offensive half and closed it out on Dust2 with a seven-round unbeaten run on the CT side, denying a red-hot René "⁠TeSeS⁠" Madsen his flowers.

It was an impressive victory and a testament of their solid team play and calling for a Natus Vincere squad that didn't have their individuals quite as in form as during their title-winning run at ESL Pro League. Inconsistent as ever, Ihor "⁠w0nderful⁠" Zhdanov in particular went missing, and Valeriy "⁠b1t⁠" Vakhovskiy took a map and a half to come online as well.

Natus Vincere still have the upper bracket final to play for a chance to skip directly to the semi-finals before they travel to Rotterdam next week, against the winner of Aurora vs. FURIA.

Meanwhile, Falcons fall to the lower bracket to face TYLOO, requiring two consecutive wins for a spot in the playoffs.
