Suggest hit the ground running with DFM in 2022. Across Week 1 and Week 2 of Japanese Challengers Stage 2, he was rated as one of the competition's best players. As Valorant entered its franchising era in 2023, DFM was selected as a partnered organization. However, Suggest's role on the team changed. That season, he competed in seven of DFM's ten matches throughout the Pacific season, including league play and the Last Chance Qualifier.

Suggest was released by DFM in 2023 after just over two years with the organization, and shared that he would take a break for the near future. His arrival to Gen.G marks his return to professional Valorant after over a year on the sidelines.
