HLTV Awards 2026 announced; Dust2 map creator to attend

The event that marks the start of a new competitive year will once again take place in Belgrade, Serbia, at the Sava Center venue that hosted the last edition of the Awards. With the support of our main partner 1xBet and partner Skin.Club, over a 1000 people will gather to celebrate greatness in Counter-Strike as we hand out awards to the best players, teams, and community members of 2026.

We also have the honor to announce that the creator Dust2, David Johnston, will attend the HLTV Awards on the 25th anniversary of the release of the most iconic FPS map of all time.

As always, HLTV will provide standout teams with flights, accommodation, and top-tier bootcamp PCs for the duration of their stay, in collaboration with Relog Media.

To ensure an exceptional, high-quality experience for our players, we will be refining our selection process to keep attendance exclusive while significantly enhancing our VIP hospitality. Nonetheless, we invite all industry members to request an invitation.

Top teams are provided travel support by HLTV

A new addition to the HLTV Awards is the Lifetime Achievement Award, which will be decided by the Hall of Fame Board and given out to a person that left a significant mark on Counter-Strike.

Updates to Hall of Fame rules and the Hall of Fame map are also coming, but more on that will follow tomorrow, when we will also release the lists of Frontrunners for most of the categories.

Community Awards will now also be given out during the main part of the ceremony. Unlike previous years when they were a part of the preshow, the best contributors to the scene will be given tribute on the big stage this time around.

Talking about the stage, returning to the hosting duties are the iconic duo of Freya "⁠Freya⁠" Spiers and Conner "⁠Scrawny⁠" Girvan, who will also be showing up on HLTV Awards content across our Twitter, Instagram, and YouTube.

  • Signups for industry member invites are open at: hltv.org/awards.

  • VIPs that previously attended Awards will receive invites via email on June 21st, after our Launch Party in Cologne.

  • Interested in taking part in the HLTV Conference by GRID as a speaker or panelist? Email sander@hltv.org with a short pitch.

  • HLTV provides travel support to top teams, finalists, and announcers that attend HLTV Awards. Interested parties can reach out to awards@hltv.org.

For those attending the HLTV Awards, you can expect the following:

  • Mingle activities with fellow attendees

  • The HLTV Conference by GRID

  • Red carpet and main ceremony

  • Afterparty

  • HLTV Shop with exclusive merch
