Parken Challenger Championship hits playoffs featuring Major hopefuls 3DMAX, BESTIA, B8

3DMAX will be hoping to sure up their Major qualification berth

The group stage of Parken Challenger Championship has concluded and there are few surprises as to who has made the playoffs, with the the three highest-ranked teams and Major hopefuls 3DMAX, B8 and BESTIA all progressing.

The group stage was not without its upsets, however, as both B8 and BESTIA suffered losses to underdog sides that will hurt their Major qualification hopes.

B8 will be hoping to go all the way in Denmark as their group stage loss came against an unranked WAZABI squad, with the defeat seriously denting B8's Major hopes and leaving them with it all to do if they are to secure attendance in Cologne. They aren't quite out of the fight, but are not far off.
