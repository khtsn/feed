Riot and TJ Sports announce official VCT China teams

1 day ago

(NOTE: Information regarding to the topic is developing. The article will be updating accordingly.)

Riot Games and TJ Sports has announced the 10 teams that will be part of the VCT China League starting next year. In addition, one more team will qualify to the league via Ascension China this December. Discussions of the VCT's expansion to China were first reported by Bloomberg in February of this year and with Riot Games COO Whalen Rozelle confirming later in July that Riot sought to have VCT China finalized to be in the ecosystem by 2024.
