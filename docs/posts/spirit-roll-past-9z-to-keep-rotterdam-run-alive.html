Spirit roll past 9z to keep Rotterdam run alive

Spirit are now one win away from playoffs after a disappointing showing against PARIVISION earlier in the event.

Spirit have secured a 2-0 victory over 9z in the Group B lower bracket of BLAST Open Rotterdam, trampling the South American squad on Overpass (13-6) and Dust2 (13-6).

Spirit will now be preparing to face off against The MongolZ for the first time in 2026 in the lower-bracket final, where the winner will come out with a quarter-final berth.

Danil "⁠donk⁠" Kryshkovets and Dmitry "⁠sh1ro⁠" Sokolov were the stars of the show on 9z's map pick of Overpass, spearheading an impressive 12-0 CT-side thanks to a whopping 11 multikills from the duo combined.

9z managed to avoid a complete 13-0 blowout with a pistol round victory, further extending their streak with a quad kill from Franco "⁠dgt⁠" Garcia, but it was only a matter of time before Spirit found the final round they need to punch in a 13-6 map win.

9z had a better go at things at first on Spirit's pick of Dust2, responding to the Russian squad's 3-0 start with a three-round streak of their own. A 2v5 with a 1v3 from Boris "⁠magixx⁠" Vorobiev snapped 9z's run, and helped Spirit regain the momentum, marching ahead to an 8-4 half.

With a CT-side pistol round under their belt, Spirit quickly put themselves within touching distance of series point. A quad kill from Matias "⁠HUASOPEEK⁠" Ibañez Hernandez helped put 9z on the board on the offense, but it did not prevent Spirit from closing out the map 13-6 with an eco victory just two rounds later.
