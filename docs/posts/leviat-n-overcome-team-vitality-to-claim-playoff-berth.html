Leviatán have advanced to playoffs as the second seed from their group and their first opponent will be drawn later tonight.

Leviatán sought to claim their first international playoff berth since Champions 2022, while Vitality continued to chase the first of their own.

“One of us is going home, and that's not going to be me,” said kiNgg in the day 10 hype trailer.

Leviatán's pick of Icebox saw constant back-and-forth swings between the two teams. Leviatán opened with the first pistol, which Vitality responded to with a thrifty round in round 5 using a full mid push. Leviatán stabilized to a 7-5 half in their favor with the help of their own thrifty in round 8 and effective B retakes.

Leviatán take the lead off the back of an Operator collateral from aspas.

The map remained on a knife's edge; Vitality made it to a 12-10 scoreline off of heroics from Sayf and trexx , but were unable to close it out. Leviatán rallied to secure four rounds in a row and take Icebox 14-12. kingg and aspas came online for Leviatán with 22 kills and 229 ACS for the in-game leader and 21 kills and 222 ACS for the duelist. trexx topped the scoreboard for the hornets with 22 kills and 225 ACS of his own.

Heroics from trexx are not enough as Mazino closes out round 20.

While Icebox was a brawl, Sunset was a stomp; Leviatán ended the first half with a 10-2 scoreline thanks to strong defensive setups and individual plays from C0M and aspas. Even when Vitality were allowed to plant the spike, they were unable to withstand the opponent's retakes.

A stunning ace clutch from C0M completely shuts down Vitality.

Vitality managed to take the second pistol round, but after a narrow failure to convert the bonus round by Sayf, Leviatán easily won the last three rounds to take Sunset 13-4.

Every player on Leviatán put up impressive numbers on the map, with four out of five of them having more than 140 ADR. Mazino eked out the top rating in the server with a 1.45 rating, 15 kills to 12 deaths, and 169 ADR, while C0M barely trailed behind with a 1.43 rating and 16 kills to only 8 deaths. aspas ended the series with 36 kills to 26 deaths overall.

With this victory, Leviatán have secured their first international playoffs berth as an organization since Champions 2022, while Vitality will end their first global appearance since LOCK//IN with a 1-2 record.

Leviatán's first playoff opponent will be drawn later today. When asked who he would prefer to face in the post-match interview, C0M declared, “Give us anyone. We'll beat them.”

Leviatán will look to follow through on C0M's statement when they return for the playoffs, which begin on Aug. 14.
