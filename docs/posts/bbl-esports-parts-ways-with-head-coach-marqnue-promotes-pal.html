In their announcement, the team stated that they conducted a “thorough evaluation of every member on [their] team” ahead of the Esports World Cup and Stage 2 and decided to make these changes as a result.

After a middling Stage 2, BBL opted for a full rebuild in the offseason, changing out all five of their players and steering away from an all-Turkish roster in favor of an English-speaking one for the first time in the organization's history. PAL was added as assistant coach during this time to round out the team.

The gambit has paid dividends for BBL so far this year.
