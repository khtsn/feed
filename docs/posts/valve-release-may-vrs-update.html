Valve release May VRS update

4-5-2026 20:51

The expanded DraculaN S7 will take invites from the May VRS update

Valve has released the May Valve Regional Standings, which will be used to send out invites to two tournaments set to happen during the summer player break.

The first event to use the ranking will be DraculaN S7 after tournament organizer Digital Crusade recently announced a prizepool expansion to their tournament, with the second one being the $1,000,000 XSE Pro League S26 at the end of June.

Below you can find a list of how those tournaments will allocate slots from the ranking:

Digital Crusade DraculaN Season 7 (June 23-28)

  • 14 Global spots to main event

XSE Pro League S26 (June 29-July 12)

  • 16 Global spots to main event
