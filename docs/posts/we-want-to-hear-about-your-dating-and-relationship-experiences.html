We Want to Hear About Your Dating and Relationship Experiences

Third Wheel

Third Wheel, a New York Times column about love, sex, dating and relationships, is seeking input from readers that will help shape our coverage.

Gina Cherelus

In the Third Wheel column, Gina Cherelus explores the delights and horrors of sex, dating and relationships.

April 11, 2025

Basically, I explore the varied and sometimes mischievous ways people are navigating romance, singlehood, relationships and breakups. And I’d love to hear from you about the crazy, romantic, shocking or unconventional dating experiences that you or someone you know may have had.

Have you ever been ghosted by a date? Do you see the silver lining in situationships? Has your experience in a throuple been wildly successful (or perhaps a total catastrophe)? Do you have any unusual insights about dating over the age of 65? Do you use a matchmaker? Any tales about dating while disabled? I’m not looking for wedding announcements or features (do consider submitting your story to Vows for that). I’m also not especially interested in publicity requests. I’m really searching for personal anecdotes and dating trends.

I read all submissions, occasionally write articles inspired by them and always reach back out to ask more questions. We don’t accept anonymous responses and I will never publish anything without your explicit permission. I also will not use your contact information for any other purpose or share it outside our newsroom. Please feel free to share this with a friend. Thanks!

Gina Cherelus covers dating, relationships and culture for The Times and writes the weekly dating column Third Wheel.
