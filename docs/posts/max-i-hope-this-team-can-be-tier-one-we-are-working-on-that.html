9z came into the Cologne Major with uncertain expectations despite their recent good results, but the South American squad quashed any doubt over their credentials so far in Germany.

They dispatched FlyQuest and Astralis to find themselves in the 2-0 pool, meaning they kept three tournament lives as they enter the best-of-three stage of the Major.

"For us it's very important to go 2-0, because in the previous tournaments we didn't start really well," Maximiliano "⁠max⁠" Gonzalez told HLTV after the win against Rasmus "⁠HooXi⁠" Nielsen's troops, while heaping praise on his teammates and coach.

Listen to the interview here.

Congrats, 2-0 after the first day, it can't really go any better. How happy are you that you passed the best-of-ones without losing a tournament life?

For us it's very important to go 2-0, because in the previous tournaments we didn't start really well. The first maps and the first half of the maps were really bad, and now we can play really good CS in the start of Dust2 and in the start of Nuke as well. I'm very happy with that.

About these slow starts, right? We sometimes see teams that come into Stage 2 come in cold against the teams that played here before and got used to the stage. But it felt like you guys really started off strong, no missed smokes and stuff like that. Do you think that's down to the preparation that you guys had, or what is the reason that you managed to get this good start?

I think so. Because in the last week, in the last two weeks actually, we grinded CS all day. We play a lot of DM, watch a lot of demos, and do a lot of scrims. We came to the Major with a 100h minimum in the last two weeks, we grinded a lot.

To talk about the recent results that you guys had. I think you won five events in a row in your domestic scene, and obviously Astana was a playoff appearance as well. Do you think you're peaking at the right moment now that the Major has come around and that you have found your top form?

Yes, absolutely. This team is working. Outside the game, we have a really good relationship and we are getting that on the server. I think this is maybe the best 9z ever, I'm very happy.

You say this is the best 9z ever. You guys had some really good results in the past years, but I felt like you were never consistently at the top for a long time. Do you think this iteration of 9z can do that? And if you do, why do you think that this is the roster that can achieve that?

I hope, I hope. Yeah, for me, there are a lot of things, but there is one big one, which is very important. That is that B1T is with me. B1T was the captain of 9z from 2020 to 2022, and then when I became IGL, he went to MIBR, and then and now he's with me. I think we understand each other a lot. At the same time, we bring Luchov, dgt back, and meyern. I hope this team can be tier-one, we are working on that.

Does having B1T back make calling easier for you when you have a coach that you're very connected with?

Yes, absolutely. He's getting the best version of me. I trust a lot in him, and I hope to work with him for many years.

You're into the best-of-threes. Some good teams will be there, probably Spirit, FUT as well. But considering you've beaten Falcons and then MOUZ in Astana, are you ready to take it against the big dogs once again?

Yes, yes. We came here to play against those types of teams. I personally like to play best-of-threes more, so tomorrow the real Major starts.
