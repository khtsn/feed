Since the end of the 2024 VCT season, YOU has competed for Xi Lai Gaming in the WALL-E Cup, where the team competed against other Chinese VCT teams and finished third. They are also set to compete at the FGC Invitational, where they will test themselves against a field of ten Chinese VCT teams.
