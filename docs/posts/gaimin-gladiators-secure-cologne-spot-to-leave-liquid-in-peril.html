Gaimin Gladiators secure Cologne spot to leave Liquid in peril

Gaimin Gladiators have progressed into the Circuit X Mayhem Sao Paulo grand final to confirm their attendance at the IEM Cologne Major.

The Brazilian side needed to place third or higher in order to secure enough points to grant safe passage to Germany, and have done so by beating Bounty Hunters in the semi-final.

It will be Henrique "⁠HEN1⁠" Teles's first Major since Copenhagen 2024, and Luca "⁠Luken⁠" Nadotti's since Antwerp 2022, as the team completed their rise from number 108 in the VRS in January to the Major by April.

The Gabriel "⁠NEKIZ⁠" Schenato-led squad have picked up enough LAN wins in the last month to fill up their bucket in VRS, in stark contrast to Liquid who have left valuable points on the table by not attending another event before the April 6 cutoff.
