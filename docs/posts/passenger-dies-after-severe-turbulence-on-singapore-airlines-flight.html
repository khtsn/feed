Passenger Dies After Severe Turbulence on Singapore Airlines Flight

Several others were injured on the Singapore Airlines flight, which took off from Heathrow Airport in London on Monday, the airline said.

May 21, 2024, 6:35 a.m. ET

At least one person died and multiple others were injured after a plane encountered severe turbulence on a flight between London and Singapore, Singapore Airlines said on Tuesday.

The plane, a Boeing 777-300ER, diverted to Bangkok, the airline said in an announcement on social media, and landed at 3:45 p.m. local time on Tuesday.

The flight, which had 211 passengers and 18 crew members on board, had left Heathrow Airport on Monday.
