dos9 joined BOOM ahead of the 2025 season, fresh off the organization's second-place finish at Pacific Ascension 2024, which eventually led to BOOM earning a spot in VCT with BLEED BLEED Inactive 's removal. He replaced ZesBeeW in the BOOM squad, and in the 2024 season played for a Revenant Esports Revenant Esports Inactive skillZ Jayanth Ramesh team that reached Ascension but ultimately finished 7th-8th.

While dos9 carried the reputation of a flex player when he first joined, his role with BOOM in 2025 was as the team's Controller. BOOM was eliminated two games into their Stage 1 playoff campaign, and failed to regain the group stage momentum through the rest of the year, going 0-5 in the Stage 2 group stage.

With a top-eight finish in Championship points, BOOM still earned the right to extend their VCT livelihoods with a slot at Pacific Ascension 2025. BOOM picked up natz and whitecat to replace dos9 and Famouz in the starting lineup, and is currently 2-1 in the group stage with a playoff slot secured.

The Kazakhstani dos9 was an import for BOOM, and should he impress an EMEA team, he would not take up an import slot for a prospective team.
