Masters Toronto Swiss stage matchups drawn

The opening matchups for the Swiss Stage of Masters Toronto have been drawn, bringing forward some intriguing matchups between regional powerhouses and their star players.

Once again, the format will follow a Swiss stage. In this format, teams will be drawn against opponents with the same record after the opening round. Qualification for the playoffs is secured when a team gets two wins, while elimination comes with two losses.

The opening matchups are as follows:

In a clash between star Duelists t3xture and aspas , Gen.G and MIBR will go head to head for the first time ever in Valorant. After qualifying for its first international event of the year, BiliBili Gaming will play Team Liquid. Sentinels, after just coming up short in the Americas Stage 1 grand final, will go up against international debutants Wolves Esports. Finally, regional powerhouses looking to return to their former glory will square off in just their second-ever meeting when Team Heretics plays Paper Rex. The first meeting between the two teams was the in the Swiss Stage of Masters Madrid.
