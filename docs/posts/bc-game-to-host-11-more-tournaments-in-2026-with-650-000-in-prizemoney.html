BC Game to host 11 more tournaments in 2026 with $650,000 in prizemoney

BC Game has announced a series of tournaments it will host in the remainder of 2026 for a total of $650,000 in prizemoney, building on the first European series that took place this month and the LAN championship set to take place in Bucharest next week.

The European series, whose first edition was won by Nemiga, will continue with three more $50,000 online cups and another $50,000 LAN before culminating with a $200,000 finals event at the end of the year.

An Asian series will run concurrently with that, featuring three $25,000 online events and a $100,000 LAN finals.

Women's Counter-Strike in Europe will also get a much-needed boost following the closing of ESL Impact last year.

Although BC Game's Masters account on X did not reach the 5,000 follower count it had set as the goal for the launch, the TO will host two $25,000 women's tournaments in Q2 and Q3.

Additional details for each event, such as the exact dates, number of teams and qualification requirements, have yet to be announced.
