IEM Cologne Major quarter-finals as they happened

On overcoming quarter-final curse during a broadcast interview: "It was kind of a mental block for the team. Today, it started very rough as well, but the most important thing is that everyone got themselves back into the game."

On improving the team's Overpass: "The meta is very CT-sided, so it's very hard to play against a team that knows how to play CT. Whoever wins pistol and controls the economy, can run away with the half."

On FalleN's impending retirement: "We are here for him, and he is here for us. He wanted to retire last year, but he sees the talent in the team. No one in the team has pressure because of that."
