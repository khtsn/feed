vs.

On overcoming quarter-final curse during a broadcast interview: "It was kind of a mental block for the team. Today, it started very rough as well, but the most important thing is that everyone got themselves back into the game."

On improving the team's Overpass: "The meta is very CT-sided, so it's very hard to play against a team that knows how to play CT. Whoever wins pistol and controls the economy, can run away with the half."

Did Astana prepare 9z for the LANXESS?

vs.

9z enter their bout against FURIA after a Cinderella run in the group stage, where they got victories over more established teams like Vitality and The MongolZ.

Everything changes now, however, as they will be sitting in front of the thousands of fans descending upon the LANXESS to view the playoffs live in an Arena environment.

"The playoffs in Astana helped us, [for this] to not be the first arena for the team," Maximiliano "⁠max⁠" Gonzalez told HLTV during the playoff's media day, but FURIA remain the more experienced team and one with notable event championships to their name.

"It's not the same to play in the group stage than with all the crowd and in the stadium, but it's more of the experience that we need," the skipper said. "It's going to be a really good match for the crowd and all the people watching from their houses."

Aurora: Playoffs regulars in 2026, but trophies remain elusive

vs.

Aurora are playoff regulars, but have not found the winning combination since their PGL Bucharest title in 2025. Their best performance this year has been a second-place finish behind Natus Vincere at the ESL Pro League Season 23 Finals, but they've mostly dropped out in the quarter-finals.

Even at DraculaN Season 6, a tournament where they were the favorites and were invited straight into the playoffs, they lost to M80 and Alliance for a 5-6th place finish in the double-elimination bracket.

Their only non-playoff finish at an MVP event was IEM Rio, where they finished in 7-8th place after losses to Natus Vincere and MOUZ.

FL4MUS: "We've been leveling up our game"

vs.

We sat down with BetBoom's stand-in, Timur "⁠FL4MUS⁠" Marev, at the media day ahead of the playoffs, where he told us about the journey the team has been on since the first stage of the Major.

"The only thing that can help us that we can take from those three stages is the way we've been leveling up our game," he told HLTV. "We have lots of trust in each other."

"We have the chance to beat them, they have a chance to beat us," he said of the upcoming playoff match. "We don't know what life is going to bring us, how we're going to feel, or how we're going to play. Everything is going to depend on us, and we will see on the 18th."
