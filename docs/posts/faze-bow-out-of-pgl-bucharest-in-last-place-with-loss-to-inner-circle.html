FaZe bow out of PGL Bucharest in last place with loss to Inner Circle

FaZe's misery continues as they exit the Romanian tournament with a 0-3 record.

FaZe have been eliminated from PGL Bucharest in 15th-16h place following a 2-0 defeat to Inner Circle in yet another blow to the team after it missed out on a spot at the Cologne Major.

Down 0-2 in the Swiss stage after forfeiting their first two matches in Bucharest to play at HLC Belgrade PRO, FaZe had no margin for error heading into the series.

A sluggish start on Nuke cost them their map pick of Nuke, and then they couldn't capitalize on a 6-0 lead on Mirage after another woeful offensive showing.
