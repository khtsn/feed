B8 beat The MongolZ to make CAC playoffs

B8 have secured a 2-1 victory over The MongolZ to lock in their spot in the CS Asia Championship playoffs.

The victory nets them a quarter-final spot at a minimum, with Andrii "⁠npl⁠" Kukharskyi and company now set to face MIBR for a top-four spot. The MongolZ, meanwhile, drop to the lower bracket to face the winner of 3DMAX vs. Liquid.

The match was a cagy affair, with the first two maps ending in 13-11 scorelines. B8 took their Ancient pick thanks to a comeback from 7-10 down before The MongolZ answered with on Nuke.

npl took over on the final map of the series with a 2.05 rating, 108.6 ADR, and a +11.62% round swing as B8 dominated the CT side with a 9-1 scoreline to secure a playoff spot in Shanghai.
