With three maps in the series, both teams got to play their map picks before it all came down to the series' decider map. Team Secret's first pick of Breeze went 13-5 in their favor, off the back of a great Jeremy " Jremy " Cabrera Jett performance. Gen.G won the second map, their map pick of Lotus, 13-9, with Kim " Meteor " Tae-o top fragging on the Killjoy to secure a near perfect attack half to come back down 8-4. With this win in the three-team round-robin that is the Pacific Kickoff play-ins, this puts Gen.G one win off a playoff spot, as a team with two wins will get that spot guaranteed. However, with the design of the round-robin, there is potential for map score to determine who makes it in, if every team ends with one win and one loss.
