With how quickly Stage 1 is going for China, with more matches continuing on Tuesday of week two, each team has already played two matches. The two groups for the start of Stage 1 are divided like so: Group Alpha consists of TYLOO, BLG, FPX, JDG, AG, and TEC, while Group Omega includes EDG, XLG, Trace, DRG, Wolves, and Nova.

The two strongest teams in the first week of Stage 1 both come from Group Omega: EDG and XLG. After making another active roster move, Jieni7 and EDG stomped over both Nova and Wolves. XLG also made waves after sweeping Ascension rivals DRG and defeating a retooled Trace team. TYLOO ended the first week as the only undefeated team in Group Alpha, but will need to keep the momentum going to face regional powerhouses like EDG and XLG.

In this first week of Stage 1, plenty of players stepped up. One of the most recent star performances was nephh , who near single-handedly earned BLG a sweep over JDG. After a loss in his first match against TYLOO, nephh turned around to finish game two with a 1.98 match rating, 45 kills, 304 ACS, and 92% KAST. As for the best player across both games and the player of the week in China, the regional consistency of ZmjjKK has yet to waver. He leads the region in ACS with 269.3, K:D with 1.64, ADR with 180.7, and KPR with 0.97.
