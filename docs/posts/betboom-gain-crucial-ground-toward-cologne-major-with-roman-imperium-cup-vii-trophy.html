BetBoom gain crucial ground toward Cologne Major with Roman Imperium Cup VII trophy

BetBoom have given life to the IEM Cologne Major hopes after beating BESTIA 2-0 in the grand final of Roman Imperium Cup VII.

The late-night match concluded close to 1AM local time after a gauntlet run of three best-of-three matches for Kirill "⁠Boombl4⁠" Mikhailov's men, who had barely scraped into the playoffs through Group A thanks to a one-round difference in head-to-head over Wildcard.

BetBoom ensured that effort didn't go to waste as they got revenge on SINNERS for knocking them out of BC Game Masters, scraped past G2 in a three-map series, and dispatched BESTIA in the single-elimination bracket to raise the champions' helmet.

The VRS points earned from the tournament victory have gone a long way in salvaging BetBoom's bid to qualify for the Major. It was the second of three LANs they are playing with Aleksandr "⁠zorte⁠" Zagodyrenko and Timur "⁠FL4MUS⁠" Marev as stand-ins due to visa issues affecting Artem "⁠ArtFr0st⁠" Kharitonov and Daniil "⁠d1Ledez⁠" Kustov, which had deflated the team's already difficult route to earning a Major berth.

But a third-place finish at BC Game Masters Championship, followed up by a trophy lift in Portugal, has seen BetBoom jump from outside of invite contention on our predicted list to 15th in Europe.

They can also take solace in their fate resting in their own hands with an appearance at Stake Ranked Episode 1 up next, where another deep run would go a long way in holding on to their spot.
