A complete rundown of the artists, albums, songs and videos that took home trophies at the 66th annual awards.

Feb. 4, 2024Updated 6:21 p.m. ET

The 2024 Grammys taking place Sunday night in Los Angeles honor recordings released from Oct. 1, 2022, through Sept. 15, 2023. SZA is the lead nominee, with nine nods for her album “SOS,” which topped the Billboard 200 for 10 straight weeks.

The R&B singer Victoria Monét and the indie rocker Phoebe Bridgers of boygenius both have seven, while Jon Batiste, boygenius, the Americana singer-songwriter Brandy Clark, Miley Cyrus, Billie Eilish, Olivia Rodrigo and Taylor Swift have six nods apiece.
