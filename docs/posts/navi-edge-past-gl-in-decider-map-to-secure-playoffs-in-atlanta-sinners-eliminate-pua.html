NAVI edge past GL in decider map to secure playoffs in Atlanta; SINNERS eliminate PUA

Natus Vincere have secured a spot in the IEM Atlanta playoffs after defeating GamerLegion, and will have a chance to go straight to the semi-finals in the Group B upper bracket final.

Natus Vincere flew out of the gate against GamerLegion with a vintage performance by Valeriy "⁠b1t⁠" Vakhovskiy, who ended the 13-4 Inferno with a 1.80 rating, followed by 1.49 and 1.48-rated performances by Aleksi "⁠Aleksib⁠" Virolainen and Ihor "⁠w0nderful⁠" Zhdanov in what looked like it would be a wash for the world's number two team.

Ancient, usually Drin "⁠makazze⁠" Shaqiri's playground, quickly became a nightmare for Natus Vincere, as GamerLegion went on a rampage, winning 11 rounds in the half and upending expectations after the first map. After securing the second pistols and the follow-up to make it a quick 1-13, the series was bound for Mirage.

Finally, the decider delivered a tight exciting map. A 6-2 start put Natus Vincere in the driver's seat, but four straight defenses by GamerLegion brought it right back to a stalemate at the half.

Neither team could settle, as back-and-forth rounds brought the game to 11-10 in favor of Natus Vincere, after which they doubled up to hit match point and, after once again maps going to both teams, they could breathe a sigh of relief by closing it in regulation, 13-11.
