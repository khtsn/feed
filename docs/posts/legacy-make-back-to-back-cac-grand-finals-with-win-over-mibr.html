Legacy make back-to-back CAC grand finals with win over MIBR

Legacy are still on course to defend their title, but have a tough matchup against Falcons on the horizon.

Legacy have continued their defense of their CS Asia Championships title with a 2-0 win over MIBR, securing the win in the Brazilian derby with a fantastic comeback on Mirage before trouncing their opponents on Inferno.

As a result Legacy can look forward to a grand final matchup against Falcons, whilst MIBR will be left to fight for third place against MOUZ.

After the relatively recent addition of Andrei "⁠arT⁠" Piovezan Legacy instantly impressed with a run to the playoffs of ESL Pro League, but then showed some struggles at PGL Bucharest and IEM Rio, suggesting a honeymoon effect may have been at play.

They returned to Brazil to win a domestic title at BetBoom Rush B Summit and it seemed to renew them, as they followed that up with a run to third at IEM Atlanta and now the final of CAC.

MIBR were a tough semi-final opponent as they were the in-form team at CAC, four of their players sitting in the top five for rating ahead of the semi as they had decimated everyone in their path up to that point. Legacy rose the occasion magnificently and can now look forward to a bout with Falcons in the final.

MIBR raced out to a convincing lead on the CT side of Mirage, Felipe "⁠insani⁠" Yuji leading the way as they dominated Legacy. MIBR's proactive defensive approach gave them so much information and Legacy couldn't find a way to adjust, leading to a 9-3 lead at the half.

insani continues MIBR's early domination with 4K

The script was a completely flipped in the second half, MIBR and insani falling off as Legacy grew into the map. Guilherme "⁠saadzin⁠" Pacheco and Eduardo "⁠dumau⁠" Wolkmer began to come to life as MIBR's one-dimensional offense fell flat time and time again, pushing the game all the way to OT.

Overtime was a straightforward affair for Legacy as they ran with their momentum, ensuring a 1-0 series lead heading into Inferno.

latto's hero AK gives Legacy series point

Legacy made sure of the victory convincingly on Inferno, putting MIBR to the sword in ruthless fashion. saadzin continued his excellent form into Inferno as Bruno "⁠latto⁠" Rebelatto rose to the top of the scoreboard alongside him, their defensive efforts limiting MIBR to a clutch and desperate pistol buy for their only T round wins.

A necessary pistol win greeted MIBR on their CT side to give them some hope, but a trademark arT B rush spelled the beginning of the end, Legacy cruising through to a 13-5 win to seal their grand final berth.
