VP transfer list FL1T and fame

Evgenii "⁠FL1T⁠" Lebedev and Petr "⁠fame⁠" Bolyshev have been benched by Virtus.pro after the team's disastrous start to 2026.

"After discussions, the club's management concluded that changes to the roster were necessary," the team's statement said. "The club is open to considering transfer offers for both players."

FL1T and fame (who is still just 22) were crucial in Virtus.pro's run at the IEM Rio Major, when they competed as Outsiders, and have been part of the furniture at the team since 2022. FL1T was in the team for even longer than fame, joining in October 2021.

The news follows Ilya "⁠Perfecto⁠" Zalutskiy's benching on February 4, when the organization announced a "temporary pause" in order to find an "optimal" lineup.

The triple benching means the team lose their VRS ranking, but few tears will be lost for the 981 points that had the Russian organization sat 93rd in the world.
