Six top ten teams decline invites to BLAST Rivals Fort Worth

Eight teams have accepted invitations to BLAST Rivals 2026 Season 1, including Vitality and FURIA.

But the level of competition drops rapidly after those two, with six of the top nine teams in the global VRS declining invitations.

Those teams to decline were MOUZ, PARIVISION, Falcons, Spirit, Aurora, and The MongolZ — though Natus Vincere, who were eighth in the relevant VRS, did accept an invite.
