Inferno's Graveyard barred and Balcony extended in new CS update

Valve has made key changes to Inferno's A Site with the latest update to Counter-Strike 2. Entry to Graveyard has been barred, the wall above Pit has been raised, and the Balcony has been extended to replace the previously existing overhang.

Some clipping adjustments were also made to the small window in Alt Mid, items listed on the community market for sale can now still be used, and players can now set a max value for the Arms Dealer to offer through Genesis Terminals.

The complete patch notes can be found below:

[MISC]

  • Starting today, items listed for sale on Steam Community Market will remain in your inventory for use while they are listed (e.g., your weapon can be equipped in your loadout while it is listed). While listed, items cannot be consumed or modified. You can cancel your listings at any time.

  • Added ability to set your max offer limit for items in the Terminal, and the Arms Dealer will only show offers up to that limit.

  • Fixed a slight tilt in the chicken running animation.
