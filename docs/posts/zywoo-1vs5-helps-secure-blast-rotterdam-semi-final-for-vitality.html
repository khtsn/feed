ZywOo 1vs5 helps secure BLAST Rotterdam semi-final for Vitality

Vitality got the better of PARIVISION in the Group B upper-bracket final of BLAST Open Rotterdam with a 2-0 scoreline (Mirage 13-9, Inferno 16-12) to qualify for the semi-finals in the Netherlands.

The series began on Mirage, where a red-hot Mathieu "⁠ZywOo⁠" Herbaut powered Vitality to an early lead before PARIVISION stabilised and came back into the game, aided by some impressive individual moments and Andrey "⁠BELCHONOKK⁠" Yasinskiy's 1vs2 clutch.

Dzhami "⁠Jame⁠" Ali's troops even found themselves in the lead late on, but Vitality's imperious nature shone through at the dying moment, with William "⁠mezii⁠" Merriman clutching a 1vs2 with low HP to put the final nail in the PARIVISION coffin.

Inferno was an even closer affair, but Vitality got off to another slow start. They recovered to take an 8-6 lead before the game descended into chaos, with a plethora of consecutive clutches putting the game on a knife-edge. Robin "⁠ropz⁠" Kool's 1vs2 broke PARIVISION's economy and looked to be the deciding moment of the game, but the Russian squad recovered to secure overtime.

They couldn't shake off what happened next, however, as ZywOo found a mind-boggling 1vs5 that broke PARIVISION's spirits and netted Vitality a semi-final spot.
