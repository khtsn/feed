PGL Cluj-Napoca playoff bracket set

The group stage of PGL Cluj-Napoca has now concluded with eight teams earning their place in the playoffs, set to begin on February 20 in the BT Arena.

The first quarter-final clash will be contested between Falcons and PARIVISION, who recently met in the BLAST Bounty S1 Finals, where Dzhami "⁠Jame⁠" Ali's troops got the better of the Maxim "⁠kyousuke⁠" Lukin-less Falcons in a best-of-five final.

MOUZ vs. Natus Vincere will come next, with the latter able to count on a raucous support for their favorite Mihai "⁠iM⁠" Ivan, who will finally appear in front of his home crowd after Natus Vincere declined their invitation to the tournament last year.

Vitality will then meet Aurora in what will be a rematch of their hard-fought IEM Krakow clash, where the teams met on the unfamiliar battleground of Ancient that was eventually decided by four overtimes.

Last but not least is FURIA vs. The MongolZ, a match that played out eight times in the last 12 months, with Gabriel "⁠FalleN⁠" Toledo and company enjoying a 5-3 record against the Mongolian squad in that time frame.
