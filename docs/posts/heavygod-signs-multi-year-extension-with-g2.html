HeavyGod signs multi-year extension with G2

Nikita "⁠HeavyGod⁠" Martynenko has inked a multi-year contract extension with G2, the German organization announced on Wednesday.

The 23-year-old signed for G2 in January 2025 in the wake of Nikola "⁠NiKo⁠" Kovač's departure to Falcons, and has averaged a 1.15 rating over 280 maps during his tenure while playing as a dedicated anchor.

The team has been a stable presence at most Big Events ever since his addition, usually reaching playoffs or falling just short in the final group stage matches.

Trophies have been harder to come by amidst a year-and-a-half dominated largely by Vitality, but the team still mustered a title-winning campaign at BLAST Open London 2025, where G2 beat Dan "⁠apEX⁠" Madesclaire's men 3-2 in the grand final and HeavyGod was named MVP, as well as a trophy lift at Stake Ranked Episode 1 shortly after Guy "⁠NertZ⁠" Iluz's signing.
