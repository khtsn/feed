Lynn Vision win Yuqilin PoB S3 as TYLOO forfeit final over medical issues

Lynn Vision won the Yuqilin Pinnacle of Battle Season 3 in Shanghai on Sunday with a walkover victory against TYLOO, who forfeited the best-of-five final only a few rounds into the first map due to medical issues.

It was the third match of the day for TYLOO, who had earlier defeated Chinggis Warriors and FlyQuest to win the XSE GangKui Cup Season 2 Finals, which were also held in Shanghai.

TYLOO took to the server against Lynn Vision with head coach WeiJie "⁠zhokiNg⁠" Zhong as a stand-in for Yi "⁠JamYoung⁠" Yang, who has been feeling unwell for several days, HLTV understands.

Qianhao "⁠Moseyuh⁠" Chen then also complained of feeling unwell and had to be carried off by medical staff, prompting TYLOO to forfeit the series only seven rounds into Nuke.

"Due to two players experiencing serious physical discomfort, including persistent coughing and chest tightness," TYLOO later wrote in a statement posted on social media.

"After an immediate evaluation and examination by the team doctor, it was confirmed that their current physical condition no longer allows them to continue participating in matches. With the highest priority placed on the health and safety of our players, and after careful communication with the tournament organizers, the club has ultimately decided to withdraw from the finals.

"We will continue to monitor the players' recovery, provide full support and necessary adjustments, and return to the competition in better form."

This was the third forfeit win for Lynn Vision in the tournament. Rare Atom forfeited the upper semi-final match following a dispute with the tournament organizer over the rules about lateness penalties, while TYLOO forfeited the upper bracket final due to a scheduling clash with the XSE GangKui Cup S2.

"We sincerely regret that the grand final could not be presented to the audience as anticipated, and we thank all participating teams, partners, and viewers for their understanding and support of Yuqilin POB S3," the organizer of Yuqilin PoB S3 said in a statement.

The tournament victory pushed Lynn Vision into third place in the Asia VRS, only behind The MongolZ and TYLOO. Zhe "⁠Westmelon⁠" Niu's troops were qualified for the Major even before the upper bracket final following the eliminations of The Huns and SemperFi from XSE GangKui Cup S2.
