Spirit vs G2 thriller sets new CS2 viewership record

The IEM Cologne quarter-final bout between Spirit and G2 had a peak viewership of 2,044,763, according to data firm Esports Charts.

The German event enters the top three Counter-Strike tournaments with the highest peak viewership, only behind CS:GO's PGL Major Stockholm (2,748,434) and PGL Major Antwerp (2,113,610).

The five-hour affair becomes the first non-grand final match to crack 2 million concurrent viewers — a milestone reached during the decider map of Mirage, a nail-biting 47-round affair full of incredible moments.

IEM Cologne has now dethroned PGL Copenhagen Major's CS2 record of 1,853,954 concurrent viewers, set during the grand final between Natus Vincere and FaZe.

Below you can find the top 5 Counter-Strike events with the highest peak viewership:

  • PGL Major Stockholm (CS:GO): 2,748,434

  • PGL Major Antwerp (CS:GO): 2,113,610

  • IEM Cologne (CS2): 2,044,763

  • PGL Major Copenhagen (CS2): 1,853,954

  • BLAST Austin Major (CS2): 1,789,038
