Sirhan Sirhan, RFK's assassin, has been denied parole

By Taylor Romine, CNN

Published 6:33 PM EST, Wed March 1, 2023

CNN  — 

Sirhan Sirhan, the man convicted of assassinating Sen. Robert F. Kennedy in 1968, was denied parole Wednesday for three years, the California Department of Corrections and Rehabilitation told CNN.
