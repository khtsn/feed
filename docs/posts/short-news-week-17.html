Short news: Week 17

In a LinkedIn post, Vitality chairman and co-founder Fabien "Neo" Devide has announced that he has taken on the role of CEO.

He replaces Nicolas Maurer, who transitions to a Chief Strategy Officer role within the French organization.

"By becoming Chief Strategy Officer (CSO), he is stepping back from day-to-day operations to dedicate himself to the evolution of our business model and our new developments," Neo wrote. "Our bond as co-founders remains unbreakable, but our roles are now clearly defined to make Vitality even stronger.

"In taking over as CEO, my objective is clear: to put our competitive DNA back at the heart of everything we do, with an absolute focus on the sporting performance of our teams, while continuing to inspire our community around the world."
