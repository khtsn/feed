Short: First update to Cache sees bomb explosion radius increased alongside minor tweaks

Valve has made its first tweaks to Cache after its release with a minor update, including an increase in the bomb's explosion radius and damage after a reddit post highlighted how easy it was to survive near the bomb even without armor.

A way to self-boost to Shroud was removed and clipping adjustments were made among several other minor tweaks. A "speculative fix" for a bug that caused all audio to drop out has also been shipped.
