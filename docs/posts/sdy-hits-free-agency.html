sdy hits free agency

ENCE and Viktor "⁠sdy⁠" Orudzhev have parted ways after the expiration of his contract, the Finnish organization announced on Tuesday.

The 29-year-old had been benched in the squad since March, when the organization decided to transfer-list its international roster to focus on a Finnish lineup moving forward, promoting their academy team in the process.

He was then loaned to Passion UA in a move that only lasted a few weeks before the organization decided to withdraw from the season early amid reports of a search for a new home for its players.
