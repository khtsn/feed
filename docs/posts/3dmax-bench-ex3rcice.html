3DMAX bench Ex3rcice

3DMAX have announced the benching of Pierre "⁠Ex3rcice⁠" Bulinge from their Counter-Strike roster.

The 26-year-old had been one of the last two standing members of the 2021 DBL PONEY squad from which the French roster originates before it signed with HEET in 2022 and then 3DMAX in late 2023.

His removal is the second change in the playing roster since the beginning of the year, following the removal of Alexandre "⁠bodyy⁠" Pianaro in favor of Kévin "⁠misutaaa⁠" Rabier in February.

It comes in the aftermath of a difficult period for the squad, which is now ranked 30th on the Valve Regional Standings after it missed out on a spot at the IEM Cologne Major due to early exits at IEM Krakow, PGL Cluj-Napoca and ESL Pro League Season 23 to start the year.

The French side is expected to announce Ex3rcice's replacement in the coming days, as it is set to travel to China at the end of June for the $1 million XSE Pro League tournament.

3DMAX's roster is down to:

Bryan "⁠Maka⁠" Canda

Damien "⁠wasiNk⁠" Dufour (coach)

Pierre "⁠Ex3rcice⁠" Bulinge (benched)
