Team Secret bid DetonatioN farewell with 2-0 win

Team Secret eliminate DetonatioN from Pacific Kickoff with a 2-0 victory. Team Secret will now go against the loser of DRX vs Talon for a chance at qualifying to the play-in.

Team Secret quickly set the pace for the match on Lotus, winning their opponent's map pick with a decisive 13-7. The match continued on Breeze, where Team Secret won 13-11.

This match continues DetonatioN's winless streak, extended to 13 losses in official VCT matches. Including OFF//SEASON and non-partnership events, the Japanese org currently has a 20 match period without a win, starting from June 2022.

The elimination means DetonatioN will not see play until April, the beginning of the 2024 Pacific League.
