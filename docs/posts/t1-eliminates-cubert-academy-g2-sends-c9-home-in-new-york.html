The sole map for this matchup between two VCT Americas teams was Haven, with C9 starting on the attack side. Both teams ran double-Controller, with C9 running Neon while G2 ran Yoru. C9 won the first pistol and antieco without much worry, but jawgemo prevented the bonus round win. The slow pace from C9 led to some struggles from G2, with jawgemo as the sole standout on the defense side. The first half ended up 8-4 for C9, with G2 needing to improve on the attack side.

C9 won the second pistol on Haven to take a larger lead, but G2 pulled out the classic Stinger buy to convert an eco. That moment was a turning point, as G2 took that economic advantage to go on a run and tie the map 9-9, with jawgemo getting an ace and valyn stepping up in both calls and shots.

It was v1c shooting two through a smoke to get the first round win, but jawgemo ended the map hot to win the map 13-11. G2 stay alive at the Red Bull Home Ground, while C9 are eliminated.
