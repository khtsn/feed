Only three top 10 teams accept invites to PGL Bucharest

PGL has announced the invites for PGL Bucharest, a $1,250,000 tournament in the organizer's studios in Romania scheduled for April 3-11.

FaZe, PARIVISION, and The MongolZ were the only top 10 teams, based on the February 2 ranking, to accept invites to the event, with the rest of the field prioritizing IEM Rio the following week from April 13-19. B8, Legacy, and 3DMAX are the only teams that will attend both tournaments.

M80 and BC.Game benefited from the large number of teams that declined, securing the last two direct invites from 21st and 22nd on the ranking, respectively. FUT, after impressing at IEM Krakow and PGL Cluj-Napoca, will also have a chance to continue cementing their status as regulars in the tier-one circuit.

Closed Qualifier invites for the four regional qualifiers were also announced, with Europe's featuring BetBoom and EYEBALLERS and South America's featuring MIBR and Imperial.
