IEM Atlanta 2026 quarter-finals as they happened

Shortly after his team's win over paiN in the quarter-finals of IEM Atlanta, HLTV caught up with GamerLegion AWPer Milan "⁠hypex⁠" Polowiec to discuss the series, his individual form, and the upcoming match against Legacy.

On making the semi-finals: "I'm feeling great. Before the game, I knew we were gonna win, and we won, so hopefully the same mentality tomorrow. It's definitely going to be a harder game, but we're here to show what we can do, and I'm hoping we're going to do the same as today."

On having the support of the American crowd: "Yeah, we have Snax, and everyone just loves Snax. It was insane, and hopefully, they will bring the same energy tomorrow. I feel confident on stage. I think I showed it today, and I'm hopefully going to show it again tomorrow, and hopefully again on Sunday."

On his strong individual performance this event: "I'm slowly finding myself, I think I'm doing better and better all the time. Last event was a bit shaky, I improved since then, rewatched everything, knew what to focus on, and I think it's working well for me for now."

On facing Legacy in the semi-finals: "It's going to be pretty hard. They're a very good team, I think. They're definitely gonna be loud, in our face. I'm just hoping we're gonna be able to prove ourselves and go knock them out."
