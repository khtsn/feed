Nova benches SWERL, moves to all Chinese roster

Nova Esports Nova Esports China Rank #5 o0o0o Ye Xiaodong (叶晓东) OBONE Chen Yijie (陈怡洁) CB Wang Qingchuan (王庆川) Ezeir Zhao Zejun (赵泽骏) GuanG Guang Honglin (光洪霖) has unveiled their roster for Stage 1 of VCT CN. Ali "SWERL" Kobraee has moved to the substitute role, with Chen "OBONE" Yijie (陈怡洁) taking his place on the starting roster.

SWERL joined Nova as an Australian import ahead of Stage 1 of 2024, bringing firepower to the duelist role. Nova was a mid-table team in China throughout 2024, but stuck with their roster going into 2025. The offseason showed promise for the team, during which they found a second-place finish at both the WALL-E Cup and the Superb Cup. However, when Kickoff rolled around, Nova went 2-2, and were eliminated in a stomp at the hands of Dragon Ranger Gaming Dragon Ranger Gaming China Rank #7 Flex1n Lo Rui (罗瑞) vo0kashu Ilya Ushakov Cangshu Zheng Yujun (手郑宇峻) Nicc Shao Yi-qun (邵逸群) SpiritZ1 Lin Dingyu (林定宇) .

Failing to make Masters Bangkok, Nova decided to experiment in the consolation China Evolution Series tournament. After riding the bench since Stage 2 of 2024, OBONE was given another chance on the starting roster in place of SWERL. With their new fifth man, Nova swept the tournament, rolling through the likes of FunPlus Phoenix FunPlus Phoenix China Rank #9 Shr1mp Yang Yong (杨勇) Autumn Kale Dunne Life Qu Donghao (瞿东豪) AAAAY Zhang Yang (张杨) yosemite Wang Lei (王磊) and Bilibili Gaming Bilibili Gaming China Rank #6 whzy Wang Haozhe (王昊哲) Levius Lu Yinzhong (卢胤中) rushia Wang Xiaojie (王晓杰) nephh Marcus Tan Knight Liu Yuxiang (刘宇翔) before dominating Wolves Esports Wolves Esports China Rank #4 SiuFatBB Pong Gaa Hei (庞加曦) Yuicaw Huang Yung-chieh (黃湧傑) Spring Liu Jiunting (刘俊霆) Lysoar Liang Youhao (梁优好) Juicy Tyler James Aeria in the grand final.

A fresh taste of victory for Nova.

After winning the tournament, CB commented that the team was previously being held back by communication issues that they experienced when playing with SWERL. He felt that their win affirmed that switching to a full Chinese roster was the correct decision.
