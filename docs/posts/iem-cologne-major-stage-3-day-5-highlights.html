Aleksib: "I've never had a feeling for a Major harder than this one"

Speaking to Tres "⁠stunna⁠" Saranthus after the match, Aleksi "⁠Aleksib⁠" Virolainen did not hide his disappointment at his team's inability to close out matches against Falcons and G2.

"I've never had a feeling for a Major harder than this one. We came from Atlanta, we beat the team we really wanted to beat all season. We had chances to go to the playoffs against Falcons yesterday, today against G2. It wasn't meant to be. It sucks. It hurts."
