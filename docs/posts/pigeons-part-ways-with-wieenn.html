Pigeons part ways with wieenN

The former Imperial Valkyries core have opted for a change.

tory's side has made a change after winning JB Pro League Female Season 1

Pigeons have parted ways with Georgian rifler Narmina "⁠wieenN⁠" Gasanova, marking an end to her stint alongside the core of Imperial Valkyries.

wieenN joined forces with Victoria "⁠tory⁠" Kazieva, Alexandra "⁠twenty3⁠" Timonina, and Katarína "⁠Kat⁠" Vašková on Imperial Valkyries in July 2025, replacing the outgoing Zainab "⁠zAAz⁠" Turkie. wieenN played a variety of online competitions with the team throughout 2025, and made two LAN appearances at Female Pro League Season 1 Finals and ESL Impact League Season 8 Finals before the team shifted to the Pigeons tag upon the expiration of their contracts at the end of the year.

This change comes not long after the orgless side's winning campaign through JB Pro League Female Season 1 in early February, where wieenN topped the online event with a 1.29 rating across 11 maps en route to Pigeons' victory.
