cb joined EDG this past offseason, replacing Jieni7 in the team's starting lineup as part of a an offseason rework in the coaching booth. cb on stage with EDG at Masters Santiago. (Photo by Riot Games)

Initially, cb joined as the team's Yoru specialist, looking to address KangKang's struggles on the agent over the past year. As China Kickoff ran on, cb's agent pool expanded to include other Duelists, such as Phoenix and Neon. EDG qualified for Masters Santiago as China's third seed, but was an early exit, going 0-2 in the Swiss Stage.

cb has been out of action for EDG since. He was replaced by Jieni7, who he initially replaced, in Stage 1, with the secondary Duelist role designated to him and Smoggy . The team returned to the top of China in Stage 1, qualifying for Masters London, where they finished third. While EDG continues forward without him, cb's EDG career comes to a close after competing in just two events.
