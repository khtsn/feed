FUT stay alive in Cologne at B8's expense

FUT got their first victory of the IEM Cologne Major Stage 3 after getting the better of B8 in a three-map series (Dust2 10-13, Mirage 13-9, Ancient 13-11).

The clash was a rematch of their Stage 2 clash, where FUT began their Major campaign with a victory.

In Stage 3, however, FUT were dealt a tough run by losing to Vitality and G2 in three-map series, but have now avoided disappointment by securing their first victory of the stage.

The series was a nervy affair, with FUT not looking like themselves early on. A 10-13 loss on Dust2 was followed up by a slow start on Mirage, where they found themselves 1-4 down, but the team of rookies finally found their groove after that.

Dmytro "⁠dem0n⁠" Myroshnychenko led the charge in a 13-9 win on Mirage and kept up his impact on Ancient, winning a crucial 1vs3 early on to give his team a platform for a 12-6 lead.

While FUT had issues closing it out, they got the job done in the final round of regulation to extend their Major for another day.
