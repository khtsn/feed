The MongolZ send B8 to 0-2 pool

The MongolZ have beaten B8 to move into the 1-1 pool at IEM Cologne.

After finally having time to practice before flying to Germany, the world number 9 is looking to make their fourth Major playoffs in a row. They were dealt a blow on day one however, when BetBoom took them down 2-1.

A 0-1 matchup against B8 therefore became about recovery, and they looked to be easing their way past the Ukrainian side after Usukhbayar "⁠910⁠" Banzragch started Mirage 12-0 en route to a 10-2 half.

Instead, their T side was just as dire as B8's had been. After a smart Danylo "⁠s1zzi⁠" Vinnyk ace, the game went to overtime, with both teams' defaults and mid control protocols in tatters.

s1zzi aces late on Mirage

In the end, one T side round in overtime was enough, after a simple A smokes execute and Ayush "⁠mzinho⁠" Batbold 3k, as The MongolZ won all three CT rounds to win 16-14.

Regardless, teams with stronger Mirages — B8 have now lost five in a row on the map — will have been licking their lips watching The MongolZ come undone in Middle time after time.

Overpass also saw a fast start from The MongolZ but on the opposite side. When they led 3-0, they had already doubled their T side total for the series, and they went on to take a 9-3 lead on what is supposed to be the far more difficult side.

An Anarbileg "⁠cobrazera⁠" Uuganbayar triple then made it 10-3, and the mistakes of Mirage were not repeated as they closed it out 13-3 and 2-0.
