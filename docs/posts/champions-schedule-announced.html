Champions schedule announced

Following the group stage reveal just three days prior, Riot Games has announced the schedule for Valorant Champions 2023.

Taking place over nearly the entirety of August, the group stage will go on from August 6 to August 13 inside Los Angeles' Shrine Auditorium. Two dark days will follow on the 14th and 15th after groups see completion.

Playoffs kicks off from August 16 to August 26 with an eight team double-elimination bracket, with the first segment of Playoffs lasting until the 20th. Another set of dark days will follow after the conclusion of the first two upper and lower bracket rounds, lasting from August 21 to August 23.

The final three days of play will include the upper and lower semifinal, lower final, and grand final, beginning August 24. Each of these matches will take place in the Kia Forum, the second venue for Champions.
