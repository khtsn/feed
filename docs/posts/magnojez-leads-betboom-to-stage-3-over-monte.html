Magnojez leads BetBoom to Stage 3 over Monte

BetBoom became the next team to qualify for the IEM Cologne Major Stage 3 after getting the better of Monte with a 2-1 scoreline.

The match began on Nuke, where BetBoom came back from a 2-6 deficit to win 13-7, with Kirill "⁠Magnojez⁠" Rodnov (1.82 map rating) and Timur "⁠FL4MUS⁠" Marev (1.69) leading the way for their squad, before Monte answered with a confident win on their Mirage pick.

The Dust2 decider proved to be the closest map in the series, as neither team could build a sizable lead early on. Magnojez continued to frag, however, and his impact proved just enough for BetBoom's 13-8 victory.

The Russian rifler put up 25 kills on the decider and a 1.47 rating overall, finishing the series 0.24 rating ahead of his closest teammate.

Monte aren't eliminated after the loss, as they are set to fight for a Stage 3 spot on Tuesday.
