Digital Crusade confirm $150k 'Super DraculaN'

Digital Crusade have announced a bumper $150,000 prize pool for DraculaN Season 7, dubbed 'Super DraculaN' by the Romanian organizer.

The event will take place from June 23-28, right after the Cologne Major, and offers a hefty chunk of VRS points to any team willing to sacrifice the tournament break for them.

XSE Pro League and Stake Ranked Episode 3 will also take place over the nominal 'tournament break' this summer.
