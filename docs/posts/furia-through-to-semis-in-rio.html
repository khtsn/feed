FURIA through to semis in Rio

FURIA have secured the second direct semi-final berth at IEM Rio after a swift 2-0 victory over MOUZ.

The home team dominated the opposition on Mirage and Dust2 both individually and through superb calling from Gabriel "⁠FalleN⁠" Toledo.

Ludvig "⁠Brollan⁠" Brolin's team only won five gunrounds over the course of the two maps, only making Dust2 look competitive thanks to both pistols and conversions.

FURIA head into the playoffs with an extra day to rest and prepare to turn out in front of their home crowd, but their campaign is set to be a difficult one nonetheless.

After Falcons ended Vitality's win streak, the world's best team fell on the Brazilian team's side of the bracket, as did Natus Vincere, whom FURIA just narrowly beat earlier in the group stage after a comeback from 3-9 down on the Nuke decider.
