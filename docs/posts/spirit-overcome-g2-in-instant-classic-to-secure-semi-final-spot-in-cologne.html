Spirit overcome G2 in instant classic to secure semi-final spot in Cologne

Spirit beat G2 in the IEM Cologne quarter-final with a 2-1 scoreline (Overpass 9-13, Dust2 16-14, Mirage 25-22) to secure a top-four spot after an amazing series of Counter-Strike.

The series began on Overpass, a map that Martin "⁠STYKO⁠" Styk described as a "must-win" on the analyst desk, and Nemanja "⁠huNter-⁠" Kovač's troops delivered a very respectable G2 T-side that ended with a 5-7 scoreline. A pistol round win and Nikita "⁠HeavyGod⁠" Martynenko's 1vs2 gave them a solid start, but a lack of entries — just three in the entire half, meant they went into the break with a small deficit.

G2 then came out on top in the force-buys on their defensive half, which helped them cruise to a relatively comfortable 13-9 victory.

HeavyGod continued his rampage on Dust2, starting the map with a crisp 4k on the B defense that let G2 go up 4-0. While Spirit equaled the score thanks to some long-awaited impact by Danil "⁠donk⁠" Kryshkovets, G2's individuals took turns in taking over and neutralized Spirit's biggest threat.

huNter-, Álvaro "⁠SunPayus⁠" García, and HeavyGod all had stand-out individual moments that delivered rounds on their own, with the former's 1vs1 clutch eventually forcing overtime. Highlight kept coming in added time too, before donk's 1vs2 secured the map for Spirit despite huNter-'s heroic efforts to turn the 3vs5.

On the Mirage decider, however, a new individual took over the game. Matúš "⁠MATYS⁠" Šimko put up 17 kills and 122 ADR to lead G2's offensive half to an 8-4 lead, providing a total of four multi-kills and six openers in the half.

A lengthy tech pause followed due to HeavyGod's game crash, but the high-octane nature of the game went on as the game unpaused. A winner couldn't be decided in regulation after Spirit won three in a row to force overtime, where MATYS found another highlight with an amazing 1vs2 clutch.

The LANXESS crowd was then treated to four overtimes, featuring a one-in-a-million Boris "⁠magixx⁠" Vorobiev 1vs4, but Spirit eventually got the job done with a 25-22 after an amazing game of CS.
