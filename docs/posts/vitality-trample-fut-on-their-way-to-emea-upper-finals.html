This win marks just the second victory the bees find over FUT, a rivalry that dates back to 2022's tier 2 VRL Finals. The Turkish squad has almost always come out on top: one of Vitality's two wins over FUT came just last year, during the third week of Stage 1. Their second win was today, and it was dominant.

Are you not entertained? (Photo by Wojciech Wandzel/Riot Games)

Vitality only gave FUT an edge when they attacked on Bind, FUT's pick. The bees were already 9-3 up by then and, while they dropped four more rounds, a timeout put them right back into the game and threw them to a 13-7 win. It was a crucial victory for Vitality — who barely lost rounds where they held a number advantage — as the next map was their pick.

On Split, there was absolutely no breathing room for the Turkish squad. It didn't matter where FUT attacked or defended, basically nothing worked. Vitality ran them over and took a 13-4 win on their pick.

xeus was a key member of keeping FUT in contention on Bind. The newest addition to the Turkish roster was seemingly at the right place and the right time in most rounds, adding up to 6 multikills, tied for the most with Derke , but that effort was not enough.

Vitality's camp showed a strong contrast to FUT on the first map, as no one single player seemed head and shoulders above the rest. The highlights go to Derke's six entry frags across 17 kills, as well as Kicks ' performance, worthy of a 1.42 rating and 179 ADR.

On Split, Derke led the charge for his team with a 3.0 KDR and 2.19 rating, accruing 27 kills and 278 ADR in one of the most individually dominant performances so far this season.

Most importantly, Vitality's dominating win sets them apart from the rest of the field. Despite other teams finding 2-0 wins so far, nobody has done so while losing as few rounds as Vitality did today.
