In a post on X, Ninjas in Pyjamas COO Grant Rousseau has shed light on the company's decision to move its operations to Abu Dhabi.

Swedish media outlet Expressen.se had reported that the organization had failed in its efforts to convince the Counter-Strike team to relocate to the UAE.

"[Neither] the CS2 team [nor] any other NIP teams have ever been asked to relocate and play from the UAE," he wrote.

"To be clear, we will always enable our teams to play from their desired location, and where the strongest practice environment is."
