Short: Media: NIP to move operations to UAE

A report by Expressen.se has said that Ninjas in Pyjamas are preparing to uproot and manage their operations from Abu Dhabi.

The Swedish office will remain as a bootcamp centre before events, the report suggests, but layoffs are likely. The organization already has a headquarters in the Emirates, where it has a five-year agreement with the government.

"This puts you at risk of being terminated from your role. No final decisions have been made," read an email reported by Expressen.

The report also states: "NIP has previously also tried to convince the players in the Counter-Strike team to move to the United Arab Emirates, but has received a categorical no."
