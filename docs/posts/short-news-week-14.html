1win clinched victory at MPKBK CIS LAN Season 4 in Moscow on Sunday after beating BET-M in the $50,000 tournament's grand final.

It is the first LAN trophy for 1win, who played the majority of the event without Aleksei "⁠Qikert⁠" Golubev. The Kazakhstani rifler was absent due to personal reasons, but returned to the lineup for the semis and title decider.

The event, despite boasting a large prize pool, only had Major implications for K27. The team had an outside chance to qualify, but had their hopes crushed after a loss to BET-M in the semi-finals and went on to lose the third-place decider to WW.
