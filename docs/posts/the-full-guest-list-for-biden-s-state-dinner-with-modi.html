More than 380 guests were invited, including government officials, business leaders, fashion designers and prominent Indian Americans.

By Chris Cameron

June 22, 2023, 7:00 p.m. ET

More than 380 guests were invited to the state dinner President Biden hosted for Prime Minister Narendra Modi of India on Thursday night, a list that included government officials, business leaders, star athletes, a renowned violinist, a Hollywood director, the son of a civil rights icon, fashion designers and prominent Indian Americans.
