The MongolZ shock Spirit to secure final Rotterdam playoffs berth

The MongolZ have beaten Spirit 2-0 in the final group stage match of BLAST Open Rotterdam, edging out victory on Nuke (13-11) and rallying from 1-7 down on Overpass (13-9) to earn a spot in the Ahoy Arena.

It is another shocking early exit for Spirit, who were sent packing from the quarter-finals of ESL Pro League Season 23 by Astralis less than two weeks ago, and a worrying sign for the team as it continues to show clear growing pains with Boris "⁠magixx⁠" Vorobiev and Myroslav "⁠zont1x⁠" Plakhotia back in tow.

That it happened despite Danil "⁠donk⁠" Kryshkovets having his best start to a Big Event map — he was, at one point, 17-1 on Overpass — is just as concerning, but credit must also go to The MongolZ for not crumbling and converting another big result with Anarbileg "⁠cobrazera⁠" Uuganbayar.

A triple kill in the pistol and quad kill against Spirit's force-buy were early precursors for what ended as a masterclass map from Ayush "⁠mzinho⁠" Batbold on Nuke, where The MongolZ went up 5-0 on the offense before multis from donk and Dmitry "⁠sh1ro⁠" Sokolov finally put Spirit on the board.

Communication on Spirit looked off at various points, and the disconnect meant their round wins were by no means comfortable. Further multi-kills from cobrazera extended his team's lead to 7-3, but Spirit were afforded some breathing room after a Lobby crunch converted a round with pistols and sh1ro added an AWP 4k to end the half.

A pistol win and a solid start to the offense then saw Spirit take the lead for the first time on the map, and they bounced back after getting shut out in two rounds to sit tantalizingly close to map point, 11-10. But even with their economy crushed, The MongolZ turned a round with MP9s in hand, and a triple from mzinho set his team on map point before they closed out the round after.

Spirit seemed set to bounce back with aplomb on Overpass, largely thanks to donk running rampant. The Russian phenom was up 17-1 and tallied multi-kills for the first five rounds straight, but the rest of Spirit struggled to rise around him.

A 1v3 clutch from Usukhbayar "⁠910⁠" Banzragch and quad kill by cobrazera proved the turning point for The MongolZ as they rallied from 1-7 down to post five at the break, and a force-buy victory after losing the pistol in the second half kicked off a dominant charge toward victory as they gave up just one more round before closing out the map.
