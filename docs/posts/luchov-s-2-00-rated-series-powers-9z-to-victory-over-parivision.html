luchov's 2.00-rated series powers 9z to victory over PARIVISION

9z continue to cruise at the IEM Cologne Major, where they went through Stage 2 with a 3-1 record, losing only to Spirit, and have now kicked off their Stage 3 journey with a 2-1 over PARIVISION in the opening round.

The main man for 9z in the series was Luciano "⁠luchov⁠" Herrera, who ended the three-mapper with a 2.00 rating,+7.41% swing, 129.1 ADR, and 91.2% KAST, further establishing himself as a force for La Violeta.

The series kicked off on Overpass, 9z's pick, with the South American team on the attack, and it didn't take long for luchov to get going, as a triple in the pistol round helped his team get the early lead.

From there on out, he put on an all-time performance, spearheading the 11-1 T side for 9z with 21 kills at the half.

The first map lasted only two more rounds, and by the end, the Argentine player cemented a memorable performance with the second-best Overpass at a Major, with a 2.83 rating, which is the fifth-highest rated map at a Major overall.

luchov gets three as 9z hit double digits

PARIVISION picked themselves up and bounced back on Dust2 despite a 1-3 start. With the game still close, 4-3, Emil "⁠nota⁠" Moskvitin got a mixed weapon ace with AK and AWP, helping propel his team to an 8-4 half.

9z won the second pistol round, making it four for four in the match up to that point, but in vain as PARIVISION went on to close the map out. luchov, still on a heater, posted a 1.68 rating in the second map despite the loss.

nota AK and AWP ace

The series turned to Inferno for the decider, and PARIVISION finally prevailed in a pistol round, taking it on the CT side. The early rounds went back-and-forth, however, with Ignacio "⁠meyern⁠" Meyer clutching a 1v3 in a forcebuy and Vladislav "⁠xiELO⁠" Lysov downing four in an eco before another 9z clutch, this time a 1v2 by Matias "⁠HUASOPEEK⁠" Ibañez Hernandez, made it 3-3.

9z then took charge momentarily, leading 7-3, before PARIVISION secured the last two to keep it close at the half. A crucial second pistol win and the following anti-forcebuy then evened things out in the 14th round.

PARIVISION's 3-0 start to the half put them in the lead, but only momentarily, as 9z turned things around with yet another fantastic showing by luchov on B. meyern, with a heroic triple on A, set up the 2v2 retake in what ended up being the final round of the match as the South American side closed it out 13-9.
