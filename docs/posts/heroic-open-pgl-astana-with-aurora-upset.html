HEROIC open PGL Astana with Aurora upset

xfl0ud and nilo powered HEROIC to a 2-0 victory to kick off the $1.6 million tournament.

nilo won two key clutches and picked up an ace to lead a superb defensive half on Dust2

PGL Astana has begun with Aurora suffering the first upset of the tournament, at the hands of a Linus "⁠nilo⁠" Bergman and Yasin "⁠xfl0ud⁠" Koç-powered HEROIC side.

The 23-year-old Turk led the way to a swift Nuke victory for HEROIC, who won the map off of an eight-round run on the CT side after punishing an ill-advised second-round Door rush through a smoke.

nilo then took over to lead another strong defensive performance on Dust2 with a 17-3 score, dominating Aurora's B approaches with multi-kill after multi-kill, including an ace, as well as two clutches.

xfl0ud then added another ace to a quickly growing tournament tally (up to three after the first couple of games, with Nemanja "⁠huNter-⁠" Kovač's against Fisher College) in the second half to deny Aurora the chance to equalize early.

In the end, the Turkish side still managed to bring it all the way to round 24, but fell apart at the hands of pistols at the final hurdle in HEROIC's B execute.
