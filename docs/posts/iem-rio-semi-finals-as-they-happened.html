IEM Rio semi-finals as they happened

On Falcons' confidence: "I feel like when we played against them in the matches before, they played more aggressive. There was more default-breaking things they were doing on CT side, for example, and it's just hard for you to play your default and end rounds. This time, it didn't happen, and when they tried to do something, they just died."

On grand final matchup: "I would like to play against FURIA, just because it would be something I've never experienced before, because of the arena. They will cheer for FURIA, and it will be loud. It's never happened to my team and to me before, so I'm just curious how it will feel. But playing against Vitality, as the best team, is interesting as well. It doesn't matter, but I think I would have something more unique if we played against FURIA."
