EDG extend dynasty with 3-1 over FPX

EDward Gaming have defeated FunPlus Phoenix 3-1, crowning themselves winners of China Kickoff. With this victory, they have claimed a top seed for Masters Madrid, and earned 3 Championship points for Champions 2024 qualification.

The match began on Breeze, which EDG was able to pull back to overtime and win 14-12. Continuing on Split, EDG kept a comfortable lead until they closed out the map 13-6. FPX bounced back on Icebox, which they took 13-8. Finally, Ascent ended 16-14 in EDG's favor.

The win marks EDG's fifth domestic title in a row, a streak that began May 2023. EDG and FPX will next play in Masters: Madrid, respectively their fifth and third international VCT event.
