DetonatioN makes Radiant Asia upper final with 2-1 against Trace

DetonatioN FocusMe has defeated Trace Esports with a 2-1 scoreline in the Radiant Asia Invitational playoffs. They will next face off against Paper Rex in the upper final, while Trace will play against DRX in a lower bracket elimination series.

The match began on Ascent, which Trace won 13-11 after recovering from an 11-5. DFM next took their map pick, Abyss with a 13-8, after a 10 round streak earned them matchpoint. DetonatioN closed out the series with a 13-7 on the decider, Haven, winning the series 2-1.

With this win, DetonatioN FocusMe remains undefeated in the Radiant Asia Invitational, as they qualified to playoffs after victories against EDward Gaming and Rex Regum Qeon.
