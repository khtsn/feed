In the weeks since lifting the Valorant Champions 2023 trophy, Evil Geniuses have been mired in controversy. The organization has pulled the plug on most of its divisions, and the Valorant players have spent the offseason in dispute with the organization. According to Richard Lewis, Evil Geniuses informed the players in early September they would either have to accept a nearly 50% paycut to remain at the organization or explore outside offers.

Days later, it was revealed EG's management had turned down several buyout offers. Among those offers were NRG's, who put a $100,000 USD bid on Demon1 and Ethan.

Heading into 2024, coach Chet "Chet" Singh has confirmed Ethan will take over the IGLing responsibilites previously filled by Pujan "FiNESSE" Mehta, who was released from his contract with NRG earlier this year alongside longstanding member Sam "s0m" Oh. Both players are expected to remain inactive in 2024.
