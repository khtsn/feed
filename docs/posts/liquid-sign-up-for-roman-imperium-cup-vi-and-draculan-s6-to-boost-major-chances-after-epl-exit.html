Liquid sign up for Roman Imperium Cup VI and DraculaN S6 to boost Major chances after EPL exit

On the heels of a 2-3 exit from ESL Pro League Season 23 Stage 1, Liquid have signed up for Roman Imperium Cup VI and DraculaN Season 6 in hopes of salvaging their campaign to qualify for the IEM Cologne Major.

The organization has yet to pay and confirm its attendance at either event, according to the sign-up sheets, but they have been listed as the final attending team on DraculaN's website.

Irrespective of attendance, Liquid's registrations alone show just how dire their situation is. The Kamil "⁠siuhy⁠" Szkaradek-led side dropped to just a 13% chance to make the Major, according to Mischief, after they were eliminated from EPL by 3DMAX. That number was based on the team attending BLAST Open Rotterdam and WINAMAX, a €15,000 LAN in France that would likely not offer enough VRS points to climb the standings unless several notable teams signed up.

If Liquid compete at both Roman Imperium Cup VI and DraculaN Season 6, their chances of making the Major will increase to 35%, Mischief told HLTV after running his calculation.
