Aurora make EPL semis over Legacy

Aurora have taken down Legacy 2-1 in a series characterised by long streaks of rounds wins from both teams.

The Turks seemed to be riding into the semi-finals at a canter after winning 19 rounds in a row over Dust2 and Overpass, but Legacy rallied to go on a 12-round streak of their own to force a decider of Inferno.

On map three, however, Legacy fell away at 6-7 as Aurora went on their final, deadly, streak of six rounds in a row to take the series away.

It's the third Aurora win in a row in this head-to-head, after the five-mapper PGL Masters Bucharest grand final and a tense group stage series in this season of Pro League.
