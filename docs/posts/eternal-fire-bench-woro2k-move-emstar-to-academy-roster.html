Eternal Fire bench Woro2k, move EMSTAR to academy roster

Eternal Fire have announced the benching of Volodymyr "⁠Woro2k⁠" Veletniuk from their main roster and the move of Emrecan "⁠EMSTAR⁠" Çalışkan to the academy squad.

The double move comes just three months after the Turkish organization went international for the second time in a year, making four new additions around the only existing player from the previous Turkish roster, EMSTAR.

Its search for an ideal lineup continues as the organization looks to build a younger roster and improve team chemistry, a representative told HLTV: "The team chemistry isn't quite there yet. We want to make the roster a bit younger and establish a dynamic game plan."

The two replacements are expected to be announced in the coming days.

Journeyman Woro2k's struggle to hold down a team sees no end. The Ukrainian has not lasted longer than a few months on any roster ever since he departed Monte in mid-2024, going through DRILLAS and its successor, Adventurers, Passion UA and Eternal Fire without much success since that time.

On Eternal Fire, the AWPer averaged a respectable 1.12 rating, most notably helping the team to a top-four finish at Digital Crusade DraculaN Season 5, its best result following the international move.
