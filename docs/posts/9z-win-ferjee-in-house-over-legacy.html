9z win FERJEE In House over Legacy

9z have secured the FERJEE In House 2026 trophy with a 3-2 win over Legacy in the grand final to further solidify their claim for a Stage 2 spot at the upcoming Cologne Major.

Ignacio "⁠meyern⁠" Meyer and company didn't lose a single game on their way to the final, beating GameHunters and Marsborne in the group stage and following that up with playoff wins over Imperial and paiN.

The best-of-five final against Legacy went the distance and was decided on the fifth map, where Matias "⁠HUASOPEEK⁠" Ibañez Hernandez crowned a fantastic grand final performance by putting up a 1.69 rating to ensure his team took home the trophy.

The VRS points from their victory have more or less locked in 9z's appearance in Stage 2 of the Cologne Major, while Legacy and paiN also racked up enough points with their respective second and third-place finishes to move into Stage 2 contention as per HLTV's prediction.

Another team that transformed their Major fortunes was Gaimin Gladiators, who rose up 16 spots in the VRS thanks to their fourth-place finish and are currently set to receive a Stage 1 invite.
