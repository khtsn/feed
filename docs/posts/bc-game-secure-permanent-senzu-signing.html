BC.Game secure permanent Senzu signing

BC.Game have announced the permanent signing of Azbayar "⁠Senzu⁠" Munkhbold from The MongolZ.

The Mongolian rifler joined the team on loan in April and played IEM Atlanta and CS Asia Championship, with both tournaments ending without a victory for BC.Game.

On Monday, BC.Game announced the Mongolian's departure from the team, but has now acquired the 19-year-old on a permanent basis.
