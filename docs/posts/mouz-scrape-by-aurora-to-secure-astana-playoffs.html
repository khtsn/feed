MOUZ scrape by Aurora to secure Astana playoffs

MOUZ have secured a spot in the PGL Astana playoffs after a hard-fought 2-0 victory over Aurora in the 2-1 pool of the Swiss stage.

New addition Adrian "⁠xelex⁠" Vincze made a big recovery as MOUZ won their opponents' pick, Dust2, 13-11.

The Hungarian and Lotan "⁠Spinx⁠" Giladi struggled as they were once again targeted on B, similarly to their game against G2, but xelex then stepped up with an 18-10 T half to close out the opener.

An overtime Nuke affair followed, where MOUZ secured the victory with a flawless second set after recovering a 7-10 deficit in the second half, spearheaded by Dorian "⁠xertioN⁠" Berman and Spinx.

xertioN's CT sides take center stage

The biggest point of criticism of the move was clashes on the CT side, where xelex had to take up unfamiliar anchor roles and jL pick up those on the other ends of the map instead of more rotation positions he had been used to from Natus Vincere.
