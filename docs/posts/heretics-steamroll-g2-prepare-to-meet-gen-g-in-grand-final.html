After two disappointing losses at the hands of North America's second seed, Heretics entered the lower final of Shanghai with a vengeance. G2's map pick of Bind could not have gone worse for North America's last hope as they were forced to submit in one of the quickest matches of the event.

Turkish flex Wo0t led his team with inhuman stats: the youngster died a total of six times on Map 1 while ending with 20 kills. A monstrous ACS of 360 accompanied by a 58% headshot percentage told the story as G2 were simply unable to find a solution for the substitute duelist. RieNs stepped up to ensure that Wo0t was not the only Turkish player to draw roars from the crowd as he ended Icebox with 21 kills and an ACS of 279.
