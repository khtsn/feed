keiko departs Team Liquid after two years

Team Liquid Team Liquid Europe Unranked nAts Ayaz Akhmetshin kamo Kamil Frąckowiak has announced the departure of Georgio "keiko" Sanassy.

keiko had been with Team Liquid for the past two seasons, joining from Apeks Apeks Inactive just after the 2023 season. Since then, he has been a constant presence in a Liquid team that has evolved several times in that time. In his first season with the team, keiko was the team's main Duelist player in a year that, overall, was relatively disappointing for the European outfit. At the end of Stage 2, Liquid was ranked eighth in the region and was absent from the international stage.

In 2025, the addition of kamo saw keiko also pick up the Controller role, primarily playing smokes for the team through EMEA Kickoff, where Liquid finished second only to a Team Vitality Team Vitality Europe Unranked Derke Nikita Sirmitev Chronicle Timofey Khromov KovaQ Blendi Kovaci Less Felipe de Loyola Basso superteam. The finish qualified Liquid for its first international event since Champions Los Angeles in 2023. As the year rolled on and the Liquid roster evolved, keiko played the Controller and Duelist roles, playing both roles and often playing a leading role for the team. In 2025, Liquid qualified for all three international events, and ended the year with a trophy at EMEA Stage 2.

Since the end of the season, Liquid has also parted ways with trexx and paTiTek , setting up for a rebuild as the team retains just two players from its 2025 roster heading into next season.
