tabseN: "Sucks to lose, but I'm really proud of the team"

BIG had an eventful few days in Cologne, securing a historic comeback from 0-12 down against NRG and progressing through Stage 2, but an appearance in front of their home crowd eluded them by the smallest of margins.

A three-map loss to B8 eliminated them with a 2-3 record, but Johannes "⁠tabseN⁠" Wodarz remained upbeat about the team's achievements and future.

"For me personally, I could not imagine to be this good at this time," he admitted to HLTV after the loss. "When I think about last year, we were just top 100 or something, so right now, I'm really happy."
