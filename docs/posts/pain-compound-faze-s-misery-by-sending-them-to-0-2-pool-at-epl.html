paiN compound FaZe's misery by sending them to 0-2 pool at EPL

Day two of ESL Pro League S23 began with paiN securing a 2-1 victory over FaZe, sending Finn "⁠karrigan⁠" Andersen's troops down to the 0-2 pool.

The result continues a dismal period for FaZe since the Budapest Major, with the European combine now sporting a 38.5% win rate in the last three months after winning just two out of their 10 matches in 2026.

The series began on Nuke, where paiN prevailed after a prolonged affair. FaZe recovered from a four-round CT half to force overtime despite Guilherme "⁠piriajr⁠" Barbosa hitting some nasty Deagle shots to turn the tide late on, but the Brazilians held on for a 16-13 victory.

Dust2 was also decided by overtime, but the map went the way of FaZe. Another slow 4-8 start was followed up by a resurgent FaZe coming back and securing overtime, aided by a miraculous 3vs4 B retake without a kit before Helvijs "⁠broky⁠" Saukants's 1vs2 clutch secured a 16-13 win.

After two close maps, Mirage was an anticlimactic end to the series. paiN were the better team from start to finish as piriajr starred for the Brazilians once again, putting up a 1.83 rating to help his team breeze to a 13-5 victory and a spot in the 1-1 pool.
