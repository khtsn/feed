In Georgia, a Test of Rudy Giuliani’s Loyalty to Trump

Michelle Cottle and David French break down the allegations in the Georgia indictment and why at least 18 of the defendants are in for “a world of hurt.”

Donald Trump has been indicted, again. But this time, he’s got company. The Opinion writers Michelle Cottle and David French discuss why that makes this indictment different — and potentially more effective — than the others.
