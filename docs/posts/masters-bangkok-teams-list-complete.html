Once the regional champions are decided, these eight teams will now go on a break for about two weeks. Masters Bangkok competition kicks off on February 20 and will last through to March 2. The rosters will be placed into an eight-team swiss stage, from where the four best teams will move to a double-elimination playoffs bracket.

The matches for the opening round of the Swiss stage will be drawn tomorrow, Feb. 9, after the conclusion of the EMEA Kickoff grand final between Team Vitality and Liquid.
