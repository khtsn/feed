Bilibili Gaming Bilibili Gaming China Rank #1 whzy Wang Haozhe (王昊哲) rushia Wang Xiaojie (王晓杰) nephh Marcus Tan Knight Liu Yuxiang (刘宇翔) bud Yang Renyu (杨韧余) has announced that, due to a medical emergency, Liu "Knight" Yuxiang (刘宇翔) has been moved to the team's reserve roster. In his place, former BLG player Zhong "Biank" Jianfei (钟剑飞) will start for BLG for its upcoming matches.

BLG's announcement shared that Knight experienced arrhythmia-related symptoms following the team's match on February 6. After a medical evaluation, Knight has been under observation and treatment as advised by doctors.

"This sudden onset of arrhythmia was accompanied by abnormal heart rate, chest tightness, and other discomfort, requiring scientific treatment and rest according to a professional medical plan," BLG's release shared. "To ensure the health of the players while maintaining the smooth progress of the competition, after thorough communication and evaluation with the hospital, the players' families, Knight himself, other team members, team management, and training staff, the club has applied to the league for an emergency roster, which has been approved by the league."
