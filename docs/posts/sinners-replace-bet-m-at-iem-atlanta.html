SINNERS replace BET-M at IEM Atlanta

ESL has announced that BET-M are unable to attend IEM Atlanta, and so SINNERS have been invited to attend in the Russian side's stead. SINNERS earned the invite as they finished third in the global closed qualifier.

The tournament would have been by far the most notable LAN the BET-M squad had ever attended, the team having earned their spot by taking down the likes of fnatic and SINNERS on their way to second-place in the closed qualifier.

Instead SINNERS will get their first shot at a Big Event in the organization's history, their previous significant LAN appearances coming at IEM Fall in 2021 and the Europe RMRs for PGL Major Antwerp and Perfect World Shanghai Major.

SINNERS have a chance to build upon their solid recent form when they attend IEM Atlanta on May 11, the European combine having recently won BC Game Masters Championship with victories over the likes of G2, BetBoom and BIG.
