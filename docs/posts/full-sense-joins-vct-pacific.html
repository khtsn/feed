Full Sense joins VCT Pacific

VCT Pacific has announced that Thai organization FULL SENSE FULL SENSE Asia-Pacific Rank #3 Leviathan Thanyathon Nakmee Killua Tanate Teerasawad thyy Anupong Preamsak primmie Papaphat Sriprapha JitBoyS Jittana Nokngam will be joining the league to replace TALON TALON Inactive Killua Tanate Teerasawad thyy Anupong Preamsak Crws Thanamethk Mahatthananuyut JitBoyS Jittana Nokngam primmie Papaphat Sriprapha , who was recently removed. As they enter the league, they have signed the full roster of Talon, with Thanyathon "Leviathan" Nakmee coming in to replace Thanamethk "Crws" Mahatthananuyut, who has moved to the coaching role.

Full Sense has been present in the Pacific scene since 2020, competing in Challengers Thailand. They were a top team in the league, and were contenders for qualifying to international events in the wider SEA region. Eventually, the orange flame was represented on the international stage for the first time at Champions 2021 after winning the APAC LCQ. Unfortunately, at Champions, Full Sense took a swift exit, being eliminated in last place without winning a map.

After 2021, Full Sense has yet to return to the international stage, but have continued to consistently field competitive Thai rosters in Pacific. In 2024 and 2025, they managed to reach Ascension Pacific as a top team from the SEA region.

Now, after three full seasons competing in Challengers, Full Sense will get to see the VCT stage for the first time in Pacific. Maintaining Thai representation in the league, they will retain Talon's previously top three roster of Thai players.
