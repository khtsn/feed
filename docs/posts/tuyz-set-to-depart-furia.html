tuyz set to depart FURIA

1 day ago

FURIA FURIA Brazil Rank #45 heat Olavo Marcelo basic Luis Henrique Ferlin Tessaro Palla Davi Alcides Urango Victor Rodrigues 's Arthur "tuyz" Andrade has announced that he has started his search for a new team.

"While still being contracted with FURIA, I'm allowed to explore my options and I'm looking for a new team!" he said. He also shared that he prefers to play Controller, but is open to any role and all VCT regions.

tuyz joined FURIA as part of a new five-man roster for the squad that was unveiled ahead of the EWC Qualifiers. He arrived as the most well-known player in the class, joining shortly after his release from Brazilian giants LOUD LOUD Brazil Rank #8 lukxo Lucca Travaioli Virtyy Leandro Moreno RobbieBk Robbie Boerkamp pANcada Bryan Luna cauanzin Cauan Pereira , where he played for just over two years. While with LOUD, his role changed several times as the roster moved around him. With FURIA in 2025, he played the Controller role, mainly on Omen while also playing Brimstone on select maps.

After crashing out of the EWC Qualifiers two games in, tuyz and FURIA went 0-5 in Stage 2, winning just one map in the process.

FURIA FURIA Brazil Rank #45 heat Olavo Marcelo basic Luis Henrique Ferlin Tessaro Palla Davi Alcides Urango Victor Rodrigues 's full roster is now:

  • Olavo "heat" Marcelo
  • Luis "basic" Henrique Ferlin Tessaro
  • Davi "Palla" Alcides
  • Victor "Urango" Rodrigues
  • Ian "shaW" Jardim (Head coach)
  • Lucas "Kamino" Kamino (Assistant coach)
