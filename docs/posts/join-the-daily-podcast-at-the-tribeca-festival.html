Join ‘The Daily’ Podcast at the Tribeca Festival

As context, nuance and history are vital to understanding current events, “The Daily,” the New York Times’s flagship news podcast, follows one story a day, helping to create a distinctive lens through which to view the world.

In a celebration of the power of audio journalism, the team from “The Daily” will appear at this year’s Tribeca Festival for a special live event featuring the podcast’s hosts, Michael Barbaro and Sabrina Tavernise.

And you can join them.

Hear how the show comes together five days a week in a conversation moderated by the writer and comedian Mo Rocca.

To learn more about the event and to purchase tickets, please visit tribecafilm.com/thedaily.

This conversation will take place on Sunday, June 9 at 5 p.m. at Spring Studios in New York City.
