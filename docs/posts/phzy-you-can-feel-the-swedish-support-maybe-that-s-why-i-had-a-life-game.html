phzy: "You can feel the Swedish support, maybe that's why I had a life game"

Love "⁠phzy⁠" Smidebrant joined Astralis as part of their international revolution at the start of the year, and the Swedish AWPer looked comfortable from the off in his new environment.

He averages a 1.11 rating in Astralis so far, and has now backed up that form in an arena match against Spirit, putting up almost 100 ADR, a +7.48 round swing, and a 1.76 in what he jokingly described as his "life game."

With Aurora and his ex-teammate Caner "⁠soulfly⁠" Kesici standing between Astralis and the EPL grand final, the Swede is relishing his team's chances of the trophy.

"Especially with the favorites like MOUZ and Spirit being out now, I think it's definitely up for grabs for any of the remaining teams," he told HLTV after the win against Spirit.

How are you feeling after that game?

Amazing. To play here in Stockholm, I knew it would be special for me, even though it's not the most prestigious... You know what I mean. But not only to play, but also to win here versus Spirit, it's great.

How does it feel having this crowd here supporting you? Because even though it went very late, a lot of people still stayed to lend you their support.

Yeah, it's amazing, and I appreciate everyone coming a lot. You can feel the Swedish support while you're playing, maybe that's why I had a life game. [smiles]

To talk about that game, was it as easy as it looked from the outside? Because it looked like you guys just dominated Spirit from start to finish.

Yeah, I mean, it's going to be a very clickbait-friendly answer. But yeah, it did feel easy, to be honest. Whether that is maybe Spirit not playing their best game or us just playing very well, I'm not sure. I'll have to watch back the game and see.

It did feel easy, to be honest

I'm sure you'll be happy to watch that one back.

Yeah, but it did feel comfortable.

This is quite a big step for Astralis as a team, right? You guys not only made the playoffs, but now top four. How does that feel for you guys as a team?

It feels great. We haven't really set any goals or expectations, just taking it one game at a time from Stage 1. Yeah, it's just nice.

Obviously, you have a game tomorrow against Aurora. How are you feeling about that matchup?

I think it's okay for us. We did lose versus them last time we played. It's always fun playing versus my brother soulfly, who I played with in Sangal, so it will be a special match for me as well.

It's always fun playing versus my brother soulfly, who I played with in Sangal

I've never made it past semifinals in Pro League before. I did play with NIP as well, where we lost to ENCE, I believe, a long time ago now. Looking forward to breaking that streak.

Considering the form that your team seems to be in, and you've got the Sweden buff going on. Does this give you a little bit of confidence that maybe you can go all the way and try and challenge for that trophy?

Yeah, maybe. Especially with the favorites like MOUZ and Spirit being out now, I think it's definitely up for grabs for any of the remaining teams. I think everyone has been looking good that's been in the playoffs so far.

Legacy making it very far and showing up a little bit out of nowhere, they had a good period before, and then FUT is always a tough opponent. Who else is it? NAVI, I believe? They're always strong. I think anyone can win at this point.
