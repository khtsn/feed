1win sign Qikert and reyoz

The Kazakh veteran is back in action following his departure from MIBR.

1win have announced the signings of Aleksei "⁠Qikert⁠" Golubev and Nikita "⁠reyoz⁠" Rolzing, bringing their roster back up to full strength after the departures of Timofey "⁠interz⁠" Yakushin and Abay "⁠HObbit⁠" Khassenov earlier on Wednesday.

Qikert's last team was a three-month loan stint with MIBR at the end of 2025. The Kazakh veteran struggled to find his place in the eclectic roster, averaging a 0.94 rating across his tenure, but did appear at the Budapest Major with the squad.

reyoz joins the team from Enjoy, whom he recently represented in the CCT Season 3 Europe Series 16 Closed Qualifier and put up a 1.31 rating. He also previously played for 1WIN Academy, but has only racked up 19 maps of play in HLTV-recorded matches in his career.
