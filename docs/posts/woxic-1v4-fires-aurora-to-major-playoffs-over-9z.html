woxic 1v4 fires Aurora to Major playoffs over 9z

Aurora have progressed to Cologne's playoffs over 9z after one of the plays of the tournament from Özgür "⁠woxic⁠" Eker.

9z took both Vitality and Spirit to their absolute limit in the last few days, but they were blown out of the water by Aurora on both maps in a swift 2-0 victory.

It goes to show what can happen if the South Americans are denied any momentum, but that is easier said than done as Counter-Strike's elite have shown so far in Cologne.

Aurora's dominance therefore becomes even more impressive. They could have lost their heads on Nuke after fumbling an unlosable 4v2, but instead it was their only round loss of an 11-1 CT half.

Credit should go to Major rookie Caner "⁠soulfly⁠" Kesici, who told Martin "⁠STYKO⁠" Styk post-game that he had a "life game" with his 2.13 rating on Nuke, his first-ever LAN map above a 2.00 rating.

Watch woxic's highlight of the year contender

Dust2 promised to be a more entertaining back-and-forth, only for woxic to end those hopes with a scintillating 1v4.

As he planted the bomb, woxic was on 25HP, with opponents coming from all three choke points. But as he manipulated each duel with some smart movement, he gave himself a slim chance and left with the unlikeliest of round wins.

And with that, Aurora have sailed into their first Major playoffs since PGL Copenhagen 2024.
