Astralis cruise into EPL semis over Spirit

For the second time this season, Spirit have been knocked out in the quarter-finals.

Playing in front of a Swedish crowd, phzy put in a great performance

Astralis are through to the semi-finals of ESL Pro League 23 after making quick work of Spirit in a 2-0 series, their victory decorated by a standout performance from AWPer Love "⁠phzy⁠" Smidebrant, who posted a series-high 1.76 rating after topping the scoreboard on both maps.

Astralis were the better team in every area as Spirit were on the back foot the entire series. It was a terrible day at the office for the Russian side, who will have only a few days to lick their wounds before BLAST Open Rotterdam begins.

"We always had a good take on the tactical side against them," Rasmus "⁠HooXi⁠" Nielsen said after the match. "It was all about the shooting. Love [phzy] had the home crowd buff today."

Looking ahead to Saturday's match against Aurora, the Danish captain said his team knows what to expect. "It's all about executing the plan and shooting them," he noted.

Spirit came out on Dust2 looking sluggish and disjointed as Astralis stormed to a 7-0 lead after a dream start to their CT side. It was only on their eighth attempt that the Russian team managed to find some success, but Astralis remained in control and went into half-time up 10-2, inspired by great performances from phzy and Gytis "⁠ryu⁠" Glušauskas.

More misery was to come for Spirit as Victor "⁠Staehr⁠" Staehr went nuclear in a 2v3 situation with three Glock headshots. Spirit responded immediately and strung a few rounds together, but Astralis never looked troubled and quickly finished the game off.

The one-way traffic continued on Ancient. Once again, Spirit found themselves with a mountain to climb after a 0-7 start as Astralis seized the game by its throat. The Russian team began to work away at the deficit before a phzy ace in the final round ensured that the momentum remained firmly with Astralis.

HooXi's charges refused to let up and quickly reached series point. With no room for error, Spirit put together a streak of rounds, but their comeback hopes were thwarted in the 20th round as Astralis flawlessly defended the B site with a trio of frags from their captain.

"It's always lovely when people decide to rush you through a smoke," HooXi said, laughing. "Even I can kill when they do that."
