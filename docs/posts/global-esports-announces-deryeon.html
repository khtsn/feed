Global Esports announces Deryeon

Global Esports Global Esports Asia-Pacific Rank #37 patrickWHO Mark Musni UdoTan Go Kyung-won (고경원) Kr1stal Savva Fedorov kellyS Kelly Sedillo Papi Federico Evangelista Deryeon Derrick Yee has signed Derrick "Deryeon" Yee for the 2025 VCT season, just under three weeks before the start of Kickoff. Global Esports owner Rushindra Sinha confirmed to vlr.gg that Deryeon will be playing in place of Mark "patrickWHO" Musni, whose visa has been delayed.

Deryeon is best known for his time on BLEED BLEED Inactive , one of the top Singaporean teams in Challengers. He joined the organization in late 2021, and led the team to consistent top-three finishes throughout 2022, including the APAC Last Chance Qualifier. The beginning of the VCT partnership league in 2023 gave BLEED free rein to dominate Challengers, capped off with an Ascension win at the end of the season.

The following year was not as successful for BLEED, as the team struggled during both Kickoff and the regular season. By August, BLEED had benched four players, including Deryeon.

In November, Deryeon joined Disguised Disguised Asia-Pacific Rank #4 Vera Rodman Yap STYRON Tidus Goh crazyguy Ngô Công Anh wayne Wayne Chang , helping the team win three off-season events, and was among the ten highest-rated players in Challengers Southeast Asia Stage 3.

Deryeon will return to VCT Pacific in Global Esports' opening series against Team Secret on January 19.
