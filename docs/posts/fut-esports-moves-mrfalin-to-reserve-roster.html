FUT Esports moves MrFaliN to reserve roster

9 hours ago

FUT Esports FUT Esports Europe Rank #12 s0pp Efe Tur xeus Doğan Gözgen MrFaliN Furkan Yeğen yetujey Eray Budak KROSTALY Okan Alaçam has moved longtime in-game leader MrFaliN to their reserve roster.

MrFaliN first joined FUT over four years ago in January 2022 and remained with the team as they entered the partnerships era. With MrFaliN at the helm, FUT went on to place third in the EMEA League, qualifying to Masters Tokyo and Champions Los Angeles. The team continued to earn international spots in 2024, with their best result being a top-six finish at Masters Shanghai.

FUT began to slide down the regional rankings in 2025. While they still placed fourth in 2025 Kickoff, they missed out on playoffs entirely in Stage 2 and exited 2026 Kickoff in joint last place.

This shift follows a cascade of other changes for the Turkish squad, with Efe "s0pp" Tur coming in following the benching of Doğukan "qRaxs" Balaban and Berke "Vlad" Kantürk taking up the head coach mantle after the departure of Eray "Gais" Sarikaya. MrFalin is the last of the team's 2023 core to leave the active roster, leaving FUT in need of a new IGL ahead of their Stage 1 debut versus Gentle Mates.
