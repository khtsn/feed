Eternal Fire complete team with MisteM

Eternal Fire have announced the signing of Young Ninjas in-game leader Gareth "⁠MisteM⁠" Ries. This move completes a roster overhaul from the Turkish organization that saw Volodymyr "⁠Woro2k⁠" Veletniuk benched to make room for Romanian AWPer Iulian "⁠regali⁠" Harjău, while Emrecan "⁠EMSTAR⁠" Çalışkan was moved to Eternal Fire Academy.

MisteM has been plying his trade with both Ninjas in Pyjamas's main lineup and academy squad since January 2024. The South African was originally signed to Young Ninjas, eventually getting promoted to the main lineup in place of captain Alejandro "⁠alex⁠" Masanet in September of that year.

His tenure on the main squad proved to be brief, however, as he was moved to the bench and eventually back to Young Ninjas after the team added Marco "⁠Snappi⁠" Pfeiffer to their ranks in January 2025.

Eternal Fire made the jump to an international lineup in January of this year, moving three Turkish players to the bench to make way for the additions of Rigon "⁠rigoN⁠" Gashi, Woro2k, and Sergiy "⁠DemQQ⁠" Demchenko. The newly international side has yet to make much headway on the Valve Regional Standings (VRS), debuting at #72 in the global rankings before peaking at #56 in late March.
