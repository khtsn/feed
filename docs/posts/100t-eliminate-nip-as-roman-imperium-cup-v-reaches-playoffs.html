100T eliminate NIP as Roman Imperium Cup V reaches playoffs

Snappi's troops were the biggest name to fall in the group stage.

The group stage of Roman Imperium Cup V reached an end on Friday evening, with 100 Thieves eliminating Ninjas in Pyjamas in a three-map series on their much-anticipated LAN debut.

The two teams were placed in Group D alongside BESTIA and Monte, and met in the opening best-of-one round, where Ninjas in Pyjamas demolished Håvard "⁠rain⁠" Nygaard and company on Nuke, 13-2.

Ninjas in Pyjamas were then beaten by Monte, who secured their spot in the playoffs, while 100 Thieves got the first win of their new era by eliminating BESTIA.

That meant the teams met for a rematch, which was eventually decided by overtime on the decider map of Ancient. In the end, 100 Thieves did enough to secure a playoff spot thanks to William "⁠sirah⁠" Kjærsgaard (1.22 rating) and André "⁠Ag1l⁠" Gil (1.20) having great games, while Ninjas in Pyjamas suffered a shock group-stage elimination.
