Nova earned two close victories to end week two 3-0, putting together a three-match win streak for the first time since Stage 1 last year. Teams will need to plan around the Split that Nova showcased against JDG, with the Raze and Deadlock combination leading to a 13-4 win.

Returning from EWC, XLG and EDG started with upset losses and rebounded to win their next two games. In particular, after a rough series against TYLOO, nobody rose to the occasion to top frag in two maps of the two EDG sweeps.

All Gamers struggled more, defeating Wolves but losing to strong teams in XLG and BLG. AG is still striving to reach the same form from Kickoff, but the team's struggles on the new map Summit led to the two series losses.

Moving on to the best players of the week, we have a new Russian import player taking the reins from last week's victor.
