paiN stay alive at FlyQuest's expense

After fighting valiantly through Stage 1, FlyQuest exit Stage 2 without a win to their name.

FlyQuest have become the first casualty of Stage 2 of the IEM Cologne Major, departing with a 0-3 record, cemented by a best-of-three defeat at the hands of paiN.

FlyQuest nearly departed from the Major in Stage 1, marching past THUNDER dOWNUNDER and Liquid in best-of-threes to dodge an early elimination. Joshua "⁠INS⁠" Potter's men had a more difficult run in Stage 2, struggling to contend with the three South American sides they faced off against during their run.

"We haven't had the best year so far, and we've had limited practice time, and we've done the best with what we have," Justin "⁠jks⁠" Savage shared after his team's elimination. "Obviously, it's a bit disappointing. I'm happy we made Stage 2, but we were hoping to show at least a better face in Stage 2. Thanks to everyone who supports us, and we'll see what happens next season."

Best-of-one losses to 9z and Legacy put FlyQuest on the verge of elimination, matching up against paiN for the opportunity to keep their Major run alive.

paiN hit the ground running in the series on their pick of Nuke, cruising to an early 8-2 lead off the back of a strong T side. FlyQuest managed to claim the final two rounds of the half, and although they lost the second pistol, a 1v2 from INS gave FlyQuest a lifeline with a force-buy win.

FlyQuest followed this with two more rounds, but once paiN's economy stabilized and Rafael "⁠saffee⁠" Costa got an AWP in his hands, the Brazilians took control of the map once again, quickly closing it out 13-7.

FlyQuest endured another slow start on their pick of Anubis, as paiN romped ahead to a 9-1 lead on the offense, powered by a 2v4 and a 3v5 conversion. FlyQuest started to piece together a comeback, claiming the final two rounds of the half and the first four rounds of their own T-side, but periodic individual highlights from paiN proved to be too much to handle.

A Deagle 3k from Guilherme "⁠piriajr⁠" Barbosa, a triple kill from João "⁠snow⁠" Vinicius during the B-site hold, and a trio of kills from Vinicius "⁠vsm⁠" Moreira in the final round gave paiN the push they needed to get Anubis over the line 13-10, taking the series 2-0.
