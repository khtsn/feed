Nominations for the 75th Emmy Awards will be announced on Wednesday. See below for a live list of nominees.

July 12, 2023Updated 11:38 a.m. ET

[Follow live updates of the 2023 Emmy nominations here.]

Nominations for the 2023 Emmy Awards are being announced through a livestream on Emmys.com and on the Television Academy’s YouTube page. This year’s announcement is hosted by the actress and comedian Yvette Nicole Brown (“Community,”“Firebuds”). Joining her is Frank Scherma, the chairman and chief executive of the Television Academy. The 75th Emmy Awards telecast is scheduled for 8 p.m. Eastern on Sept. 18 on Fox, but the ceremony may be postponed if the Hollywood writers’ strike continues.

Follow below for a full list of nominees, which will be updated as announcements are made.
