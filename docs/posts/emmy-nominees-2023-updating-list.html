Emmy Nominees 2023: Updating List

Nominations for the 75th Emmy Awards will be announced on Wednesday. See below for a live list of nominees.

July 12, 2023, 10:05 a.m. ET

[Follow live updates of the 2023 Emmy nominations here.]

Nominations for the 2023 Emmy Awards are to be announced on Wednesday at 11:30 a.m. Eastern time and can be watched via a livestream on Emmys.com and on the Television Academy’s YouTube page. This year’s announcement will be hosted by the actress and comedian Yvette Nicole Brown (“Community,”“Firebuds”). Joining her is Frank Scherma, the chairman and chief executive of the Television Academy. The 75th Emmy Awards telecast is scheduled for 8 p.m. Eastern on Sept. 18 on Fox, but the ceremony may be postponed if the Hollywood writers’ strike continues.
