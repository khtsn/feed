MAJ3R: "Some old aim that I had in 1.6 just came up yesterday"

Aurora are one match away from reaching the playoffs at the IEM Cologne Major after beating G2 in a two-map series, carrying the kind of form into day three that had seen them go a map up against Spirit after a Engin "⁠MAJ3R⁠" Küpeli masterclass the day before before they were decimated on the next two.

"Yesterday, if we were able to manage to win some rounds on CT side on Anubis, we had a chance to win it," the Turkish captain told HLTV. "But today it was 6-6, so I had better cards, better chances for my T-side, but yesterday was really difficult with 10-2."

MAJ3R also reiterated his thoughts from PGL Astana regarding his and Aurora's future as the off-season approaches, saying he is staying fully focused on the present and keeping everything else out of his mind.

"I'm not thinking about it," he said of the upcoming off-season. "I don't know. I have zero information. I am under contract, I'm a player of Aurora, it's a very good organization. I try my best to win some games, and I'm not thinking about the past, and I'm not thinking about the future. I'm only focused on the present because this is only what matters for now, for me."

Listen to the interview here.

Congratulations, a 2-0 over G2. Must feel pretty good after the way yesterday ended, right?

Yes, good day after all. Yesterday, it was a little difficult against Spirit. Even [though] we won Dust2, the last two maps had no score; we didn't fight. But today was a good day, good preparation, and I'm very happy that we managed to win 2-0 against a good team like G2.

I heard you talking about it a little bit just now, but Anubis went a lot differently today. Definitely helps that it wasn't a 2-10 CT side this time around, right?

Yes, yes. Yesterday, if we were able to manage to win some rounds on CT side on Anubis, we had a chance to win it. But today it was 6-6, so I had better cards, better chances for my T-side, but yesterday was really difficult with 10-2.

Talk to me about your calling, how you've come into this Major, what you've tried to work on, because Aurora has been trying for a while to find some of these tournament victories. How have you changed things up coming into this Major?

Bro, I just try to call some win runs, not thinking too much. My only goal is to win right now, and I'm doing this. It's like I have some cards in my hands, like in poker, and I have to do some play. I try my best with the players I have, with the teams I have, and I try to make my players play good Counter-Strike and also myself in my system. So overall, I just want to play good Counter-Strike and show good Counter-Strike. That's all.

Talking about you playing good Counter-Strike, yesterday on Dust2, an insane game from you. How were you feeling in that game?

I felt really good. I heard that they said there was a lot of preparation in this game, but I didn't prepare it. I just pushed Long one time and I saw there was a gap on the map, and they didn't see me, so I did it multiple times to get more information and it paid off. Also, individually, I got back to my 1.6 [ability] (laughs), some old aim that I had in 1.6 just came up yesterday.

So it's good, that means that I have the potential to show it, so I try my best to help my team individually because I think nowadays it's very important in the Counter-Strike arena that we play right now, in this year. Overall, it's important for everyone to put up some numbers, like in-game leaders, everyone, and I try my best for that to help my team.

You're 2-1 now. The kind of form you showed today, the kind of form you showed yesterday on Dust2, is that what you're hoping to carry into your next game and potentially into the playoffs?

Yeah, hopefully. The goal is to go to the playoffs, and I hope that we can do it here.

I remember talking to you and Astana, and you said that these rumors about Aurora, you try to completely put them out of your mind. How is it coming into this Major with the atmosphere in the team? A lot of things happen post-Major, so how are you guys trying to keep this out of your mind?

I'm not thinking about it. I don't know. I have zero information. I am under contract, I'm a player of Aurora, it's a very good organization. I try my best to win some games, and I'm not thinking about the past, and I'm not thinking about the future. I'm only focused on the present because this is only what matters for now, for me.

Full focus on the Major.

Full focus.
