BLAST Rivals Fort Worth playoff bracket set

It'll be broky Friday in the Dickies Arena

Six teams are headed to the Dickies Arena for the playoffs of BLAST Rivals 2026 Season 1, with FURIA being the surprise team to miss out alongside a Laurențiu "⁠lauNX⁠" Țârlea-less FUT.

The final group stage games saw Vitality and Natus Vincere prevail in their seeding matches over G2 and GamerLegion, respectively, to advance directly to the semi-finals, setting up the potential grand final clash that Aleksi "⁠Aleksib⁠" Virolainen desires for his team to feel like they deserved the trophy.

Russel "⁠Twistzz⁠" Van Dulken called two masterful T sides to outclass FURIA in Group B's lower bracket and earn FaZe their first arena appearance since the playoffs of the StarLadder Budapest Major, setting up a quarter-final bout against a G2 side that is looking more and more formidable since adding Guy "⁠NertZ⁠" Iluz.

The teams last faced online in ESL Pro League Season 23, where FaZe barely avoided a 0-13 defeat and lost the series in two.

GamerLegion also benefited from FURIA's dismal showing in Texas, earning a spot in the playoffs after winning their opener despite a flat showing against Natus Vincere in the seeding match, and will be looking for revenge against Astralis after getting blown out in their last meeting at IEM Krakow.
