sycrone and Xyp9x renew deals at MOUZ

MOUZ have extended the contracts of Dennis "⁠sycrone⁠" Nielsen and Andreas "⁠Xyp9x⁠" Højsleth, tying down their Danish coaches for an undisclosed length of time.

sycrone has emerged as one of the scene's top coaches since stepping up from MOUZ NXT in 2022 and has been with the German organization for his entire coaching career.

"sycrone is in my view the most complete head coach in CS2," said Jan Dominicus, Chief Business Officer at MOUZ, a sign of the faith the organization have in their head coach. "He embodies the long-term approach we believe in," CEO Stefan Wendt added.

Hopefully, the conversation around this team will increasingly return to the depth of our Counter-Strike

MOUZ, the number three team in the world, made nine semi-finals in a row in 2025 but only won one trophy, at PGL Cluj-Napoca.

Having failed to take the next step towards being regular trophy winners, they have been targets of community scrutiny and sycrone has paid attention to that in the press release.

"Hopefully, along the way," he said, "the conversation around this team will increasingly return to the depth of our Counter-Strike, the quality of our work, and the level we are building rather than focusing too quickly on young individuals who are still in the process of developing both as players and personalities."

I see similarities to the Astralis days, where we didn’t break through

Assistant coach Xyp9x signed in March 2024, and has attempted to transfer his experience of going from being seen as chokers to winning four Major titles to his young troops.

"I see similarities to the Astralis days, where we didn’t break through semi-finals for a long period of time," Xyp9x said in the MOUZ press release. "I firmly believe our main goal now is to unlock those final five percent through sheer tenacity, unity, and hard work."
