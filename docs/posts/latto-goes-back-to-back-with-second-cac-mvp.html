latto goes back-to-back with second CAC MVP

The Brazilian was the best player of CS Asia Championships in 2025, and has done so again one year later.

Bruno "⁠latto⁠" Rebelatto has retained his MVP at CS Asia Championships from 2025 by going back-to-back in 2026.

He went into the grand final with a narrow lead in the race over a more evenly distributed Falcons, but still upset the odds by dominating the world number four in the final.

His magnificent 1.52 rating today capped an already excellent event, which featured a 1.40 rating in playoffs, 111.1 ADR in round wins, and 0.85 kills per round.

latto had a 1.43 rating in map wins, including peaks like his 33-frag Overpass against TYLOO in double overtime, 1.81 and 1.50 ratings against The MongolZ, and 1.71 against MIBR.
