IEM to return to USA in 2027

This year's IEM event in the USA was hosted in Atlanta

ESL has announced that it will host another event in the USA in 2027 on April 5-11, with the host city yet to be announced.

The tournament will be a $1,000,000 event and feature 16 teams, 11 receiving their invite via the global VRS, one invite each going out via the North America and South America VRS, with the final three teams coming from qualifiers, two global and one Americas.

The event will run a group stage with two groups of eight teams, playing double-elimination, with each group sending four teams to the playoffs. The eight-team playoffs will feature a full schedule: four quarter-finals on the Friday, two semi-finals on the Saturday, and the third-place playoff and grand final on Sunday.

In contrast to this year's IEM Atlanta, which was hosted on the show floor at a DreamHack convention, a Product Manager at ESL has stated that the plan is to host the 2027 event in an arena.
