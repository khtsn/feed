Sources: br0 set to join Eternal Fire

br0 is close to joining Eternal Fire

Eternal Fire are on the verge of signing of NRG benched player Alexander "⁠br0⁠" Bro, sources with knowledge of the situation told HLTV. Eternal Fire's interest in the Danish player was previously reported in Turkey by TRCS Topluluk.

br0 was moved to NRG's bench last month as the North American organization explored its options in the off-season. He made his final appearance for NRG at the IEM Cologne Major, where the team went out of Stage 1 after losing to BIG in heartbreaking fashion in the last Swiss round.
