OG sign pr1metapz on loan from Sangal

The 25-year-old German only lasted three months on Sangal before he made the step up to the cadiaN-led side.

Luca "⁠pr1metapz⁠" Voigt has joined OG on a loan deal from Sangal, the two organizations have announced.

The new addition replaces Rafael "⁠arrozdoce⁠" Wing on the roster after the Portuguese player was benched from the team on Tuesday, bringing an end to his nine-month tenure with the team.

The changes were announced just as the team is traveling to Vila Nova de Gaia, Portugal, for its debut at the BC.Game Masters Championship Season 2.

There, the new OG will play in a group that has been depleted to four teams following the withdrawal of Passion UA, featuring Metizport, Betclic and Rebels.

With pr1metapz, who averaged a 1.06 rating in his brief stint with Sangal, Casper "⁠cadiaN⁠" Møller and company will hope to turn their form around after a series of disappointing results that led to their failure to qualify for the IEM Cologne Major at the beginning of April.

In the aftermath of the dip, OG is now ranked 72nd in the world as of the May 20 Valve Regional Standings.
