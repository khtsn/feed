A year ago after the midterm elections, the Democratic National Committee, the political arm of the Democratic Party which is functionally controlled by the White House, made the announcement that they wanted to switch the order of the states voting in the presidential primary.

They want to honor their new state order and make clear that South Carolina is the first place where their presidential primary officially starts.
