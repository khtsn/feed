9z duo among top Fantasy picks for PGL Astana Playoffs

max is third-most picked player in the game

PGL Astana 2026 - Playoffs

Format

  • You can create your teams on the Fantasy page here.

  • Eight-team single-elimination bracket with a third-place decider.

  • All matches are BO3 except for the grand final, which is BO5.

Teams

Things to note 

  • The quarter-final losers will get two elimination rounds, while the winners will play out the rest of the bracket. With 9z vs. magic (the lowest-ranked playoff teams) meeting in the first round, the winning team might have a tough time against higher-ranked opposition in the two following rounds.

Player prices & pick stats

  • Myroslav "⁠zont1x⁠" Plakhotia and Danil "⁠donk⁠" Kryshkovets are the two most-picked players heading into the playoffs, each featuring on 37% of teams. The Ukrainian is available for just $188,000.

  • 9z's Maximiliano "⁠max⁠" Gonzalez and Luciano "⁠luchov⁠" Herrera are among the top five most-picked players. What’s remarkable is that the latter is up there while not being a bronze card, which is likely due to his 1.35 rating in the Swiss stage. As mentioned above, the viability of picking 9z's players depends not only on the result of their game against magic, but also on their performance in the two following matches in case of a quarter-final win.
