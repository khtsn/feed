MOUZ cruise past NAVI in Cluj-Napoca

MOUZ have secured a spot in the 2-0 pool at PGL Cluj-Napoca thanks to a comfortable win over Natus Vincere, taking victories on Inferno (13-9) and Mirage (13-7).

This series felt like an important litmus test for both teams, a chance to truly test their mettle now the season is well underway. It was a test that MOUZ passed with flying colours, leaving Natus Vincere scratching their heads as they were a class below their opponents.

Natus Vincere kicked off Inferno by winning a force-buy after losing the pistol, but that was just about all the joy they got on their CT side. MOUZ found success on both bombsites with a varied offensive approach, garnering a solid 8-4 lead by halftime.
