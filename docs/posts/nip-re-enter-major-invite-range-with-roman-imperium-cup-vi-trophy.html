NIP re-enter Major invite range with Roman Imperium Cup VI trophy

Ninjas in Pyjamas have claimed victory at Roman Imperium Cup VI after reverse-sweeping Casper "⁠cadiaN⁠" Møller's OG in the grand final (7-13 Overpass, 13-3 Nuke, 13-10 Dust2).

It is the second Roman Imperium trophy for the organization after it won the third iteration of the event in December, and the first title since the team added Artem "⁠cairne⁠" Mushynskyi in January.

The win comes at a critical juncture for NIP after a rocky start to the year has seen them slide down the VRS and exit the invite range for the IEM Cologne Major. The team lost to Monte and 100 Thieves at Roman Imperium Cup V and exited Stage 1 of ESL Pro League Season 23 with a 2-3 record, a run which included losses to Legacy and M80 and only marginal point gains in wins over NRG and Gaimin Gladiators.
