Roman Imperium Cup VII reaches playoffs

The group stage of Roman Imperium Cup VII has concluded after a round-robin group stage, which saw eight teams secure spots in the tournament's single-elimination playoff bracket.

BESTIA, HEROIC, and G2 all topped their respective groups without dropping a map, while SINNERS secured the top seed from Group C thanks to their head-to-head victory over OG.

BetBoom, who are playing their second of three LAN events with two stand-ins, scraped into the playoffs in a three-way tie in Group A thanks to a single round difference over Wildcard (ten to nine), while Fluxo, OG, and Acend claimed the other spots.
