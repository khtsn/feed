NiKo on karrigan rumors: "There is really no distraction from our side at all"

Falcons began IEM Rio with a win over 3DMAX, getting the job done despite going 1-9 down at the start of the series.

Most of the discourse around the team is centered around a roster change, however, as the star-studded roster is reported to be adding Finn "⁠karrigan⁠" Andersen in place of Damjan "⁠kyxsan⁠" Stoilkovski after the Brazilian tournament.

Media: karrigan to join Falcons

Nikola "⁠NiKo⁠" Kovač remained tight-lipped when asked about the change, but did squelch the rumors about him and karrigan not being on the best of terms.

"I'm not going to talk about anything about Finn [karrigan], but people really think that we had some bad blood between each other after what happened in FaZe in 2018, and there is nothing bad between us. That's it," the Bosnian rifler told HLTV in Rio.

Congrats, started off with a win, but it didn't begin the way you wanted it to with a bit of a shaky game on Inferno. You got it over the line in the end, so talk me through that game, what led to the slow start?

Yeah, it was not the best from us. Our game plan didn't really work the way we wanted it to work; they were the ones putting pressure on us. Which we kind of knew, I just think we didn't execute things properly in Banana and it just felt like we couldn't really play out the rounds.

They would do some kind of take on Banana and we would lose two guys, then we would just lose the round straight up, pretty much. So definitely something to look at and improve on for the next game, but getting the two rounds in the end on CT side helped us a lot and kinda carried the momentum on T side as well.

And then as soon as we won pistol, you just kinda... even though we were down 9-6, we felt in control of the game. I don't know, it's just very weird in these MR12 games that you're getting smashed, but all of a sudden we're the ones in control of the map. Yeah, just happy that we managed to stay in the game, close it out, and avoid overtime as well.

It's just very weird in these MR12 games that you're getting smashed, but all of a sudden we're the ones in control

A lot of the focus coming into Rio has been on Vitality's Grand Slam and whether they can get it done. Falcons, meanwhile, have been hunting for a trophy for a long time, and Vitality are looking a bit unstoppable, so what has the focus been on for you? How are you approaching that?

For us, it's been a weird season with the results. Losing two events in a row to PARIVISION, it just... it felt like we didn't really get tested. I mean, PARIVISION is one of the best teams in the world right now, but we didn't play Vitality, obviously MOUZ hasn't been that good this season, and we beat FURIA.

So it's just that we kinda really underperformed versus PARIVISION, whilst they're playing very well as well, but for us... obviously we all want to win a trophy in the team, but we know that we are not playing the best CS this season as we would have liked to play in some of these games. So I'm not sure if our level is there yet, as I said I would like to see us play against Vitality in playoffs, I would like to see us play against FURIA, MOUZ, NAVI in playoffs, so I'm looking forward to if we get to play versus Vitality.

Obviously we all want to win a trophy in the team, but we know that we are not playing the best CS this season

It's maybe going to be a match for the semi-finals, it's obviously not going to feel the same, but I always felt okay playing versus Vitality with Falcons, but also in my previous teams. So I'm looking forward if we get to play versus them, but I don't think I can say we're thinking about their Grand Slam and everything, it's really not in our control yet. We have to get to play them. But what they are doing is super impressive, I just wish to play them.

And while there has been focus on one end on Vitality, coming into this event, there has been a lot of focus on Falcons with the report that came out saying karrigan is coming into the team, potentially after this event. Can you talk to me about that, and how the team is approaching it with that report going out?

Yeah, I mean for us, we're just doing what we usually do. There is really no distraction from our side at all. I don't want to comment too much about it, and for us, I want to make sure that we give ourselves a higher chance of winning this event or going as far as possible. We are going to do everything in our power to get over the edge that we have struggled to so far this season, and this could be the event, who knows? I'm happy that we started very well versus 3DMAX, so for us, nothing has changed, honestly. We just want to focus on ourselves and try to get a good result here.

As you mentioned, it has been hard for you to get these results, get these trophies. Do you feel like, with this potential move happening, that it has taken a bit of pressure off of this event for you?

(smiles) It's a bit of a tricky question that I cannot really answer, so... yeah, we just want to, as I said, do our best in this event, and we will see how it goes.
