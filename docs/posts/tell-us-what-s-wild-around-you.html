We’d love to hear about the wildlife in your life, especially if you live in a city.

Cities are, by definition, human inventions. But there are all kinds of wild creatures that have adapted themselves to urban life.

Depending on the city or town, they’re home to foxes, mountain lions, skunks, monkeys, geese, parakeets, and yes, owls. Maybe you’ve seen them make a nest near your home. Or maybe you’ve had to stop your car so they can cross the street. Or maybe you’ve persuaded your local officials to create a wildlife corridor for them to cross. Maybe in your faith community, taking care of a wild creature brings good fortune.

Please tell us: Which wild creatures live in your city? How do you relate to them? What role do they play for you and your community?
