BC.Game bench aragornN and MUTiRiS

BC.Game have announced the benching of Christopher "⁠MUTiRiS⁠" Fernandes and António "⁠aragornN⁠" Barbosa, bringing to an end the pair's four-month stint on the starting roster.

The news comes soon after BC.Game's early exit from PGL Bucharest, where the team managed a single series win over a Voca side fielding a stand-in.

"I can confirm that Mutiris and Aragorn have been moved to the inactive roster as part of a planned roster evaluation." BC.Game's CEO Ali Muhanned said in a statement. "We recognise that we have not yet achieved the results our fans and community expect from us. We hear them, and we are committed to taking the necessary steps to compete at the highest level."

BC.Game's decision to shake up their roster comes as no surprise as the team have struggled for meaningful results since bringing in the former SAW core of MUTiRiS, aragornN and Adones "⁠krazy⁠" Nobre at the beginning of the year.

The team's first notable outing at IEM Krakow was a reasonably successful one as they made it through Stage 1 with wins over Legacy and Ninjas in Pyjamas, but results soured from there. The team suffered a string of poor results in lower-tier LAN events before pulling out of ROG Journey Spring at the end of March to "fix their chemistry."

The extended break did little for BC.Game as their form showed no signs of improvement at PGL Bucharest, where they claimed a single series win before exiting the event. All did not seem well within the camp, as MUTiRiS spoke of how "difficult" his position was within the team, saying he felt like he was "more the IGL, not the captain."
