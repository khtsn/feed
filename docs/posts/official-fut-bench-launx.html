Official: FUT bench lauNX

The Romanian teased his next move saying "both sides will have a lot to gain."

FUT have confirmed the benching of Laurențiu "⁠lauNX⁠" Țârlea in a statement on X, bringing an end to the Romanian's year-long tenure with the Turkish organization.

The announcement comes following an HLTV report that revealed the Romanian asked FUT to explore his options in the lead-up to the IEM Cologne Major.

Since then, rumors have linked the 21-year-old with HEROIC, who would be expected to trade Yasin "⁠xfl0ud⁠" Koç to FUT in return.

lauNX did not specify what his future holds, but hinted on X that it has already been determined.

"Not much can be said about what's coming next, but I can only say one thing. I have learned in life that sometimes it's good to let things go in order to have better growth, and I feel that from my next move, both sides will have a lot to gain," he said.
