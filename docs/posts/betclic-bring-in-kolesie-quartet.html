Betclic bring in KOLESIE quartet

Betclic have announced their new core lineup with the signings of Mateusz "⁠ex1st⁠" Witman and Konrad "⁠Qlocuu⁠" Klocek and the addition of Sebastian "⁠fr3nd⁠" Kuśmierz as a stand-in.

Alongside the three player additions, Grzegorz "⁠SZPERO⁠" Dziamałek joins the Polish side as its new head coach.

The quartet joins the Polish organization from KOLESIE, effectively transferring its #76 placing on the Valve Regional Standings as of July 22 to Betclic in the process.

On the new squad, they link up with Patryk "⁠Demho⁠" Tomaszewski and Wojciech "⁠Prism⁠" Zięba, the only members to remain on Betclic following the departures of Adrian "⁠Dr3nquu⁠" Wrześniewski, Olek "⁠hades⁠" Miskiewicz, Michał "⁠misho⁠" Staniszewski and Jędrzej "⁠Bogdan⁠" Rokita over the past month.

The core has already been seen playing together in the qualifiers for Esports Nations Cup as representatives of Poland, with Roland "⁠ultimate⁠" Tomkowiak playing instead of fr3nd.
