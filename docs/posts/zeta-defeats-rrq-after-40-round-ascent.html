The first half of the series began with pure dominance from RRQ on Haven. With xffero on 18 kills and the team as a whole outplaying ZETA at every given moment, there was little hope for the Japanese hopefuls. A 10-2 half from RRQ sealed ZETA's fate for the map.

A second pistol win and conversion for RRQ brought the lead to 12-2. A lead that led to the quick end of the map as RRQ took Haven 13-2 in destructive fashion. RRQ held an 80% win rate on the map throughout the VCT Pacific regular season and displayed their clear prowess on the map versus ZETA.

The second map, ZETA's pick of Split, started off similarly to the first. RRQ took the pistol and comfortably handled a second-round force buy from ZETA. ZETA took their first pistol of the series coming into the second half and followed with a bonus round win to increase their lead to 10-5. ZETA looked much more proficient on the second map and utilized their double duelist composition to great success. The duelists for ZETA, TENNN and Dep ran over their competition to secure the map 13-8 and force Ascent.

Ascent began with ZETA taking control of the early game for the first time in the series. ZETA's defense was impeccable for a majority of the half with every RRQ win being immediately shut down the following round. Dep continued to provide highlights, this time on the Sova, as ZETA led 8-4 at halftime.

RRQ took a full handle of the match to start the second half. After winning the final two rounds of the first half, they extended their streak to eight straight rounds to lead 10-8. ZETA came back late in the half to narrowly reach 12 first, however, RRQ quickly returned to force overtime.

The trading of rounds seemed infinite in overtime as attacking rounds went both ways. It wasn't until the 13th round the audience in Seoul saw a defensive round won. Despite how even the score was, RRQ's round wins seemed so much more convincing than ZETA's. However, the heroes of SugarZ3ro , Dep, and Laz provided clutches and hero plays to take a gruelling Ascent over the line for ZETA with a 21-19 win.
