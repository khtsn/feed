CS enrollments have nearly doubled in 5 years

Public undergraduate enrollments in CompSci have grown 40% from 440K in 2018 to 630K in 2023; graduate enrollments in CompSci have gone from 120K in 2019 to 220K in 2023; CompSci has comfortably edged out previously more popular majors - psych., bio., and engineering https://nscresearchcenter.org/c
