“A problem that I believe that FNATIC had for this year, at least at the start of the year, maybe is that they were scared of experimenting a bit,” Benjamin "benjyfishy" David Fish commented. “We were changing comps so many times throughout the season and I think that's why we were so good. He also doesn't believe that the experience Heretics gained this year will stifle their motivation or their identity as a young and hungry team. “All of us still have the hunger to want to win and the hunger to play and grind and everything you need to be champions, but we've also got that experience that young players don't have on big stages…so I only see it as a good thing that we got all the experience this year.”

Although confident, the team isn't feeling any pressure to perform right away in Red Bull. “If we lose, we lose,” benjyfishy said. “Naturally people are going to have high standards for us, but we never put expectations on ourselves; we never take outside views or outside perspectives.”

As for the 2025 season, things like expectations or winning it all haven't even crossed his mind. “I like to try and live in the present, and right now all I'm focused on is what I've got to do today.”

“Maybe when I come back to Berlin in like two months for the season…but for now, I'm playing, I'm practicing, I'm trying to work on myself, get better as a player, as a person, and hopefully this event will help [me] do that.”
