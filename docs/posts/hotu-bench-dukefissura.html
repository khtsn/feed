HOTU bench dukefissura

HOTU have announced the benching of Suleımen "⁠dukefissura⁠" Orazaly, bringing to an end the 29-year-old's year-long stint on the starting roster.

The news comes in the wake of HOTU's appearance at IEM Rio, where the team grabbed a win over Legacy but exited the tournament in 9-12th after losses to Aurora and Natus Vincere.

"This was not an easy decision for us to make," HOTU said in their announcement on Telegram. "We have shared many matches, emotions and hard work together. We are grateful to him for his contribution to the team and for the journey we have taken together."

dukefissura joined HOTU in April 2025 and has been a part of the best period in the organization's history, during which time they have attended a number of high-profile international LANs and cemented themselves as one of the better squads outside of tier-one, peaking at No. 19 in the VRS.

HOTU's most memorable run during dukefissura's tenure came at ESL Pro League Season 22, where the team stormed through Stage 1 with a 3-0 record that featured a win over FURIA in the opening round. The team bagged another notable win over The MongolZ in Stage 2 before exiting the tournament prior to the playoffs.

Throughout his time with the team dukefissura served as one of their most aggressive pieces, taking the most opening attempts on both T and CT side, but was also one of their worst performers statistically.
