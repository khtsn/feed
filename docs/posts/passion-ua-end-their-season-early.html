Passion UA end their season early

The Ukranian organization has withdrawn from BC Game Masters Championship S2.

Passion UA are ending their season prematurely by withdrawing from BC Game Masters Championship Season 2, the team have announced.

The squad went out of IEM Atlanta in last place last week, in what has proved to be their final result of the season.
