IEM Cologne Stage 3 opening matches set

After two Swiss stages at IEM Cologne, the final 16 teams are finally ready to play the final group stage, all in best-of-threes, to decide what eight teams will advance to the playoffs at the LANXESS Arena.

Teams like BetBoom and Monte made it to Stage 3 all the way from Stage 1, and were joined by 9z, FUT, Legacy, G2, Spirit, and Monte in Stage 2. Now, they go up against the eight teams invited directly to the final stage: Vitality, Natus Vincere, MOUZ, Falcons, The MongolZ, Aurora, FURIA, and PARIVISION.

The match to watch in the opening round of best-of-threes will be between two top-five teams: Natus Vincere vs. Spirit. The two met in Cologne last year, clashing in the semi-finals, where Danil "⁠donk⁠" Kryshkovets and his teammates prevailed in three maps. They only met one more time since then, with Spirit doubling down to take another victory at IEM Krakow.

The world's best, Vitality, open against the rising stars of FUT, while Falcons' Nikola "⁠NiKo⁠" Kovač — now under the leadership of Finn "⁠karrigan⁠" Andersen —, will go up against his cousin and former teammate Nemanja "⁠huNter-⁠" Kovač and his G2.
