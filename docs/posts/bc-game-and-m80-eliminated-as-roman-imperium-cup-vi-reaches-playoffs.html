BC.Game and M80 eliminated as Roman Imperium Cup VI reaches playoffs

BC.Game, the highest-ranked VRS team of the event, had a default win and three losses.

The group stage of the Roman Imperium Cup VI came to an end on Friday, with BC.Game and M80 among the teams that have been sent packing before the playoffs.

Ranked 32nd in the world heading into the event, BC.Game finished fourth in their group after losses to Alliance, Nexus and OG. Their other game was a default win over GRINGOS, who didn't make the trip to Portugal.

It is yet another blow to a team that has been sliding down the rankings since their promising debut at IEM Kraków. A top-six finish at Parken Challenger Championship Season 1 and a group stage exit at Roman Imperium Cup have left them sitting at 42nd in the global VRS.
