Sentinels allow pANcada to look for offers with no buyout

1 day ago

Sentinels Sentinels North America Rank #3 TenZ Tyson Ngo Zellsis Jordan Montemurro zekken Zachary Patrone johnqt Mohamed Amine Ouarid Sacy Gustavo Rossi announced that they're allowing their sixth man pANcada to look for other competitive Valorant offers, with no buyout for his contract.

Entering the 2024 season, pANcada looked to be part of Sentinels' future. Picking up Zellsis as a sixth man proved to be the key to Sentinels' success both in the off-season and the Americas Kickoff. He filled in for pANcada, and their victory in the first tournament of the season solidified their starting roster heading into Masters Madrid.

With that outcome, Sentinels have allowed the Brazilian star and former world champion in 2022 to look for other teams to join in VCT. He could have faced contractual obligations that held him away from a quick move, similarly to Evil Geniuses Evil Geniuses North America Rank #1 Derrek Derrek Ha NaturE Nicholas Garrison supamen Phat Le Apoth Vincent Le jawgemo Alexander Mor ' players in the 2023 off-season, but Sentinels stated that pANcada has no buyout. This means that any team can get the player as long as they agree to a contract.

Due to the lack of a buyout, pANcada is now a talented free agent, available as other VCT teams look to make changes before the regular season begins.
