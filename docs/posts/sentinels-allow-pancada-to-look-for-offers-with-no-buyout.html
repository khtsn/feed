Sentinels allow pANcada to look for offers with no buyout

Sentinels Sentinels North America Rank #3 TenZ Tyson Ngo Zellsis Jordan Montemurro zekken Zachary Patrone johnqt Mohamed Amine Ouarid Sacy Gustavo Rossi announced that they're allowing their sixth man pANcada to look for other competitive Valorant offers, with no buyout for his contract.

Entering the 2024 season, pANcada looked to be part of Sentinels' future. Picking up Zellsis as a sixth man proved to be the key to Sentinels' success both in the off-season and the Americas Kickoff. He filled in for pANcada, and their victory in the first tournament of the season solidifed their starting roster heading into Masters Madrid.

With that outcome, Sentinels have allowed the Brazilian star and former world champion in 2022 to look for other teams to join in VCT. Unlike other contractual obligations that held players away from quick moves in the 2023 off-season, pANcada notably has no buyout, meaning that any team can get the player as long as they agree to a contract.
