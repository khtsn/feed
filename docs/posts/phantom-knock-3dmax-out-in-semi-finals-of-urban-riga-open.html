Phantom knock 3DMAX out in semi-finals of Urban Riga Open

Phantom have knocked 3DMAX out of Urban Riga Open Season 3 at the semi-final stage, recovering from a drubbing on Nuke (5-13) by securing victories on Dust2 (13-11) and Ancient (13-10).

The result not only sends 3DMAX home from the competition, but also puts a dent in their hopes of qualification for IEM Cologne Major.

The French side were, according to Mischief's prediction, in pole position amongst those chasing one of the last few Europe qualification spots, but this result sees them end the tournament on essentially the same VRS points total as when they started.
