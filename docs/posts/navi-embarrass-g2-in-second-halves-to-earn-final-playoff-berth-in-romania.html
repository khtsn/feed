NAVI embarrass G2 in second halves to earn final playoff berth in Romania

Natus Vincere locked in the final playoff berth at PGL Cluj-Napoca over G2 after coming alive late on Dust2 (13-7) and Ancient (13-9), recovering from 3-7 and 1-9 deficits, respectively, off the back of stellar halves by Mihai "⁠iM⁠" Ivan and Valeriy "⁠b1t⁠" Vakhovskiy.

G2 suffered an early trip to the 0-2 pool in Cluj after losses to Vitality and PARIVISION, but earned a shot at playoffs after beating an ailing paiN and an Alimzhan "⁠Alkaren⁠" Bitimbay-less HEROIC. But those victories will offer small solace to G2 after they fell apart against NAVI, letting big leads slip on both maps.

iM helped lead the charge in NAVI's recovery on Dust2 to earn himself an appearance in front of his home crowd, while b1t took over on Ancient to pull his team out of a rut after a miserable first 10 rounds.

G2 struggled in opening duels for much of the first half of Dust2, but made up for it by punishing Natus Vincere for sloppy mistakes in the mid-round. Multi-kills from Nikita "⁠HeavyGod⁠" Martynenko, Mario "⁠malbsMd⁠" Samayoa, and Nemanja "⁠huNter-⁠" Kovač spurred G2 to a 7-3 lead, with NAVI unable to build upon their second-round force-buy win, but iM salvaged the last two of the half with back-to-back triple kills to give his team a fighting chance.

The Romanian took over after the swap, finding four multi-kills — including two from 3v5 disadvantages — to power an unstoppable second half for Natus Vincere. Two clutches going their way, one from Aleksi "⁠Aleksib⁠" Virolainen and one from b1t, helped that charge, and NAVI ended the map winning ten rounds straight to seal victory.

The tables turned at the start of Ancient, with HeavyGod and Álvaro "⁠SunPayus⁠" García seemingly unkillable on the defense. Natus Vincere fumbled mid-round after mid-round and couldn't find a way out despite several advantageous situations, and were left staring down a 1-9 deficit and an almost guaranteed trip to the Inferno decider.

But the wheels fell off the bus at that moment as b1t took over, grabbing a double kill to get NAVI their second, a quad kill and 1v2 clutch for their third, and another triple kill and 1v2 clutch to secure the second pistol round. G2 looked lost and capitulated entirely to an immovable NAVI defense, and the few rounds they had a shot in, they fumbled.

b1t tallied an 18-5 K-D, 156.7 ADR, +18.91% Round Swing, and 2.79 rating on the CT side as NAVI won 12 rounds straight to embarrass G2 out of the server and seal the final playoffs berth.
