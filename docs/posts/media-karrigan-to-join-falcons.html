Media: karrigan to join Falcons

Finn "⁠karrigan⁠" Andersen is set to join forces with Falcons, according to French insider Sebastien "⁠KRL⁠" Perez, a move corroborated by HLTV's own sources. karrigan's addition to the team is expected to come to fruition after the conclusion of Falcons' next outing at IEM Rio.

The Danish skipper is expected to take the place of Damjan "⁠kyxsan⁠" Stoilkovski, putting an end to the North Macedonian's year-and-a-half-long tenure as Falcons' captain.

This comes just a week after FaZe's desperate attempt to qualify for the IEM Cologne Major. The international side was far from guaranteed a place at the Major after a string of dismal results at tier-one LANs, but ended up missing out entirely after failing to go far enough in DraculaN Season 6 and HLC Belgrade PRO, leaving them just outside the ranking cutoff.

karrigan's addition to Falcons would mark the first change to the Saudi organization's lineup since the team acquired Maksim "⁠kyousuke⁠" Lukin in June of last year. Following the landmark signing, Falcons have failed to secure any trophies, with their best showings being runner-up finishes at ESL Pro League Season 22, BLAST Rivals 2025 Season 2, and BLAST Bounty 2026 Season 1.

This move would reunite karrigan with long-time FaZe teammate Nikola "⁠NiKo⁠" Kovač for the first time in over seven years. karrigan would also be once again joining forces with coach Danny "⁠zonic⁠" Sørensen, having previously worked with him on Astralis in 2016.

Falcons will have two outings ahead of the Major following IEM Rio, scheduled to play PGL Astana and CS Asia Championships in May. In the meantime, FaZe have just over two weeks to figure out their starting five for their next outing at BLAST Rivals 2026 Season 1, slated to begin April 29.
