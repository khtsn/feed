BLEED announce roster overhaul, bench four players

1 day ago

BLEED BLEED Asia-Pacific Rank #25 sScary Nutchapon Matarat crazyguy Ngô Công Anh have announced plans to overhaul their roster for VCT Pacific 2025. Derrick "Deryeon" Yee, Jorell "Retla" Teo, and Kim "Zest" Ki-seok (김기석) have joined Jaccob "yay" Whiteaker on the inactive roster. All four players are now able to explore their options as restricted free agents.

BLEED freshly entered VCT Pacific this year as the champions of Pacific Ascension 2023. During the offseason, Tyler "Juicy" James Aeria and Marcus "nephh" Tan departed from the team due to the former's ineligibility to play in VCT Pacific and the latter's required military service. They were subsequently replaced by yay and Javier "Egoist" Chua coming into Kickoff.

Following an unceremonious 0-2 elimination from the group stage of Kickoff to start their season, BLEED continued to make roster moves. They parted ways with Egoist and benched Ngô "crazyguy" Công Anh, bringing in Zest and Retla and moving Nutchapon "sScary" Matarat to the in-game leader role for Stage 1. These changes did not improve the team's results as they went 1-4 in Stage 1, their only win for the season coming against DetonatioN FocusMe DetonatioN FocusMe Japan Rank #6 Medusa An Min-cheol (안민철) neth Yusuke Matsuda Meiy Ibuki Seki SSeeS Tomonori Okimura Anthem Yoshitaka Enomoto .

Following Stage 1, yay stepped back from the starting roster due to health reasons and crazyguy returned to the in-game leader position. Despite taking maps off of strong opponents such as Team Secret Team Secret Asia-Pacific Rank #8 invy Adrian Jiggs Reyes JessieVash Jessie Cuyco Wild0reoo Brheyanne Christ Reyes 2ge James Goopio Jremy Jeremy Cabrera and Gen.G Gen.G Korea Rank #1 Munchkin Byeon Sang-beom (변상범) t3xture Kim Na-ra (김나라) Meteor Kim Tae-O (김태오) Lakia Kim Jong-min (김종민) Karon Kim Won-tae (김원태) , BLEED failed to secure a win in Stage 2 and did not qualify for playoffs, cutting their year short.

BLEED have promised more updates on their roster in the coming months. The players have not yet announced their respective plans for next season.
