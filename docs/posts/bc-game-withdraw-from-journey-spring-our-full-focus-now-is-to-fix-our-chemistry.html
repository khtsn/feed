BC.Game withdraw from JOURNEY Spring: "Our full focus now is to fix our chemistry"

BC.Game have withdrawn from ROG JOURNEY Spring, tournament organizer Black Molly Entertainment has announced, resulting in the team forfeiting its matches at the $30,000 LAN in Stockholm, Sweden.

Groups had already been drawn for the event, which is scheduled for March 23-28, with BC.Game placed in Group C alongside Betclic, HEROIC Academy, and facing a VRS unranked team, Västerås, in their opener.

The decision means BC.Game will tumble even further down the VRS, where they are currently ranked 41st and were already all but out of contention for a spot at the IEM Cologne Major.
