ESL announce IEM Rio talent list

ESL has announced the list of broadcast talent for IEM Rio, slated to run from April 13-19 with a $1,000,000 prize pool.

The tournament organizer's lineup will feature a new face alongside its regulars Freya "⁠Freya⁠" Spiers, Tres "⁠stunna⁠" Saranthus, Janko "⁠YNk⁠" Paunović and Jacob "⁠Pimp⁠" Winneche, with Martin "⁠STYKO⁠" Styk joining an ESL broadcast for the first time.

The Slovakian twice featured as an analyst at BLAST Fall and World Finals in 2024, and will reprise the role of sideline reporter and interviewer that he occupied at StarLadder Budapest Major and Stake Ranked Episode 1.

ESL's regular commentary duos of Chad "⁠SPUNJ⁠" Burchill and Alex "⁠Machine⁠" Richardson and Harry "⁠JustHarry⁠" Russell and Hugo "⁠Hugo⁠" Byron will walk viewers through the action on the A stream, while B stream action will be covered by Neo "⁠Ne0kai⁠" Caine, Harvey "Skriv" Rodgers, Matthew "HAzE" Stott, and Travis "Trav" Landaw Mott.

Four Brazilian commentators also feature on the list for the organizer's local broadcast, while Nyvi "⁠Nyvi Estephan⁠" Estephan will handle sideline and stage reporting.
