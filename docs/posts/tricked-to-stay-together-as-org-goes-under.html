Tricked to stay together as org goes under

The POWER Ligaen Season 31 champions will remain together

Legendary Gaming Group, the company co-founded by Danish footballer Nicklas Bendtner, Tricked's original founder Morten "⁠Phy⁠" Jensen, and Atle Stehouwer, which was created to keep the Tricked brand in CS, is going out of business.

A parallel venture run by Stehouwer, New Breed Agency, which ran the Parken Challenger Championship, is also in the process of filing for bankruptcy after announcing the cancellation of all future tournaments. Legendary Gaming Group helped finance the TO.

Tricked's coach, Allan "⁠Rejin⁠" Petersen, has stated that the Danish POWER Ligaen Season 31 champions and 50th-ranked team in the VRS will stay together while looking for a new home.

"The last period has been turbulent and far from easy, but we strongly believe in each other and are confident we have a bright future ahead," he wrote on X.

"We have a big LAN coming up and are determined to use it as a chance to prove that we belong among the best," he added. "We’re actively looking for a new organization that sees the potential in this roster."

Tricked, active in the tier-two VRS LAN circuit leading up to the Major, were recently top four at Urban Riga Open Season 4 and top eight at BC Gaming Masters Championship Season 1, where they beat the likes of Passion UA and HEROIC.
