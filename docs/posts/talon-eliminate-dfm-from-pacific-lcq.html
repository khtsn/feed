Talon eliminate DFM from Pacific LCQ

DFM came into the series as the only team without a win to their name in VCT, while Talon hoped to build upon their improvements found towards the end of the VCT Pacific regular season.

The series began on DFM's map pick of Fracture where they quickly came onto the back foot. Four straight rounds for the opposition in convincing fashion left hopes low for the DFM side before a 1v2 from Anthem turned the tide of the half. DFM took the next five in cleanly before Talon could answer back, tying the score at five. Talon managed to take control towards the tail end of their attack following a 1v2 from foxz , despite a 17/8 KD from Anthem.

After a strong showing in the initial few gun rounds of the second half, a brutal blunder from takej in a 1v1 against foxz handed Talon a thrifty win that spiralled into four straight rounds to take the first map of the series 13-7. Regardless of his team's loss, Anthem topped the server with 22 kills, looking like a shining light for a struggling DFM.

DFM switched things up for their opponent's map pick of Ascent with Seoldam subbing in for xnfri to play Jett. However, it was Anthem who would continue to provide dividends for his team with a 1v2 win in the pistol with two shock dart kills. After faltering in the first four rounds Talon came back into the game in stride with seven out of the next eight rounds to lead 7-5 at halftime.

After conceding the pistol, DFM looked towards Anthem for a hero play in round 17 with a 1v3 win to stabilize a worsening half. However, a thrifty win for Talon put the nail in the coffin of DFM as they secured the map at 12-8 and the series 2-0.

DFM remains winless in VCT and have been eliminated from the Pacific LCQ.
