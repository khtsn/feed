    ESL has released an update to its Annual Club Incentive standings, showcasing the points teams earned for their highest average concurrent viewership between Stage 1 and 2.

    Spirit topped the board to earn full marks along with G2, Natus Vincere and FURIA. All points-earning teams can be found below:

    1. Spirit - 170,973 average CCV (14 points)

    2. G2 - 156,459 (14)

    3. Natus Vincere - 144,181 (14)

    4. FURIA - 142,104 (14)

    5. B8 - 132,571 (10.5)

    6. Astralis - 130,525 (10.5)

    7. 3DMAX - 123,247 (10.5)

    8. PARIVISION - 115,546 (10.5)

    9. MOUZ - 114,762 (7)

    10. The MongolZ - 113,797 (7)

    11. FaZe - 102,839 (7)

    12. Legacy - 102,242 (7)

    The full standings after two ESL events have been played can be found in ESL's post.

    FACEIT has announced that starting with Season 8, the available map pool for games on its platform will expand from the regular seven Active Duty maps to eight.

    A community vote will decide the extra map from a shortlist of three created by the 1,000 highest Elo players, who have narrowed the selection down to Vertigo, Train, and Cache.

    Aurora have announced they will be without coach Sezgin "⁠Fabre⁠" Kalaycı at DraculaN Season 6, where the Turkish team will begin its run from the double-elimination playoffs bracket.

    "Unfortunately, coach Fabre will not be able to be with us throughout the tournament due to family reasons," the organization said in a post on X.

    "As a team, we will continue to give our best in the tournament, carrying forward the dedication and hard work that Fabre is sharing with us."
