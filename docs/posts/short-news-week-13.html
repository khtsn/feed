Short news: Week 13

RELOG have posted an admission of an error in their seeding process that saw four teams be added to FaZe's group at HLC.

"There was an error in the seeding process. Initially, we placed one Cypriot team in each group, which would have been correct if all 16 teams had been confirmed.

"However, after consulting HLTV and reviewing the situation internally, we have been advised to include the Cypriot teams in the stardard serpentine seeding used for Group Stage. As a result, Re’Di will now be placed in Group D alongside another team who qualified from Cyprus qualifier."

Dangerous day to be a reporter...

The next five Parken Challenger events will be significantly more accessible after the tournament organizers slashed the entry fee from €1,200 to €300 per team.

"The decision comes after strong feedback from teams, players and the wider community across the opening events of the circuit," a statement said. "Importantly, the reduce fee does not change the product. Teams entering events 4-8 will still receive the same event quality, the same production quality, and the same guaranteed prize pool as previously announced"
