He made his debut on the international stage at Masters Tokyo, although his team was quickly eliminated in two matches.

With the introduction of the CN league, monk earned a coveted spot in the partnership league with All Gamers. In 2024, they were a mid-table team, ending the regular season with a 5-5 record and qualifying to the playoffs of both Stage 1 and Stage 2. While they didn't make it past the first round of either playoffs, it was a decent first year for the organization.

In 2025, All Gamers took an unfortunate tumble down the rankings. With their massive roster and revolving door of players, their quick 0-2 exit from Kickoff led to monk being taken off the starting roster to make room to debut their new pickups.

With monk now being released, All Gamers has reduced their player roster size to nine.
