100 Thieves to make LAN debut vs NIP at Roman Imperium Cup V

100 Thieves will take on NIP on LAN later this month

The organizers behind the Roman Imperium Cup V have released the schedule for the upcoming tournament, which will take place at the SAW Esports Arena in Vila Nova de Gaia between February 19-22.

The 16 teams have been split into four double-elimination groups, with seeding determined by the February 2 invitational ranking. That means that the new 100 Thieves lineup, which remains unranked, has been placed in a very tricky group that also includes Ninjas in Pyjamas, BESTIA and Monte.

Groups A and B will take place on opening day, February 19, while C and B will follow on February 20. The quarter-finals will go live on the 21st, and the semi-finals, third-place decider, and grand final will put an end to the event when a champion is crowned on the 22nd.
