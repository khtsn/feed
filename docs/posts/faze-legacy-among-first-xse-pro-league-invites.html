FaZe, Legacy among first XSE Pro League invites

The organizers of XSE Pro League 2026 have announced the first eight invites for the $1,000,000 tournament held in Guangzhou, China.

The event is set to be played from July 1-12, which places it inside the summer break, but some notable names have already confirmed their attendance.

The team list is headlined by Legacy, who recently rose to No. 6 in the VRS thanks to their CS Asia Championship triumph, and FaZe, who will be looking to get back on track under Russel "⁠Twistzz⁠" Van Dulken's leadership.

Swedish duo Alliance and EYEBALLERS have also confirmed their attendance, as have BetBoom and Ninjas in Pyjamas.

Eight more teams will be directly invited via the Global VRS, with the sixteen teams then starting the event with a Swiss bracket feeding into an eight-team playoff.
