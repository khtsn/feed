CLZ was on FENNEL's roster for three years before the move to ZETA, mostly playing the Initiator role for the squad while also showing flexibility in other roles, like Sage or other Sentinels. "I can play any position or role and will strive to perform my best in the role assigned to me," CLZ said. "I believe I can prove my strength and am confident in raising a trophy."
