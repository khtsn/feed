PARIVISION bench nota; sign slaxejezzz

PARIVISION has announced that 19-year-old Emil "⁠nota⁠" Moskvitin, one of the youngsters who helped the team rise to prominence with a victory at BLAST Bounty Season 1 earlier in the year, has been moved to the bench and transfer-listed.

Taking his place in the starting five will be Vyacheslav "⁠slaxejezzz⁠" Vinokurov, currently #47 on the HLTV Prospects ranking as of the June update.

The 18-year-old has a 1.25 rating in the past three months, and has started to make a name for himself after playing in low-ranked Russian teams appearing on HLTV since February of 2025.
