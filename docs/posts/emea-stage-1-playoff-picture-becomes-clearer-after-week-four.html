EMEA Stage 1 playoff picture becomes clearer after week four

The fourth week of VCT EMEA Stage 1 has wrapped up, with the playoff field now shaping up with one week left to go in the group stage.

Here's how the week four matches played out:

  • Eternal Fire 2-1 PCIFIC Esports
  • Team Liquid 2-1 Natus Vincere
  • FNATIC 2-1 GIANTX
  • FUT Esports 2-1 Karmine Corp
  • Team Heretics 2-0 Gentle Mates
  • BBL Esports 2-1 Team Vitality

Now, here's a look at the standings in each group and the work left to be done for each team to qualify for the playoffs.

FUT has completed one of the most impressive mid-season roster rebuilds that VCT has seen in recent years, going from 0-3 in Kickoff to 4-0 in Stage 1 with Group Alpha's top seed secured. This week, they secured a 2-1 win over Karmine Corp, bouncing back from a rough first map on Haven to seal wins on Lotus and Bind. xeus had a match-high 56 kills on Phoenix, Chamber, and Raze, while s0pp was on Neon duty for the entirety of the series.

Playoff outlook: Playoffs and first seed secured thanks to head-to-head advantage over Team Liquid

Apart from its loss to FUT, Team Liquid has been the next-most dominant force in Group Alpha. Against NAVI this week, Liquid grinded out a 2-1 win, with both teams winning their respective map picks before in-game leader nAts called a near-perfect map on Lotus to lead the team to a 13-7 win. kamo was especially impressive in this series, dropping 65 kills while going 13-5 in first duels.

Team Heretics has had one of the more chaotic Stage 1 campaigns, parting ways with ComeBack and bringing in koshmaras in his place. The move has shifted Wo0t into the team's primary Duelist role, and resulted in the team going 2-1 since. With a much more rigid double-Duelist structure, Team Heretics posted RieNs on Fade, benjyfishy as Sentinel, and Boo as Controller in the team's 2-0 win over Gentle Mates. koshmaras had his most impressive outing in a Heretics jersey, dropping a match-high 44 kills to lead Heretics to a crucial win.

As one of the teams most affected by the Yoru nerfs, Gentle Mates has had an up-and-down Stage 1, suffering a 2-0 loss to Team Heretics last week. While marteen has adapted by picking up agents like Phoenix, Raze, Waylay, and Veto, the team surrounding him looks to be different from the team that took to the stage at Masters Santiago. The Mates will secure playoffs with a win over Team Liquid, but will need help from elsewhere if they wish to start in the upper bracket.

Playoff outlook: Playoffs secured. Could secure second seed with win over TL and TH loss. Could be third or fourth with win, dependent on other results. Could be in three-way tie with loss to TL, TH loss to FUT, and NAVI win over KC.

NAVI needs an attainable win over Karmine Corp and losses for two other teams in order to even have a chance at reaching the playoffs.
