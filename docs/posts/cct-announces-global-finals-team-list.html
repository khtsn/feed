CCT announces Global Finals team list

100 Thieves return to Vila Nova de Gaia, where they first debuted with a top-eight finish at Roman Imperium Cup V

CCT has announced the eight teams invited to compete at the Global Finals, a $75,000 competition taking place from April 23-26 in Vila Nova de Gaia, Portugal.

Monte, HEROIC, illwill, Ninjas in Pyjamas, BIG and Sharks received direct invites via their Global standing on the VRS as of March 2.

They will be joined by two wildcard invites: Betclic, who won the second Parken Challenger Championship (PCC), and 100 Thieves.

GamerLegion had originally been slated to receive the second wildcard invite after their win at the first PCC, but declined due to their participation at BLAST Rivals, set to start three days following the conclusion of the CCT Global Finals.

The Håvard "⁠rain⁠" Nygaard-led squad was chosen as the replacement, eligible for the wildcard invite thanks to its runners-up finish at DraculaN Season 5.
