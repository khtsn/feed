VCT Champions: China Qualifier Playoffs viewer's guide

China will see its first LAN event just ahead of the official launch for Valorant in the region with twelve teams competing for the coveted three slots at Champions later this August. The play-in and double elimination bracket will be played between July 3 and 16 in Shanghai.

The winner of each play-in matches will meet the top seeded teams - who gained a bye into the tournament based on their previous results in Act 1 and Act 2 of the FGC Valorant Invitational this year. The top seeded teams are:

The eight preliminary teams will have to face each other in a single-elimination qualifier bracket before moving further into the tournament, where the winners of each bracket match will face against their respective top seeded teams in a double-elimination format.

Unlike most VCT events, only the knockout round, upper quarterfinals, and the first two lower bracket rounds that will be a Bo3. The rest of the tournament will be played in a Bo5.

The top three teams from this qualifier will represent China in Champions Los Angeles this August.
