FaZe to begin PGL Bucharest 0-2 amid HLC Belgrade PRO attendance

Another notable result on the first day was FOKUS taking down 3DMAX in a 2-1 affair. Though the young international side helmed by Erik "⁠ztr⁠" Gustafsson and coach Jakub "⁠kuben⁠" Gurczyński have been together for just under two months, they have already notched some respectable results, namely qualifying for PGL Bucharest via the open qualifier, hoisting the title at Urban Riga Open Season 3, and now securing an opening best-of-three victory in their first big LAN outing.

Elsewhere on day one, NRG secured a lopsided victory over domestic rivals Voca, FUT came back from a map deficit to claim a win in their series against Inner Circle, and The MongolZ bested BC.Game in two maps to move on to the 1-0 pool.

Though FOKUS will be celebrating their win over 3DMAX to start their Bucharest run, they now face a daunting matchup against PARIVISION, the highest-ranked side in attendance, in their 1-0 series. The other 1-0 games will feature The MongolZ clashing with EYEBALLERS, Astralis tussling with B8, and FUT taking on NRG.
