Player prices & pick stats

  • Danil "⁠donk⁠" Kryshkovets once again becomes most-picked player despite the high price and also being on the deadly side of the bracket with Falcons or Vitality as Spirit’s potential semi-final opponents. Whether picking donk will pay off or not will heavily depend on the outcome of the first two games, and unlike previous stages, Spirit are not clear favorites.

  • The bracket situation partly informed the prices that lead to relatively high prices for FURIA who are favorites on their side of the bracket, leading to Kaike "⁠KSCERATO⁠" Cerato and Danil "⁠molodoy⁠" Golubenko both being golden cards.

  • Kirill "⁠Boombl4⁠" Mikhailov remains in the top picks even for playoffs after heroics in previous stages, including the 2-2 match against FUT where the Rusisan IGL had put up a rating of 1.66. At $175,000, he’s one of the cheapest players in this game as well, making him an alluring pick for those who want to accommodate the heavy hitters like donk and Mathieu "⁠ZywOo⁠" Herbaut.

  • Finn "⁠karrigan⁠" Andersen’s case is the opposite of Boombl4: the Danish IGL’s performance was record-low, and it lead to him being the cheapest player in the game at $167,000, which is even less than the cheapest players on the playoffs underdog teams. Normally, such a cheap IGL on a team that is a relative favorite is a steal, but this time karrigan is only the tenth most picked on the game.
