FURIA battle past NAVI into Rio playoffs

FURIA have claimed a playoff berth at IEM Rio by virtue of a 2-1 victory over Natus Vincere, coming back from a spanking on Mirage (3-13) with wins on Dust2 (13-4) and Nuke (16-13).

After the teams traded two blowout maps, Natus Vincere will be left scratching their heads as to how they let a 9-3 lead slip on Nuke, particularly as they were heading into the favored CT side.

The European combine had chances to seal the deal in the second half, but FURIA were simply more resilient in late-round scenarios, finishing the map 3-0 on clutches and taking the series.

FURIA can now look forward to a playoff campaign in their core's home country, whilst Natus Vincere will have to fight through the lower bracket, starting with the winner of HOTU vs. Legacy

Natus Vincere cruised to an easy victory on Mirage with Mihai "⁠iM⁠" Ivan and Ihor "⁠w0nderful⁠" Zhdanov having a field day. The European combine won out the force-buy wars early on their T half to take full control of the economy and sail to a 9-3 halftime lead, before sealing the deal quickly with a short and sweet CT half.

The shoe was on the other foot on Dust2 as it was FURIA who dominated, Gabriel "⁠FalleN⁠" Toledo calling fantastic mid-rounds as Danil "⁠molodoy⁠" Golubenko and Yuri "⁠yuurih⁠" Santos came alive to put Natus Vincere to the sword in a one-sided 10-2 T half.

Natus Vincere teased a flash of a comeback in the second half, but a high-impact FalleN multi-kill with the Scout put the map to bed before Natus Vincere could really get going.

Natus Vincere's varied T-side playbook was too much for FURIA to handle on Nuke, Aleksi "⁠Aleksib⁠" Virolainen utilizing that depth to the fullest to secure a 9-3 lead at the half. FURIA answered back however, late-round resilience seeing them claw their way back into the game.

With a FURIA comeback win seeming a certainty Natus Vincere responded, stopping two Upper hits in a row to get to series point, but FURIA's Tec-9s prevailed to send the series to OT, where they duly converted and bagged a playoff berth.
