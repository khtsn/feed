NAVI's season did not start in the way they had perhaps hoped. The Masters Copenhagen winners found themselves on the losing end of a 14-round overtime against KOI KOI Europe Rank #26 starxo Patryk Kopczynski koldamenta Jose Luis Aranguren sheydos Bogdan Naumov trexx Nikita Cherednichenko Wolfen Berkant Joshkun on Monday, resulting in a 2-0 loss against the Spanish org. In a test of endurance, NAVI walked away with a 13-7 win on the final map, which ended up being the only one-sided map all day. The series lasted nearly four hours from start to finish as NAVI and Giants duked it out in several long overtimes, totalling 12 extra rounds across the series.

Even in regulation, the teams were closely matched. Giants opened strong with a 4-0 start on Icebox, but NAVI responded with four of their own. After both teams picked up a pair of rounds each, the score stood level at halftime. NAVI earned themselves a lead in the second half despite losing the pistol round, and ANGE1 's squad got to map point first at 12-10. However, Giants kept their cool, picked up the last two rounds, and secured overtime.

In overtime, the squads found themselves stalemated, with both sides trading defensive rounds back and forth. In round 32, nukkye finally managed to break the deadlock, taking three scalps and securing his team a 17-15 victory. The Lithuanian had been a star for Giants all game, putting up 30 kills and a 1.46 rating.

nukkye takes down four on his own.

Despite losing the first two rounds, NAVI took control of the second map with a six-round streak. Giants regained their footing a little in the second half, but faced a 7-5 deficit at halftime nevertheless. The teams were evenly matched in the second half, but NAVI got to map point first at 12-10 thanks to their previous advantage. However, like on Icebox, Giants were strong in these dying moments of regulation and secured overtime on map two.

Giants drew first blood in overtime, but NAVI showed resilience by taking three rounds in a row and securing a 15-13 victory on Split. For the Ukrainian team, this was only their first map win in VCT EMEA, and it was thanks to a team effort on the scoreboard. All five NAVI players were within three kills of each other, with Shao , Zyppan and cNed all sharing the top spot at 20.

NAVI decided they had seen enough overtime and closed the series out in regulation on Pearl. The first half was nonetheless as close as the rest of the series had been, with no team being able to string together more than two rounds, and NAVI only eking out a slight 7-5 advantage. In the second half, NAVI broke away at last, winning six of eight attacking rounds to take a 13-7 victory and the series win.

For both NAVI and Giants, the super week now draws to a close. Despite a difficult loss on Monday, NAVI recovered and now sit midtable with a 1-1 record. However, they were just one Split overtime round away from starting the season without a map win, which would have been less than ideal for the LOCK//IN semifinalists.

Giants currently occupy the bottom spot in the table with a 0-2 record. However, there's no reason to sweat just yet for the Spanish org, as they got two difficult fixtures out of the way against the two highest-placing EMEA teams at LOCK//IN in FNATIC and NAVI.
