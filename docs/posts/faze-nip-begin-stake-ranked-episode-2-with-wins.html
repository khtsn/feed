FaZe, NIP begin Stake Ranked Episode 2 with wins

Stake Ranked Episode 2 began with the first four opening matches being played out on Wednesday.

magic got an upset win over 3DMAX in the first match of the day before Russel "⁠Twistzz⁠" Van Dulken helped FaZe beat Alliance in a two-map series with a 1.70 rating.
