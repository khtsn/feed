PARIVISION eliminate BIG from XSE Pro League

The Jame-led squad took the series in three maps to secure a spot in the semi-finals.

PARIVISION are through to the XSE Pro League Guangzhou semi-finals after defeating BIG 2-1 in the first round of the playoff bracket.

The series kicked off on the newest addition to the map pool, Cache, where BIG started with a strong 9-3 CT half. Although they did go on to win it in the end, it was after a double overtime, when three CT rounds in a row opened the door for a 19-15 victory.

PARIVISION then dominated on their Mirage pick, where Vyacheslav "⁠slaxejezzz⁠" Vinokurov (#44 HLTV Prospects) posted a 2.08 rating in the 13-5 victory to tie the series back up and force the decider.

PARIVISION's newest addition was again the highest-rated player on the server, this time with a 1.53 in the 13-9 win on Ancient that punched their ticket to the next round of the playoffs.

A poor showing on Cache kept slaxejezzz second on the board after the series, with a 1.23 average after the three maps, while Ivan "⁠zweih⁠" Gogin's 1.40 put him at the top, but the newcomer led the charge in his team's map wins.

Now, PARIVISION will face one of two big hitters at XPL: Major quarter-finalists BetBoom, who are so far undefeated in Guangzhou, or FaZe, who turned a slow 0-2 start in the group stage best-of-ones into a playoff run with excellent play by a youngster of their own, Jason "⁠JBOEN⁠" Boe Nielsen.
