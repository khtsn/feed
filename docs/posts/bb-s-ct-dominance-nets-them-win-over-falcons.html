BB's CT dominance nets them win over Falcons

BetBoom continued their surprise run in Cologne by entering the 2-0 pool of Stage 3 with a statement win over Falcons (Nuke 13-11, Ancient 13-10).

Kirill "⁠Boombl4⁠" Mikhailov's troops, who began their Major campaign all the way back in Stage 1, are now just one victory away from a shock playoff appearance, while Falcons drop down to the 1-1 pool.

The match began like a dream for Falcons, who came out swinging with an 11-3 lead thanks to a strong CT side and a T-side pistol. BetBoom rallied and began to chip away at the deficit once their guns came out, however, with Ilya "⁠m0NESY⁠" Osipov's 1vs2 attempt being the closest Falcons got to a round as they faltered to an 11-13 loss.

Falcons' T side didn't fare much better on Ancient, with BetBoom putting up another imperious defensive side by winning nine rounds as Falcons couldn't string more than one consecutive round together thanks to some brilliant output by BetBoom's individuals.

While Falcons once again looked much better on the CT side, BetBoom stayed in the game and eventually secured a 13-10 win after m0NESY went for an ill-advised overpeek during an A execute.

Falcons drop another massive lead

The series started like a dream for Falcons, but they dropped an 11-4 lead on the Nuke offense to put another choke on their résumé, with René "⁠TeSeS⁠" Madsen once again the finishing as the highest-rated player once Falcons were up against the wall.

Finn "⁠karrigan⁠" Andersen was brought into the squad to fix their issues with closing out maps, but so far the Dane hasn't found the magic formula.
