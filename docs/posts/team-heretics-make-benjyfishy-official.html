Enterprise still attended the VCT Ascension EMEA Play-In stage, but placed bottom of their group with a 1-1 record due to a small round differential in the tiebreakers.

benjyfishy is joining a Heretics roster that has put up lackluster results this year. An end-of-season move that saw assistant coach Brandon "weber" Weber replace Aleksander "zeek" Zygmunt on the roster was not enough to stop the team from finishing eighth in the VCT EMEA League, barring them from playoffs and leaving them to fight in the regional LCQ.
