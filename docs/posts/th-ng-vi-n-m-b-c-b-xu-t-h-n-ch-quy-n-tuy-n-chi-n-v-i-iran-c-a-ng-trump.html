However, after a shaky start to China Stage 1, Trace made a change.

Four games into the stage, after a 2-2 start, Trace picked up Midi to replace FengF in the starting roster. After finishing fourth in their group, Trace entered the playoffs in the lower bracket, where one win was the most that they could manage before being eliminated.
