NRG began their CAC campaign with a 13-10 defeat to Legacy. A loss to MOUZ would see them exit their second tournament in a row in last place after going out of IEM Atlanta in 13th-16th place following 0-2 defeats to FUT and FaZe.

daps is no stranger to standing in for players when needed. He notably replaced Nick "⁠nitr0⁠" Cannella at the StarLadder Budapest Major as the American IGL missed the event to attend the birth of his child. daps averaged a 0.70 rating as NRG went 2-3 in Stage 1.
