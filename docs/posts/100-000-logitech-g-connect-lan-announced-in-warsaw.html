$100,000 Logitech G Connect LAN announced in Warsaw

A new $100,000 LAN has been announced by Logitech G in partnership with Fantasyexpo and GAM3RS_X.

With an arena playoffs in Warsaw's EXPO XXI, the 12-team LAN will take place from September 17-23 with open and closed qualifiers beforehand, and six teams taking spots in the arena playoffs.

"With Logitech G Connect, we’re committed to making the next breakthrough possible for every hardworking team, not just the established names," said Sebastiaan de Lange, Head of Gaming Europe at Logitech G.

The event format will be two round robin groups of six teams each, with all games being best-of-ones. The top three teams from each group advance to playoffs, with 2nd vs 3rd in the quarter-finals and top spot going to the semi-finals.
