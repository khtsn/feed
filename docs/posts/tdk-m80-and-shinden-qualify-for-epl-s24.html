TDK, M80 and ShindeN qualify for EPL S24

The three teams won the ECL Season 51 Finals in their respective regions to secure spots in Katowice.

ShindeN have earned their first international LAN appearance

TDK, M80 and ShindeN have all secured berths at ESL Pro League Season 24, doing so by winning the ESL Challenger League Season 51 finals in Europe, North America and South America respectively.

The trio join TYLOO in qualifying for the $1,000,000 LAN event, which is set to run October 3-11 in Katowice, Poland. They will be joined by 12 teams invited via the global VRS, with those invites set to be confirmed August 3.

Vladislav "⁠nafany⁠" Gorshkov's TDK squad began life back in February with a couple of strong results in tier-two online competitions before form began to wane, but they have secured an important international LAN appearance with their hard-fought victory over Nemesis.

In a marathon five-map series TDK had to fight back from a 2-1 series deficit, in no small part down to the consistent output of Sergey "⁠Ax1Le⁠" Rykhtorov, who posted over 100 ADR combined across the last two maps to help secure his team's spot in Poland.

M80 grabbed their EPL berth with a 3-1 victory over Voca, bouncing back from two underwhelming showings internationally with yet another regional victory.

After dominating Voca on Dust2, M80 found their opponents offering far stiffer resistance on the remaining three maps, but Fritz "⁠slaxz-⁠" Dietrich's excellent form on Anubis and Mirage was enough to ensure the series win.
