BESTIA's Cologne Major dream ends with loss to Passion UA in Riga

BESTIA are out of the running for a spot in Stage 1 of the Cologne Major after a 2-0 loss to Passion UA in the semi-finals of the Urban Riga Open Season 4.

It's a heartbreaking end to the season for BESTIA, who have played 50 maps over the last month in a last-ditch effort to earn enough points to qualify for the Major.

Prior to the trip to Latvia, the team attended BetBoom Circuit X Pantanal Cuiabá, the Roman Imperium Cup VI and VII, the BC Game Masters Championship, and the Parken Challenger Championship Season 3.

BESTIA needed to win the Urban Riga Open Season 4 to have a chance of securing the last Americas spot in Cologne but fell short against Passion UA, who will take on Sangal in the final.

"Fate didn't want it to be this time," BESTIA wrote in a post on X. "But we won't stop until we achieve our dream.

"Show your support for the players, who left their lives, their families and their friends behind to fight for these colors."

As things stand, Passion UA are still in the running for a Major spot, but even a win in the Riga final might not be enough for them to overtake Liquid in the Americas VRS.
