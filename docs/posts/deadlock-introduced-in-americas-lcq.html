Deadlock introduced in Americas LCQ

Deadlock will join the VCT in the Americas Last Chance Qualifier, the AVPA confirmed yesterday.

The AVPA also confirmed that the last event to be played in patch 6.11 will be Americas Ascension, which kicks off on June 30. The first events with Deadlock enabled will be the second NA Game Changers and the Americas LCQ, which both start July 13.

Deadlock will be allowed in accordance with Section 4.4.1 of the VCT 2023 Competition Ruleset, which states that agents will be restricted for two weeks. Deadlock's official release was June 28, meaning July 12 is the latest date by which she could be allowed in the VCT.

However, League Officials have the final say, meaning new agents may be restricted past their minimum of two weeks in EMEA and the Pacific. VLR.gg has reached out to representatives of both leagues for comment.

Deadlock was released with patch 7.0 on June 28, which also launched the new Team Deathmatch alongside an overhaul to the progression system. Her gameplay reveal can be found here.
