Official: BESTIA recall timo, buda transitions to IGL, and shaRpy takes over as coach

BESTIA has announced a restructuring of its roster, moving veteran in-game leader Nicolás "⁠Noktse⁠" Dávila to the bench and handing leadership duties to Nicolás "⁠buda⁠" Kramer.

The vacant roster spot has been filled by Cristhian "⁠timo⁠" Perez, who has been recalled from his loan to Isurus, and the organization has promoted assistant coach Afonso "⁠shaRpy⁠" Bastos to take over as skipper from Rodrigo "⁠pino⁠" Manarino.

The changes confirm a report by HLTV and Dust2 Brasil that indicated BESTIA would enact several changes after a difficult start to the season. The Argentine team is currently ranked 14th on the Americas VRS after placing 5-8th at BetBoom Circuit X Cuiabá, 3rd-4th at FiRe CONTER Season 1, 13th-16th at Roman Imperium Cup V, and 9th-12th at DraculaN Season 5.
