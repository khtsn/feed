Eternal Fire part ways with DemQQ, rigoN

Eternal Fire have announced the departures of Sergiy "⁠DemQQ⁠" Demchenko and Rigon "⁠rigoN⁠" Gashi, ending a partnership that began in January 2026.

The Turkish organization had a turbulent year so far, already enacting a double change by adding Iulian "⁠regali⁠" Harjău and Gareth "⁠MisteM⁠" Ries in place of Volodymyr "⁠Woro2k⁠" Veletniuk and Emrecan "⁠EMSTAR⁠" Çalışkan in April.

That move failed to change the team's fortunes, however, as they continued to hover outside of the top 50 in the VRS standings after struggling to make deep runs in online and lower-ranked LAN competitions, and have now once again looked to the transfer market.
