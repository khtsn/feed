ZywOo recovers from first map disappearance to dominate FUT

ZywOo scored two aces and a flashy clutch in Vitality's first match

Vitality got off to a winning start at the IEM Cologne Major after a 2-1 victory over FUT, recovering from an Anubis drubbing in the opener to dominate the young squad on Overpass and Nuke.

Mathieu "⁠ZywOo⁠" Herbaut played a crucial role just how one-sided the map wins felt, as he went from a 0.68-rated Anubis (his second-worst Major map) to 2.38 on Overpass (his second-best) and 2.14 on Nuke (his third-best).

The French phenom picked up two aces along the way, one completed by a sneaky play through smoke for a 1v3 on Overpass and the second with Dualies in hand to kickstart the Nuke decider.

He went on to put some more flair on the superb performance with a flashy 1v2 featuring an AWP noscope, helping Vitality rack up a 10-round defense and close it down without too much trouble in the second half after a cosmetic FUT comeback.

Shahar "⁠flameZ⁠" Shushan played a great series of his own, recording his best-ever Major map on Overpass (2.47) and topping it off with a solid performance on Nuke as well.
