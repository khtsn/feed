Gen.G Gen.G Korea Rank #1 Foxy9 Jung Jae-sung (정재성) t3xture Kim Na-ra (김나라) Munchkin Byeon Sang-beom (변상범) yoman Chae Young-moon (채영문) Karon Kim Won-tae (김원태) has signed former DRX DRX Korea Rank #2 Flashback Cho Min-hyuk (조민혁) MaKo Kim Myeong-gwan (김명관) freeing No Ha-jun (노하준) HYUNMIN Song Hyun-min (송현민) player Jung "Foxy9" Jae-sung (정재성).

Foxy9 made his debut in 2021 in the second tier of Valorant, playing in South Korea and Japan with World Game Star World Game Star Korea Rank #25 Wenty Yang Geon-mo (양건모) YUNSEUNG apeX , Feel So Good Feel So Good Inactive Hound Oh Yun-Sung Eliza Kang Hee-won (강희원) Alley rolex Yoon Jae-Ho ACCEL Kwon Ki-Hyun , and REIGNITE REIGNITE Japan Rank #20 . He made the jump to VCT towards the end of the 2022 season, signing with Korean giants DRX. Earlier this month, DRX announced that he and teammate BeYN were restricted free agents.

In 2023, his first season with DRX, he competed for the team's fifth starting spot with veteran Zest , who was with the team since its Vision Strikers Vision Strikers Inactive stax Kim Gu-taek (김구택) Rb Goo Sang-min (구상민) Zest Kim Ki-seok (김기석) BuZz Yu Byung-chul (유병철) MaKo Kim Myeong-gwan (김명관) days. He was benched two games into the team's Masters Tokyo campaign, and would not see the stage at Champions Los Angeles.

Foxy9 stayed on DRX for 2024, a tumultuous season for the team that saw them shuffle their roster and depart with longtime player stax . He was most known for playing the Duelist role heading into the year, but took on the Sentinel role, playing Killjoy and Cypher more. As the season progressed, his agent pool expanded to include Initiators such as Breach, KAY/O, and Gekko.

With mid-year roster turnover and a changing meta, DRX did not qualify for an international event until Champions Seoul, where they finished 5th-6th.

Foxy9 joins a Gen.G team that had a dominant 2024 season which ended in a disappointing group stage elimination at Champions. They held onto core pieces t3xture , Karon , and Munchkin while signing Korean tier-two player yoman .

Gen.G are set to compete at the TEN Valorant Asia Invitational and then the Gwangju Esports Series Asia 2024 in the offseason before the end of the calendar year.

  • Byeon "Munchkin" Sang-beom (변상범)
