GamerLegion suffer last-place exit at Super DraculaN

GamerLegion suffered a shock last-place exit at Super DraculaN after a 0-2 loss to 9INE meant they left Bucharest without a victory to their name.

Janusz "⁠Snax⁠" Pogorzelski's troops, who came to the tournament following their 0-3 exit in the IEM Cologne Stage 2, were hoping to gain some spots in the VRS, but consecutive losses to Acend and 9INE mean they leave Romania having lost 58 points and two places.

Meanwhile, 9INE have Alan "⁠flayy⁠" Krupa to thank for their victory, as the Polish AWPer put up a 1.68 rating, 100 ADR, and a +7.32 round swing across the series.

Elsewhere in the tournament, Eternal Fire and OG were also eliminated without a victory, continuing a dismal run of form for Casper "⁠cadiaN⁠" Møller and company, while Acend, Sharks, Echo, and Walczaki secured spots in their respective groups' upper-bracket finals.
