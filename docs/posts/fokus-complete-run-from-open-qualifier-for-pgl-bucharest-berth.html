FOKUS complete run from open qualifier for PGL Bucharest berth

FOKUS have beaten Inner Circle 3-1 to qualify for PGL Bucharest 2026.

The new Jakub "⁠kuben⁠" Gurczyński-coached combine were announced 17 days ago, but have already picked up wins over BetBoom, EYEBALLERS, and Inner Circle (twice) to book their ticket to Romania.

The team is full of reunions: Sebastian "⁠volt⁠" Maloș with Erik "⁠ztr⁠" Gustafsson from GamerLegion, volt and Matias "⁠Banjo⁠" Kivistö from fnatic Rising, plus Mateo "⁠Matheos⁠" Prišlin with #36 prospect Georgi "⁠Jorko⁠" Mitev from CPH Wolves.

And that prior chemistry has already been put to good use. If you go back to the start of the second open qualifier (they were knocked out of the first 16-14 by Eternal Fire in the round of 64), that's a run of eleven straight wins in the gauntlet of online European CS for the brand new lineup.

In the 17 maps that earned HLTV coverage, Finnish lurker Banjo has been their top player with a 1.18 rating, with Matheos on a 1.16 just behind.

"1st Feb first time meeting with everyone, 1st March qualified for our first LAN together," volt posted on X after the match, highlighting the team's remarkable rise.
