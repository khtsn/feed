PARIVISION edge past FUT to secure playoff spot in Cluj-Napoca

Jame and company held their composure on Ancient to secure the three-map series

PARIVISION are through to the PGL Cluj-Napoca playoffs after a close three-map affair with FUT, who fought from beginning to end but failed to capitalize on big moments when the pressure was highest late in the decider.

The series kicked off on Dust2, and PARIVISION were made to work to take their map pick. A back-and-forth first half with FUT on the defense ended 6-6, and neither team could run away with a lead in the second half, ending in a 12-12 stalemate in regulation.

Late on Dust2, it was Emil "⁠nota⁠" Moskvitin who came in clutch, literally, winning a 1v2 to put his PARIVISION ahead before coming on top in another 1v2 in the second set of overtime rounds to make it 17-19 in his team's favor.

The series moved to FUT's Mirage, where FUT got off to a flying 10-0, but then much like in their series agianst MOUZ, where they gave up a 12-3 lead, they started to choke on the attack, letting PARIVISION get all the way to within two, 11-9.

Laurențiu "⁠lauNX⁠" Țârlea's ambition to play in front of his home crowd in Romania was most visible on Mirage, where he posted a 1.94 rating and +9.40% swing. Most visibly, it came first with a 1v3 clutch early on, and late in the map with a big two entries that jumpstarted FUT's final charge to a 13-10 victory that forced the series to Ancient.

The series moved to Ancient, where PARIVISION got ahead 3-0, but one triple by lauNX and back-to-back AWP triples by Nikita "⁠cmtry⁠" Samolotov kept things close early on.

A big moment came when FUT were up 9-7 and Andrey "⁠BELCHONOKK⁠" Yasinskiy was allowed to stick a 10-second defuse, keeping it close as the two teams remained inseparable up to the 20th round, when the scoreboard read 10-10.

Ultimately, though, PARIVISION, who recently won a big trophy and have the leadership of the veterans like Dzhami "⁠Jame⁠" Ali and Dastan "⁠dastan⁠" Akbayev, managed to get the marginal gains necessary to pull ahead on the CT side, winning out 13-10 to secure their place in the playoffs while handing FUT another painful defeat that will hopefully serve the young team under the orders of András "⁠coolio⁠" Fercsák as fuel for growth as they continue to reach for new heights in 2026.
