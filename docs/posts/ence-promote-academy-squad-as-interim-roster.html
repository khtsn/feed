ENCE promote academy squad as interim roster

ENCE have announced the promotion of the ENCE Academy squad to the main roster, stating the move will serve to create an "interim transition-period roster while we evaluate and plan potential further changes."

The organization's statement said that the change will give the academy players the chance to "earn their spot on the roster," but that they will also be "scouting and trialling players outside of our own roster."

ENCE stated that the move to promote the academy squad will give them more time to build their future roster. "As this is a significant change for us, we want to take our time with the building process and do it as meticulously as possible, avoiding any rushed decisions."
