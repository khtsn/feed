Team Vitality shuts down T1 in Masters debut

With a strong 2-0 win, Team Vitality has defeated T1 in their opening Masters: Bangkok match. The victory puts Vitality one win away from making playoffs, while T1 needs to avoid a loss to continue in the tournament.

The match began on Vitality's pick, Fracture. The team had a rough start, but dominated the second half to win the map 13-10. Vitality kept up the strong performance on Bind, ending the series with a 13-9 on the second map and closing out the series 2-0.
