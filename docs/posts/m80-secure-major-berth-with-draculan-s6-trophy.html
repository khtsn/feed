M80 secure Major berth with DraculaN S6 trophy

M80 have been crowned champions of DraculaN Season 6 after beating Monte 2-1 in the grand final (8-13 Inferno, 13-3 Dust2, 13-10 Mirage).

The American team's odds of making the Major had taken a hit after a group stage exit at Roman Imperium Cup VI and an elimination by HEROIC Academy in the quarter-finals of ROG Journey Spring, prompting them to sign up for Gamers Assembly Festival in case things went awry at DraculaN Season 6.

But the French LAN, instead of being a last-ditch hope to qualify for the Major, will instead offer M80 the chance to secure a Stage 2 invite after a spectacular run at DraculaN has done more than enough to secure them a Stage 1 spot.

Elias "⁠s1n⁠" Stein's men notched several important wins en route to the final, including a key victory over Aurora in the upper bracket semis that earned them a significant share of VRS points.

A loss to Monte in their next match was shrugged off as M80 reverse-swept Alliance with two dominant map wins in the consolidation final, and revenge was served in the title decider with another reverse-sweep to secure the team the trophy.

Monte were in no danger of losing their Major invite in the final, but have lost hold of their Stage 2 spot in a neck-and-neck battle with GamerLegion, who notched a win over Ninjas in Pyjamas at Stake Ranked Episode 1 to pull ahead.
