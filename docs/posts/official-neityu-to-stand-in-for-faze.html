Official: Neityu to stand in for FaZe

FaZe have announced that benched ENCE rifler Ryan "⁠Neityu⁠" Aubry will stand in for the team at their upcoming appearances at BLAST Rivals Season 1, IEM Atlanta, and Stake Ranked Episode 2.

The news confirms an earlier report by Croissant Strike's Guillaume "neL" Rathier that indicated the French rifler would temporarily step in for the team in the wake of Finn "⁠karrigan⁠" Andersen's departure for Falcons.

Neityu has been sidelined since March 11, when ENCE benched the entirety of its roster and pivoted to its local Finnish scene to develop talent. The 20-year-old plied his trade for ENCE for almost 18 months and averaged a 1.06 rating after making the jump up to tier-two from MOUZ NXT.

He reunites with his former ENCE coach, Niclas "⁠enkay J⁠" Krumhorn, who was announced as the new head coach of FaZe on April 16.

According to neL, Russel "⁠Twistzz⁠" Van Dulken is expected to take over leadership duties during Neityu's stand-in stint, but it remains unclear whether that will carry on into the future.

Neityu's debut for FaZe will come at BLAST Rivals Fort Worth on April 29, where FaZe will take on Natus Vincere in their opening match.
