DRX reverse sweep Paper Rex to reach Pacific grand final

DRX have defeated Paper Rex 3-2 in the VCT Pacific 2024 lower final, punching their ticket to a grand final matchup against Gen.G Esports. Paper Rex, now eliminated from playoffs, will take the third seed at Champions Seoul.

The series began with two Paper Rex wins, going up 13-10 on Abyss and 13-7 on Lotus. DRX bounced back with 13-10s on Bind and Icebox, closing out the match after a 13-7 on Ascent.

This series is the ninth encounter between both organizations, with the last six matchups going Paper Rex's way. The victory sets up a South Korean grand final ahead of the first VCT international event held in the country.
