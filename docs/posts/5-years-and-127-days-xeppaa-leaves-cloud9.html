5 years and 127 days: Xeppaa leaves Cloud9

Cloud9 Cloud9 North America Unranked FireBallOps Cameron Tomaselli Zellsis Jordan Montemurro Notexxd Jaden Anderson Jackk Jack Foote OXY Francis Hoang has announced the departure of longtime player Erick "Xeppaa" Bach, ending a stay of five years and 127 days. The announcement comes just over two weeks after Cloud9 and Xeppaa shared that he would be taking a break from competition.

"We fully support his personal decision and are incredibly thankful for everything he's provided for the team over the years," Cloud9 said in its statement. "While Xeppaa remains on our roster, we are beginning trials for potential replacement around our EWC26 qualifier matches. We're focused on finding the right fit for the team and building for the remainder of the season and beyond."

Xeppaa shared that this decision to step away has been a long time in the making.

"I've been thinking about taking a break for a while now, and never really found the right time to bring it up to the team, but after the split ended with penny and v1c leaving, I realized that I really needed a break and told the team," he said. "C9's been really great to me over the years, and it still doesn't feel real, but I feel like it is the right time for me right now. For now, I will be taking my time and streaming in the meantime. I am currently still in the team while they trial."

Xeppaa joined Cloud9 Valorant in 2021 after competing for the org's Counter-Strike: Global Offensive team starting earlier that year. His decision to step away from competing comes in the midst of what is his 10th straight year of competing in esports across CS:GO and Valorant. He's played several roles over the years, but is most well-known for his play as C9's Initiator player.

The 2026 season has been tumultuous for C9. The year started at Kickoff with the team needing to find a substitute for OXY during his leave of absence, and picked up Demon1 . The org also signed assistant coach Veer , who filled in for Xeppaa during the team's EWC Qualification matches, after Kickoff. In Stage 1, the org has parted ways with penny by way of mutual agreement, signed Jackk , parted ways with v1c, and signed Notexxd .
