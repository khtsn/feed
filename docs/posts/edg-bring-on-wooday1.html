EDG bring on WoodAy1

1 day ago

EDward Gaming EDward Gaming China Rank #1 WoodAy1 Lin Weihong (林伟宏) ZmjjKK Zheng Yongkang (郑永康) nobody Wang Senxu (王森旭) CHICHOO Wan Shunji (万顺治) Smoggy Zhang Zhao (张钊) Haodong Guo Haodong (郭浩东) have announced the signing of Lin "WoodAy1" Weihong (林伟宏) ahead of Split 2 of the Chinese League. This roster move also sees Tang "Muggle" Shijun (唐时俊) transition back into a coaching role.

WoodAy1 has had limited exposure to the public Valorant sphere. Before signing with EDG, he competed in Crossfire HD under KingZone Gaming, taking home first in Seasons 3 and 4.

This year's VCT has been underwhelming for EDG. The team continued its regional dominance in both China Kickoff and Split 1, qualifying them for both Masters Madrid and Shanghai. Unfortunately, the squad was only able to pick up a singular win across both international events.

The roster looks to break past their previous results and into the upper echelons of international competition, which may be why the organization chose to sign WoodAy1 so close to Champions. It is unknown who the new talent will sub in for, as the current number of active players on EDG sits at six.

The first foes EDG will face in Split 2 are Wolves Esports Wolves Esports China Rank #13 SiuFatBB Pong Gaa Hei (庞加曦) Yuicaw Huang Yung-chieh (黃湧傑) Spring Liu Jiunting (刘俊霆) aluba Zhong Haojun (钟皓钧) pl1xx Christopher Li , who managed to take a map off of China's kings during Kickoff. EDG currently sit atop of the Chinese leaderboards at 11 Championship Points. The team simply needs to keep up their form should they wish to qualify to Champions Seoul.
