Porto to stage BLAST Open 2026 Season 2 playoffs

BLAST has announced that the playoff stages of the second Open event of 2026 will take place at the Super Bock Arena – Pavilhão Rosa Mota in Porto from September 4-6.

This will mark BLAST's fourth visit to Portugal, following events in Lisbon in 2018, 2022 and 2025.

BLAST Open Porto will kick off in BLAST's Copenhagen studio on August 24, where 16 teams will duke it out for six spots in the playoffs. The tournament will feature a $1.1 million prize pool, split between a player share of $400,000 and a club share of $700,000.

"Bringing BLAST Premier back to Portugal is a special moment for us," said Anne Banschbach, Programme Director for BLAST Premier.

"Portugal has consistently delivered events with some of the most passionate Counter-Strike fans in the world, and bringing our events to Porto for the first time is another exciting step in a milestone year for BLAST Premier.

"We're proud to continue growing our global circuit in new cities, and we can't wait to deliver an unforgettable experience inside the Super Bock Arena this September."
