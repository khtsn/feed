FERJEE Rainhas do Clutch 2026 Finals to feature $50,000 prize pool

FERJEE, Rio de Janeiro's State Federation of Electronic Sports, has announced more details about the 2026 edition of Rainhas do Clutch, its women/inclusive Counter-Strike circuit.

After two editions for Brazilian teams only, Rainhas do Clutch is going international in 2026 with an event in Rio de Janeiro from June 23-26 with approximately $30,000 in prize money and $20,000 in club share.

The four spots in the tournament will be decided through regional qualifiers — two for South America, one for North America, and one for Europe. The qualifiers will be held from May 19 through 24, with the registration process beginning on May 13.

Rainhas do Clutch 2026 arrives at a critical moment for the women's circuit, which has been in a battle for survival since ESL shut down Impact at the end of last year.
