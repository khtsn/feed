Live updates from ESL Pro League S23 semi-finals

FUT captain Aulon "⁠Krabeni⁠" Fazlija spoke with HLTV shortly after his team's loss to Natus Vincere to share his feelings following their semi-final elimination.

On feeling after loss: "I think... shit... nothing kind of good. We had it in our hands on Dust2 T-side. If we could win after the Scout round, then we would have had a chance."

On why FUT lost; "On Mirage they had a good plan on how to punish us in Middle. We also did some bad mistakes, I would say. On Dust2 6-2 round we had a 2v1 clutch that we played one by one, late rounds we also fucked up a bit. Nothing we could do."

On feelings about overall result: "Maybe the team feels proud, but for me this is not that an insane achievement to go to the semis. For me, I know we can do more."
