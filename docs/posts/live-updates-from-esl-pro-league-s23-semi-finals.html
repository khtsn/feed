    The sole remaining Swede looks to build on "life game"

    19:00

    After Drin "⁠makazze⁠" Shaqiri, the next highest-rated player of the tournament is Love "⁠phzy⁠" Smidebrant, who is now the only remaining Swede in the tournament following the elimination of Ludvig "⁠Brollan⁠" Brolin.

    He surpassed all expectations in a "life game" against Spirit yesterday, shutting down Danil "⁠donk⁠" Kryshkovets time and time again.

    "You can feel the Swedish support while you're playing, maybe that's why I had a life game," phzy told HLTV yesterday. "It did feel easy, to be honest. Whether that is maybe Spirit not playing their best game or us just playing very well, I'm not sure."

    Today, he and the rest of Astralis are going up against Aurora in hopes of getting revenge for their loss in the PGL Cluj-Napoca 2-2 decider.
