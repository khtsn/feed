Live updates from ESL Pro League S23 semi-finals

Astralis's map pick of Nuke is set to kick off the second semi-final matchup of ESL Pro League Season 23, followed by Aurora's choice of Dust2. Should a decider be necessary, the two sides will duke it out on Inferno to end the series.

On quarter-final against Legacy: "It's always hard to play against Legacy, when we play against them, it's always going to third maps and overtime and that sort of stuff. We managed to win, even though on Overpass we choked a bit."

On soulfly: "I think he's a cool guy. He doesn't shout a lot, he doesn't scream a lot, and he dosen't get nervous a lot. He's just doing his job in the server."

On Astralis matchup: "I think I can say they're playing good individually, phzy, ryu, and jabbi, their performances increased a lot. I think we should be careful about them.
