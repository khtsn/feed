BLAST unveils final two 2028 events with another potential clash

BLAST has announced the final two dates for its 2028 circuit: August 14-28 and September 11-18.

The announcement follows the first four dates BLAST announced earlier this month, out of which two clashed with events previously announced by PGL and ESL in January and April.

HLTV's previous post outlining these dates noted another clash in February, but this was taking into account a PGL event that was likely mistakenly announced by the TO as ending on a Monday, the only day that would overlap with the relevant BLAST event, rather than the usual Sunday.

Now, one of the last two events could also overlap with another tier-one competition. PGL previously announced an August event on nearly the same dates, but it is unclear whether it will happen, as the Romanian TO has since amended its plan saying it would host at least six events instead of eight, without specifying which dates are certain to remain.
