Andre Braugher, Actor on ‘Brooklyn Nine Nine’ and ‘Homicide,’ Dies at 61

Andre Braugher, an actor who starred in “Brooklyn Nine-Nine,” “Homicide: Life on the Street” and other shows and films, died on Monday. He was 61.

His death was confirmed on Tuesday by his longtime publicist Jennifer Allen. She said he had died after a brief illness. No other information was immediately available.
