SAG Awards 2025 Red Carpet Photos: Jane Fonda, Timothée Chalamet and More

See what film and television stars wore as they arrived for the Screen Actors Guild Awards on Sunday night.

Feb. 23, 2025Updated 10:34 p.m. ET

In a season marked by many awards, the Screen Actors Guild ceremony does something few others can: It focuses strictly on the stars. (Those who are more behind the scenes will get their due next weekend.) On Sunday night, at the 31st SAG Awards, television and film actors were celebrated, as individuals and as ensembles. Winners were handed a heavy, as they consistently remarked, statuette depicting a nude male figure.

The evening’s attendees were notably clothed. Here’s what some of them, including Jane Fonda, Ariana Grande, Kieran Culkin, Pamela Anderson and, of course, Jeremy Strong, wore on their way into the Shrine Auditorium in Los Angeles.
