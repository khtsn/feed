beastik on 0-3 exit: "It feels even worse than not qualifying"

SINNERS finally overcame their past RMR troubles by qualifying for the IEM Cologne Major, but their campaign from Germany was far from a dream.

A 0-3 exit after losses to FlyQuest, NRG, and TYLOO is a disappointing one for the team, even if some of the games were incredibly close and could have gone either way.

"We were always telling ourselves when we didn't qualify like, 'Bro, we would beat these guys, we would beat these guys, we would be in Stage 2, we would be in Stage 3,'" he told HLTV after the loss.

"But when we are there, we are just… I don't know, choking. We are not present in the game, we are making tons of mistakes."

Listen to the interview here.

Commiserations on the loss, not the way you would want to go out with 0-3. How would you rate the entire tournament from your side?

Disaster, I would say, we did not come here for 0-3. I'm not happy with it. Even though today we kind of played okay-ish, I would say, but I would still expect more from us. What we have shown in the past, I think we have more [to show], so I'm very disappointed. At the same time, I'm kind of like... I don't know. We have mixed emotions, I would say.

Does it feel worse in a way because it got so close? Overtime against FlyQuest and today as well, and the lead that you had on Inferno. Is it just about those small differences and those small bits of experience that cost you in the end?

Yeah, of course. It's not like we are getting smashed like 13-0 or 13-1, we are still putting up a fight, but I still feel like we could have won against FlyQuest. It could have been different. If we had won Ancient, maybe we would have been playing Mirage now. Things could have been very different, but… If we are showing what we are showing and we cannot close the games like that, then we don't deserve it even if it's close.

When the disappointment will go away in a few weeks, will you look at this whole experience in a more positive way? Because you had a lot of heartbreak in RMRs previously and this time you made it. Do you think the experience will be more positive in a bit of time?

I'm not sure because, you know, we had a lot of heartbreaks before when we didn't qualify for Major. Now we did, but at the same time, it feels even worse than not qualifying. We just go here, show absolutely nothing, and just go out 0-3. Being like the worst team, most likely with Gaimin Gladiators, that's something that I just don't understand.

But when we are there, we are just… I don't know, choking. We are not present in the game, we are making tons of mistakes. Of course, we can gain some experience forward for the next tournaments, but at the moment, I cannot say.

You guys rose through the VRS quite a lot this season, so you have a chance to get some invites for the next season. Is this experience that you got in Atlanta and here something that you think will help you reach the next level and qualify for some more tier-one events?

I'm not sure, because I feel like with 0-3, we are out of the invite range. The last tournament that we will squeeze in is the China XSE tournament. If we don't perform well there, we'll be out of tournaments, and we'll go back online, where we were before, which is what we wanted to avoid.

We were talking about it, and we wanted to avoid it here, but even more things happened here. That's just not like gaining experience. It's getting worse and worse, and that's what I don't like.
