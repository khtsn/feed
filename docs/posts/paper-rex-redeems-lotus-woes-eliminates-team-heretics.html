Heretics took A-site fast and forced an uncomfortable retake from PRX to win the first pistol, and continued that aggression to convert the bonus. That extended the lead to 12-4, and Paper Rex wasn't going to throw away the lead this time. Paper Rex won the map 13-4, the series 2-1, and kept their Champions hopes alive by eliminating Team Heretics.

The best player in the win was f0rsakeN, who ended the series with a 1.22 match rating, 53 kills, 245 ACS, 78% KAST, and 153 ADR on the Controller role.
