FlyQuest dump out THUNDER dOWNUNDER from Cologne

FlyQuest have eliminated their countrymen from THUNDER dOWNUNDER from the IEM Cologne Major after dealing them their third loss in Stage 1.

The team around Christopher "⁠dexter⁠" Nong came into the event as one of the biggest underdogs, having only competed regionally to earn their spot in Cologne, but looked competitive on day one with an upset win over MIBR and a close affair against B8.

But after THUNDER dOWNUNDER fell to the elimination zone at the hands of BIG the next day, the Australian derby went clearly in favor of FlyQuest, who dominated on the defense with an eight-round run at the beginning of Anubis and a 12-0 CT side on Inferno.

FlyQuest now move on to the 2-2 pool, where they will play a do-or-die match for qualification for Stage 2 on Friday.
