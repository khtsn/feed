PGL announce 2027 China event

PGL have announced China as the location of one of their 2027 events, set to run from April 14-26.

The tournament will award a $1,000,000 prize pool alongside a $300,000 VRS invite bonus and feature 16 teams, 14 receiving their invite via the global VRS from the April 2027 ranking update.

The other two spots will go to the winners of the European and Asian qualifiers, set to begin with the open stage in January 2027.

PGL have shared no additional info about the tournament's format, only saying that it will be played entirely in a LAN format in the China visa region.
