PARIVISION Fans Come Here ft. Mauisnake

PARIVISION have enjoyed a successful start to 2026, adding a trophy to the cabinet at BLAST Bounty Finals and now shrugging off claims of that run being a fluke by going all the way to the final at one of the most stacked events of the year, PGL Cluj-Napoca.

Whether that is sustainable in the long run is a different question, and one explored in the latest Come Here podcast by Danish "⁠Nohte⁠" Allana, Harry "⁠NER0⁠" Richards, and returning guest Alex "⁠Mauisnake⁠" Ellenberg.

The trio also delved into the players' roles, team structure, statistical spread in performances, playstyle, and more, with Mauisnake ending the show by doubling down on a bold claim.

"I went on tedd's podcast shortly after PARIVISION won Bounty, and I immediately said they will not be lifting another trophy this season, and I will stand by that," he said.

"I enjoy watching PARIVISION, I like how they have changed the scene. I find them extremely enjoyable, [...] I just like that Jame, when he has a hunch in a round, they follow his command. [...] I'm going to continue enjoying them, but I don't think they're going to win anything."
