cmtry: "After we beat MOUZ I gained a lot of confidence, and now it's easier to play"

FUT are set for a grand final bout with Astralis at PGL Bucharest, their first chance for a trophy at a LAN event of this significance. The shot at the title comes after a meteoric rise for the young squad, who have wowed many in the community with their impressive aim and fearless brand of CS.

Nikita "⁠cmtry⁠" Samolotov is enjoying one of the best tournaments of his career in Romania, having previously usually sat towards the bottom of the rating charts for his team. "Before, the last tournaments, were shit for me," he admitted to HLTV in an interview beating The MongolZ to secure a grand final berth.

"I couldn't hit guys, I couldn't move well, but now after we played in the arena in Stockholm during EPL, I gained a lot of confidence. Now it's a bit easier to play for me."

Ahead of a grand final matchup with Astralis, cmtry spoke with HLTV about his team's campaign in Bucharest, his own recent improvement, and the upcoming final.

How are you feeling?

I'm feeling great. It was a hard game, I like to play in games with some overtimes. We lost some stupid rounds, they lost some stupid rounds, I like these games, it's fun.

It's fun when you win!

(laughs) Yes, it's fun when you win.

In general this tournament has been good for FUT, not only have you made the final but also played well along the way. Do you feel like you've proved your level right now? It's a tournament without the best teams, but you have proved you are better than the rest.

I think we proved that we can play better teams, play teams like The MongolZ and PARIVISION. I think we proved that we have started to be a top-tier team, so yes. Here we proved a lot and I want to win tomorrow, I will do my best to prove that we can win it, the whole tournament.

I think we proved that we can play with better teams, play with teams like The MongolZ and PARIVISION. I think we proved that we have started to be a top-tier team

Before the tournament, what were your expectations? Did you think that you could go to the grand final and win it?

To be honest, we had a talk with the whole team about that. Our expectations were for sure to be in the playoffs, but my own expectations were to make it to the final or be in the semi-finals. Stuff like this.

You personally are having a really good tournament, you're one of the top-rated players in the tournament. In fact, it might be your best tournament for a long time. Why do you think you are playing so well?

I was thinking about it, but I don't know how to answer… I think I'm just playing well; I'm hitting some shots that before, in the last tournaments, were shit for me. I couldn't hit guys, I couldn't move well, but now after we played in the arena in Stockholm during EPL, I gained a lot of confidence. Now it's a bit easier to play for me because I don't have any pressure on me.

For me, I was under pressure in Stockholm not because of the arena, but because of the big teams

Is it the difference between an arena and a studio? It makes it a lot easier to play in a studio like this?

For me, I was under pressure in Stockholm not because of the arena, but because of the big teams. I was a bit like… It was the same at PGL Cluj for example, I had a bit of pressure on me because of the big teams. After we beat MOUZ and showed good results at EPL, I gained a lot of confidence and now it's just easier to play. I feel myself super confident and not having any pressure on me.

Tomorrow you are playing Astralis who are a team you've played a lot, who you're very familiar with, and you've both won games against each other. How are you feeling about that match?

To be honest, I think it's going to be a very cool game. I think it's our first BO5, maybe we played one BO5 in NAVI Junior, but I'm not sure. It's going to be our first big BO5, and Astralis have been playing so well lately; they are beating good teams. I was checking their games, they are playing well, I like it, so I think it's going to be a good fight.

Like you say, it's a best-of-five. Are you prepared for that as a team? Because it really tests your map pool and your stamina in the server.

Yes I think we are prepared, our five maps, all of them. We'll be fine for sure.
