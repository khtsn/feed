PxS and 2Game Esports part ways

2Game Esports 2Game Esports Brazil Rank #5 gobera Vitor Gobo lz Luiz Reche spike Rodrigo Lombardi silentzz Caio Morita Askia Estevão Ferreira has announced the free agency of Josh "PxS" Brunelli. The split marks the end of a month-long partnership between the team and the player, which came to fruition seemingly out of nowhere.

PxS first signed with 2Game as a result of the team and in-game leader Zap parting ways due to “ongoing and escalating disagreements”. As a Brazilian-American, PxS fit into 2G's team with no language barrier, and also did not face the same visa issues that other non-American teams like FURIA FURIA Brazil Rank #8 heat Olavo Marcelo raafa Rafael Lima havoc Ilan Eloy Khalil Khalil Schmidt pryze Luis-Henrique Viveiros have in the past.

Against all odds, the Americas Ascension winners defeated LOUD in two maps, securing what would be their first and only win thus far in VCT.

"I'm truly grateful for the opportunity and the experience I've gained. I gave my all to showcase my work and will continue striving to grow and reach new heights," PxS said on X. "With that said, I'm now F/A and open to offers. I have strong experience as a duelist and sentinel, hold U.S. citizenship, and am open to opportunities in both NA and EMEA."

With Stage 2 set to start in just under two months on June 22, 2Game will have ample time to find a replacement for PxS and workshop in-game leader duties. It's believed that lz took up the IGL role following Zap's sudden departure in March.
