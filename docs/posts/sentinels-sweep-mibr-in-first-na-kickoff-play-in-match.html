Sentinels Sentinels North America Rank #4 TenZ Tyson Ngo Zellsis Jordan Montemurro zekken Zachary Patrone johnqt Mohamed Amine Ouarid Sacy Gustavo Rossi beat MIBR MIBR Brazil Rank #3 jzz João Pedro mazin Matheus Araújo artzin Arthur Araujo RgLMeister Matheus Rodigoli frz Leandro Gomes 2-0 in the first game of the VCT 2024 NA Kickoff play-ins. This puts Sentinels in a great position to make it to the playoffs, while MIBR will need to win their next game in at least a 2-0 fashion to keep their playoff, and Masters Madrid, hopes alive.

The series saw the maps of Split from Sentinels, Ascent from MIBR, leaving the decider of Sunset, which ended up not being needed.

Split was dominant for Sentinels, hitting the half up 10-2 after a rock solid defense. MIBR weren't able to replicate it, losing 13-7 on Split. For Ascent, Gustavo " Sacy " Rossi went on his signature Sova, and it helped lead Sentinels to a small 7-5 halftime lead. However, even on their map pick, MIBR couldn't slow down Tyson " TenZ " Ngo, losing the map 13-6 and the series 2-0.

As the winner of the match, Sentinels are 1-0 in the three-team round-robin that is the play-in portion of the Kickoff. Similarly to the Pacific and EMEA play-ins that recently concluded, each team faces each other once. If one team wins both of their games, they automatically qualify to the playoffs. If each team wins once, map differential will determine which team gets that final regional playoff spot.
