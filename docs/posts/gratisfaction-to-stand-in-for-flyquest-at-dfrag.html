Gratisfaction to stand in for FlyQuest at DFRAG

FlyQuest will use Sean "⁠Gratisfaction⁠" Kaiwai as a stand-in at the DFRAG HyperX Nationals, the organization announced on Tuesday.

The tournament will take place in Brisbane, Australia from July 17-19 and have approximately $7,000 on offer. Other top sides from Oceania, such as THUNDER dOWNUNDER, Rooster and Ground Zero, feature on the eight-team list.

The event will begin with a GSL-style group stage, with FlyQuest seeded atop of Group B, where they will be pitted against DXA in the first match.
