Earlier in the offseason, hvoya announced that Xi Lai had allowed him to explore his options, but after undergoing a roster rebuild of sorts, has decided to bring him back.

"The new roster looks really promising, thanks to Xi Lai Gaming for trusting in me and giving me another opportunity to step in stage along my teammates," he said.

hvoya joined XLG ahead of the 2024 season, leading the team to trophy-winning campaigns at the China National Competition: Season 2 and eventually China Ascension to earn a VCT slot for the 2025 season. With hvoya in the coaching booth, XLG's debut campaign in Valorant's top tier could easily be described as a success. After finishing 5th-6th at China Kickoff, XLG dropped just one match throughout all of Stage 1 to lift its first VCT trophy and qualify for Masters Toronto. While the team's international debut was disappointing, they had enough championship points even after a fourth-place finish at Stage 2 to qualify for Champions Paris.
