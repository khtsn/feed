G2-PARIVISION elimination clash to headline PGL Astana day four

The two teams have surprisingly found themselves in the 1-2 pool.

After an exciting day of action in Kazakhstan during which Spirit and 9z became the first two teams to secure a playoff appearance at PGL Astana, the group stage has reached its penultimate day.

In the 2-1 pool, the action will start with MOUZ taking on Aurora before Falcons clash with The MongolZ. The final playoff spot to be awarded on Tuesday will go to the winner of FURIA vs. Gentle Mates.
