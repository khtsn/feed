Falcons earn hard-fought win over Monte

Falcons are through to the 2-1 pool at PGL Astana thanks to a 2-0 victory over Monte, getting the job done with close wins on Dust2 (13-11) and Ancient (13-11).

Despite powerful offensive halves Falcons were not able to seal either map comfortably, allowing Monte to get their foot in the door on both occasions.

m0NESY closes dominant half with 4K

Falcons would have hoped to win a series like this in more dominant fashion, and they seemed set to roll over Monte after the first half on Dust2, where Ilya "⁠m0NESY⁠" Osipov, Maksim "⁠kyousuke⁠" Lukin and Nikola "⁠NiKo⁠" Kovač ran riot.

Monte began to come back when they switched over to the offense, however, powered by a resurgence from Oscar "⁠AZUWU⁠" Bell, who had posted a 0-12 first half. Monte did ride their luck at times and eventually that luck run out, granting Falcons a 1-0 series lead.

m0NESY wrecks four with the Dualies in 1v5, but has no kit

Ancient was much the same story with Falcons running out to a convincing lead on their T side, with NiKo utterly dominating in this instance, before stuttering after halftime.

Once again AZUWU came alive after a quiet first half to drive his team's attack foward, but m0NESY's impact with the AWP shone through in an unlikely retake, which granted his team the runway needed to seal the series.
