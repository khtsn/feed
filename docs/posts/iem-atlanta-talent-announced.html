IEM Atlanta talent announced

YouM3 will be one of analysts in Atlanta

ESL has announced its on-air team for the upcoming IEM event in Atlanta, which will take place between May 11-17.

The most notable member of the crew is Michael "⁠YouM3⁠" Cassidy, who will be sharing analyst duties with Jacob "⁠Pimp⁠" Winneche and Janko "⁠YNk⁠" Paunović. The presenter/stage host/sideline reporter trio is made up of Freya "⁠Freya⁠" Spiers, Tres "⁠stunna⁠" Saranthus and Martin "⁠STYKO⁠" Styk.
