xertioN shines again as MOUZ beat G2

The new MOUZ have picked up their second consecutive win at PGL Astana after surviving two tough maps against G2 before dominating them on the deciding Inferno.

Having topped the board with a 1.52 rating in MOUZ's opening game against Gentle Mates, newly-minted IGL Dorian "⁠xertioN⁠" Berman put on another big performance, especially with a 20-4 scoreline on the CT side of the Inferno decider.

Before that, he was also the top performer in MOUZ's overtime loss on Dust2 and a key factor in ensuring their survival in the series as they recovered a 7-10 deficit on the CT side of Mirage.

Still coming to grips with his new anchoring role, Adrian "⁠xelex⁠" Vincze struggled as he was heavily targeted on Dust2, but then played a crucial role with a 1.67-rated performance on Mirage.

The other new addition to the team, Justinas "⁠jL⁠" Lekavicius, was largely quiet, on the other hand.
