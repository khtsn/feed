NAVI recover 5-11 deficit to beat MongolZ

NAVI breathed a massive sigh of relief after Ancient

Natus Vincere have moved up to the 2-1 pool at Stage 3 of the IEM Cologne Major after a hard-fought win over The MongolZ.

The world's No. 2 team found itself up against the ropes after losing Nuke and at a 5-11 deficit on Ancient, but persevered to come back from behind through clutches and several retakes that rested on a knife's edge on the CT side.

It all could have crumbled before that comeback happened, when Sodbayar "⁠Techno⁠" Munkhbold was just a bullet away from securing match point for the Mongolian side, but after he missed out on the ace clutch, everything went right for Natus Vincere.

That included a last-moment defuse at 11-11, as well as a 3v5 turnaround in the first round of overtime, which NAVI used to go into Mirage with wind in their sails.

Once on the decider, the favorites had it all under control, denying The MongolZ early momentum with a forcebuy win and racing to a comfortable 13-6 victory from there off the back of Ihor "⁠w0nderful⁠" Zhdanov's impact on the AWP.
