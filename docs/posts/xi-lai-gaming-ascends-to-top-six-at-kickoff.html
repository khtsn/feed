Xi Lai Gaming ascends to top six at Kickoff

With a 2-1 win against JD Gaming, VCT newcomer Xi Lai Gaming has reached the top six at Kickoff China. They will next play against 2024 Champions EDward Gaming, with the winner of the series going up against either Dragon Ranger Gaming or FunPlus Phoenix to continue their lower bracket run.

The series began on Pearl, JDG's pick. stew dominated the map, his 28 kills leading to a 13-9 win for JDG. XLG bounced back on Bind, with Rarga and YOU stepping up for an XLG 13-4. The decider, Haven, saw Xi Lai Gaming come out on top, winning 13-6 and ending the series 2-1.

JD Gaming is now eliminated from Kickoff China and Masters: Bangkok contention. Xi Lai, meanwhile, has an opportunity to continue their lower bracket run and earn Championship Points and attend their first VCT international event.
