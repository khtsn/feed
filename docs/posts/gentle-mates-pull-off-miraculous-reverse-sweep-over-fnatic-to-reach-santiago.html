On Haven, a clash of comps promised an explosive finish to yet another instant classic Bo5 series. M8's run of three straight rounds starting with its first buy round was the longest run that either team went on in the first half, which ended with M8 up 7-5. M8 won three of the first four rounds of the second half to hit double digits and threaten to close things out, but a sudden barrage of thrifty rounds from both sides sent the map into chaos.

FNATIC used serious momentum that it gained from winning lower buy rounds to win four straight rounds and claim series point for the second time, but M8 again came back from the dead to force overtime.

M8 played with some serious composure in overtime, playing well with each other to close out the first, defending round, and then put FNATIC on the back foot to win its attacking round in quick fashion, capping off the reverse-sweep with a 14-12 win.
