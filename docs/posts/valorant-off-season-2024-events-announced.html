Valorant OFF//SEASON 2024 events announced

Riot Games has revealed an initial list of events scheduled for the 2024 OFF//SEASON, which officially starts following the end of Champions 2024. Events will take place worldwide, with the first wave of events set to kick off starting in September and continuing all the way until January.

The planned list of events are as follows:

  • REIGNITE/RENDEZVOUS (Oct 16 - Nov 22*)
  • Limitless Invitational (Oct 16 - 20*)
  • Mineski VxV Qualifiers (Oct 18 - Nov 3*)
  • Red Bull Home Ground APAC Qualifier (Oct 19 - 20*)
  • Penta OFF//SEASON (Oct 21 - 27)
  • TEN 2024 VALORANT GLOBAL INVITATIONAL (Oct 26 - 27)
  • One Game Showdown Qualifiers (Oct 28 - Nov 7)
  • 2024 GES Asia (Nov 2 - 3)
  • One Game Showdown Qualifiers (Oct 28 - Nov 7)
  • Mineski VxV Playoffs (Nov 22 - Dec 2)
  • UNSTOPPABLE '24 (Nov 28 - Dec 1)
  • One Game Showdown Playoffs (Nov 29 - Dec 1)
  • Convergence 2024 (Dec 2 - 8)
  • SOOP VALORANT LEAGUE 2024 (Dec 10 - 15)
  • Predator League 2025 (Jan 8 - 12)
  • Lioness Cup VALORANT (Oct 19 - 25)
  • Superdome 2024 Phase 1 (Oct 27 - Nov 1)
  • Coastal Combat Qualifiers (Nov 18 - 27)
  • Red Bull Home Ground (Nov 20 - 23)
  • Spike Nations International Phase (Nov 25 - 30)
  • Superdome 2024 Playoffs (Nov 28 - 30)
  • Spike Nations Grand Finals (Dec 3 - 4)
  • Coastal Combat Main Event (Dec 3 - 11)
  • Neon Dream Invitational (Sept 16 - 17)
  • MEG Qualifiers (Sept 27 - 29)
  • Red Bull Home Grounds NA Qualifier (Sept 29)
  • Sentinels Showmatch (Oct 9 & Oct 22)
  • Ludwig x Tarik Invitational 3 (Oct 12 - 13)
  • SUPER STARS Showmatch (Oct 19)
  • Sen City Classic (Nov 1 - 3)
  • MEG Finals (Nov 23)
  • VALORANT Underground (LAS) (Dec 2-8)
  • VALORANT Underground (LAN) (Dec 9-15)
  • Shanghai Esports Masters (Dec 6 - 7)
  • East Asia Tri-Cup (Nov 21- Dec 1)
