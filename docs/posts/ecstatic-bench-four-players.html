ECSTATIC bench four players

ECSTATIC have announced that Nico "⁠nicoodoz⁠" Tamjidi, Thomas "⁠TMB⁠" Bundsbæk, Anton "⁠Anlelele⁠" Huynh and Christian "⁠Buzz⁠" Andersen have been moved to the bench, leaving the team with only Martin "⁠nut nut⁠" Holm Vestergaard on the starting roster.

"We appreciate the commitment and effort they have shown throughout this project, although it didn’t come together the way we expected," ECSTATIC said of the newly-benched foursome.

The organization have stated their intention is to rebuild around nut nut, with Martin "⁠Sinzey⁠" Rian Christensen serving as the head coach.

The team are currently participating in an ongoing event, Tipsport Conquest of Prague, but made no comment as to whether they would see out the tournament. Their next match is scheduled for April 19 versus ESC.

ECSTATIC's roster came together earlier this year with the high-profile additions of Buzz and nicoodoz, bolstering the lineup with some well-known names in the wake of the departures of Frederik "⁠acoR⁠" Gyldstrand and William "⁠sirah⁠" Kjærsgaard.

Early in the lineup's tenure ECSTATIC enjoyed some promising results online but the team struggled to make headway in LAN events, finding themselves short of the best tier-two European squads and even falling to third at POWER Ligaen Season 31, behind fellow Danish sides Sashi and Tricked.

Results soured in particular in the last few weeks, the team exiting BC Game Masters and CCT Series 20 early doors.

The benching of nicoodoz will raise some eyebrows as the AWPer was ECSTATIC's best player during his tenure by some margin, posting a 1.19 rating overall and a 1.21 rating on LAN.

ECSTATIC are left with:

Martin "⁠nut nut⁠" Holm Vestergaard

Martin "⁠Sinzey⁠" Rian Christensen (coach)
