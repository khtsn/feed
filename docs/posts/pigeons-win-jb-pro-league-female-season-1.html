Pigeons win JB Pro League Female Season 1

vicu has her first title as a Pigeon

Pigeons have won the first major women's event of 2026 after a 2-1 win over Let Her Cook.

The $25,000 JB Pro League Female Season 1 was tournament organizer VANTA's first entry into the space as the women's scene grapples with its post-Impact future.

The grand final was a true test, though. Let Her Cook, who are led by the Pigeons core's old in-game leader Ksenia "⁠vilga⁠" Kluenkova, got to the final by beating 2025 TOTY BIG EQUIPA in the lower bracket.

And they backed up that form with a strong performance on championship Sunday to win map two, Ancient, 13-4.

They also came back from 9-5 down to lead 11-9 over Pigeons on the decider of Dust2, after some miraculous retakes. A Tec-9 push up Short, however, undid Let Her Cook's seven-round streak as Pigeons re-asserted their grip on the trophy to win the map 13-11.

The firepower of Victoria "⁠tory⁠" Kazieva — who averaged a 1.49 rating vs Let Her Cook over two series in the double elimination playoffs — was particularly helpful, but it was Narmina "⁠wieenN⁠" Gasanova who topped the rating boards for the event with a 1.29 and 1.45 in the grand final.
