G2 complete r1nkle signing

r1nkle takes up the AWP from SunPayus

G2 have announced that they have completed the signing of Ukrainian AWPer Artem "⁠r1nkle⁠" Moroz from Ninjas in Pyjamas to fill the vacancy created by Álvaro "⁠SunPayus⁠" García's benching.

Signed from B8 in April 2024 to replace compatriot Daniil "⁠headtr1ck⁠" Valitov, r1nkle showed flashes of potential in his first 18 months with the team. However, a poor run of form at the start of 2026 saw him average a 1.02 rating against top-50 opposition, forcing the team to seek a different AWPer after they failed to qualify for the IEM Cologne Major.

To replace r1nkle, the Swedish organization signed Martin "⁠stavn⁠" Lund, who transitioned to a primary AWP role.

G2 will be banking on Nemanja "⁠huNter-⁠" Kovač and head coach Eetu "⁠sAw⁠" Saha to get more out of r1nkle and help him develop into a reliable difference-maker.
