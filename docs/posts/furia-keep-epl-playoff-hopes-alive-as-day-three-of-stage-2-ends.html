FURIA keep EPL playoff hopes alive as day three of Stage 2 ends

KSCERATO top-fragged for FURIA as they squeezed past HEROIC

FURIA have moved into the 1-2 pool in Stage 2 of ESL Pro League 23 after a 2-1 victory against HEROIC, who have been eliminated from this stage in last place alongside Monte.

The Brazilian team delivered a strong performance on Dust2 but appeared lackluster and out of sync on their pick of Overpass, where they have often struggled in the last three months.

HEROIC's bounce-back performance was spearheaded by Christoffer "⁠Chr1zN⁠" Storgaard, who ended that map with a stunning 2.03 rating and +11.52% round swing.

The Danish IGL carried that form into Inferno, where he had a stunning quad-kill on the CT side, but a six-round deficit after the first half proved too big to overcome in the end.

FURIA will be happier with the result on Inferno than the performance as they almost let the game go to overtime before closing it out on a shoddy buy. They will certainly need to do much better to survive two more elimination matches, starting with a Brazilian clash with paiN in the 1-2 pool.
