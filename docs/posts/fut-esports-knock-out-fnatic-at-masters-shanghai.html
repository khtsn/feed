FUT Esports knock out Fnatic at Masters Shanghai

FUT Esports FUT Esports Europe Rank #3 MrFaliN Furkan Yeğen yetujey Eray Budak AtaKaptan Ata Tan cNed Mehmet Yağız İpek qRaxs Doğukan Balaban defeated FNATIC FNATIC Europe Rank #1 Leo Leo Jannesson Derke Nikita Sirmitev Boaster Jake Howlett Chronicle Timofey Khromov Alfajer Emir Ali Beder 2-1, eliminating Fnatic from Masters Shanghai. FUT Esports will stay alive in the lower bracket, one loss away from also returning to Europe.

This battle was between the top seed and third seed from EMEA, who both lost in the first round of the Masters Shanghai playoffs. These two teams faced each other recently at the EMEA Stage 1 playoffs, where Fnatic beat FUT Esports in the lower bracket final 3-1.

The map veto began with FUT picking Split and Fnatic picking Lotus, leaving Ascent as the decider.

Fnatic won the first pistol round, as they used their double duelist defense to prevent FUT from entering either site. However, when FUT were able to win duels and get control of the site, their post-plant setup worked to secure round wins. Fnatic entered the half only up 7-5, but their attack half was perfect. The combination of Breach, Yoru, and Raze allowed for a lot of space creation while Chronicle held down the flanks on Cypher. A rare tripwire kill from Chronicle bookended the map 13-5.

Lotus began with FUT winning the first pistol round, as they took advantage of some early whiffs from Fnatic. cNed picked up an early Vandal to secure the bonus round, which forced an early timeout. Fnatic had to try and overcome the 9-3 curse, but a 12-5 deficit was too much to overcome. AtaKaptan dropped 23 kills as the map ended 13-8, forcing Ascent.

Ascent was a mirror matchup, a true test of map understanding and strategy. FUT began with the pistol win, continuing that form to dominate duels across the first half. Fnatic entered the half down 9-3, with two of their round wins relying on hero moments from Derke. Losing the second pistol proved to be too big of a hill to climb, as Fnatic crumbled to lose the map 13-3 and the series 2-1.

Fnatic were unable to win a single series at Masters Shanghai, despite earning a bye to the playoff round after winning Stage 1 in EMEA.
