paiN end Astralis' Major with dominant victory

paiN have eliminated Astralis from the IEM Cologne Major Stage 2 after a one-sided two-map win (Nuke 13-11, Overpass 13-4).

Nuke was the first map of the match, where Astralis got off to a solid start on their defensive half. They were never able to build a sizable lead, however, with the biggest margin coming at 10-6 in Astralis' favor after a second-round force victory.

That was as good as it got for Astralis, as paiN powered up on their CT side to take a 13-11 win thanks to some late impact by Rafael "⁠saffee⁠" Costa.

The winner of Overpass was never in doubt, as paiN put up a wall on the defense to secure a flawless 12-0 half, and while Astralis avoided a 0-13 embarrassment, they never got close to mounting a comeback.

Staehr not enough on his own

Astralis individuals, bar Victor "⁠Staehr⁠" Staehr, had a sub-par Major. The 21-year-old averaged a 1.17 rating across the event, 0.22 ahead of his closest teammate.

Love "⁠phzy⁠" Smidebrant was especially anonymous and failed to record a single +1.00 rated map in Germany after averaging a 1.11 across the season before the Major.
