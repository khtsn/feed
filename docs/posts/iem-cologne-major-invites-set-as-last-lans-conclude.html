IEM Cologne Major invites set as last LANs conclude

Just ahead of the IEM Cologne Major invite deadline on April 6, the invitations for the German Major have taken shape following the conclusions of Urban Riga Open Season 4 and HLC Belgrade PRO.

For the last two weeks, teams like BIG, HEROIC, SINNERS and Gaimin Gladiators, who were far from guaranteed places at the Major, have been competing in a variety of local LANs across the globe to make a last-ditch effort to claim their spots.
