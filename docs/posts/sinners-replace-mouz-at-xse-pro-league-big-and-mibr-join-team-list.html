SINNERS replace MOUZ at XSE Pro League, BIG and MIBR join team list

MOUZ are missing the fifth player following the expiry of jL's loan

The organizers of XSE Pro League have announced that SINNERS have replaced MOUZ at the $1 million tournament taking place in Guangzhou, China from July 1-12.

The replacement came after MOUZ decided to withdraw from the tournament due to being unable to provide a complete lineup, missing the fifth player following the expiry of Justinas "⁠jL⁠" Lekavicius's loan from Natus Vincere.

The organizers also announced that BIG and MIBR have been invited to the tournament, which means only one team is left to be revealed.
