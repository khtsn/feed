TvirusLuke announces retirement from competitive play

Following a mutual termination of his contract with Titan Esports Club Titan Esports Club China Rank #15 Haodong Guo Haodong (郭浩东) lucas Yunkun Lu (路昀昆) Dynamite Zhao Zihao (赵梓浩) Abo Han Bohao (韩博皓) Coco Ding Dajian (丁达建) , Chen "TvirusLuke" Cheng-ching (陈振庆) has announced his retirement from competitive play.

"We will always remember your dedication on the field, your hard work in the training room, your high-five celebration with your teammates, and your consistent tenacity and responsibility in adversity," Titan said. "Every bit of blood and every drop of sweat you have paid has turned into the eternal starlight of this stadium."

TvirusLuke got his start in Hong Kong/Taiwan during professional Valorant's First Strike era in 2020. Over the next two years, he competed in the region's Challengers scene for a handful of different teams, notably K7 Esports K7 Esports Inactive y1hua 0n1y He Yiting (何奕霆) TvirusLuke Chen Cheng-ching (陈振庆) Aclx maou Yoyo Xu Youxiang (徐佑翔) Tuboshu popoman4Q and LP33 LP33 Inactive yTao maou 0n1y He Yiting (何奕霆) . In early 2023, he moved to Dragon Ranger Gaming Dragon Ranger Gaming China Rank #3 Flex1n Lo Rui (罗瑞) vo0kashu Ilya Ushakov Akeman Zhang Haoran (张浩然) Nicc Shao Yi-qun (邵逸群) SpiritZ1 Lin Dingyu (林定宇) , helping the team to a China Ascension win at the end of the year to earn a spot in VCT China for 2024. A breakthrough Stage 1 helped the team qualify for Masters Shanghai as the team's primary Duelist player. The team disappointed to close out 2024, and after a fourth-place finish in Kickoff saw DRG miss out on Masters Bangkok, TvirusLuke transferred to TEC.

Despite a promising Stage 1, which ended with Titan finishing 7th-8th, Titan ended the year 11th-12th at Stage 2. While the 2025 offseason has been busy for many teams in China, it has been a rather quiet one for Titan. TvirusLuke's departure is the first transaction for the team. During the season, the team parted ways with Rb and B1ack , while picking up Coco , lucas , and sora .

TvirusLuke's departure will likely result in Dynamite or kawaii , the team's substitutes in 2025, being elevated to the starting lineup for 2026.
