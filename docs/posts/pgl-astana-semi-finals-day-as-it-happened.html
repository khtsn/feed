Pressure is off magic

vs.

For magic, it's all about getting more arena games, and regardless of the result today they're guaranteed to play two more.

"I wanted to win this game because I wanted to play two more matches in this arena, and that's it," Abdurakhim "⁠mo0N⁠" Issa told HLTV last night.

But that doesn't mean they haven't put in the work. Vitaliy "⁠sFade8⁠" Marushka and coach Danila "⁠eksiver⁠" Yurkov have been sleepless for most of the tournament, preparing for their upcoming matches, and the one against Falcons may be their hardest yet.

"When we came here, the first games when we were 0-2, sFade8 and my coach, eksiver, they weren't sleeping. They [were] just always training for the opponents, and we always have a game plan for these games. That's why I think we [made] playoffs."

"Yesterday they slept 10 hours because we had a one-day holiday, and today, they again sleep 30 minutes or one hour."
