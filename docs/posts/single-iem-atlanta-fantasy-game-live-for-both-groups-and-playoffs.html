IEM Atlanta 2026

Format

  • You can create your teams on the Fantasy page here.

  • Two eight-team double-elimination groups with upper finalists and lower final winners making playoffs.

  • Six-team single-elimination playoffs with upper final winners starting from the semi-finals.

  • Except for the BO5 Grand Final, all matches are BO3

Teams

Things to note 

  • There will be a single Fantasy game for the entire event instead of separate games for groups and playoffs. The reasons for this are format, schedule, team list, and a concurrently running event in Astana. Six-team playoff brackets with only one full break day always make suboptimal Fantasy games, but with the Atlanta event competing with another tier one LAN (with an arguably stronger team attendance) that has a more favorable format, it makes even more sense to run this event as a single Fantasy game.

Player prices & pick stats

  • Oleksandr "⁠s1mple⁠" Kostyliev is the fifth-most-picked player in this fantasy game despite being on the lowest-ranked team. The $186,000 price tag might seem alluring, but every round skipped due to elimination will incur your team -3 points, which might be worth it as long as your player earns good points before elimination. Whether it’s something to count on is up to you.
