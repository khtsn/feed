Global Esports Global Esports Asia-Pacific Rank #7 PatMen Patrick Mendoza UdoTan Go Kyung-won (고경원) Kr1stal Savva Fedorov xavi8k Xavier Juan Autumn Kale Dunne analyst Thong "balax" Chern Kwi has shared that he has left the team following the Pacific Stage 1 group stage.

"This was not an easy decision for me as it was a very enjoyable opportunity and one that I will never forget," he said. "... These past six months have been very fun, and being able to go to Korea to meet the team was everything and more [than] I could have hoped for. We all worked hard towards a common goal, and it was an amazing feeling to be part of this project. Despite some of the results not being what we expected, we learned a lot about ourselves, and I'm sure it helped everyone for what's ahead."
