neT captures Cloud9's final roster spot

12 hours ago

Cloud9 Cloud9 North America Rank #7 Xeppaa Erick Bach v1c Victor Truong mitch Mitch Semago neT Michael Bernet OXY Francis Hoang has announced Michael "neT" Bernet as their final roster member for 2025. This addition comes shortly after releasing moose .

neT started the partnership era of VCT in Challengers NA with The Guard The Guard Inactive JonahP Jonah Pulice trent Trent Cairns neT Michael Bernet valyn Jacob Batio tex Ian Botsch , where he and his teammates would run the gauntlet to win Ascension and become the first team to be promoted into VCT Americas.

After signing with G2 Esports G2 Esports North America Rank #3 jawgemo Alexander Mor trent Trent Cairns valyn Jacob Batio JonahP Jonah Pulice leaf Nathan Orf , neT and The Guard core made their VCT debut at Kickoff. They would qualify out of their group for the play-in stage, but an upset loss to MIBR MIBR Brazil Rank #19 artzin Arthur Araujo xenom Eduardo Soeiro cortezia Gabriel Cortez nzr Agustin Ibarra aspas Erick Santos eliminated them from Masters Madrid contention. Prior to Split 1, G2 then made a roster change, replacing neT with the newcomer icy .

neT was forced to rejoin the challengers league, this time with M80, to try and win Ascension for the second time in a row. After the addition of neT, M80 regained their place as the top team from North America, winning Stage 2 to return to Ascension. However, neT would not be able to repeat his previous victory, as he found himself on the other side of grand finals that year as M80 was defeated by Brazil's 2Game Esports 2Game Esports Brazil Rank #1 gobera Vitor Gobo Zap Brenno Roberto pryze Luis-Henrique Viveiros lz Luiz Reche spike Rodrigo Lombardi silentzz Caio Morita . Following the loss, M80 disbanded their roster.

neT steps into Cloud9 amidst a pre-season roster shuffle. After putting together the initial roster, the team decided it needed to make changes. Their IGL Rossy was swapped out for mitch , and the team began trialing replacements for moose. neT now completes Cloud9's new roster as they head into the 2025 season.
