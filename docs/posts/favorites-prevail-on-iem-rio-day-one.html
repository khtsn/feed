Favorites prevail on IEM Rio day one

The first day of IEM Rio came to an end with Aurora securing a win over HOTU, which meant that all eight high seeds won their opening matches in Brazil.

The results of the opening round set up some exciting clashes on day two.

Vitality will meet G2 in the first progression match of the day, before Spirit and Falcons clash for a spot in the playoffs.

In Group B, one of Natus Vincere and FURIA will lock in their playoff appearance on day two, as will the winner of MOUZ vs. Aurora.
