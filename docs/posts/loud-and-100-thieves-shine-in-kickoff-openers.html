Loud and 100 Thieves shine in Kickoff openers

VCT Americas started its 2025 season today with Kickoff, featuring a pair of North America vs. Brazil showdowns.

VCT Americas opened with a storied match-up, pitting two champion-winning organizations against each other. However, 2024 was challenging for the former titans of Americas, with both missing Stage 2 playoffs. EG looked to renew its roster with icy and yay , while Loud brought in dgzin and v1nny after saadhak and Less moved to EMEA.

Split was chosen as the first map, with yay making his VCT Americas debut on Chamber. The pick proved decisive, contributing to a strong 8-4 defensive half. Though Loud closed the gap in the second half, pulling off a comeback into 14-12 overtime win. dgzin dominated on Raze, ending the game with an outstanding 28 kills.

Moving on to Pearl, both teams traded rounds in a tightly contested 6-6 first half. Despite EG winning both pistols, Loud pulled ahead in the second half with electric performances from v1nny and cauanzin , closing out the map 13-10.

A match-up immortalized by TxoziN 's history-changing knife kill in 2023, 100 Thieves faced off against MIBR in the second match of Kickoff. Starting on Lotus, 100T launched an explosive attack spearheaded by Asuna on Neon, ending the half 10-2. MIBR could not stop the bleeding in the second half, losing 4-13 behind Asuna's 26 kills and six first-bloods.
