Media: 3DMAX to swap bodyy for misutaaa, NBK- to join as assistant coach

3DMAX are set to make changes by bringing onboard a trio from GenOne according to French journalist neL, with Kévin "⁠misutaaa⁠" Rabier replacing Alexandre "⁠bodyy⁠" Pianaro, French legend Nathan "⁠NBK-⁠" Schmitt joining the staff as assistant coach, and Damien "⁠wasiNk⁠" Dufour returning as head coach in place of Pierre "⁠YouKnow⁠" Marco.

The news comes after a slow start to the season for 3DMAX, who currently sport a 3-7 series record in HLTV-recorded matches after early exits from BLAST Bounty, IEM Krakow and PGL Cluj-Napoca. The French team also suffered a dire result in RES Showdown 3, the BLAST Open Rotterdam qualifier, losing to mix team M1X KS.

Should the news come to pass it would represent a first move into coaching for NBK-, who began his professional career back in 2009 in CS:Source. The 31-year-old has won numerous trophies across his career including two Majors, representing teams such as VeryGames, LDLC, Envy, Vitality and G2 in a trophy-laden six years at the start of CS:GO.

Most recently NBK- was playing alongside younger French-speaking talents in GenOne, struggling for consistent runs in lower tier online competition but enjoying brief highlights such as a run to top four in NODWIN Clutch Series 4.

misutaaa would join 3DMAX from GenOne alongside NBK-, bringing the rifler to the top majority-French team for the first time since he departed Vitality in 2022. Since that time misutaaa tried his hand on international lineups, representing Into the Breach, 9INE and FUT, before returning to a French-speaking squad late last year in the form of GenOne.

wasiNk is familiar with 3DMAX having coached the team for over a year across 2024 and 2025, standing behind the squad at two Majors during that time, Perfect World Shanghai Major and BLAST.tv Austin Major.
