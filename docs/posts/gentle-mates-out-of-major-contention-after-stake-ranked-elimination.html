Gentle Mates out of Major contention after Stake Ranked elimination

Gentle Mates are out of the running for an invite to the IEM Cologne Major after an elimination at Stake Ranked Episode 1 by 9INE.

Alejandro "⁠alex⁠" Masanet's side was inside the predicted invite range for close to a month after a trophy lift at IstanbuLAN and a runners-up finish at Roman Imperium Cup V in February, but played no events for a month while several teams below them surged up the standings.

Their odds took a turn for the worse after a quarter-final exit at BC Game Masters Championship, where they beat HYPERSPIRIT and lost to BIG, making a good result at Stake Ranked crucial to rescuing their Major campaign from outside the invite threshold.

A gauntlet lower bracket run in Barcelona became a necessity after Gentle Mates lost a pivotal match in their opener to Ninjas in Pyjamas, who were in an almost identical spot, and the Spaniards couldn't live up to the challenge.

They took a map lead in their series against 9INE, a team they held a 4-0 record over in a head-to-head that dates back to August last year, but watched their Major chances slip away after a blowout loss on their own Nuke pick (4-13) and a flat offense on the Overpass decider (9-13).
