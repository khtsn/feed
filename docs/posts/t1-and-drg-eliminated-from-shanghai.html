T1 and DRG eliminated from Shanghai

In a relatively quick four-map day, T1 and DRG became the first teams eliminated from Masters Shanghai. LEV dominated T1, and FUT squeaked by DRG to qualify to the deciding round of the Swiss stage.

The matchups for the deciding round have been drawn. FUT will face LEV while the two teams that lost in the winners matches, Heretics and FPX, will match up.
