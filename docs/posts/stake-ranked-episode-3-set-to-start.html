Stake Ranked Episode 3 set to start

Stake Ranked Episode 3 is set to begin on Wednesday in Barcelona, Spain, featuring a $100,000 prize pool and utilizing a double-elimination, best-of-three bracket until the best-of-five grand final to crown a champion.

The eight-team tournament will mark the debut of HEROIC's new roster with Antonio "⁠MartinezSa⁠" Martinez and Ludvig "⁠Brollan⁠" Brolin, with the team facing off against 3DMAX in their opening game.

Also among the attendees are paiN, Gentle Mates, Wildcard, and reigning Stake Ranked champions Ninjas in Pyjamas, who will play their second LAN with academy member Roman "⁠n0te⁠" Hamze after suffering an early elimination at XSE Pro League Guangzhou.

Gentle Mates will also play the event with Joey "⁠CRUC1AL⁠" Steusel as a stand-in after losing MartinezSa to HEROIC. The French organization is in the midst of negotiating the sale of its roster and an exit from Counter-Strike, and a good result to increase its VRS rank would go a long way in helping those talks after losing a key member of the roster.
