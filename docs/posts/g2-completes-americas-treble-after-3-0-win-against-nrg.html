G2 completes Americas treble after 3-0 win against NRG

G2 Esports has defeated NRG 3-0 in the Americas Stage 2 grand final, winning their third-straight Americas trophy. They will go to Champions: Paris as the top Americas seed, and await for the conclusion of the group draw to learn their opponents in the top VCT event.

The match opened on G2's pick, Bind, with NRG going up in the first half. A classic 9-3 curse followed, however, as G2 recovered to win the map 13-11.

G2 remained in control during Lotus, for a less close result on their opponent's map. G2 went up 7-5 in the first half, and a round winstreak after the break helped them reach match point. NRG briefly delayed the inevitable, but G2 ultimately ended the map 13-8, one game away from a perfect regional season.

The final map would be Ascent, where G2 took a strong lead despite their relatively defensive composition. NRG came close to equalizing, looking for a comeback, but G2 was able to deny them recovery to win the third map 13-10.

The victory comes after ups-and-downs for G2, having had to replace veteran player leaf with BABYBAY earlier this stage, as well as capping off a redemption year after a relatively dry spell in 2024 after Ascension.

Both teams will return in a few weeks to represent Americas in Paris. Joining them will be Sentinels and MIBR, who qualified off of points thanks to their performance throughout the season.
