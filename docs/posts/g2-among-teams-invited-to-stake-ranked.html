G2 among teams invited to Stake Ranked

Six teams hoping to acquire last-minute points before the IEM Cologne Major invite cut-off have accepted invitations to play in Barcelona.

The organizers of Stake Ranked Episode 1 have announced the six teams who have accepted invites to compete at the event taking place in Barcelona, Spain from April 1-4.

G2, GamerLegion, HEROIC, Gentle Mates, Ninjas in Pyjamas and BetBoom will participate in the $100,000 tournament with hopes of accruing some last-minute VRS points ahead of the invite cut-off for the IEM Cologne Major.

Other than G2, these are all teams under significant pressure with one month to go, currently projected to finish either just about within or outside of the 17 invites slated to go to European teams on April 6.

Two more teams will join the aforementioned six at the Barcelona event, coming from the 16-team closed qualifier, which features the likes of Passion UA and fnatic and kicks off on Wednesday.
