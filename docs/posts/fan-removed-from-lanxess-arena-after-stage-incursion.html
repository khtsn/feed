Fan removed from LANXESS Arena after stage incursion

ESL increased security measures before and after the incident.

Additional security was posted around the catwalk for the second semi-final between Falcons and Spirit

An audience member was removed from the LANXESS Arena premises at the IEM Cologne Major after he ran onto the stage during the second map of the semi-final match between Falcons and Spirit.

ESL had pre-emptively increased security around the catwalk ahead of the match in anticipation of such an incident, HLTV knows, but was unable to stop the incursion.

More security members were posted around the catwalk and stage after the incursion took place "to help prevent similar incidents from occurring in the future."

ESL provided the following comment to HLTV about the situation: "Earlier today, a security breach occurred when a fan gained unauthorized access to the stage area. We took immediate action and removed the offender from the venue," the statement reads.

"Additional security measures have since been implemented to help prevent similar incidents from occurring in the future. The safety and security of players, talent, staff, and attendees remain our top priority, and we take matters of this nature very seriously."
