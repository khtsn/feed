See the looks from fashion’s biggest night.

The Styles Desk

May 6, 2024Updated 6:24 p.m. ET

Curiosity around how guests would interpret this year’s “Garden of Time” theme was quickly answered with a flurry of florals, vintage, leather and more at this year’s Met Gala, fashion’s biggest night held on the first Monday in May.

Celebrities, executives, musicians, politicians and more showed off every angle of every look for photographers and fans. Guests sashayed down an off-white carpet dotted with teal borders and posed in front of backdrops of vegetation and flora, creating an ethereal quality to the evening’s festivities.
