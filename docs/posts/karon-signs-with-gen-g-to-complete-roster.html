Karon signs with Gen.G to complete roster

14 hours ago

Gen.G Gen.G Korea Rank #4 Munchkin Byeon Sang-beom (변상범) t3xture Kim Na-ra (김나라) Meteor Kim Tae-O (김태오) Lakia Kim Jong-min (김종민) Karon Kim Won-tae (김원태) have onboarded Kim "Karon" Won-tae (김원태) to finalize their roster ahead of 2024.

The talent hails from pug team No No No No No No Inactive Impact Cloy Karon Kim Won-tae (김원태) Rico Pooky , a roster that competed in the Korea Challengers League and missed out on the Advance Stage by just one map, crushing any dreams of making Pacific Ascension early on.

Gen.G recently released most of their roster after failing to break out of their regional leagues, most notably placing fourth in the playoffs of the Pacific League, just one placement away from a ticket to Masters: Tokyo.

Relegated to the Pacific LCQ, Gen.G battled through the event in hopes of making Champions, but were sent home by Team Secret Team Secret Asia-Pacific Rank #13 invy Adrian Jiggs Reyes BORKUM Jim Timbreza JessieVash Jessie Cuyco Jremy Jeremy Cabrera NDG Noel De Guia with a top three placement. Shortly after, the organization opted for a rebuild around Kim "Meteor" Tae-O (김태오) and signed various players from multiple different teams after announcing tryouts.

Karon's signing brings the active player count on the team to five and allows for a couple weeks of practice ahead of the recently-announced Convergence OFF//SEASON event.
