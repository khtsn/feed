Oct. 6, 2023Updated 7:28 a.m. ET

Listen and follow The Daily

The ouster of House Speaker Kevin McCarthy a few days ago demonstrated how powerful a small group of hard-right House Republicans have become and how deep their grievances run.

We speak to one of the eight republicans who brought down Mr. McCarthy: Representative Tim Burchett of Tennessee.

On today’s episode

  • Representative Tim Burchett of Tennessee’s 2nd Congressional District.

Background reading

  • How have the Republicans who ousted Mr. McCarthy antagonized him before?

  • Although some names have started to be bandied about, there is no clear replacement candidate for the speaker’s position.
