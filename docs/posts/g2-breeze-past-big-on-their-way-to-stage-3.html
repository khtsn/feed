G2 breeze past BIG on their way to Stage 3

G2 have breezed into the IEM Cologne Stage 3 after securing a dominant win over BIG (Inferno 13-3, Mirage 13-8).

Nemanja "⁠huNter-⁠" Kovač's troops, who came into the stage as one of the favorites to advance, righted their wrongs from Sunday's loss to FUT to meet expectations with a 3-1 record that also includes wins over Monte and M80.

Guy "⁠NertZ⁠" Iluz was the star of the show in their match against the majority-German squad, first putting up an incredible 2.69 rating on Inferno before putting the final nail in the BIG coffin with a nice double spraydown on Mirage.

BIG have one more chance to secure an appearance in front of their home crowd in Stage 3, with their 2-2 match coming against B8 or M80.
