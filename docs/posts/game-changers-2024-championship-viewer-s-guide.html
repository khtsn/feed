Game Changers 2024 Championship viewer's guide

After an exciting year throughout the VCT, the pinnacle of Game Changers competition has returned for yet another LAN event. The Game Changers Championship returns to Berlin, Germany for its third appearance since 2022.

The competition will run from Nov. 8 to Nov. 17 and – for the first time since its inception – will feature not eight, but 10 total teams from around the globe. Each team fought their way to Berlin through qualifiers, main events, or circuit point accumulation. Both former GCC winners, G2 Gozen G2 Gozen Europe Rank #4 rezq Eda Naz Akçocuk amy Amy Lai Vania Aleyna Keskin Petra Petra Stoker mimi Michaela Lintrup and Shopify Rebellion Shopify Rebellion North America Rank #23 meL Melanie Capone alexis Alexis Guarrasi Noia Nicole Tierce florescent Ava Eugene sarahcat Sarah Simpson , are among the teams vying for the trophy.

The standard double-elimination bracket will continue to be utilized throughout the event, this time with two extra upper rounds to allocate for the extra teams. Each matchup will be Bo3 save for the lower and grand finals, which will both feature Bo5 matches.

In an interesting turn of events, teams from the same region have been seeded on the same side of the bracket, which was avoided last year to ensure diverse matchups.

Broadcast drops are back, and this time, Riot have added additional options for viewers to obtain rewards. Viewers can obtain drops by connecting their Valorant accounts with their Twitch or YouTube accounts and watching official streams.

The “Main Character” title is obtainable by watching any match throughout the window of Nov. 8 - Nov. 16.

The “Game Changers 2024” player card can be obtained by watching the grand finals on Nov. 17.

Four exclusive titles are also available by watching official on-site watchparties. By tuning in anytime between Nov. 15 - Nov. 17, viewers can earn the following titles as well as the “Main Character” title:

  • “Ribbit” - Kydae

  • “I am Her” - Helydia

  • “13-0 Club” - Mixwell

  • “Queen” - Sliggy

Riot will stream the entire event on their Twitch and YouTube channels. Viewers can also watch different streams in different languages.
