Short news: Week 27

The Moroccan open qualifier for the Esports Nations Cup has come to an end, with the roster headlined by Adil "⁠ScreaM⁠" Benrlitom, Nabil "⁠Nivera⁠" Benrlitom and Ali "⁠hAdji⁠" Haïnouss cruising through the 11-team competition.

The team is rounded out by Mohamed "⁠Hawk⁠" El Kbiach and Rayan "⁠Yeneurs⁠" Admi.

The team will now compete in the North African qualifier against the winners of the qualifiers from Algeria, Tunisia, and Egypt, with the winner booking a spot at the Esports Nations Cup.
