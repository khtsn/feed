Liquid bench siuhy

The Polish in-game leader has been sidelined ahead of the addition of JT.

Liquid has announced that Kamil "⁠siuhy⁠" Szkaradek has been moved to the bench, ending the Pole's 15-month tenure on the active roster.

He will be replaced by Johnny "⁠JT⁠" Theodosiou, who was submitted as part of the organization's roster for BLAST Bounty Season 2 but has yet to be formally announced.

siuhy joined Liquid in April 2025 after his removal from MOUZ three months prior, but never came close to the kind of success he'd had in the German organization in what became a dismal year with Liquid.

The team cascaded down the VRS after repeated group stage exits and a miserable run of results, including fumbling at tier-two LANs they attended to just barely salvage invites to Majors.

Liquid sign Jorko

Several roster changes did little to curtail the downward spiral in the new year, with Liquid only reaching the playoffs at BLAST Bounty Season 1 through the online group stage and ending the season with a 2-3 exit from Stage 1 of the IEM Cologne Major.

During their 2026 run, Liquid averaged among the worst T-side win rates in the entire scene — a far cry from the level siuhy showed on MOUZ.

His removal and the impending addition of JT is the second move of Liquid's off-season after the organization replaced Roland "⁠ultimate⁠" Tomkowiak with Georgi "⁠Jorko⁠" Mitev, with the new lineup set for a trial by fire as it will debut against Vitality on July 24 in BLAST Bounty Season 2.
