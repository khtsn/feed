SOOP Valorant League reaches final four

The SOOP Valorant Legaue has concluded its group stage, narrowing a field of eight teams and two groups into a four-team bracket. The competition's group stage mirrors the group stage used at Champions Seoul, arranging teams in groups of four with matches to be played in a double-elimination tournament.

With powerhouse teams from North America, China, the host nation South Korea, and qualified teams from Thailand and Vietnam, the group stage was filled with tight matches, upsets, and the birth of a Cinderella story.
