Paper Rex runs over G2 Esports with 2-0 win

Paper Rex continues their run with a 2-0 victory against the top Americas seed, G2 Esports, in the opening Masters: Toronto playoffs match. As a top seed, G2 had picked Paper Rex as their opponent during the live draw.

The match began with Paper Rex's map pick, with a strong showing from G2 to start the map. Paper Rex was able to mount a comeback after a PatMen clutch, winning the opener 13-10. Lotus capped off the series, with a 13-11 closing out the series 2-0 and sending Paper Rex onwards through the upper bracket.

Paper Rex will next play the winner of Sentinels vs. Xi Lai Gaming, while G2 will descend to the lower bracket, awaiting an opponent for their elimination series.
