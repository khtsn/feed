Oxygen Esports disbands Valorant roster

This article was updated to reflect dapr and Reduxx's recent announcements of a full team disbanding.

After a fourth-place finish in Challengers playoffs and missing out on Ascension, Oxygen Esports Oxygen Esports Inactive skuba Logan Jenkins mitch Mitch Semago Verno Andrew Maust Reduxx Yassin Aboulalazm dapr Michael Gulino has disbanded their Valorant roster of Mitch "mitch" Semago, Logan "skuba" Jenkins, Michael "dapr" Gulino, Andrew "Verno" Maust, Yassin "Reduxx" Aboulalazm and in addition to head coach Malkolm "bonkar" Renchand assistant coach, Rustun "Rustun" Lusk.

Oxygen entered into NA with its academy roster in 2022, featuring emerging talents like Reduxx and Verno, who would eventually join the main lineup. However, they did not participate in VCT. By the end of 2022, Oxygen replaced their academy team with a squad designed to compete in Challengers and ascend to the Americas League. Retaining Verno, they added seasoned players, ex-C9 pro mitch and controller-player skuba. The team easily qualified for Challengers and steadily improved over the season, bolstered by Reduxx's return after he reached the age threshold. Nevertheless, a loss to eventual Ascension winner, The Guard The Guard Inactive JonahP Jonah Pulice trent Trent Cairns neT Michael Bernet valyn Jacob Batio tex Ian Botsch , ended their playoff run at fourth place.

Heading into 2024, Oxygen added Masters-winning sentinel dapr, forming the latest iteration of the roster. They dominated the offseason, winning multiple tournaments, including the Ludwig x Tarik Invitational 2, and were favorites to reach Ascension. Early in the year, they lived up to expectations, achieving a 3-2 regular season record and winning the Mid-Season Cup. So far, the organization has not announced whether it plans to continue participating in NA Challengers in 2025 or is permanently exiting the scene.

Despite missing out on Ascension, the former Oxygen squad, now free agents, will be compelling options for Partnership teams looking to rebuild for 2025, though Reduxx will turn 18 midway through next season.
