But Ivan "⁠zweih⁠" Gogin insists the collapse against The MongolZ had nothing to do with such public criticism from their coach, speaking to HLTV.

"No, [it didn't affect us]," he said. "They just get some fun out of it, they understand that dastan was a person like that for all his career and they just need to accept it, like don't do anything with it. I don't think it mattered in this game."

That's not to say zweih did not take the loss seriously. "It's fully our game and we just trolled," the 18-year-old says. "We can't lose 10 CT rounds in a row like that."
