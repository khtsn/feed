Kursy joins 3DMAX as rifler

The Frenchman returns to the server after nine months of inactivity.

3DMAX have announced the signing of Jeremy "⁠Kursy⁠" Gast, with the ex-GamerLegion sniper set to take Pierre "⁠Ex3rcice⁠" Bulinge's spot on the roster after the 26-year-old was benched earlier on Wednesday.

Kursy will join the team as a rifler due to Bryan "⁠Maka⁠" Canda already donning the Big Green in the squad, as he returns to the role he held early in his career.

The 24-year-old joins the team after a nine-month period of inactivity following his benching on GamerLegion, where he struggled for impact and averaged a 0.95 rating across his tenure.

His first appearance in a 3DMAX jersey will come at XSE Pro League 2026, where the French squad has been matched up against Ninjas in Pyjamas in the first round.
