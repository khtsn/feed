Super DraculaN 1 groups announced

GamerLegion will travel to Bucharest next week for Super DraculaN

Digital Crusade has unveiled the full list of participants and the groups for its next event, Super DraculaN Season 1. Featuring a $150,000 prize pool, the Romanian tournament will be one of the biggest LAN events held during the player break.

GamerLegion, Sharks, FOKUS and OG are among the headline names on the list of 16 teams for the tournament, which will run from June 23-28.

TYLOO and Wildcard were initially invited but later withdrew. The Chinese team will attend the XSE Pro League instead, while the Americas side was announced as one of the eight teams attending Stake Ranked Episode 3 in mid-July.

Super DraculaN will begin with two double-elimination groups of eight teams, with the top three teams from each group advancing to the single-elimination brackets. All matches will be played in a best-of-three format except the grand final, which will be a best-of-five clash.
