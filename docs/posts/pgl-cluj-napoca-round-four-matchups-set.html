PGL Cluj-Napoca round four matchups set

The matchups for the 2-1 and 1-2 pool of PGL Cluj-Napoca's Swiss group stage have been set following the conclusion of day three, where Falcons and Vitality secured their spots in the arena while 3DMAX and paiN suffered elimination.

In the 2-1 pool, MOUZ are set to face off against talk of the town FUT before Natus Vincere take on The MongolZ. A rematch of the BLAST Bounty semi-final, FURIA vs. PARIVISION, will be the final progression game of the day.

Among the teams facing elimination, FaZe will fight for their tournament lives against Astralis, B8 will face Aurora, and G2 will take on HEROIC.
