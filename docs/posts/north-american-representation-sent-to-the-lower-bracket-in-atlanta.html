North American representation sent to the lower bracket in Atlanta

Four of four pistols were not enough for FaZe to win their opener

The first day of IEM Atlanta has come to an end, with eight winners advancing to the group stage's upper bracket semi-finals and eight teams knocked down to the lower bracket. It was a particularly hard day for North American fans, as FaZe, Liquid, NRG, Passion UA, and M80 were all sent to the lower bracket.
