FURIA facing elimination as day two of EPL S23 Stage 2 ends

Day two of ESL Pro League Season 23 Stage 2 has drawn to a close, with big names FURIA and FaZe sinking to the 0-2 pool after enduring losses to 3DMAX and paiN, respectively.

Rounding out the 0-2 pool with FaZe and FURIA are HEROIC and Monte. Meanwhile, MOUZ, G2, The MongolZ, and Spirit are each one win away from playoffs after securing spots in the 2-0 pool.

Group Swiss

FaZe and FURIA on the verge of 0-3 exits

After FaZe suffered a narrow 1-2 loss at the hands of paiN earlier in the day to get sent to the 0-2 pool, FURIA have similarly joined them after a 0-2 loss to the new-look 3DMAX.

FURIA suffered slow starts on both 3DMAX's pick of Dust2 and their own pick of Overpass, recording 4-8 and 3-9 first halves respectively. FURIA managed to make Dust2 competitive on the defense, clawing back from a 4-10 deficit, but 3DMAX managed to clinch extra rounds where they went on to take the map 19-16 in double overtime. FURIA had a harder time getting back into Overpass, ultimately falling 8-13 after a vain attempt on the CT side.

3DMAX's victory over FURIA marks the largest scalp that the French side has claimed since adding Kévin "⁠misutaaa⁠" Rabier to their ranks in place of Alexandre "⁠bodyy⁠" Pianaro just less than two weeks ago. 3DMAX, who began their run in this event in Stage 1, will now face misutaaa's former FUT teammates in the 1-1 pool.

MOUZ and Spirit avoid upsets of their own

While FURIA and FaZe languish at the prospect of facing elimination, MOUZ and Spirit have locked in their spots in the 2-0 pool, just one more win away from playoffs.

This did not come easy, however, as MOUZ and Spirit nearly slipped against their respective opponents of Astralis and B8. MOUZ endured a 14-16 overtime loss on Ancient to kick off their series against Astralis, bottling a 9-2 lead, but recovered with statement 13-1 and 13-3 map wins to clinch their place in the 2-0 pool.

Spirit, meanwhile, got trounced 4-13 on B8's map pick of Mirage, but composed themselves in the latter two maps of the series. After a tight 6-6 first half on their pick of Nuke, Spirit rallied on the defense to take the map 13-9, before finishing off B8 on Anubis 13-7 thanks to a stellar showing from Dmitry "⁠sh1ro⁠" Sokolov.

FUT stave off HEROIC

Among the day's other matches, FUT, who moved through Stage 1 of the event with a flawless 3-0 record, managed to avoid being sent to the 0-2 pool after scoring a 2-1 victory over HEROIC.

The two sides took turns trading lopsided map victories; FUT kicked the series off with a 13-3 Nuke victory where HEROIC's only saving grace was a Tec-9 1v4 from Simon "⁠yxngstxr⁠" Boije. HEROIC rebounded with a 13-5 win on FUT's pick of Ancient, before Christoffer "⁠Chr1zN⁠" Storgaard's men were ultimately trounced on Mirage 13-3 to get shipped off to the 0-2 pool.
