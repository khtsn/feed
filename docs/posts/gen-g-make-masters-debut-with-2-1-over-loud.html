Gen.G make Masters debut with 2-1 over LOUD

Gen.G have defeated LOUD 2-1. They are now one win away from making playoffs and top four at their first-ever Masters event. LOUD, meanwhile, need to avoid a second loss to stay in the tournament.

The match started on Icebox, where Gen.G denied a comeback from LOUD with a 13-11 win. A surprise Breeze appearance went LOUD's way, closing the map 13-8. Ascent went back in Gen.G's favor, a dominant 13-5 awarding them the series win.

LOUD are the third top team to be defeated by Gen.G, who qualified to Madrid with victories against DRX and Paper Rex.
