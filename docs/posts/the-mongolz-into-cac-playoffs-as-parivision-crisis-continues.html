The MongolZ into CAC playoffs as PARIVISION crisis continues

The MongolZ have booked a quarter-final berth at CS Asia Championships thanks to a 2-0 victory over PARIVISION, consigning the Russian side to another disappointing tournament exit.

With their victory The MongolZ have bounced back into the playoffs after a disappointing campaign at PGL Astana, and can look forward to a bout with Legacy later today.

The recent nose-dive of PARIVISION's form has been concerning, and those concerns will only grow after this tournament performance. The Russian side were particularly woeful on the T side today, struggling for opening kills and being far too easy for The MongolZ to read.
