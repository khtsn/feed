NertZ: "When your coach and captain show you [trust], it's super important because I don't think it works like this in every team"

G2 are slowly integrating NertZ map by map after signing him halfway through the season.

G2 endured a few frustrating hiccups en route to Stage 3, but got the job done against BIG to secure their passage into the next round.

The scoreline itself was convincing, especially on Inferno when Guy "⁠NertZ⁠" Iluz posted a 2.69 rating after dominating the B bomb-site. Having permabanned the map on ENCE and HEROIC, it's a map NertZ used to struggle on but a few tweaks at boot camp has got him firing on all cylinders.

G2 breeze past BIG on their way to Stage 3

Since he joined mid-season, NertZ tells us that feeling comfortable on every map is still a work in progress.

But his ideas are being listened to, the reunion with Eetu "⁠sAw⁠" Saha and Álvaro "⁠SunPayus⁠" García fits him nicely, and, map by map, he is getting there.

As for Stage 3, his goal is simple: Against tougher opposition, "we need to show that we're going to play the same as we are playing right now."
